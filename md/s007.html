Al-A'raaf
The Heights, The Faculty of Discernment
7:[1-10] The Rasools as well as the people to whom they were sent shall be questioned on the Day of Judgment
7:1
المص
Alif Lam Meem Sad.(These letters are one of the miracles of the Quran (القرآن) and none but Allah (الله) (Alone) knows their meanings).
7:2
كِتَابٌ أُنْزِلَ إِلَيْكَ فَلَا يَكُنْ فِي صَدْرِكَ حَرَجٌ مِنْهُ لِتُنْذِرَ بِهِ وَذِكْرَى لِلْمُؤْمِنِينَ
(This is the) Book (the Quran (القرآن)) sent down unto you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW), so let not your breast be narrow therefrom, that you warn thereby, and a reminder unto the believers.
7:3
اتَّبِعُوا مَا أُنْزِلَ إِلَيْكُمْ مِنْ رَبِّكُمْ وَلَا تَتَّبِعُوا مِنْ دُونِهِ أَوْلِيَاءَ ۗ قَلِيلًا مَا تَذَكَّرُونَ
(Say (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) to these idolaters (pagan Arabs) of your folk:)
Follow what has been sent down unto you from your Lord (the Quran (القرآن) and Prophet Muhammad (محمد) SAW‘s Sun (الشمس)nah), and follow not any Auliya (protectors and helpers, etc. who order you to associate partners in worship with Allah (الله)), besides Him (Allah (الله)). Little do you remember!
7:4
وَكَمْ مِنْ قَرْيَةٍ أَهْلَكْنَاهَا فَجَاءَهَا بَأْسُنَا بَيَاتًا أَوْ هُمْ قَائِلُونَ
And a great number of towns (their population) We destroyed (for their crimes). Our torment came upon them (suddenly) by night or while they were sleeping for their afternoon rest.
7:5
فَمَا كَانَ دَعْوَاهُمْ إِذْ جَاءَهُمْ بَأْسُنَا إِلَّا أَنْ قَالُوا إِنَّا كُنَّا ظَالِمِينَ
No cry did they utter when Our Torment came upon them but this: ‘Verily, we were Zalimoon (polytheists and wrongdoers, etc.)‘.
7:6
فَلَنَسْأَلَنَّ الَّذِينَ أُرْسِلَ إِلَيْهِمْ وَلَنَسْأَلَنَّ الْمُرْسَلِينَ
Then surely, We shall question those (people) to whom it (the Book) was sent and verily, We shall question the Messengers.
7:7
فَلَنَقُصَّنَّ عَلَيْهِمْ بِعِلْمٍ ۖ وَمَا كُنَّا غَائِبِينَ
Then surely, We shall narrate unto them (their whole story) with knowledge, and indeed We were not absent.
7:8
وَالْوَزْنُ يَوْمَئِذٍ الْحَقُّ ۚ
And the weighing on that day (Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة)) will be the true (weighing).
فَمَنْ ثَقُلَتْ مَوَازِينُهُ فَأُولَئِكَ هُمُ الْمُفْلِحُونَ
So as for those whose scale (of good deeds) will be heavy, they will be the successful (by entering Paradise (الجنة)).
7:9
وَمَنْ خَفَّتْ مَوَازِينُهُ فَأُولَئِكَ الَّذِينَ خَسِرُوا أَنْفُسَهُمْ بِمَا كَانُوا بِآيَاتِنَا يَظْلِمُونَ
And as for those whose scale will be light, they are those who will lose their own selves (by entering Hell (جهنم))because they denied and rejected Our Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, etc).
7:10
وَلَقَدْ مَكَّنَّاكُمْ فِي الْأَرْضِ وَجَعَلْنَا لَكُمْ فِيهَا مَعَايِشَ ۗ قَلِيلًا مَا تَشْكُرُونَ
And surely, We gave you authority on the earth and appointed for you therein provisions (for your life). Little thanks do you give.
7:[11-18] The story of Adam and Iblees (Shaitãn) Shaitãn vowed to mislead Adam and his descendants
7:[19-25] Shaitãn cunningly seduced Adam and Eve to disobey Allah. Their repentance and Allah‘s conditional acceptance
7:11
وَلَقَدْ خَلَقْنَاكُمْ ثُمَّ صَوَّرْنَاكُمْ ثُمَّ قُلْنَا لِلْمَلَائِكَةِ اسْجُدُوا لِآدَمَ
And surely, We created you (your father Adam (آدم)) and then gave you shape (the noble shape of a human being), then We told the angels, ‘Prostrate to Adam (آدم)‘,
فَسَجَدُوا إِلَّا إِبْلِيسَ لَمْ يَكُنْ مِنَ السَّاجِدِينَ
and they prostrated, except Iblees (إبليس) (Satan (الشيطان)), he refused to be of those who prostrate.
7:12
قَالَ مَا مَنَعَكَ أَلَّا تَسْجُدَ إِذْ أَمَرْتُكَ ۖ
(Allah (الله)) said: ‘What prevented you (O Iblees (إبليس)) that you did not prostrate, when I commanded you?‘
قَالَ أَنَا خَيْرٌ مِنْهُ خَلَقْتَنِي مِنْ نَارٍ وَخَلَقْتَهُ مِنْ طِينٍ
Iblees (إبليس) said: ‘I am better than him (Adam (آدم)), You created me from fire, and him You created from clay.‘
7:13
قَالَ فَاهْبِطْ مِنْهَا فَمَا يَكُونُ لَكَ أَنْ تَتَكَبَّرَ فِيهَا فَاخْرُجْ إِنَّكَ مِنَ الصَّاغِرِينَ
(Allah (الله)) said: ‘(O Iblees (إبليس)) get down from this (Paradise (الجنة)), it is not for you to be arrogant here. Get out, for you are of those humiliated and disgraced.‘
7:14
قَالَ أَنْظِرْنِي إِلَى يَوْمِ يُبْعَثُونَ
(Iblees (إبليس)) said: ‘Allow me respite till the Day they are raised up (i.e. the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة)).‘
7:15
قَالَ إِنَّكَ مِنَ الْمُنْظَرِينَ
(Allah (الله)) said: ‘You are of those allowed respite.‘
7:16
قَالَ فَبِمَا أَغْوَيْتَنِي لَأَقْعُدَنَّ لَهُمْ صِرَاطَكَ الْمُسْتَقِيمَ
(Iblees (إبليس)) said: ‘Because You have sent me astray, surely I will sit in wait against them (human beings) on Your Straight Path.
7:17
ثُمَّ لَآتِيَنَّهُمْ مِنْ بَيْنِ أَيْدِيهِمْ وَمِنْ خَلْفِهِمْ وَعَنْ أَيْمَانِهِمْ وَعَنْ شَمَائِلِهِمْ ۖ
Then I will come to them from before them and behind them, from their right and from their left,
وَلَا تَجِدُ أَكْثَرَهُمْ شَاكِرِينَ
and You will not find most of them as thankful ones (i.e. they will not be dutiful to You).‘
7:18
قَالَ اخْرُجْ مِنْهَا مَذْءُومًا مَدْحُورًا ۖ
(Allah (الله)) said (to Iblees (إبليس)) ‘Get out from this (Paradise (الجنة)) disgraced and expelled.
لَمَنْ تَبِعَكَ مِنْهُمْ لَأَمْلَأَنَّ جَهَنَّمَ مِنْكُمْ أَجْمَعِينَ
Whoever of them (mankind) will follow you, then surely I will fill Hell (جهنم) with you all.‘
7:19
‏وَيَا آدَمُ اسْكُنْ أَنْتَ وَزَوْجُكَ الْجَنَّةَ فَكُلَا مِنْ حَيْثُ شِئْتُمَا
And O Adam (آدم)! Dwell you and your wife in Paradise (الجنة), and eat thereof as you both wish,
وَلَا تَقْرَبَا هَذِهِ الشَّجَرَةَ فَتَكُونَا مِنَ الظَّالِمِينَ
but approach not this tree otherwise you both will be of the Zalimoon (unjust and wrongdoers).‘
7:20
فَوَسْوَسَ لَهُمَا الشَّيْطَانُ لِيُبْدِيَ لَهُمَا مَا وُورِيَ عَنْهُمَا مِنْ سَوْآتِهِمَا
Then Shaitan (Satan (الشيطان)) whispered suggestions to them both in order to uncover that which was hidden from them of their private parts (before);
وَقَالَ مَا نَهَاكُمَا رَبُّكُمَا عَنْ هَذِهِ الشَّجَرَةِ إِلَّا أَنْ تَكُونَا مَلَكَيْنِ أَوْ تَكُونَا مِنَ الْخَالِدِينَ
he said: ‘Your Lord did not forbid you this tree save you should become angels or become of the immortals.‘
7:21
وَقَاسَمَهُمَا إِنِّي لَكُمَا لَمِنَ النَّاصِحِينَ
And he (Shaitan (Satan (الشيطان))) swore by Allah (الله) to them both (saying): ‘Verily, I am one of the sincere well wishers for you both.‘
7:22
فَدَلَّاهُمَا بِغُرُورٍ ۚ
So he misled them with deception.
فَلَمَّا ذَاقَا الشَّجَرَةَ بَدَتْ لَهُمَا سَوْآتُهُمَا وَطَفِقَا يَخْصِفَانِ عَلَيْهِمَا مِنْ وَرَقِ الْجَنَّةِ ۖ
Then when they tasted of the tree, that which was hidden from them of their shame (private parts) became manifest to them and they began to stick together the leaves of Paradise (الجنة) over themselves (in order to cover their shame).
وَنَادَاهُمَا رَبُّهُمَا أَلَمْ أَنْهَكُمَا عَنْ تِلْكُمَا الشَّجَرَةِ وَأَقُلْ لَكُمَا إِنَّ الشَّيْطَانَ لَكُمَا عَدُوٌّ مُبِينٌ
And their Lord called out to them (saying): ‘Did I not forbid you that tree and tell you: Verily, Shaitan (Satan (الشيطان)) is an open enemy unto you?‘
7:23
قَالَا رَبَّنَا ظَلَمْنَا أَنْفُسَنَا وَإِنْ لَمْ تَغْفِرْ لَنَا وَتَرْحَمْنَا لَنَكُونَنَّ مِنَ الْخَاسِرِينَ
They said: ‘Our Lord! We have wronged ourselves. If You forgive us not, and bestow not upon us Your Mercy, we shall certainly be of the losers.‘
7:24
قَالَ اهْبِطُوا بَعْضُكُمْ لِبَعْضٍ عَدُوٌّ ۖ وَلَكُمْ فِي الْأَرْضِ مُسْتَقَرٌّ وَمَتَاعٌ إِلَى حِينٍ
(Allah (الله)) said: ‘Get down, one of you an enemy to the other (i.e. Adam (آدم), Hawwa (Eve), and Shaitan (Satan (الشيطان)), etc.). On earth will be a dwelling place for you and an enjoyment, - for a time.‘
7:25
قَالَ فِيهَا تَحْيَوْنَ وَفِيهَا تَمُوتُونَ وَمِنْهَا تُخْرَجُونَ
He said: ‘Therein you shall live, and therein you shall die, and from it you shall be brought out (i.e.resurrected).‘
7:[26-31] Children of Adam are warned not to fall into the trap of Shaitãn like Adam. Allah never commands what is shameful
7:26
يَا بَنِي آدَمَ قَدْ أَنْزَلْنَا عَلَيْكُمْ لِبَاسًا يُوَارِي سَوْآتِكُمْ وَرِيشًا ۖ
O Children of Adam (آدم)! We have bestowed raiment upon you to cover yourselves (screen your private parts, etc.) and as an adornment,
وَلِبَاسُ التَّقْوَى ذَلِكَ خَيْرٌ ۚ
and the raiment of righteousness, that is better.
ذَلِكَ مِنْ آيَاتِ اللَّهِ لَعَلَّهُمْ يَذَّكَّرُونَ
Such are among the Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, etc.) of Allah (الله), that they may remember (i.e. leave falsehood and follow truth).
7:27
يَا بَنِي آدَمَ
O Children of Adam (آدم)!
لَا يَفْتِنَنَّكُمُ الشَّيْطَانُ كَمَا أَخْرَجَ أَبَوَيْكُمْ مِنَ الْجَنَّةِ يَنْزِعُ عَنْهُمَا لِبَاسَهُمَا لِيُرِيَهُمَا سَوْآتِهِمَا ۗ
Let not Shaitan (Satan (الشيطان)) deceive you, as he got your parents (Adam (آدم) and Hawwa (Eve)) out of Paradise (الجنة), stripping them of their raiments, to show them their private parts.
إِنَّهُ يَرَاكُمْ هُوَ وَقَبِيلُهُ مِنْ حَيْثُ لَا تَرَوْنَهُمْ ۗ
Verily, he and Qabeeluhu (his soldiers from the jinns or his tribe) see you from where you cannot see them.
إِنَّا جَعَلْنَا الشَّيَاطِينَ أَوْلِيَاءَ لِلَّذِينَ لَا يُؤْمِنُونَ
Verily, We made the Shayatin (devils) Auliya (protectors and helpers) for those who believe not.
7:28
وَإِذَا فَعَلُوا فَاحِشَةً قَالُوا وَجَدْنَا عَلَيْهَا آبَاءَنَا وَاللَّهُ أَمَرَنَا بِهَا ۗ
And when they commit a Fahisha (evil deed, going round the Kaaba (الكعبة) in naked state, every kind of unlawful sexual intercourse, etc.), they say: ‘We found our fathers doing it, and Allah (الله) has commanded us of it.‘
قُلْ إِنَّ اللَّهَ لَا يَأْمُرُ بِالْفَحْشَاءِ ۖ أَتَقُولُونَ عَلَى اللَّهِ مَا لَا تَعْلَمُونَ
Say: ‘Nay, Allah (الله) never commands of Fahisha. Do you say of Allah (الله) what you know not?
7:29
قُلْ أَمَرَ رَبِّي بِالْقِسْطِ ۖ وَأَقِيمُوا وُجُوهَكُمْ عِنْدَ كُلِّ مَسْجِدٍ وَادْعُوهُ مُخْلِصِينَ لَهُ الدِّينَ ۚ
Say (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW): My Lord has commanded justice and (said) that you should face Him only (i.e. worship none but Allah (الله) and face the Qiblah (قبلة), i.e. the Kaaba (الكعبة) at Makkah (مكة) during prayers) in each and every place of worship, in prayers (and not to face other false deities and idols), and invoke Him only making your religion sincere to Him (by not joining in worship any partner to Him and with the intention that you are doing your deeds for Allah (الله)‘s sake only.
كَمَا بَدَأَكُمْ تَعُودُونَ
As He brought you (into being) in the beginning, so shall you be brought into being (on the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة)) (in two groups, one as a blessed one (believers), and the other as a wretched one (disbelievers)).
7:30
فَرِيقًا هَدَى وَفَرِيقًا حَقَّ عَلَيْهِمُ الضَّلَالَةُ ۗ
A group He has guided, and a group deserved to be in error;
إِنَّهُمُ اتَّخَذُوا الشَّيَاطِينَ أَوْلِيَاءَ مِنْ دُونِ اللَّهِ وَيَحْسَبُونَ أَنَّهُمْ مُهْتَدُونَ
(because) surely they took the Shayatin (devils) as Auliya (protectors and helpers) instead of Allah (الله), and consider that they are guided.
7:31
يَا بَنِي آدَمَ خُذُوا زِينَتَكُمْ عِنْدَ كُلِّ مَسْجِدٍ وَكُلُوا وَاشْرَبُوا وَلَا تُسْرِفُوا ۚ
O Children of Adam (آدم)! Take your adornment (by wearing your clean clothes), while praying and going round (the Tawaf of ) the Kaaba (الكعبة), and eat and drink but waste not by extravagance,
إِنَّهُ لَا يُحِبُّ الْمُسْرِفِينَ
certainly He (Allah (الله)) likes not Al-Musrifoon (those who waste by extravagance).
7:[32-34] Command of Allah to wear proper and decent dress and eat good food
7:[35-39] Children of Adam are directed to follow the Guidance of Allah provided to them through His Rasools
7:32
قُلْ مَنْ حَرَّمَ زِينَةَ اللَّهِ الَّتِي أَخْرَجَ لِعِبَادِهِ وَالطَّيِّبَاتِ مِنَ الرِّزْقِ ۚ
Say (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW): ‘Who has forbidden the adoration with clothes given by Allah (الله), which He has produced for his slaves, and At-Taiyibat (all kinds of Halal (lawful) things) of food?‘
قُلْ هِيَ لِلَّذِينَ آمَنُوا فِي الْحَيَاةِ الدُّنْيَا خَالِصَةً يَوْمَ الْقِيَامَةِ ۗ
Say: ‘They are, in the life of this world, for those who believe, (and) exclusively for them (believers) on the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة) (the disbelievers will not share them).‘
كَذَلِكَ نُفَصِّلُ الْآيَاتِ لِقَوْمٍ يَعْلَمُونَ
Thus We explain the Ayat (Islam (الإسلام)ic laws) in detail for people who have knowledge.
7:33
قُلْ إِنَّمَا حَرَّمَ رَبِّيَ الْفَوَاحِشَ مَا ظَهَرَ مِنْهَا وَمَا بَطَنَ وَالْإِثْمَ وَالْبَغْيَ بِغَيْرِ الْحَقِّ
Say (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW): ‘(But) the things that my Lord has indeed forbidden are AlFawahish (great evil sins, every kind of unlawful sexual intercourse, etc.) whether committed openly or secretly, sins (of all kinds), unrighteous oppression,
وَأَنْ تُشْرِكُوا بِاللَّهِ مَا لَمْ يُنَزِّلْ بِهِ سُلْطَانًاوَأَنْ تَقُولُوا عَلَى اللَّهِ مَا لَا تَعْلَمُونَ
joining partners (in worship) with Allah (الله) for which He has given no authority, and saying things about Allah (الله) of which you have no knowledge.‘
7:34
وَلِكُلِّ أُمَّةٍ أَجَلٌ ۖ فَإِذَا جَاءَ أَجَلُهُمْ لَا يَسْتَأْخِرُونَ سَاعَةً ۖ وَلَا يَسْتَقْدِمُونَ
And every nation has its appointed term; when their term is reached, neither can they delay it nor can they advance it an hour (or a moment).
7:35
يَا بَنِي آدَمَ إِمَّا يَأْتِيَنَّكُمْ رُسُلٌ مِنْكُمْ يَقُصُّونَ عَلَيْكُمْ آيَاتِي ۙ
O Children of Adam (آدم)! If there come to you Messengers from amongst you, reciting to you, My Verses,
فَمَنِ اتَّقَى وَأَصْلَحَ فَلَا خَوْفٌ عَلَيْهِمْ وَلَا هُمْ يَحْزَنُونَ
then whosoever becomes pious and righteous, on them shall be no fear, nor shall they grieve.
7:36
‏وَالَّذِينَ كَذَّبُوا بِآيَاتِنَا وَاسْتَكْبَرُوا عَنْهَا أُولَئِكَ أَصْحَابُ النَّارِ ۖ هُمْ فِيهَا خَالِدُونَ
But those who reject Our Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, etc.) and treat them with arrogance, they are the dwellers of the (Hell (جهنم)) Fire, they will abide therein forever.
7:37
فَمَنْ أَظْلَمُ مِمَّنِ افْتَرَى عَلَى اللَّهِ كَذِبًا أَوْ كَذَّبَ بِآيَاتِهِ ۚ
Who is more unjust than one who invents a lie against Allah (الله) or rejects His Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, etc.)?
أُولَئِكَ يَنَالُهُمْ نَصِيبُهُمْ مِنَ الْكِتَابِ ۖ
For such their appointed portion (good things of this worldly life and their period of stay therein) will reach them from the Book (of Decrees)
حَتَّى إِذَا جَاءَتْهُمْ رُسُلُنَا يَتَوَفَّوْنَهُمْ قَالُوا أَيْنَ مَا كُنْتُمْ تَدْعُونَ مِنْ دُونِ اللَّهِ ۖ
until, when Our Messengers (the angel of death and his assistants) come to them to take their souls, they (the angels) will say: ‘Where are those whom you used to invoke and worship besides Allah (الله),‘
قَالُوا ضَلُّوا عَنَّا وَشَهِدُوا عَلَى أَنْفُسِهِمْ أَنَّهُمْ كَانُوا كَافِرِينَ
they will reply, ‘They have vanished and deserted us.‘ And they will bear witness against themselves, that they were disbelievers.
7:38
قَالَ ادْخُلُوا فِي أُمَمٍ قَدْ خَلَتْ مِنْ قَبْلِكُمْ مِنَ الْجِنِّ وَالْإِنْسِ فِي النَّارِ ۖ
(Allah (الله)) will say: ‘Enter you in the company of nations who passed away before you, of men and jinns, into the Fire.‘
كُلَّمَا دَخَلَتْ أُمَّةٌ لَعَنَتْ أُخْتَهَا ۖ
Every time a new nation enters, it curses its sister nation (that went before),
حَتَّى إِذَا ادَّارَكُوا فِيهَا جَمِيعًا قَالَتْ أُخْرَاهُمْ لِأُولَاهُمْ
until they will be gathered all together in the Fire. The last of them will say to the first of them:
رَبَّنَا هَؤُلَاءِ أَضَلُّونَا فَآتِهِمْ عَذَابًا ضِعْفًا مِنَ النَّارِ ۖ
Our Lord! These misled us, so give them a double torment of the Fire.‘
قَالَ لِكُلٍّ ضِعْفٌ وَلَكِنْ لَا تَعْلَمُونَ
He will say: ‘For each one there is double (torment), but you know not.‘
7:39
وَقَالَتْ أُولَاهُمْ لِأُخْرَاهُمْ فَمَا كَانَ لَكُمْ عَلَيْنَا مِنْ فَضْلٍ فَذُوقُوا الْعَذَابَ بِمَا كُنْتُمْ تَكْسِبُونَ
The first of them will say to the last of them: ‘You were not better than us, so taste the torment for what you used to earn.‘
7:[40-41] The gates of heaven shall not be opened for the disbelievers
7:[42-43] Only believers shall enter paradise
7:[44-47] Dialogue between the residents of paradise and the inmates of hell
7:40
إِنَّ الَّذِينَ كَذَّبُوا بِآيَاتِنَا وَاسْتَكْبَرُوا عَنْهَا لَا تُفَتَّحُ لَهُمْ أَبْوَابُ السَّمَاءِ
Verily, those who belie Our Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, etc.) and treat them with arrogance, for them the gates of heaven will not be opened,
وَلَا يَدْخُلُونَ الْجَنَّةَ حَتَّى يَلِجَ الْجَمَلُ فِي سَمِّ الْخِيَاطِ ۚ
and they will not enter Paradise (الجنة) until the camel goes through the eye of the needle (which is impossible).
وَكَذَلِكَ نَجْزِي الْمُجْرِمِينَ
Thus do We recompense the Mujrimoon (criminals, polytheists, sinners, etc.).
7:41
لَهُمْ مِنْ جَهَنَّمَ مِهَادٌ وَمِنْ فَوْقِهِمْ غَوَاشٍ ۚ
Theirs will be a bed of Hell (جهنم) (Fire), and over them coverings (of Hell (جهنم)-fire).
وَكَذَلِكَ نَجْزِي الظَّالِمِينَ
Thus do We recompense the Zalimoon (polytheists and wrongdoers, etc.).
7:42
وَالَّذِينَ آمَنُوا وَعَمِلُوا الصَّالِحَاتِ لَا نُكَلِّفُ نَفْسًا إِلَّا وُسْعَهَاأُولَئِكَ أَصْحَابُ الْجَنَّةِ ۖ
But those who believed (in the Oneness of Allah (الله) - Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism), and worked righteousness - We tax not any person beyond his scope, such are the dwellers of Paradise (الجنة).
هُمْ فِيهَا خَالِدُونَ
They will abide therein.
7:43
وَنَزَعْنَا مَا فِي صُدُورِهِمْ مِنْ غِلٍّ
And We shall remove from their breasts any (mutual) hatred or sense of injury (which they had, if at all, in the life of this world);
تَجْرِي مِنْ تَحْتِهِمُ الْأَنْهَارُ ۖ
rivers flowing under them,
وَقَالُوا الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذِي هَدَانَا لِهَذَا
and they will say:
All the praises and thanks be to Allah (الله), Who has guided us to this,
وَمَا كُنَّا لِنَهْتَدِيَ لَوْلَا أَنْ هَدَانَا اللَّهُ ۖ لَقَدْ جَاءَتْ رُسُلُ رَبِّنَا بِالْحَقِّ ۖ
never could we have found guidance, were it not that Allah (الله) had guided us! Indeed, the Messengers of our Lord did come with the truth.‘
وَنُودُوا أَنْ تِلْكُمُ الْجَنَّةُ أُورِثْتُمُوهَا بِمَا كُنْتُمْ تَعْمَلُونَ
And it will be cried out to them: ‘This is the Paradise (الجنة) which you have inherited for what you used to do.‘
7:44
وَنَادَى أَصْحَابُ الْجَنَّةِ أَصْحَابَ النَّارِ أَنْ قَدْ وَجَدْنَا مَا وَعَدَنَا رَبُّنَا حَقًّا
And the dwellers of Paradise (الجنة) will call out to the dwellers of the Fire (saying):
We have indeed found true what our Lord had promised us;
فَهَلْ وَجَدْتُمْ مَا وَعَدَ رَبُّكُمْ حَقًّا ۖ
have you also found true, what your Lord promised (warnings, etc.)?‘
قَالُوا نَعَمْ ۚ
They shall say: ‘Yes.‘
فَأَذَّنَ مُؤَذِّنٌ بَيْنَهُمْ أَنْ لَعْنَةُ اللَّهِ عَلَى الظَّالِمِينَ
Then a crier will proclaim between them: ‘The Curse of Allah (الله) is on the Zalimoon (polytheists and wrongdoers, etc.),‘
7:45
الَّذِينَ يَصُدُّونَ عَنْ سَبِيلِ اللَّهِ وَيَبْغُونَهَا عِوَجًا وَهُمْ بِالْآخِرَةِ كَافِرُونَ
Those who hindered (men) from the Path of Allah (الله), and would seek to make it crooked, and they were disbelievers in the Hereafter.
7:46
وَبَيْنَهُمَا حِجَابٌ ۚ
And between them will be a barrier screen
وَعَلَى الْأَعْرَافِ رِجَالٌ يَعْرِفُونَ كُلًّا بِسِيمَاهُمْ ۚ
and on AlAraf (a wall with elevated places) will be men (whose good and evil deeds would be equal in scale), who would recognise all (of the Paradise (الجنة) and Hell (جهنم) people), by their marks (the dwellers of Paradise (الجنة) by their white faces and the dwellers of Hell (جهنم) by their black faces),
وَنَادَوْا أَصْحَابَ الْجَنَّةِ أَنْ سَلَامٌ عَلَيْكُمْ ۚ
they will call out to the dwellers of Paradise (الجنة), ‘Salamun Alaikoom‘ (peace be on you),
لَمْ يَدْخُلُوهَا وَهُمْ يَطْمَعُونَ
and at that time they (men on Al-Araf) will not yet have entered it (Paradise (الجنة)), but they will hope to enter (it) with certainty.
7:47
وَإِذَا صُرِفَتْ أَبْصَارُهُمْ تِلْقَاءَ أَصْحَابِ النَّارِ قَالُوا رَبَّنَا لَا تَجْعَلْنَا مَعَ الْقَوْمِ الظَّالِمِينَ
And when their eyes will be turned towards the dwellers of the Fire, they will say: ‘Our Lord! Place us not with the people who are Zalimoon (polytheists and wrongdoers).‘
7:[48-53] Dialogue between the people of A‘rãf and the inmates of hell. Inmates of hell shall beg for water and food from the residents of paradise
7:48
وَنَادَى أَصْحَابُ الْأَعْرَافِ رِجَالًا يَعْرِفُونَهُمْ بِسِيمَاهُمْ قَالُوا
And the men on AlAraf (the wall) will call unto the men whom they would recognise by their marks, saying:
مَا أَغْنَى عَنْكُمْ جَمْعُكُمْ وَمَا كُنْتُمْ تَسْتَكْبِرُونَ
Of what benefit to you were your great numbers (and hoards of wealth), and your arrogance against Faith?‘
7:49
أَهَؤُلَاءِ الَّذِينَ أَقْسَمْتُمْ لَا يَنَالُهُمُ اللَّهُ بِرَحْمَةٍ ۚ
Are they those, of whom you swore that Allah (الله) would never show them mercy. (Behold! It has been said to them):
ادْخُلُوا الْجَنَّةَ لَا خَوْفٌ عَلَيْكُمْ وَلَا أَنْتُمْ تَحْزَنُونَ
Enter Paradise (الجنة), no fear shall be on you, nor shall you grieve.‘
7:50
وَنَادَى أَصْحَابُ النَّارِ أَصْحَابَ الْجَنَّةِ أَنْ أَفِيضُوا عَلَيْنَا مِنَ الْمَاءِ أَوْ مِمَّا رَزَقَكُمُ اللَّهُ ۚ
And the dwellers of the Fire will call to the dwellers of Paradise (الجنة): ‘Pour on us some water or anything that Allah (الله) has provided you with.‘
قَالُوا إِنَّ اللَّهَ حَرَّمَهُمَا عَلَى الْكَافِرِينَ
They will say: ‘Both (water and provision) Allah (الله) has forbidden to the disbelievers.‘
7:51
الَّذِينَ اتَّخَذُوا دِينَهُمْ لَهْوًا وَلَعِبًا وَغَرَّتْهُمُ الْحَيَاةُ الدُّنْيَا ۚ
Who took their religion as an amusement and play, and the life of the world deceived them.‘
فَالْيَوْمَ نَنْسَاهُمْ كَمَا نَسُوا لِقَاءَ يَوْمِهِمْ هَذَا وَمَا كَانُوا بِآيَاتِنَا يَجْحَدُونَ
So this Day We shall forget them as they forgot their meeting of this Day, and as they used to reject Our Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, etc.).
7:52
‏وَلَقَدْ جِئْنَاهُمْ بِكِتَابٍ فَصَّلْنَاهُ عَلَى عِلْمٍ هُدًى وَرَحْمَةً لِقَوْمٍ يُؤْمِنُونَ
Certainly, We have brought to them a Book (the Quran (القرآن)) which We have explained in detail with knowledge, - a guidance and a mercy to a people who believe.
7:53
هَلْ يَنْظُرُونَ إِلَّا تَأْوِيلَهُ ۚ
Await they just for the final fulfillment of the event?
يَوْمَ يَأْتِي تَأْوِيلُهُ يَقُولُ الَّذِينَ نَسُوهُ مِنْ قَبْلُ قَدْ جَاءَتْ رُسُلُ رَبِّنَا بِالْحَقِّ
On the Day the event is finally fulfilled (i.e. the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة)), those who neglected it before will say:
Verily, the Messengers of our Lord did come with the truth,
فَهَلْ لَنَا مِنْ شُفَعَاءَ فَيَشْفَعُوا لَنَا
now are there any intercessors for us that they might intercede on our behalf?
أَوْ نُرَدُّ فَنَعْمَلَ غَيْرَ الَّذِي كُنَّا نَعْمَلُ ۚ
Or could we be sent back (to the first life of the world) so that we might do (good) deeds other than those (evil) deeds which we used to do?‘
قَدْ خَسِرُوا أَنْفُسَهُمْ وَضَلَّ عَنْهُمْ مَا كَانُوا يَفْتَرُونَ
Verily, they have lost their ownselves (i.e. destroyed themselves) and that which they used to fabricate (invoking and worshipping others besides Allah (الله)) has gone away from them.
7:[54-58] Allah is the One Who created this universe. Pray to Allah with fear and hope
7:54
إِنَّ رَبَّكُمُ اللَّهُ الَّذِي خَلَقَ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضَ فِي سِتَّةِ أَيَّامٍ ثُمَّ اسْتَوَى عَلَى الْعَرْشِ
Indeed your Lord is Allah (الله), Who created the heavens and the earth in Six Days, and then He Istawa (rose over) the Throne (really in a manner that suits His Majesty).
يُغْشِي اللَّيْلَ النَّهَارَ يَطْلُبُهُ حَثِيثًا
He brings the night as a cover over the day, seeking it rapidly,
وَالشَّمْسَ وَالْقَمَرَ وَالنُّجُومَ مُسَخَّرَاتٍ بِأَمْرِهِ ۗ
and (He created) the sun, the moon, the stars subjected to His Command.
أَلَا لَهُ الْخَلْقُ وَالْأَمْرُ ۗ تَبَارَكَ اللَّهُ رَبُّ الْعَالَمِينَ
Surely, His is the Creation and Commandment. Blessed be Allah (الله), the Lord of the Alameen (mankind, jinns and all that exists)!
7:55
ادْعُوا رَبَّكُمْ تَضَرُّعًا وَخُفْيَةً ۚ
Invoke your Lord with humility and in secret.
إِنَّهُ لَا يُحِبُّ الْمُعْتَدِينَ
He likes not the aggressors.
7:56
وَلَا تُفْسِدُوا فِي الْأَرْضِ بَعْدَ إِصْلَاحِهَا وَادْعُوهُ خَوْفًا وَطَمَعًا ۚ
And do not do mischief on the earth, after it has been set in order, and invoke Him with fear and hope;
إِنَّ رَحْمَتَ اللَّهِ قَرِيبٌ مِنَ الْمُحْسِنِينَ
Surely, Allah (الله)s Mercy is (ever) near unto the gooddoers.
7:57
وَهُوَ الَّذِي يُرْسِلُ الرِّيَاحَ بُشْرًا بَيْنَ يَدَيْ رَحْمَتِهِ ۖ
And it is He Who sends the winds as heralds of glad tidings, going before His Mercy (rain).
حَتَّى إِذَا أَقَلَّتْ سَحَابًا ثِقَالًا سُقْنَاهُ لِبَلَدٍ مَيِّتٍ فَأَنْزَلْنَا بِهِ الْمَاءَ فَأَخْرَجْنَا بِهِ مِنْ كُلِّ الثَّمَرَاتِ ۚ
Till when they have carried a heavy-laden cloud, We drive it to a land that is dead, then We cause water (rain) to descend thereon. Then We produce every kind of fruit therewith.
كَذَلِكَ نُخْرِجُ الْمَوْتَى لَعَلَّكُمْ تَذَكَّرُونَ
Similarly, We shall raise up the dead, so that you may remember or take heed.
7:58
وَالْبَلَدُ الطَّيِّبُ يَخْرُجُ نَبَاتُهُ بِإِذْنِ رَبِّهِ ۖ وَالَّذِي خَبُثَ لَا يَخْرُجُ إِلَّا نَكِدًا ۚ
The vegetation of a good land comes forth (easily) by the Permission of its Lord, and that which is bad, brings forth nothing but a little with difficulty.
كَذَلِكَ نُصَرِّفُ الْآيَاتِ لِقَوْمٍ يَشْكُرُونَ
Thus do We explain variously the Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, etc.) for a people who give thanks.
7:[59-64] Prophet Nüh‘s address to his people, their disbelief and their fate
7:59
لَقَدْ أَرْسَلْنَا نُوحًا إِلَى قَوْمِهِ فَقَالَ يَا قَوْمِ اعْبُدُوا اللَّهَ مَا لَكُمْ مِنْ إِلَهٍ غَيْرُهُ
Indeed, We sent Nooh (Noah) to his people and he said:
O my people! Worship Allah (الله)! You have no other Ilah (God) but Him. (La ilaha ill-Allah (الله): none has the right to be worshipped but Allah (الله)).
إِنِّي أَخَافُ عَلَيْكُمْ عَذَابَ يَوْمٍ عَظِيمٍ
Certainly, I fear for you the torment of a Great Day!‘
7:60
قَالَ الْمَلَأُ مِنْ قَوْمِهِ إِنَّا لَنَرَاكَ فِي ضَلَالٍ مُبِينٍ
The leaders of his people said: ‘Verily, we see you in plain error.‘
7:61
قَالَ يَا قَوْمِ لَيْسَ بِي ضَلَالَةٌ وَلَكِنِّي رَسُولٌ مِنْ رَبِّ الْعَالَمِينَ
(Nooh (Noah)) said: ‘O my people! There is no error in me, but I am a Messenger from the Lord of the Alameen (mankind, jinns and all that exists)!
7:62
أُبَلِّغُكُمْ رِسَالَاتِ رَبِّي وَأَنْصَحُ لَكُمْ وَأَعْلَمُ مِنَ اللَّهِ مَا لَا تَعْلَمُونَ
I convey unto you the Messages of my Lord and give sincere advice to you. And I know from Allah (الله) what you know not.
7:63
أَوَعَجِبْتُمْ أَنْ جَاءَكُمْ ذِكْرٌ مِنْ رَبِّكُمْ عَلَى رَجُلٍ مِنْكُمْ لِيُنْذِرَكُمْ وَلِتَتَّقُوا وَلَعَلَّكُمْ تُرْحَمُونَ
Do you wonder that there has come to you a Reminder from your Lord through a man from amongst you, that he may warn you, so that you may fear Allah (الله) and that you may receive (His) Mercy?‘
7:64
فَكَذَّبُوهُ فَأَنْجَيْنَاهُ وَالَّذِينَ مَعَهُ فِي الْفُلْكِ وَأَغْرَقْنَا الَّذِينَ كَذَّبُوا بِآيَاتِنَا ۚ
But they belied him, so We saved him and those along with him in the ship, and We drowned those who belied Our Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, etc.).
إِنَّهُمْ كَانُوا قَوْمًا عَمِينَ
They were indeed a blind people.
7:[65-72] Prophet Hüd‘s address to his people, their disbelief and their fate
7:65
وَإِلَى عَادٍ أَخَاهُمْ هُودًا ۗ قَالَ يَا قَوْمِ اعْبُدُوا اللَّهَ مَا لَكُمْ مِنْ إِلَهٍ غَيْرُهُ ۚ
And to Ad (people, We sent) their brother Hood. He said:
O my people! Worship Allah (الله)! You have no other Ilah (God) but Him. (La ilaha ill-Allah (الله): none has the right to be worshipped but Allah (الله)).
أَفَلَا تَتَّقُونَ
Will you not fear (Allah (الله))?‘
7:66
قَالَ الْمَلَأُ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا مِنْ قَوْمِهِ إِنَّا لَنَرَاكَ فِي سَفَاهَةٍ وَإِنَّا لَنَظُنُّكَ مِنَ الْكَاذِبِينَ
The leaders of those who disbelieved among his people said:
Verily, we see you in foolishness, and verily, we think you are one of the liars.‘
7:67

And if (again) the offer of the like (evil pleasures of this world) came their way, they would (again) seize them (would commit those sins).
أَلَمْ يُؤْخَذْ عَلَيْهِمْ مِيثَاقُ الْكِتَابِ أَنْ لَا يَقُولُوا عَلَى اللَّهِ إِلَّا الْحَقَّ

وَالدَّارُ الْآخِرَةُ خَيْرٌ لِلَّذِينَ يَتَّقُونَ ۗ أَفَلَا تَعْقِلُونَ

7:170

And as to those who hold fast to the Book (i.e. act on its teachings) and perform As-Salat (الصَّلَاةَ) (Iqamat-as-Salat), certainly, We shall never waste the reward of those who do righteous deeds.

And (remember) when We raised the mountain over them as if it had been a canopy, and they thought that it was going to fall on them.

(We said): ‘Hold firmly to what We have given you (i.e. the Taurat (Torah (التوراة))), and remember that which is therein (act on its commandments), so that you may fear Allah (الله) and obey Him.‘

And if you call them to guidance, they hear not and you will see them looking at you, yet they see not.
7:199
خُذِ الْعَفْوَ وَأْمُرْ بِالْعُرْفِ وَأَعْرِضْ عَنِ الْجَاهِلِينَ
Show forgiveness, enjoin what is good, and turn away from the foolish (i.e. dont punish them).
7:200
وَإِمَّا يَنْزَغَنَّكَ مِنَ الشَّيْطَانِ نَزْغٌ فَاسْتَعِذْ بِاللَّهِ ۚ
And if an evil whisper comes to you from Shaitan (Satan (الشيطان)) then seek refuge with Allah (الله).
إِنَّهُ سَمِيعٌ عَلِيمٌ
Verily, He is All-Hearer, All-Knower (Al-‘Alee (ٱلْعَلِيُّ)m (ٱلْعَلِيمُ)).
7:201
‏إِنَّ الَّذِينَ اتَّقَوْا إِذَا مَسَّهُمْ طَائِفٌ مِنَ الشَّيْطَانِ تَذَكَّرُوا فَإِذَا هُمْ مُبْصِرُونَ
Verily, those who are Al-Muttaqoon (ْالمُتَّقِينَ) (the pious - see V.2:2), when an evil thought comes to them from Shaitan (Satan (الشيطان)), they remember (Allah (الله)), and (indeed) they then see (aright).
7:202
‏وَإِخْوَانُهُمْ يَمُدُّونَهُمْ فِي الْغَيِّ ثُمَّ لَا يُقْصِرُونَ
But (as for) their brothers (the devils) they (i.e. the devils) plunge them deeper into error, and they never stop short.
7:203
وَإِذَا لَمْ تَأْتِهِمْ بِآيَةٍ قَالُوا لَوْلَا اجْتَبَيْتَهَا ۚ
And if you do not bring them a miracle (according to their (i.e. Quraish-pagans) proposal), they say: ‘Why have you not brought it?‘
قُلْ إِنَّمَا أَتَّبِعُ مَا يُوحَى إِلَيَّ مِنْ رَبِّي ۚ
Say: ‘I but follow what is revealed to me from my Lord.
هَذَا بَصَائِرُ مِنْ رَبِّكُمْ وَهُدًى وَرَحْمَةٌ لِقَوْمٍ يُؤْمِنُونَ
This (the Quran (القرآن)) is nothing but evidences from your Lord, and a guidance and a mercy for a people who believe.‘
7:204
وَإِذَا قُرِئَ الْقُرْآنُ فَاسْتَمِعُوا لَهُ وَأَنْصِتُوا لَعَلَّكُمْ تُرْحَمُونَ
So, when the Quran (القرآن) is recited, listen to it, and be silent that you may receive mercy.(i.e. during the compulsory congregational prayers when the Imam (of a mosque) is leading the prayer (except Soorat Al-Fatiha), and also when he is delivering the Friday (الجمعة)-prayer Khutbah).(Tafsir At-Tabari, Vol.9, Pages 162-4)
7:205
وَاذْكُرْ رَبَّكَ فِي نَفْسِكَ تَضَرُّعًا وَخِيفَةً وَدُونَ الْجَهْرِ مِنَ الْقَوْلِ بِالْغُدُوِّ وَالْآصَالِ وَلَا تَكُنْ مِنَ الْغَافِلِينَ
And remember your Lord by your tongue and within yourself, humbly and with fear without loudness in words in the mornings, and in the afternoons and be not of those who are neglectful.
7:206
إِنَّ الَّذِينَ عِنْدَ رَبِّكَ لَا يَسْتَكْبِرُونَ عَنْ عِبَادَتِهِ وَيُسَبِّحُونَهُ وَلَهُ يَسْجُدُونَ ۩
Surely, those who are with your Lord (angels) are never too proud to perform acts of worship to Him, but they glorify His Praise and prostrate before Him.*********
