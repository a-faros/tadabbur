Al-Qasas
The Narrations, The Stories, The Story
28:[1-3] Story of the Prophet Musa
28:[4-13] Fir‘on plotted to kill the sons of the Israelites to save his kingship, while Allah planned to bring up one of them in Fir‘on‘s own household
28:1
طسٓمٓ
Ta Seen Meem(These letters are one of the miracles of the Quran, and none but Allah (Alone) knows their meanings)
28:2
تِلۡكَ ءَايَـٰتُ ٱلۡكِتَـٰبِ ٱلۡمُبِينِ
These are Verses of the manifest Book (that makes clear truth from falsehood, good from evil, etc.).
28:3
نَتۡلُواْ عَلَيۡكَ مِن نَّبَإِ مُوسَىٰ وَفِرۡعَوۡنَ بِٱلۡحَقِّ لِقَوۡمٍ۬ يُؤۡمِنُونَ
We recite to you some of the news of Musa (Moosa) and Firaun (Pharaoh) in truth, for a people who believe (those who believe in this Quran, and in the Oneness of Allah).
28:4
إِنَّ فِرۡعَوۡنَ عَلَا فِى ٱلۡأَرۡضِ وَجَعَلَ أَهۡلَهَا شِيَعً۬ا
Verily, Firaun (Pharaoh) exalted himself in the land and made its people sects,
يَسۡتَضۡعِفُ طَآٮِٕفَةً۬ مِّنۡہُمۡ يُذَبِّحُ أَبۡنَآءَهُمۡ وَيَسۡتَحۡىِۦ نِسَآءَهُمۡ‌ۚ
weakening (oppressing) a group (i.e. Children of Israel) among them, killing their sons, and letting their females live.
إِنَّهُ ۥ كَانَ مِنَ ٱلۡمُفۡسِدِينَ
Verily, he was of the Mufsidoon (i.e. those who commit great sins and crimes, oppressors, tyrants, etc.).
28:5
وَنُرِيدُ أَن نَّمُنَّ عَلَى ٱلَّذِينَ ٱسۡتُضۡعِفُواْ فِى ٱلۡأَرۡضِ
And We wished to do a favour to those who were weak (and oppressed) in the land,
وَنَجۡعَلَهُمۡ أَٮِٕمَّةً۬ وَنَجۡعَلَهُمُ ٱلۡوَٲرِثِينَ
and to make them rulers and to make them the inheritors,
28:6
وَنُمَكِّنَ لَهُمۡ فِى ٱلۡأَرۡضِ
And to establish them in the land,
وَنُرِىَ فِرۡعَوۡنَ وَهَـٰمَـٰنَ وَجُنُودَهُمَا مِنۡهُم مَّا ڪَانُواْ يَحۡذَرُونَ
and We let Firaun (Pharaoh) and Haman and their hosts receive from them that which they feared.
28:7
وَأَوۡحَيۡنَآ إِلَىٰٓ أُمِّ مُوسَىٰٓ أَنۡ أَرۡضِعِيهِ‌ۖ
And We inspired the mother of Musa (Moosa), (saying): ‘Suckle him (Musa (Moosa)),
فَإِذَا خِفۡتِ عَلَيۡهِ فَأَلۡقِيهِ فِى ٱلۡيَمِّ وَلَا تَخَافِى وَلَا تَحۡزَنِىٓ‌ۖ
but when you fear for him, then cast him into the river and fear not, nor grieve.
إِنَّا رَآدُّوهُ إِلَيۡكِ وَجَاعِلُوهُ مِنَ ٱلۡمُرۡسَلِينَ
Verily! We shall bring him back to you, and shall make him one of (Our) Messengers.‘
28:8
فَٱلۡتَقَطَهُ ۥۤ ءَالُ فِرۡعَوۡنَ لِيَڪُونَ لَهُمۡ عَدُوًّ۬ا وَحَزَنًا‌ۗ
Then the household of Firaun (Pharaoh) picked him up, that he might become for them an enemy and a (cause of) grief.
إِنَّ فِرۡعَوۡنَ وَهَـٰمَـٰنَ وَجُنُودَهُمَا ڪَانُواْ خَـٰطِـِٔينَ
Verily! Firaun (Pharaoh), Haman and their hosts were sinners.
28:9
وَقَالَتِ ٱمۡرَأَتُ فِرۡعَوۡنَ قُرَّتُ عَيۡنٍ۬ لِّى وَلَكَ‌ۖ
And the wife of Firaun (Pharaoh) said: ‘A comfort of the eye for me and for you.
لَا تَقۡتُلُوهُ عَسَىٰٓ أَن يَنفَعَنَآ أَوۡ نَتَّخِذَهُ ۥ وَلَدً۬ا
Kill him not, perhaps he may be of benefit to us, or we may adopt him as a son.‘
وَهُمۡ لَا يَشۡعُرُونَ
And they perceive not (the result of that).
28:10
وَأَصۡبَحَ فُؤَادُ أُمِّ مُوسَىٰ فَـٰرِغًا‌ۖ
And the heart of the mother of Musa (Moosa) became empty (from every thought, except the thought of Musa (Moosa)).
إِن ڪَادَتۡ لَتُبۡدِى بِهِۦ لَوۡلَآ أَن رَّبَطۡنَا عَلَىٰ قَلۡبِهَا لِتَكُونَ مِنَ ٱلۡمُؤۡمِنِينَ
She was very near to disclose his (case, i.e. the child is her son), had We not strengthened her heart (with Faith), so that she might remain as one of the believers.
28:11
وَقَالَتۡ لِأُخۡتِهِۦ قُصِّيهِ‌ۖ
And she said to his (Musas (Moses)) sister: ‘Follow him.‘
فَبَصُرَتۡ بِهِۦ عَن جُنُبٍ۬ وَهُمۡ لَا يَشۡعُرُونَ
So she (his sister) watched him from a far place secretly, while they perceived not.
28:12
وَحَرَّمۡنَا عَلَيۡهِ ٱلۡمَرَاضِعَ مِن قَبۡلُ
And We had already forbidden (other) foster suckling mothers for him,
فَقَالَتۡ هَلۡ أَدُلُّكُمۡ عَلَىٰٓ أَهۡلِ بَيۡتٍ۬ يَكۡفُلُونَهُ ۥ لَڪُمۡ وَهُمۡ لَهُ ۥ نَـٰصِحُونَ
until she (his sister came up and) said: ‘Shall I direct you to a household who will rear him for you, and sincerely they will look after him in a good manner?‘
28:13
فَرَدَدۡنَـٰهُ إِلَىٰٓ أُمِّهِۦ كَىۡ تَقَرَّ عَيۡنُهَا وَلَا تَحۡزَنَ
So did We restore him to his mother, that she might be delighted, and that she might not grieve,
وَلِتَعۡلَمَ أَنَّ وَعۡدَ ٱللَّهِ حَقٌّ۬ وَلَـٰكِنَّ أَڪۡثَرَهُمۡ لَا يَعۡلَمُونَ
and that she might know that the Promise of Allah is true. But most of them know not.
28:[14-21] The Prophet Musa‘s youth, his folly of killing a man, and his escape from Fir‘on‘s retribution
28:14
وَلَمَّا بَلَغَ أَشُدَّهُ ۥ وَٱسۡتَوَىٰٓ ءَاتَيۡنَـٰهُ حُكۡمً۬ا وَعِلۡمً۬ا‌ۚ
And when he attained his full strength, and was perfect (in manhood), We bestowed on him Hukman (Prophethood, right judgement of the affairs) and religious knowledge (of the religion of his forefathers i.e. Islamic Monotheism).
وَكَذَٲلِكَ نَجۡزِى ٱلۡمُحۡسِنِينَ
And thus do We reward the Muhsinoon (i.e. good doers - see the footnote of V.9:120).
28:15
وَدَخَلَ ٱلۡمَدِينَةَ عَلَىٰ حِينِ غَفۡلَةٍ۬ مِّنۡ أَهۡلِهَا فَوَجَدَ فِيہَا رَجُلَيۡنِ يَقۡتَتِلَانِ
And he entered the city at a time of unawareness of its people, and he found there two men fighting, -
هَـٰذَا مِن شِيعَتِهِۦ وَهَـٰذَا مِنۡ عَدُوِّهِۦ‌ۖ
one of his party (his religion - from the Children of Israel), and the other of his foes.
فَٱسۡتَغَـٰثَهُ ٱلَّذِى مِن شِيعَتِهِۦ عَلَى ٱلَّذِى مِنۡ عَدُوِّهِۦ
The man of his (own) party asked him for help against his foe,
فَوَكَزَهُ ۥ مُوسَىٰ فَقَضَىٰ عَلَيۡهِ‌ۖ
so Musa (Moosa) struck him with his fist and killed him.
قَالَ هَـٰذَا مِنۡ عَمَلِ ٱلشَّيۡطَـٰنِ‌ۖ إِنَّهُ ۥ عَدُوٌّ۬ مُّضِلٌّ۬ مُّبِينٌ۬
He said: ‘This is of Shaitans (Satan) doing, verily, he is a plain misleading enemy.‘
28:16
قَالَ رَبِّ إِنِّى ظَلَمۡتُ نَفۡسِى فَٱغۡفِرۡ لِى
He said: ‘My Lord! Verily, I have wronged myself, so forgive me.‘
فَغَفَرَ لَهُ ۥۤ‌ۚ
Then He forgave him.
إِنَّهُ ۥ هُوَ ٱلۡغَفُورُ ٱلرَّحِيمُ
Verily, He is the Oft-Forgiving, the Most Merciful (Ar-Raheem).
28:17
قَالَ رَبِّ بِمَآ أَنۡعَمۡتَ عَلَىَّ فَلَنۡ أَكُونَ ظَهِيرً۬ا لِّلۡمُجۡرِمِينَ
He said: ‘My Lord! For that with which You have favoured me, I will never more be a helper for the Mujrimoon (criminals, disobedient to Allah, polytheists, sinners, etc.)!
28:18
فَأَصۡبَحَ فِى ٱلۡمَدِينَةِ خَآٮِٕفً۬ا يَتَرَقَّبُ فَإِذَا ٱلَّذِى ٱسۡتَنصَرَهُ ۥ بِٱلۡأَمۡسِ يَسۡتَصۡرِخُهُ ۥ‌ۚ
So he became afraid, looking about in the city (waiting as to what will be the result of his crime of killing), when behold, the man who had sought his help the day before, called for his help (again).
قَالَ لَهُ ۥ مُوسَىٰٓ إِنَّكَ لَغَوِىٌّ۬ مُّبِينٌ۬
Musa (Moosa) said to him: ‘Verily, you are a plain misleader!‘
28:19
فَلَمَّآ أَنۡ أَرَادَ أَن يَبۡطِشَ بِٱلَّذِى هُوَ عَدُوٌّ۬ لَّهُمَا قَالَ
Then when he decided to seize the man who was an enemy to both of them, the man said:
يَـٰمُوسَىٰٓ أَتُرِيدُ أَن تَقۡتُلَنِى كَمَا قَتَلۡتَ نَفۡسَۢا بِٱلۡأَمۡسِ‌ۖ
O Musa (Moosa)! Is it your intention to kill me as you killed a man yesterday?
إِن تُرِيدُ إِلَّآ أَن تَكُونَ جَبَّارً۬ا فِى ٱلۡأَرۡضِ وَمَا تُرِيدُ أَن تَكُونَ مِنَ ٱلۡمُصۡلِحِينَ
Your aim is nothing but to become a tyrant in the land, and not to be one of those who do right.‘
28:20
وَجَآءَ رَجُلٌ۬ مِّنۡ أَقۡصَا ٱلۡمَدِينَةِ يَسۡعَىٰ
And there came a man running, from the farthest end of the city.
قَالَ يَـٰمُوسَىٰٓ إِنَّ ٱلۡمَلَأَ يَأۡتَمِرُونَ بِكَ لِيَقۡتُلُوكَ فَٱخۡرُجۡ
He said: ‘O Musa (Moosa)! Verily, the chiefs are taking counsel together about you, to kill you, so escape.
إِنِّى لَكَ مِنَ ٱلنَّـٰصِحِينَ
Truly, I am to you of those who give sincere advice.‘
28:21
فَخَرَجَ مِنۡہَا خَآٮِٕفً۬ا يَتَرَقَّبُ‌ۖ
So he escaped from there, looking about in a state of fear.
قَالَ رَبِّ نَجِّنِى مِنَ ٱلۡقَوۡمِ ٱلظَّـٰلِمِينَ
He said: ‘My Lord! Save me from the people who are Zalimoon (polytheists and wrong-doers)!‘
28:[22-28] His arrival at Madyan, acceptance of a ten year term of employment, and marriage
28:22
وَلَمَّا تَوَجَّهَ تِلۡقَآءَ مَدۡيَنَ قَالَ
And when he went towards (the land of) Madyan (Midian) he said:
عَسَىٰ رَبِّىٓ أَن يَهۡدِيَنِى سَوَآءَ ٱلسَّبِيلِ
It may be that my Lord guides me to the Right Way.‘
28:23
وَلَمَّا وَرَدَ مَآءَ مَدۡيَنَ وَجَدَ عَلَيۡهِ أُمَّةً۬ مِّنَ ٱلنَّاسِ يَسۡقُونَ
And when he arrived at the water of Madyan (Midian) he found there a group of men watering (their flocks),
وَوَجَدَ مِن دُونِهِمُ ٱمۡرَأَتَيۡنِ تَذُودَانِ‌ۖ
and besides them he found two women who were keeping back (their flocks).
قَالَ مَا خَطۡبُكُمَا‌ۖ
He said: ‘What is the matter with you?‘
قَالَتَا لَا نَسۡقِى حَتَّىٰ يُصۡدِرَ ٱلرِّعَآءُ‌ۖ وَأَبُونَا شَيۡخٌ۬ ڪَبِيرٌ۬
They said: ‘We cannot water (our flocks) until the shepherds take (their flocks). And our father is a very old man.‘
28:24
فَسَقَىٰ لَهُمَا ثُمَّ تَوَلَّىٰٓ إِلَى ٱلظِّلِّ فَقَالَ
So he watered (their flocks) for them, then he turned back to shade, and said:
رَبِّ إِنِّى لِمَآ أَنزَلۡتَ إِلَىَّ مِنۡ خَيۡرٍ۬ فَقِيرٌ۬
My Lord! Truly, I am in need of whatever good that You bestow on me!‘
28:25
فَجَآءَتۡهُ إِحۡدَٮٰهُمَا تَمۡشِى عَلَى ٱسۡتِحۡيَآءٍ۬
Then there came to him one of the two women, walking shyly.
قَالَتۡ إِنَّ أَبِى يَدۡعُوكَ لِيَجۡزِيَكَ أَجۡرَ مَا سَقَيۡتَ لَنَا‌ۚ
She said: ‘Verily, my father calls you that he may reward you for having watered (our flocks) for us.‘
فَلَمَّا جَآءَهُ ۥ وَقَصَّ عَلَيۡهِ ٱلۡقَصَصَ قَالَ لَا تَخَفۡ‌ۖ
So when he came to him and narrated the story, he said: ‘Fear you not.
نَجَوۡتَ مِنَ ٱلۡقَوۡمِ ٱلظَّـٰلِمِينَ
You have escaped from the people who are Zalimoon (polytheists, disbelievers, and wrong-doers).‘
28:26
قَالَتۡ إِحۡدَٮٰهُمَا يَـٰٓأَبَتِ ٱسۡتَـٔۡجِرۡهُ‌ۖ
And said one of them (the two women): ‘O my father! Hire him!
إِنَّ خَيۡرَ مَنِ ٱسۡتَـٔۡجَرۡتَ ٱلۡقَوِىُّ ٱلۡأَمِينُ
Verily, the best of men for you to hire is the strong, the trustworthy.‘
28:27
قَالَ إِنِّىٓ أُرِيدُ أَنۡ أُنكِحَكَ إِحۡدَى ٱبۡنَتَىَّ هَـٰتَيۡنِ عَلَىٰٓ أَن تَأۡجُرَنِى ثَمَـٰنِىَ حِجَجٍ۬‌ۖ
He said: ‘I intend to wed one of these two daughters of mine to you, on condition that you serve me for eight years,
فَإِنۡ أَتۡمَمۡتَ عَشۡرً۬ا فَمِنۡ عِندِكَ‌ۖ
but if you complete ten years, it will be (a favour) from you.
وَمَآ أُرِيدُ أَنۡ أَشُقَّ عَلَيۡكَ‌ۚ
But I intend not to place you under a difficulty.
سَتَجِدُنِىٓ إِن شَآءَ ٱللَّهُ مِنَ ٱلصَّـٰلِحِينَ
If Allah will, you will find me one of the righteous.‘
28:28
قَالَ ذَٲلِكَ بَيۡنِى وَبَيۡنَكَ‌ۖ
He (Musa (Moosa)) said: ‘That (is settled) between me and you
أَيَّمَا ٱلۡأَجَلَيۡنِ قَضَيۡتُ فَلَا عُدۡوَٲنَ عَلَىَّ‌ۖ
whichever of the two terms I fulfill, there will be no injustice to me,
وَٱللَّهُ عَلَىٰ مَا نَقُولُ وَڪِيلٌ۬
and Allah is Surety over what we say.‘
28:[29-35] His arrival at Mount Tür, seeing a fire, conversation with Allah, and his appointment as a Rasool to Fir‘on and his chiefs
28:[36-42] Fir‘on and his chiefs disbelieved; as a result, Allah destroyed them but saved the Children of Israel
28:29
فَلَمَّا قَضَىٰ مُوسَى ٱلۡأَجَلَ وَسَارَ بِأَهۡلِهِۦۤ ءَانَسَ مِن جَانِبِ ٱلطُّورِ نَارً۬ا
Then, when Musa (Moosa) had fulfilled the term, and was travelling with his family, he saw a fire in the direction of Toor (Mount).
قَالَ لِأَهۡلِهِ ٱمۡكُثُوٓاْ إِنِّىٓ ءَانَسۡتُ نَارً۬ا
He said to his family: ‘Wait, I have seen a fire;
لَّعَلِّىٓ ءَاتِيكُم مِّنۡهَا بِخَبَرٍ أَوۡ جَذۡوَةٍ۬ مِّنَ ٱلنَّارِ لَعَلَّكُمۡ تَصۡطَلُونَ
perhaps I may bring to you from there some information, or a burning fire-brand that you may warm yourselves.‘
28:30
فَلَمَّآ أَتَٮٰهَا نُودِىَ مِن شَـٰطِىِٕ ٱلۡوَادِ ٱلۡأَيۡمَنِ فِى ٱلۡبُقۡعَةِ ٱلۡمُبَـٰرَڪَةِ مِنَ ٱلشَّجَرَةِ
So when he reached it (the fire), he was called from the right side of the valley, in the blessed place from the tree:
أَن يَـٰمُوسَىٰٓ إِنِّىٓ أَنَا ٱللَّهُ رَبُّ ٱلۡعَـٰلَمِينَ
O Musa (Moosa)! Verily! I am Allah, the Lord of the Alameen (mankind, jinns and all that exists)!
28:31
وَأَنۡ أَلۡقِ عَصَاكَ‌ۖ
And throw your stick!‘
فَلَمَّا رَءَاهَا تَہۡتَزُّ كَأَنَّہَا جَآنٌّ۬ وَلَّىٰ مُدۡبِرً۬ا وَلَمۡ يُعَقِّبۡ‌ۚ
But when he saw it moving as if it were a snake, he turned in flight, and looked not back.
يَـٰمُوسَىٰٓ أَقۡبِلۡ وَلَا تَخَفۡ‌ۖ إِنَّكَ مِنَ ٱلۡأَمِنِينَ
(It was said): ‘O Musa (Moosa)! Draw near, and fear not. Verily, you are of those who are secure.
28:32
ٱسۡلُكۡ يَدَكَ فِى جَيۡبِكَ تَخۡرُجۡ بَيۡضَآءَ مِنۡ غَيۡرِ سُوٓءٍ۬
Put your hand in your bosom, it will come forth white without a disease,
وَٱضۡمُمۡ إِلَيۡكَ جَنَاحَكَ مِنَ ٱلرَّهۡبِ‌ۖ
and draw your hand close to your side to be free from fear (that which you suffered from the snake, and also by that your hand will return to its original state).
فَذَٲنِكَ بُرۡهَـٰنَانِ مِن رَّبِّكَ إِلَىٰ فِرۡعَوۡنَ وَمَلَإِيْهِۦۤ‌ۚ
these are two Burhan (signs, miracles, evidences, proofs) from your Lord to Firaun (Pharaoh) and his chiefs.
إِنَّهُمۡ ڪَانُواْ قَوۡمً۬ا فَـٰسِقِينَ

28:66
فَعَمِيَتۡ عَلَيۡہِمُ ٱلۡأَنۢبَآءُ يَوۡمَٮِٕذٍ۬ فَهُمۡ لَا يَتَسَآءَلُونَ
Then the news of a good answer will be obscured to them on that day, and they will not be able to ask one another.
28:67
فَأَمَّا مَن تَابَ وَءَامَنَ وَعَمِلَ صَـٰلِحً۬ا فَعَسَىٰٓ أَن يَكُونَ مِنَ ٱلۡمُفۡلِحِينَ
But as for him who repented (from polytheism and sins, etc.), believed (in the Oneness of Allah, and in His Messenger Muhammad SAW), and did righteous deeds (in the life of this world), then he will be among those who are successful.
28:68
وَرَبُّكَ يَخۡلُقُ مَا يَشَآءُ وَيَخۡتَارُ‌ۗ مَا ڪَانَ لَهُمُ ٱلۡخِيَرَةُ‌ۚ
And your Lord creates whatsoever He wills and chooses, no choice have they (in any matter).
سُبۡحَـٰنَ ٱللَّهِ وَتَعَـٰلَىٰ عَمَّا يُشۡرِڪُونَ
Glorified be Allah, and exalted above all that they associate as partners (with Him).
28:69
وَرَبُّكَ يَعۡلَمُ مَا تُكِنُّ صُدُورُهُمۡ وَمَا يُعۡلِنُونَ
And your Lord knows what their breasts conceal, and what they reveal.
28:70
وَهُوَ ٱللَّهُ لَآ إِلَـٰهَ إِلَّا هُوَ‌ۖ
And He is Allah; La ilaha illa Huwa (none has the right to be worshipped but He).
لَهُ ٱلۡحَمۡدُ فِى ٱلۡأُولَىٰ وَٱلۡأَخِرَةِ‌ۖ
His is all praise, in the first (i.e. in this world) and in the last (i.e. in the Hereafter).
وَلَهُ ٱلۡحُكۡمُ وَإِلَيۡهِ تُرۡجَعُونَ
And for Him is the Decision, and to Him shall you (all) be returned.
28:71
قُلۡ أَرَءَيۡتُمۡ إِن جَعَلَ ٱللَّهُ عَلَيۡڪُمُ ٱلَّيۡلَ سَرۡمَدًا إِلَىٰ يَوۡمِ ٱلۡقِيَـٰمَةِ
Say (O Muhammad SAW): ‘Tell me! If Allah made night continuous for you till the Day of Resurrection,
مَنۡ إِلَـٰهٌ غَيۡرُ ٱللَّهِ يَأۡتِيڪُم بِضِيَآءٍ‌ۖ
who is an ilah (a god) besides Allah who could bring you light?
أَفَلَا تَسۡمَعُونَ
Will you not then hear?‘
28:72
قُلۡ أَرَءَيۡتُمۡ إِن جَعَلَ ٱللَّهُ عَلَيۡڪُمُ ٱلنَّہَارَ سَرۡمَدًا إِلَىٰ يَوۡمِ ٱلۡقِيَـٰمَةِ
Say (O Muhammad SAW): ‘Tell me! If Allah made day continuous for you till the Day of Resurrection,
مَنۡ إِلَـٰهٌ غَيۡرُ ٱللَّهِ يَأۡتِيڪُم بِلَيۡلٍ۬ تَسۡكُنُونَ فِيهِ‌ۖ
who is an ilah (a god) besides Allah who could bring you night wherein you rest?
أَفَلَا تُبۡصِرُونَ
Will you not then see?‘
28:73
وَمِن رَّحۡمَتِهِۦ جَعَلَ لَكُمُ ٱلَّيۡلَ وَٱلنَّهَارَ لِتَسۡكُنُواْ فِيهِ وَلِتَبۡتَغُواْ مِن فَضۡلِهِۦ
It is out of His Mercy that He has put for you night and day, that you may rest therein (i.e. during the night) and that you may seek of His Bounty (i.e. during the day),
وَلَعَلَّكُمۡ تَشۡكُرُونَ
and in order that you may be grateful.
28:74
وَيَوۡمَ يُنَادِيهِمۡ فَيَقُولُ أَيۡنَ شُرَڪَآءِىَ ٱلَّذِينَ كُنتُمۡ تَزۡعُمُونَ
And (remember) the Day when He (your Lord Allah) will call them (those who worshipped others along with Allah), and will say: ‘Where are My (so-called) partners, whom you used to assert?‘
28:75
وَنَزَعۡنَا مِن ڪُلِّ أُمَّةٍ۬ شَهِيدً۬ا فَقُلۡنَا هَاتُواْ بُرۡهَـٰنَكُمۡ
And We shall take out from every nation a witness, and We shall say: ‘Bring your proof.‘
فَعَلِمُوٓاْ أَنَّ ٱلۡحَقَّ لِلَّهِ وَضَلَّ عَنۡہُم مَّا ڪَانُواْ يَفۡتَرُونَ
Then they shall know that the truth is with Allah (Alone), and the lies (false gods) which they invented will disappear from them.
28:[76-82] Story of Qarün, the rich man, who was from the people of Musa but he rebelled against the guidance of Allah
28:76
إِنَّ قَـٰرُونَ ڪَانَ مِن قَوۡمِ مُوسَىٰ فَبَغَىٰ عَلَيۡهِمۡ‌ۖ
Verily, Qaroon (Korah) was of Moosas (Moses) people, but he behaved arrogantly towards them.
وَءَاتَيۡنَـٰهُ مِنَ ٱلۡكُنُوزِ مَآ إِنَّ مَفَاتِحَهُ ۥ لَتَنُوٓأُ بِٱلۡعُصۡبَةِ أُوْلِى ٱلۡقُوَّةِ
And We gave him of the treasures, that of which the keys would have been a burden to a body of strong men.
إِذۡ قَالَ لَهُ ۥ قَوۡمُهُ ۥ لَا تَفۡرَحۡ‌ۖ إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ لَا يُحِبُّ ٱلۡفَرِحِينَ
When his people said to him: ‘Do not be glad (with ungratefulness to Allah‘s Favours). Verily! Allah likes not those who are glad (with ungratefulness to Allahs Favours).
28:77
وَٱبۡتَغِ فِيمَآ ءَاتَٮٰكَ ٱللَّهُ ٱلدَّارَ ٱلۡأَخِرَةَ‌ۖ
But seek, with that (wealth) which Allah has bestowed on you, the home of the Hereafter,
وَلَا تَنسَ نَصِيبَكَ مِنَ ٱلدُّنۡيَا‌ۖ وَأَحۡسِن ڪَمَآ أَحۡسَنَ ٱللَّهُ إِلَيۡكَ‌ۖ
and forget not your portion of legal enjoyment in this world, and do good as Allah has been good to you,
وَلَا تَبۡغِ ٱلۡفَسَادَ فِى ٱلۡأَرۡضِ‌ۖ إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ لَا يُحِبُّ ٱلۡمُفۡسِدِينَ
and seek not mischief in the land. Verily, Allah likes not the Mufsidoon (those who commit great crimes and sins, oppressors, tyrants, mischief-makers, corrupts).
28:78
قَالَ إِنَّمَآ أُوتِيتُهُ ۥ عَلَىٰ عِلۡمٍ عِندِىٓ‌ۚ
He said: ‘This has been given to me only because of knowledge I possess.‘
أَوَلَمۡ يَعۡلَمۡ أَنَّ ٱللَّهَ قَدۡ أَهۡلَكَ مِن قَبۡلِهِۦ مِنَ ٱلۡقُرُونِ مَنۡ هُوَ أَشَدُّ مِنۡهُ قُوَّةً۬ وَأَڪۡثَرُ جَمۡعً۬ا‌ۚ
Did he not know that Allah had destroyed before him generations, men who were stronger than him in might and greater in the amount (of riches) they had collected.
وَلَا يُسۡـَٔلُ عَن ذُنُوبِهِمُ ٱلۡمُجۡرِمُونَ
But the Mujrimoon (criminals, disbelievers, polytheists, sinners, etc.) will not be questioned of their sins (because Allah knows them well, so they will be punished without account).
28:79
فَخَرَجَ عَلَىٰ قَوۡمِهِۦ فِى زِينَتِهِۦ‌ۖ
So he went forth before his people in his pomp.
قَالَ ٱلَّذِينَ يُرِيدُونَ ٱلۡحَيَوٰةَ ٱلدُّنۡيَا
Those who were desirous of the life of the world, said:
يَـٰلَيۡتَ لَنَا مِثۡلَ مَآ أُوتِىَ قَـٰرُونُ إِنَّهُ ۥ لَذُو حَظٍّ عَظِيمٍ۬
Ah, would that we had the like of what Qaroon (Korah) has been given?
Verily! He is the owner of a great fortune.‘
28:80
وَقَالَ ٱلَّذِينَ أُوتُواْ ٱلۡعِلۡمَ
But those who had been given (religious) knowledge said:
وَيۡلَڪُمۡ ثَوَابُ ٱللَّهِ خَيۡرٌ۬ لِّمَنۡ ءَامَنَ وَعَمِلَ صَـٰلِحً۬ا
Woe to you! The Reward of Allah (in the Hereafter) is better for those who believe and do righteous good deeds,
وَلَا يُلَقَّٮٰهَآ إِلَّا ٱلصَّـٰبِرُونَ
and this none shall attain except those who are patient (in following the truth).‘
28:81
فَخَسَفۡنَا بِهِۦ وَبِدَارِهِ ٱلۡأَرۡضَ
So We caused the earth to swallow him and his dwelling place.
فَمَا ڪَانَ لَهُ ۥ مِن فِئَةٍ۬ يَنصُرُونَهُ ۥ مِن دُونِ ٱللَّهِ وَمَا كَانَ مِنَ ٱلۡمُنتَصِرِينَ
Then he had no group or party to help him against Allah, nor was he one of those who could save themselves.
28:82
وَأَصۡبَحَ ٱلَّذِينَ تَمَنَّوۡاْ مَكَانَهُ ۥ بِٱلۡأَمۡسِ يَقُولُونَ
And those who had desired (for a position like) his position the day before, began to say:
وَيۡكَأَنَّ ٱللَّهَ يَبۡسُطُ ٱلرِّزۡقَ لِمَن يَشَآءُ مِنۡ عِبَادِهِۦ وَيَقۡدِرُ‌ۖ
Know you not that it is Allah Who enlarges the provision or restricts it to whomsoever He pleases of His slaves.
لَوۡلَآ أَن مَّنَّ ٱللَّهُ عَلَيۡنَا لَخَسَفَ بِنَا‌ۖ
Had it not been that Allah was Gracious to us, He could have caused the earth to swallow us up (also)!
وَيۡكَأَنَّهُ ۥ لَا يُفۡلِحُ ٱلۡكَـٰفِرُونَ
Know you not that the disbelievers will never be successful.
28:[83-88] Revelation of The Qur‘an is the mercy of Allah, let no one turn you away from it
28:83
تِلۡكَ ٱلدَّارُ ٱلۡأَخِرَةُ نَجۡعَلُهَا لِلَّذِينَ لَا يُرِيدُونَ عُلُوًّ۬ا فِى ٱلۡأَرۡضِ وَلَا فَسَادً۬ا‌ۚ
That home of the Hereafter (i.e. Paradise), We shall assign to those who rebel not against the truth with pride and oppression in the land nor do mischief by committing crimes.
وَٱلۡعَـٰقِبَةُ لِلۡمُتَّقِينَ
And the good end is for the Muttaqoon (pious - see V.2:2).
28:84
مَن جَآءَ بِٱلۡحَسَنَةِ فَلَهُ ۥ خَيۡرٌ۬ مِّنۡہَا‌ۖ
Whosoever brings good (Islamic Monotheism along with righteous deeds), he shall have the better thereof,
وَمَن جَآءَ بِٱلسَّيِّئَةِ فَلَا يُجۡزَى ٱلَّذِينَ عَمِلُواْ ٱلسَّيِّـَٔاتِ إِلَّا مَا كَانُواْ يَعۡمَلُونَ
and whosoever brings evil (polytheism along with evil deeds) then, those who do evil deeds will only be requited for what they used to do.
28:85
إِنَّ ٱلَّذِى فَرَضَ عَلَيۡكَ ٱلۡقُرۡءَانَ لَرَآدُّكَ إِلَىٰ مَعَادٍ۬‌ۚ
Verily, He Who has given you (O Muhammad SAW) the Quran (i.e. ordered you to act on its laws and to preach it to others) will surely bring you back to the Maad (place of return, either to Makkah or to Paradise after your death, etc.).
قُل رَّبِّىٓ أَعۡلَمُ مَن جَآءَ بِٱلۡهُدَىٰ وَمَنۡ هُوَ فِى ضَلَـٰلٍ۬ مُّبِينٍ۬
Say (O Muhammad SAW): ‘My Lord is Aware of him who brings guidance, and he who is in manifest error.‘
28:86
وَمَا كُنتَ تَرۡجُوٓاْ أَن يُلۡقَىٰٓ إِلَيۡكَ ٱلۡڪِتَـٰبُ إِلَّا رَحۡمَةً۬ مِّن رَّبِّكَ‌ۖ
And you were not expecting that the Book (this Quran) would be sent down to you, but it is a mercy from your Lord.
فَلَا تَكُونَنَّ ظَهِيرً۬ا لِّلۡكَـٰفِرِينَ
So never be a supporter of the disbelievers.
28:87
وَلَا يَصُدُّنَّكَ عَنۡ ءَايَـٰتِ ٱللَّهِ بَعۡدَ إِذۡ أُنزِلَتۡ إِلَيۡكَ‌ۖ
And let them not turn you (O Muhammad SAW) away from (preaching) the Ayat (revelations and verses) of Allah after they have been sent down to you,
وَٱدۡعُ إِلَىٰ رَبِّكَ‌ۖ وَلَا تَكُونَنَّ مِنَ ٱلۡمُشۡرِڪِينَ
and invite (men) to (believe in) your Lord, (i.e: in the Oneness (Tauheed) of Allah
(1) Oneness of the Lordship of Allah;
(2) Oneness of the worship of Allah;
(3) Oneness of the Names and Qualities of Allah)
and be not of Al- Mushrikoon (those who associate partners with Allah, e.g. polytheists, pagans, idolaters, those who disbelieve in the Oneness of Allah and deny the Prophethood of Messenger Muhammad SAW).
28:88
وَلَا تَدۡعُ مَعَ ٱللَّهِ إِلَـٰهًا ءَاخَرَ‌ۘ
And invoke not any other ilah (god) along with Allah,
لَآ إِلَـٰهَ إِلَّا هُوَ‌ۚ
La ilaha illa Huwa (none has the right to be worshipped but He).
كُلُّ شَىۡءٍ هَالِكٌ إِلَّا وَجۡهَهُ ۥ‌ۚ
Everything will perish save His Face.
لَهُ ٱلۡحُكۡمُ وَإِلَيۡهِ تُرۡجَعُونَ
His is the Decision, and to Him you (all) shall be returned.*********
