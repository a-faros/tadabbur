Al-Humaza
The Scandalmonger, The Traducer, The Gossipmonger, The Slanderer
104:[1-9] The slanderer, defamer and stingy will be thrown in the fire
104:1
وَيۡلٌ۬ لِّڪُلِّ هُمَزَةٍ۬ لُّمَزَةٍ
Woe to every slanderer and backbiter.
104:2
ٱلَّذِى جَمَعَ مَالاً۬ وَعَدَّدَهُ ۥ
Who has gathered wealth and counted it,
104:3
يَحۡسَبُ أَنَّ مَالَهُ ۥۤ أَخۡلَدَهُ ۥ
He thinks that his wealth will make him last forever!
104:4
كَلَّا‌ۖ لَيُنۢبَذَنَّ فِى ٱلۡحُطَمَةِ
Nay! Verily, he will be thrown into the crushing Fire.
104:5
وَمَآ أَدۡرَٮٰكَ مَا ٱلۡحُطَمَةُ
And what will make you know what the crushing Fire is?
104:6
نَارُ ٱللَّهِ ٱلۡمُوقَدَةُ
The fire of Allâh, kindled,
104:7
ٱلَّتِى تَطَّلِعُ عَلَى ٱلۡأَفۡـِٔدَةِ
Which leaps up over the hearts,
104:8
إِنَّہَا عَلَيۡہِم مُّؤۡصَدَةٌ۬
Verily, it shall be closed in on them,
104:9
فِى عَمَدٍ۬ مُّمَدَّدَةِۭ
In pillars stretched forth.(i.e. they will be punished in the Fire with pillars, etc.)*********
