Al-Ikhlaas
Purity of Faith, The Fidelity, Tawheed (Oneness of God), The Declaration of [God's] Perfection
112:[1-4] Tawhïd - the unique attribute of Allah
112:1
قُلۡ هُوَ ٱللَّهُ أَحَدٌ
Say (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW): ‘He is Allâh, (the) One.
112:2
ٱللَّهُ ٱلصَّمَدُ
Allâh-us-Samad‘(The Self-Sufficient Master, Whom all creatures need, He neither eats nor drinks).
112:3
لَمۡ يَلِدۡ وَلَمۡ يُولَدۡ
He begets not, nor was He begotten;
112:4
وَلَمۡ يَكُن لَّهُ ۥ ڪُفُوًا أَحَدُۢ
And there is none co-equal or comparable unto Him.‘*********
