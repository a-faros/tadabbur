Al-Anfaal
The Spoils of War
8:[1-10] Commandment relating to the spoils of war (booty). Battle of Badr, a battle between truth and falsehood
8:1
يَسْأَلُونَكَ عَنِ الْأَنْفَالِ ۖ
They ask you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) about the spoils of war.
قُلِ الْأَنْفَالُ لِلَّهِ وَالرَّسُولِ ۖ
Say: ‘The spoils are for Allah (الله) and the Messenger.‘
فَاتَّقُوا اللَّهَ وَأَصْلِحُوا ذَاتَ بَيْنِكُمْ ۖ
So fear Allah (الله) and adjust all matters of difference among you,
وَأَطِيعُوا اللَّهَ وَرَسُولَهُ إِنْ كُنْتُمْ مُؤْمِنِينَ
and obey Allah (الله) and His Messenger (Muhammad (محمد) SAW), if you are believers.
8:2
إِنَّمَا الْمُؤْمِنُونَ الَّذِينَ إِذَا ذُكِرَ اللَّهُ وَجِلَتْ قُلُوبُهُمْ
The believers are only those who, when Allah (الله) is mentioned, feel a fear in their hearts
وَإِذَا تُلِيَتْ عَلَيْهِمْ آيَاتُهُ زَادَتْهُمْ إِيمَانًا
and when His Verses (this Quran (القرآن)) are recited unto them, they (i.e. the Verses) increase their Faith;
وَعَلَى رَبِّهِمْ يَتَوَكَّلُونَ
and they put their trust in their Lord (Alone);
8:3
الَّذِينَ يُقِيمُونَ الصَّلَاةَ وَمِمَّا رَزَقْنَاهُمْ يُنْفِقُونَ
Who perform As-Salat (الصَّلَاةَ) (IqamatasSalat) and spend out of that We have provided them.
8:4
أُولَئِكَ هُمُ الْمُؤْمِنُونَ حَقًّا ۚ
It is they who are the believers in truth.
لَهُمْ دَرَجَاتٌ عِنْدَ رَبِّهِمْ وَمَغْفِرَةٌ وَرِزْقٌ كَرِيمٌ
For them are grades of dignity with their Lord, and Forgiveness and a generous provision (Paradise (الجنة)).
8:5
كَمَا أَخْرَجَكَ رَبُّكَ مِنْ بَيْتِكَ بِالْحَقِّ
As your Lord caused you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) to go out from your home with the truth,
وَإِنَّ فَرِيقًا مِنَ الْمُؤْمِنِينَ لَكَارِهُونَ
and verily, a party among the believers disliked it;
8:6
يُجَادِلُونَكَ فِي الْحَقِّ بَعْدَمَا تَبَيَّنَ كَأَنَّمَا يُسَاقُونَ إِلَى الْمَوْتِ وَهُمْ يَنْظُرُونَ
Disputing with you concerning the truth after it was made manifest, as if they were being driven to death, while they were looking (at it).
8:7
وَإِذْ يَعِدُكُمُ اللَّهُ إِحْدَى الطَّائِفَتَيْنِ أَنَّهَا لَكُمْ
And (remember) when Allah (الله) promised you (Muslims) one of the two parties (of the enemy i.e. either the army or the caravan) that it should be yours,
وَتَوَدُّونَ أَنَّ غَيْرَ ذَاتِ الشَّوْكَةِ تَكُونُ لَكُمْ
you wished that the one not armed (the caravan) should be yours,
وَيُرِيدُ اللَّهُ أَنْ يُحِقَّ الْحَقَّ بِكَلِمَاتِهِ وَيَقْطَعَ دَابِرَ الْكَافِرِينَ
but Allah (الله) willed to justifya the truth by His Words and to cut off the roots of the disbelievers (i.e. in the battle of Badr (بدر)).
8:8
لِيُحِقَّ الْحَقَّ وَيُبْطِلَ الْبَاطِلَ وَلَوْ كَرِهَ الْمُجْرِمُونَ
That He might cause the truth to triumph and bring falsehood to nothing, even though the Mujrimoon (disbelievers, polytheists, sinners, criminals, etc.) hate it.
8:9
إِذْ تَسْتَغِيثُونَ رَبَّكُمْ فَاسْتَجَابَ لَكُمْ
(Remember) when you sought help of your Lord and He answered you (saying):
أَنِّي مُمِدُّكُمْ بِأَلْفٍ مِنَ الْمَلَائِكَةِ مُرْدِفِينَ
I will help you with a thousand of the angels each behind the other (following one another) in succession.‘
8:10
وَمَا جَعَلَهُ اللَّهُ إِلَّا بُشْرَى وَلِتَطْمَئِنَّ بِهِ قُلُوبُكُمْ ۚ
Allah (الله) made it only as glad tidings, and that your hearts be at rest therewith.
وَمَا النَّصْرُ إِلَّا مِنْ عِنْدِ اللَّهِ ۚ
And there is no victory except from Allah (الله).
إِنَّ اللَّهَ عَزِيزٌ حَكِيمٌ
Verily, Allah (الله) is All-Mighty, All-Wise (Al-Hakeem (ٱلْحَكِيمُ)).
8:[11-19] Allah‘s help during the Battle of Badr. Allah‘s decision between Muslims and kuffar
8:11
إِذْ يُغَشِّيكُمُ النُّعَاسَ أَمَنَةً مِنْهُ
(Remember) when He covered you with a slumber as a security from Him,
وَيُنَزِّلُ عَلَيْكُمْ مِنَ السَّمَاءِ مَاءً لِيُطَهِّرَكُمْ بِهِ وَيُذْهِبَ عَنْكُمْ رِجْزَ الشَّيْطَانِ
and He caused water (rain) to descend on you from the sky, to clean you thereby and to remove from you the Rijz (whispering, evil-suggestions, etc.) of Shaitan (Satan (الشيطان)),
وَلِيَرْبِطَ عَلَى قُلُوبِكُمْ وَيُثَبِّتَ بِهِ الْأَقْدَامَ
and to strengthen your hearts, and make your feet firm thereby.
8:12
إِذْ يُوحِي رَبُّكَ إِلَى الْمَلَائِكَةِ أَنِّي مَعَكُمْ فَثَبِّتُوا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا ۚ
(Remember) when your Lord inspired the angels,
Verily, I am with you, so keep firm those who have believed.
سَأُلْقِي فِي قُلُوبِ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا الرُّعْبَ فَاضْرِبُوا فَوْقَ الْأَعْنَاقِ وَاضْرِبُوا مِنْهُمْ كُلَّ بَنَانٍ
I will cast terror into the hearts of those who have disbelieved, so strike them over the necks, and smite over all their fingers and toes.‘
8:13
ذَلِكَ بِأَنَّهُمْ شَاقُّوا اللَّهَ وَرَسُولَهُ ۚ
This is because they defied and disobeyed Allah (الله) and His Messenger.
وَمَنْ يُشَاقِقِ اللَّهَ وَرَسُولَهُ فَإِنَّ اللَّهَ شَدِيدُ الْعِقَابِ
And whoever defies and disobeys Allah (الله) and His Messenger, then verily, Allah (الله) is Severe in punishment.
8:14
ذَلِكُمْ فَذُوقُوهُ وَأَنَّ لِلْكَافِرِينَ عَذَابَ النَّارِ
This is the torment, so taste it, and surely for the disbelievers is the torment of the Fire.
8:15
يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا إِذَا لَقِيتُمُ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا زَحْفًا فَلَا تُوَلُّوهُمُ الْأَدْبَارَ
O you who believe! When you meet those who disbelieve, in a battle-field, never turn your backs to them.
8:16
وَمَنْ يُوَلِّهِمْ يَوْمَئِذٍ دُبُرَهُ إِلَّا مُتَحَرِّفًا لِقِتَالٍ أَوْ مُتَحَيِّزًا إِلَى فِئَةٍ فَقَدْ بَاءَ بِغَضَبٍ مِنَ اللَّهِ
And whoever turns his back to them on such a day - unless it be a stratagem of war, or to retreat to a troop (of his own), - he indeed has drawn upon himself wrath from Allah (الله).
وَمَأْوَاهُ جَهَنَّمُ ۖ وَبِئْسَ الْمَصِيرُ
And his abode is Hell (جهنم), and worst indeed is that destination!
8:17
فَلَمْ تَقْتُلُوهُمْ وَلَكِنَّ اللَّهَ قَتَلَهُمْ ۚ
You killed them not, but Allah (الله) killed them.
وَمَا رَمَيْتَ إِذْ رَمَيْتَ وَلَكِنَّ اللَّهَ رَمَى ۚ
And you (Muhammad (محمد) SAW) threw not when you did throw but Allah (الله) threw,
وَلِيُبْلِيَ الْمُؤْمِنِينَ مِنْهُ بَلَاءً حَسَنًا ۚ
that He might test the believers by a fair trial from Him.
إِنَّ اللَّهَ سَمِيعٌ عَلِيمٌ
Verily, Allah (الله) is All- Hearer, All-Knower (Al-‘Alee (ٱلْعَلِيُّ)m (ٱلْعَلِيمُ)).
8:18
ذَلِكُمْ وَأَنَّ اللَّهَ مُوهِنُ كَيْدِ الْكَافِرِينَ
This (is the fact) and surely, Allah (الله) weakens the deceitful plots of the disbelievers.
8:19
إِنْ تَسْتَفْتِحُوا فَقَدْ جَاءَكُمُ الْفَتْحُ ۖ
(O disbelievers) if you ask for a judgement, now has the judgement come unto you
وَإِنْ تَنْتَهُوا فَهُوَ خَيْرٌ لَكُمْ ۖ
and if you cease (to do wrong), it will be better for you,
وَإِنْ تَعُودُوا نَعُدْ وَلَنْ تُغْنِيَ عَنْكُمْ فِئَتُكُمْ شَيْئًا وَلَوْ كَثُرَتْ
and if you return (to the attack), so shall We return, and your forces will be of no avail to you, however numerous it be,
وَأَنَّ اللَّهَ مَعَ الْمُؤْمِنِينَ
and verily, Allah (الله) is with the believers.
8:[20-28] The worst people in the sight of Allah are those who do not use their common sense. Guard yourselves against temptations
8:20
يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا أَطِيعُوا اللَّهَ وَرَسُولَهُ وَلَا تَوَلَّوْا عَنْهُ وَأَنْتُمْ تَسْمَعُونَ
O you who believe! Obey Allah (الله) and His Messenger, and turn not away from him (i.e. Messenger Muhammad (محمد) SAW) while you are hearing.
8:21
وَلَا تَكُونُوا كَالَّذِينَ قَالُوا سَمِعْنَا وَهُمْ لَا يَسْمَعُونَ
And be not like those who say: ‘We have heard,‘ but they hear not.
8:22
إِنَّ شَرَّ الدَّوَابِّ عِنْدَ اللَّهِ الصُّمُّ الْبُكْمُ الَّذِينَ لَا يَعْقِلُونَ
Verily! The worst of (moving) living creatures with Allah (الله) are the deaf and the dumb, those who understand not (i.e. the disbelievers).
8:23
وَلَوْ عَلِمَ اللَّهُ فِيهِمْ خَيْرًا لَأَسْمَعَهُمْ ۖ
Had Allah (الله) known of any good in them, He would indeed have made them listen,
وَلَوْ أَسْمَعَهُمْ لَتَوَلَّوْا وَهُمْ مُعْرِضُونَ
and even if He had made them listen, they would but have turned away, averse (to the truth).
8:24
يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا اسْتَجِيبُوا لِلَّهِ وَلِلرَّسُولِ إِذَا دَعَاكُمْ لِمَا يُحْيِيكُمْ ۖ
O you who believe!
Answer Allah (الله) (by obeying Him) and (His) Messenger when he (SAW) calls you to that which will give you life,
وَاعْلَمُوا أَنَّ اللَّهَ يَحُولُ بَيْنَ الْمَرْءِ وَقَلْبِهِ
and know that Allah (الله) comes in between a person and his heart (i.e. He prevents an evil person to decide anything).
وَأَنَّهُ إِلَيْهِ تُحْشَرُونَ
And verily to Him you shall (all) be gathered.
8:25
وَاتَّقُوا فِتْنَةً لَا تُصِيبَنَّ الَّذِينَ ظَلَمُوا مِنْكُمْ خَاصَّةً ۖ
And fear the Fitnah (affliction and trial, etc.) which affects not in particular (only) those of you who do wrong (but it may afflict all the good and the bad people),
وَاعْلَمُوا أَنَّ اللَّهَ شَدِيدُ الْعِقَابِ
and know that Allah (الله) is Severe in punishment.
8:26
وَاذْكُرُوا إِذْ أَنْتُمْ قَلِيلٌ مُسْتَضْعَفُونَ فِي الْأَرْضِ تَخَافُونَ أَنْ يَتَخَطَّفَكُمُ النَّاسُ
And remember when you were few and were reckoned weak in the land, and were afraid that men might kidnap you,
فَآوَاكُمْ وَأَيَّدَكُمْ بِنَصْرِهِ وَرَزَقَكُمْ مِنَ الطَّيِّبَاتِ لَعَلَّكُمْ تَشْكُرُونَ
but He provided a safe place for you, strengthened you with His Help, and provided you with good things so that you might be grateful.
8:27
يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا لَا تَخُونُوا اللَّهَ وَالرَّسُولَ وَتَخُونُوا أَمَانَاتِكُمْ وَأَنْتُمْ تَعْلَمُونَ
O you who believe! Betray not Allah (الله) and His Messenger, nor betray knowingly your Amanat (things entrusted to you, and all the duties which Allah (الله) has ordained for you).
8:28
وَاعْلَمُوا أَنَّمَا أَمْوَالُكُمْ وَأَوْلَادُكُمْ فِتْنَةٌ وَأَنَّ اللَّهَ عِنْدَهُ أَجْرٌ عَظِيمٌ
And know that your possessions and your children are but a trial and that surely with Allah (الله) is a mighty reward.
8:[29-37] If you become Godfearing, He will grant you wisdom to judge between right and wrong. The lawful guardians of Kaa‘bah are those who have fear of Allah
8:29
يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا إِنْ تَتَّقُوا اللَّهَ يَجْعَلْ لَكُمْ فُرْقَانًا
O you who believe!
If you obey and fear Allah (الله), He will grant you Furqan a criterion ((to judge between right and wrong), or (Makhraj, i.e. making a way for you to get out from every difficulty)),
وَيُكَفِّرْ عَنْكُمْ سَيِّئَاتِكُمْ وَيَغْفِرْ لَكُمْ ۗ
and will expiate for you your sins, and forgive you,
وَاللَّهُ ذُو الْفَضْلِ الْعَظِيمِ
and Allah (الله) is the Owner of the Great Bounty.
8:30
وَإِذْ يَمْكُرُ بِكَ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا لِيُثْبِتُوكَ أَوْ يَقْتُلُوكَ أَوْ يُخْرِجُوكَ ۚ
And (remember) when the disbelievers plotted against you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) to imprison you, or to kill you, or to get you out (from your home, i.e. Makkah (مكة));
وَيَمْكُرُونَ وَيَمْكُرُ اللَّهُ ۖ وَاللَّهُ خَيْرُ الْمَاكِرِينَ
they were plotting and Allah (الله) too was planning, and Allah (الله) is the Best of the planners.
8:31
وَإِذَا تُتْلَى عَلَيْهِمْ آيَاتُنَا قَالُوا قَدْ سَمِعْنَا لَوْ نَشَاءُ لَقُلْنَا مِثْلَ هَذَا ۙ إِنْ هَذَا إِلَّا أَسَاطِيرُ الْأَوَّلِينَ
And when Our Verses (of the Quran (القرآن)) are recited to them, they say:
We have heard this (the Quran (القرآن)); if we wish we can say the like of this. This is nothing but the tales of the ancients.‘
8:32
وَإِذْ قَالُوا
And (remember) when they said:
اللَّهُمَّ إِنْ كَانَ هَذَا هُوَ الْحَقَّ مِنْ عِنْدِكَ فَأَمْطِرْ عَلَيْنَا حِجَارَةً مِنَ السَّمَاءِ أَوِ ائْتِنَا بِعَذَابٍ أَلِيمٍ
O Allah (الله)! If this (the Quran (القرآن)) is indeed the truth (revealed) from You, then rain down stones on us from the sky or bring on us a painful torment.‘
8:33
‏وَمَا كَانَ اللَّهُ لِيُعَذِّبَهُمْ وَأَنْتَ فِيهِمْ ۚ
And Allah (الله) would not punish them while you (Muhammad (محمد) SAW) are amongst them,
وَمَا كَانَ اللَّهُ مُعَذِّبَهُمْ وَهُمْ يَسْتَغْفِرُونَ
nor will He punish them while they seek (Allah (الله)‘s) Forgiveness.
8:34
وَمَا لَهُمْ أَلَّا يُعَذِّبَهُمُ اللَّهُ وَهُمْ يَصُدُّونَ عَنِ الْمَسْجِدِ الْحَرَامِ وَمَا كَانُوا أَوْلِيَاءَهُ ۚ
And why should not Allah (الله) punish them while they stop (men) from Al-Masjid-al-Haram, and they are not its guardians?
إِنْ أَوْلِيَاؤُهُ إِلَّا الْمُتَّقُونَ وَلَكِنَّ أَكْثَرَهُمْ لَا يَعْلَمُونَ
None can be its guardian except Al-Muttaqoon (ْالمُتَّقِينَ) (the pious - see V.2:2), but most of them know not.
8:35
وَمَا كَانَ صَلَاتُهُمْ عِنْدَ الْبَيْتِ إِلَّا مُكَاءً وَتَصْدِيَةً ۚ
Their Salat (prayer) at the House (of Allah (الله), i.e. the Kaaba (الكعبة) at Makkah (مكة)) was nothing but whistling and clapping of hands.
فَذُوقُوا الْعَذَابَ بِمَا كُنْتُمْ تَكْفُرُونَ
Therefore taste the punishment because you used to disbelieve.
8:36
إِنَّ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا يُنْفِقُونَ أَمْوَالَهُمْ لِيَصُدُّوا عَنْ سَبِيلِ اللَّهِ ۚ
Verily, those who disbelieve spend their wealth to hinder (men) from the Path of Allah (الله),
فَسَيُنْفِقُونَهَا ثُمَّ تَكُونُ عَلَيْهِمْ حَسْرَةً ثُمَّ يُغْلَبُونَ ۗ
and so will they continue to spend it; but in the end it will become an anguish for them. Then they will be overcome.
وَالَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا إِلَى جَهَنَّمَ يُحْشَرُونَ
And those who disbelieve will be gathered unto Hell (جهنم).
8:37
لِيَمِيزَ اللَّهُ الْخَبِيثَ مِنَ الطَّيِّبِ
In order that Allah (الله) may distinguish the wicked (disbelievers, polytheists and doers of evil deeds) from the good (believers of Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism and doers of righteous deeds),
وَيَجْعَلَ الْخَبِيثَ بَعْضَهُ عَلَى بَعْضٍ فَيَرْكُمَهُ جَمِيعًا فَيَجْعَلَهُ فِي جَهَنَّمَ ۚ
and put the wicked (disbelievers, polytheists and doers of evil deeds) one on another, heap them together and cast them into Hell (جهنم).
أُولَئِكَ هُمُ الْخَاسِرُونَ
Those! it is they who are the losers.
8:[38-40] For the unbelievers, who embrace Islam, their past is forgiven
8:38
قُلْ لِلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا إِنْ يَنْتَهُوا يُغْفَرْ لَهُمْ مَا قَدْ سَلَفَ
Say to those who have disbelieved, if they cease (from disbelief) their past will be forgiven.
وَإِنْ يَعُودُوا فَقَدْ مَضَتْ سُنَّتُ الْأَوَّلِينَ
But if they return (thereto), then the examples of those (punished) before them have already preceded (as a warning).
8:39
وَقَاتِلُوهُمْ حَتَّى لَا تَكُونَ فِتْنَةٌ وَيَكُونَ الدِّينُ كُلُّهُ لِلَّهِ ۚ
And fight them until there is no more Fitnah (disbelief and polytheism: i.e. worshipping others besides Allah (الله)) and the religion (worship) will all be for Allah (الله) Alone (in the whole of the world).
فَإِنِ انْتَهَوْا فَإِنَّ اللَّهَ بِمَا يَعْمَلُونَ بَصِيرٌ
But if they cease (worshipping others besides Allah (الله)), then certainly, Allah (الله) is All-Seer (Al-Baseer (ٱلْبَصِيرُ)) of what they do.
8:40
وَإِنْ تَوَلَّوْا فَاعْلَمُوا أَنَّ اللَّهَ مَوْلَاكُمْ ۚ
And if they turn away, then know that Allah (الله) is your Maula (Patron, Lord, Protector and Supporter, etc.),
نِعْمَ الْمَوْلَى وَنِعْمَ النَّصِيرُ
(what) an Excellent Maula, and (what) an Excellent Helper!
8:[41-44] Rules about the distribution of spoils of war
8:41
وَاعْلَمُوا أَنَّمَا غَنِمْتُمْ مِنْ شَيْءٍ فَأَنَّ لِلَّهِ خُمُسَهُ وَلِلرَّسُولِ
And know that whatever of war-booty that you may gain, verily one-fifth (1/5th) of it is assigned to Allah (الله), and to the Messenger,
وَلِذِي الْقُرْبَى وَالْيَتَامَى وَالْمَسَاكِينِ وَابْنِ السَّبِيلِ
and to the near relatives (of the Messenger (Muhammad (محمد) SAW)), (and also) the orphans, Al-Masakin (the poor) and the wayfarer,
إِنْ كُنْتُمْ آمَنْتُمْ بِاللَّهِ وَمَا أَنْزَلْنَا عَلَى عَبْدِنَا يَوْمَ الْفُرْقَانِ يَوْمَ الْتَقَى الْجَمْعَانِ ۗ
if you have believed in Allah (الله) and in that which We sent down to Our slave (Muhammad (محمد) SAW) on the Day of criterion (between right and wrong), the Day when the two forces met (the battle of Badr (بدر)) -
وَاللَّهُ عَلَى كُلِّ شَيْءٍ قَدِيرٌ
And Allah (الله) is Able to do all things.
8:42
إِذْ أَنْتُمْ بِالْعُدْوَةِ الدُّنْيَا وَهُمْ بِالْعُدْوَةِ الْقُصْوَى وَالرَّكْبُ أَسْفَلَ مِنْكُمْ ۚ
(And remember) when you (the Muslim army) were on the near side of the valley, and they on the farther side, and the caravan on the ground lower than you.
وَلَوْ تَوَاعَدْتُمْ لَاخْتَلَفْتُمْ فِي الْمِيعَادِ ۙ وَلَكِنْ لِيَقْضِيَ اللَّهُ أَمْرًا كَانَ مَفْعُولًا
Even if you had made a mutual appointment to meet, you would certainly have failed in the appointment, but (you met) that Allah (الله) might accomplish a matter already ordained (in His Knowledge);
لِيَهْلِكَ مَنْ هَلَكَ عَنْ بَيِّنَةٍ وَيَحْيَى مَنْ حَيَّ عَنْ بَيِّنَةٍ ۗ
so that those who were to be destroyed (for their rejecting the Faith) might be destroyed after a clear evidence, and those who were to live (i.e. believers) might live after a clear evidence.
وَإِنَّ اللَّهَ لَسَمِيعٌ عَلِيمٌ
And surely, Allah (الله) is All-Hearer, All-Knower (Al-‘Alee (ٱلْعَلِيُّ)m (ٱلْعَلِيمُ)).
8:43
إِذْ يُرِيكَهُمُ اللَّهُ فِي مَنَامِكَ قَلِيلًا ۖ
(And remember) when Allah (الله) showed them to you as few in your (i.e. Muhammad (محمد) SAW‘s SAW) dream,
وَلَوْ أَرَاكَهُمْ كَثِيرًا لَفَشِلْتُمْ وَلَتَنَازَعْتُمْ فِي الْأَمْرِ
if He had shown them to you as many, you would surely have been discouraged, and you would surely have disputed in making a decision.
وَلَكِنَّ اللَّهَ سَلَّمَ ۗ
But Allah (الله) saved (you).
إِنَّهُ عَلِيمٌ بِذَاتِ الصُّدُورِ
Certainly, He is the All-Knower (Al-‘Alee (ٱلْعَلِيُّ)m (ٱلْعَلِيمُ)) of what is in the breasts.
8:44
وَإِذْ يُرِيكُمُوهُمْ إِذِ الْتَقَيْتُمْ فِي أَعْيُنِكُمْ قَلِيلًا وَيُقَلِّلُكُمْ فِي أَعْيُنِهِمْ
And (remember) when you met (the army of the disbelievers on the Day of the battle of Badr (بدر)), He showed them to you as few in your eyes and He made you appear as few in their eyes,
لِيَقْضِيَ اللَّهُ أَمْرًا كَانَ مَفْعُولًا ۗ
so that Allah (الله) might accomplish a matter already ordained (in His Knowledge),
وَإِلَى اللَّهِ تُرْجَعُ الْأُمُورُ
and to Allah (الله) return all matters (for decision).
8: Order of Allah to remain firm during combat against enemy
[45-48]
8:45
يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا إِذَا لَقِيتُمْ فِئَةً فَاثْبُتُوا وَاذْكُرُوا اللَّهَ كَثِيرًا لَعَلَّكُمْ تُفْلِحُونَ
O you who believe!
When you meet (an enemy) force, take a firm stand against them and remember the Name of Allah (الله) much (both with tongue and mind), so that you may be successful.
8:46
وَأَطِيعُوا اللَّهَ وَرَسُولَهُ
And obey Allah (الله) and His Messenger,
وَلَا تَنَازَعُوا فَتَفْشَلُوا وَتَذْهَبَ رِيحُكُمْ ۖ
and do not dispute (with one another) lest you lose courage and your strength depart,
وَاصْبِرُوا ۚ
and be patient.
إِنَّ اللَّهَ مَعَ الصَّابِرِينَ
Surely, Allah (الله) is with those who are As-Sabirin (the patient ones, etc.).
8:47
وَلَا تَكُونُوا كَالَّذِينَ خَرَجُوا مِنْ دِيَارِهِمْ بَطَرًا وَرِئَاءَ النَّاسِ وَيَصُدُّونَ عَنْ سَبِيلِ اللَّهِ ۚ
And be not like those who come out of their homes boastfully and to be seen of men, and hinder (men) from the Path of Allah (الله).
وَاللَّهُ بِمَا يَعْمَلُونَ مُحِيطٌ
and Allah (الله) is Muheetun (encircling and thoroughly comprehending) all that they do.
8:48
وَإِذْ زَيَّنَ لَهُمُ الشَّيْطَانُ أَعْمَالَهُمْ وَقَالَ لَا غَالِبَ لَكُمُ الْيَوْمَ مِنَ النَّاسِ وَإِنِّي جَارٌ لَكُمْ ۖ
And (remember) when Shaitan (Satan (الشيطان)) made their (evil) deeds seem fair to them and said,
No one of mankind can overcome you this Day (of the battle of Badr (بدر)) and verily, I am your neighbour (for each and every help).‘
فَلَمَّا تَرَاءَتِ الْفِئَتَانِ نَكَصَ عَلَى عَقِبَيْهِ وَقَالَ إِنِّي بَرِيءٌ مِنْكُمْ
But when the two forces came in sight of each other, he ran away and said
Verily, I have nothing to do with you.
إِنِّي أَرَى مَا لَا تَرَوْنَ إِنِّي أَخَافُ اللَّهَ ۚ
Verily! I see what you see not. Verily! I fear Allah (الله)
وَاللَّهُ شَدِيدُ الْعِقَابِ
for Allah (الله) is Severe in punishment.‘
8:[49-52] Victory of the believers and the painful death of the unbelievers
8:[53-54] Allah does not change His blessings unless people change themselves
8:[55-58] Treaties must be honored unless broken with proper notification
8:49
إِذْ يَقُولُ الْمُنَافِقُونَ وَالَّذِينَ فِي قُلُوبِهِمْ مَرَضٌ غَرَّ هَؤُلَاءِ دِينُهُمْ ۗ
When the hypocrites and those in whose hearts was a disease (of disbelief) said: ‘These people (Muslims) are deceived by their religion.‘
وَمَنْ يَتَوَكَّلْ عَلَى اللَّهِ فَإِنَّ اللَّهَ عَزِيزٌ حَكِيمٌ
But whoever puts his trust in Allah (الله), then surely, Allah (الله) is All-Mighty, All-Wise (Al-Hakeem (ٱلْحَكِيمُ)).
8:50
وَلَوْ تَرَى إِذْ يَتَوَفَّى الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا ۙ الْمَلَائِكَةُ يَضْرِبُونَ وُجُوهَهُمْ وَأَدْبَارَهُمْ
And if you could see when the angels take away the souls of those who disbelieve (at death), they smite their faces and their backs, (saying):
وَذُوقُوا عَذَابَ الْحَرِيقِ
Taste the punishment of the blazing Fire.‘
8:51
ذَلِكَ بِمَا قَدَّمَتْ أَيْدِيكُمْ
This is because of that which your hands had forwarded.
وَأَنَّ اللَّهَ لَيْسَ بِظَلَّامٍ لِلْعَبِيدِ
And verily, Allah (الله) is not unjust to His slaves.‘
8:52
كَدَأْبِ آلِ فِرْعَوْنَ ۙ وَالَّذِينَ مِنْ قَبْلِهِمْ ۚ
Similar to the behaviour of the people of Firaun (Pharaoh (فرعون)), and of those before them;
كَفَرُوا بِآيَاتِ اللَّهِ فَأَخَذَهُمُ اللَّهُ بِذُنُوبِهِمْ ۗ
they rejected the Ayat (proofs, verses, etc.) of Allah (الله), so Allah (الله) punished them for their sins.
إِنَّ اللَّهَ قَوِيٌّ شَدِيدُ الْعِقَابِ
Verily, Allah (الله) is All-Strong, Severe in punishment.
8:53
ذَلِكَ بِأَنَّ اللَّهَ لَمْ يَكُ مُغَيِّرًا نِعْمَةً أَنْعَمَهَا عَلَى قَوْمٍ حَتَّى يُغَيِّرُوا مَا بِأَنْفُسِهِمْ ۙ
That is so because Allah (الله) will never change a grace which He has bestowed on a people until they change what is in their ownselves.
وَأَنَّ اللَّهَ سَمِيعٌ عَلِيمٌ
And verily, Allah (الله) is All-Hearer, All-Knower (Al-‘Alee (ٱلْعَلِيُّ)m (ٱلْعَلِيمُ)).
8:54
كَدَأْبِ آلِ فِرْعَوْنَ ۙ وَالَّذِينَ مِنْ قَبْلِهِمْ ۚ
Similar to the behaviour of the people of Firaun (Pharaoh (فرعون)), and those before them.
كَذَّبُوا بِآيَاتِ رَبِّهِمْ فَأَهْلَكْنَاهُمْ بِذُنُوبِهِمْ وَأَغْرَقْنَا آلَ فِرْعَوْنَ ۚ
They belied the Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, etc.), of their Lord, so We destroyed them for their sins, and We drowned the people of Firaun (Pharaoh (فرعون))
وَكُلٌّ كَانُوا ظَالِمِينَ
for they were all Zalimoon (polytheists and wrong-doers, etc.).
8:55
إِنَّ شَرَّ الدَّوَابِّ عِنْدَ اللَّهِ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا فَهُمْ لَا يُؤْمِنُونَ
Verily, The worst of moving (living) creatures before Allah (الله) are those who disbelieve, - so they shall not believe.
8:56
الَّذِينَ عَاهَدْتَ مِنْهُمْ ثُمَّ يَنْقُضُونَ عَهْدَهُمْ فِي كُلِّ مَرَّةٍ وَهُمْ لَا يَتَّقُونَ
They are those with whom you made a covenant, but they break their covenant every time and they do not fear Allah (الله).
8:57
فَإِمَّا تَثْقَفَنَّهُمْ فِي الْحَرْبِ فَشَرِّدْ بِهِمْ مَنْ خَلْفَهُمْ لَعَلَّهُمْ يَذَّكَّرُونَ
So if you gain the mastery over them in war, punish them severely in order to disperse those who are behind them, so that they may learn a lesson.
8:58
وَإِمَّا تَخَافَنَّ مِنْ قَوْمٍ خِيَانَةً فَانْبِذْ إِلَيْهِمْ عَلَى سَوَاءٍ ۚ
If you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) fear treachery from any people throw back (their covenant) to them (so as to be) on equal terms (that there will be no more covenant between you and them).
إِنَّ اللَّهَ لَا يُحِبُّ الْخَائِنِينَ
Certainly Allah (الله) likes not the treacherous.
8:[59-64] Order to remain prepared for war against the unbelievers. Make peace, if the enemy is inclined towards peace
8:59
وَلَا يَحْسَبَنَّ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا سَبَقُوا ۚ
And let not those who disbelieve think that they can outstrip (escape from the punishment).
إِنَّهُمْ لَا يُعْجِزُونَ
Verily, they will never be able to save themselves (from Allah (الله)‘s Punishment).
8:60
وَأَعِدُّوا لَهُمْ مَا اسْتَطَعْتُمْ مِنْ قُوَّةٍ وَمِنْ رِبَاطِ الْخَيْلِ تُرْهِبُونَ بِهِ عَدُوَّ اللَّهِ وَعَدُوَّكُمْ
And make ready against them all you can of power, including steeds of war (tanks, planes, missiles, artillery, etc.) to threaten the enemy of Allah (الله) and your enemy,
وَآخَرِينَ مِنْ دُونِهِمْ لَا تَعْلَمُونَهُمُ اللَّهُ يَعْلَمُهُمْ ۚ
and others besides whom, you may not know but whom Allah (الله) does know.
وَمَا تُنْفِقُوا مِنْ شَيْءٍ فِي سَبِيلِ اللَّهِ يُوَفَّ إِلَيْكُمْ وَأَنْتُمْ لَا تُظْلَمُونَ
And whatever you shall spend in the Cause of Allah (الله) shall be repaid unto you, and you shall not be treated unjustly.
8:61
وَإِنْ جَنَحُوا لِلسَّلْمِ فَاجْنَحْ لَهَا وَتَوَكَّلْ عَلَى اللَّهِ ۚ
But if they incline to peace, you also incline to it, and (put your) trust in Allah (الله).
إِنَّهُ هُوَ السَّمِيعُ الْعَلِيمُ
Verily, He is the All-Hearer, the All-Knower (Al-‘Alee (ٱلْعَلِيُّ)m (ٱلْعَلِيمُ)).
8:62
وَإِنْ يُرِيدُوا أَنْ يَخْدَعُوكَ فَإِنَّ حَسْبَكَ اللَّهُ ۚ
And if they intend to deceive you, then verily, Allah (الله) is All-Sufficient (Al-Waasi’ (ٱلْوَاسِعُ)) for you.
هُوَ الَّذِي أَيَّدَكَ بِنَصْرِهِ وَبِالْمُؤْمِنِينَ
He it is Who has supported you with His Help and with the believers.
8:63
وَأَلَّفَ بَيْنَ قُلُوبِهِمْ ۚ
And He has united their (i.e. believers) hearts.
لَوْ أَنْفَقْتَ مَا فِي الْأَرْضِ جَمِيعًا مَا أَلَّفْتَ بَيْنَ قُلُوبِهِمْ وَلَكِنَّ اللَّهَ أَلَّفَ بَيْنَهُمْ ۚ
If you had spent all that is in the earth, you could not have united their hearts, but Allah (الله) has united them.
إِنَّهُ عَزِيزٌ حَكِيمٌ
Certainly He is All-Mighty, All-Wise (Al-Hakeem (ٱلْحَكِيمُ)).
8:64
يَا أَيُّهَا النَّبِيُّ حَسْبُكَ اللَّهُ وَمَنِ اتَّبَعَكَ مِنَ الْمُؤْمِنِينَ
O Prophet (Muhammad (محمد) SAW)! Allah (الله) is Sufficient for you and for the believers who follow you.
8:[65-69] Allah‘s promise to make the believers victorious over armies 2 to 10 times larger than the believers
8:65
يَا أَيُّهَا النَّبِيُّ حَرِّضِ الْمُؤْمِنِينَ عَلَى الْقِتَالِ ۚ
O Prophet (Muhammad (محمد) SAW)! Urge the believers to fight.
إِنْ يَكُنْ مِنْكُمْ عِشْرُونَ صَابِرُونَ يَغْلِبُوا مِائَتَيْنِ ۚ
If there are twenty steadfast persons amongst you, they will overcome two hundred,
وَإِنْ يَكُنْ مِنْكُمْ مِائَةٌ يَغْلِبُوا أَلْفًا مِنَ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا بِأَنَّهُمْ قَوْمٌ لَا يَفْقَهُونَ
and if there be a hundred steadfast persons they will overcome a thousand of those who disbelieve, because they (the disbelievers) are people who do not understand.
8:66
الْآنَ خَفَّفَ اللَّهُ عَنْكُمْ وَعَلِمَ أَنَّ فِيكُمْ ضَعْفًا ۚ
Now Allah (الله) has lightened your (task), for He knows that there is weakness in you.
فَإِنْ يَكُنْ مِنْكُمْ مِائَةٌ صَابِرَةٌ يَغْلِبُوا مِائَتَيْنِ ۚ
So if there are of you a hundred steadfast persons, they shall overcome two hundred,
وَإِنْ يَكُنْ مِنْكُمْ أَلْفٌ يَغْلِبُوا أَلْفَيْنِ بِإِذْنِ اللَّهِ ۗ
and if there are a thousand of you, they shall overcome two thousand with the Leave of Allah (الله).
وَاللَّهُ مَعَ الصَّابِرِينَ
And Allah (الله) is with As-Sabirin (the patient ones, etc).
8:67
مَا كَانَ لِنَبِيٍّ أَنْ يَكُونَ لَهُ أَسْرَى حَتَّى يُثْخِنَ فِي الْأَرْضِ ۚ
It is not for a Prophet that he should have prisoners of war (and free them with ransom) until he had made a great slaughter (among his enemies) in the land.
تُرِيدُونَ عَرَضَ الدُّنْيَا وَاللَّهُ يُرِيدُ الْآخِرَةَ ۗ
You desire the good of this world (i.e. the money of ransom for freeing the captives), but Allah (الله) desires (for you) the Hereafter.
وَاللَّهُ عَزِيزٌ حَكِيمٌ
And Allah (الله) is All-Mighty, All-Wise (Al-Hakeem (ٱلْحَكِيمُ)).
8:68
لَوْلَا كِتَابٌ مِنَ اللَّهِ سَبَقَ لَمَسَّكُمْ فِيمَا أَخَذْتُمْ عَذَابٌ عَظِيمٌ
Were it not a previous ordainment from Allah (الله), a severe torment would have touched you for what you took.
8:69
فَكُلُوا مِمَّا غَنِمْتُمْ حَلَالًا طَيِّبًا ۚ
So enjoy what you have gotten of booty in war, lawful and good,
وَاتَّقُوا اللَّهَ ۚ
and be afraid of Allah (الله).
إِنَّ اللَّهَ غَفُورٌ رَحِيمٌ
Certainly, Allah (الله) is Oft-Forgiving, Most Merciful (Ar-Raheem (ٱلْرَّحِيْمُ)).
8: Treatment of prisoners of war who embrace Islam
8: [70-71] Muslims living in non-Muslim countries
[ 72-75]
8:70
يَا أَيُّهَا النَّبِيُّ قُلْ لِمَنْ فِي أَيْدِيكُمْ مِنَ الْأَسْرَى
O Prophet! Say to the captives that are in your hands:
إِنْ يَعْلَمِ اللَّهُ فِي قُلُوبِكُمْ خَيْرًا يُؤْتِكُمْ خَيْرًا مِمَّا أُخِذَ مِنْكُمْ وَيَغْفِرْ لَكُمْ ۗ
If Allah (الله) knows any good in your hearts, He will give you something better than what has been taken from you, and He will forgive you,
وَاللَّهُ غَفُورٌ رَحِيمٌ
and Allah (الله) is Oft-Forgiving, Most Merciful (Ar-Raheem (ٱلْرَّحِيْمُ)).‘
8:71
وَإِنْ يُرِيدُوا خِيَانَتَكَ فَقَدْ خَانُوا اللَّهَ مِنْ قَبْلُ فَأَمْكَنَ مِنْهُمْ ۗ
But if they intend to betray you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW), they have already betrayed Allah (الله) before. So He gave (you) power over them.
وَاللَّهُ عَلِيمٌ حَكِيمٌ
And Allah (الله) is All-Knower (Al-‘Alee (ٱلْعَلِيُّ)m (ٱلْعَلِيمُ)), All-Wise (Al-Hakeem (ٱلْحَكِيمُ)).
8:72
إِنَّ الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا وَهَاجَرُوا وَجَاهَدُوا بِأَمْوَالِهِمْ وَأَنْفُسِهِمْ فِي سَبِيلِ اللَّهِ
Verily, those who believed, and emigrated and strove hard and fought with their property and their lives in the Cause of Allah (الله)
وَالَّذِينَ آوَوْا وَنَصَرُوا أُولَئِكَ بَعْضُهُمْ أَوْلِيَاءُ بَعْضٍ ۚ
as well as those who gave (them) asylum and help, - these are (all) allies to one another.
وَالَّذِينَ آمَنُوا وَلَمْ يُهَاجِرُوا مَا لَكُمْ مِنْ وَلَايَتِهِمْ مِنْ شَيْءٍ حَتَّى يُهَاجِرُوا ۚ
And as to those who believed but did not emigrate (to you O Muhammad (محمد) SAW), you owe no duty of protection to them until they emigrate,
وَإِنِ اسْتَنْصَرُوكُمْ فِي الدِّينِ فَعَلَيْكُمُ النَّصْرُ إِلَّا عَلَى قَوْمٍ بَيْنَكُمْ وَبَيْنَهُمْ مِيثَاقٌ ۗ
but if they seek your help in Religion, it is your duty to help them except against a people with whom you have a treaty of mutual alliance,
وَاللَّهُ بِمَا تَعْمَلُونَ بَصِيرٌ
and Allah (الله) is the All-Seer (Al-Baseer (ٱلْبَصِيرُ)) of what you do.
8:73
وَالَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا بَعْضُهُمْ أَوْلِيَاءُ بَعْضٍ ۚ
And those who disbelieve are allies to one another,
إِلَّا تَفْعَلُوهُ تَكُنْ فِتْنَةٌ فِي الْأَرْضِ وَفَسَادٌ كَبِيرٌ
(and) if you (Muslims of the whole world collectively) do not do so (i.e. become allies, as one united block with one Khalifah - chief Muslim ruler for the whole Muslim world to make victorious Allah (الله)‘s Religion of Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism), there will be Fitnah (wars, battles, polytheism, etc.) and oppression on earth, and a great mischief and corruption (appearance of polytheism).
8:74
وَالَّذِينَ آمَنُوا وَهَاجَرُوا وَجَاهَدُوا فِي سَبِيلِ اللَّهِ
And those who believed, and emigrated and strove hard in the Cause of Allah (الله) (Al-Jihad),
وَالَّذِينَ آوَوْا وَنَصَرُوا أُولَئِكَ هُمُ الْمُؤْمِنُونَ حَقًّا ۚ
as well as those who gave (them) asylum and aid; - these are the believers in truth,
لَهُمْ مَغْفِرَةٌ وَرِزْقٌ كَرِيمٌ
for them is forgiveness and Rizqun Kareem (a generous provision i.e. Paradise (الجنة)).
8:75
وَالَّذِينَ آمَنُوا مِنْ بَعْدُ وَهَاجَرُوا وَجَاهَدُوا مَعَكُمْ فَأُولَئِكَ مِنْكُمْ ۚ
And those who believed afterwards, and emigrated and strove hard along with you, (in the Cause of Allah (الله)) they are of you.
وَأُولُو الْأَرْحَامِ بَعْضُهُمْ أَوْلَى بِبَعْضٍ فِي كِتَابِ اللَّهِ ۗ
But kindred by blood are nearer to one another regarding inheritance in the decree ordained by Allah (الله).
إِنَّ اللَّهَ بِكُلِّ شَيْءٍ عَلِيمٌ
Verily, Allah (الله) is the All-Knower (Al-‘Alee (ٱلْعَلِيُّ)m (ٱلْعَلِيمُ)) of everything*********
