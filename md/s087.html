Al-A'laa
The Most High, The All-Highest, Glory To Your Lord In The Highest
87:[1-19] Glorify Allah; Allah has taken the responsibility of the Prophet‘s memory about The Qur‘an. It is a reminder and those who heed its reminders will be successful in the hereafter
87:1
سَبِّحِ ٱسۡمَ رَبِّكَ ٱلۡأَعۡلَى
Glorify the Name of your Lord, the Most High,
87:2
ٱلَّذِى خَلَقَ فَسَوَّىٰ
Who has created (everything), and then proportioned it;
87:3
وَٱلَّذِى قَدَّرَ فَهَدَىٰ
And Who has measured (preordainments for each and everything even to be blessed or wretched); then guided (i.e. showed mankind the right as well as wrong paths, and guided the animals to pasture);
87:4
وَٱلَّذِىٓ أَخۡرَجَ ٱلۡمَرۡعَىٰ
And Who brings out the pasturage,
87:5
فَجَعَلَهُ ۥ غُثَآءً أَحۡوَىٰ
And then makes it dark stubble.
87:6
سَنُقۡرِئُكَ فَلَا تَنسَىٰٓ
We shall make you to recite (the Qur‘ân), so you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) shall not forget (it),
87:7
إِلَّا مَا شَآءَ ٱللَّهُ‌ۚ
Except what Allâh, may will,
إِنَّهُ ۥ يَعۡلَمُ ٱلۡجَهۡرَ وَمَا يَخۡفَىٰ
He knows what is apparent and what is hidden.
87:8
وَنُيَسِّرُكَ لِلۡيُسۡرَىٰ
And We shall make easy for you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) the easy way (i.e. the doing of righteous deeds).
87:9
فَذَكِّرۡ إِن نَّفَعَتِ ٱلذِّكۡرَىٰ
Therefore remind (men) in case the reminder profits (them).
87:10
سَيَذَّكَّرُ مَن يَخۡشَىٰ
The reminder will be received by him who fears (Allâh),
87:11
وَيَتَجَنَّبُہَا ٱلۡأَشۡقَى
But it will be avoided by the wretched,
87:12
ٱلَّذِى يَصۡلَى ٱلنَّارَ ٱلۡكُبۡرَىٰ
Who will enter the great Fire and made to taste its burning,
87:13
ثُمَّ لَا يَمُوتُ فِيہَا وَلَا يَحۡيَىٰ
Wherein he will neither die (to be in rest) nor live (a good living).
87:14
قَدۡ أَفۡلَحَ مَن تَزَكَّىٰ
Indeed whosoever purifies himself (by avoiding polytheism and accepting Islâmic Monotheism) shall achieve success,
87:15
وَذَكَرَ ٱسۡمَ رَبِّهِۦ فَصَلَّىٰ
And remembers (glorifies) the Name of his Lord (worships none but Allâh), and prays (five compulsory prayers and Nawâfil additional prayers).
87:16
بَلۡ تُؤۡثِرُونَ ٱلۡحَيَوٰةَ ٱلدُّنۡيَا
Nay, you prefer the life of this world;
87:17
وَٱلۡأَخِرَةُ خَيۡرٌ۬ وَأَبۡقَىٰٓ
Although the Hereafter is better and more lasting.
87:18
إِنَّ هَـٰذَا لَفِى ٱلصُّحُفِ ٱلۡأُولَىٰ
Verily! This is in the former Scriptures,
87:19
صُحُفِ إِبۡرَٲهِيمَ وَمُوسَىٰ
The Scriptures of Ibrâhim (Abraham) and Mûsa (Moses).*********
