At-Takwir
The Folding Up, The Overthrowing, Shrouding in Darkness
81:[1-14] A dreadful scene of the Doomsday
81:[15-29] The Qur‘an is conveyed to the Prophet through angel Gabriel. This message is for all the people of the world
81:1
إِذَا ٱلشَّمۡسُ كُوِّرَتۡ
When the sun Kuwwirat (wound round and lost its light and is overthrown ).
81:2
وَإِذَا ٱلنُّجُومُ ٱنكَدَرَتۡ
And when the stars shall fall;
81:3
وَإِذَا ٱلۡجِبَالُ سُيِّرَتۡ
And when the mountains shall made to pass away;
81:4
وَإِذَا ٱلۡعِشَارُ عُطِّلَتۡ
And when the pregnant she-camels shall be neglected;
81:5
وَإِذَا ٱلۡوُحُوشُ حُشِرَتۡ
And when the wild beasts shall be gathered together;
81:6
وَإِذَا ٱلۡبِحَارُ سُجِّرَتۡ
And when the seas shall become as blazing Fire or shall overflow;
81:7
وَإِذَا ٱلنُّفُوسُ زُوِّجَتۡ
And when the souls shall be joined with their bodies;
81:8
وَإِذَا ٱلۡمَوۡءُ ۥدَةُ سُٮِٕلَتۡ
And when the female (infant) buried alive (as the pagan Arabs used to do) shall be questioned.
81:9
بِأَىِّ ذَنۢبٍ۬ قُتِلَتۡ
For what sin she was killed?
81:10
وَإِذَا ٱلصُّحُفُ نُشِرَتۡ
And when the written pages of deeds (good and bad) of every person shall be laid open;
81:11
وَإِذَا ٱلسَّمَآءُ كُشِطَتۡ
And when the heaven shall be stripped off and taken away from its place;
81:12
وَإِذَا ٱلۡجَحِيمُ سُعِّرَتۡ
And when Hell-fire shall be kindled to fierce ablaze.
81:13
وَإِذَا ٱلۡجَنَّةُ أُزۡلِفَتۡ
And when Paradise shall be brought near,
81:14
عَلِمَتۡ نَفۡسٌ۬ مَّآ أَحۡضَرَتۡ
(Then) every person will know what he has brought (of good and evil).
81:15
فَلَآ أُقۡسِمُ بِٱلۡخُنَّسِ
So verily, I swear by the planets that recede (i.e. disappear during the day and appear during the night).
81:16
ٱلۡجَوَارِ ٱلۡكُنَّسِ
And by the planets that move swiftly and hide themselves,
81:17
وَٱلَّيۡلِ إِذَا عَسۡعَسَ
And by the night as it departs;
81:18
وَٱلصُّبۡحِ إِذَا تَنَفَّسَ
And by the dawn as it brightens;
81:19
إِنَّهُ ۥ لَقَوۡلُ رَسُولٍ۬ كَرِيمٍ۬
Verily, this is the Word (this Quran brought by) a most honourable messenger (Jibrael (Jibreel), from Allah to the Prophet Muhammad SAW).
81:20
ذِى قُوَّةٍ عِندَ ذِى ٱلۡعَرۡشِ مَكِينٍ۬
Owner of power, and high rank with (Allah) the Lord of the Throne,
81:21
مُّطَاعٍ۬ ثَمَّ أَمِينٍ۬
Obeyed (by the angels), trustworthy there (in the heavens).
81:22
وَمَا صَاحِبُكُم بِمَجۡنُونٍ۬
And (O people) your companion (Muhammad SAW) is not a madman;
81:23
وَلَقَدۡ رَءَاهُ بِٱلۡأُفُقِ ٱلۡمُبِينِ
And indeed he (Muhammad SAW) saw him (Jibrael (Jibreel)) in the clear horizon (towards the east).
81:24
وَمَا هُوَ عَلَى ٱلۡغَيۡبِ بِضَنِينٍ۬
And he (Muhammad SAW) withholds not a knowledge of the unseen.
81:25
وَمَا هُوَ بِقَوۡلِ شَيۡطَـٰنٍ۬ رَّجِيمٍ۬
And it (the Quran) is not the word of the outcast Shaitan (Satan).
81:26
فَأَيۡنَ تَذۡهَبُونَ
Then where are you going?
81:27
إِنۡ هُوَ إِلَّا ذِكۡرٌ۬ لِّلۡعَـٰلَمِينَ
Verily, this (the Quran) is no less than a Reminder to (all) the Alameen (mankind and jinns).
81:28
لِمَن شَآءَ مِنكُمۡ أَن يَسۡتَقِيمَ
To whomsoever among you who wills to walk straight,
81:29
وَمَا تَشَآءُونَ إِلَّآ أَن يَشَآءَ ٱللَّهُ رَبُّ ٱلۡعَـٰلَمِينَ
And you will not, unless (it be) that Allah wills, the Lord of the Alameen (mankind, jinns and all that exists).*********
