Al-Ma'aarij
The Ways of Ascent, The Ascending Stairways
70:[1-35] The Day of Judgment will be equal to fifty thousand years. Disbelievers will wish to save themselves from the punishment at the expense of their children, wives, brothers and relatives, but it will not happen
70:1
سَأَلَ سَآٮِٕلُۢ بِعَذَابٍ۬ وَاقِعٍ۬
A questioner asked concerning a torment about to befall
70:2
لِّلۡڪَـٰفِرِينَ لَيۡسَ لَهُ ۥ دَافِعٌ۬
Upon the disbelievers, which none can avert,
70:3
مِّنَ ٱللَّهِ ذِى ٱلۡمَعَارِجِ
From Allah (الله), the Lord of the ways of ascent.
70:4
تَعۡرُجُ ٱلۡمَلَـٰٓٮِٕڪَةُ وَٱلرُّوحُ إِلَيۡهِ فِى يَوۡمٍ۬ كَانَ مِقۡدَارُهُ ۥ خَمۡسِينَ أَلۡفَ سَنَةٍ۬
The angels and the Rooh (Jibrael (Jibreel (جبريل))) ascend to Him in a Day the measure whereof is fifty thousand years,
70:5
فَٱصۡبِرۡ صَبۡرً۬ا جَمِيلاً
So be patient (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW ), with a good patience.
70:6
إِنَّہُمۡ يَرَوۡنَهُ ۥ بَعِيدً۬ا
Verily! They see it (the torment) afar off,
70:7
وَنَرَٮٰهُ قَرِيبً۬ا
But We see it (quite) near.
70:8
يَوۡمَ تَكُونُ ٱلسَّمَآءُ كَٱلۡمُهۡلِ
The Day that the sky will be like the boiling filth of oil, (or molten copper or silver or lead, etc.).
70:9
وَتَكُونُ ٱلۡجِبَالُ كَٱلۡعِهۡنِ
And the mountains will be like flakes of wool,
70:10
وَلَا يَسۡـَٔلُ حَمِيمٌ حَمِيمً۬ا
And no friend will ask of a friend,
70:11
يُبَصَّرُونَہُمۡ‌ۚ
Though they shall be made to see one another, -((i.e. on the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة)), there will be none but see his father, children and relatives, but he will neither speak to them nor will ask them for any help))
يَوَدُّ ٱلۡمُجۡرِمُ لَوۡ يَفۡتَدِى مِنۡ عَذَابِ يَوۡمِٮِٕذِۭ بِبَنِيهِ
the Mujrim, (criminal, sinner, disbeliever, etc.) would desire to ransom himself from the punishment of that Day by his children.
70:12
وَصَـٰحِبَتِهِۦ وَأَخِيهِ
And his wife and his brother,
70:13
وَفَصِيلَتِهِ ٱلَّتِى تُـٔۡوِيهِ
And his kindred who sheltered him,
70:14
وَمَن فِى ٱلۡأَرۡضِ جَمِيعً۬ا ثُمَّ يُنجِيهِ
And all that are in the earth, so that it might save him .
70:15
كَلَّآ‌ۖ إِنَّہَا لَظَىٰ
By no means! Verily, it will be the Fire of Hell (جهنم)!
70:16
نَزَّاعَةً۬ لِّلشَّوَىٰ
Taking away (burning completely) the head skin!
70:17
تَدۡعُواْ مَنۡ أَدۡبَرَ وَتَوَلَّىٰ
Calling: ‘(O Kafir (O disbeliever in Allah (الله), His angels, His Book, His Messengers, Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة) and in Al-Qadar (Divine Preordainments), O Mushrik (O polytheist, disbeliever in the Oneness of Allah (الله))) (all) such as turn their backs and turn away their faces (from Faith) (picking and swallowing them up from that great gathering of mankind (on the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة)) just as a bird picks up a food-grain from the earth with its beak and swallows it up)(Tafsir Al-Qurtubee, Vol. 18, Page 289)
70:18
وَجَمَعَ فَأَوۡعَىٰٓ
And collect (wealth) and hide it (from spending it in the Cause of Allah (الله)).
70:19
إِنَّ ٱلۡإِنسَـٰنَ خُلِقَ هَلُوعًا
Verily, man (disbeliever) was created very impatient;
70:20
إِذَا مَسَّهُ ٱلشَّرُّ جَزُوعً۬ا
Irritable (discontented) when evil touches him;
70:21
وَإِذَا مَسَّهُ ٱلۡخَيۡرُ مَنُوعًا
And niggardly when good touches him;-
70:22
إِلَّا ٱلۡمُصَلِّينَ
Except those devoted to Salat (prayers)
70:23
ٱلَّذِينَ هُمۡ عَلَىٰ صَلَاتِہِمۡ دَآٮِٕمُونَ
Those who remain constant in their Salat (prayers);
70:24
وَٱلَّذِينَ فِىٓ أَمۡوَٲلِهِمۡ حَقٌّ۬ مَّعۡلُومٌ۬
And those in whose wealth there is a known right,
70:25
لِّلسَّآٮِٕلِ وَٱلۡمَحۡرُومِ
For the beggar who asks, and for the unlucky who has lost his property and wealth, (and his means of living has been straitened);
70:26
وَٱلَّذِينَ يُصَدِّقُونَ بِيَوۡمِ ٱلدِّينِ
And those who believe in the Day of Recompense,
70:27
وَٱلَّذِينَ هُم مِّنۡ عَذَابِ رَبِّہِم مُّشۡفِقُونَ
And those who fear the torment of their Lord,
70:28
إِنَّ عَذَابَ رَبِّہِمۡ غَيۡرُ مَأۡمُونٍ۬
Verily! The torment of their Lord is that before which none can feel secure,
70:29
وَٱلَّذِينَ هُمۡ لِفُرُوجِهِمۡ حَـٰفِظُونَ
And those who guard their chastity (i.e. private parts from illegal sexual acts) .
70:30
إِلَّا عَلَىٰٓ أَزۡوَٲجِهِمۡ أَوۡ مَا مَلَكَتۡ أَيۡمَـٰنُہُمۡ فَإِنَّہُمۡ غَيۡرُ مَلُومِينَ
Except with their wives and the (women slaves and captives) whom their right hands possess, for (then) they are not to be blamed,
70:31
فَمَنِ ٱبۡتَغَىٰ وَرَآءَ ذَٲلِكَ فَأُوْلَـٰٓٮِٕكَ هُمُ ٱلۡعَادُونَ
But whosoever seeks beyond that, then it is those who are trespassers.
70:32
وَٱلَّذِينَ هُمۡ لِأَمَـٰنَـٰتِہِمۡ وَعَهۡدِهِمۡ رَٲعُونَ
And those who keep their trusts and covenants;
70:33
وَٱلَّذِينَ هُم بِشَہَـٰدَٲتِہِمۡ قَآٮِٕمُونَ
And those who stand firm in their testimonies;
70:34
وَٱلَّذِينَ هُمۡ عَلَىٰ صَلَاتِہِمۡ يُحَافِظُونَ
And those who guard their Salat (prayers) well ,
70:35
أُوْلَـٰٓٮِٕكَ فِى جَنَّـٰتٍ۬ مُّكۡرَمُونَ
Such shall dwell in the Gardens (i.e. Paradise (الجنة)) honoured.
70:[36-44] Paradise is not for the disbelievers. Disbelievers will have downcast eyes and countenances distorted with shame
70:36
فَمَالِ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُواْ قِبَلَكَ مُهۡطِعِينَ
So what is the matter with those who disbelieve that they hasten to listen from you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW), in order to belie you and to mock at you, and at Allah (الله)‘s Book (this Quran (القرآن)).
70:37
عَنِ ٱلۡيَمِينِ وَعَنِ ٱلشِّمَالِ عِزِينَ
(Sitting) in groups on the right and on the left (of you, O Muhammad (محمد) SAW)?
70:38
أَيَطۡمَعُ ڪُلُّ ٱمۡرِىٍٕ۬ مِّنۡہُمۡ أَن يُدۡخَلَ جَنَّةَ نَعِيمٍ۬
Does every man of them hope to enter the Paradise (الجنة) of delight?
70:39
كَلَّآ‌ۖ إِنَّا خَلَقۡنَـٰهُم مِّمَّا يَعۡلَمُونَ
No, that is not like that! Verily, We have created them out of that which they know!
70:40
فَلَآ أُقۡسِمُ بِرَبِّ ٱلۡمَشَـٰرِقِ وَٱلۡمَغَـٰرِبِ إِنَّا لَقَـٰدِرُونَ
So I swear by the Lord of all (the three hundred and sixty (360)) points of sunrise and sunset in the east and the west that surely We are Able
70:41
عَلَىٰٓ أَن نُّبَدِّلَ خَيۡرً۬ا مِّنۡهُمۡ وَمَا نَحۡنُ بِمَسۡبُوقِينَ
To replace them by (others) better than them; and We are not to be outrun.
70:42
فَذَرۡهُمۡ يَخُوضُواْ وَيَلۡعَبُواْ حَتَّىٰ يُلَـٰقُواْ يَوۡمَهُمُ ٱلَّذِى يُوعَدُونَ
So leave them to plunge in vain talk and play about, until they meet their Day which they are promised.
70:43
يَوۡمَ يَخۡرُجُونَ مِنَ ٱلۡأَجۡدَاثِ سِرَاعً۬ا كَأَنَّہُمۡ إِلَىٰ نُصُبٍ۬ يُوفِضُونَ
The Day when they will come out of the graves quickly as racing to a goal,
70:44
خَـٰشِعَةً أَبۡصَـٰرُهُمۡ تَرۡهَقُهُمۡ ذِلَّةٌ۬‌ۚ
With their eyes lowered in fear and humility, ignominy covering them (all over)!
ذَٲلِكَ ٱلۡيَوۡمُ ٱلَّذِى كَانُواْ يُوعَدُونَ
That is the Day which they were promised!*********
