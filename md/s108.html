Al-Kawthar
Abundance, Plenty, Good in Abundance
108:[1-3] Allah has made Muhammad‘s name everlasting
108:1
إِنَّآ أَعۡطَيۡنَـٰكَ ٱلۡكَوۡثَرَ
Verily, We have granted you (O Muhammad SAW) Al-Kauthar (a river in Paradise);
108:2
فَصَلِّ لِرَبِّكَ وَٱنۡحَرۡ
Therefore turn in prayer to your Lord and sacrifice (to Him only).
108:3
إِنَّ شَانِئَكَ هُوَ ٱلۡأَبۡتَرُ
For he who makes you angry (O Muhammad SAW), - he will be cut off (from every good thing in this world and in the Hereafter).*********
