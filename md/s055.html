Ar-Rahmaan
The Most Merciful, The Most Gracious, The Beneficent
55:[1-16] It is Allah Who created man, taught the Qur‘an and taught him how to convey his feelings and thoughts
55:[17-25] Allah is the Rabb of the easts and wests and He puts the laws to regulate oceans, its products and ships
55:1
ٱلرَّحۡمَـٰنُ
The Most Beneficent (Ar-Rahmaan (ٱلْرَّحْمَـانُ))!
55:2
عَلَّمَ ٱلۡقُرۡءَانَ
Has taught (you mankind) the Quran (القرآن) (by His Mercy).
55:3
خَلَقَ ٱلۡإِنسَـٰنَ
He created man.
55:4
عَلَّمَهُ ٱلۡبَيَانَ
He taught him eloquent speech.
55:5
ٱلشَّمۡسُ وَٱلۡقَمَرُ بِحُسۡبَانٍ۬
The sun and the moon run on their fixed courses (exactly) calculated with measured out stages for each (for reckoning, etc.).
55:6
وَٱلنَّجۡمُ وَٱلشَّجَرُ يَسۡجُدَانِ
And the herbs (or stars) and the trees both prostrate.
55:7
وَٱلسَّمَآءَ رَفَعَهَا وَوَضَعَ ٱلۡمِيزَانَ
And the heaven He has raised high, and He has set up the Balance.
55:8
أَلَّا تَطۡغَوۡاْ فِى ٱلۡمِيزَانِ
In order that you may not transgress (due) balance.
55:9
وَأَقِيمُواْ ٱلۡوَزۡنَ بِٱلۡقِسۡطِ وَلَا تُخۡسِرُواْ ٱلۡمِيزَانَ
And observe the weight with equity and do not make the balance deficient.
55:10
وَٱلۡأَرۡضَ وَضَعَهَا لِلۡأَنَامِ
And the earth He has put for the creatures.
55:11
فِيہَا فَـٰكِهَةٌ۬ وَٱلنَّخۡلُ ذَاتُ ٱلۡأَكۡمَامِ
Therein are fruits, date-palms producing sheathed fruit-stalks (enclosing dates).
55:12
وَٱلۡحَبُّ ذُو ٱلۡعَصۡفِ وَٱلرَّيۡحَانُ
And also corn, with (its) leaves and stalk for fodder, and sweet-scented plants.
55:13
فَبِأَىِّ ءَالَآءِ رَبِّكُمَا تُكَذِّبَانِ
Then which of the Blessings of your Lord will you both (jinns and men) deny?
55:14
خَلَقَ ٱلۡإِنسَـٰنَ مِن صَلۡصَـٰلٍ۬ كَٱلۡفَخَّارِ
He created man (Adam (آدم)) from sounding clay like the clay of pottery.
55:15
وَخَلَقَ ٱلۡجَآنَّ مِن مَّارِجٍ۬ مِّن نَّارٍ۬
And the jinns did He create from a smokeless flame of fire.
55:16
فَبِأَىِّ ءَالَآءِ رَبِّكُمَا تُكَذِّبَانِ
Then which of the Blessings of your Lord will you both (jinns and men) deny?
55:17
رَبُّ ٱلۡمَشۡرِقَيۡنِ وَرَبُّ ٱلۡمَغۡرِبَيۡنِ
(He is) the Lord of the two easts (places of sunrise during early summer and early winter) and the Lord of the two wests (places of sunset during early summer and early winter).
55:18
فَبِأَىِّ ءَالَآءِ رَبِّكُمَا تُكَذِّبَانِ
Then which of the Blessings of your Lord will you both (jinns and men) deny?
55:19
مَرَجَ ٱلۡبَحۡرَيۡنِ يَلۡتَقِيَانِ
He has let loosed the two seas (the salt water and the sweet) meeting together.
55:20
بَيۡنَہُمَا بَرۡزَخٌ۬ لَّا يَبۡغِيَانِ
Between them is a barrier which none of them can transgress.
55:21
فَبِأَىِّ ءَالَآءِ رَبِّكُمَا تُكَذِّبَانِ
Then which of the Blessings of your Lord will you both (jinns and men) deny?
55:22
يَخۡرُجُ مِنۡہُمَا ٱللُّؤۡلُؤُ وَٱلۡمَرۡجَانُ
Out of them both come out pearl and coral.
55:23
فَبِأَىِّ ءَالَآءِ رَبِّكُمَا تُكَذِّبَانِ
Then which of the Blessings of your Lord will you both (jinns and men) deny?
55:24
وَلَهُ ٱلۡجَوَارِ ٱلۡمُنشَـَٔاتُ فِى ٱلۡبَحۡرِ كَٱلۡأَعۡلَـٰمِ
And His are the ships going and coming in the seas, like mountains.
55:25
فَبِأَىِّ ءَالَآءِ رَبِّكُمَا تُكَذِّبَانِ
Then which of the Blessings of your Lord will you both (jinns and men) deny?
55:[26-32] All that exists will perish except Allah, Who is busy in mighty tasks all the time
55:[33-45] No one can run away from the jurisdiction of Allah. Sinners will be punished in hell
55:26
كُلُّ مَنۡ عَلَيۡہَا فَانٍ۬
Whatsoever is on it (the earth) will perish.
55:27
وَيَبۡقَىٰ وَجۡهُ رَبِّكَ ذُو ٱلۡجَلَـٰلِ وَٱلۡإِكۡرَامِ
And the Face of your Lord full of Majesty and Honour will abide forever.
55:28
فَبِأَىِّ ءَالَآءِ رَبِّكُمَا تُكَذِّبَانِ
Then which of the Blessings of your Lord will you both (jinns and men) deny?
55:29
يَسۡـَٔلُهُ ۥ مَن فِى ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٲتِ وَٱلۡأَرۡضِ‌ۚ
Whosoever is in the heavens and on earth begs of Him (its needs from Him).
كُلَّ يَوۡمٍ هُوَ فِى شَأۡنٍ۬
Every day He has a matter to bring forth (such as giving honour to some, disgrace to some, life to some, death to some, etc.)!
55:30
فَبِأَىِّ ءَالَآءِ رَبِّكُمَا تُكَذِّبَانِ
Then which of the Blessings of your Lord will you both (jinns and men) deny?
55:31
سَنَفۡرُغُ لَكُمۡ أَيُّهَ ٱلثَّقَلَانِ
We shall attend to you, O you two classes (jinns and men)!
55:32
فَبِأَىِّ ءَالَآءِ رَبِّكُمَا تُكَذِّبَانِ
Then which of the Blessings of your Lord will you both (jinns and men) deny?
55:33
يَـٰمَعۡشَرَ ٱلۡجِنِّ وَٱلۡإِنسِ
O assembly of jinns and men!
إِنِ ٱسۡتَطَعۡتُمۡ أَن تَنفُذُواْ مِنۡ أَقۡطَارِ ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٲتِ وَٱلۡأَرۡضِ فَٱنفُذُواْ‌ۚ
If you have power to pass beyond the zones of the heavens and the earth, then pass (them)!
لَا تَنفُذُونَ إِلَّا بِسُلۡطَـٰنٍ۬
But you will never be able to pass them, except with authority (from Allah (الله))!
55:34
فَبِأَىِّ ءَالَآءِ رَبِّكُمَا تُكَذِّبَانِ
Then which of the Blessings of your Lord will you both (jinns and men) deny?
55:35
يُرۡسَلُ عَلَيۡكُمَا شُوَاظٌ۬ مِّن نَّارٍ۬ وَنُحَاسٌ۬ فَلَا تَنتَصِرَانِ
There will be sent against you both, smokeless flames of fire and (molten) brass, and you will not be able to defend yourselves.
55:36
فَبِأَىِّ ءَالَآءِ رَبِّكُمَا تُكَذِّبَانِ
Then which of the Blessings of your Lord will you both (jinns and men) deny?
55:37
فَإِذَا ٱنشَقَّتِ ٱلسَّمَآءُ فَكَانَتۡ وَرۡدَةً۬ كَٱلدِّهَانِ
Then when the heaven is rent asunder, and it becomes rosy or red like red-oil, or red hide.
55:38
فَبِأَىِّ ءَالَآءِ رَبِّكُمَا تُكَذِّبَانِ
Then which of the Blessings of your Lord will you both (jinns and men) deny?
55:39
فَيَوۡمَٮِٕذٍ۬ لَّا يُسۡـَٔلُ عَن ذَنۢبِهِۦۤ إِنسٌ۬ وَلَا جَآنٌّ۬
So on that Day no question will be asked of man or jinn as to his sin, (because they have already been known from their faces either white or black).
55:40
فَبِأَىِّ ءَالَآءِ رَبِّڪُمَا تُكَذِّبَانِ
Then which of the Blessings of your Lord will you both (jinns and men) deny?
55:41
يُعۡرَفُ ٱلۡمُجۡرِمُونَ بِسِيمَـٰهُمۡ
The Mujrimoon (polytheists, criminals, sinners, etc.) will be known by their marks (black faces),
فَيُؤۡخَذُ بِٱلنَّوَٲصِى وَٱلۡأَقۡدَامِ
and they will be seized by their forelocks and their feet.
55:42
فَبِأَىِّ ءَالَآءِ رَبِّكُمَا تُكَذِّبَانِ
Then which of the Blessings of your Lord will you both (jinns and men) deny?
55:43
هَـٰذِهِۦ جَهَنَّمُ ٱلَّتِى يُكَذِّبُ بِہَا ٱلۡمُجۡرِمُونَ
This is Hell (جهنم) which the Mujrimoon (polytheists, criminals, sinners, etc.) denied.
55:44
يَطُوفُونَ بَيۡنَہَا وَبَيۡنَ حَمِيمٍ ءَانٍ۬
They will go between it (Hell (جهنم)) and the boiling hot water!

55:[46-78] Righteous will be rewarded in paradise with lush gardens, springs, fruits, bashful virgins and much more
55:46
وَلِمَنۡ خَافَ مَقَامَ رَبِّهِۦ جَنَّتَانِ
But for him who (the true believer of Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism who performs all the duties ordained by Allah (الله) and His Messenger Muhammad (محمد) SAW , and keeps away (abstain) from all kinds of sin and evil deeds prohibited in Islam (الإسلام) and) fears the standing before his Lord, there will be two Gardens (i.e. in Paradise (الجنة)).
