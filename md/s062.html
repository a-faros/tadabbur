Al-Jumu'a
Congregation, Friday
62:[1-4] Allah appointed Muhammad as a Rasool
62:[5-8] Allah rebutted the claim of Jews to be the favorites of God to the exclusion of others
62:1
يُسَبِّحُ لِلَّهِ مَا فِى ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٲتِ وَمَا فِى ٱلۡأَرۡضِ
Whatsoever is in the heavens and whatsoever is on the earth glorifies Allah (الله),
ٱلۡمَلِكِ ٱلۡقُدُّوسِ ٱلۡعَزِيزِ ٱلۡحَكِيمِ
the King (ملك) )of everything), the Holy, the All-Mighty, the All-Wise (Al-Hakeem (ٱلْحَكِيمُ)).
62:2
هُوَ ٱلَّذِى بَعَثَ فِى ٱلۡأُمِّيِّـۧنَ رَسُولاً۬ مِّنۡہُمۡ
He it is Who sent among the unlettered ones a Messenger )Muhammad (محمد) SAW) from among themselves,
يَتۡلُواْ عَلَيۡہِمۡ ءَايَـٰتِهِۦ وَيُزَكِّيہِمۡ وَيُعَلِّمُهُمُ ٱلۡكِتَـٰبَ وَٱلۡحِكۡمَةَ
reciting to them His Verses, purifying them )from the filth of disbelief and polytheism), and teaching them the Book )this Quran (القرآن), Islam (الإسلام)ic laws and Islam (الإسلام)ic jurisprudence) and Al-Hikmah )As-Sun (الشمس)nah: legal ways, orders, acts of worship, etc. of Prophet Muhammad (محمد) SAW).
وَإِن كَانُواْ مِن قَبۡلُ لَفِى ضَلَـٰلٍ۬ مُّبِينٍ۬
And verily, they had been before in manifest error;
62:3
وَءَاخَرِينَ مِنۡہُمۡ لَمَّا يَلۡحَقُواْ بِہِمۡ‌ۚ
And He has sent him )Prophet Muhammad (محمد) SAW) also to others among them )Muslims) who have not yet joined them )but they will come).
وَهُوَ ٱلۡعَزِيزُ ٱلۡحَكِيمُ
And He )Allah (الله)) is the All-Mighty, the All-Wise (Al-Hakeem (ٱلْحَكِيمُ)).
62:4
ذَٲلِكَ فَضۡلُ ٱللَّهِ يُؤۡتِيهِ مَن يَشَآءُ‌ۚ
That is the Grace of Allah (الله), which He bestows on whom He wills.
وَٱللَّهُ ذُو ٱلۡفَضۡلِ ٱلۡعَظِيمِ
And Allah (الله) is the Owner of Mighty Grace.
62:5
مَثَلُ ٱلَّذِينَ حُمِّلُواْ ٱلتَّوۡرَٮٰةَ ثُمَّ لَمۡ يَحۡمِلُوهَا كَمَثَلِ ٱلۡحِمَارِ يَحۡمِلُ أَسۡفَارَۢا‌ۚ
The likeness of those who were entrusted with the )obligation of the) Taurat )Torah (التوراة)) )i.e. to obey its commandments and to practise its legal laws), but who subsequently failed in those )obligations), is as the likeness of a donkey who carries huge burdens of books )but understands nothing from them).
بِئۡسَ مَثَلُ ٱلۡقَوۡمِ ٱلَّذِينَ كَذَّبُواْ بِـَٔايَـٰتِ ٱللَّهِ‌ۚ
How bad is the example )or the likeness) of people who deny the Ayat )proofs, evidences, verses, signs, revelations, etc.) of Allah (الله).
وَٱللَّهُ لَا يَہۡدِى ٱلۡقَوۡمَ ٱلظَّـٰلِمِينَ
And Allah (الله) guides not the people who are Zalimoon )polytheists, wrong-doers, disbelievers, etc.).
62:6
قُلۡ يَـٰٓأَيُّہَا ٱلَّذِينَ هَادُوٓاْ إِن زَعَمۡتُمۡ أَنَّكُمۡ أَوۡلِيَآءُ لِلَّهِ مِن دُونِ ٱلنَّاسِ
Say )O Muhammad (محمد) SAW): ‘O you Jews (هُودًا)! If you pretend that you are friends of Allah (الله), to the exclusion of )all) other mankind,
فَتَمَنَّوُاْ ٱلۡمَوۡتَ إِن كُنتُمۡ صَـٰدِقِينَ
then long for death if you are truthful.‘
62:7
وَلَا يَتَمَنَّوۡنَهُ ۥۤ أَبَدَۢا بِمَا قَدَّمَتۡ أَيۡدِيهِمۡ‌ۚ
But they will never long for it )death), because of what )deeds) their hands have sent before them!
وَٱللَّهُ عَلِيمُۢ بِٱلظَّـٰلِمِينَ
And Allah (الله) knows well the Zalimoon )polytheists, wrong-doers, disbelievers, etc.).
62:8
قُلۡ إِنَّ ٱلۡمَوۡتَ ٱلَّذِى تَفِرُّونَ مِنۡهُ فَإِنَّهُ ۥ مُلَـٰقِيڪُمۡ‌ۖ
Say )to them): ‘Verily, the death from which you flee will surely meet you, then you will be sent back to )Allah (الله)),
ثُمَّ تُرَدُّونَ إِلَىٰ عَـٰلِمِ ٱلۡغَيۡبِ وَٱلشَّهَـٰدَةِ فَيُنَبِّئُكُم بِمَا كُنتُمۡ تَعۡمَلُونَ
the All-Knower (Al-‘Alee (ٱلْعَلِيُّ)m (ٱلْعَلِيمُ)) of the unseen and the seen, and He will tell you what you used to do.‘
62:[9-11] Commandment relating to the obligation of Friday Prayers
62:9
يَـٰٓأَيُّہَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوٓاْ إِذَا نُودِىَ لِلصَّلَوٰةِ مِن يَوۡمِ ٱلۡجُمُعَةِ فَٱسۡعَوۡاْ إِلَىٰ ذِكۡرِ ٱللَّهِ وَذَرُواْ ٱلۡبَيۡعَ‌ۚ
O you who believe )Muslims)!
When the call is proclaimed for the Salat )prayer) on the day of Friday (الجمعة) )Jumuah prayer), come to the remembrance of Allah (الله) )Jumuah religious talk )Khutbah) and Salat )prayer)) and leave off business )and every other thing),
ذَٲلِكُمۡ خَيۡرٌ۬ لَّكُمۡ إِن كُنتُمۡ تَعۡلَمُونَ
that is better for you if you did but know!
62:10
فَإِذَا قُضِيَتِ ٱلصَّلَوٰةُ فَٱنتَشِرُواْ فِى ٱلۡأَرۡضِ وَٱبۡتَغُواْ مِن فَضۡلِ ٱللَّهِ
Then when the )Jumuah) Salat )prayer) is finished, you may disperse through the land, and seek the Bounty of Allah (الله) )by working, etc.),
وَٱذۡكُرُواْ ٱللَّهَ كَثِيرً۬ا لَّعَلَّكُمۡ تُفۡلِحُونَ
and remember Allah (الله) much, that you may be successful.
62:11
وَإِذَا رَأَوۡاْ تِجَـٰرَةً أَوۡ لَهۡوًا ٱنفَضُّوٓاْ إِلَيۡہَا وَتَرَكُوكَ قَآٮِٕمً۬ا‌ۚ
And when they see some merchandise or some amusement )beating of Tambur )drum) etc.) they disperse headlong to it, and leave you )Muhammad (محمد) SAW) standing )while delivering Jumuahs religious talk )Khutbah)).
قُلۡ مَا عِندَ ٱللَّهِ خَيۡرٌ۬ مِّنَ ٱللَّهۡوِ وَمِنَ ٱلتِّجَـٰرَةِ‌ۚ
Say ‘That which Allah (الله) has is better than any amusement or merchandise!
وَٱللَّهُ خَيۡرُ ٱلرَّٲزِقِينَ
And Allah (الله) is the Best of providers.‘*********
