Al-Lail
The Night
92:[1-21] For good people Allah will facilitate the easy way and for the wicked the hard way. What benefit will he get from wealth, if he himself is doomed?
92:1
وَٱلَّيۡلِ إِذَا يَغۡشَىٰ
By the night as it envelops;
92:2
وَٱلنَّہَارِ إِذَا تَجَلَّىٰ
And by the day as it appears in brightness;
92:3
وَمَا خَلَقَ ٱلذَّكَرَ وَٱلۡأُنثَىٰٓ
And by Him Who created male and female;
92:4
إِنَّ سَعۡيَكُمۡ لَشَتَّىٰ
Certainly, your efforts and deeds are diverse (different in aims and purposes);
92:5
فَأَمَّا مَنۡ أَعۡطَىٰ وَٱتَّقَىٰ
As for him who gives (in charity) and keeps his duty to Allâh and fears Him,
92:6
وَصَدَّقَ بِٱلۡحُسۡنَىٰ
And believes in Al-Husna.
92:7
فَسَنُيَسِّرُهُ ۥ لِلۡيُسۡرَىٰ
We will make smooth for him the path of ease (goodness).
92:8
وَأَمَّا مَنۢ بَخِلَ وَٱسۡتَغۡنَىٰ
But he who is greedy miser and thinks himself self-sufficient.
92:9
وَكَذَّبَ بِٱلۡحُسۡنَىٰ
And gives the lie to Al-Husna
92:10
فَسَنُيَسِّرُهُ ۥ لِلۡعُسۡرَىٰ
We will make smooth for him the path for evil;
92:11
وَمَا يُغۡنِى عَنۡهُ مَالُهُ ۥۤ إِذَا تَرَدَّىٰٓ
And what will his wealth benefit him when he goes down (in destruction).
92:12
إِنَّ عَلَيۡنَا لَلۡهُدَىٰ
Truly! Ours it is (to give) guidance,
92:13
وَإِنَّ لَنَا لَلۡأَخِرَةَ وَٱلۡأُولَىٰ
And truly, unto Us (belong) the last (Hereafter) and the first (this world).
92:14
فَأَنذَرۡتُكُمۡ نَارً۬ا تَلَظَّىٰ
Therefore I have warned you of a Fire blazing fiercely (Hell);
92:15
لَا يَصۡلَٮٰهَآ إِلَّا ٱلۡأَشۡقَى
None shall enter it save the most wretched,
92:16
ٱلَّذِى كَذَّبَ وَتَوَلَّىٰ
Who denies and turns away.
92:17
وَسَيُجَنَّبُہَا ٱلۡأَتۡقَى
And Al-Muttaqûn (the pious and righteous - see V.2:2) will be far removed from it (Hell).
92:18
ٱلَّذِى يُؤۡتِى مَالَهُ ۥ يَتَزَكَّىٰ
He who spends his wealth for increase in self-purification,
92:19
وَمَا لِأَحَدٍ عِندَهُ ۥ مِن نِّعۡمَةٍ۬ تُجۡزَىٰٓ
And have in his mind no favour from anyone for which a reward is expected in return,
92:20
إِلَّا ٱبۡتِغَآءَ وَجۡهِ رَبِّهِ ٱلۡأَعۡلَىٰ
Except only the desire to seek the Countenance of his Lord, the Most High;
92:21
وَلَسَوۡفَ يَرۡضَىٰ
He surely will be pleased (when he will enter Paradise).*********
