Fussilat
Expounded, Explained In Detail, Clearly Spelled Out
41:[1-8] The Qur‘an is a giver of good news and an admonition. Woe to those who deny the hereafter and do not pay Zakah
41:1
حمٓ
Ha Meem.(These letters are one of the miracles of the Quran (القرآن), and none but Allah (الله) (Alone) knows their meanings.)
41:2
تَنزِيلٌ۬ مِّنَ ٱلرَّحۡمَـٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ
A revelation from Allah (الله), the Most Beneficent (Ar-Rahmaan (ٱلْرَّحْمَـانُ)), the Most Merciful (Ar-Raheem (ٱلْرَّحِيْمُ)).
41:3
كِتَـٰبٌ۬ فُصِّلَتۡ ءَايَـٰتُهُ ۥ
A Book whereof the Verses are explained in detail;
قُرۡءَانًا عَرَبِيًّ۬ا لِّقَوۡمٍ۬ يَعۡلَمُونَ
A Quran (القرآن) in Arabic (العربية) for people who know.
41:4
بَشِيرً۬ا وَنَذِيرً۬ا
Giving glad tidings (of Paradise (الجنة) to the one who believes in the Oneness of Allah (الله) (i.e. Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism) and fears Allah (الله) much (abstains from all kinds of sins and evil deeds) and loves Allah (الله) much (performing all kinds of good deeds which He has ordained)), and warning (of punishment in the Hell (جهنم) Fire to the one who disbelieves in the Oneness of Allah (الله)),
فَأَعۡرَضَ أَڪۡثَرُهُمۡ فَهُمۡ لَا يَسۡمَعُونَ
but most of them turn away, so they listen not.
41:5
وَقَالُواْ قُلُوبُنَا فِىٓ أَڪِنَّةٍ۬ مِّمَّا تَدۡعُونَآ إِلَيۡهِ
And they say:
Our hearts are under coverings (screened) from that to which you invite us,
وَفِىٓ ءَاذَانِنَا وَقۡرٌ۬ وَمِنۢ بَيۡنِنَا وَبَيۡنِكَ حِجَابٌ۬
and in our ears is deafness, and between us and you is a screen, so work you (on your way);
فَٱعۡمَلۡ إِنَّنَا عَـٰمِلُونَ
verily, we are working (on our way).‘
41:6
قُلۡ إِنَّمَآ أَنَا۟ بَشَرٌ۬ مِّثۡلُكُمۡ يُوحَىٰٓ إِلَىَّ أَنَّمَآ إِلَـٰهُكُمۡ إِلَـٰهٌ۬ وَٲحِدٌ۬
Say (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW): ‘I am only a human being like you. It is inspired in me that your Ilah (God) is One Ilah (God - Allah (الله)),
فَٱسۡتَقِيمُوٓاْ إِلَيۡهِ وَٱسۡتَغۡفِرُوهُ‌ۗ
therefore take Straight Path to Him (with true Faith Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism) and obedience to Him, and seek forgiveness of Him.
وَوَيۡلٌ۬ لِّلۡمُشۡرِكِينَ
And woe to Al-Mushrikoon (الْمُشْرِكِينَ) (the disbelievers in the Oneness of Allah (الله), polytheists, idolaters, etc. - see V.2:105).
41:7
ٱلَّذِينَ لَا يُؤۡتُونَ ٱلزَّڪَوٰةَ وَهُم بِٱلۡأَخِرَةِ هُمۡ كَـٰفِرُونَ
Those who give not the Zakat and they are disbelievers in the Hereafter.
41:8
إِنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُواْ وَعَمِلُواْ ٱلصَّـٰلِحَـٰتِ لَهُمۡ أَجۡرٌ غَيۡرُ مَمۡنُونٍ۬
Truly, those who believe (in the Oneness of Allah (الله) Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism, and in His Messenger Muhammad (محمد) SAW) and do righteous good deeds, for them will be an endless reward that will never stop (i.e. Paradise (الجنة)).
41:[9-12] The Story of the creation of the earth, mountains, seasons, skies and heavens
41:[13-18] Warning to the disbelievers and the example of Allah‘s scourge upon the nations of ‘Ad and Thamüd
41:9
قُلۡ أَٮِٕنَّكُمۡ لَتَكۡفُرُونَ بِٱلَّذِى خَلَقَ ٱلۡأَرۡضَ فِى يَوۡمَيۡنِ وَتَجۡعَلُونَ لَهُ ۥۤ أَندَادً۬ا‌ۚ
Say (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW): ‘Do you verily disbelieve in Him Who created the earth in two Days and you set up rivals (in worship) with Him?
ذَٲلِكَ رَبُّ ٱلۡعَـٰلَمِينَ
That is the Lord of the Alameen (mankind, jinns and all that exists).
41:10
وَجَعَلَ فِيہَا رَوَٲسِىَ مِن فَوۡقِهَا وَبَـٰرَكَ فِيہَا
He placed therein (i.e. the earth) firm mountains from above it, and He blessed it,
وَقَدَّرَ فِيہَآ أَقۡوَٲتَہَا فِىٓ أَرۡبَعَةِ أَيَّامٍ۬ سَوَآءً۬ لِّلسَّآٮِٕلِينَ
and measured therein its sustenance (for its dwellers) in four Days equal (i.e. all these four days were equal in the length of time), for all those who ask (about its creation).
41:11
ثُمَّ ٱسۡتَوَىٰٓ إِلَى ٱلسَّمَآءِ وَهِىَ دُخَانٌ۬
Then He Istawa (rose over) towards the heaven when it was smoke,
فَقَالَ لَهَا وَلِلۡأَرۡضِ ٱئۡتِيَا طَوۡعًا أَوۡ كَرۡهً۬ا
and said to it and to the earth: ‘Come both of you willingly or unwillingly.‘
قَالَتَآ أَتَيۡنَا طَآٮِٕعِينَ
They both said: ‘We come, willingly.‘
41:12
فَقَضَٮٰهُنَّ سَبۡعَ سَمَـٰوَاتٍ۬ فِى يَوۡمَيۡنِ وَأَوۡحَىٰ فِى كُلِّ سَمَآءٍ أَمۡرَهَا‌ۚ
Then He completed and finished from their creation (as) seven heavens in two Days and He made in each heaven its affair.
وَزَيَّنَّا ٱلسَّمَآءَ ٱلدُّنۡيَا بِمَصَـٰبِيحَ وَحِفۡظً۬ا‌ۚ
And We adorned the nearest (lowest) heaven with lamps (stars) to be an adornment as well as to guard (from the devils by using them as missiles against the devils).
ذَٲلِكَ تَقۡدِيرُ ٱلۡعَزِيزِ ٱلۡعَلِيمِ
Such is the Decree of Him the All-Mighty, the All-Knower (Al-‘Alee (ٱلْعَلِيُّ)m (ٱلْعَلِيمُ)).
41:13
فَإِنۡ أَعۡرَضُواْ فَقُلۡ أَنذَرۡتُكُمۡ صَـٰعِقَةً۬ مِّثۡلَ صَـٰعِقَةِ عَادٍ۬ وَثَمُودَ
But if they turn away, then say (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW): ‘I have warned you of a Saiqah (a destructive awful cry, torment, hit, a thunderbolt) like the Saiqah which overtook Ad and Thamood (people).‘
41:14
إِذۡ جَآءَتۡہُمُ ٱلرُّسُلُ مِنۢ بَيۡنِ أَيۡدِيهِمۡ وَمِنۡ خَلۡفِهِمۡ أَلَّا تَعۡبُدُوٓاْ إِلَّا ٱللَّهَ‌ۖ
When the Messengers came to them, from before them and behind them (saying): ‘Worship none but Allah (الله)‘
قَالُواْ لَوۡ شَآءَ رَبُّنَا لَأَنزَلَ مَلَـٰٓٮِٕكَةً۬ فَإِنَّا بِمَآ أُرۡسِلۡتُم بِهِۦ كَـٰفِرُونَ
They said: ‘If our Lord had so willed, He would surely have sent down the angels. So indeed! We disbelieve in that with which you have been sent.‘
41:15
فَأَمَّا عَادٌ۬ فَٱسۡتَڪۡبَرُواْ فِى ٱلۡأَرۡضِ بِغَيۡرِ ٱلۡحَقِّ وَقَالُواْ مَنۡ أَشَدُّ مِنَّا قُوَّةً‌ۖ
As for Ad, they were arrogant in the land without right, and they said: ‘Who is mightier than us in strength?‘
أَوَلَمۡ يَرَوۡاْ أَنَّ ٱللَّهَ ٱلَّذِى خَلَقَهُمۡ هُوَ أَشَدُّ مِنۡہُمۡ قُوَّةً۬‌ۖ
See they not that Allah (الله), Who created them was mightier in strength than them.
وَكَانُواْ بِـَٔايَـٰتِنَا يَجۡحَدُونَ
And they used to deny Our Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, revelations, etc.)!
41:16
فَأَرۡسَلۡنَا عَلَيۡہِمۡ رِيحً۬ا صَرۡصَرً۬ا فِىٓ أَيَّامٍ۬ نَّحِسَاتٍ۬ لِّنُذِيقَهُمۡ عَذَابَ ٱلۡخِزۡىِ فِى ٱلۡحَيَوٰةِ ٱلدُّنۡيَا‌ۖ
So We sent upon them furious wind in days of evil omen (for them) that We might give them a taste of disgracing torment in this present worldly life,
وَلَعَذَابُ ٱلۡأَخِرَةِ أَخۡزَىٰ‌ۖ وَهُمۡ لَا يُنصَرُونَ
but surely the torment of the Hereafter will be more disgracing, and they will never be helped.
41:17
وَأَمَّا ثَمُودُ فَهَدَيۡنَـٰهُمۡ فَٱسۡتَحَبُّواْ ٱلۡعَمَىٰ عَلَى ٱلۡهُدَىٰ
And as for Thamood, We showed and made clear to them the Path of Truth (Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism) through Our Messenger, (i.e. showed them the way of success), but they preferred blindness to guidance,
فَأَخَذَتۡہُمۡ صَـٰعِقَةُ ٱلۡعَذَابِ ٱلۡهُونِ بِمَا كَانُواْ يَكۡسِبُونَ
so the Saiqah (a destructive awful cry, torment, hit, a thunderbolt) of disgracing torment seized them, because of what they used to earn.
41:18
وَنَجَّيۡنَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُواْ وَكَانُواْ يَتَّقُونَ
And We saved those who believed and used to fear Allah (الله), keep their duty to Him and avoid evil.
41:[19-25] On the Day of Judgment people‘s own ears, eyes and skins will bear witness against them relating to their misdeeds
41:19
وَيَوۡمَ يُحۡشَرُ أَعۡدَآءُ ٱللَّهِ إِلَى ٱلنَّارِ فَهُمۡ يُوزَعُونَ
And (remember) the Day that the enemies of Allah (الله) will be gathered to the Fire, so they will be collected there (the first and the last).
41:20
حَتَّىٰٓ إِذَا مَا جَآءُوهَا شَہِدَ عَلَيۡہِمۡ سَمۡعُهُمۡ وَأَبۡصَـٰرُهُمۡ وَجُلُودُهُم بِمَا كَانُواْ يَعۡمَلُونَ
Till, when they reach it (Hell (جهنم)-fire), their hearing (ears) and their eyes, and their skins will testify against them as to what they used to do.
41:21
وَقَالُواْ لِجُلُودِهِمۡ لِمَ شَهِدتُّمۡ عَلَيۡنَا‌ۖ
And they will say to their skins, ‘Why do you testify against us?‘
قَالُوٓاْ أَنطَقَنَا ٱللَّهُ ٱلَّذِىٓ أَنطَقَ كُلَّ شَىۡءٍ۬
They will say: ‘Allah (الله) has caused us to speak, as He causes all things to speak,
وَهُوَ خَلَقَكُمۡ أَوَّلَ مَرَّةٍ۬ وَإِلَيۡهِ تُرۡجَعُونَ
and He created you the first time, and to Him you are made to return.‘
41:22
وَمَا كُنتُمۡ تَسۡتَتِرُونَ أَن يَشۡہَدَ عَلَيۡكُمۡ سَمۡعُكُمۡ وَلَآ أَبۡصَـٰرُكُمۡ وَلَا جُلُودُكُمۡ
And you have not been hiding against yourselves, lest your ears, and your eyes, and your skins testify against you,
وَلَـٰكِن ظَنَنتُمۡ أَنَّ ٱللَّهَ لَا يَعۡلَمُ كَثِيرً۬ا مِّمَّا تَعۡمَلُونَ
but you thought that Allah (الله) knew not much of what you were doing.
41:23
وَذَٲلِكُمۡ ظَنُّكُمُ ٱلَّذِى ظَنَنتُم بِرَبِّكُمۡ أَرۡدَٮٰكُمۡ فَأَصۡبَحۡتُم مِّنَ ٱلۡخَـٰسِرِينَ
And that thought of yours which you thought about your Lord, has brought you to destruction, and you have become (this Day) of those utterly lost!
41:24
فَإِن يَصۡبِرُواْ فَٱلنَّارُ مَثۡوً۬ى لَّهُمۡ‌ۖ
Then, if they have patience, yet the Fire will be a home for them,
وَإِن يَسۡتَعۡتِبُواْ فَمَا هُم مِّنَ ٱلۡمُعۡتَبِينَ
and if they beg for to be excused, yet they are not of those who will ever be excused.
41:25
وَقَيَّضۡنَا لَهُمۡ قُرَنَآءَ فَزَيَّنُواْ لَهُم مَّا بَيۡنَ أَيۡدِيہِمۡ وَمَا خَلۡفَهُمۡ
And We have assigned them (devils) intimate companions (in this world), who have made fair seeming to them, what was before them (evil deeds which they were doing in the present worldly life and disbelief in the Reckoning and the Resurrection, etc.) and what was behind them (denial of the matters in the coming life of the Hereafter as regards punishment or reward, etc.).
وَحَقَّ عَلَيۡهِمُ ٱلۡقَوۡلُ فِىٓ أُمَمٍ۬ قَدۡ خَلَتۡ مِن قَبۡلِهِم مِّنَ ٱلۡجِنِّ وَٱلۡإِنسِ‌ۖ
And the Word (i.e. the torment) is justified against them as it was justified against those who were among the previous generations of jinns and men that had passed away before them.
إِنَّهُمۡ كَانُواْ خَـٰسِرِينَ
Indeed they (all) were the losers.
41:[26-32] Those who do not listen to the Qur‘an shall be sternly punished. Those who say our God is Allah and then stay firm on it, angels are assigned for their protection
41:26
وَقَالَ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُواْ لَا تَسۡمَعُواْ لِهَـٰذَا ٱلۡقُرۡءَانِ وَٱلۡغَوۡاْ فِيهِ لَعَلَّكُمۡ تَغۡلِبُونَ
And those who disbelieve say: ‘Listen not to this Quran (القرآن), and make noise in the midst of its (recitation) that you may overcome.‘
41:27
فَلَنُذِيقَنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُواْ عَذَابً۬ا شَدِيدً۬اوَلَنَجۡزِيَنَّہُمۡ أَسۡوَأَ ٱلَّذِى كَانُواْ يَعۡمَلُونَ
But surely, We shall cause those who disbelieve to taste a severe torment, and certainly, We shall requite them the worst of what they used to do.
41:28
ذَٲلِكَ جَزَآءُ أَعۡدَآءِ ٱللَّهِ ٱلنَّارُ‌ۖ
That is the recompense of the enemies of Allah (الله): The Fire,
لَهُمۡ فِيہَا دَارُ ٱلۡخُلۡدِ‌ۖ جَزَآءَۢ بِمَا كَانُواْ بِـَٔايَـٰتِنَا يَجۡحَدُونَ
therein will be for them the eternal home, a (deserving) recompense for that they used to deny Our Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, etc.).
41:29
وَقَالَ ٱلَّذِينَ ڪَفَرُواْ رَبَّنَآ أَرِنَا ٱلَّذَيۡنِ أَضَلَّانَا مِنَ ٱلۡجِنِّ وَٱلۡإِنسِ
And those who disbelieve will say:
Our Lord! Show us those among jinns and men who led us astray,
نَجۡعَلۡهُمَا تَحۡتَ أَقۡدَامِنَا لِيَكُونَا مِنَ ٱلۡأَسۡفَلِينَ
we shall crush them under our feet, so that they become the lowest.‘
41:30
إِنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ قَالُواْ رَبُّنَا ٱللَّهُ ثُمَّ ٱسۡتَقَـٰمُواْ تَتَنَزَّلُ عَلَيۡهِمُ ٱلۡمَلَـٰٓٮِٕڪَةُ
Verily, those who say: ‘Our Lord is Allah (الله) (Alone),‘ and then they Istaqamoo, on them the angels will descend (at the time of their death) (saying):
أَلَّا تَخَافُواْ وَلَا تَحۡزَنُواْ وَأَبۡشِرُواْ بِٱلۡجَنَّةِ ٱلَّتِى كُنتُمۡ تُوعَدُونَ
Fear not, nor grieve! But receive the glad tidings of Paradise (الجنة) which you have been promised!
41:31
نَحۡنُ أَوۡلِيَآؤُكُمۡ فِى ٱلۡحَيَوٰةِ ٱلدُّنۡيَا وَفِى ٱلۡأَخِرَةِ‌ۖ
We have been your friends in the life of this world and are (so) in the Hereafter.
وَلَكُمۡ فِيهَا مَا تَشۡتَهِىٓ أَنفُسُكُمۡ وَلَكُمۡ فِيهَا مَا تَدَّعُونَ
Therein you shall have (all) that your inner-selves desire, and therein you shall have (all) for which you ask for.
41:32
نُزُلاً۬ مِّنۡ غَفُورٍ۬ رَّحِيمٍ۬
An entertainment from (Allah (الله)), the Oft-Forgiving, Most Merciful (Ar-Raheem (ٱلْرَّحِيْمُ)).‘
says: The best in speech is the one who calls people towards Allah, does good deeds and
41:[33-44] ‘I am a Muslim‘. Example of Allah‘s signs. Nothing is said to Muhammad which was not said to the prior Prophets; the Qur‘an is a guide and healing for the believers
41:33
وَمَنۡ أَحۡسَنُ قَوۡلاً۬ مِّمَّن دَعَآ إِلَى ٱللَّهِ وَعَمِلَ صَـٰلِحً۬ا وَقَالَ إِنَّنِى مِنَ ٱلۡمُسۡلِمِينَ
And who is better in speech than he who (says: ‘My Lord is Allah (الله) (believes in His Oneness),‘ and then stands straight (acts upon His Order), and) invites (men) to Allah (الله)‘s (Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism), and does righteous deeds, and says: ‘I am one of the Muslims.‘
41:34
وَلَا تَسۡتَوِى ٱلۡحَسَنَةُ وَلَا ٱلسَّيِّئَةُ‌ۚ
The good deed and the evil deed cannot be equal.
ٱدۡفَعۡ بِٱلَّتِى هِىَ أَحۡسَنُ
Repel (the evil) with one which is better(i.e. Allah (الله) ordered the faithful believers to be patient at the time of anger, and to excuse those who treat them badly),
فَإِذَا ٱلَّذِى بَيۡنَكَ وَبَيۡنَهُ ۥ عَدَٲوَةٌ۬ كَأَنَّهُ ۥ وَلِىٌّ حَمِيمٌ۬
then verily! he, between whom and you there was enmity, (will become) as though he was a close friend.
41:35
وَمَا يُلَقَّٮٰهَآ إِلَّا ٱلَّذِينَ صَبَرُواْوَمَا يُلَقَّٮٰهَآ إِلَّا ذُو حَظٍّ عَظِيمٍ۬
But none is granted it (the above quality) except those who are patient, and none is granted it except the owner of the great portion (of the happiness in the Hereafter i.e. Paradise (الجنة) and in this world of a high moral character).
41:36
وَإِمَّا يَنزَغَنَّكَ مِنَ ٱلشَّيۡطَـٰنِ نَزۡغٌ۬ فَٱسۡتَعِذۡ بِٱللَّهِ‌ۖ
And if an evil whisper from Shaitan (Satan (الشيطان)) tries to turn you away (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) (from doing good, etc.), then seek refuge in Allah (الله).
إِنَّهُ ۥ هُوَ ٱلسَّمِيعُ ٱلۡعَلِيمُ
Verily, He is the All-Hearer, the All-Knower (Al-‘Alee (ٱلْعَلِيُّ)m (ٱلْعَلِيمُ)).
41:37
وَمِنۡ ءَايَـٰتِهِ ٱلَّيۡلُ وَٱلنَّهَارُ وَٱلشَّمۡسُ وَٱلۡقَمَرُ‌ۚ
And from among His Signs are the night and the day, and the sun and the moon.
لَا تَسۡجُدُواْ لِلشَّمۡسِ وَلَا لِلۡقَمَرِ
Prostrate not to the sun nor to the moon,
وَٱسۡجُدُواْ لِلَّهِ ٱلَّذِى خَلَقَهُنَّ إِن ڪُنتُمۡ إِيَّاهُ تَعۡبُدُونَ
but prostrate to Allah (الله) Who created them, if you (really) worship Him.
41:38
فَإِنِ ٱسۡتَڪۡبَرُواْ
But if they are too proud (to do so),
فَٱلَّذِينَ عِندَ رَبِّكَ يُسَبِّحُونَ لَهُ ۥ بِٱلَّيۡلِ وَٱلنَّہَارِ وَهُمۡ لَا يَسۡـَٔمُونَ ۩
then there are those who are with your Lord (angels) glorify Him night and day, and never are they tired. ۩
41:39
وَمِنۡ ءَايَـٰتِهِۦۤ أَنَّكَ تَرَى ٱلۡأَرۡضَ خَـٰشِعَةً۬ فَإِذَآ أَنزَلۡنَا عَلَيۡہَا ٱلۡمَآءَ ٱهۡتَزَّتۡ وَرَبَتۡ‌ۚ
And among His Signs (in this), that you see the earth barren, but when We send down water (rain) to it, it is stirred to life and growth (of vegetations).
إِنَّ ٱلَّذِىٓ أَحۡيَاهَا لَمُحۡىِ ٱلۡمَوۡتَىٰٓ‌ۚ
Verily, He Who gives it life, surely, (He) is Able to give life to the dead (on the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة)).
إِنَّهُ ۥ عَلَىٰ كُلِّ شَىۡءٍ۬ قَدِيرٌ
Indeed! He is Able to do all things.
41:40
إِنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ يُلۡحِدُونَ فِىٓ ءَايَـٰتِنَا لَا يَخۡفَوۡنَ عَلَيۡنَآ‌ۗ
Verily, those who turn away from Our Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, etc. by attacking, distorting and denying them), are not hidden from Us.
أَفَمَن يُلۡقَىٰ فِى ٱلنَّارِ خَيۡرٌ أَم مَّن يَأۡتِىٓ ءَامِنً۬ا يَوۡمَ ٱلۡقِيَـٰمَةِ‌ۚ
Is he who is cast into the Fire better or he who comes secure on the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة)?
ٱعۡمَلُواْ مَا شِئۡتُمۡ‌ۖإِنَّهُ ۥ بِمَا تَعۡمَلُونَ بَصِيرٌ
Do what you will. Verily! He is All-Seer (Al-Baseer (ٱلْبَصِيرُ)) of what you do (this is a severe threat to the disbelievers).
41:41
إِنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُواْ بِٱلذِّكۡرِ لَمَّا جَآءَهُمۡ‌ۖ
Verily, those who disbelieved in the Reminder (i.e. the Quran (القرآن)) when it came to them (shall receive the punishment).
وَإِنَّهُ ۥ لَكِتَـٰبٌ عَزِيزٌ۬
And verily, it is an honourable respected Book (because it is Allah (الله)‘s Speech, and He has protected it from corruption, etc.). (See V.15:9)
41:42
لَّا يَأۡتِيهِ ٱلۡبَـٰطِلُ مِنۢ بَيۡنِ يَدَيۡهِ وَلَا مِنۡ خَلۡفِهِۦ‌ۖ
Falsehood cannot come to it from before it or behind it
تَنزِيلٌ۬ مِّنۡ حَكِيمٍ حَمِيدٍ۬
(it is) sent down by the All-Wise (Al-Hakeem (ٱلْحَكِيمُ)), Worthy of all praise (Allah (الله)).
41:43
مَّا يُقَالُ لَكَ إِلَّا مَا قَدۡ قِيلَ لِلرُّسُلِ مِن قَبۡلِكَ‌ۚ
Nothing is said to you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) except what was said to the Messengers before you.
إِنَّ رَبَّكَ لَذُو مَغۡفِرَةٍ۬ وَذُو عِقَابٍ أَلِيمٍ۬
Verily, your Lord is the Possessor of forgiveness, and (also) the Possessor of painful punishment.
41:44
وَلَوۡ جَعَلۡنَـٰهُ قُرۡءَانًا أَعۡجَمِيًّ۬ا لَّقَالُواْ لَوۡلَا فُصِّلَتۡ ءَايَـٰتُهُ ۥۤ‌ۖ ءَ
And if We had sent this as a Quran (القرآن) in a foreign language other than Arabic (العربية), they would have said: ‘Why are not its Verses explained in detail (in our language)?
ا۠عۡجَمِىٌّ۬ وَعَرَبِىٌّ۬‌ۗ
What! (A Book) not in Arabic (العربية) and (the Messenger) an Arab?‘
قُلۡ هُوَ لِلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُواْ هُدً۬ى وَشِفَآءٌ۬‌ۖ
Say: ‘It is for those who believe, a guide and a healing.
وَٱلَّذِينَ لَا يُؤۡمِنُونَ فِىٓ ءَاذَانِهِمۡ وَقۡرٌ۬ وَهُوَ عَلَيۡهِمۡ عَمًى‌ۚ
And as for those who disbelieve, there is heaviness (deafness) in their ears, and it (the Quran (القرآن)) is blindness for them.
أُوْلَـٰٓٮِٕكَ يُنَادَوۡنَ مِن مَّكَانِۭ بَعِيدٍ۬
They are those who are called from a place far away (so they neither listen nor understand).
41:[45-46] The Book given to the Prophet Musa was similar to The Qur‘an
41:45
وَلَقَدۡ ءَاتَيۡنَا مُوسَى ٱلۡكِتَـٰبَ فَٱخۡتُلِفَ فِيهِ‌ۗ
And indeed We gave Musa (Moosa (موسى)) the Scripture, but dispute arose therein.
وَلَوۡلَا ڪَلِمَةٌ۬ سَبَقَتۡ مِن رَّبِّكَ لَقُضِىَ بَيۡنَهُمۡ‌ۚ
And had it not been for a Word that went forth before from your Lord, (the torment would have overtaken them) and the matter would have been settled between them.
وَإِنَّهُمۡ لَفِى شَكٍّ۬ مِّنۡهُ مُرِيبٍ۬
But truly, they are in grave doubt thereto (i.e. about the Quran (القرآن)).(Tafsir Al-Qurtubi, Vol. 15, Page 370)
41:46
مَّنۡ عَمِلَ صَـٰلِحً۬ا فَلِنَفۡسِهِۦ‌ۖ
Whosoever does righteous good deed it is for (the benefit of) his ownself,
وَمَنۡ أَسَآءَ فَعَلَيۡهَا‌ۗ
and whosoever does evil, it is against his ownself,
وَمَا رَبُّكَ بِظَلَّـٰمٍ۬ لِّلۡعَبِيدِ
and your Lord is not at all unjust to (His) slaves.
41:[47-51] On the Day of Judgment all other gods to whom people worship besides Allah shall vanish
41:[52-54] Have you ever considered that if The Qur‘an is really from Allah and you deny it, what will happen to you
41:47
إِلَيۡهِ يُرَدُّ عِلۡمُ ٱلسَّاعَةِ‌ۚ
(The learned men) refer to Him (Alone) the knowledge of the Hour.
وَمَا تَخۡرُجُ مِن ثَمَرَٲتٍ۬ مِّنۡ أَكۡمَامِهَا وَمَا تَحۡمِلُ مِنۡ أُنثَىٰ وَلَا تَضَعُ إِلَّا بِعِلۡمِهِۦ‌ۚ
No fruit comes out of its sheath, nor does a female conceive (within her womb), nor brings forth (young), except by His Knowledge.
وَيَوۡمَ يُنَادِيہِمۡ أَيۡنَ شُرَڪَآءِى قَالُوٓاْ
And on the Day when He will call unto them (polytheists) (saying): ‘Where are My (so-called) partners (whom you did invent)?‘
ءَاذَنَّـٰكَ مَا مِنَّا مِن شَہِيدٍ۬
They will say: ‘We inform You that none of us bears witness to it (that they are Your partners)!‘
41:48
وَضَلَّ عَنۡہُم مَّا كَانُواْ يَدۡعُونَ مِن قَبۡلُ‌ۖ وَظَنُّواْ مَا لَهُم مِّن مَّحِيصٍ۬
And those whom they used to invoke before will fail them, and they will perceive that they have no place of refuge (from Allah (الله)‘s punishment).
41:49
لَّا يَسۡـَٔمُ ٱلۡإِنسَـٰنُ مِن دُعَآءِ ٱلۡخَيۡرِ وَإِن مَّسَّهُ ٱلشَّرُّ فَيَـُٔوسٌ۬ قَنُوطٌ۬
Man (the disbeliever) does not get tired of asking good (things from Allah (الله)), but if an evil touches him, then he gives up all hope and is lost in despair.
41:50
وَلَٮِٕنۡ أَذَقۡنَـٰهُ رَحۡمَةً۬ مِّنَّا مِنۢ بَعۡدِ ضَرَّآءَ مَسَّتۡهُ لَيَقُولَنَّ هَـٰذَا لِى
And truly, if We give him a taste of mercy from us, after some adversity (severe poverty or disease, etc.) has touched him, he is sure to say:
This is for me (due to my merit),
وَمَآ أَظُنُّ ٱلسَّاعَةَ قَآٮِٕمَةً۬ وَلَٮِٕن رُّجِعۡتُ إِلَىٰ رَبِّىٓ إِنَّ لِى عِندَهُ ۥ لَلۡحُسۡنَىٰ‌ۚ
I think not that the Hour will be established. But if I am brought back to my Lord, Surely, there will be for me the best (wealth, etc.) with Him.
فَلَنُنَبِّئَنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُواْ بِمَا عَمِلُواْ وَلَنُذِيقَنَّهُم مِّنۡ عَذَابٍ غَلِيظٍ۬
Then, We verily, will show to the disbelievers what they have done and We shall make them taste a severe torment.
41:51
وَإِذَآ أَنۡعَمۡنَا عَلَى ٱلۡإِنسَـٰنِ أَعۡرَضَ وَنَـَٔا بِجَانِبِهِۦ
And when We show favour to man, he withdraws and turns away,
وَإِذَا مَسَّهُ ٱلشَّرُّ فَذُو دُعَآءٍ عَرِيضٍ۬
but when evil touches him, then he has recourse to long supplications.
41:52
قُلۡ أَرَءَيۡتُمۡ إِن ڪَانَ مِنۡ عِندِ ٱللَّهِ ثُمَّ ڪَفَرۡتُم بِهِۦ
Say: ‘Tell me, if it (the Quran (القرآن)) is from Allah (الله), and you disbelieve in it,
مَنۡ أَضَلُّ مِمَّنۡ هُوَ فِى شِقَاقِۭ بَعِيدٍ۬
who is more astray than one who is in opposition far away (from Allah (الله)‘s Right Path and His obedience).
41:53
سَنُرِيهِمۡ ءَايَـٰتِنَا فِى ٱلۡأَفَاقِ وَفِىٓ أَنفُسِہِمۡ حَتَّىٰ يَتَبَيَّنَ لَهُمۡ أَنَّهُ ٱلۡحَقُّ‌ۗ
We will show them Our Signs in the universe, and in their ownselves, until it becomes manifest to them that this (the Quran (القرآن)) is the truth.
أَوَلَمۡ يَكۡفِ بِرَبِّكَ أَنَّهُ ۥ عَلَىٰ كُلِّ شَىۡءٍ۬ شَہِيدٌ
Is it not sufficient in regard to your Lord that He is a Witness over all things?
41:54
أَلَآ إِنَّہُمۡ فِى مِرۡيَةٍ۬ مِّن لِّقَآءِ رَبِّهِمۡ‌ۗ
Verily! They are in doubt concerning the Meeting with their Lord?(i.e. Resurrection after their deaths, and their return to their Lord)
أَلَآ إِنَّهُ ۥ بِكُلِّ شَىۡءٍ۬ مُّحِيطُۢ
Verily! He it is Who is surrounding all things!*********
