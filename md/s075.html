Al-Qiyaama
Resurrection, The Day of Resurrection, Rising Of The Dead
75:[1-15] Be aware of the Day of Resurrection and Judgment, from which there is no escape. There is no escape from it
75:[16-30] Allah Himself took the responsibility of The Qur‘an. The last moments of disbelievers‘ death
75:1
لَآ أُقۡسِمُ بِيَوۡمِ ٱلۡقِيَـٰمَةِ
I swear by the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة);
75:2
وَلَآ أُقۡسِمُ بِٱلنَّفۡسِ ٱللَّوَّامَةِ
And I swear by the self-reproaching person (a believer).
75:3
أَيَحۡسَبُ ٱلۡإِنسَـٰنُ أَلَّن نَّجۡمَعَ عِظَامَهُ ۥ
Does man (a disbeliever) think that We shall not assemble his bones?
75:4
بَلَىٰ قَـٰدِرِينَ عَلَىٰٓ أَن نُّسَوِّىَ بَنَانَهُ ۥ
Yes, We are Able to put together in perfect order the tips of his fingers.
75:5
بَلۡ يُرِيدُ ٱلۡإِنسَـٰنُ لِيَفۡجُرَ أَمَامَهُ ۥ
Nay! (Man denies Resurrection and Reckoning. So) he desires to continue committing sins.
75:6
يَسۡـَٔلُ أَيَّانَ يَوۡمُ ٱلۡقِيَـٰمَةِ
He asks: ‘When will be this Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة)?‘
75:7
فَإِذَا بَرِقَ ٱلۡبَصَرُ
So, when the sight shall be dazed,
75:8
وَخَسَفَ ٱلۡقَمَرُ
And the moon will be eclipsed,
75:9
وَجُمِعَ ٱلشَّمۡسُ وَٱلۡقَمَرُ
And the sun and moon will be joined together (by going one into the other or folded up ordeprived of their light, etc.)
75:10
يَقُولُ ٱلۡإِنسَـٰنُ يَوۡمَٮِٕذٍ أَيۡنَ ٱلۡمَفَرُّ
On that Day man will say: ‘Where (is the refuge) to flee?‘
75:11
كَلَّا لَا وَزَرَ
No! There is no refuge!
75:12
إِلَىٰ رَبِّكَ يَوۡمَٮِٕذٍ ٱلۡمُسۡتَقَرُّ
Unto your Lord (Alone) will be the place of rest that Day.
75:13
يُنَبَّؤُاْ ٱلۡإِنسَـٰنُ يَوۡمَٮِٕذِۭ بِمَا قَدَّمَ وَأَخَّرَ
On that Day man will be informed of what he sent forward (of his evil or good deeds), and what he left behind (of his good or evil traditions).
75:14
بَلِ ٱلۡإِنسَـٰنُ عَلَىٰ نَفۡسِهِۦ بَصِيرَةٌ۬
Nay! Man will be a witness against himself (as his body parts (skin, hands, legs, etc.) will speak about his deeds).
75:15
وَلَوۡ أَلۡقَىٰ مَعَاذِيرَهُ ۥ
Though he may put forth his excuses (to cover his evil deeds).
75:16
لَا تُحَرِّكۡ بِهِۦ لِسَانَكَ لِتَعۡجَلَ بِهِۦۤ
Move not your tongue concerning (the Quran (القرآن), O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) to make haste therewith.
75:17
إِنَّ عَلَيۡنَا جَمۡعَهُ ۥ وَقُرۡءَانَهُ ۥ
It is for Us to collect it and to give you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) the ability to recite it (the Quran (القرآن)),
75:18
فَإِذَا قَرَأۡنَـٰهُ فَٱتَّبِعۡ قُرۡءَانَهُ ۥ
And when We have recited it to you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW through Jibrael (Jibreel (جبريل))), then follow you its (the Quran (القرآن)s) recital.
75:19
ثُمَّ إِنَّ عَلَيۡنَا بَيَانَهُ ۥ
Then it is for Us (Allah (الله)) to make it clear to you,
75:20
كَلَّا بَلۡ تُحِبُّونَ ٱلۡعَاجِلَةَ
Not (as you think, that you (mankind) will not be resurrected and recompensed for your deeds), but (you men) love the present life of this world,
75:21
وَتَذَرُونَ ٱلۡأَخِرَةَ
And leave (neglect) the Hereafter.
75:22
وُجُوهٌ۬ يَوۡمَٮِٕذٍ۬ نَّاضِرَةٌ
Some faces that Day shall be Nadirah (shining and radiant).
75:23
إِلَىٰ رَبِّہَا نَاظِرَةٌ۬
Looking at their Lord (Allah (الله));
75:24
وَوُجُوهٌ۬ يَوۡمَٮِٕذِۭ بَاسِرَةٌ۬
And some faces, that Day, will be Basirah (dark, gloomy, frowning, and sad),
75:25
تَظُنُّ أَن يُفۡعَلَ بِہَا فَاقِرَةٌ۬
Thinking that some calamity was about to fall on them;
75:26
كَلَّآ إِذَا بَلَغَتِ ٱلتَّرَاقِىَ
Nay, when (the soul) reaches to the collar bone (i.e. up to the throat in its exit),
75:27
وَقِيلَ مَنۡۜ رَاقٍ۬
And it will be said: ‘Who can cure him and save him from death?‘
75:28
وَظَنَّ أَنَّهُ ٱلۡفِرَاقُ
And he (the dying person) will conclude that it was (the time) of departing (death);
75:29
وَٱلۡتَفَّتِ ٱلسَّاقُ بِٱلسَّاقِ
And leg will be joined with another leg (shrouded)
75:30
إِلَىٰ رَبِّكَ يَوۡمَٮِٕذٍ ٱلۡمَسَاقُ
The drive will be, on that Day, to your Lord (Allah (الله))!
75:[31-40] The disbelievers do not believe because they never took Al-Islam seriously
75:31
فَلَا صَدَّقَ وَلَا صَلَّىٰ
So he (the disbeliever) neither believed (in this Quran (القرآن), in the Message of Muhammad (محمد) SAW) nor prayed!
75:32
وَلَـٰكِن كَذَّبَ وَتَوَلَّىٰ
But on the contrary, he belied (this Quran (القرآن) and the Message of Muhammad (محمد) SAW) and turned away!ثُمَّ ذَهَبَ إِلَىٰٓ أَهۡلِهِۦ يَتَمَطَّىٰٓ
75:33
ثُمَّ ذَهَبَ إِلَىٰٓ أَهۡلِهِۦ يَتَمَطَّىٰٓ
Then he walked in full pride to his family admiring himself!
75:34
أَوۡلَىٰ لَكَ فَأَوۡلَىٰ
Woe to you (O man (disbeliever))! And then (again) woe to you!
75:35
ثُمَّ أَوۡلَىٰ لَكَ فَأَوۡلَىٰٓ
Again, woe to you (O man (disbeliever))! And then (again) woe to you!
75:36
أَيَحۡسَبُ ٱلۡإِنسَـٰنُ أَن يُتۡرَكَ سُدًى
Does man think that he will be left Suda (neglected without being punished or rewarded for the obligatory duties enjoined by his Lord (Allah (الله)) on him)?
75:37
أَلَمۡ يَكُ نُطۡفَةً۬ مِّن مَّنِىٍّ۬ يُمۡنَىٰ
Was he not a Nutfah (mixed male and female discharge of semen) poured forth?
75:38
ثُمَّ كَانَ عَلَقَةً۬ فَخَلَقَ فَسَوَّىٰ
Then he became an Alaq (علق)a (a clot); then (Allah (الله)) shaped and fashioned (him) in due proportion.
75:39
فَجَعَلَ مِنۡهُ ٱلزَّوۡجَيۡنِ ٱلذَّكَرَ وَٱلۡأُنثَىٰٓ
And made him in two sexes, male and female.
75:40
أَلَيۡسَ ذَٲلِكَ بِقَـٰدِرٍ عَلَىٰٓ أَن يُحۡـِۧىَ ٱلۡمَوۡتَىٰ
Is not He (Allah (الله) Who does that), Able to give life to the dead?(Yes! He is Able to do all things).*********
