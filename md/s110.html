An-Nasr
The Help, Divine Support, Victory, Succour
110:[1-3] Victory comes with the help of Allah
110:1
إِذَا جَآءَ نَصۡرُ ٱللَّهِ وَٱلۡفَتۡحُ
When comes the Help of Allâh (to you, O Muhammad (محمد) SAW against your enemies) and the conquest (of Makkah (مكة)),
110:2
وَرَأَيۡتَ ٱلنَّاسَ يَدۡخُلُونَ فِى دِينِ ٱللَّهِ أَفۡوَاجً۬ا
And you see that the people enter Allâh‘s religion (Islâm) in crowds,
110:3
فَسَبِّحۡ بِحَمۡدِ رَبِّكَ وَٱسۡتَغۡفِرۡهُ‌ۚ
So glorify the Praises of your Lord, and ask for His Forgiveness.
إِنَّهُ ۥ ڪَانَ تَوَّابَۢا
Verily, He is the One Who accepts the repentance and forgives.*********
