At-Takaathur
The Piling Up, Rivalry in World Increase, Competition, Greed for More and More
102:[1-8] The cause of man‘s destruction is mutual rivalry for worldly gains. Real success is in working for the life hereafter
102:1
أَلۡهَٮٰكُمُ ٱلتَّكَاثُرُ
The mutual rivalry for piling up of worldly things diverts you,
102:2
حَتَّىٰ زُرۡتُمُ ٱلۡمَقَابِرَ
Until you visit the graves (i.e. till you die).
102:3
كَلَّا سَوۡفَ تَعۡلَمُونَ
Nay! You shall come to know!
102:4
ثُمَّ كَلَّا سَوۡفَ تَعۡلَمُونَ
Again, Nay! You shall come to know!
102:5
كَلَّا لَوۡ تَعۡلَمُونَ عِلۡمَ ٱلۡيَقِينِ
Nay! If you knew with a sure knowledge(the end result of piling up, you would not have occupied yourselves in worldly things)
102:6
لَتَرَوُنَّ ٱلۡجَحِيمَ
Verily, You shall see the blazing Fire (Hell (جهنم))!
102:7
ثُمَّ لَتَرَوُنَّہَا عَيۡنَ ٱلۡيَقِينِ
And again, you shall see it with certainty of sight!
102:8
ثُمَّ لَتُسۡـَٔلُنَّ يَوۡمَٮِٕذٍ عَنِ ٱلنَّعِيمِ
Then, on that Day, you shall be asked about the delight (you indulged in, in this world)!*********
