Al-Muddaththir
The One Wrapped Up, The Cloaked One, The Man Wearing A Cloak, The Enfolded One
74:[1-7] Instructions to the Prophet for cleanliness and patience
74:[8-31] The Day of Judgment will be very difficult, especially for those who deny Allah‘s revelations and oppose His cause
74:1
يَـٰٓأَيُّہَا ٱلۡمُدَّثِّرُ
O you (Muhammad (محمد) SAW) enveloped (in garments)!
74:2
قُمۡ فَأَنذِرۡ
Arise and warn!
74:3
وَرَبَّكَ فَكَبِّرۡ
And your Lord (Allah (الله)) magnify!
74:4
وَثِيَابَكَ فَطَهِّرۡ
And your garments purify!
74:5
وَٱلرُّجۡزَ فَٱهۡجُرۡ
And keep away from Ar-Rujz (the idols)!
74:6
وَلَا تَمۡنُن تَسۡتَكۡثِرُ
And give not a thing in order to have more(or consider not your deeds of Allah (الله)s obedience as a favour to Allah (الله)).
74:7
وَلِرَبِّكَ فَٱصۡبِرۡ
And be patient for the sake of your Lord (i.e. perform your duty to Allah (الله))!
74:8
فَإِذَا نُقِرَ فِى ٱلنَّاقُورِ
Then, when the Trumpet is sounded (i.e. its second blowing);
74:9
فَذَٲلِكَ يَوۡمَٮِٕذٍ۬ يَوۡمٌ عَسِيرٌ
Truly, that Day will be a Hard Day.
74:10
عَلَى ٱلۡكَـٰفِرِينَ غَيۡرُ يَسِيرٍ۬
Far from easy for the disbelievers.
74:11
ذَرۡنِى وَمَنۡ خَلَقۡتُ وَحِيدً۬ا
Leave Me Alone (to deal) with whom I created Alone (without any means, i.e. Al-Waleed bin Al-Mugheerah Al-Makhzoomee)!
74:12
وَجَعَلۡتُ لَهُ ۥ مَالاً۬ مَّمۡدُودً۬ا
And then granted him resources in abundance.
74:13
وَبَنِينَ شُہُودً۬ا
And children to be by his side!
74:14
وَمَهَّدتُّ لَهُ ۥ تَمۡهِيدً۬ا
And made life smooth and comfortable for him!
74:15
ثُمَّ يَطۡمَعُ أَنۡ أَزِيدَ
After all that he desires that I should give more;
74:16
كَلَّآ‌ۖ إِنَّهُ ۥ كَانَ لِأَيَـٰتِنَا عَنِيدً۬ا
Nay! Verily, he has been stubborn and opposing Our Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, etc.).
74:17
سَأُرۡهِقُهُ ۥ صَعُودًا
I shall oblige him to (climb a slippery mountain in the Hell (جهنم)-fire called AsSaood, or to) face a severe torment!
74:18
إِنَّهُ ۥ فَكَّرَ وَقَدَّرَ
Verily, he thought and plotted;
74:19
فَقُتِلَ كَيۡفَ قَدَّرَ
So let him be cursed! How he plotted!
74:20
ثُمَّ قُتِلَ كَيۡفَ قَدَّرَ
And once more let him be cursed, how he plotted!
74:21
ثُمَّ نَظَرَ
Then he thought;
74:22
ثُمَّ عَبَسَ وَبَسَرَ
Then he frowned and he looked in a bad tempered way;
74:23
ثُمَّ أَدۡبَرَ وَٱسۡتَكۡبَرَ
Then he turned back and was proud;
74:24
فَقَالَ إِنۡ هَـٰذَآ إِلَّا سِحۡرٌ۬ يُؤۡثَرُ
Then he said: ‘This is nothing but magic from that of old;
74:25
إِنۡ هَـٰذَآ إِلَّا قَوۡلُ ٱلۡبَشَرِ
This is nothing but the word of a human being!‘
74:26
سَأُصۡلِيهِ سَقَرَ
I will cast him into Hell (جهنم)-fire
74:27
وَمَآ أَدۡرَٮٰكَ مَا سَقَرُ
And what will make you know exactly what Hell (جهنم)-fire is?
74:28
لَا تُبۡقِى وَلَا تَذَرُ
It spares not (any sinner), nor does it leave (anything unburnt)!
74:29
لَوَّاحَةٌ۬ لِّلۡبَشَرِ
Burning the skins!
74:30
عَلَيۡہَا تِسۡعَةَ عَشَرَ
Over it are nineteen (angels as guardians and keepers of Hell (جهنم)).
74:31
وَمَا جَعَلۡنَآ أَصۡحَـٰبَ ٱلنَّارِ إِلَّا مَلَـٰٓٮِٕكَةً۬‌ۙ
And We have set none but angels as guardians of the Fire,
وَمَا جَعَلۡنَا عِدَّتَہُمۡ إِلَّا فِتۡنَةً۬ لِّلَّذِينَ كَفَرُواْ
and We have fixed their number (19) only as a trial for the disbelievers,
لِيَسۡتَيۡقِنَ ٱلَّذِينَ أُوتُواْ ٱلۡكِتَـٰبَ وَيَزۡدَادَ ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوٓاْ إِيمَـٰنً۬ا‌ۙ
in order that the people of the Scripture (Jews (هُودًا) and Christians (نَصَارَىٰ)) may arrive at a certainty (that this Quran (القرآن) is the truth as it agrees with their Books i.e. their number (19) is written in the Taurat (Torah (التوراة)) and the Injil (Injeel (الإنجيل))) and the believers may increase in Faith (as this Quran (القرآن) is the truth)
وَلَا يَرۡتَابَ ٱلَّذِينَ أُوتُواْ ٱلۡكِتَـٰبَ وَٱلۡمُؤۡمِنُونَ‌ۙ
and that no doubts may be left for the people of the Scripture and the believers,
وَلِيَقُولَ ٱلَّذِينَ فِى قُلُوبِہِم مَّرَضٌ۬ وَٱلۡكَـٰفِرُونَ مَاذَآ أَرَادَ ٱللَّهُ بِہَـٰذَا مَثَلاً۬‌ۚ
and that those in whose hearts is a disease (of hypocrisy) and the disbelievers may say: ‘What Allah (الله) intends by this (curious) example ?‘
كَذَٲلِكَ يُضِلُّ ٱللَّهُ مَن يَشَآءُ وَيَہۡدِى مَن يَشَآءُ‌ۚ
Thus Allah (الله) leads astray whom He wills and guides whom He wills.
وَمَا يَعۡلَمُ جُنُودَ رَبِّكَ إِلَّا هُوَ‌ۚ
And none can know the hosts of your Lord but He.
وَمَا هِىَ إِلَّا ذِكۡرَىٰ لِلۡبَشَرِ
And this (Hell (جهنم)) is nothing else than a (warning) reminder to mankind.
are: Actions which lead to hellfire
74:[32-56] Not offering Salah, not feeding the poor, wasting time in vain talk and denying the Day of Judgment
74:32
كَلَّا وَٱلۡقَمَرِ
Nay, and by the moon,
74:33
وَٱلَّيۡلِ إِذۡ أَدۡبَرَ
And by the night when it withdraws,
74:34
وَٱلصُّبۡحِ إِذَآ أَسۡفَرَ
And by the dawn when it brightens,
74:35
إِنَّہَا لَإِحۡدَى ٱلۡكُبَرِ
Verily, it (Hell (جهنم), or their denial of the Prophet Muhammad (محمد) SAW , or the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة)) is but one of the greatest calamities.
74:36
نَذِيرً۬ا لِّلۡبَشَرِ
A warning to mankind,
74:37
لِمَن شَآءَ مِنكُمۡ أَن يَتَقَدَّمَ أَوۡ يَتَأَخَّرَ
To any of you that chooses to go forward (by working righteous deeds), or to remain behind (by commiting sins),
74:38
كُلُّ نَفۡسِۭ بِمَا كَسَبَتۡ رَهِينَةٌ
Every person is a pledge for what he has earned,
74:39
إِلَّآ أَصۡحَـٰبَ ٱلۡيَمِينِ
Except those on the Right, (i.e. the pious true believers of Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism);
74:40
فِى جَنَّـٰتٍ۬ يَتَسَآءَلُونَ
In Gardens (Paradise (الجنة)) they will ask one another,
74:41
عَنِ ٱلۡمُجۡرِمِينَ
About Al-Mujrimoon (polytheists, criminals, disbelievers, etc.),
74:42
مَا سَلَڪَكُمۡ فِى سَقَرَ
(And they will say to them):‘What has caused you to enter Hell (جهنم)?‘
74:43
قَالُواْ لَمۡ نَكُ مِنَ ٱلۡمُصَلِّينَ
They will say: ‘We were not of those who used to offer their Salat (prayers)
74:44
وَلَمۡ نَكُ نُطۡعِمُ ٱلۡمِسۡكِينَ
Nor we used to feed Al-Miskin (the poor);
74:45
وَڪُنَّا نَخُوضُ مَعَ ٱلۡخَآٮِٕضِينَ
And we used to talk falsehood (all that which Allah (الله) hated) with vain talkers.
74:46
وَكُنَّا نُكَذِّبُ بِيَوۡمِ ٱلدِّينِ
And we used to belie the Day of Recompense
74:47
حَتَّىٰٓ أَتَٮٰنَا ٱلۡيَقِينُ
Until there came to us (the death) that is certain.‘
74:48
فَمَا تَنفَعُهُمۡ شَفَـٰعَةُ ٱلشَّـٰفِعِينَ
So no intercession of intercessors will be of any use to them.
74:49
فَمَا لَهُمۡ عَنِ ٱلتَّذۡكِرَةِ مُعۡرِضِينَ
Then what is wrong with them (i.e. the disbelievers) that they turn away from (receiving) admonition?
74:50
كَأَنَّهُمۡ حُمُرٌ۬ مُّسۡتَنفِرَةٌ۬
As if they were frightened (wild) donkeys.
74:51
فَرَّتۡ مِن قَسۡوَرَةِۭ
Fleeing from a hunter, or a lion, or a beast of prey.
74:52
بَلۡ يُرِيدُ كُلُّ ٱمۡرِىٍٕ۬ مِّنۡہُمۡ أَن يُؤۡتَىٰ صُحُفً۬ا مُّنَشَّرَةً۬
Nay, everyone of them desires that he should be given pages spread out(coming from Allah (الله) with a writing that Islam (الإسلام) is the right religion, and Muhammad (محمد) SAW has come with the truth from Allah (الله) the Lord of the heavens and earth, etc.).
74:53
كَلَّا‌ۖ بَل لَّا يَخَافُونَ ٱلۡأَخِرَةَ
Nay! But they fear not the Hereafter (from Allah (الله)‘s punishment).
74:54
ڪَلَّآ إِنَّهُ ۥ تَذۡكِرَةٌ۬
Nay, verily, this (Quran (القرآن)) is an admonition,
74:55
فَمَن شَآءَ ذَڪَرَهُ ۥ
So whosoever will (let him read it), and receive admonition (from it)!
74:56
وَمَا يَذۡكُرُونَ إِلَّآ أَن يَشَآءَ ٱللَّهُ‌ۚ
And they will not receive admonition unless Allah (الله) wills;
هُوَ أَهۡلُ ٱلتَّقۡوَىٰ وَأَهۡلُ ٱلۡمَغۡفِرَةِ
He (Allah (الله)) is the One, deserving thatmankind should be afraid of, and should be dutiful to Him, and should not take any Ilah (God) along with Him, and He is the One Who forgives (sins).*********
