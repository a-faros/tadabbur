Al-Fil
The Elephant
105:[1-5] Allah has the power to defeat an army with elephants through the flock of birds
105:1
أَلَمۡ تَرَ كَيۡفَ فَعَلَ رَبُّكَ بِأَصۡحَـٰبِ ٱلۡفِيلِ
Have you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) not seen how your Lord dealt with the Owners of the Elephant?[The elephant army which came from Yemen under the command of Abrahah Al-Ashram intending to destroy the Ka‘bah at Makkah (مكة)].
105:2
أَلَمۡ يَجۡعَلۡ كَيۡدَهُمۡ فِى تَضۡلِيلٍ۬
Did He not make their plot go astray?
105:3
وَأَرۡسَلَ عَلَيۡہِمۡ طَيۡرًا أَبَابِيلَ
And sent against them birds, in flocks,
105:4
تَرۡمِيهِم بِحِجَارَةٍ۬ مِّن سِجِّيلٍ۬
Striking them with stones of Sijjîl.
105:5
فَجَعَلَهُمۡ كَعَصۡفٍ۬ مَّأۡڪُولِۭ
And made them like an empty field of stalks (of which the corn has been eaten up by cattle).*********
