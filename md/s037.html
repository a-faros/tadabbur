As-Saaffaat
Those Who Set The Ranks, Drawn Up In Ranks, Those Ranged in Ranks
37:[1-11] Allah testifies that your God is one God and that shaitãns do not have excess to the exalted assembly of angels
37:[12-21] Life in the hereafter and the Day of Judgement are real
37:1
وَٱلصَّـٰٓفَّـٰتِ صَفًّ۬ا
By those (angels) ranged in ranks (or rows).
37:2
فَٱلزَّٲجِرَٲتِ زَجۡرً۬ا
By those (angels) who drive the clouds in a good way.
37:3
فَٱلتَّـٰلِيَـٰتِ ذِكۡرًا
By those (angels) who bring the Book and the Quran from Allah to mankind.(Tafsir Ibn Katheer).
37:4
إِنَّ إِلَـٰهَكُمۡ لَوَٲحِدٌ۬
Verily your Ilah (God) is indeed One (i.e. Allah);
37:5
رَّبُّ ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٲتِ وَٱلۡأَرۡضِ وَمَا بَيۡنَہُمَا وَرَبُّ ٱلۡمَشَـٰرِقِ
Lord of the heavens and of the earth, and all that is between them, and Lord of every point of the suns risings.(None has the right to be worshipped but Allah).
37:6
إِنَّا زَيَّنَّا ٱلسَّمَآءَ ٱلدُّنۡيَا بِزِينَةٍ ٱلۡكَوَاكِبِ
Verily! We have adorned the near heaven with the stars (for beauty).
37:7
وَحِفۡظً۬ا مِّن كُلِّ شَيۡطَـٰنٍ۬ مَّارِدٍ۬
And to guard against every rebellious devil.
37:8
لَّا يَسَّمَّعُونَ إِلَى ٱلۡمَلَإِ ٱلۡأَعۡلَىٰ وَيُقۡذَفُونَ مِن كُلِّ جَانِبٍ۬
They cannot listen to the higher group (angels) for they are pelted from every side.
37:9
دُحُورً۬ا‌ۖ وَلَهُمۡ عَذَابٌ۬ وَاصِبٌ
Outcast, and theirs is a constant (or painful) torment.
37:10
إِلَّا مَنۡ خَطِفَ ٱلۡخَطۡفَةَ فَأَتۡبَعَهُ ۥ شِہَابٌ۬ ثَاقِبٌ۬
Except such as snatch away something by stealing and they are pursued by a flaming fire of piercing brightness.
37:11
فَٱسۡتَفۡتِہِمۡ أَهُمۡ أَشَدُّ خَلۡقًا أَم مَّنۡ خَلَقۡنَآ‌ۚ
Then ask them (i.e. these polytheists, O Muhammad SAW): ‘Are they stronger as creation,
or those (others like the heavens and the earth and the mountains, etc.) whom We have created?‘
إِنَّا خَلَقۡنَـٰهُم مِّن طِينٍ۬ لَّازِبِۭ
Verily, We created them of a sticky clay.
37:12
بَلۡ عَجِبۡتَ وَيَسۡخَرُونَ
Nay, you (O Muhammad SAW) wondered (at their insolence) while they mock (at you and at the Quran).
37:13
وَإِذَا ذُكِّرُواْ لَا يَذۡكُرُونَ
And when they are reminded, they pay no attention.
37:14
وَإِذَا رَأَوۡاْ ءَايَةً۬ يَسۡتَسۡخِرُونَ
And when they see an Ayah (a sign, a proof, or an evidence) from Allah, they mock at it.
37:15
وَقَالُوٓاْ إِنۡ هَـٰذَآ إِلَّا سِحۡرٌ۬ مُّبِينٌ
And they say: ‘This is nothing but evident magic!
37:16
أَءِذَا مِتۡنَا وَكُنَّا تُرَابً۬ا وَعِظَـٰمًا أَءِنَّا لَمَبۡعُوثُونَ
When we are dead and have become dust and bones, shall we (then) verily be resurrected?
37:17
أَوَءَابَآؤُنَا ٱلۡأَوَّلُونَ
And also our fathers of old?‘
37:18
قُلۡ نَعَمۡ وَأَنتُمۡ دَٲخِرُونَ
Say (O Muhammad SAW): ‘Yes, and you shall then be humiliated.‘
37:19
فَإِنَّمَا هِىَ زَجۡرَةٌ۬ وَٲحِدَةٌ۬ فَإِذَا هُمۡ يَنظُرُونَ
It will be a single Zajrah (shout (i.e. the second blowing of the Trumpet)), and behold, they will be staring!
37:20
وَقَالُواْ يَـٰوَيۡلَنَا هَـٰذَا يَوۡمُ ٱلدِّينِ
They will say: ‘Woe to us! This is the Day of Recompense!‘
37:21
هَـٰذَا يَوۡمُ ٱلۡفَصۡلِ ٱلَّذِى كُنتُم بِهِۦ تُكَذِّبُونَ
(It will be said):
This is the Day of Judgement which you used to deny.‘
37:[22-39] A scene from the Day of Judgement and a treat for the wrongdoers. A dialogue between the followers and the leaders who mislead them
37:[40-61] A view from the scenes of paradise. An example of conversation from a resident of paradise
37:[62-74] A scene from the scenes of hell
37:22
ٱحۡشُرُواْ ٱلَّذِينَ ظَلَمُواْ وَأَزۡوَٲجَهُمۡ وَمَا كَانُواْ يَعۡبُدُونَ
(It will be said to the angels):
Assemble those who did wrong, together with their companions (from the devils) and what they used to worship.
37:23
مِن دُونِ ٱللَّهِ فَٱهۡدُوهُمۡ إِلَىٰ صِرَٲطِ ٱلۡجَحِيمِ
‘Instead of Allah, and lead them on to the way of flaming Fire (Hell);
37:24
وَقِفُوهُمۡ‌ۖ إِنَّہُم مَّسۡـُٔولُونَ
But stop them, verily they are to be questioned.
37:25
مَا لَكُمۡ لَا تَنَاصَرُونَ
What is the matter with you?
Why do you not help one another (as you used to do in the world)?‘
37:26
بَلۡ هُمُ ٱلۡيَوۡمَ مُسۡتَسۡلِمُونَ
Nay, but that Day they shall surrender,
37:27
وَأَقۡبَلَ بَعۡضُهُمۡ عَلَىٰ بَعۡضٍ۬ يَتَسَآءَلُونَ
And they will turn to one another and question one another.
37:28
قَالُوٓاْ إِنَّكُمۡ كُنتُمۡ تَأۡتُونَنَا عَنِ ٱلۡيَمِينِ
They will say: ‘It was you who used to come to us from the right side.‘(i.e. from the right side of one of us and beautify for us every evil, order us for polytheism, and stop us from the truth i.e. Islamic Monotheism and from every good deed)
37:29
قَالُواْ بَل لَّمۡ تَكُونُواْ مُؤۡمِنِينَ
They will reply: ‘Nay, you yourselves were not believers.
37:30
وَمَا كَانَ لَنَا عَلَيۡكُم مِّن سُلۡطَـٰنِۭ‌ۖ
And we had no authority over you.
بَلۡ كُنتُمۡ قَوۡمً۬ا طَـٰغِينَ
Nay! But you were transgressing people (disobedient, polytheists, and disbelievers).
37:31
فَحَقَّ عَلَيۡنَا قَوۡلُ رَبِّنَآ‌ۖ إِنَّا لَذَآٮِٕقُونَ
So now the Word of our Lord has been justified against us, that we shall certainly (have to) taste (the torment).
37:32
فَأَغۡوَيۡنَـٰكُمۡ إِنَّا كُنَّا غَـٰوِينَ
So we led you astray because we were ourselves astray.‘
37:33
فَإِنَّہُمۡ يَوۡمَٮِٕذٍ۬ فِى ٱلۡعَذَابِ مُشۡتَرِكُونَ
Then verily, that Day, they will (all) share in the torment.
37:34
إِنَّا كَذَٲلِكَ نَفۡعَلُ بِٱلۡمُجۡرِمِينَ
Certainly, that is how We deal with Al Mujrimoon (polytheists, sinners, criminals, the disobedient to Allah, etc.).
37:35
إِنَّہُمۡ كَانُوٓاْ إِذَا قِيلَ لَهُمۡ لَآ إِلَـٰهَ إِلَّا ٱللَّهُ يَسۡتَكۡبِرُونَ
Truly, when it was said to them: La ilaha ill-Allah ‘(none has the right to be worshipped but Allah),‘ they puffed themselves up with pride (i.e. denied it).
37:36
وَيَقُولُونَ أَٮِٕنَّا لَتَارِكُوٓاْ ءَالِهَتِنَا لِشَاعِرٍ۬ مَّجۡنُونِۭ
And (they) said: ‘Are we going to abandon our aliha (gods) for the sake of a mad poet?
37:37
بَلۡ جَآءَ بِٱلۡحَقِّ وَصَدَّقَ ٱلۡمُرۡسَلِينَ
Nay! he (Muhammad SAW) has come with the truth (i.e. Allahs Religion - Islamic Monotheism and this Quran) and he confirms the Messengers (before him who brought Allah‘s religion - Islamic Monotheism).
37:38
إِنَّكُمۡ لَذَآٮِٕقُواْ ٱلۡعَذَابِ ٱلۡأَلِيمِ
Verily, you (pagans of Makkah) are going to taste the painful torment;
37:39
وَمَا تُجۡزَوۡنَ إِلَّا مَا كُنتُمۡ تَعۡمَلُونَ
And you will be requited nothing except for what you used to do (evil deeds, sins, and Allah‘s disobedience which you used to do in this world);
37:40
إِلَّا عِبَادَ ٱللَّهِ ٱلۡمُخۡلَصِينَ
Save the chosen slaves of Allah (faithful, obedient, true believers of Islamic Monotheism).
37:41
أُوْلَـٰٓٮِٕكَ لَهُمۡ رِزۡقٌ۬ مَّعۡلُومٌ۬
For them there will be a known provision (in Paradise).
37:42
فَوَٲكِهُ‌ۖ وَهُم مُّكۡرَمُونَ
Fruits;
and they shall be honoured,
37:43
فِى جَنَّـٰتِ ٱلنَّعِيمِ
In the Gardens of delight (Paradise),
37:44
عَلَىٰ سُرُرٍ۬ مُّتَقَـٰبِلِينَ
Facing one another on thrones,
37:45
يُطَافُ عَلَيۡہِم بِكَأۡسٍ۬ مِّن مَّعِينِۭ
Round them will be passed a cup of pure wine;
37:46
بَيۡضَآءَ لَذَّةٍ۬ لِّلشَّـٰرِبِينَ
White, delicious to the drinkers,
37:47
لَا فِيہَا غَوۡلٌ۬ وَلَا هُمۡ عَنۡہَا يُنزَفُونَ
Neither they will have Ghoul (any kind of hurt, abdominal pain, headache, a sin, etc.) from that, nor will they suffer intoxication therefrom.
37:48
وَعِندَهُمۡ قَـٰصِرَٲتُ ٱلطَّرۡفِ عِينٌ۬
And with them will be chaste females, restraining their glances (desiring none except their husbands), with wide and beautiful eyes.
37:49
كَأَنَّہُنَّ بَيۡضٌ۬ مَّكۡنُونٌ۬
(Delicate and pure) as if they were (hidden) eggs (well) preserved.
37:50
فَأَقۡبَلَ بَعۡضُہُمۡ عَلَىٰ بَعۡضٍ۬ يَتَسَآءَلُونَ
Then they will turn to one another, mutually questioning.
37:51
قَالَ قَآٮِٕلٌ۬ مِّنۡہُمۡ إِنِّى كَانَ لِى قَرِينٌ۬
A speaker of them will say: ‘Verily, I had a companion (in the world),
37:52
يَقُولُ أَءِنَّكَ لَمِنَ ٱلۡمُصَدِّقِينَ
Who used to say: ‘Are you among those who believe (in resurrection after death).
37:53
أَءِذَا مِتۡنَا وَكُنَّا تُرَابً۬ا وَعِظَـٰمًا أَءِنَّا لَمَدِينُونَ
(That) when we die and become dust and bones, shall we indeed (be raised up) to receive reward or punishment (according to our deeds)?‘
37:54
قَالَ هَلۡ أَنتُم مُّطَّلِعُونَ
(The man) said: ‘Will you look down?‘
37:55
فَٱطَّلَعَ فَرَءَاهُ فِى سَوَآءِ ٱلۡجَحِيمِ
So he looked down and saw him in the midst of the Fire.
37:56
قَالَ تَٱللَّهِ إِن كِدتَّ لَتُرۡدِينِ
He said: ‘By Allah! You have nearly ruined me.
37:57
وَلَوۡلَا نِعۡمَةُ رَبِّى لَكُنتُ مِنَ ٱلۡمُحۡضَرِينَ
Had it not been for the Grace of my Lord, I would certainly have been among those brought forth (to Hell).‘
37:58
أَفَمَا نَحۡنُ بِمَيِّتِينَ
(Allah informs about that true believer that he said):
Are we then not to die (any more)?
37:59
إِلَّا مَوۡتَتَنَا ٱلۡأُولَىٰ وَمَا نَحۡنُ بِمُعَذَّبِينَ
‘Except our first death, and we shall not be punished?‘ (after we have entered Paradise)
37:60
إِنَّ هَـٰذَا لَهُوَ ٱلۡفَوۡزُ ٱلۡعَظِيمُ
Truly, this is the supreme success!
37:61
لِمِثۡلِ هَـٰذَا فَلۡيَعۡمَلِ ٱلۡعَـٰمِلُونَ
For the like of this let the workers work.
37:62
أَذَٲلِكَ خَيۡرٌ۬ نُّزُلاً أَمۡ شَجَرَةُ ٱلزَّقُّومِ
Is that (Paradise) better entertainment or the tree of Zaqqoom (a horrible tree in Hell)?
37:63
إِنَّا جَعَلۡنَـٰهَا فِتۡنَةً۬ لِّلظَّـٰلِمِينَ
Truly We have made it (as) a trail for the Zalimoon (polytheists, disbelievers, wrong-doers, etc.).
37:64
إِنَّهَا شَجَرَةٌ۬ تَخۡرُجُ فِىٓ أَصۡلِ ٱلۡجَحِيمِ
Verily, it is a tree that springs out of the bottom of Hell-fire,
37:65
طَلۡعُهَا كَأَنَّهُ ۥ رُءُوسُ ٱلشَّيَـٰطِينِ
The shoots of its fruit-stalks are like the heads of Shayatin (devils);
37:66
فَإِنَّہُمۡ لَأَكِلُونَ مِنۡہَا فَمَالِـُٔونَ مِنۡہَا ٱلۡبُطُونَ
Truly, they will eat thereof and fill their bellies therewith.
37:67
ثُمَّ إِنَّ لَهُمۡ عَلَيۡہَا لَشَوۡبً۬ا مِّنۡ حَمِيمٍ۬
Then on the top of that they will be given boiling water to drink so that it becomes a mixture (of boiling water and Zaqqoom in their bellies).
37:68
ثُمَّ إِنَّ مَرۡجِعَهُمۡ لَإِلَى ٱلۡجَحِيمِ
Then thereafter, verily, their return is to the flaming fire of Hell.
37:69
إِنَّہُمۡ أَلۡفَوۡاْ ءَابَآءَهُمۡ ضَآلِّينَ
Verily, they found their fathers on the wrong path;
37:70
فَهُمۡ عَلَىٰٓ ءَاثَـٰرِهِمۡ يُہۡرَعُونَ
So they (too) made haste to follow in their footsteps!
37:71
وَلَقَدۡ ضَلَّ قَبۡلَهُمۡ أَڪۡثَرُ ٱلۡأَوَّلِينَ
And indeed most of the men of old went astray before them;
37:72
وَلَقَدۡ أَرۡسَلۡنَا فِيہِم مُّنذِرِينَ
And indeed We sent among them warners (Messengers);
37:73
فَٱنظُرۡ ڪَيۡفَ كَانَ عَـٰقِبَةُ ٱلۡمُنذَرِينَ
Then see what was the end of those who were warned (but heeded not).
37:74
إِلَّا عِبَادَ ٱللَّهِ ٱلۡمُخۡلَصِينَ
Except the chosen slaves of Allah (faithful, obedient, and true believers of Islamic Monotheism).

37:[83-98] The story of the Prophet Ibrãhïm, ‘The Friend of Allah‘
37:[99-113] The Prophet Ibrãhïm was asked to offer his only son in sacrifice as a test and he fulfilled it
37:75
وَلَقَدۡ نَادَٮٰنَا نُوحٌ۬ فَلَنِعۡمَ ٱلۡمُجِيبُونَ
And indeed Nooh (Noah) invoked Us, and We are the Best of those who answer (the request).
37:76
وَنَجَّيۡنَـٰهُ وَأَهۡلَهُ ۥ مِنَ ٱلۡكَرۡبِ ٱلۡعَظِيمِ
And We rescued him and his family from the great distress (i.e. drowning),
37:77
وَجَعَلۡنَا ذُرِّيَّتَهُ ۥ هُمُ ٱلۡبَاقِينَ
And, his progeny, them We made the survivors (i.e. Shem, Ham and Japheth).
37:78
وَتَرَكۡنَا عَلَيۡهِ فِى ٱلۡأَخِرِينَ
And left for him (a goodly remembrance) among generations to come in later times:
37:79
سَلَـٰمٌ عَلَىٰ نُوحٍ۬ فِى ٱلۡعَـٰلَمِينَ
Salamun (peace) be upon Nooh (Noah) (from Us) among the Alameen (mankind, jinns and all that exists)!‘
37:80
إِنَّا كَذَٲلِكَ نَجۡزِى ٱلۡمُحۡسِنِينَ
Verily, thus We reward the Muhsinoon (good-doers - see V.2:112).
37:81
إِنَّهُ ۥ مِنۡ عِبَادِنَا ٱلۡمُؤۡمِنِينَ
Verily, he (Nooh (Noah)) was one of Our believing slaves.
37:82
ثُمَّ أَغۡرَقۡنَا ٱلۡأَخَرِينَ
Then We drowned the other (disbelievers and polytheists, etc.).
37:83
وَإِنَّ مِن شِيعَتِهِۦ لَإِبۡرَٲهِيمَ
And, verily, among those who followed his (Noohs (Noah)) way (Islamic Monotheism) was Abraham (Ibrahim).
37:84
إِذۡ جَآءَ رَبَّهُ ۥ بِقَلۡبٍ۬ سَلِيمٍ
When he came to his Lord with a pure heart (attached to Allah Alone and none else, worshipping none but Allah Alone true Islamic Monotheism, pure from the filth of polytheism).
37:85
إِذۡ قَالَ لِأَبِيهِ وَقَوۡمِهِۦ مَاذَا تَعۡبُدُونَ
When he said to his father and to his people: ‘What is it that which you worship?
37:86
أَٮِٕفۡكًا ءَالِهَةً۬ دُونَ ٱللَّهِ تُرِيدُونَ
Is it a falsehood aliha (gods) other than Allah that you seek?
37:87
فَمَا ظَنُّكُم بِرَبِّ ٱلۡعَـٰلَمِينَ
Then what do you think about the Lord of the Alameen (mankind, jinns, and all that exists)?‘
37:88
فَنَظَرَ نَظۡرَةً۬ فِى ٱلنُّجُومِ
Then he cast a glance at the stars (to deceive them),
37:89
فَقَالَ إِنِّى سَقِيمٌ۬
And he said: ‘Verily, I am sick (with plague. He did this trick to remain in their temple of idols to destroy them and not to accompany them to the pagans feast).‘
37:90
فَتَوَلَّوۡاْ عَنۡهُ مُدۡبِرِينَ
So they turned away from him, and departed (for fear of the disease).
37:91
فَرَاغَ إِلَىٰٓ ءَالِهَتِہِمۡ فَقَالَ أَلَا تَأۡكُلُونَ
Then he turned to their aliha (gods) and said: ‘Will you not eat (of the offering before you)?
37:92
مَا لَكُمۡ لَا تَنطِقُونَ
What is the matter with you that you speak not?‘
37:93
فَرَاغَ عَلَيۡہِمۡ ضَرۡبَۢا بِٱلۡيَمِينِ
Then he turned upon them, striking (them) with (his) right hand.
