Az-Zalzala
The Earthquake
99:[1-8] The earth will report whatever happened on her and men shall be shown their Books of Deeds
99:1
إِذَا زُلۡزِلَتِ ٱلۡأَرۡضُ زِلۡزَالَهَا
When the earth is shaken with its (final) earthquake.
99:2
وَأَخۡرَجَتِ ٱلۡأَرۡضُ أَثۡقَالَهَا
And when the earth throws out its burdens,
99:3
وَقَالَ ٱلۡإِنسَـٰنُ مَا لَهَا
And man will say: ‘What is the matter with it?‘
99:4
يَوۡمَٮِٕذٍ۬ تُحَدِّثُ أَخۡبَارَهَا
That Day it will declare its information (about all what happened over it of good or evil).
99:5
بِأَنَّ رَبَّكَ أَوۡحَىٰ لَهَا
Because your Lord has inspired it.
99:6
يَوۡمَٮِٕذٍ۬ يَصۡدُرُ ٱلنَّاسُ أَشۡتَاتً۬ا لِّيُرَوۡاْ أَعۡمَـٰلَهُمۡ
That Day mankind will proceed in scattered groups that they may be shown their deeds.
99:7
فَمَن يَعۡمَلۡ مِثۡقَالَ ذَرَّةٍ خَيۡرً۬ا يَرَهُ ۥ
So whosoever does good equal to the weight of an atom (or a small ant), shall see it.
99:8
وَمَن يَعۡمَلۡ مِثۡقَالَ ذَرَّةٍ۬ شَرًّ۬ا يَرَهُ ۥ
And whosoever does evil equal to the weight of an atom (or a small ant), shall see it.*********
