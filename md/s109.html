Al-Kaafiroon
The Disbelievers, The Kuffar, Those Who Deny the Truth
109:[1-6] The commandment not to compromise in the matters of religion
109:1
قُلۡ يَـٰٓأَيُّہَا ٱلۡڪَـٰفِرُونَ
Say (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW to these Mushrikûn and Kâfirûn): ‘O Al-Kâfirûn(disbelievers in Allâh, in His Oneness, in His Angel (الملائكة)s, in His Books, in His Messengers, in the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة), and in Al-Qadar, etc.)!
109:2
لَآ أَعۡبُدُ مَا تَعۡبُدُونَ
I worship not that which you worship,
109:3
وَلَآ أَنتُمۡ عَـٰبِدُونَ مَآ أَعۡبُدُ
Nor will you worship that which I worship.
109:4
وَلَآ أَنَا۟ عَابِدٌ۬ مَّا عَبَدتُّمۡ
And I shall not worship that which you are worshipping.
109:5
وَلَآ أَنتُمۡ عَـٰبِدُونَ مَآ أَعۡبُدُ
Nor will you worship that which I worship.
109:6
لَكُمۡ دِينُكُمۡ وَلِىَ دِينِ
To you be your religion, and to me my religion (Islâmic Monotheism).‘*********
