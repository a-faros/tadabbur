Name
Def
1:[1-7] Supplication to Allah for guidance taught by Allah Himself.
1:[1-4] Allah, the Rabb, description of a master.
1:[5-5] You (Alone) we worship, and You (Alone) we ask for help (for each and everything).
1:[6-7] The initial help we (his slave) request for.
1:1
بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَنِ الرَّحِيمِ
In the Name of Allah, the Most Beneficent (Ar-Rahmaan), the Most Merciful (Ar-Raheem).
1:2
الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ رَبِّ الْعَالَمِينَ
All the praises and thanks be to Allah, the Lord of the Alameen (mankind, jinns and all that exists).
1:3
الرَّحْمَنِ الرَّحِيمِ
The Most Beneficent (Ar-Rahmaan), the Most Merciful (Ar-Raheem).
1:4
مَالِكِ يَوْمِ الدِّينِ
The Only Owner (and the Only Ruling Judge) of the Day of Recompense (i.e. the Day of Resurrection)
1:5
إِيَّاكَ نَعْبُدُ وَإِيَّاكَ نَسْتَعِينُ
You (Alone) we worship, and You (Alone) we ask for help (for each and everything).
1:6
اهْدِنَا الصِّرَاطَ الْمُسْتَقِيمَ
Guide us to the Straight Way
1:7
صِرَاطَ الَّذِينَ أَنْعَمْتَ عَلَيْهِمْ
The Way of those on whom You have bestowed Your Grace ,
غَيْرِ الْمَغْضُوبِ عَلَيْهِمْ وَلَا الضَّالِّينَ
not (the way) of those who earned Your Anger (such as the Jews), nor of those who went astray (such as the Christians).
