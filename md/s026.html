Ash-Shu'araa
The Poets
26:[1-9] Dedication of the Prophet Muhammad (pbuh) to the guidance of mankind
26:1
طسٓمٓ
Ta Seen Meem.
(These letters are one of the miracles of the Quran, and none but Allah (Alone) knows their meanings.)
26:2
تِلۡكَ ءَايَـٰتُ ٱلۡكِتَـٰبِ ٱلۡمُبِينِ
These are the Verses of the manifest Book (this Quran, which was promised by Allah in the Taurat (Torah) and the Injil (Injeel), makes things clear).
26:3
لَعَلَّكَ بَـٰخِعٌ۬ نَّفۡسَكَ أَلَّا يَكُونُواْ مُؤۡمِنِينَ
It may be that you (O Muhammad SAW) are going to kill yourself with grief, that they do not become believers (in your Risalah (Messengership) and in your Message of Islamic Monotheism).
26:4
إِن نَّشَأۡ نُنَزِّلۡ عَلَيۡہِم مِّنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ ءَايَةً۬ فَظَلَّتۡ أَعۡنَـٰقُهُمۡ لَهَا خَـٰضِعِينَ
If We will, We could send down to them from the heaven a sign, to which they would bend their necks in humility.
26:5
وَمَا يَأۡتِيہِم مِّن ذِكۡرٍ۬ مِّنَ ٱلرَّحۡمَـٰنِ مُحۡدَثٍ إِلَّا كَانُواْ عَنۡهُ مُعۡرِضِينَ
And never comes there unto them a Reminder as a recent revelation from the Most Beneficent (Ar-Rahmaan), but they turn away therefrom.
26:6
فَقَدۡ كَذَّبُواْ فَسَيَأۡتِيہِمۡ أَنۢبَـٰٓؤُاْ مَا كَانُواْ بِهِۦ يَسۡتَہۡزِءُونَ
So they have indeed denied (the truth this Quran), then the news of what they mocked at, will come to them.
26:7
أَوَلَمۡ يَرَوۡاْ إِلَى ٱلۡأَرۡضِ كَمۡ أَنۢبَتۡنَا فِيہَا مِن كُلِّ زَوۡجٍ۬ كَرِيمٍ
Do they not observe the earth, how much of every good kind We cause to grow therein?
26:8
إِنَّ فِى ذَٲلِكَ لَأَيَةً۬‌ۖ وَمَا كَانَ أَكۡثَرُهُم مُّؤۡمِنِينَ
Verily, in this is an Ayah (proof or sign), yet most of them (polytheists, pagans, etc., who do not believe in Resurrection) are not believers.
26:9
وَإِنَّ رَبَّكَ لَهُوَ ٱلۡعَزِيزُ ٱلرَّحِيمُ
And verily, your Lord! He is truly the All-Mighty, the Most Merciful (Ar-Raheem).
26:[10-33] The assignment of Musa as a Rasool and his dialogue with Fir‘on. The miracles of the Prophet Musa
26:10
وَإِذۡ نَادَىٰ رَبُّكَ مُوسَىٰٓ أَنِ ٱئۡتِ ٱلۡقَوۡمَ ٱلظَّـٰلِمِينَ
And (remember) when your Lord called Musa (Moosa) (saying): ‘Go to the people who are Zalimoon (polytheists and wrong-doing),
26:11
قَوۡمَ فِرۡعَوۡنَ‌ۚ
The people of Firaun (Pharaoh).
أَلَا يَتَّقُونَ
Will they not fear Allah and become righteous?‘
26:12
قَالَ رَبِّ إِنِّىٓ أَخَافُ أَن يُكَذِّبُونِ
He said: ‘My Lord! Verily, I fear that they will belie me,
26:13
وَيَضِيقُ صَدۡرِى وَلَا يَنطَلِقُ لِسَانِى فَأَرۡسِلۡ إِلَىٰ هَـٰرُونَ
And my breast straitens, and my tongue expresses not well. So send for Haroon (Aaron) (to come along with me).
26:14
وَلَهُمۡ عَلَىَّ ذَنۢبٌ۬ فَأَخَافُ أَن يَقۡتُلُونِ
And they have a charge of crime against me, and I fear they will kill me.‘
26:15
قَالَ كَلَّا‌
Allah said: ‘Nay!
فَٱذۡهَبَا بِـَٔايَـٰتِنَآ‌ۖإِنَّا مَعَكُم مُّسۡتَمِعُونَ
Go you both with Our Signs. Verily! We shall be with you, listening.
26:16
فَأۡتِيَا فِرۡعَوۡنَ فَقُولَآ إِنَّا رَسُولُ رَبِّ ٱلۡعَـٰلَمِينَ
And when you both come to Firaun (Pharaoh), say: We are the Messengers of the Lord of the Alameen (mankind, jinns and all that exists),
26:17
أَنۡ أَرۡسِلۡ مَعَنَا بَنِىٓ إِسۡرَٲٓءِيلَ
So allow the Children of Israel to go with us. ‘
26:18
قَالَ أَلَمۡ نُرَبِّكَ فِينَا وَلِيدً۬اوَلَبِثۡتَ فِينَا مِنۡ عُمُرِكَ سِنِينَ
(Firaun (Pharaoh)) said (to Musa (Moosa)):
Did we not bring you up among us as a child?
And you did dwell many years of your life with us.
26:19
وَفَعَلۡتَ فَعۡلَتَكَ ٱلَّتِى فَعَلۡتَ وَأَنتَ مِنَ ٱلۡكَـٰفِرِينَ
‘And you did your deed, which you did (i.e. the crime of killing a man). And you are one of the ingrates.‘
26:20
قَالَ فَعَلۡتُهَآ إِذً۬ا وَأَنَا۟ مِنَ ٱلضَّآلِّينَ
Musa (Moosa) said: ‘I did it then, when I was an ignorant (as regards my Lord and His Message).
26:21
فَفَرَرۡتُ مِنكُمۡ لَمَّا خِفۡتُكُمۡ
So I fled from you when I feared you.
فَوَهَبَ لِى رَبِّى حُكۡمً۬ا وَجَعَلَنِى مِنَ ٱلۡمُرۡسَلِينَ
But my Lord has granted me Hukman (i.e. religious knowledge, right judgement of the affairs and Prophethood), and appointed me as one of the Messengers.
26:22
وَتِلۡكَ نِعۡمَةٌ۬ تَمُنُّہَا عَلَىَّ أَنۡ عَبَّدتَّ بَنِىٓ إِسۡرَٲٓءِيلَ
And this is the past favour with which you reproach me, that you have enslaved the Children of Israel.‘
26:23
قَالَ فِرۡعَوۡنُ وَمَا رَبُّ ٱلۡعَـٰلَمِينَ
Firaun (Pharaoh) said: ‘And what is the Lord of the Alameen (mankind, jinns and all that exists)?‘
26:24
قَالَ رَبُّ ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٲتِ وَٱلۡأَرۡضِ وَمَا بَيۡنَهُمَآ‌ۖ
Musa (Moosa) said: ‘Lord of the heavens and the earth, and all that is between them,
إِن كُنتُم مُّوقِنِينَ
if you seek to be convinced with certainty.‘
26:25
قَالَ لِمَنۡ حَوۡلَهُ ۥۤ أَلَا تَسۡتَمِعُونَ
Firaun (Pharaoh) said to those around: ‘Do you not hear (what he says)?‘
26:26
قَالَ رَبُّكُمۡ وَرَبُّ ءَابَآٮِٕكُمُ ٱلۡأَوَّلِينَ
Musa (Moosa) said: ‘Your Lord and the Lord of your ancient fathers!‘
26:27
قَالَ إِنَّ رَسُولَكُمُ ٱلَّذِىٓ أُرۡسِلَ إِلَيۡكُمۡ لَمَجۡنُونٌ۬
Firaun (Pharaoh) said: ‘Verily, your Messenger who has been sent to you is a madman!‘
26:28
قَالَ رَبُّ ٱلۡمَشۡرِقِ وَٱلۡمَغۡرِبِ وَمَا بَيۡنَہُمَآ‌ۖ
Musa (Moosa) said: ‘Lord of the east and the west, and all that is between them,
إِن كُنتُمۡ تَعۡقِلُونَ
if you did but understand!‘
26:29
قَالَ لَٮِٕنِ ٱتَّخَذۡتَ إِلَـٰهًا غَيۡرِى لَأَجۡعَلَنَّكَ مِنَ ٱلۡمَسۡجُونِينَ
Firaun (Pharaoh) said: ‘If you choose an ilah (god) other than me, I will certainly put you among the prisoners.‘
26:30
قَالَ أَوَلَوۡ جِئۡتُكَ بِشَىۡءٍ۬ مُّبِينٍ۬
Musa (Moosa) said: ‘Even if I bring you something manifest (and convincing)?‘
26:31
قَالَ فَأۡتِ بِهِۦۤ إِن ڪُنتَ مِنَ ٱلصَّـٰدِقِينَ
Firaun (Pharaoh) said: ‘Bring it forth then, if you are of the truthful!‘
26:32
فَأَلۡقَىٰ عَصَاهُ فَإِذَا هِىَ ثُعۡبَانٌ۬ مُّبِينٌ۬
So (Musa (Moosa)) threw his stick, and behold, it was a serpent, manifest.
26:33
وَنَزَعَ يَدَهُ ۥ فَإِذَا هِىَ بَيۡضَآءُ لِلنَّـٰظِرِينَ
And he drew out his hand, and behold, it was white to all beholders!
26:[34-51] Fir‘on took the Prophet Musa‘s miracles as magic and summoned the magicians to compete with him. After witnessing a miracle, all the magicians embraced Islam
26:34
قَالَ لِلۡمَلَإِ حَوۡلَهُ ۥۤ إِنَّ هَـٰذَا لَسَـٰحِرٌ عَلِيمٌ۬
(Firaun (Pharaoh)) said to the chiefs around him:
Verily! This is indeed a well-versed sorcerer.
26:35
يُرِيدُ أَن يُخۡرِجَكُم مِّنۡ أَرۡضِڪُم بِسِحۡرِهِۦ
‘He wants to drive you out of your land by his sorcery, then what is it your counsel,
فَمَاذَا تَأۡمُرُونَ
and what do you command?‘
26:36
قَالُوٓاْ أَرۡجِهۡ وَأَخَاهُ وَٱبۡعَثۡ فِى ٱلۡمَدَآٮِٕنِ حَـٰشِرِينَ
They said: ‘Put him off and his brother (for a while), and send callers to the cities;
26:37
يَأۡتُوكَ بِڪُلِّ سَحَّارٍ عَلِيمٍ۬
To bring up to you every well-versed sorcerer.‘
26:38
فَجُمِعَ ٱلسَّحَرَةُ لِمِيقَـٰتِ يَوۡمٍ۬ مَّعۡلُومٍ۬
So the sorcerers were assembled at a fixed time on a day appointed.
26:39
وَقِيلَ لِلنَّاسِ هَلۡ أَنتُم مُّجۡتَمِعُونَ
And it was said to the people: ‘Are you (too) going to assemble?
26:40
لَعَلَّنَا نَتَّبِعُ ٱلسَّحَرَةَ إِن كَانُواْ هُمُ ٱلۡغَـٰلِبِينَ
That we may follow the sorcerers (who were on Firauns (Pharaoh) religion of disbelief) if they are the winners.‘
26:41
فَلَمَّا جَآءَ ٱلسَّحَرَةُ قَالُواْ لِفِرۡعَوۡنَ أَٮِٕنَّ لَنَا لَأَجۡرًا إِن كُنَّا نَحۡنُ ٱلۡغَـٰلِبِينَ
So when the sorcerers arrived, they said to Firaun (Pharaoh): ‘Will there surely be a reward for us if we are the winners?‘
26:42
قَالَ نَعَمۡ وَإِنَّكُمۡ إِذً۬ا لَّمِنَ ٱلۡمُقَرَّبِينَ
He said: ‘Yes, and you shall then verily be of those brought near (to myself).‘
26:43
قَالَ لَهُم مُّوسَىٰٓ أَلۡقُواْ مَآ أَنتُم مُّلۡقُونَ
Musa (Moosa) said to them: ‘Throw what you are going to throw!‘
26:44
فَأَلۡقَوۡاْ حِبَالَهُمۡ وَعِصِيَّهُمۡ وَقَالُواْ بِعِزَّةِ فِرۡعَوۡنَ إِنَّا لَنَحۡنُ ٱلۡغَـٰلِبُونَ
So they threw their ropes and their sticks, and said: ‘By the might of Firaun (Pharaoh), it is we who will certainly win!‘
26:45
فَأَلۡقَىٰ مُوسَىٰ عَصَاهُ فَإِذَا هِىَ تَلۡقَفُ مَا يَأۡفِكُونَ
Then Musa (Moosa) threw his stick, and behold, it swallowed up all the falsehoods which they showed!
26:46
فَأُلۡقِىَ ٱلسَّحَرَةُ سَـٰجِدِينَ
And the sorcerers fell down prostrate.
26:47
قَالُوٓاْ ءَامَنَّا بِرَبِّ ٱلۡعَـٰلَمِينَ
Saying: ‘We believe in the Lord of the Alameen (mankind, jinns and all that exists).
26:48
رَبِّ مُوسَىٰ وَهَـٰرُونَ
The Lord of Musa (Moosa) and Haroon (Aaron).‘
26:49
قَالَ ءَامَنتُمۡ لَهُ ۥ قَبۡلَ أَنۡ ءَاذَنَ لَكُمۡ‌ۖ
(Firaun (Pharaoh)) said:
You have believed in him before I give you leave.
إِنَّهُ ۥ لَكَبِيرُكُمُ ٱلَّذِى عَلَّمَكُمُ ٱلسِّحۡرَفَلَسَوۡفَ تَعۡلَمُونَ‌ۚ
Surely, he indeed is your chief, who has taught you magic! So verily, you shall come to know.
لَأُقَطِّعَنَّ أَيۡدِيَكُمۡ وَأَرۡجُلَكُم مِّنۡ خِلَـٰفٍ۬ وَلَأُصَلِّبَنَّكُمۡ أَجۡمَعِينَ
Verily, I will cut off your hands and your feet on opposite sides, and I will crucify you all.‘
26:50
قَالُواْ لَا ضَيۡرَ‌ۖ إِنَّآ إِلَىٰ رَبِّنَا مُنقَلِبُونَ
They said: ‘No harm! Surely, to our Lord (Allah) we are to return;
26:51
إِنَّا نَطۡمَعُ أَن يَغۡفِرَ لَنَا رَبُّنَا خَطَـٰيَـٰنَآ أَن كُنَّآ أَوَّلَ ٱلۡمُؤۡمِنِينَ
Verily! We really hope that our Lord will forgive us our sins, as we are the first of the believers (in Musa (Moosa) and in the Monotheism which he has brought from Allah).‘
26:[52-68] Deliverance of the Children of Israel and the destruction of Fir‘on and his chiefs
26:52
وَأَوۡحَيۡنَآ إِلَىٰ مُوسَىٰٓ أَنۡ أَسۡرِ بِعِبَادِىٓ إِنَّكُم مُّتَّبَعُونَ
And We inspired Musa (Moosa), saying: ‘Take away My slaves by night, verily, you will be pursued.‘
26:53
فَأَرۡسَلَ فِرۡعَوۡنُ فِى ٱلۡمَدَآٮِٕنِ حَـٰشِرِينَ
Then Firaun (Pharaoh) sent callers to (all) the cities.إِنَّ هَـٰٓؤُلَآءِ لَشِرۡذِمَةٌ۬ قَلِيلُونَ إِنَّ هَـٰٓؤُلَآءِ لَشِرۡذِمَةٌ۬ قَلِيلُونَ
26:54
إِنَّ هَـٰٓؤُلَآءِ لَشِرۡذِمَةٌ۬ قَلِيلُونَ
(Saying): ‘Verily! These indeed are but a small band.
26:55
وَإِنَّہُمۡ لَنَا لَغَآٮِٕظُونَ
And verily, they have done what has enraged us;
26:56
وَإِنَّا لَجَمِيعٌ حَـٰذِرُونَ
But we are host all assembled, amply fore-warned.‘
26:57
فَأَخۡرَجۡنَـٰهُم مِّن جَنَّـٰتٍ۬ وَعُيُونٍ۬
So, We expelled them from gardens and springs,
26:58
وَكُنُوزٍ۬ وَمَقَامٍ۬ كَرِيمٍ۬
Treasures, and every kind of honourable place.
26:59
كَذَٲلِكَ وَأَوۡرَثۡنَـٰهَا بَنِىٓ إِسۡرَٲٓءِيلَ
Thus (We turned them (Pharaohs people) out) and We caused the Children of Israel to inherit them.
26:60
فَأَتۡبَعُوهُم مُّشۡرِقِينَ
So they pursued them at sunrise.
26:61
فَلَمَّا تَرَٲٓءَا ٱلۡجَمۡعَانِ قَالَ أَصۡحَـٰبُ مُوسَىٰٓ إِنَّا لَمُدۡرَكُونَ
And when the two hosts saw each other, the companions of Musa (Moosa) said: ‘We are sure to be overtaken.‘
26:62
قَالَ كَلَّآ‌ۖ إِنَّ مَعِىَ رَبِّى سَيَہۡدِينِ
(Musa (Moosa)) said: ‘Nay, verily! With me is my Lord, He will guide me.‘
26:63
فَأَوۡحَيۡنَآ إِلَىٰ مُوسَىٰٓ أَنِ ٱضۡرِب بِّعَصَاكَ ٱلۡبَحۡرَ‌ۖ
Then We inspired Musa (Moosa) (saying): ‘Strike the sea with your stick.‘
فَٱنفَلَقَ فَكَانَ كُلُّ فِرۡقٍ۬ كَٱلطَّوۡدِ ٱلۡعَظِيمِ
And it parted, and each separate part (of that sea water) became like the huge, firm mass of a mountain.
26:64
وَأَزۡلَفۡنَا ثَمَّ ٱلۡأَخَرِينَ
Then We brought near the others (Firauns (Pharaoh) party) to that place.
26:65
وَأَنجَيۡنَا مُوسَىٰ وَمَن مَّعَهُ ۥۤ أَجۡمَعِينَ
And We saved Musa (Moosa) and all those with him.
26:66
ثُمَّ أَغۡرَقۡنَا ٱلۡأَخَرِينَ
Then We drowned the others.
26:67
إِنَّ فِى ذَٲلِكَ لَأَيَةً۬‌ۖ وَمَا كَانَ أَكۡثَرُهُم مُّؤۡمِنِينَ
Verily! In this is indeed a sign (or a proof), yet most of them are not believers.
26:68
وَإِنَّ رَبَّكَ لَهُوَ ٱلۡعَزِيزُ ٱلرَّحِيمُ
And verily, your Lord! He is truly the All-Mighty, the Most Merciful (Ar-Raheem).
26:[69-104] The story of the Prophet Ibrãhïm and his arguments against idol worshipping. The prayer of the Prophet Ibrãhïm for this life and the life of hereafter. The mushrikïn and their gods both will be toppled into hell.
26:69
وَٱتۡلُ عَلَيۡهِمۡ نَبَأَ إِبۡرَٲهِيمَ
And recite to them the story of Abraham (Ibrahim).
26:70
إِذۡ قَالَ لِأَبِيهِ وَقَوۡمِهِۦ مَا تَعۡبُدُونَ
When he said to his father and his people: ‘What do you worship?‘
26:71
قَالُواْ نَعۡبُدُ أَصۡنَامً۬ا فَنَظَلُّ لَهَا عَـٰكِفِينَ
They said: ‘We worship idols, and to them we are ever devoted.‘
26:72
قَالَ هَلۡ يَسۡمَعُونَكُمۡ إِذۡ تَدۡعُونَ
He said: ‘Do they hear you, when you call (on them)?
26:73
أَوۡ يَنفَعُونَكُمۡ أَوۡ يَضُرُّونَ
Or do they benefit you or do they harm (you)?‘
26:74
قَالُواْ بَلۡ وَجَدۡنَآ ءَابَآءَنَا كَذَٲلِكَ يَفۡعَلُونَ
They said: ‘Nay, but we found our fathers doing so.‘
26:75
قَالَ أَفَرَءَيۡتُم مَّا كُنتُمۡ تَعۡبُدُونَ
He said: ‘Do you observe that which you have been worshipping,
26:76
أَنتُمۡ وَءَابَآؤُڪُمُ ٱلۡأَقۡدَمُونَ
You and your ancient fathers?
26:77
فَإِنَّہُمۡ عَدُوٌّ۬ لِّىٓ إِلَّا رَبَّ ٱلۡعَـٰلَمِينَ
Verily! They are enemies to me, save the Lord of the Alameen (mankind, jinns and all that exists);
26:78
ٱلَّذِى خَلَقَنِى فَهُوَ يَہۡدِينِ
Who has created me, and it is He Who guides me;
26:79
وَٱلَّذِى هُوَ يُطۡعِمُنِى وَيَسۡقِينِ
And it is He Who feeds me and gives me to drink.
26:80
وَإِذَا مَرِضۡتُ فَهُوَ يَشۡفِينِ
And when I am ill, it is He who cures me;
26:81
وَٱلَّذِى يُمِيتُنِى ثُمَّ يُحۡيِينِ
And Who will cause me to die, and then will bring me to life (again);
26:82
وَٱلَّذِىٓ أَطۡمَعُ أَن يَغۡفِرَ لِى خَطِيٓـَٔتِى يَوۡمَ ٱلدِّينِ
And Who, I hope will forgive me my faults on the Day of Recompense, (the Day of Resurrection),‘
26:83
رَبِّ هَبۡ لِى حُڪۡمً۬ا وَأَلۡحِقۡنِى بِٱلصَّـٰلِحِينَ
My Lord! Bestow Hukman (religious knowledge, right judgement of the affairs and Prophethood) on me, and join me with the righteous;
26:84
وَٱجۡعَل لِّى لِسَانَ صِدۡقٍ۬ فِى ٱلۡأَخِرِينَ
And grant me an honourable mention in later generations;
26:85
وَٱجۡعَلۡنِى مِن وَرَثَةِ جَنَّةِ ٱلنَّعِيمِ
And make me one of the inheritors of the Paradise of Delight;
26:86
وَٱغۡفِرۡ لِأَبِىٓ إِنَّهُ ۥ كَانَ مِنَ ٱلضَّآلِّينَ
And forgive my father, verily he is of the erring;
26:87
وَلَا تُخۡزِنِى يَوۡمَ يُبۡعَثُونَ
And disgrace me not on the Day when (all the creatures) will be resurrected;
26:88
يَوۡمَ لَا يَنفَعُ مَالٌ۬ وَلَا بَنُونَ
The Day whereon neither wealth nor sons will avail,
26:89
إِلَّا مَنۡ أَتَى ٱللَّهَ بِقَلۡبٍ۬ سَلِيمٍ۬
Except him who brings to Allah a clean heart (clean from Shirk (polytheism) and Nifaq (hypocrisy)).
26:90
وَأُزۡلِفَتِ ٱلۡجَنَّةُ لِلۡمُتَّقِينَ
And Paradise will be brought near to the Muttaqoon (pious - see V.2:2).
26:91
وَبُرِّزَتِ ٱلۡجَحِيمُ لِلۡغَاوِينَ
And the (Hell) Fire will be placed in full view of the erring.
26:92
وَقِيلَ لَهُمۡ أَيۡنَ مَا كُنتُمۡ تَعۡبُدُونَ
And it will be said to them: ‘Where are those (the false gods whom you used to set up as rivals with Allah) that you used to worship
26:93
مِن دُونِ ٱللَّهِ هَلۡ يَنصُرُونَكُمۡ أَوۡ يَنتَصِرُونَ
Instead of Allah? Can they help you or (even) help themselves?‘
26:94
فَكُبۡكِبُواْ فِيہَا هُمۡ وَٱلۡغَاوُ ۥنَ
Then they will be thrown on their faces into the (Fire), They and the Ghawoon (devils, and those who were in error).
26:95
وَجُنُودُ إِبۡلِيسَ أَجۡمَعُونَ
And the whole hosts of Iblees (Satan) together.
26:96
قَالُواْ وَهُمۡ فِيہَا يَخۡتَصِمُونَ
They will say while contending therein,
26:97
تَٱللَّهِ إِن كُنَّا لَفِى ضَلَـٰلٍ۬ مُّبِينٍ
By Allah, we were truly in a manifest error,
26:98
إِذۡ نُسَوِّيكُم بِرَبِّ ٱلۡعَـٰلَمِينَ
When We held you (false gods) as equals (in worship) with the Lord of the Alameen (mankind, jinns and all that exists);
26:99
وَمَآ أَضَلَّنَآ إِلَّا ٱلۡمُجۡرِمُونَ
And none has brought us into error except the Mujrimoon (Iblees (Satan) and those of human beings who commit crimes, murderers, polytheists, oppressors, etc.).
26:100
فَمَا لَنَا مِن شَـٰفِعِينَ
Now we have no intercessors,
26:101
وَلَا صَدِيقٍ حَمِيمٍ۬
Nor a close friend (to help us).
26:102
فَلَوۡ أَنَّ لَنَا كَرَّةً۬ فَنَكُونَ مِنَ ٱلۡمُؤۡمِنِينَ
(Alas!) If we only had a chance to return (to the world), we shall truly be among the believers!
26:103
إِنَّ فِى ذَٲلِكَ لَأَيَةً۬‌ۖ وَمَا كَانَ أَكۡثَرُهُم مُّؤۡمِنِينَ
Verily! In this is indeed a sign, yet most of them are not believers.
26:104
وَإِنَّ رَبَّكَ لَهُوَ ٱلۡعَزِيزُ ٱلرَّحِيمُ
And verily, your Lord! He is truly the All-Mighty, the Most Merciful (Ar-Raheem).
26:[105-122] The story of the Prophet Nüh, his dialogue with his people, and the destruction of the disbelievers
26:105
كَذَّبَتۡ قَوۡمُ نُوحٍ ٱلۡمُرۡسَلِينَ
The people of Nooh (Noah) belied the Messengers.
26:106
إِذۡ قَالَ لَهُمۡ أَخُوهُمۡ نُوحٌ أَلَا تَتَّقُونَ
When their brother Nooh (Noah) said to them: ‘Will you not fear Allah and obey Him?
26:107
إِنِّى لَكُمۡ رَسُولٌ أَمِينٌ۬
I am a trustworthy Messenger to you.
26:108
فَٱتَّقُواْ ٱللَّهَ وَأَطِيعُونِ
So fear Allah, keep your duty to Him, and obey me.
26:109
وَمَآ أَسۡـَٔلُكُمۡ عَلَيۡهِ مِنۡ أَجۡرٍ‌ۖ إِنۡ أَجۡرِىَ إِلَّا عَلَىٰ رَبِّ ٱلۡعَـٰلَمِينَ
No reward do I ask of you for it (my Message of Islamic Monotheism), my reward is onlyfrom the Lord of the Alameen (mankind, jinns and all that exists).
26:110
فَٱتَّقُواْ ٱللَّهَ وَأَطِيعُونِ
So keep your duty to Allah, fear Him and obey me.‘
26:111
قَالُوٓاْ أَنُؤۡمِنُ لَكَ وَٱتَّبَعَكَ ٱلۡأَرۡذَلُونَ
They said: ‘Shall we believe in you, when the meanest (of the people) follow you?‘
26:112
قَالَ وَمَا عِلۡمِى بِمَا كَانُواْ يَعۡمَلُونَ
He said: ‘And what knowledge have I of what they used to do?
26:113
إِنۡ حِسَابُہُمۡ إِلَّا عَلَىٰ رَبِّى‌ۖ لَوۡ تَشۡعُرُونَ
Their account is only with my Lord, if you could (but) know.
26:114
وَمَآ أَنَا۟ بِطَارِدِ ٱلۡمُؤۡمِنِينَ
And I am not going to drive away the believers.
26:115
إِنۡ أَنَا۟ إِلَّا نَذِيرٌ۬ مُّبِينٌ۬
I am only a plain warner.‘
26:116
قَالُواْ لَٮِٕن لَّمۡ تَنتَهِ يَـٰنُوحُ لَتَكُونَنَّ مِنَ ٱلۡمَرۡجُومِينَ
They said: ‘If you cease not, O Nooh (Noah)! You will surely be among those stoned (to death).‘
26:117
قَالَ رَبِّ إِنَّ قَوۡمِى كَذَّبُونِ
He said: ‘My Lord! Verily, my people have belied me.
26:118
فَٱفۡتَحۡ بَيۡنِى وَبَيۡنَهُمۡ فَتۡحً۬ا
Therefore judge You between me and them,
وَنَجِّنِى وَمَن مَّعِىَ مِنَ ٱلۡمُؤۡمِنِينَ
and save me and those of the believers who are with me.‘
26:119
فَأَنجَيۡنَـٰهُ وَمَن مَّعَهُ ۥ فِى ٱلۡفُلۡكِ ٱلۡمَشۡحُونِ
And We saved him and those with him in the laden ship.
26:120
ثُمَّ أَغۡرَقۡنَا بَعۡدُ ٱلۡبَاقِينَ
Then We drowned the rest (disbelievers) thereafter.
26:121
إِنَّ فِى ذَٲلِكَ لَأَيَةً۬‌ۖ
Verily, in this is indeed a sign,
وَمَا ڪَانَ أَكۡثَرُهُم مُّؤۡمِنِينَ
yet most of them are not believers.
26:122
وَإِنَّ رَبَّكَ لَهُوَ ٱلۡعَزِيزُ ٱلرَّحِيمُ
And verily! Your Lord, He is indeed the All-Mighty, the Most Merciful (Ar-Raheem).
26:[123-140] The story of the Prophet Hüd, his address to his people, and the destruction of disbelievers
26:123
كَذَّبَتۡ عَادٌ ٱلۡمُرۡسَلِينَ
Ad (people) belied the Messengers.
26:124
إِذۡ قَالَ لَهُمۡ أَخُوهُمۡ هُودٌ أَلَا تَتَّقُونَ
When their brother Hood said to them:
26:125
إِنِّى لَكُمۡ رَسُولٌ أَمِينٌ۬
Will you not fear Allah and obey Him?
26:126
فَٱتَّقُواْ ٱللَّهَ وَأَطِيعُونِ
Verily! I am a trustworthy Messenger to you.
26:127
وَمَآ أَسۡـَٔلُكُمۡ عَلَيۡهِ مِنۡ أَجۡرٍ‌ۖ إِنۡ أَجۡرِىَ إِلَّا عَلَىٰ رَبِّ ٱلۡعَـٰلَمِينَ

26:128
أَتَبۡنُونَ بِكُلِّ رِيعٍ ءَايَةً۬ تَعۡبَثُونَ
Do you build high palaces on every high place, while you do not live in them?
26:129
وَتَتَّخِذُونَ مَصَانِعَ لَعَلَّكُمۡ تَخۡلُدُونَ
And do you get for yourselves palaces (fine buildings) as if you will live therein for ever.
26:130
وَإِذَا بَطَشۡتُم بَطَشۡتُمۡ جَبَّارِينَ
And when you seize, seize you as tyrants?
26:131
فَٱتَّقُواْ ٱللَّهَ وَأَطِيعُونِ
So fear Allah, keep your duty to Him, and obey me.
26:132
وَٱتَّقُواْ ٱلَّذِىٓ أَمَدَّكُم بِمَا تَعۡلَمُونَ
And keep your duty to Him, fear Him Who has aided you with all (good things) that you know.
26:133
أَمَدَّكُم بِأَنۡعَـٰمٍ۬ وَبَنِينَ
He has aided you with cattle and children.
26:134
وَجَنَّـٰتٍ۬ وَعُيُونٍ
And gardens and springs.
26:135
إِنِّىٓ أَخَافُ عَلَيۡكُمۡ عَذَابَ يَوۡمٍ عَظِيمٍ۬
Verily, I fear for you the torment of a Great Day.‘
26:136
قَالُواْ سَوَآءٌ عَلَيۡنَآ أَوَعَظۡتَ أَمۡ لَمۡ تَكُن مِّنَ ٱلۡوَٲعِظِينَ
They said: ‘It is the same to us whether you preach or be not of those who preach.
26:137
إِنۡ هَـٰذَآ إِلَّا خُلُقُ ٱلۡأَوَّلِينَ
This is no other than the false-tales and religion of the ancients,(Tafsir At-Tabaree, Vol.19, Page 97)
26:138
وَمَا نَحۡنُ بِمُعَذَّبِينَ
And we are not going to be punished.‘
26:139
فَكَذَّبُوهُ فَأَهۡلَكۡنَـٰهُمۡ‌ۗ
So they belied him, and We destroyed them.
إِنَّ فِى ذَٲلِكَ لَأَيَةً۬‌ۖ وَمَا كَانَ أَكۡثَرُهُم مُّؤۡمِنِينَ
Verily! In this is indeed a sign, yet most of them are not believers.
26:140
وَإِنَّ رَبَّكَ لَهُوَ ٱلۡعَزِيزُ ٱلرَّحِيمُ

Thamood (people) belied the Messenger.

إِذۡ قَالَ لَهُمۡ أَخُوهُمۡ صَـٰلِحٌ أَلَا تَتَّقُونَ
When their brother Salih (Saleh) said to them: ‘Will you not fear Allah and obey Him?

وَمَآ أَسۡـَٔلُكُمۡ عَلَيۡهِ مِنۡ أَجۡرٍ‌ۖ إِنۡ أَجۡرِىَ إِلَّا عَلَىٰ رَبِّ ٱلۡعَـٰلَمِينَ

أَتُتۡرَكُونَ فِى مَا هَـٰهُنَآ ءَامِنِينَ

26:147

In gardens and springs.
26:148
وَزُرُوعٍ۬ وَنَخۡلٍ۬ طَلۡعُهَا هَضِيمٌ۬

And you carve houses out of mountains with great skill.
26:150

26:152
ٱلَّذِينَ يُفۡسِدُونَ فِى ٱلۡأَرۡضِ وَلَا يُصۡلِحُونَ
Who make mischief in the land, and reform not.‘
26:153
قَالُوٓاْ إِنَّمَآ أَنتَ مِنَ ٱلۡمُسَحَّرِينَ

26:154
مَآ أَنتَ إِلَّا بَشَرٌ۬ مِّثۡلُنَافَأۡتِ بِـَٔايَةٍ إِن كُنتَ مِنَ ٱلصَّـٰدِقِينَ
You are but a human being like us. Then bring us a sign if you are of the truthful.‘
26:155

He said: ‘Here is a she-camel; it has a right to drink (water), and you have a right to drink (water) (each) on a day, known.
26:156
وَلَا تَمَسُّوهَا بِسُوٓءٍ۬ فَيَأۡخُذَكُمۡ عَذَابُ يَوۡمٍ عَظِيمٍ۬
And touch her not with harm, lest the torment of a Great Day seize you.‘
26:157

But they killed her, and then they became regretful.
26:158
فَأَخَذَهُمُ ٱلۡعَذَابُ‌ۗ
So the torment overtook them.
