Ad-Dhuhaa
The Glorious Morning Light, The Forenoon, Morning Hours, Morning Bright, The Bright Morning Hours
93:[1-11] Good news to Muhammad (pbuh) that the later period will be better for him than the earlier
93:1
وَٱلضُّحَىٰ
By the forenoon (after sun-rise);
93:2
وَٱلَّيۡلِ إِذَا سَجَىٰ
And by the night when it is still (or darkens);
93:3
مَا وَدَّعَكَ رَبُّكَ وَمَا قَلَىٰ
Your Lord (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) has neither forsaken you nor hated you.
93:4
وَلَلۡأَخِرَةُ خَيۡرٌ۬ لَّكَ مِنَ ٱلۡأُولَىٰ
And indeed the Hereafter is better for you than the present (life of this world).
93:5
وَلَسَوۡفَ يُعۡطِيكَ رَبُّكَ فَتَرۡضَىٰٓ
And verily, your Lord will give you (all i.e. good) so that you shall be well-pleased.
93:6
أَلَمۡ يَجِدۡكَ يَتِيمً۬ا فَـَٔاوَىٰ
Did He not find you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) an orphan and gave you a refuge?
93:7
وَوَجَدَكَ ضَآلاًّ۬ فَهَدَىٰ
And He found you unaware (of the Qur‘ân, its legal laws, and Prophethood, etc.) and guided you?
93:8
وَوَجَدَكَ عَآٮِٕلاً۬ فَأَغۡنَىٰ
And He found you poor, and made you rich (selfsufficient with selfcontentment, etc.)?
93:9
فَأَمَّا ٱلۡيَتِيمَ فَلَا تَقۡهَرۡ
Therefore, treat not the orphan with oppression,
93:10
وَأَمَّا ٱلسَّآٮِٕلَ فَلَا تَنۡہَرۡ
And repulse not the beggar;
93:11
وَأَمَّا بِنِعۡمَةِ رَبِّكَ فَحَدِّثۡ
And proclaim the Grace of your Lord (i.e. the Prophethood and all other Graces).*********
