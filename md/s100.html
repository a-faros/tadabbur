Al-Aadiyaat
The Courser, The Chargers, The War Horse
100:[1-11] An example of horses who are more grateful to their owners than men are to their Rabb
100:1
وَٱلۡعَـٰدِيَـٰتِ ضَبۡحً۬ا
By the (steeds) that run, with panting (breath)
100:2
فَٱلۡمُورِيَـٰتِ قَدۡحً۬ا
Striking sparks of fire (by their hooves)
100:3
فَٱلۡمُغِيرَٲتِ صُبۡحً۬ا
And scouring to the raid at dawn
100:4
فَأَثَرۡنَ بِهِۦ نَقۡعً۬ا
And raise the dust in clouds the while,
100:5
فَوَسَطۡنَ بِهِۦ جَمۡعًا
Pen (قلم)etrating forthwith as one into the midst (of the foe);
100:6
إِنَّ ٱلۡإِنسَـٰنَ لِرَبِّهِۦ لَكَنُودٌ۬
Verily! Man (disbeliever) is ungrateful to his Lord;
100:7
وَإِنَّهُ ۥ عَلَىٰ ذَٲلِكَ لَشَہِيدٌ۬
And to that fact he bears witness (by his deeds);
100:8
وَإِنَّهُ ۥ لِحُبِّ ٱلۡخَيۡرِ لَشَدِيدٌ
And verily, he is violent in the love of wealth.
100:9
أَفَلَا يَعۡلَمُ إِذَا بُعۡثِرَ مَا فِى ٱلۡقُبُورِ
Knows he not that when the contents of the graves are brought out and poured forth (all mankind is resurrected).
100:10
وَحُصِّلَ مَا فِى ٱلصُّدُورِ
And that which is in the breasts (of men) shall be made known.
100:11
إِنَّ رَبَّہُم بِہِمۡ يَوۡمَٮِٕذٍ۬ لَّخَبِيرُۢ
Verily, that Day (i.e. the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة)) their Lord will be Well-Acquainted with them (as to their deeds) (and will reward them for their deeds).*********
