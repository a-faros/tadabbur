Ash-Shura
The Consultation
42:[1-9] The heavens might have broken apart from above those who elevate Allah‘s creatures to His rank if the angels were not begging forgiveness for the residents of earth
42:1
حمٓ
Ha Meem.
42:2
عٓسٓقٓ
Aeen Seen Qaf.(These letters are one of the miracles of the Quran, and none but Allah (Alone) knows their meanings).
42:3
كَذَٲلِكَ يُوحِىٓ إِلَيۡكَ وَإِلَى ٱلَّذِينَ مِن قَبۡلِكَ ٱللَّهُ ٱلۡعَزِيزُ ٱلۡحَكِيمُ
Likewise Allah, the All-Mighty, the All-Wise (Al-Hakeem) inspires you (O Muhammad SAW) as (He inspired) those before you.
42:4
لَهُ ۥ مَا فِى ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٲتِ وَمَا فِى ٱلۡأَرۡضِ‌ۖ
To Him belongs all that is in the heavens and all that is in the earth,
وَهُوَ ٱلۡعَلِىُّ ٱلۡعَظِيمُ
and He is the Most High, the Most Great.
42:5
تَكَادُ ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٲتُ يَتَفَطَّرۡنَ مِن فَوۡقِهِنَّ‌ۚ
Nearly the heavens might rent asunder from above them (by His Majesty),
وَٱلۡمَلَـٰٓٮِٕكَةُ يُسَبِّحُونَ بِحَمۡدِ رَبِّہِمۡ وَيَسۡتَغۡفِرُونَ لِمَن فِى ٱلۡأَرۡضِ‌ۗ
and the angels glorify the praises of their Lord, and ask for forgiveness for those on the earth,
أَلَآ إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ هُوَ ٱلۡغَفُورُ ٱلرَّحِيمُ
verily, Allah is the Oft- Forgiving, the Most Merciful (Ar-Raheem).
42:6
وَٱلَّذِينَ ٱتَّخَذُواْ مِن دُونِهِۦۤ أَوۡلِيَآءَ ٱللَّهُ حَفِيظٌ عَلَيۡہِمۡ
And as for those who take as Auliya (guardians, supporters, helpers, protectors, etc.) others besides Him (i.e. they take false deities other than Allah (as) protectors, and they worship them) Allah is Hafeez (Protector) over them (i.e. takes care of their deeds and will recompense them),
وَمَآ أَنتَ عَلَيۡہِم بِوَكِيلٍ۬
and you (O Muhammad SAW) are not a Wakeel (guardian or a disposer of their affairs) over them (to protect their deeds, etc.).
42:7
وَكَذَٲلِكَ أَوۡحَيۡنَآ إِلَيۡكَ قُرۡءَانًا عَرَبِيًّ۬ا لِّتُنذِرَ أُمَّ ٱلۡقُرَىٰ وَمَنۡ حَوۡلَهَا
And thus We have inspired unto you (O Muhammad SAW) a Quran (in Arabic) that you may warn the Mother of the Towns (Makkah) and all around it.
وَتُنذِرَ يَوۡمَ ٱلۡجَمۡعِ لَا رَيۡبَ فِيهِ‌ۚ
And warn of the Day of Assembling, of which there is no doubt,
فَرِيقٌ۬ فِى ٱلۡجَنَّةِ وَفَرِيقٌ۬ فِى ٱلسَّعِيرِ
when a party will be in Paradise(those who believed in Allah and followed what Allah‘s Messenger SAW brought them)
and a party in the blazing Fire (Hell)(those who disbelieved in Allah and followed not what Allahs Messenger SAW brought them)
42:8
وَلَوۡ شَآءَ ٱللَّهُ لَجَعَلَهُمۡ أُمَّةً۬ وَٲحِدَةً۬وَلَـٰكِن يُدۡخِلُ مَن يَشَآءُ فِى رَحۡمَتِهِۦ‌ۚ
And if Allah had willed, He could have made them one nation, but He admits whom He wills to His Mercy.
وَٱلظَّـٰلِمُونَ مَا لَهُم مِّن وَلِىٍّ۬ وَلَا نَصِيرٍ
And the Zalimoon (polytheists and wrong-doers, etc.) will have neither a Walee (protector) nor a helper.
42:9
أَمِ ٱتَّخَذُواْ مِن دُونِهِۦۤ أَوۡلِيَآءَ‌ۖ
Or have they taken (for worship) Auliya (guardians, supporters, helpers, protectors, etc.) besides Him?
فَٱللَّهُ هُوَ ٱلۡوَلِىُّ
But Allah, He Alone is the Walee (Protector, etc.).
وَهُوَ يُحۡىِ ٱلۡمَوۡتَىٰ وَهُوَ عَلَىٰ كُلِّ شَىۡءٍ۬ قَدِيرٌ۬
And it is He Who gives life to the dead, and He is Able to do all things.
42:[10-19] Islam is the same Deen (way of life) which was enjoined on Nüh, Ibrãhïm, Musa (Moses) and Isa (Jesus). They were all ordered to establish Deen-al-Islam and do not create division (sects) in it
42:10
وَمَا ٱخۡتَلَفۡتُمۡ فِيهِ مِن شَىۡءٍ۬ فَحُكۡمُهُ ۥۤ إِلَى ٱللَّهِ‌ۚ
And in whatsoever you differ, the decision thereof is with Allah (He is the ruling Judge).
ذَٲلِكُمُ ٱللَّهُ رَبِّى عَلَيۡهِ تَوَڪَّلۡتُ وَإِلَيۡهِ أُنِيبُ
(And say O Muhammad SAW to these polytheists:)
Such is Allah, my Lord in Whom I put my trust, and to Him I turn in all of my affairs and in repentance.
42:11
فَاطِرُ ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٲتِ وَٱلۡأَرۡضِ‌ۚ
The Creator of the heavens and the earth.
جَعَلَ لَكُم مِّنۡ أَنفُسِكُمۡ أَزۡوَٲجً۬ا وَمِنَ ٱلۡأَنۡعَـٰمِ أَزۡوَٲجً۬ا
He has made for you mates from yourselves, and for the cattle (also) mates.
يَذۡرَؤُكُمۡ فِيهِ‌ۚ
By this means He creates you (in the wombs).
لَيۡسَ كَمِثۡلِهِۦ شَىۡءٌ۬‌ۖ وَهُوَ ٱلسَّمِيعُ ٱلۡبَصِيرُ
There is nothing like unto Him, and He is the All-Hearer, the All-Seer (Al-Baseer).
42:12
لَهُ ۥ مَقَالِيدُ ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٲتِ وَٱلۡأَرۡضِ‌ۖ
To Him belong the keys of the heavens and the earth,
يَبۡسُطُ ٱلرِّزۡقَ لِمَن يَشَآءُ وَيَقۡدِرُ‌ۚ
He enlarges provision for whom He wills, and straitens (it for whom He wills).
إِنَّهُ ۥ بِكُلِّ شَىۡءٍ عَلِيمٌ۬
Verily! He is the All-Knower (Al-‘Aleem) of everything.
42:13
شَرَعَ لَكُم مِّنَ ٱلدِّينِ مَا وَصَّىٰ بِهِۦ نُوحً۬ا
He (Allah) has ordained for you the same religion (Islam) which He ordained for Nooh (Noah),
وَٱلَّذِىٓ أَوۡحَيۡنَآ إِلَيۡكَ
and that which We have inspired in you (O Muhammad SAW),
وَمَا وَصَّيۡنَا بِهِۦۤ إِبۡرَٲهِيمَ وَمُوسَىٰ وَعِيسَىٰٓ‌ۖ
and that which We ordained for Ibraheem (Abraham), Musa (Moosa) and Isa (Iesa)
أَنۡ أَقِيمُواْ ٱلدِّينَ وَلَا تَتَفَرَّقُواْ فِيهِ‌ۚ
saying you should establish religion (i.e. to do what it orders you to do practically), and make no divisions in it (religion) (i.e. various sects in religion).
كَبُرَ عَلَى ٱلۡمُشۡرِكِينَ مَا تَدۡعُوهُمۡ إِلَيۡهِ‌ۚ
Intolerable for the Mushrikoon, is that to which you (O Muhammad SAW) call them.
ٱللَّهُ يَجۡتَبِىٓ إِلَيۡهِ مَن يَشَآءُ وَيَہۡدِىٓ إِلَيۡهِ مَن يُنِيبُ
Allah chooses for Himself whom He wills, and guides unto Himself who turns to Him in repentance and in obedience.
42:14
وَمَا تَفَرَّقُوٓاْ إِلَّا مِنۢ بَعۡدِ مَا جَآءَهُمُ ٱلۡعِلۡمُ بَغۡيَۢا بَيۡنَہُمۡ‌ۚ
And they divided not till after knowledge had come to them, through selfish transgression between themselves.
وَلَوۡلَا كَلِمَةٌ۬ سَبَقَتۡ مِن رَّبِّكَ إِلَىٰٓ أَجَلٍ۬ مُّسَمًّ۬ى لَّقُضِىَ بَيۡنَہُمۡ‌ۚ
And had it not been for a Word that went forth before from your Lord for an appointed term, the matter would have been settled between them.
وَإِنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ أُورِثُواْ ٱلۡكِتَـٰبَ مِنۢ بَعۡدِهِمۡ لَفِى شَكٍّ۬ مِّنۡهُ مُرِيبٍ۬
And verily, those who were made to inherit the Scripture (i.e. the Taurah (Torah) and the Injil (Injeel)) after them (i.e. Jews and Christians) are in grave doubt concerning it (i.e. Allah‘s true religion Islam or the Quran).
42:15
فَلِذَٲلِكَ فَٱدۡعُ‌ۖ
So unto this (religion of Islam, alone and this Quran) then invite (people) (O Muhammad SAW),
وَٱسۡتَقِمۡ ڪَمَآ أُمِرۡتَ‌ۖ وَلَا تَتَّبِعۡ أَهۡوَآءَهُمۡ‌ۖ
and Istaqim ((i.e. stand firm and straight on Islamic Monotheism by performing all that is ordained by Allah (good deeds, etc.), and by abstaining from all that is forbidden by Allah (sins and evil deeds, etc.)), as you are commanded, and follow not their desires
وَقُلۡ ءَامَنتُ بِمَآ أَنزَلَ ٱللَّهُ مِن ڪِتَـٰبٍ۬‌ۖ
but say: ‘I believe in whatsoever Allah has sent down of the Book (all the holy Books, this Quran and the Books of the old from the Taurat (Torah), or the Injil (Injeel) or the Pages of Abraham (Ibrahim))
وَأُمِرۡتُ لِأَعۡدِلَ بَيۡنَكُمُ‌ۖ
and I am commanded to do justice among you,
ٱللَّهُ رَبُّنَا وَرَبُّكُمۡ‌ۖ
Allah is our Lord and your Lord.
لَنَآ أَعۡمَـٰلُنَا وَلَكُمۡ أَعۡمَـٰلُڪُمۡ‌ۖ
For us our deeds and for you your deeds.
لَا حُجَّةَ بَيۡنَنَا وَبَيۡنَكُمُ‌ۖ
There is no dispute between us and you.
ٱللَّهُ يَجۡمَعُ بَيۡنَنَا‌ۖوَإِلَيۡهِ ٱلۡمَصِيرُ
Allah will assemble us (all), and to Him is the final return.
42:16
وَٱلَّذِينَ يُحَآجُّونَ فِى ٱللَّهِ مِنۢ بَعۡدِ مَا ٱسۡتُجِيبَ لَهُ ۥ حُجَّتُهُمۡ دَاحِضَةٌ عِندَ رَبِّہِمۡ
And those who dispute concerning Allah (His Religion of Islamic Monotheism, with which Muhammad SAW has been sent), after it has been accepted (by the people), of no use is their dispute before their Lord,
وَعَلَيۡہِمۡ غَضَبٌ۬ وَلَهُمۡ عَذَابٌ۬ شَدِيدٌ
and on them is wrath, and for them will be a severe torment.
42:17
ٱللَّهُ ٱلَّذِىٓ أَنزَلَ ٱلۡكِتَـٰبَ بِٱلۡحَقِّ وَٱلۡمِيزَانَ‌ۗ
It is Allah Who has sent down the Book (the Quran) in truth, and the Balance (i.e. to act justly).
وَمَا يُدۡرِيكَ لَعَلَّ ٱلسَّاعَةَ قَرِيبٌ۬
And what can make you know that perhaps the Hour is close at hand?
42:18
يَسۡتَعۡجِلُ بِہَا ٱلَّذِينَ لَا يُؤۡمِنُونَ بِہَا‌ۖ
Those who believe not therein seek to hasten it,
وَٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُواْ مُشۡفِقُونَ مِنۡہَا وَيَعۡلَمُونَ أَنَّهَا ٱلۡحَقُّ‌ۗ
while those who believe are fearful of it, and know that it is the very truth.
أَلَآ إِنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ يُمَارُونَ فِى ٱلسَّاعَةِ لَفِى ضَلَـٰلِۭ بَعِيدٍ
Verily, those who dispute concerning the Hour are certainly in error far away.
42:19
ٱللَّهُ لَطِيفُۢ بِعِبَادِهِۦ يَرۡزُقُ مَن يَشَآءُ‌ۖ
Allah is very Gracious and Kind to His slaves. He gives provisions to whom He wills.
وَهُوَ ٱلۡقَوِىُّ ٱلۡعَزِيزُ
And He is the All-Strong, the All-Mighty.
42:[20-29] He who desires the harvest in the hereafter shall be given many folds, but he who desires it in this life shall be given a portion here but shall have no share in the hereafter
42:20
مَن كَانَ يُرِيدُ حَرۡثَ ٱلۡأَخِرَةِ نَزِدۡ لَهُ ۥ فِى حَرۡثِهِۦ‌ۖ
Whosoever desires (with his deeds) the reward of the Hereafter, We give him increase in his reward,
وَمَن كَانَ يُرِيدُ حَرۡثَ ٱلدُّنۡيَا نُؤۡتِهِۦ مِنۡہَا وَمَا لَهُ ۥ فِى ٱلۡأَخِرَةِ مِن نَّصِيبٍ
and whosoever desires the reward of this world (with his deeds), We give him thereof (what is written for him), and he has no portion in the Hereafter.
42:21
أَمۡ لَهُمۡ شُرَڪَـٰٓؤُاْ شَرَعُواْ لَهُم مِّنَ ٱلدِّينِ مَا لَمۡ يَأۡذَنۢ بِهِ ٱللَّهُ‌ۚ
Or have they partners with Allah (false gods), who have instituted for them a religion which Allah has not allowed.
وَلَوۡلَا ڪَلِمَةُ ٱلۡفَصۡلِ لَقُضِىَ بَيۡنَہُمۡ‌ۗ
And had it not been for a decisive Word (gone forth already), the matter would have been judged between them.
وَإِنَّ ٱلظَّـٰلِمِينَ لَهُمۡ عَذَابٌ أَلِيمٌ۬
And verily, for the Zalimoon (polytheists and wrong-doers), there is a painful torment.
42:22
تَرَى ٱلظَّـٰلِمِينَ مُشۡفِقِينَ مِمَّا ڪَسَبُواْ وَهُوَ وَاقِعُۢ بِهِمۡ‌ۗ
You will see (on the Day of Resurrection), the Zalimoon (polytheists and wrong-doers, etc.) fearful of that which they have earned, and it (Allah‘s Torment) will surely befall them,
وَٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُواْ وَعَمِلُواْ ٱلصَّـٰلِحَـٰتِ فِى رَوۡضَاتِ ٱلۡجَنَّاتِ‌ۖ
while those who believe (in the Oneness of Allah Islamic Monotheism) and do righteous deeds (will be) in the flowering meadows of the Gardens (Paradise),
لَهُم مَّا يَشَآءُونَ عِندَ رَبِّهِمۡ‌ۚ
having what they wish from their Lord.
ذَٲلِكَ هُوَ ٱلۡفَضۡلُ ٱلۡكَبِيرُ
That is the supreme Grace, (Paradise).
42:23
ذَٲلِكَ ٱلَّذِى يُبَشِّرُ ٱللَّهُ عِبَادَهُ ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُواْ وَعَمِلُواْ ٱلصَّـٰلِحَـٰتِ‌ۗ
That is (the Paradise) whereof Allah gives glad tidings to His slaves who believe (in the Oneness of Allah Islamic Monotheism) and do righteous good deeds.
قُل لَّآ أَسۡـَٔلُكُمۡ عَلَيۡهِ أَجۡرًا إِلَّا ٱلۡمَوَدَّةَ فِى ٱلۡقُرۡبَىٰ‌ۗ
Say (O Muhammad SAW): ‘No reward do I ask of you for this except to be kind to me for my kinship with you.‘
وَمَن يَقۡتَرِفۡ حَسَنَةً۬ نَّزِدۡ لَهُ ۥ فِيہَا حُسۡنًا‌ۚ
And whoever earns a good righteous deed, We shall give him an increase of good in respect thereof.
إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ غَفُورٌ۬ شَكُورٌ
Verily, Allah is Oft- Forgiving, Most Ready to appreciate (the deeds of those who are obedient to Him).
42:24
أَمۡ يَقُولُونَ ٱفۡتَرَىٰ عَلَى ٱللَّهِ كَذِبً۬ا‌ۖ
Or say they: ‘He has invented a lie against Allah?‘
فَإِن يَشَإِ ٱللَّهُ يَخۡتِمۡ عَلَىٰ قَلۡبِكَ‌ۗ
If Allah willed, He could have sealed your heart (so that you forget all that you know of the Quran).
وَيَمۡحُ ٱللَّهُ ٱلۡبَـٰطِلَ وَيُحِقُّ ٱلۡحَقَّ بِكَلِمَـٰتِهِۦۤ‌ۚ
And Allah wipes out falsehood, and establishes the truth (Islam) by His Word (this Quran).
إِنَّهُ ۥ عَلِيمُۢ بِذَاتِ ٱلصُّدُورِ
Verily, He knows well what (the secrets) are in the breasts (of mankind).
42:25
وَهُوَ ٱلَّذِى يَقۡبَلُ ٱلتَّوۡبَةَ عَنۡ عِبَادِهِۦ وَيَعۡفُواْ عَنِ ٱلسَّيِّـَٔاتِ
And He it is Who accepts repentance from His slaves, and forgives sins,
وَيَعۡلَمُ مَا تَفۡعَلُونَ
and He knows what you do.
42:26
وَيَسۡتَجِيبُ ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُواْ وَعَمِلُواْ ٱلصَّـٰلِحَـٰتِ وَيَزِيدُهُم مِّن فَضۡلِهِۦ‌ۚ
And He answers (the invocation of) those who believe (in the Oneness of Allah Islamic Monotheism) and do righteous good deeds, and gives them increase of His Bounty.
وَٱلۡكَـٰفِرُونَ لَهُمۡ عَذَابٌ۬ شَدِيدٌ۬
And as for the disbelievers, theirs will be a severe torment.
42:27
وَلَوۡ بَسَطَ ٱللَّهُ ٱلرِّزۡقَ لِعِبَادِهِۦ لَبَغَوۡاْ فِى ٱلۡأَرۡضِ وَلَـٰكِن يُنَزِّلُ بِقَدَرٍ۬ مَّا يَشَآءُ‌ۚ
And if Allah were to enlarge the provision for His slaves, they would surely rebel in the earth, but He sends down by measure as He wills.
إِنَّهُ ۥ بِعِبَادِهِۦ خَبِيرُۢ بَصِيرٌ۬
Verily! He is in respect of His slaves, the Well-Aware, the All-Seer (Al-Baseer) (of things that benefit them).
42:28
وَهُوَ ٱلَّذِى يُنَزِّلُ ٱلۡغَيۡثَ مِنۢ بَعۡدِ مَا قَنَطُواْ وَيَنشُرُ رَحۡمَتَهُ ۥ‌ۚ
And He it is Who sends down the rain after they have despaired, and spreads abroad His Mercy.
وَهُوَ ٱلۡوَلِىُّ ٱلۡحَمِيدُ
And He is the Walee (Helper, Supporter, Protector, etc.), Worthy of all Praise.
42:29
وَمِنۡ ءَايَـٰتِهِۦ خَلۡقُ ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٲتِ وَٱلۡأَرۡضِ وَمَا بَثَّ فِيهِمَا مِن دَآبَّةٍ۬‌ۚ
And among His Ayat (proofs, evidences, lessons, signs, etc.) is the creation of the heavens and the earth, and whatever moving (living) creatures He has dispersed in them both.
وَهُوَ عَلَىٰ جَمۡعِهِمۡ إِذَا يَشَآءُ قَدِيرٌ۬
And He is All- Potent over their assembling (i.e. resurrecting them on the Day of Resurrection after their death, and dispersion of their bodies) whenever He will.
42:[30-43] Whatever afflictions befall upon people are the result of their own misdeeds. True believers are those who establish Salah, give charity and defend themselves when oppressed
42:30
وَمَآ أَصَـٰبَڪُم مِّن مُّصِيبَةٍ۬ فَبِمَا كَسَبَتۡ أَيۡدِيكُمۡ
And whatever of misfortune befalls you, it is because of what your hands have earned.
وَيَعۡفُواْ عَن كَثِيرٍ۬
And He pardons much. (See the Quran Verse 35:45)
42:31
وَمَآ أَنتُم بِمُعۡجِزِينَ فِى ٱلۡأَرۡضِ‌ۖ
And you cannot escape from Allah (i.e. His Punishment) in the earth,
وَمَا لَكُم مِّن دُونِ ٱللَّهِ مِن وَلِىٍّ۬ وَلَا نَصِيرٍ۬
and besides Allah you have neither any Walee (guardian or a protector) nor any helper.
42:32
وَمِنۡ ءَايَـٰتِهِ ٱلۡجَوَارِ فِى ٱلۡبَحۡرِ كَٱلۡأَعۡلَـٰمِ
And among His Signs are the ships, in the sea, like mountains.
42:33
إِن يَشَأۡ يُسۡكِنِ ٱلرِّيحَ فَيَظۡلَلۡنَ رَوَاكِدَ عَلَىٰ ظَهۡرِهِۦۤ‌ۚ
If He wills, He causes the wind to cease, then they would become motionless on the back (of the sea).
إِنَّ فِى ذَٲلِكَ لَأَيَـٰتٍ۬ لِّكُلِّ صَبَّارٍ۬ شَكُورٍ
Verily, in this are signs for everyone patient and grateful.
42:34
أَوۡ يُوبِقۡهُنَّ بِمَا كَسَبُواْ وَيَعۡفُ عَن كَثِيرٍ۬
Or He may destroy them (by drowning) because of that which their (people) have earned. And He pardons much.
42:35
وَيَعۡلَمَ ٱلَّذِينَ يُجَـٰدِلُونَ فِىٓ ءَايَـٰتِنَامَا لَهُم مِّن مَّحِيصٍ۬
And those who dispute (polytheists, etc. with Our Messenger Muhammad SAW) as regards Our Ayat (proofs, signs, verses, etc. of Islamic Monotheism) may know that there is no place of refuge for them (from Allah‘s punishment).
42:36
فَمَآ أُوتِيتُم مِّن شَىۡءٍ۬ فَمَتَـٰعُ ٱلۡحَيَوٰةِ ٱلدُّنۡيَا‌ۖ
So whatever you have been given is but a passing enjoyment for this worldly life,
وَمَا عِندَ ٱللَّهِ خَيۡرٌ۬ وَأَبۡقَىٰ
but that which is with Allah (Paradise) is better and more lasting
لِلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُواْ وَعَلَىٰ رَبِّہِمۡ يَتَوَكَّلُونَ
for those who believe (in the Oneness of Allah Islamic Monotheism) and put their trust in their Lord (concerning all of their affairs).
42:37
وَٱلَّذِينَ يَجۡتَنِبُونَ كَبَـٰٓٮِٕرَ ٱلۡإِثۡمِ وَٱلۡفَوَٲحِشَ
And those who avoid the greater sins, and Al-Fawahish (illegal sexual intercourse, etc.),
وَإِذَا مَا غَضِبُواْ هُمۡ يَغۡفِرُونَ
and when they are angry, they forgive
42:38
وَٱلَّذِينَ ٱسۡتَجَابُواْ لِرَبِّہِمۡ وَأَقَامُواْ ٱلصَّلَوٰةَ
And those who answer the Call of their Lord (i.e. to believe that He is the only One Lord (Allah), and to worship none but Him Alone), and perform As-Salat (Iqamat-as-Salat),
وَأَمۡرُهُمۡ شُورَىٰ بَيۡنَہُمۡ
and who (conduct) their affairs by mutual consultation,
وَمِمَّا رَزَقۡنَـٰهُمۡ يُنفِقُونَ
and who spend of what We have bestowed on them;
42:39
وَٱلَّذِينَ إِذَآ أَصَابَہُمُ ٱلۡبَغۡىُ هُمۡ يَنتَصِرُونَ
And those who, when an oppressive wrong is done to them, they take revenge.
42:40
وَجَزَٲٓؤُاْ سَيِّئَةٍ۬ سَيِّئَةٌ۬ مِّثۡلُهَا‌ۖ
The recompense for an evil is an evil like thereof,
فَمَنۡ عَفَا وَأَصۡلَحَ فَأَجۡرُهُ ۥ عَلَى ٱللَّهِ‌ۚ
but whoever forgives and makes reconciliation, his reward is due from Allah.
إِنَّهُ ۥ لَا يُحِبُّ ٱلظَّـٰلِمِينَ
Verily, He likes not the Zalimoon (oppressors, polytheists, and wrong-doers, etc.).
42:41
وَلَمَنِ ٱنتَصَرَ بَعۡدَ ظُلۡمِهِۦ فَأُوْلَـٰٓٮِٕكَ مَا عَلَيۡہِم مِّن سَبِيلٍ
And indeed whosoever takes revenge after he has suffered wrong, for such there is no way (of blame) against them.
42:42
إِنَّمَا ٱلسَّبِيلُ عَلَى ٱلَّذِينَ يَظۡلِمُونَ ٱلنَّاسَ وَيَبۡغُونَ فِى ٱلۡأَرۡضِ بِغَيۡرِ ٱلۡحَقِّ‌ۚ
The way (of blame) is only against those who oppress men and wrongly rebel in the earth,
أُوْلَـٰٓٮِٕكَ لَهُمۡ عَذَابٌ أَلِيمٌ۬
for such there will be a painful torment.
42:43
وَلَمَن صَبَرَ وَغَفَرَ إِنَّ ذَٲلِكَ لَمِنۡ عَزۡمِ ٱلۡأُمُورِ
And verily, whosoever shows patience and forgives that would truly be from the things recommended by Allah.
42:[44-48] The real losers are those who will lose on the Day of Resurrection
42:[49-50] It is Allah Who gives daughters and sons as He pleases
42:[51-53] It is not vouchsafed for any human being that Allah should speak to him face to face
42:44
وَمَن يُضۡلِلِ ٱللَّهُ فَمَا لَهُ ۥ مِن وَلِىٍّ۬ مِّنۢ بَعۡدِهِۦ‌ۗ
And whomsoever Allah sends astray, for him there is no Walee (protector) after Him.
وَتَرَى ٱلظَّـٰلِمِينَ لَمَّا رَأَوُاْ ٱلۡعَذَابَ يَقُولُونَ هَلۡ إِلَىٰ مَرَدٍّ۬ مِّن سَبِيلٍ۬
And you will see the Zalimoon (polytheists, wrong-doers, oppressors, etc.) when they behold the torment, they will say: ‘Is there any way of return (to the world)?‘
42:45
وَتَرَٮٰهُمۡ يُعۡرَضُونَ عَلَيۡهَا خَـٰشِعِينَ مِنَ ٱلذُّلِّ يَنظُرُونَ مِن طَرۡفٍ خَفِىٍّ۬‌ۗ
And you will see them brought forward to it (Hell) made humble by disgrace, (and) looking with stealthy glance.
وَقَالَ ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوٓاْ إِنَّ ٱلۡخَـٰسِرِينَ ٱلَّذِينَ خَسِرُوٓاْ أَنفُسَہُمۡ وَأَهۡلِيهِمۡ يَوۡمَ ٱلۡقِيَـٰمَةِ‌ۗ
And those who believe will say: ‘Verily, the losers are they who lose themselves and their families on the Day of Resurrection.
أَلَآ إِنَّ ٱلظَّـٰلِمِينَ فِى عَذَابٍ۬ مُّقِيمٍ۬
Verily, the Zalimoon (i.e. Al-Kafiroon (disbelievers in Allah, in His Oneness and in His Messenger SAW, polytheists, wrong-doers, etc.)) will be in a lasting torment.
42:46
وَمَا كَانَ لَهُم مِّنۡ أَوۡلِيَآءَ يَنصُرُونَهُم مِّن دُونِ ٱللَّهِ‌ۗ
And they will have no Auliya (protectors) to help them other than Allah.
وَمَن يُضۡلِلِ ٱللَّهُ فَمَا لَهُ ۥ مِن سَبِيلٍ
And he whom Allah sends astray, for him there is no way.
42:47
ٱسۡتَجِيبُواْ لِرَبِّكُم مِّن قَبۡلِ أَن يَأۡتِىَ يَوۡمٌ۬ لَّا مَرَدَّ لَهُ ۥ مِنَ ٱللَّهِ‌ۚ
Answer the Call of your Lord (i.e. accept the Islamic Monotheism, O mankind, and jinns) before there comes from Allah a Day which cannot be averted.
مَا لَكُم مِّن مَّلۡجَإٍ۬ يَوۡمَٮِٕذٍ۬ وَمَا لَكُم مِّن نَّڪِيرٍ۬
You will have no refuge on that Day nor there will be for you any denying (of your crimes as they are all recorded in the Book of your deeds).
42:48
فَإِنۡ أَعۡرَضُواْ فَمَآ أَرۡسَلۡنَـٰكَ عَلَيۡہِمۡ حَفِيظًا‌ۖ
But if they turn away (O Muhammad SAW from the Islamic Monotheism, which you have brought to them). We have not sent you (O Muhammad SAW) as a Hafeez (protector) over them (i.e. to take care of their deeds and to recompense them).
إِنۡ عَلَيۡكَ إِلَّا ٱلۡبَلَـٰغُ‌ۗ
Your duty is to convey (the Message).
وَإِنَّآ إِذَآ أَذَقۡنَا ٱلۡإِنسَـٰنَ مِنَّا رَحۡمَةً۬ فَرِحَ بِہَا‌ۖ
And verily, when We cause man to taste of Mercy from Us, he rejoices thereat,
وَإِن تُصِبۡہُمۡ سَيِّئَةُۢ بِمَا قَدَّمَتۡ أَيۡدِيهِمۡ
but when some ill befalls them because of the deeds which their hands have sent forth,
فَإِنَّ ٱلۡإِنسَـٰنَ كَفُورٌ۬
then verily, man (becomes) ingrate!
42:49
لِّلَّهِ مُلۡكُ ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٲتِ وَٱلۡأَرۡضِ‌ۚ
To Allah belongs the kingdom of the heavens and the earth.
يَخۡلُقُ مَا يَشَآءُ‌ۚ
He creates what He wills.
يَہَبُ لِمَن يَشَآءُ إِنَـٰثً۬ا وَيَهَبُ لِمَن يَشَآءُ ٱلذُّكُورَ
He bestows female (offspring) upon whom He wills, and bestows male (offspring) upon whom He wills.
42:50
أَوۡ يُزَوِّجُهُمۡ ذُكۡرَانً۬ا وَإِنَـٰثً۬ا‌ۖوَيَجۡعَلُ مَن يَشَآءُ عَقِيمًا‌ۚ
Or He bestows both males and females, and He renders barren whom He wills.
إِنَّهُ ۥ عَلِيمٌ۬ قَدِيرٌ۬
Verily, He is the All-Knower (Al-‘Aleem) and is Able to do all things.
42:51
وَمَا كَانَ لِبَشَرٍ أَن يُكَلِّمَهُ ٱللَّهُ إِلَّا وَحۡيًا أَوۡ مِن وَرَآىِٕ حِجَابٍ
It is not given to any human being that Allah should speak to him unless (it be) by Inspiration, or from behind a veil,
أَوۡ يُرۡسِلَ رَسُولاً۬ فَيُوحِىَ بِإِذۡنِهِۦ مَا يَشَآءُ‌ۚ
or (that) He sends a Messenger to reveal what He wills by His Leave.
إِنَّهُ ۥ عَلِىٌّ حَڪِيمٌ۬
Verily, He is Most High, Most Wise .
42:52
وَكَذَٲلِكَ أَوۡحَيۡنَآ إِلَيۡكَ رُوحً۬ا مِّنۡ أَمۡرِنَا‌ۚ
And thus We have sent to you (O Muhammad SAW) Ruhan (an Inspiration, and a Mercy) of Our Command.
مَا كُنتَ تَدۡرِى مَا ٱلۡكِتَـٰبُ وَلَا ٱلۡإِيمَـٰنُ
You knew not what is the Book, nor what is Faith?
وَلَـٰكِن جَعَلۡنَـٰهُ نُورً۬ا نَّہۡدِى بِهِۦ مَن نَّشَآءُ مِنۡ عِبَادِنَا‌ۚ
But We have made it (this Quran) a light wherewith We guide whosoever of Our slaves We will.
وَإِنَّكَ لَتَہۡدِىٓ إِلَىٰ صِرَٲطٍ۬ مُّسۡتَقِيمٍ۬
And verily, you (O Muhammad SAW) are indeed guiding (mankind) to the Straight Path (i.e. Allah‘s religion of Islamic Monotheism).
42:53
صِرَٲطِ ٱللَّهِ ٱلَّذِى لَهُ ۥ مَا فِى ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٲتِ وَمَا فِى ٱلۡأَرۡضِ‌ۗ
The Path of Allah, to Whom belongs all that is in the heavens and all that is in the earth.
أَلَآ إِلَى ٱللَّهِ تَصِيرُ ٱلۡأُمُورُ
Verily, all the matters at the end go to Allah (for decision).*********
