Saba
Sheba
34:[1-5] The Hour is surely going to come and Judgement shall be established to reward the believers and punish those who discredit Allah‘s revelations
34:[6-9] Those who do not believe in the hereafter are doomed
34:1
ٱلۡحَمۡدُ لِلَّهِ ٱلَّذِى لَهُ ۥ مَا فِى ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٲتِ وَمَا فِى ٱلۡأَرۡضِ
All the praises and thanks be to Allah (الله), to Whom belongs all that is in the heavens and all that is in the earth.
وَلَهُ ٱلۡحَمۡدُ فِى ٱلۡأَخِرَةِۚ
His is all the praises and thanks in the Hereafter,
وَهُوَ ٱلۡحَكِيمُ ٱلۡخَبِيرُ
and He is the All Wise, the All Aware.
34:2
يَعۡلَمُ مَا يَلِجُ فِى ٱلۡأَرۡضِ وَمَا يَخۡرُجُ مِنۡہَا
He knows that which goes into the earth and that which comes forth from it,
وَمَا يَنزِلُ مِنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ وَمَا يَعۡرُجُ فِيہَاۚ
and that which descend from the heaven and that which ascends to it.
وَهُوَ ٱلرَّحِيمُ ٱلۡغَفُورُ
And He is the Most Merciful (Ar-Raheem (ٱلْرَّحِيْمُ)), the Oft Forgiving.
34:3
وَقَالَ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُواْ لَا تَأۡتِينَا ٱلسَّاعَةُۖ
Those who disbelieve say: ‘The Hour will not come to us.‘
قُلۡ بَلَىٰ وَرَبِّى لَتَأۡتِيَنَّڪُمۡ عَـٰلِمِ ٱلۡغَيۡبِۖ
Say: ‘Yes, by my Lord, it will come to you.‘
(Allah (الله), He is) the All Knower of the unseen,
لَا يَعۡزُبُ عَنۡهُ مِثۡقَالُ ذَرَّةٍ۬ فِى ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٲتِ وَلَا فِى ٱلۡأَرۡضِ وَلَآ أَصۡغَرُ مِن ذَٲلِكَ وَلَآ أَڪۡبَرُ إِلَّا فِى ڪِتَـٰبٍ۬ مُّبِينٍ۬
not even the weight of an atom (or a small ant) or less than that or greater, escapes from His Knowledge in the heavens or in the earth, but it is in a Clear Book (Al Lauh Al Mahfooz).
34:4
لِّيَجۡزِىَ ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُواْ وَعَمِلُواْ ٱلصَّـٰلِحَـٰتِۚ
That He may recompense those who believe (in the Oneness of Allah (الله) Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism) and do righteous good deeds.
أُوْلَـٰٓٮِٕكَ لَهُم مَّغۡفِرَةٌ۬ وَرِزۡقٌ۬ ڪَرِيمٌ۬
Those, theirs is forgiveness and Rizqun Kareem (generous provision, i.e. Paradise (الجنة)).
34:5
وَٱلَّذِينَ سَعَوۡ فِىٓ ءَايَـٰتِنَا مُعَـٰجِزِينَ أُوْلَـٰٓٮِٕكَ لَهُمۡ عَذَابٌ۬ مِّن رِّجۡزٍ أَلِيمٌ۬
But those who strive against Our Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, etc.) to frustrate them, those, for them will be a severe painful torment.
34:6
وَيَرَى ٱلَّذِينَ أُوتُواْ ٱلۡعِلۡمَ ٱلَّذِىٓ أُنزِلَ إِلَيۡكَ مِن رَّبِّكَ هُوَ ٱلۡحَقَّ
And those who have been given knowledge see that what is revealed to you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) from your Lord is the truth,
وَيَهۡدِىٓ إِلَىٰ صِرَٲطِ ٱلۡعَزِيزِ ٱلۡحَمِيدِ
and guides to the Path of the Exalted in Might, Owner of all praise.
34:7
وَقَالَ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُواْ هَلۡ نَدُلُّكُمۡ عَلَىٰ رَجُلٍ۬ يُنَبِّئُكُمۡ
Those who disbelieve say: ‘Shall we direct you to a man (Muhammad (محمد) SAW) who will tell you (that)
إِذَا مُزِّقۡتُمۡ كُلَّ مُمَزَّقٍ إِنَّكُمۡ لَفِى خَلۡقٍ۬ جَدِيدٍ
when you have become fully disintegrated into dust with full dispersion, then, you will be created (again) anew?‘
34:8
أَفۡتَرَىٰ عَلَى ٱللَّهِ كَذِبًا أَم بِهِۦ جِنَّۗةُۢ
Has he (Muhammad (محمد) SAW) invented a lie against Allah (الله), or is there a madness in him?
بَلِ ٱلَّذِينَ لَا يُؤۡمِنُونَ بِٱلۡأَخِرَةِ فِى ٱلۡعَذَابِ وَٱلضَّلَـٰلِ ٱلۡبَعِيدِ
Nay, but those who disbelieve in the Hereafter are (themselves) in a torment, and in far error.
34:9
أَفَلَمۡ يَرَوۡاْ إِلَىٰ مَا بَيۡنَ أَيۡدِيهِمۡ وَمَا خَلۡفَهُم مِّنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ وَٱلۡأَرۡضِۚ
If We will, We shall sink the earth with them, or cause a piece of the heaven to fall upon them.
إِن نَّشَأۡ نَخۡسِفۡ بِهِمُ ٱلۡأَرۡضَ أَوۡ نُسۡقِطۡ عَلَيۡہِمۡ كِسَفً۬ا مِّنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِۚ
See they not what is before them and what is behind them, of the heaven and the earth?
إِنَّ فِى ذَٲلِكَ لَأَيَةً۬ لِّكُلِّ عَبۡدٍ۬ مُّنِيبٍ۬
Verily, in this is a sign for every faithful believer that (believes in the Oneness of Allah (الله)), and turns to Allah (الله) (in all affairs with humility and in repentance).
34:[10-14] The mountains and birds used to sing Allah‘s praise with the Prophet Dawõõd. Allah subjected the winds and Jinns to the Prophet Sulaimãn. Claim of people that Jinns know the unseen is wrong
34:[15-21] The people of Sabã rejected Allah‘s blessings and disbelieved in the hereafter, so Allah made them merely a tale of the past
34:10
وَلَقَدۡ ءَاتَيۡنَا دَاوُ ۥدَ مِنَّا فَضۡلاً۬ۖ
And indeed We bestowed grace on David from Us (saying):
يَـٰجِبَالُ أَوِّبِى مَعَهُ ۥ وَٱلطَّيۡرَۖ
O you mountains. Glorify (Allah (الله)) with him! And you birds (also)!
وَأَلَنَّا لَهُ ٱلۡحَدِيدَ
And We made the iron soft for him.‘
34:11
أَنِ ٱعۡمَلۡ سَـٰبِغَـٰتٍ۬ وَقَدِّرۡ فِى ٱلسَّرۡدِۖ
Saying: ‘Make you perfect coats of mail, balancing well the rings of chain armour,
وَٱعۡمَلُواْ صَـٰلِحًاۖ
and work you (men) righteousness.
إِنِّى بِمَا تَعۡمَلُونَ بَصِيرٌ۬
Truly, I am All Seer of what you do.‘
34:12
وَلِسُلَيۡمَـٰنَ ٱلرِّيحَ غُدُوُّهَا شَہۡرٌ۬ وَرَوَاحُهَا شَہۡرٌ۬ۖ
And to Solomon (We subjected) the wind, its morning (stride from sunrise till midnoon) was a months (journey), and its afternoon (stride from the midday decline of the sun to sunset) was a months (journey i.e. in one day he could travel two months journey).
وَأَسَلۡنَا لَهُ ۥ عَيۡنَ ٱلۡقِطۡرِۖ
And We caused a fount of (molten) brass to flow for him,
وَمِنَ ٱلۡجِنِّ مَن يَعۡمَلُ بَيۡنَ يَدَيۡهِ بِإِذۡنِ رَبِّهِۖۦ
and there were jinns that worked in front of him, by the Leave of his Lord,
وَمَن يَزِغۡ مِنۡہُمۡ عَنۡ أَمۡرِنَا نُذِقۡهُ مِنۡ عَذَابِ ٱلسَّعِيرِ
and whosoever of them turned aside from Our Command, We shall cause him to taste of the torment of the blazing Fire.
34:13
يَعۡمَلُونَ لَهُ ۥ مَا يَشَآءُ مِن مَّحَـٰرِيبَ وَتَمَـٰثِيلَ وَجِفَانٍ۬ كَٱلۡجَوَابِ وَقُدُورٍ۬ رَّاسِيَـٰتٍۚ
They worked for him what he desired, (making) high rooms, images, basins as large as reservoirs, and (cooking) cauldrons fixed (in their places).
ٱعۡمَلُوٓاْ ءَالَ دَاوُ ۥدَ شُكۡرً۬اۚ
Work you, O family of Dawud (David), with thanks!‘
وَقَلِيلٌ۬ مِّنۡ عِبَادِىَ ٱلشَّكُورُ
But few of My slaves are grateful.
34:14
فَلَمَّا قَضَيۡنَا عَلَيۡهِ ٱلۡمَوۡتَ مَا دَلَّهُمۡ عَلَىٰ مَوۡتِهِۦۤ إِلَّا دَآبَّةُ ٱلۡأَرۡضِ تَأۡڪُلُ مِنسَأَتَهُۖ ۥ
Then when We decreed death for him (Suleiman (سليمان) (Solomon)), nothing informed them (jinns) of his death except a little worm of the earth, which kept (slowly) gnawing away at his stick,
فَلَمَّا خَرَّ تَبَيَّنَتِ ٱلۡجِنُّ أَن لَّوۡ كَانُواْ يَعۡلَمُونَ ٱلۡغَيۡبَ مَا لَبِثُواْ فِى ٱلۡعَذَابِ ٱلۡمُهِينِ
so when he fell down, the jinns saw clearly that if they had known the unseen, they would not have stayed in the humiliating torment.
34:15
لَقَدۡ كَانَ لِسَبَإٍ۬ فِى مَسۡكَنِهِمۡ ءَايَةٌ۬ۖ
Indeed there was for Saba (Sheba (سبأ)) a sign in their dwelling place,
جَنَّتَانِ عَن يَمِينٍ۬ وَشِمَالٍ۬ۖ

two gardens on the right hand and on the left (and it was said to them)
كُلُواْ مِن رِّزۡقِ رَبِّكُمۡ وَٱشۡكُرُواْ لَهُۚ ۥ
Eat of the provision of your Lord, and be grateful to Him,
بَلۡدَةٌ۬ طَيِّبَةٌ۬ وَرَبٌّ غَفُورٌ۬
a fair land and an Oft Forgiving Lord.
34:16
فَأَعۡرَضُواْ فَأَرۡسَلۡنَا عَلَيۡہِمۡ سَيۡلَ ٱلۡعَرِمِ
But they turned away (from the obedience of Allah (الله)), so We sent against them Sail AlArim (flood released from the dam),
وَبَدَّلۡنَـٰهُم بِجَنَّتَيۡہِمۡ جَنَّتَيۡنِ ذَوَاتَىۡ أُڪُلٍ خَمۡطٍ۬ وَأَثۡلٍ۬ وَشَىۡءٍ۬ مِّن سِدۡرٍ۬ قَلِيلٍ۬
and We converted their two gardens into gardens producing bitter bad fruit, and tamarisks, and some few lote trees.
34:17
ذَٲلِكَ جَزَيۡنَـٰهُم بِمَا كَفَرُواْۖ
Like this We requited them because they were ungrateful disbelievers.
وَهَلۡ نُجَـٰزِىٓ إِلَّا ٱلۡكَفُورَ
And never do We requite in such a way except those who are ungrateful, (disbelievers).
34:18
وَجَعَلۡنَا بَيۡنَہُمۡ وَبَيۡنَ ٱلۡقُرَى ٱلَّتِى بَـٰرَڪۡنَا فِيہَا قُرً۬ى ظَـٰهِرَةً۬
And We placed between them and the towns which We had blessed, towns easy to be seen,
وَقَدَّرۡنَا فِيہَا ٱلسَّيۡرَۖ
and We made the stages (of journey) between them easy (saying):
سِيرُواْ فِيہَا لَيَالِىَ وَأَيَّامًا ءَامِنِينَ
Travel in them safely both by night and day.‘
34:19
فَقَالُواْ رَبَّنَا بَـٰعِدۡ بَيۡنَ أَسۡفَارِنَاوَظَلَمُوٓاْ أَنفُسَہُمۡ
But they said: ‘Our Lord! Make the stages between our journey longer,‘ and they wronged themselves,
فَجَعَلۡنَـٰهُمۡ أَحَادِيثَ وَمَزَّقۡنَـٰهُمۡ كُلَّ مُمَزَّقٍۚ
so We made them as tales (in the land), and We dispersed them all, totally.
إِنَّ فِى ذَٲلِكَ لَأَيَـٰتٍ۬ لِّكُلِّ صَبَّارٍ۬ شَكُورٍ۬
Verily, in this are indeed signs for every steadfast grateful (person).
34:20
وَلَقَدۡ صَدَّقَ عَلَيۡہِمۡ إِبۡلِيسُ ظَنَّهُ فَٱتَّبَعُوهُ إِلَّا فَرِيقً۬ا مِّنَ ٱلۡمُؤۡمِنِينَ
And indeed Iblees (إبليس) (Satan (الشيطان)) did prove true his thought about them, and they followed him, all except a group of true believers (in the Oneness of Allah (الله)).
34:21
وَمَا ڪَانَ لَهُ ۥ عَلَيۡہِم مِّن سُلۡطَـٰنٍ
And he (Iblees (إبليس) Satan (الشيطان)) had no authority over them,
إِلَّا لِنَعۡلَمَ مَن يُؤۡمِنُ بِٱلۡأَخِرَةِ مِمَّنۡ هُوَ مِنۡهَا فِى شَكٍّ۬ۗ
except that We might test him, who believes in the Hereafter from him who is in doubt about it.
وَرَبُّكَ عَلَىٰ كُلِّ شَىۡءٍ حَفِيظٌ۬
And your Lord is a Hafiz over everything. (All Knower of everything i.e. He keeps record of each and every person as regards deeds, and then He will reward them accordingly).
34:[22-30] No intercession before Allah can avail anyone except for whom He permits it. The Prophet Muhammad is sent for the entire mankind
34:22
قُلِ ٱدۡعُواْ ٱلَّذِينَ زَعَمۡتُم مِّن دُونِ ٱللَّهِۖ
Say: (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW to those polytheists, pagans, etc.)
Call upon those whom you assert (to be associate gods) besides Allah (الله),
لَا يَمۡلِڪُونَ مِثۡقَالَ ذَرَّةٍ۬ فِى ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٲتِ وَلَا فِى ٱلۡأَرۡضِ
they possess not even the weight of an atom (or a small ant), either in the heavens or on the earth,
وَمَا لَهُمۡ فِيهِمَا مِن شِرۡكٍ۬ وَمَا لَهُ ۥ مِنۡہُم مِّن ظَهِيرٍ۬
nor have they any share in either, nor there is for Him any supporter from among them.
34:23
وَلَا تَنفَعُ ٱلشَّفَـٰعَةُ عِندَهُ ۥۤ إِلَّا لِمَنۡ أَذِنَ لَهُۚ ۥ
Intercession with Him profits not, except for him whom He permits.
حَتَّىٰٓ إِذَا فُزِّعَ عَن قُلُوبِهِمۡ قَالُواْ مَاذَا قَالَ رَبُّكُمۡۖ
Until when fear is banished from their (angels) hearts, they (angels) say: ‘What is it that your Lord has said?‘
قَالُواْ ٱلۡحَقَّۖ
They say: ‘The truth.
وَهُوَ ٱلۡعَلِىُّ ٱلۡكَبِيرُ
And He is the Most High, the Most Great.‘
34:24
قُلۡ مَن يَرۡزُقُكُم مِّنَ ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٲتِ وَٱلۡأَرۡضِۖ
Say (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW to these polytheists, pagans, etc.) ‘Who gives you provision from the heavens and the earth?‘
قُلِ ٱللَّهُۖ
Say: ‘Allah (الله),
وَإِنَّآ أَوۡ إِيَّاڪُمۡ لَعَلَىٰ هُدًى أَوۡ فِى ضَلَـٰلٍ۬ مُّبِينٍ۬
And verily, (either) we or you are rightly guided or in a plain error.‘
34:25
قُل لَّا تُسۡـَٔلُونَ عَمَّآ أَجۡرَمۡنَا وَلَا نُسۡـَٔلُ عَمَّا تَعۡمَلُونَ
Say (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW to these polytheists, pagans, etc.) ‘You will not be asked about our sins, nor shall we be asked of what you do.‘
34:26
قُلۡ يَجۡمَعُ بَيۡنَنَا رَبُّنَا ثُمَّ يَفۡتَحُ بَيۡنَنَا بِٱلۡحَقِّ
Say: ‘Our Lord will assemble us all together (on the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة)), then He will judge between us with truth.
وَهُوَ ٱلۡفَتَّاحُ ٱلۡعَلِيمُ
And He is the (Most Trustworthy) All-Knowing (Al-‘Alee (ٱلْعَلِيُّ)m (ٱلْعَلِيمُ)) Judge.‘
34:27
قُلۡ أَرُونِىَ ٱلَّذِينَ أَلۡحَقۡتُم بِهِۦ شُرَڪَآءَۖ
Say (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW to these polytheists and pagans): ‘Show me those whom you have joined to Him as partners.
كَلَّاۚ
Nay (there are not at all any partners with Him)!
بَلۡ هُوَ ٱللَّهُ ٱلۡعَزِيزُ ٱلۡحَكِيمُ
But He is Allah (الله) (Alone), the Almighty, the All Wise.‘
34:28
وَمَآ أَرۡسَلۡنَـٰكَ إِلَّا ڪَآفَّةً۬ لِّلنَّاسِ بَشِيرً۬ا وَنَذِيرً۬اوَلَـٰكِنَّ أَڪۡثَرَ ٱلنَّاسِ لَا يَعۡلَمُونَ
And We have not sent you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) except as a giver of glad tidings and a warner to all mankind, but most of men know not.
34:29
وَيَقُولُونَ مَتَىٰ هَـٰذَا ٱلۡوَعۡدُ إِن ڪُنتُمۡ صَـٰدِقِينَ
And they say: ‘When is this promise (i.e. the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة) will be fulfilled) if you are truthful?‘
34:30
قُل لَّكُم مِّيعَادُ يَوۡمٍ۬ لَّا تَسۡتَـٔۡخِرُونَ عَنۡهُ سَاعَةً۬ وَلَا تَسۡتَقۡدِمُونَ
Say (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW): ‘The appointment to you is for a Day, which you cannot put back for an hour (or a moment) nor put forward.‘
34:[31-36] Those who disbelieve in the Qur‘an and prior scriptures, will have yokes placed around their necks before being tossed into hell. Wealth and children are not the indications of Allah‘s pleasure
34:31
وَقَالَ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُواْ لَن نُّؤۡمِنَ بِهَـٰذَا ٱلۡقُرۡءَانِ وَلَا بِٱلَّذِى بَيۡنَ يَدَيۡهِۗ
And those who disbelieve say: ‘We believe not in this Quran (القرآن) nor in that which was before it,‘
وَلَوۡ تَرَىٰٓ إِذِ ٱلظَّـٰلِمُونَ مَوۡقُوفُونَ عِندَ رَبِّہِمۡ يَرۡجِعُ بَعۡضُهُمۡ إِلَىٰ بَعۡضٍ ٱلۡقَوۡلَ
but if you could see when the Zalimoon (polytheists and wrongdoers, etc.) will be made to stand before their Lord, how they will cast the (blaming) word one to another!
يَقُولُ ٱلَّذِينَ ٱسۡتُضۡعِفُواْ لِلَّذِينَ ٱسۡتَكۡبَرُواْلَوۡلَآ أَنتُمۡ لَكُنَّا مُؤۡمِنِينَ
Those who were deemed weak will say to those who were arrogant: ‘Had it not been for you, we should certainly have been believers!‘
34:32
قَالَ ٱلَّذِينَ ٱسۡتَكۡبَرُواْ لِلَّذِينَ ٱسۡتُضۡعِفُوٓاْ
And those who were arrogant will say to those who were deemed weak:
أَنَحۡنُ صَدَدۡنَـٰكُمۡ عَنِ ٱلۡهُدَىٰ بَعۡدَ إِذۡ جَآءَكُمۖ
Did we keep you back from guidance after it had come to you?
بَلۡ كُنتُم مُّجۡرِمِينَ
Nay, but you were Mujrimoon (polytheists, sinners, criminals, disobedient to Allah (الله), etc.).
34:33
وَقَالَ ٱلَّذِينَ ٱسۡتُضۡعِفُواْ لِلَّذِينَ ٱسۡتَكۡبَرُواْ
Those who were deemed weak will say to those who were arrogant:
بَلۡ مَكۡرُ ٱلَّيۡلِ وَٱلنَّهَارِ
Nay, but it was your plotting by night and day,
إِذۡ تَأۡمُرُونَنَآ أَن نَّكۡفُرَ بِٱللَّهِ وَنَجۡعَلَ لَهُ ۥۤ أَندَادً۬اۚ
when you ordered us to disbelieve in Allah (الله) and set up rivals to Him!‘
وَأَسَرُّواْ ٱلنَّدَامَةَ لَمَّا رَأَوُاْ ٱلۡعَذَابَ وَجَعَلۡنَا ٱلۡأَغۡلَـٰلَ فِىٓ أَعۡنَاقِ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُواْۚ
And each of them (parties) will conceal their own regrets (for disobeying Allah (الله) during this worldly life), when they behold the torment. And We shall put iron collars round the necks of those who disbelieved.
هَلۡ يُجۡزَوۡنَ إِلَّا مَا كَانُواْ يَعۡمَلُونَ
Are they requited aught except what they used to do?
34:34
وَمَآ أَرۡسَلۡنَا فِى قَرۡيَةٍ۬ مِّن نَّذِيرٍ إِلَّا قَالَ مُتۡرَفُوهَآ
And We did not send a warner to a township, but those who were given the worldly wealth and luxuries among them said:
إِنَّا بِمَآ أُرۡسِلۡتُم بِهِۦ كَـٰفِرُونَ
We believe not in the (Message) with which you have been sent.‘
34:35
وَقَالُواْ نَحۡنُ أَڪۡثَرُ أَمۡوَٲلاً۬ وَأَوۡلَـٰدً۬ا وَمَا نَحۡنُ بِمُعَذَّبِينَ
And they say: ‘We are more in wealth and in children, and we are not going to be punished.‘
34:36
قُلۡ إِنَّ رَبِّى يَبۡسُطُ ٱلرِّزۡقَ لِمَن يَشَآءُ وَيَقۡدِرُ
Say (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW): ‘Verily, my Lord enlarges and restricts the provision to whom He pleases,
وَلَـٰكِنَّ أَڪۡثَرَ ٱلنَّاسِ لَا يَعۡلَمُونَ
but most men know not.‘
34:[37-45] It is belief that brings you close to Allah, not the wealth or children. Whatever you spend in charity, Allah will pay you back. Unbeliever‘s statements about the Prophet and Al-Qur‘an
34:37
وَمَآ أَمۡوَٲلُكُمۡ وَلَآ أَوۡلَـٰدُكُم بِٱلَّتِى تُقَرِّبُكُمۡ عِندَنَا زُلۡفَىٰٓ
And it is not your wealth, nor your children that bring you nearer to Us (i.e. pleases Allah (الله)),
إِلَّا مَنۡ ءَامَنَ وَعَمِلَ صَـٰلِحً۬ا
but only he (will please Us) who believes (in the Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism), and does righteous deeds;
فَأُوْلَـٰٓٮِٕكَ لَهُمۡ جَزَآءُ ٱلضِّعۡفِ بِمَا عَمِلُواْوَهُمۡ فِى ٱلۡغُرُفَـٰتِ ءَامِنُونَ
as for such, there will be twofold reward for what they did, and they will reside in the high dwellings (Paradise (الجنة)) in peace and security.
34:38
وَٱلَّذِينَ يَسۡعَوۡنَ فِىٓ ءَايَـٰتِنَا مُعَـٰجِزِينَ أُوْلَـٰٓٮِٕكَ فِى ٱلۡعَذَابِ مُحۡضَرُونَ
And those who strive against Our Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, etc.), to frustrate them, will be brought to the torment.
34:39
قُلۡ إِنَّ رَبِّى يَبۡسُطُ ٱلرِّزۡقَ لِمَن يَشَآءُ مِنۡ عِبَادِهِۦ وَيَقۡدِرُ لَهُ ۥۚ
Say: ‘Truly, my Lord enlarges the provision for whom He wills of His slaves, and (also) restricts (it) for him,
وَمَآ أَنفَقۡتُم مِّن شَىۡءٍ۬ فَهُوَ يُخۡلِفُهُۖ ۥ
and whatsoever you spend of anything (in Allah (الله)‘s Cause), He will replace it.
وَهُوَ خَيۡرُ ٱلرَّٲزِقِينَ
And He is the Best of providers.‘
34:40
وَيَوۡمَ يَحۡشُرُهُمۡ جَمِيعً۬ا ثُمَّ يَقُولُ لِلۡمَلَـٰٓٮِٕكَةِ
And (remember) the Day when He will gather them all together, and then will say to the angels:
أَهَـٰٓؤُلَآءِ إِيَّاكُمۡ ڪَانُواْ يَعۡبُدُونَ
Was it you that these people used to worship?‘
34:41
قَالُواْ سُبۡحَـٰنَكَ أَنتَ وَلِيُّنَا مِن دُونِهِمۖ
They (angels) will say: ‘Glorified be You! You are our Walee (Lord) instead of them.
بَلۡ كَانُواْ يَعۡبُدُونَ ٱلۡجِنَّۖ أَڪۡثَرُهُم بِہِم مُّؤۡمِنُونَ
Nay, but they used to worship the jinns; most of them were believers in them.‘
34:42
فَٱلۡيَوۡمَ لَا يَمۡلِكُ بَعۡضُكُمۡ لِبَعۡضٍ۬ نَّفۡعً۬ا وَلَا ضَرًّ۬ا
So Today (i.e. the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة)), none of you can profit or harm one another.
وَنَقُولُ لِلَّذِينَ ظَلَمُواْ ذُوقُواْ عَذَابَ ٱلنَّارِ ٱلَّتِى كُنتُم بِہَا تُكَذِّبُونَ
And We shall say to those who did wrong:(i.e. worshipped others (like angels, jinns, prophets, saints, righteous persons, etc.) along with Allah (الله)) ‘Taste the torment of the Fire which you used to belie.
34:43
وَإِذَا تُتۡلَىٰ عَلَيۡہِمۡ ءَايَـٰتُنَا بَيِّنَـٰتٍ۬ قَالُواْ
And when Our Clear Verses are recited to them, they say:
مَا هَـٰذَآ إِلَّا رَجُلٌ۬ يُرِيدُ أَن يَصُدَّكُمۡ عَمَّا كَانَ يَعۡبُدُ ءَابَآؤُكُمۡ
This (Muhammad (محمد) SAW) is naught but a man who wishes to hinder you from that which your fathers used to worship.‘
وَقَالُواْ مَا هَـٰذَآ إِلَّآ إِفۡكٌ۬ مُّفۡتَرً۬ىۚ
And they say: ‘This is nothing but an invented lie.‘
وَقَالَ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُواْ لِلۡحَقِّ لَمَّا جَآءَهُمۡ إِنۡ هَـٰذَآ إِلَّا سِحۡرٌ۬ مُّبِينٌ۬
And those who disbelieve say of the truth when it has come to them: (i.e. Prophet Muhammad (محمد) SAW when Allah (الله) sent him as a Messenger with proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, etc.) ‘This is nothing but evident magic!‘
34:44
وَمَآ ءَاتَيۡنَـٰهُم مِّن كُتُبٍ۬ يَدۡرُسُونَہَاۖ
And We had not given them Scriptures which they could study,
وَمَآ أَرۡسَلۡنَآ إِلَيۡہِمۡ قَبۡلَكَ مِن نَّذِيرٍ۬
nor sent to them before you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) any warner (Messenger).
34:45
وَكَذَّبَ ٱلَّذِينَ مِن قَبۡلِهِمۡ
And those before them belied;
وَمَا بَلَغُواْ مِعۡشَارَ مَآ ءَاتَيۡنَـٰهُمۡ فَكَذَّبُواْ رُسُلِىۖ
these have not received one tenth (1/10th) of what We had granted to those (of old), yet they belied My Messengers,
فَكَيۡفَ كَانَ نَكِيرِ then how (terrible) was My denial (punishment)!
34:[46-50] The unbelievers are asked to ponder upon their wrong statements. The truth has come, falsehood neither originates nor restores anything
34:[51-54] On the Day of Judgement the unbelievers will want to believe but it will be of no avail to them
34:46
قُلۡ إِنَّمَآ أَعِظُكُم بِوَٲحِدَةٍۖ
Say (to them O Muhammad (محمد) SAW): ‘I exhort you on one (thing) only:
أَن تَقُومُواْ لِلَّهِ مَثۡنَىٰ وَفُرَٲدَىٰ ثُمَّ تَتَفَڪَّرُواْۚ
that you stand up for Allah (الله)‘s sake in pairs and singly, and reflect (within yourselves the life history of the Prophet SAW):
مَا بِصَاحِبِكُم مِّن جِنَّةٍۚ
there is no madness in your companion (Muhammad (محمد) SAW),
إِنۡ هُوَ إِلَّا نَذِيرٌ۬ لَّكُم بَيۡنَ يَدَىۡ عَذَابٍ۬ شَدِيدٍ۬
he is only a warner to you in face of a severe torment.‘
34:47
قُلۡ مَا سَأَلۡتُكُم مِّنۡ أَجۡرٍ۬ فَهُوَ لَكُمۡۖ
Say (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW): ‘Whatever wage I might have asked of you is yours.
إِنۡ أَجۡرِىَ إِلَّا عَلَى ٱللَّهِۖ
My wage is from Allah (الله) only.
وَهُوَ عَلَىٰ كُلِّ شَىۡءٍ۬ شَہِيدٌ۬
and He is Witness over all things.‘
34:48
قُلۡ إِنَّ رَبِّى يَقۡذِفُ بِٱلۡحَقِّ عَلَّـٰمُ ٱلۡغُيُوبِ
Say (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW): ‘Verily! My Lord sends down Inspiration and makes apparent the truth (i.e. this Revelation that had come to me), the All Knower of the Ghaib (unseen).
34:49
قُلۡ جَآءَ ٱلۡحَقُّ وَمَا يُبۡدِئُ ٱلۡبَـٰطِلُ وَمَا يُعِيدُ
Say (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW): ‘The truth (the Quran (القرآن) and Allah (الله)‘s Inspiration) has come, and AlBatil (falsehood - Iblees (إبليس) (Satan (الشيطان))) can neither create anything nor resurrect (anything).‘
34:50
قُلۡ إِن ضَلَلۡتُ فَإِنَّمَآ أَضِلُّ عَلَىٰ نَفۡسِىۖ
Say:‘If (even) I go astray, I shall stray only to my own loss.
وَإِنِ ٱهۡتَدَيۡتُ فَبِمَا يُوحِىٓ إِلَىَّ رَبِّىٓۚ
But if I remain guided, it is because of the Inspiration of my Lord to me.
إِنَّهُ ۥ سَمِيعٌ۬ قَرِيبٌ۬
Truly, He is All Hearer, Ever Near (to all things).‘
34:51
وَلَوۡ تَرَىٰٓ إِذۡ فَزِعُواْ فَلَا فَوۡتَ وَأُخِذُواْ مِن مَّكَانٍ۬ قَرِيبٍ۬
And if you could but see, when they will be terrified with no escape (for them), and they will be seized from a near place.
34:52
وَقَالُوٓاْ ءَامَنَّا بِهِۦ
And they will say (in the Hereafter): ‘We do believe (now);‘
وَأَنَّىٰ لَهُمُ ٱلتَّنَاوُشُ مِن مَّكَانِۭ بَعِيدٍ۬
but how could they receive (Faith and the acceptance of their repentance by Allah (الله)) from a place so far off (i.e. to return to the worldly life again).
34:53
وَقَدۡ ڪَفَرُواْ بِهِۦ مِن قَبۡلُۖ وَيَقۡذِفُونَ بِٱلۡغَيۡبِ مِن مَّكَانِۭ بَعِيدٍ۬
Indeed they did disbelieve (in the Oneness of Allah (الله), Islam (الإسلام), the Quran (القرآن) and Muhammad (محمد) SAW) before (in this world), and they (used to) conjecture about the unseen (i.e. the Hereafter, Hell (جهنم), Paradise (الجنة), Resurrection and the Promise of Allah (الله), etc. (by saying) all that is untrue), from a far place.
34:54
وَحِيلَ بَيۡنَہُمۡ وَبَيۡنَ مَا يَشۡتَہُونَ كَمَا فُعِلَ بِأَشۡيَاعِهِم مِّن قَبۡلُۚ
And a barrier will be set between them and that which they desire (i.e. At-Taubah (turning to Allah (الله) in repentance) and the accepting of Faith etc.), as was done in the past with the people of their kind.
إِنَّہُمۡ كَانُواْ فِى شَكٍّ۬ مُّرِيبِۭ
Verily, they have been in grave doubt.*********
