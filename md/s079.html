An-Naazi'aat
Those Who Tear Out, Those Who Drag Forth, Soul-snatchers, Those That Rise
79:[1-14] Death, Day of Resurrection and Life after death
79:[15-26] The story of Musa when he called Fir‘on to his Rabb, he denied Allah and was seized for punishment
79:1
وَٱلنَّـٰزِعَـٰتِ غَرۡقً۬ا
By those (angels) who pull out (the souls of the disbelievers and the wicked) with great violence;
79:2
وَٱلنَّـٰشِطَـٰتِ نَشۡطً۬ا
By those (angels) who gently take out (the souls of the believers);
79:3
وَٱلسَّـٰبِحَـٰتِ سَبۡحً۬ا
And by those that swim along (i.e. angels or planets in their orbits, etc.).
79:4
فَٱلسَّـٰبِقَـٰتِ سَبۡقً۬ا
And by those that press forward as in a race (i.e. the angels or stars or the horses, etc.).
79:5
فَٱلۡمُدَبِّرَٲتِ أَمۡرً۬ا
And by those angels who arrange to do the Commands of their Lord,(so verily, you disbelievers will be called to account)
79:6
يَوۡمَ تَرۡجُفُ ٱلرَّاجِفَةُ
On the Day (when the first blowing of the Trumpet is blown), the earth and the mountains will shake violently (and everybody will die),
79:7
تَتۡبَعُهَا ٱلرَّادِفَةُ
The second blowing of the Trumpet follows it (and everybody will be raised up),
79:8
قُلُوبٌ۬ يَوۡمَٮِٕذٍ۬ وَاجِفَةٌ
(Some) hearts that Day will shake with fear and anxiety.
79:9
أَبۡصَـٰرُهَا خَـٰشِعَةٌ۬
Their eyes cast down.
79:10
يَقُولُونَ أَءِنَّا لَمَرۡدُودُونَ فِى ٱلۡحَافِرَةِ
They say: ‘Shall we indeed be returned to (our) former state of life?
79:11
أَءِذَا كُنَّا عِظَـٰمً۬ا نَّخِرَةً۬
Even after we are crumbled bones?‘
79:12
قَالُواْ تِلۡكَ إِذً۬ا كَرَّةٌ خَاسِرَةٌ۬
They say: ‘It would in that case, be a return with loss!‘
79:13
فَإِنَّمَا هِىَ زَجۡرَةٌ۬ وَٲحِدَةٌ۬
But only, it will be a single Zajrah (shout (i.e., the second blowing of the Trumpet)).(See Verse37:19).
79:14
فَإِذَا هُم بِٱلسَّاهِرَةِ
When, behold, they find themselves over the earth alive after their death,
79:15
هَلۡ أَتَٮٰكَ حَدِيثُ مُوسَىٰٓ
Has there come to you the story of Musa (Moosa (موسى))?
79:16
إِذۡ نَادَٮٰهُ رَبُّهُ ۥ بِٱلۡوَادِ ٱلۡمُقَدَّسِ طُوًى
When his Lord called him in the sacred valley of Toowa,
79:17
ٱذۡهَبۡ إِلَىٰ فِرۡعَوۡنَ إِنَّهُ ۥ طَغَىٰ
Go to Firaun (Pharaoh (فرعون)), verily, he has transgressed all bounds (in crimes, sins, polytheism, disbelief, etc.).
79:18
فَقُلۡ هَل لَّكَ إِلَىٰٓ أَن تَزَكَّىٰ
And say to him: ‘Would you purify yourself (from the sin of disbelief by becoming a believer)‘,
79:19
وَأَهۡدِيَكَ إِلَىٰ رَبِّكَ فَتَخۡشَىٰ
And that I guide you to your Lord, so you should fear Him?
79:20
فَأَرَٮٰهُ ٱلۡأَيَةَ ٱلۡكُبۡرَىٰ
Then (Musa (Moosa (موسى))) showed him the great sign (miracles).
79:21
فَكَذَّبَ وَعَصَىٰ
But (Firaun (Pharaoh (فرعون))) belied and disobeyed;
79:22
ثُمَّ أَدۡبَرَ يَسۡعَىٰ
Then he turned his back, striving hard (against Allah (الله)).
79:23
فَحَشَرَ فَنَادَىٰ
Then he gathered his people and cried aloud,
79:24
فَقَالَ أَنَا۟ رَبُّكُمُ ٱلۡأَعۡلَىٰ
Saying: ‘I am your lord, most high‘,
79:25
فَأَخَذَهُ ٱللَّهُ نَكَالَ ٱلۡأَخِرَةِ وَٱلۡأُولَىٰٓ
So Allah (الله), seized him with punishment for his last (i.e. his saying: ‘I am your lord, most high‘) (see Verse 79:24)) and first ((i.e. his saying, ‘O chiefs! I know not that you have a god other than I‘ (see Verse 28:38)) transgression.
79:26
إِنَّ فِى ذَٲلِكَ لَعِبۡرَةً۬ لِّمَن يَخۡشَىٰٓ
Verily, in this is an instructive admonition for whosoever fears Allah (الله).
79:[27-33] The creation of man is no harder than the creation of heavens, earth and its contents
79:[34-46] Punishment and reward on the Day of Judgment
79:27
ءَأَنتُمۡ أَشَدُّ خَلۡقًا أَمِ ٱلسَّمَآءُ‌ۚ
Are you more difficult to create, or is the heaven
بَنَٮٰهَا
that He constructed?
79:28
رَفَعَ سَمۡكَهَا فَسَوَّٮٰهَا
He raised its height, and He has equally ordered it,
79:29
وَأَغۡطَشَ لَيۡلَهَا وَأَخۡرَجَ ضُحَٮٰهَا
Its night He covers with darkness, and its forenoon He brings out (with light).
79:30
وَٱلۡأَرۡضَ بَعۡدَ ذَٲلِكَ دَحَٮٰهَآ
And after that He spread the earth;
79:31
أَخۡرَجَ مِنۡہَا مَآءَهَا وَمَرۡعَٮٰهَا
And brought forth therefrom its water and its pasture;
79:32
وَٱلۡجِبَالَ أَرۡسَٮٰهَا
And the mountains He has fixed firmly;
79:33
مَتَـٰعً۬ا لَّكُمۡ وَلِأَنۡعَـٰمِكُمۡ
(To be) a provision and benefit for you and your cattle.
79:34
فَإِذَا جَآءَتِ ٱلطَّآمَّةُ ٱلۡكُبۡرَىٰ
But when there comes the greatest catastrophe (i.e. the Day of Recompense, etc.),
79:35
يَوۡمَ يَتَذَكَّرُ ٱلۡإِنسَـٰنُ مَا سَعَىٰ
The Day when man shall remember what he strove for,
79:36
وَبُرِّزَتِ ٱلۡجَحِيمُ لِمَن يَرَىٰ
And Hell (جهنم)-fire shall be made apparent in full view for (every) one who sees,
79:37
فَأَمَّا مَن طَغَىٰ
Then, for him who Tagha (transgressed all bounds, in disbelief, oppression and evil deeds of disobedience to Allah (الله)).
79:38
وَءَاثَرَ ٱلۡحَيَوٰةَ ٱلدُّنۡيَا
And preferred the life of this world (by following his evil desires and lusts),
79:39
فَإِنَّ ٱلۡجَحِيمَ هِىَ ٱلۡمَأۡوَىٰ
Verily, his abode will be Hell (جهنم)-fire;
79:40
وَأَمَّا مَنۡ خَافَ مَقَامَ رَبِّهِۦ وَنَهَى ٱلنَّفۡسَ عَنِ ٱلۡهَوَىٰ
But as for him who feared standing before his Lord, and restrained himself from impure evil desires, and lusts.
79:41
فَإِنَّ ٱلۡجَنَّةَ هِىَ ٱلۡمَأۡوَىٰ
Verily, Paradise (الجنة) will be his abode.
79:42
يَسۡـَٔلُونَكَ عَنِ ٱلسَّاعَةِ أَيَّانَ مُرۡسَٮٰهَا
They ask you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) about the Hour, - when will be its appointed time?
79:43
فِيمَ أَنتَ مِن ذِكۡرَٮٰهَآ
You have no knowledge to say anything about it,
79:44
إِلَىٰ رَبِّكَ مُنتَہَٮٰهَآ
To your Lord belongs (the knowledge of) the term thereof?
79:45
إِنَّمَآ أَنتَ مُنذِرُ مَن يَخۡشَٮٰهَا
You (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) are only a warner for those who fear it,
79:46
كَأَنَّہُمۡ يَوۡمَ يَرَوۡنَہَا لَمۡ يَلۡبَثُوٓاْ إِلَّا عَشِيَّةً أَوۡ ضُحَٮٰهَا
The Day they see it, (it will be) as if they had not tarried (in this world) except an afternoon or a morning.*********
