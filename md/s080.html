Abasa
He Frowned
80:[1-16] The seekers of guidance should be given preference in conveying Allah‘s message
80:[17-32] Recognize your Creator and fulfill your obligations
80:[33-42] On the Day of Judgment, no one shall care about his own mother, father, brother or children
80:1
عَبَسَ وَتَوَلَّىٰٓ
(The Prophet SAW) frowned and turned away,
80:2
أَن جَآءَهُ ٱلۡأَعۡمَىٰ
Because there came to him the blind man.(i.e. Abdullah bin Umm-Maktoom, who came to the Prophet SAW while he was preaching to one or some of the Quraish chiefs).
80:3
وَمَا يُدۡرِيكَ لَعَلَّهُ ۥ يَزَّكَّىٰٓ
But what could tell you that per chance he might become pure (from sins)?
80:4
أَوۡ يَذَّكَّرُ فَتَنفَعَهُ ٱلذِّكۡرَىٰٓ
Or that he might receive admonition, and that the admonition might profit him?
80:5
أَمَّا مَنِ ٱسۡتَغۡنَىٰ
As for him who thinks himself self-sufficient,
80:6
فَأَنتَ لَهُ ۥ تَصَدَّىٰ
To him you attend;
80:7
وَمَا عَلَيۡكَ أَلَّا يَزَّكَّىٰ
What does it matter to you if he will not become pure (from disbelief, you are only a Messenger,your duty is to convey the Message of Allah (الله)).
80:8
وَأَمَّا مَن جَآءَكَ يَسۡعَىٰ
But as to him who came to you running.
80:9
وَهُوَ يَخۡشَىٰ
And is afraid (of Allah (الله) and His Punishment),
80:10
فَأَنتَ عَنۡهُ تَلَهَّىٰ
Of him you are neglectful and divert your attention to another,
80:11
كَلَّآ إِنَّہَا تَذۡكِرَةٌ۬
Nay, (do not do like this), indeed it (these Verses of this Quran (القرآن)) are an admonition,
80:12
فَمَن شَآءَ ذَكَرَهُ ۥ
So whoever wills, let him pay attention to it.
80:13
فِى صُحُفٍ۬ مُّكَرَّمَةٍ۬
(It is) in Records held (greatly) in honour (Al-Lauh Al-Mahfooz).
80:14
مَّرۡفُوعَةٍ۬ مُّطَهَّرَةِۭ
Exalted (in dignity), purified,
80:15
بِأَيۡدِى سَفَرَةٍ۬
In the hands of scribes (angels).
80:16
كِرَامِۭ بَرَرَةٍ۬
Honourable and obedient.
80:17
قُتِلَ ٱلۡإِنسَـٰنُ مَآ أَكۡفَرَهُ ۥ
Be cursed (the disbelieving) man! How ungrateful he is!
80:18
مِنۡ أَىِّ شَىۡءٍ خَلَقَهُ ۥ
From what thing did He create him?
80:19
مِن نُّطۡفَةٍ خَلَقَهُ ۥ فَقَدَّرَهُ
From Nutfah (male and female semen drops) He created him, and then set him in due proportion;
80:20
ثُمَّ ٱلسَّبِيلَ يَسَّرَهُ ۥ
Then He makes the Path easy for him;
80:21
ثُمَّ أَمَاتَهُ ۥ فَأَقۡبَرَهُ ۥ
Then He causes him to die, and puts him in his grave;
80:22
ثُمَّ إِذَا شَآءَ أَنشَرَهُ ۥ
Then, when it is His Will, He will resurrect him (again).
80:23
كَلَّا لَمَّا يَقۡضِ مَآ أَمَرَهُ ۥ
Nay, but (man) has not done what He commanded him.
80:24
فَلۡيَنظُرِ ٱلۡإِنسَـٰنُ إِلَىٰ طَعَامِهِۦۤ
Then let man look at his food,
80:25
أَنَّا صَبَبۡنَا ٱلۡمَآءَ صَبًّ۬ا
That We pour forth water in abundance,
80:26
ثُمَّ شَقَقۡنَا ٱلۡأَرۡضَ شَقًّ۬ا
And We split the earth in clefts,
80:27
فَأَنۢبَتۡنَا فِيہَا حَبًّ۬ا
And We cause therein the grain to grow,
80:28
وَعِنَبً۬ا وَقَضۡبً۬ا
And grapes and clover plants (i.e. green fodder for the cattle),
80:29
وَزَيۡتُونً۬ا وَنَخۡلاً۬
And olives and date-palms,
80:30
وَحَدَآٮِٕقَ غُلۡبً۬ا
And gardens, dense with many trees,
80:31
وَفَـٰكِهَةً۬ وَأَبًّ۬ا
And fruits and Abba (herbage, etc.),
80:32
مَّتَـٰعً۬ا لَّكُمۡ وَلِأَنۡعَـٰمِكُمۡ
(To be) a provision and benefit for you and your cattle.
80:33
فَإِذَا جَآءَتِ ٱلصَّآخَّةُ
Then, when there comes As-Sakhkhah (the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة)s second blowing of Trumpet),
80:34
يَوۡمَ يَفِرُّ ٱلۡمَرۡءُ مِنۡ أَخِيهِ
That Day shall a man flee from his brother,
80:35
وَأُمِّهِۦ وَأَبِيهِ
And from his mother and his father,
80:36
وَصَـٰحِبَتِهِۦ وَبَنِيهِ
And from his wife and his children.
80:37
لِكُلِّ ٱمۡرِىٍٕ۬ مِّنۡہُمۡ يَوۡمَٮِٕذٍ۬ شَأۡنٌ۬ يُغۡنِيهِ
Everyman, that Day, will have enough to make him careless of others.
80:38
وُجُوهٌ۬ يَوۡمَٮِٕذٍ۬ مُّسۡفِرَةٌ۬
Some faces that Day, will be bright (true believers of Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism).
80:39
ضَاحِكَةٌ۬ مُّسۡتَبۡشِرَةٌ۬
Laughing, rejoicing at good news (of Paradise (الجنة)).
80:40
وَوُجُوهٌ۬ يَوۡمَٮِٕذٍ عَلَيۡہَا غَبَرَةٌ۬
And other faces, that Day, will be dust-stained;
80:41
تَرۡهَقُهَا قَتَرَةٌ
Darkness will cover them,
80:42
أُوْلَـٰٓٮِٕكَ هُمُ ٱلۡكَفَرَةُ ٱلۡفَجَرَةُ
Such will be the Kafarah (disbelievers in Allah (الله), in His Oneness, and in His Messenger Muhammad (محمد) SAW, etc.), the Fajarah (wicked evil doers).*********
