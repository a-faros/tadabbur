11:[1-5] Teachings of the Qur‘an
11:1
الر ۚ
Alif-Lam-Ra.(These letters are one of the miracles of the Quran (القرآن) and none but Allah (الله) (Alone) knows their meanings).
كِتَابٌ أُحْكِمَتْ آيَاتُهُ ثُمَّ فُصِّلَتْ مِنْ لَدُنْ حَكِيمٍ خَبِيرٍ
(This is) a Book, the Verses whereof are perfected (in every sphere of knowledge, etc.), and then explained in detail from One (Allah (الله)), Who is All-Wise (Al-Hakeem (ٱلْحَكِيمُ)) and Well- Acquainted (with all things).
11:2
أَلَّا تَعْبُدُوا إِلَّا اللَّهَ ۚ
(Saying) worship none but Allah (الله).
إِنَّنِي لَكُمْ مِنْهُ نَذِيرٌ وَبَشِيرٌ
Verily, I (Muhammad (محمد) SAW) am unto you from Him a warner and a bringer of glad tidings.
11:3
وَأَنِ اسْتَغْفِرُوا رَبَّكُمْ ثُمَّ تُوبُوا إِلَيْهِ يُمَتِّعْكُمْ مَتَاعًا حَسَنًا إِلَى أَجَلٍ مُسَمًّى
And (commanding you): ‘Seek the forgiveness of your Lord, and turn to Him in repentance, that He may grant you good enjoyment, for a term appointed,
وَيُؤْتِ كُلَّ ذِي فَضْلٍ فَضْلَهُ ۖ
and bestow His abounding Grace to every owner of grace.(i.e. the one who helps and serves needy and deserving, physically and with his wealth, and even with good words)
وَإِنْ تَوَلَّوْا فَإِنِّي أَخَافُ عَلَيْكُمْ عَذَابَ يَوْمٍ كَبِيرٍ
But if you turn away, then I fear for you the torment of a Great Day (i.e. the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة)).
11:4
إِلَى اللَّهِ مَرْجِعُكُمْ ۖ وَهُوَ عَلَى كُلِّ شَيْءٍ قَدِيرٌ
To Allah (الله) is your return, and He is Able to do all things.‘
11:5
أَلَا إِنَّهُمْ يَثْنُونَ صُدُورَهُمْ لِيَسْتَخْفُوا مِنْهُ ۚ
No doubt! They did fold up their breasts, that they may hide from Him.
أَلَا حِينَ يَسْتَغْشُونَ ثِيَابَهُمْ يَعْلَمُ مَا يُسِرُّونَ وَمَا يُعْلِنُونَ ۚ
Surely, even when they cover themselves with their garments, He knows what they conceal and what they reveal.
إِنَّهُ عَلِيمٌ بِذَاتِ الصُّدُورِ
Verily, He is the All-Knower (Al-‘Alee (ٱلْعَلِيُّ)m (ٱلْعَلِيمُ)) of the (innermost secrets) of the breasts.
11:[6-8] Allah is the Sustainer of all creatures
11:6
وَمَا مِنْ دَابَّةٍ فِي الْأَرْضِ إِلَّا عَلَى اللَّهِ رِزْقُهَا
And no (moving) living creature is there on earth but its provision is due from Allah (الله).
وَيَعْلَمُ مُسْتَقَرَّهَا وَمُسْتَوْدَعَهَا ۚ
And He knows its dwelling place and its deposit (in the uterous, grave, etc.).
كُلٌّ فِي كِتَابٍ مُبِينٍ
all is in a Clear Book (Al-Lauh Al-Mahfooz - the Book of Decrees with Allah (الله)).
11:7
وَهُوَ الَّذِي خَلَقَ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضَ فِي سِتَّةِ أَيَّامٍ وَكَانَ عَرْشُهُ عَلَى الْمَاءِ
And He it is Who has created the heavens and the earth in six Days and His Throne was on the water,
لِيَبْلُوَكُمْ أَيُّكُمْ أَحْسَنُ عَمَلًا ۗ
that He might try you, which of you is the best in deeds.
وَلَئِنْ قُلْتَ إِنَّكُمْ مَبْعُوثُونَ مِنْ بَعْدِ الْمَوْتِ
But if you were to say to them: ‘You shall indeed be raised up after death,‘
لَيَقُولَنَّ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا إِنْ هَذَا إِلَّا سِحْرٌ مُبِينٌ
those who disbelieve would be sure to say, ‘This is nothing but obvious magic.‘
11:8
‏وَلَئِنْ أَخَّرْنَا عَنْهُمُ الْعَذَابَ إِلَى أُمَّةٍ مَعْدُودَةٍ لَيَقُولُنَّ مَا يَحْبِسُهُ ۗ
And if We delay the torment for them till a determined term, they are sure to say, ‘What keeps it back?‘
أَلَا يَوْمَ يَأْتِيهِمْ لَيْسَ مَصْرُوفًا عَنْهُمْ وَحَاقَ بِهِمْ مَا كَانُوا بِهِ يَسْتَهْزِئُونَ
Verily, on the day it reaches them, nothing will turn it away from them, and they will be surrounded by (fall in) that at which they used to mock!
11:[9-14] Man is ever ungrateful to Allah except believers. Al-Qur‘an is not forged by the Prophet
kinds: The People of the Book (Jews & Christians) are of two
11:[15-24] those, who can see the Truth, and those who choose not to
11:9
وَلَئِنْ أَذَقْنَا الْإِنْسَانَ مِنَّا رَحْمَةً ثُمَّ نَزَعْنَاهَا مِنْهُ إِنَّهُ لَيَئُوسٌ كَفُورٌ
And if We give man a taste of Mercy from Us, and then withdraw it from him, verily! He is despairing, ungrateful.
11:10
وَلَئِنْ أَذَقْنَاهُ نَعْمَاءَ بَعْدَ ضَرَّاءَ مَسَّتْهُ لَيَقُولَنَّ ذَهَبَ السَّيِّئَاتُ عَنِّي ۚ
But if We let him taste good (favour) after evil (poverty and harm) has touched him, he is sure to say: ‘Ills have departed from me.‘
‏إِنَّهُ لَفَرِحٌ فَخُورٌ
Surely, he is exultant, and boastful (ungrateful to Allah (الله)).
11:11
إِلَّا الَّذِينَ صَبَرُوا وَعَمِلُوا الصَّالِحَاتِ أُولَئِكَ لَهُمْ مَغْفِرَةٌ وَأَجْرٌ كَبِيرٌ
Except those who show patience and do righteous good deeds, those: theirs will be forgiveness and a great reward (Paradise (الجنة)).
11:12
فَلَعَلَّكَ تَارِكٌ بَعْضَ مَا يُوحَى إِلَيْكَ وَضَائِقٌ بِهِ صَدْرُكَ أَنْ يَقُولُوا
So perchance you (Muhammad (محمد) SAW) may give up a part of what is revealed unto you, and that your breast feels straitened for it because they say,
لَوْلَا أُنْزِلَ عَلَيْهِ كَنْزٌ أَوْ جَاءَ مَعَهُ مَلَكٌ ۚ
Why has not a treasure been sent down unto him, or an angel has come with him?‘
إِنَّمَا أَنْتَ نَذِيرٌ ۚ
But you are only a warner.
وَاللَّهُ عَلَى كُلِّ شَيْءٍ وَكِيلٌ
And Allah (الله) is a Wakeel (Disposer of affairs, Trustee, Guardian, etc.) over all things.
11:13
أَمْ يَقُولُونَ افْتَرَاهُ ۖ
Or they say, ‘He (Prophet Muhammad (محمد) SAW) forged it (the Quran (القرآن)).‘
قُلْ فَأْتُوا بِعَشْرِ سُوَرٍ مِثْلِهِ مُفْتَرَيَاتٍ وَادْعُوا مَنِ اسْتَطَعْتُمْ مِنْ دُونِ اللَّهِ إِنْ كُنْتُمْ صَادِقِينَ
Say: ‘Bring you then ten forged Soorah (chapters) like unto it, and call whomsoever you can, other than Allah (الله) (to your help), if you speak the truth!‘
11:14
فَإِلَّمْ يَسْتَجِيبُوا لَكُمْ فَاعْلَمُوا أَنَّمَا أُنْزِلَ بِعِلْمِ اللَّهِ
If then they answer you not, know then that the Revelation (this Quran (القرآن)) is sent down with the Knowledge of Allah (الله)
وَأَنْ لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا هُوَ ۖ
and that La ilaha illa Huwa: (none has the right to be worshipped but He)!
فَهَلْ أَنْتُمْ مُسْلِمُونَ
Will you then be Muslims (those who submit to Islam (الإسلام))?
11:15
مَنْ كَانَ يُرِيدُ الْحَيَاةَ الدُّنْيَا وَزِينَتَهَا نُوَفِّ إِلَيْهِمْ أَعْمَالَهُمْ فِيهَا
Whosoever desires the life of the world and its glitter; to them We shall pay in full (the wages of) their deeds therein,
‏وَهُمْ فِيهَا لَا يُبْخَسُونَ
and they will have no diminution therein.
11:16
أُولَئِكَ الَّذِينَ لَيْسَ لَهُمْ فِي الْآخِرَةِ إِلَّا النَّارُ ۖ
They are those for whom there is nothing in the Hereafter but Fire;
وَحَبِطَ مَا صَنَعُوا فِيهَا وَبَاطِلٌ مَا كَانُوا يَعْمَلُونَ
and vain are the deeds they did therein. And of no effect is that which they used to do.
11:17
أَفَمَنْ كَانَ عَلَى بَيِّنَةٍ مِنْ رَبِّهِ وَيَتْلُوهُ شَاهِدٌ مِنْهُ
Can they (Muslims) who rely on a clear proof (the Quran (القرآن)) from their Lord, and whom a witness (Prophet Muhammad (محمد) SAW through Jibrael (Gabriel OaCa)) from Him follows it (can they be equal with the disbelievers);
وَمِنْ قَبْلِهِ كِتَابُ مُوسَى إِمَامًا وَرَحْمَةً ۚ
and before it, came the Book of Musa (Moosa (موسى)), a guidance and a mercy,
‏أُولَئِكَ يُؤْمِنُونَ بِهِ ۚ
they believe therein,
وَمَنْ يَكْفُرْ بِهِ مِنَ الْأَحْزَابِ فَالنَّارُ مَوْعِدُهُ ۚ
but those of the sects (Jews (هُودًا), Christians (نَصَارَىٰ) and all the other non-Muslim nations) that reject it (the Quran (القرآن)), the Fire will be their promised meeting-place.
فَلَا تَكُ فِي مِرْيَةٍ مِنْهُ ۚ
So be not in doubt about it.(i.e. those who denied Prophet Muhammad (محمد) SAW and also denied all that which he brought from Allah (الله), surely, they will enter Hell (جهنم))
إِنَّهُ الْحَقُّ مِنْ رَبِّكَ وَلَكِنَّ أَكْثَرَ النَّاسِ لَا يُؤْمِنُونَ
Verily, it is the truth from your Lord, but most of the mankind believe not.
11:18
وَمَنْ أَظْلَمُ مِمَّنِ افْتَرَى عَلَى اللَّهِ كَذِبًا ۚ
And who does more wrong than he who invents a lie against Allah (الله).
أُولَئِكَ يُعْرَضُونَ عَلَى رَبِّهِمْ وَيَقُولُ الْأَشْهَادُ هَؤُلَاءِ الَّذِينَ كَذَبُوا عَلَى رَبِّهِمْ ۚ
Such will be brought before their Lord, and the witnesses will say, ‘These are the ones who lied against their Lord!‘
أَلَا لَعْنَةُ اللَّهِ عَلَى الظَّالِمِينَ
No doubt! the curse of Allah (الله) is on the Zalimoon (polytheists, wrong-doers, oppressors, etc.).
11:19
الَّذِينَ يَصُدُّونَ عَنْ سَبِيلِ اللَّهِ وَيَبْغُونَهَا عِوَجًا
Those who hinder (others) from the Path of Allah (الله) (Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism), and seek a crookedness therein,
وَهُمْ بِالْآخِرَةِ هُمْ كَافِرُونَ
while they are disbelievers in the Hereafter.
11:20
أُولَئِكَ لَمْ يَكُونُوا مُعْجِزِينَ فِي الْأَرْضِ وَمَا كَانَ لَهُمْ مِنْ دُونِ اللَّهِ مِنْ أَوْلِيَاءَ ۘ
By no means will they escape (from Allah (الله)‘s Torment) on earth, nor have they protectors besides Allah (الله)!
يُضَاعَفُ لَهُمُ الْعَذَابُ ۚ
Their torment will be doubled!
مَا كَانُوا يَسْتَطِيعُونَ السَّمْعَ وَمَا كَانُوا يُبْصِرُونَ
They could not bear to hear (the preachers of the truth) and they used not to see (the truth because of their severe aversin, inspite of the fact that they had the sense of hearing and sight).
11:21
أُولَئِكَ الَّذِينَ خَسِرُوا أَنْفُسَهُمْ وَضَلَّ عَنْهُمْ مَا كَانُوا يَفْتَرُونَ
They are those who have lost their ownselves, and their invented false deities will vanish from them.
11:22
لَا جَرَمَ أَنَّهُمْ فِي الْآخِرَةِ هُمُ الْأَخْسَرُونَ
Certainly, they are those who will be the greatest losers in the Hereafter.
11:23
إِنَّ الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا وَعَمِلُوا الصَّالِحَاتِ وَأَخْبَتُوا إِلَى رَبِّهِمْ
Verily, those who believe (in the Oneness of Allah (الله) - Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism) and do righteous good deeds, and humble themselves (in repentance and obedience) before their Lord, -
أُولَئِكَ أَصْحَابُ الْجَنَّةِ ۖ هُمْ فِيهَا خَالِدُونَ
they will be dwellers of Paradise (الجنة) to dwell therein forever.
11:24
مَثَلُ الْفَرِيقَيْنِ كَالْأَعْمَى وَالْأَصَمِّ وَالْبَصِيرِ وَالسَّمِيعِ ۚ
The likeness of the two parties is as the blind and the deaf and the seer and the hearer.
هَلْ يَسْتَوِيَانِ مَثَلًا ۚ
Are they equal when compared?
أَفَلَا تَذَكَّرُونَ
Will you not then take heed?
11:[25-35] The Prophet Nüh‘s address to his people. Nüh‘s people challenged him and asked for the scourge of Allah
11:25
وَلَقَدْ أَرْسَلْنَا نُوحًا إِلَى قَوْمِهِ إِنِّي لَكُمْ نَذِيرٌ مُبِينٌ
And indeed We sent Nooh (Noah) to his people (and he said): ‘I have come to you as a plain warner.‘
11:26
أَنْ لَا تَعْبُدُوا إِلَّا اللَّهَ ۖ
That you worship none but Allah (الله),
إِنِّي أَخَافُ عَلَيْكُمْ عَذَابَ يَوْمٍ أَلِيمٍ
surely, I fear for you the torment of a painful Day.‘
11:27
فَقَالَ الْمَلَأُ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا مِنْ قَوْمِهِ مَا نَرَاكَ إِلَّا بَشَرًا مِثْلَنَا
The chiefs of the disbelievers among his people said: ‘We see you but a man like ourselves,
وَمَا نَرَاكَ اتَّبَعَكَ إِلَّا الَّذِينَ هُمْ أَرَاذِلُنَا بَادِيَ الرَّأْيِ
nor do we see any follow you but the meanest among us and they (too) followed you without thinking.
وَمَا نَرَى لَكُمْ عَلَيْنَا مِنْ فَضْلٍ بَلْ نَظُنُّكُمْ كَاذِبِينَ
And we do not see in you any merit above us, in fact we think you are liars.‘
11:28
قَالَ يَا قَوْمِ أَرَأَيْتُمْ إِنْ كُنْتُ عَلَى بَيِّنَةٍ مِنْ رَبِّي وَآتَانِي رَحْمَةً مِنْ عِنْدِهِ فَعُمِّيَتْ عَلَيْكُمْ
He said: ‘O my people! Tell me, if I have a clear proof from my Lord, and a Mercy (Prophethood, etc.) has come to me from Him, but that (Mercy) has been obscured from your sight.
أَنُلْزِمُكُمُوهَا وَأَنْتُمْ لَهَا كَارِهُونَ
Shall we compel you to accept it (Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism) when you have a strong hatred for it?
11:29
وَيَا قَوْمِ لَا أَسْأَلُكُمْ عَلَيْهِ مَالًا ۖ إِنْ أَجْرِيَ إِلَّا عَلَى اللَّهِ ۚ
And O my people! I ask of you no wealth for it, my reward is from none but Allah (الله).
وَمَا أَنَا بِطَارِدِ الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا ۚ
I am not going to drive away those who have believed.
إِنَّهُمْ مُلَاقُو رَبِّهِمْ وَلَكِنِّي أَرَاكُمْ قَوْمًا تَجْهَلُونَ
Surely, they are going to meet their Lord, but I see that you are a people that are ignorant.
11:30
وَيَا قَوْمِ مَنْ يَنْصُرُنِي مِنَ اللَّهِ إِنْ طَرَدْتُهُمْ ۚ
And O my people! Who will help me against Allah (الله), if I drove them away?
أَفَلَا تَذَكَّرُونَ
Will you not then give a thought?
11:31
وَلَا أَقُولُ لَكُمْ عِنْدِي خَزَائِنُ اللَّهِ
And I do not say to you that with me are the Treasures of Allah (الله),
وَلَا أَعْلَمُ الْغَيْبَ
Nor that I know the Ghaib (unseen);
وَلَا أَقُولُ إِنِّي مَلَكٌ
nor do I say I am an angel,
وَلَا أَقُولُ لِلَّذِينَ تَزْدَرِي أَعْيُنُكُمْ لَنْ يُؤْتِيَهُمُ اللَّهُ خَيْرًا ۖ
and I do not say of those whom your eyes look down upon that Allah (الله) will not bestow any good on them.
‏اللَّهُ أَعْلَمُ بِمَا فِي أَنْفُسِهِمْ ۖ
Allah (الله) knows what is in their inner-selves (as regards belief, etc.).
إِنِّي إِذًا لَمِنَ الظَّالِمِينَ
In that case, I should, indeed be one of the Zalimoon (wrong-doers, oppressors, etc.).‘
11:32
قَالُوا يَا نُوحُ قَدْ جَادَلْتَنَا فَأَكْثَرْتَ جِدَالَنَا فَأْتِنَا بِمَا تَعِدُنَا إِنْ كُنْتَ مِنَ الصَّادِقِينَ
They said: ‘O Nooh (Noah)! You have disputed with us and much have you prolonged the dispute with us, now bring upon us what you threaten us with, if you are of the truthful.‘
11:33
قَالَ إِنَّمَا يَأْتِيكُمْ بِهِ اللَّهُ إِنْ شَاءَ وَمَا أَنْتُمْ بِمُعْجِزِينَ
He said: ‘Only Allah (الله) will bring it (the punishment) on you, if He will, and then you will escape not.
11:34

verily, his work is unrighteous, so ask not of Me that of which you have no knowledge!
إِنِّي أَعِظُكَ أَنْ تَكُونَ مِنَ الْجَاهِلِينَ
I admonish you, lest you be one of the ignorants.‘

I put my trust in Allah (الله), my Lord and your Lord!

There is not a moving (living) creature but He has grasp of its forelock.

Verily, my Lord is on the Straight Path (the truth).

فَإِنْ تَوَلَّوْا فَقَدْ أَبْلَغْتُكُمْ مَا أُرْسِلْتُ بِهِ إِلَيْكُمْ ۚ
So if you turn away, still I have conveyed the Message with which I was sent to you.
وَيَسْتَخْلِفُ رَبِّي قَوْمًا غَيْرَكُمْ وَلَا تَضُرُّونَهُ شَيْئًا ۚ
My Lord will make another people succeed you, and you will not harm Him in the least.
إِنَّ رَبِّي عَلَى كُلِّ شَيْءٍ حَفِيظٌ
Surely, my Lord is Guardian over all things.‘

And when Our Commandment came, We saved Hood and those who believed with him by a Mercy from Us,

and We saved them from a severe torment.
11:59
وَتِلْكَ عَادٌ ۖ
Such were Ad (people).

They rejected the Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, etc.) of their Lord and disobeyed His Messengers, and followed the command of every proud obstinate (oppressor of the truth, from their leaders).

And they were pursued by a curse in this world and (so they will be) on the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة).

No doubt! Verily, Ad disbelieved in their Lord. So away with Ad, the people of Hood.

Then you increase me not but in loss.

and touch her not with evil, lest a near torment will seize you.‘

Verily, your Lord, He is the All-Strong, the All-Mighty.

قَالُوا سَلَامًا ۖ
They said: Salam (greetings or peace!)
قَالَ سَلَامٌ ۖ فَمَا لَبِثَ أَنْ جَاءَ بِعِجْلٍ حَنِيذٍ
He answered, Salam (greetings or peace!) and he hastened to entertain them with a roasted calf.
11:70
فَلَمَّا رَأَى أَيْدِيَهُمْ لَا تَصِلُ إِلَيْهِ نَكِرَهُمْ وَأَوْجَسَ مِنْهُمْ خِيفَةً ۚ
But when he saw their hands went not towards it (the meal), he felt some mistrust of them, and conceived a fear of them.
قَالُوا لَا تَخَفْ إِنَّا أُرْسِلْنَا إِلَى قَوْمِ لُوطٍ
They said: ‘Fear not, we have been sent against the people of Lout (Lot).‘
11:71
وَامْرَأَتُهُ قَائِمَةٌ فَضَحِكَتْ
And his wife was standing (there), and she laughed (either, because the Messengers did not eat their food or for being glad for the destruction of the people of Lout (Lot).
فَبَشَّرْنَاهَا بِإِسْحَاقَ وَمِنْ وَرَاءِ إِسْحَاقَ يَعْقُوبَ
But We gave her glad tidings of Ishaque (Ishak (إسحاق)), and after him, of Yaqoob (Yaqqub (يعقوب)).
11:72
قَالَتْ يَا وَيْلَتَى أَأَلِدُ وَأَنَا عَجُوزٌ وَهَذَا بَعْلِي شَيْخًا ۖ
She said (in astonishment): ‘Woe unto me! Shall I bear a child while I am an old woman, and here is my husband, an old man?
إِنَّ هَذَا لَشَيْءٌ عَجِيبٌ
Verily! This is a strange thing!‘
11:73
قَالُوا أَتَعْجَبِينَ مِنْ أَمْرِ اللَّهِ ۖ
They said: ‘Do you wonder at the Decree of Allah (الله)?
رَحْمَتُ اللَّهِ وَبَرَكَاتُهُ عَلَيْكُمْ أَهْلَ الْبَيْتِ ۚ
The Mercy of Allah (الله) and His Blessings be on you, O the family (of Ibraheem (Abraham)).
إِنَّهُ حَمِيدٌ مَجِيدٌ
Surely, He (Allah (الله)) is All-Praiseworthy, All-Glorious.‘
11:74
فَلَمَّا ذَهَبَ عَنْ إِبْرَاهِيمَ الرَّوْعُ وَجَاءَتْهُ الْبُشْرَى يُجَادِلُنَا فِي قَوْمِ لُوطٍ
Then when the fear had gone away from (the mind of) Ibraheem (Abraham), and the glad tidings had reached him, he began to plead with Us (Our Messengers) for the people of Lout (Lot).
11:75
إِنَّ إِبْرَاهِيمَ لَحَلِيمٌ أَوَّاهٌ مُنِيبٌ
Verily, Ibraheem (Abraham) was, without doubt, forbearing, used to invoke Allah (الله) with humility, and was repentant (to Allah (الله) all the time, again and again).
11:76
يَا إِبْرَاهِيمُ أَعْرِضْ عَنْ هَذَا ۖ
O Ibraheem (Abraham)! Forsake this.
إِنَّهُ قَدْ جَاءَ أَمْرُ رَبِّكَ ۖ
Indeed, the Commandment of your Lord has gone forth.
وَإِنَّهُمْ آتِيهِمْ عَذَابٌ غَيْرُ مَرْدُودٍ
Verily, there will come a torment for them which cannot be turned back.‘
11:77
وَلَمَّا جَاءَتْ رُسُلُنَا لُوطًا سِيءَ بِهِمْ وَضَاقَ بِهِمْ ذَرْعًا
And when Our Messengers came to Lout (Lot), he was grieved on their account and felt himself straitened for them (lest the town people should approach them to commit sodomy with them).
وَقَالَ هَذَا يَوْمٌ عَصِيبٌ
He said: ‘This is a distressful day.‘
11:78
وَجَاءَهُ قَوْمُهُ يُهْرَعُونَ إِلَيْهِ وَمِنْ قَبْلُ كَانُوا يَعْمَلُونَ السَّيِّئَاتِ ۚ
And his people came rushing towards him, and since aforetime they used to commit crimes (sodomy, etc.),
قَالَ يَا قَوْمِ هَؤُلَاءِ بَنَاتِي هُنَّ أَطْهَرُ لَكُمْ ۖ
he said: ‘O my people! Here are my daughters (i.e. the daughters of my nation), they are purer for you (if you marry them lawfully).
فَاتَّقُوا اللَّهَ وَلَا تُخْزُونِ فِي ضَيْفِي ۖ
So fear Allah (الله) and degrade me not as regards my guests!
أَلَيْسَ مِنْكُمْ رَجُلٌ رَشِيدٌ
Is there not among you a single right-minded man?‘
11:79
قَالُوا لَقَدْ عَلِمْتَ مَا لَنَا فِي بَنَاتِكَ مِنْ حَقٍّ وَإِنَّكَ لَتَعْلَمُ مَا نُرِيدُ
They said: ‘Surely you know that we have neither any desire nor in need of your daughters, and indeed you know well what we want!‘
11:80
قَالَ لَوْ أَنَّ لِي بِكُمْ قُوَّةً
He said: ‘Would that I had strength (men) to overpower you,
أَوْ آوِي إِلَى رُكْنٍ شَدِيدٍ
or that I could betake myself to some powerful support (to resist you).‘
11:81
قَالُوا يَا لُوطُ إِنَّا رُسُلُ رَبِّكَ لَنْ يَصِلُوا إِلَيْكَ ۖ
They (Messengers) said:
O Lout (Lot)! Verily, we are the Messengers from your Lord!
فَأَسْرِ بِأَهْلِكَ بِقِطْعٍ مِنَ اللَّيْلِ وَلَا يَلْتَفِتْ مِنْكُمْ أَحَدٌ إِلَّا امْرَأَتَكَ ۖ
They shall not reach you! So travel with your family in a part of the night, and let not any of you look back, but your wife (will remain behind),
إِنَّهُ مُصِيبُهَا مَا أَصَابَهُمْ ۚ
verily, the punishment which will afflict them, will afflict her.
إِنَّ مَوْعِدَهُمُ الصُّبْحُ ۚ
Indeed, morning is their appointed time.
أَلَيْسَ الصُّبْحُ بِقَرِيبٍ
Is not the morning near?‘
11:82
فَلَمَّا جَاءَ أَمْرُنَا جَعَلْنَا عَالِيَهَا سَافِلَهَا وَأَمْطَرْنَا عَلَيْهَا حِجَارَةً مِنْ سِجِّيلٍ مَنْضُودٍ
So when Our Commandment came, We turned (the towns of Sodom in Palestine) upside down, and rained on them stones of baked clay, piled up;
11:83
مُسَوَّمَةً عِنْدَ رَبِّكَ ۖ
Marked from your Lord,
وَمَا هِيَ مِنَ الظَّالِمِينَ بِبَعِيدٍ
and they are not ever far from the Zalimoon (polytheists, evil-doers, etc.).
11:[84-90] The Prophet Shu‘aib‘s address to his people who were cheating in their business transactions
11:[91-95] Their negative response and its consequences
11:84
وَإِلَى مَدْيَنَ أَخَاهُمْ شُعَيْبًا ۚ
And to the Madyan (مدين) (Midian) people (We sent) their brother Shuaib.
قَالَ يَا قَوْمِ اعْبُدُوا اللَّهَ مَا لَكُمْ مِنْ إِلَهٍ غَيْرُهُ ۖ
He said: ‘O my people! Worship Allah (الله), you have no other Ilah (God) but Him,
وَلَا تَنْقُصُوا الْمِكْيَالَ وَالْمِيزَانَ ۚ
and give not short measure or weight,
إِنِّي أَرَاكُمْ بِخَيْرٍ
I see you in prosperity;
وَإِنِّي أَخَافُ عَلَيْكُمْ عَذَابَ يَوْمٍ مُحِيطٍ
and verily I fear for you the torment of a Day encompassing.
11:85
‏وَيَا قَوْمِ أَوْفُوا الْمِكْيَالَ وَالْمِيزَانَ بِالْقِسْطِ ۖ
And O my people! Give full measure and weight in justice
وَلَا تَبْخَسُوا النَّاسَ أَشْيَاءَهُمْ
and reduce not the things that are due to the people,
وَلَا تَعْثَوْا فِي الْأَرْضِ مُفْسِدِينَ
and do not commit mischief in the land, causing corruption.
11:86
بَقِيَّتُ اللَّهِ خَيْرٌ لَكُمْ إِنْ كُنْتُمْ مُؤْمِنِينَ ۚ
That which is left by Allah (الله) for you (after giving the rights of the people) is better for you, if you are believers.
وَمَا أَنَا عَلَيْكُمْ بِحَفِيظٍ
And I am not set over you as a guardian.
11:87
قَالُوا يَا شُعَيْبُ أَصَلَاتُكَ تَأْمُرُكَ أَنْ نَتْرُكَ مَا يَعْبُدُ آبَاؤُنَا
They said: ‘O Shuaib! Does your Salat (prayer) (i.e. the prayers which you offer has spoiled your mind, so you) command that we leave off what our fathers used to worship,
أَوْ أَنْ نَفْعَلَ فِي أَمْوَالِنَا مَا نَشَاءُ ۖ
or that we leave off doing what we like with our property?
إِنَّكَ لَأَنْتَ الْحَلِيمُ الرَّشِيدُ
Verily, you are the forbearer, right-minded!‘ (They said this sarcastically).
11:88
قَالَ يَا قَوْمِ أَرَأَيْتُمْ إِنْ كُنْتُ عَلَى بَيِّنَةٍ مِنْ رَبِّي وَرَزَقَنِي مِنْهُ رِزْقًا حَسَنًا ۚ
He said: ‘O my people! Tell me, if I have a clear evidence from my Lord, and He has given me a good sustenance from Himself (shall I corrupt it by mixing it with the unlawfully earned money).
وَمَا أُرِيدُ أَنْ أُخَالِفَكُمْ إِلَى مَا أَنْهَاكُمْ عَنْهُ ۚ
I wish not, in contradiction to you, to do that which I forbid you.
إِنْ أُرِيدُ إِلَّا الْإِصْلَاحَ مَا اسْتَطَعْتُ ۚ
I only desire reform so far as I am able, to the best of my power.
وَمَا تَوْفِيقِي إِلَّا بِاللَّهِ ۚ
And my guidance cannot come except from Allah (الله),
عَلَيْهِ تَوَكَّلْتُ وَإِلَيْهِ أُنِيبُ
in Him I trust and unto Him I repent.
11:89
وَيَا قَوْمِ لَا يَجْرِمَنَّكُمْ شِقَاقِي أَنْ يُصِيبَكُمْ مِثْلُ مَا أَصَابَ قَوْمَ نُوحٍ أَوْ قَوْمَ هُودٍ أَوْ قَوْمَ صَالِحٍ ۚ
And O my people! Let not my Shiqaq cause you to suffer the fate similar to that of the people of Nooh (Noah) or of Hood or of Salih (Saleh),
وَمَا قَوْمُ لُوطٍ مِنْكُمْ بِبَعِيدٍ
and the people of Lout (Lot) are not far off from you!
11:90
وَاسْتَغْفِرُوا رَبَّكُمْ ثُمَّ تُوبُوا إِلَيْهِ ۚ
And ask forgiveness of your Lord and turn unto Him in repentance.
إِنَّ رَبِّي رَحِيمٌ وَدُودٌ
Verily, my Lord is Most Merciful (Ar-Raheem (ٱلْرَّحِيْمُ)), Most Loving.‘
11:91
قَالُوا يَا شُعَيْبُ مَا نَفْقَهُ كَثِيرًا مِمَّا تَقُولُ وَإِنَّا لَنَرَاكَ فِينَا ضَعِيفًا ۖ
They said: ‘O Shuaib! We do not understand much of what you say, and we see you a weak (man, it is said that he was a blind man) among us.
وَلَوْلَا رَهْطُكَ لَرَجَمْنَاكَ ۖ وَمَا أَنْتَ عَلَيْنَا بِعَزِيزٍ
Were it not for your family, we should certainly have stoned you and you are not powerful against us.‘
11:92
قَالَ يَا قَوْمِ أَرَهْطِي أَعَزُّ عَلَيْكُمْ مِنَ اللَّهِ
He said: ‘O my people! Is then my family of more weight with you than Allah (الله)?
وَاتَّخَذْتُمُوهُ وَرَاءَكُمْ ظِهْرِيًّا ۖ
And you have cast Him away behind your backs.
إِنَّ رَبِّي بِمَا تَعْمَلُونَ مُحِيطٌ
Verily, my Lord is surrounding all that you do.
11:93
وَيَا قَوْمِ اعْمَلُوا عَلَى مَكَانَتِكُمْ إِنِّي عَامِلٌ ۖ
And O my people! Act according to your ability and way, and I am acting (on my way).
سَوْفَ تَعْلَمُونَ مَنْ يَأْتِيهِ عَذَابٌ يُخْزِيهِ وَمَنْ هُوَ كَاذِبٌ ۖ
You will come to know who it is on whom descends the torment that will cover him with ignominy, and who is a liar!
وَارْتَقِبُوا إِنِّي مَعَكُمْ رَقِيبٌ
And watch you! Verily, I too am watching with you.‘
11:94
وَلَمَّا جَاءَ أَمْرُنَا نَجَّيْنَا شُعَيْبًا وَالَّذِينَ آمَنُوا مَعَهُ بِرَحْمَةٍ مِنَّا
And when Our Commandment came, We saved Shuaib and those who believed with him by a Mercy from Us.
وَأَخَذَتِ الَّذِينَ ظَلَمُوا الصَّيْحَةُ فَأَصْبَحُوا فِي دِيَارِهِمْ جَاثِمِينَ
And As-Saeehah (torment - awful cry, etc.) seized the wrong-doers, and they lay (dead) prostrate in their homes.
11:95
كَأَنْ لَمْ يَغْنَوْا فِيهَا ۗ
As if they had never lived there!
أَلَا بُعْدًا لِمَدْيَنَ كَمَا بَعِدَتْ ثَمُودُ
So away with Madyan (مدين) (Midian)! As away with Thamud (ثمود)! (All these nations were destroyed).
11:[96-109] Fate of Fir‘on and his chiefs who were given the warning but they gave no heed
11:96
وَلَقَدْ أَرْسَلْنَا مُوسَى بِآيَاتِنَا وَسُلْطَانٍ مُبِينٍ
And indeed We sent Musa (Moosa (موسى)) with Our Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, etc.) and a manifest authority;
11:97
إِلَى فِرْعَوْنَ وَمَلَئِهِ
To Firaun (Pharaoh (فرعون)) and his chiefs,
فَاتَّبَعُوا أَمْرَ فِرْعَوْنَ ۖ وَمَا أَمْرُ فِرْعَوْنَ بِرَشِيدٍ
but they followed the command of Firaun (Pharaoh (فرعون)), and the command of Firaun (Pharaoh (فرعون)) was no right guide.
11:98
يَقْدُمُ قَوْمَهُ يَوْمَ الْقِيَامَةِ فَأَوْرَدَهُمُ النَّارَ ۖ
He will go ahead of his people on the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة), and will lead them into the Fire,
وَبِئْسَ الْوِرْدُ الْمَوْرُودُ
and evil indeed is the place to which they are led.
11:99
وَأُتْبِعُوا فِي هَذِهِ لَعْنَةً وَيَوْمَ الْقِيَامَةِ ۚ
They were pursued by a curse in this (deceiving life of this world) and (so they will be pursued by a curse) on the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة).
بِئْسَ الرِّفْدُ الْمَرْفُودُ
How bad is the curse (in this world) pursued by another curse (in the Hereafter).
11:100
ذَلِكَ مِنْ أَنْبَاءِ الْقُرَى نَقُصُّهُ عَلَيْكَ ۖ
These are some of the news of the (population of) towns which We relate unto you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW);
مِنْهَا قَائِمٌ وَحَصِيدٌ
of them, some are standing, and some have been (already) reaped.
11:101
وَمَا ظَلَمْنَاهُمْ وَلَكِنْ ظَلَمُوا أَنْفُسَهُمْ ۖ
We wronged them not, but they wronged themselves.
فَمَا أَغْنَتْ عَنْهُمْ آلِهَتُهُمُ الَّتِي يَدْعُونَ مِنْ دُونِ اللَّهِ مِنْ شَيْءٍ لَمَّا جَاءَ أَمْرُ رَبِّكَ ۖ
So their aliha (gods), other than Allah (الله), whom they invoked, profited them naught when there came the Command of your Lord,
وَمَا زَادُوهُمْ غَيْرَ تَتْبِيبٍ
nor did they add aught (to their lot) but destruction.
11:102
وَكَذَلِكَ أَخْذُ رَبِّكَ إِذَا أَخَذَ الْقُرَى وَهِيَ ظَالِمَةٌ ۚ
Such is the Seizure of your Lord when He seizes the (population of) towns while they are doing wrong.
إِنَّ أَخْذَهُ أَلِيمٌ شَدِيدٌ
Verily, His Seizure is painful, and severe.
11:103
إِنَّ فِي ذَلِكَ لَآيَةً لِمَنْ خَافَ عَذَابَ الْآخِرَةِ ۚ
Indeed in that (there) is a sure lesson for those who fear the torment of the Hereafter.
ذَلِكَ يَوْمٌ مَجْمُوعٌ لَهُ النَّاسُ وَذَلِكَ يَوْمٌ مَشْهُودٌ
That is a Day whereon mankind will be gathered together, and that is a Day when all (the dwellers of the heavens and the earth) will be present.
11:104
وَمَا نُؤَخِّرُهُ إِلَّا لِأَجَلٍ مَعْدُودٍ
And We delay it only for a term (already) fixed.
11:105
يَوْمَ يَأْتِ لَا تَكَلَّمُ نَفْسٌ إِلَّا بِإِذْنِهِ ۚ
On the Day when it comes, no person shall speak except by His (Allah (الله)‘s) Leave.
فَمِنْهُمْ شَقِيٌّ وَسَعِيدٌ
Some among them will be wretched and (others) blessed.
11:106
فَأَمَّا الَّذِينَ شَقُوا فَفِي النَّارِ لَهُمْ فِيهَا زَفِيرٌ وَشَهِيقٌ
As for those who are wretched, they will be in the Fire, sighing in a high and low tone.
11:107
خَالِدِينَ فِيهَا مَا دَامَتِ السَّمَاوَاتُ وَالْأَرْضُ إِلَّا مَا شَاءَ رَبُّكَ ۚ
They will dwell therein for all the time that the heavens and the earth endure, except as your Lord wills.
إِنَّ رَبَّكَ فَعَّالٌ لِمَا يُرِيدُ
Verily, your Lord is the doer of what He wills.
11:108
وَأَمَّا الَّذِينَ سُعِدُوا فَفِي الْجَنَّةِ
And those who are blessed, they will be in Paradise (الجنة),
خَالِدِينَ فِيهَا مَا دَامَتِ السَّمَاوَاتُ وَالْأَرْضُ إِلَّا مَا شَاءَ رَبُّكَ ۖ
abiding therein for all the time that the heavens and the earth endure, except as your Lord will,
عَطَاءً غَيْرَ مَجْذُوذٍ
a gift without an end.
11:109
فَلَا تَكُ فِي مِرْيَةٍ مِمَّا يَعْبُدُ هَؤُلَاءِ ۚ
So be not in doubt (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) as to what these (pagans and polytheists) men worship.
مَا يَعْبُدُونَ إِلَّا كَمَا يَعْبُدُ آبَاؤُهُمْ مِنْ قَبْلُ ۚ
They worship nothing but what their fathers worshipped before (them).
وَإِنَّا لَمُوَفُّوهُمْ نَصِيبَهُمْ غَيْرَ مَنْقُوصٍ
And verily, We shall repay them in full their portion without diminution.
11:[110-113] Differences arose about the book given to Musa for his followers‘ lack of belief
11:[114-117] Virtues remove evils and Allah does not let the reward of the righteous be wasted
11:[118-123] Freedom of choice given to mankind is the Will of Allah
11:110
وَلَقَدْ آتَيْنَا مُوسَى الْكِتَابَ فَاخْتُلِفَ فِيهِ ۚ
Indeed, We gave the Book to Musa (Moosa (موسى)), but differences arose therein,
وَلَوْلَا كَلِمَةٌ سَبَقَتْ مِنْ رَبِّكَ لَقُضِيَ بَيْنَهُمْ ۚ
and had it not been for a Word that had gone forth before from your Lord, the case would have been judged between them,
وَإِنَّهُمْ لَفِي شَكٍّ مِنْهُ مُرِيبٍ
and indeed they are in grave doubt concerning it (this Quran (القرآن)).
11:111
وَإِنَّ كُلًّا لَمَّا لَيُوَفِّيَنَّهُمْ رَبُّكَ أَعْمَالَهُمْ ۚ
And verily, to each of them your Lord will repay their works in full.
إِنَّهُ بِمَا يَعْمَلُونَ خَبِيرٌ
Surely, He is All-Aware (Al-‘Alee (ٱلْعَلِيُّ)m (ٱلْعَلِيمُ)) of what they do.
11:112
فَاسْتَقِمْ كَمَا أُمِرْتَ وَمَنْ تَابَ مَعَكَ وَلَا تَطْغَوْا ۚ
So stand (ask Allah (الله) to make) you (Muhammad (محمد) SAW) firm and straight (on the religion of Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism) as you are commanded and those (your companions) who turn in repentance (unto Allah (الله)) with you, and transgress not (Allah (الله)‘s legal limits).
إِنَّهُ بِمَا تَعْمَلُونَ بَصِيرٌ
Verily, He is All-Seer (Al-Baseer (ٱلْبَصِيرُ)) of what you do.
11:113
وَلَا تَرْكَنُوا إِلَى الَّذِينَ ظَلَمُوا فَتَمَسَّكُمُ النَّارُ
And incline not toward those who do wrong, lest the Fire should touch you,
وَمَا لَكُمْ مِنْ دُونِ اللَّهِ مِنْ أَوْلِيَاءَ ثُمَّ لَا تُنْصَرُونَ
and you have no protectors other than Allah (الله), nor you would then be helped.
11:114
‏وَأَقِمِ الصَّلَاةَ طَرَفَيِ النَّهَارِ وَزُلَفًا مِنَ اللَّيْلِ ۚ
And perform As-Salat (الصَّلَاةَ) (Iqamat-as-Salat), at the two ends of the day and in some hours of the night (i.e. the five compulsory Salat (prayers)).
إِنَّ الْحَسَنَاتِ يُذْهِبْنَ السَّيِّئَاتِ ۚ
Verily, the good deeds remove the evil deeds (i.e. small sins).
ذَلِكَ ذِكْرَى لِلذَّاكِرِينَ
That is a reminder (an advice) for the mindful (those who accept advice).
11:115
وَاصْبِرْ فَإِنَّ اللَّهَ لَا يُضِيعُ أَجْرَ الْمُحْسِنِينَ
And be patient; verily, Allah (الله) loses not the reward of the good-doers.
11:116
فَلَوْلَا كَانَ مِنَ الْقُرُونِ مِنْ قَبْلِكُمْ أُولُو بَقِيَّةٍ يَنْهَوْنَ عَنِ الْفَسَادِ فِي الْأَرْضِ
If only there had been among the generations before you, persons having wisdom, prohibiting (others) from Al-Fasad (disbelief, polytheism, and all kinds of crimes and sins) in the earth,
إِلَّا قَلِيلًا مِمَّنْ أَنْجَيْنَا مِنْهُمْ ۗ
except a few of those whom We saved from among them.
وَاتَّبَعَ الَّذِينَ ظَلَمُوا مَا أُتْرِفُوا فِيهِ وَكَانُوا مُجْرِمِينَ
Those who did wrong pursued the enjoyment of good things of (this worldly) life, and were Mujrimoon (criminals, disbelievers in Allah (الله), polytheists, sinners, etc.).
11:117
وَمَا كَانَ رَبُّكَ لِيُهْلِكَ الْقُرَى بِظُلْمٍ وَأَهْلُهَا مُصْلِحُونَ
And your Lord would never destroy the towns wrongfully, while their people were right-doers.
11:118
وَلَوْ شَاءَ رَبُّكَ لَجَعَلَ النَّاسَ أُمَّةً وَاحِدَةً ۖ
And if your Lord had so willed, He could surely have made mankind one Ummah (nation or community (following one religion only i.e. Islam (الإسلام))),
وَلَا يَزَالُونَ مُخْتَلِفِينَ
but they will not cease to disagree,-
11:119
إِلَّا مَنْ رَحِمَ رَبُّكَ ۚ
Except him on whom your Lord has bestowed His Mercy (the follower of truth - Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism)
وَلِذَلِكَ خَلَقَهُمْ ۗ
and for that did He create them.
وَتَمَّتْ كَلِمَةُ رَبِّكَ لَأَمْلَأَنَّ جَهَنَّمَ مِنَ الْجِنَّةِ وَالنَّاسِ أَجْمَعِينَ
And the Word of your Lord has been fulfilled (i.e. His Saying): ‘Surely, I shall fill Hell (جهنم) with jinns and men all together.‘
11:120
وَكُلًّا نَقُصُّ عَلَيْكَ مِنْ أَنْبَاءِ الرُّسُلِ مَا نُثَبِّتُ بِهِ فُؤَادَكَ ۚ
And all that We relate to you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) of the news of the Messengers is in order that We may make strong and firm your heart thereby.
وَجَاءَكَ فِي هَذِهِ الْحَقُّ وَمَوْعِظَةٌ وَذِكْرَى لِلْمُؤْمِنِينَ
And in this (chapter of the Quran (القرآن)) has come to you the truth, as well as an admonition and a reminder for the believers.
11:121
وَقُلْ لِلَّذِينَ لَا يُؤْمِنُونَ اعْمَلُوا عَلَى مَكَانَتِكُمْ إِنَّا عَامِلُونَ
And say to those who do not believe:
Act according to your ability and way, We are acting (in our way).
11:122
وَانْتَظِرُوا إِنَّا مُنْتَظِرُونَ
And you wait ! We (too) are waiting.‘
11:123
وَلِلَّهِ غَيْبُ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضِ وَإِلَيْهِ يُرْجَعُ الْأَمْرُ كُلُّهُ
And to Allah (الله) belongs the Ghaib (unseen) of the heavens and the earth, and to Him return all affairs (for decision).
فَاعْبُدْهُ وَتَوَكَّلْ عَلَيْهِ ۚ
So worship Him (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) and put your trust in Him.
وَمَا رَبُّكَ بِغَافِلٍ عَمَّا تَعْمَلُونَ
And your Lord is not unaware of what you (people) do.‘*********
