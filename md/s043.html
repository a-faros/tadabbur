Az-Zukhruf
The Gold Adornments, The Ornaments of Gold, Luxury, Gold
43:[1-8] The Qur‘an is a transcript from the Mother Book which is in Allah‘s keeping
43:[9-15] Even the mushrikïn believe that heavens, earth and all therein is created by Allah. Supplication before riding a conveyance
43:1
حمٓ
Ha Meem.(These letters are one of the miracles of the Quran, and none but Allah (Alone) knows their meanings).
43:2
وَٱلۡكِتَـٰبِ ٱلۡمُبِينِ
By the manifest Book (that makes things clear, i.e. this Quran).
43:3
إِنَّا جَعَلۡنَـٰهُ قُرۡءَٲنًا عَرَبِيًّ۬ا لَّعَلَّڪُمۡ تَعۡقِلُونَ
We verily, have made it a Quran in Arabic, that you may be able to understand (its meanings and its admonitions).
43:4
وَإِنَّهُ ۥ فِىٓ أُمِّ ٱلۡكِتَـٰبِ لَدَيۡنَا لَعَلِىٌّ حَكِيمٌ
And Verily, it (this Quran) is in the Mother of the Book (i.e. Al-Lauh Al-Mahfooz), before Us, indeed Exalted, full of Wisdom.
43:5
أَفَنَضۡرِبُ عَنكُمُ ٱلذِّڪۡرَ صَفۡحًا أَن ڪُنتُمۡ قَوۡمً۬ا مُّسۡرِفِينَ
Shall We then (warn you not and) take away the Reminder (this Quran) from you, because you are a people Musrifoon.
43:6
وَكَمۡ أَرۡسَلۡنَا مِن نَّبِىٍّ۬ فِى ٱلۡأَوَّلِينَ
And how many a Prophet have We sent amongst the men of old.
43:7
وَمَا يَأۡتِيهِم مِّن نَّبِىٍّ إِلَّا كَانُواْ بِهِۦ يَسۡتَہۡزِءُونَ
And never came there a Prophet to them but they used to mock at him.
43:8
فَأَهۡلَكۡنَآ أَشَدَّ مِنۡہُم بَطۡشً۬ا وَمَضَىٰ مَثَلُ ٱلۡأَوَّلِينَ
Then We destroyed men stronger (in power) than these, and the example of the ancients has passed away (before them).
43:9
وَلَٮِٕن سَأَلۡتَهُم مَّنۡ خَلَقَ ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٲتِ وَٱلۡأَرۡضَ
And indeed if you ask them, ‘Who has created the heavens and the earth?‘
لَيَقُولُنَّ خَلَقَهُنَّ ٱلۡعَزِيزُ ٱلۡعَلِيمُ
They will surely say: ‘The All-Mighty, the All-Knower (Al-‘Aleem) created them.‘
43:10
ٱلَّذِى جَعَلَ لَڪُمُ ٱلۡأَرۡضَ مَهۡدً۬اوَجَعَلَ لَكُمۡ فِيہَا سُبُلاً۬ لَّعَلَّكُمۡ تَهۡتَدُونَ
Who has made for you the earth like a bed, and has made for you roads therein, in order that you may find your way,
43:11
وَٱلَّذِى نَزَّلَ مِنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ مَآءَۢ بِقَدَرٍ۬فَأَنشَرۡنَا بِهِۦ بَلۡدَةً۬ مَّيۡتً۬ا‌ۚ
And Who sends down water (rain) from the sky in due measure. Then We revive a dead land therewith,
كَذَٲلِكَ تُخۡرَجُونَ
and even so you will be brought forth (from the dead),
43:12
وَٱلَّذِى خَلَقَ ٱلۡأَزۡوَٲجَ كُلَّهَا
And Who has created all the pairs
وَجَعَلَ لَكُم مِّنَ ٱلۡفُلۡكِ وَٱلۡأَنۡعَـٰمِ مَا تَرۡكَبُونَ
and has appointed for you ships and cattle on which you ride,
43:13
لِتَسۡتَوُ ۥاْ عَلَىٰ ظُهُورِهِۦثُمَّ تَذۡكُرُواْ نِعۡمَةَ رَبِّكُمۡ إِذَا ٱسۡتَوَيۡتُمۡ عَلَيۡهِ وَتَقُولُواْ
In order that you may mount firmly on their backs, and then may remember the Favour of your Lord when you mount thereon, and say:
سُبۡحَـٰنَ ٱلَّذِى سَخَّرَ لَنَا هَـٰذَا وَمَا ڪُنَّا لَهُ ۥ مُقۡرِنِينَ
Glory to Him who has subjected this to us, and we could never have it (by our efforts).‘
43:14
وَإِنَّآ إِلَىٰ رَبِّنَا لَمُنقَلِبُونَ
And verily, to Our Lord we indeed are to return!
43:15
وَجَعَلُواْ لَهُ ۥ مِنۡ عِبَادِهِۦ جُزۡءًا‌ۚ
Yet they assign to some of His slaves a share with Him (by pretending that He has children, and considering them as equals or co-partners in worship with Him).
إِنَّ ٱلۡإِنسَـٰنَ لَكَفُورٌ۬ مُّبِينٌ
Verily, man is indeed a manifest ingrate!
43:[16-25] Some mushrikïn regard the angels to be the female divinities being the daughters of Allah
43:16
أَمِ ٱتَّخَذَ مِمَّا يَخۡلُقُ بَنَاتٍ۬ وَأَصۡفَٮٰكُم بِٱلۡبَنِينَ
Or has He taken daughters out of what He has created, and He has selected for you sons?
43:17
وَإِذَا بُشِّرَ أَحَدُهُم بِمَا ضَرَبَ لِلرَّحۡمَـٰنِ مَثَلاً۬ ظَلَّ وَجۡهُهُ ۥ مُسۡوَدًّ۬ا وَهُوَ كَظِيمٌ
And if one of them is informed of the news of (the birth of) that which he set forth as a parable to the Most Beneficent (Ar-Rahmaan) (i.e. of a girl), his face becomes dark, gloomy, and he is filled with grief!
43:18
أَوَمَن يُنَشَّؤُاْ فِى ٱلۡحِلۡيَةِ وَهُوَ فِى ٱلۡخِصَامِ غَيۡرُ مُبِينٍ۬
(Do they then like for Allah)
a creature who is brought up in adornments (wearing silk and gold ornaments, i.e. women), and in dispute cannot make herself clear?
43:19
وَجَعَلُواْ ٱلۡمَلَـٰٓٮِٕكَةَ ٱلَّذِينَ هُمۡ عِبَـٰدُ ٱلرَّحۡمَـٰنِ إِنَـٰثًا‌ۚ
And they make the angels who themselves are slaves to the Most Beneficent (Ar-Rahmaan) females.
أَشَهِدُواْ خَلۡقَهُمۡ‌ۚ
Did they witness their creation?
سَتُكۡتَبُ شَهَـٰدَتُہُمۡ وَيُسۡـَٔلُونَ
Their evidence will be recorded, and they will be questioned!
43:20
وَقَالُواْ لَوۡ شَآءَ ٱلرَّحۡمَـٰنُ مَا عَبَدۡنَـٰهُم‌ۗ
And they said: ‘If it had been the Will of the Most Beneficent (Ar-Rahmaan), we should not have worshipped them (false deities).‘
مَّا لَهُم بِذَٲلِكَ مِنۡ عِلۡمٍ‌ۖ
They have no knowledge whatsoever of that.
إِنۡ هُمۡ إِلَّا يَخۡرُصُونَ
They do nothing but lie!
43:21
أَمۡ ءَاتَيۡنَـٰهُمۡ ڪِتَـٰبً۬ا مِّن قَبۡلِهِۦ فَهُم بِهِۦ مُسۡتَمۡسِكُونَ
Or have We given them any Book before this (the Quran), to which they are holding fast?
43:22
بَلۡ قَالُوٓاْ إِنَّا وَجَدۡنَآ ءَابَآءَنَا عَلَىٰٓ أُمَّةٍ۬ وَإِنَّا عَلَىٰٓ ءَاثَـٰرِهِم مُّهۡتَدُونَ
Nay! They say: ‘We found our fathers following a certain way and religion, and we guide ourselves by their footsteps.‘
43:23
وَكَذَٲلِكَ مَآ أَرۡسَلۡنَا مِن قَبۡلِكَ فِى قَرۡيَةٍ۬ مِّن نَّذِيرٍإِلَّا قَالَ مُتۡرَفُوهَآ
And similarly, We sent not a warner before you (O Muhammad SAW) to any town (people) but the luxurious ones among them said:
إِنَّا وَجَدۡنَآ ءَابَآءَنَا عَلَىٰٓ أُمَّةٍ۬ وَإِنَّا عَلَىٰٓ ءَاثَـٰرِهِم مُّقۡتَدُونَ
We found our fathers following a certain way and religion, and we will indeed follow their footsteps.‘
43:24
قَـٰلَ أَوَلَوۡ جِئۡتُكُم بِأَهۡدَىٰ مِمَّا وَجَدتُّمۡ عَلَيۡهِ ءَابَآءَكُمۡ‌ۖ
(The warner) said: ‘Even if I bring you better guidance than that which you found your fathers following?‘
قَالُوٓاْ إِنَّا بِمَآ أُرۡسِلۡتُم بِهِۦ كَـٰفِرُونَ
They said: ‘Verily, We disbelieve in that with which you have been sent.‘
43:25
فَٱنتَقَمۡنَا مِنۡہُمۡ‌ۖ
So We took revenge of them,
فَٱنظُرۡ كَيۡفَ كَانَ عَـٰقِبَةُ ٱلۡمُكَذِّبِينَ
then see what was the end of those who denied (Islamic Monotheism).
43:[26-35] The Prophet Ibrãhïm recognized the Oneness of Allah and rejected Shirk (Polytheism).If it were not that all mankind will become one race of unbelievers, Allah would have given the unbelievers houses made with sterling silver
43:26
وَإِذۡ قَالَ إِبۡرَٲهِيمُ لِأَبِيهِ وَقَوۡمِهِۦۤ
And (remember) when Ibraheem (Abraham) said to his father and his people:
إِنَّنِى بَرَآءٌ۬ مِّمَّا تَعۡبُدُونَ
Verily, I am innocent of what you worship,
43:27
إِلَّا ٱلَّذِى فَطَرَنِى فَإِنَّهُ ۥ سَيَہۡدِينِ
Except Him (i.e. I worship none but Allah Alone) Who did create me, and verily, He will guide me.‘
43:28
وَجَعَلَهَا كَلِمَةَۢ بَاقِيَةً۬ فِى عَقِبِهِۦ لَعَلَّهُمۡ يَرۡجِعُونَ
And he made it (i.e. La ilaha ill-Allah (none has the right to be worshipped but Allah Alone)) a Word lasting among his offspring (True Monotheism), that they may turn back (i.e. to repent to Allah or receive admonition).
43:29
بَلۡ مَتَّعۡتُ هَـٰٓؤُلَآءِ وَءَابَآءَهُمۡ حَتَّىٰ جَآءَهُمُ ٱلۡحَقُّ وَرَسُولٌ۬ مُّبِينٌ۬
Nay, but I gave (the good things of this life) to these (polytheists) and their fathers to enjoy, till there came to them the truth (the Quran), and a Messenger (Muhammad SAW) making things clear.
43:30
وَلَمَّا جَآءَهُمُ ٱلۡحَقُّ قَالُواْ هَـٰذَا سِحۡرٌ۬ وَإِنَّا بِهِۦ كَـٰفِرُونَ
And when the truth (this Quran) came to them, they (the disbelievers in this Quran) said: ‘This is magic, and we disbelieve therein.‘
43:31
وَقَالُواْ لَوۡلَا نُزِّلَ هَـٰذَا ٱلۡقُرۡءَانُ عَلَىٰ رَجُلٍ۬ مِّنَ ٱلۡقَرۡيَتَيۡنِ عَظِيمٍ
And they say: ‘Why is not this Quran sent down to some great man of the two towns (Makkah and Taif)?‘
43:32
أَهُمۡ يَقۡسِمُونَ رَحۡمَتَ رَبِّكَ‌ۚ
Is it they who would portion out the Mercy of your Lord?
نَحۡنُ قَسَمۡنَا بَيۡنَہُم مَّعِيشَتَہُمۡ فِى ٱلۡحَيَوٰةِ ٱلدُّنۡيَا‌ۚ
It is We Who portion out between them their livelihood in this world,
وَرَفَعۡنَا بَعۡضَہُمۡ فَوۡقَ بَعۡضٍ۬ دَرَجَـٰتٍ۬ لِّيَتَّخِذَ بَعۡضُہُم بَعۡضً۬ا سُخۡرِيًّ۬ا‌ۗ
and We raised some of them above others in ranks, so that some may employ others in their work.
وَرَحۡمَتُ رَبِّكَ خَيۡرٌ۬ مِّمَّا يَجۡمَعُونَ
But the Mercy (Paradise) of your Lord (O Muhammad SAW) is better than the (wealth of this world) which they amass.
43:33
وَلَوۡلَآ أَن يَكُونَ ٱلنَّاسُ أُمَّةً۬ وَٲحِدَةً۬ لَّجَعَلۡنَا لِمَن يَكۡفُرُ بِٱلرَّحۡمَـٰنِ لِبُيُوتِہِمۡ سُقُفً۬ا مِّن فِضَّةٍ۬ وَمَعَارِجَ عَلَيۡہَا يَظۡهَرُونَ
And were it not that all mankind would have become of one community (all disbelievers, desiring worldly life only), We would have provided for those who disbelieve in the Most Beneficent (Ar-Rahmaan), silver roofs for their houses, and elevators (and stair-ways, etc. of silver) whereby they ascend,
43:34
وَلِبُيُوتِہِمۡ أَبۡوَٲبً۬ا وَسُرُرًا عَلَيۡہَا يَتَّكِـُٔونَ
And for their houses, doors (of silver), and thrones (of silver) on which they could recline,
43:35
وَزُخۡرُفً۬ا‌ۚ
And adornments of gold.
وَإِن ڪُلُّ ذَٲلِكَ لَمَّا مَتَـٰعُ ٱلۡحَيَوٰةِ ٱلدُّنۡيَا‌ۚ
Yet all this (i.e. the roofs, doors, stairs, elevators, thrones etc. of their houses) would have been nothing but an enjoyment of this world.
وَٱلۡأَخِرَةُ عِندَ رَبِّكَ لِلۡمُتَّقِينَ
And the Hereafter with your Lord is only for the Muttaqoon.
43:[36-45] He who turns away from the remembrance of Allah, Allah appoints a shaitãn to be his intimate friend. Hold fast to The Qur‘an if you want to be rightly guided
43:36
وَمَن يَعۡشُ عَن ذِكۡرِ ٱلرَّحۡمَـٰنِ نُقَيِّضۡ لَهُ ۥ شَيۡطَـٰنً۬ا فَهُوَ لَهُ ۥ قَرِينٌ۬
And whosoever turns away (blinds himself) from the remembrance of the Most Beneficent (Ar-Rahmaan) (i.e. this Quran and worship of Allah), We appoint for him Shaitan (Satan devil) to be a Qareen (an intimate companion) to him.
43:37
وَإِنَّہُمۡ لَيَصُدُّونَہُمۡ عَنِ ٱلسَّبِيلِ وَيَحۡسَبُونَ أَنَّہُم مُّهۡتَدُونَ
And verily, they (Satans / devils) hinder them from the Path (of Allah), but they think that they are guided aright!
43:38
حَتَّىٰٓ إِذَا جَآءَنَا قَالَ يَـٰلَيۡتَ بَيۡنِى وَبَيۡنَكَ بُعۡدَ ٱلۡمَشۡرِقَيۡنِ
Till, when (such a one) comes to Us, he says (to his Qareen (Satan / devil companion)) ‘Would that between me and you were the distance of the two easts (or the east and west)‘
فَبِئۡسَ ٱلۡقَرِينُ
a worst (type of) companion (indeed)!
43:39
وَلَن يَنفَعَڪُمُ ٱلۡيَوۡمَ إِذ ظَّلَمۡتُمۡ أَنَّكُمۡ فِى ٱلۡعَذَابِ مُشۡتَرِكُونَ
It will profit you not this Day (O you who turn away from Allah‘s remembrance and His worship, etc.) as you did wrong, (and) that you will be sharers (you and your Qareen) in the punishment.
43:40
أَفَأَنتَ تُسۡمِعُ ٱلصُّمَّ
Can you (O Muhammad SAW) make the deaf to hear,
أَوۡ تَہۡدِى ٱلۡعُمۡىَ
or can you guide the blind
وَمَن كَانَ فِى ضَلَـٰلٍ۬ مُّبِينٍ۬
or him who is in manifest error?
43:41
فَإِمَّا نَذۡهَبَنَّ بِكَ فَإِنَّا مِنۡہُم مُّنتَقِمُونَ
And even if We take you (O Muhammad SAW) away, We shall indeed take vengeance on them.
43:42
أَوۡ نُرِيَنَّكَ ٱلَّذِى وَعَدۡنَـٰهُمۡ
Or (if) We show you that wherewith We threaten them,
فَإِنَّا عَلَيۡہِم مُّقۡتَدِرُونَ
then verily, We have perfect command over them.
43:43
فَٱسۡتَمۡسِكۡ بِٱلَّذِىٓ أُوحِىَ إِلَيۡكَ‌ۖ
So hold you (O Muhammad SAW) fast to that which is inspired in you.
إِنَّكَ عَلَىٰ صِرَٲطٍ۬ مُّسۡتَقِيمٍ۬
Verily, you are on a Straight Path.
43:44
وَإِنَّهُ ۥ لَذِكۡرٌ۬ لَّكَ وَلِقَوۡمِكَ‌ۖ
And verily, this (the Quran) is indeed a Reminder for you (O Muhammad SAW) and your people (Quraish people, or your followers),
وَسَوۡفَ تُسۡـَٔلُونَ
and you will be questioned (about it).
43:45
وَسۡـَٔلۡ مَنۡ أَرۡسَلۡنَا مِن قَبۡلِكَ مِن رُّسُلِنَآ
And ask (O Muhammad SAW) those of Our Messengers whom We sent before you:
أَجَعَلۡنَا مِن دُونِ ٱلرَّحۡمَـٰنِ ءَالِهَةً۬ يُعۡبَدُونَ
Did We ever appoint aliha (gods) to be worshipped besides the Most Beneficent (Ar-Rahmaan)?‘
43:[46-56] The Prophet Musa was sent to Fir‘on and his chiefs with signs but they ridiculed him and the signs, as a result, Allah drowned them all
43:46
وَلَقَدۡ أَرۡسَلۡنَا مُوسَىٰ بِـَٔايَـٰتِنَآ إِلَىٰ فِرۡعَوۡنَ وَمَلَإِيْهِۦ
And indeed We did send Musa (Moosa) with Our Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, etc.) to Firaun (Pharaoh) and his chiefs (inviting them to Allahs Religion of Islam)
فَقَالَ إِنِّى رَسُولُ رَبِّ ٱلۡعَـٰلَمِينَ
He said: ‘Verily, I am a Messenger of the Lord of the Alameen (mankind, jinns and all that exists).‘
43:47
فَلَمَّا جَآءَهُم بِـَٔايَـٰتِنَآ إِذَا هُم مِّنۡہَا يَضۡحَكُونَ
But when he came to them with Our Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, etc.) behold! They laughed at them.
43:48
وَمَا نُرِيهِم مِّنۡ ءَايَةٍ إِلَّا هِىَ أَڪۡبَرُ مِنۡ أُخۡتِهَا‌ۖ
And not an Ayah (sign, etc.) We showed them but it was greater than its fellow,
وَأَخَذۡنَـٰهُم بِٱلۡعَذَابِ لَعَلَّهُمۡ يَرۡجِعُونَ
and We seized them with torment, in order that they might turn (from their polytheism to Allah‘s Religion (Islamic Monotheism)).
43:49
وَقَالُواْ يَـٰٓأَيُّهَ ٱلسَّاحِرُ
And they said (to Musa (Moosa)): ‘O you sorcerer!
ٱدۡعُ لَنَا رَبَّكَ بِمَا عَهِدَ عِندَكَ إِنَّنَا لَمُهۡتَدُونَ
Invoke your Lord for us according to what He has covenanted with you. Verily, We shall guide ourselves (aright).‘
43:50
