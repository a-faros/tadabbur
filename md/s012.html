12:[1-3] The Qur‘an is revealed in the Arabic language
12:[4-6] Story of the Prophet Yüsuf (Joseph)
12:1
الر ۚ
Alif-Lam-Ra.(These letters are one of the miracles of the Quran (القرآن), and none but Allah (الله) (Alone) knows their meanings).
تِلْكَ آيَاتُ الْكِتَابِ الْمُبِينِ
These are the Verses of the Clear Book (the Quran (القرآن) that makes clear the legal and illegal things, legal laws, a guidance and a blessing).
12:2
إِنَّا أَنْزَلْنَاهُ قُرْآنًا عَرَبِيًّا لَعَلَّكُمْ تَعْقِلُونَ
Verily, We have sent it down as an Arabic (العربية) Quran (القرآن) in order that you may understand.
12:3
نَحْنُ نَقُصُّ عَلَيْكَ أَحْسَنَ الْقَصَصِ بِمَا أَوْحَيْنَا إِلَيْكَ هَذَا الْقُرْآنَ
We relate unto you (Muhammad (محمد) SAW) the best of stories through Our Revelations unto you, of this Quran (القرآن).
وَإِنْ كُنْتَ مِنْ قَبْلِهِ لَمِنَ الْغَافِلِينَ
And before this (i.e. before the coming of Divine Inspiration to you), you were among those who knew nothing about it (the Quran (القرآن)).
12:4
إِذْ قَالَ يُوسُفُ لِأَبِيهِ
(Remember) when Yoosuf (Joseph) said to his father:
يَا أَبَتِ إِنِّي رَأَيْتُ أَحَدَ عَشَرَ كَوْكَبًا وَالشَّمْسَ وَالْقَمَرَ رَأَيْتُهُمْ لِي سَاجِدِينَ
O my father! Verily, I saw (in a dream) eleven stars and the sun and the moon, I saw them prostrating themselves to me.‘
12:5
قَالَ يَا بُنَيَّ لَا تَقْصُصْ رُؤْيَاكَ عَلَى إِخْوَتِكَ فَيَكِيدُوا لَكَ كَيْدًا ۖ
He (the father) said: ‘O my son! Relate not your vision to your brothers, lest they arrange a plot against you.
إِنَّ الشَّيْطَانَ لِلْإِنْسَانِ عَدُوٌّ مُبِينٌ
Verily! Shaitan (Satan (الشيطان)) is to man an open enemy!
12:6
وَكَذَلِكَ يَجْتَبِيكَ رَبُّكَ وَيُعَلِّمُكَ مِنْ تَأْوِيلِ الْأَحَادِيثِ
Thus will your Lord choose you and teach you the interpretation of dreams (and other things)
وَيُتِمُّ نِعْمَتَهُ عَلَيْكَ وَعَلَى آلِ يَعْقُوبَ
and perfect His Favour on you and on the offspring of Yaqoob (Yaqqub (يعقوب)),
كَمَا أَتَمَّهَا عَلَى أَبَوَيْكَ مِنْ قَبْلُ إِبْرَاهِيمَ وَإِسْحَاقَ ۚ
as He perfected it on your fathers, Ibraheem (Abraham) and Ishaque (Ishak (إسحاق)) aforetime!
إِنَّ رَبَّكَ عَلِيمٌ حَكِيمٌ
Verily, your Lord is All-Knowing (Al-‘Alee (ٱلْعَلِيُّ)m (ٱلْعَلِيمُ)), All- Wise.‘
12:[7-10] In this story there are lessons for the inquirers
12:[11-15] The stepbrothers of Yüsuf asked their father to send him with them on a hunting trip and threw him in a dark well
12:[16-18] They told their father that Yüsuf was eaten by a wolf
12:[19-20] A caravan kidnapped him, brought him to Egypt, and sold him
12:7
لَقَدْ كَانَ فِي يُوسُفَ وَإِخْوَتِهِ آيَاتٌ لِلسَّائِلِينَ
Verily, in Yoosuf (Joseph) and his brethren, there were Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, etc.) for those who ask.
12:8
إِذْ قَالُوا لَيُوسُفُ وَأَخُوهُ أَحَبُّ إِلَى أَبِينَا مِنَّا وَنَحْنُ عُصْبَةٌ
When they said: ‘Truly, Yoosuf (Joseph) and his brother (Benjamin) are loved more by our father than we, but we are Usbah (a strong group).
إِنَّ أَبَانَا لَفِي ضَلَالٍ مُبِينٍ
Really, our father is in a plain error.
12:9
اقْتُلُوا يُوسُفَ أَوِ اطْرَحُوهُ أَرْضًا يَخْلُ لَكُمْ وَجْهُ أَبِيكُمْ
Kill Yoosuf (Joseph) or cast him out to some (other) land, so that the favour of your father may be given to you alone,
وَتَكُونُوا مِنْ بَعْدِهِ قَوْمًا صَالِحِينَ
and after that you will be righteous folk (by intending repentance before committing the sin).‘
12:10
قَالَ قَائِلٌ مِنْهُمْ
One from among them said:
لَا تَقْتُلُوا يُوسُفَ وَأَلْقُوهُ فِي غَيَابَتِ الْجُبِّ يَلْتَقِطْهُ بَعْضُ السَّيَّارَةِ إِنْ كُنْتُمْ فَاعِلِينَ
Kill not Yoosuf (Joseph), but if you must do something, throw him down to the bottom of a well, he will be picked up by some caravan of travellers.‘
12:11
قَالُوا يَا أَبَانَا مَا لَكَ لَا تَأْمَنَّا عَلَى يُوسُفَ وَإِنَّا لَهُ لَنَاصِحُونَ
They said: ‘O our father! Why do you not trust us with Yoosuf (Joseph), - when we are indeed his well-wishers?‘
12:12
أَرْسِلْهُ مَعَنَا غَدًا يَرْتَعْ وَيَلْعَبْ وَإِنَّا لَهُ لَحَافِظُونَ
Send him with us tomorrow to enjoy himself and play, and verily, we will take care of him.‘
12:13
قَالَ إِنِّي لَيَحْزُنُنِي أَنْ تَذْهَبُوا بِهِ
He (Yaqoob (Jacob)) said: ‘Truly, it saddens me that you should take him away.
وَأَخَافُ أَنْ يَأْكُلَهُ الذِّئْبُ وَأَنْتُمْ عَنْهُ غَافِلُونَ
I fear lest a wolf should devour him, while you are careless of him.‘
12:14
قَالُوا لَئِنْ أَكَلَهُ الذِّئْبُ وَنَحْنُ عُصْبَةٌ إِنَّا إِذًا لَخَاسِرُونَ
They said: ‘If a wolf devours him, while we are Usbah (a strong group) (to guard him), then surely, we are the losers.‘
12:15
فَلَمَّا ذَهَبُوا بِهِ وَأَجْمَعُوا أَنْ يَجْعَلُوهُ فِي غَيَابَتِ الْجُبِّ ۚ
So, when they took him away, they all agreed to throw him down to the bottom of the well,
وَأَوْحَيْنَا إِلَيْهِ لَتُنَبِّئَنَّهُمْ بِأَمْرِهِمْ هَذَا وَهُمْ لَا يَشْعُرُونَ
and We inspired in him: ‘Indeed, you shall (one day) inform them of this their affair, when they know (you) not.‘
12:16
وَجَاءُوا أَبَاهُمْ عِشَاءً يَبْكُونَ
And they came to their father in the early part of the night weeping.
12:17
قَالُوا يَا أَبَانَا إِنَّا ذَهَبْنَا نَسْتَبِقُ وَتَرَكْنَا يُوسُفَ عِنْدَ مَتَاعِنَا فَأَكَلَهُ الذِّئْبُ ۖ
They said: ‘O our father! We went racing with one another, and left Yoosuf (Joseph) by our belongings and a wolf devoured him;
وَمَا أَنْتَ بِمُؤْمِنٍ لَنَا وَلَوْ كُنَّا صَادِقِينَ
but you will never believe us even when we speak the truth.‘
12:18
وَجَاءُوا عَلَى قَمِيصِهِ بِدَمٍ كَذِبٍ ۚ
And they brought his shirt stained with false blood.
قَالَ بَلْ سَوَّلَتْ لَكُمْ أَنْفُسُكُمْ أَمْرًا ۖ
He said: ‘Nay, but your ownselves have made up a tale.
فَصَبْرٌ جَمِيلٌ ۖ
So (for me) patience is most fitting.
وَاللَّهُ الْمُسْتَعَانُ عَلَى مَا تَصِفُونَ
And it is Allah (الله) (Alone) Whose help can be sought against that which you assert.‘
12:19
وَجَاءَتْ سَيَّارَةٌ فَأَرْسَلُوا وَارِدَهُمْ فَأَدْلَى دَلْوَهُ ۖ
And there came a caravan of travellers; they sent their water-drawer, and he let down his bucket (into the well).
قَالَ يَا بُشْرَى هَذَا غُلَامٌ ۚ
He said: ‘What good news! Here is a boy.‘
وَأَسَرُّوهُ بِضَاعَةً ۚ
So they hid him as merchandise (a slave).
وَاللَّهُ عَلِيمٌ بِمَا يَعْمَلُونَ
And Allah (الله) was the All-Knower (Al-‘Alee (ٱلْعَلِيُّ)m (ٱلْعَلِيمُ)) of what they did.
12:20
وَشَرَوْهُ بِثَمَنٍ بَخْسٍ دَرَاهِمَ مَعْدُودَةٍ
And they sold him for a low price, - for a few Dirhams (i.e. for a few silver coins).
وَكَانُوا فِيهِ مِنَ الزَّاهِدِينَ
And they were of those who regarded him insignificant.
12:[21-22] The Egyptian who bought him was a good man
12:[23-29] His master‘s wife tried to seduce him but Allah saved him
12:21
وَقَالَ الَّذِي اشْتَرَاهُ مِنْ مِصْرَ لِامْرَأَتِهِ
And he (the man) from Egypt (مصر) who bought him, said to his wife:
أَكْرِمِي مَثْوَاهُ عَسَى أَنْ يَنْفَعَنَا أَوْ نَتَّخِذَهُ وَلَدًا ۚ
Make his stay comfortable, may be he will profit us or we shall adopt him as a son.‘
وَكَذَلِكَ مَكَّنَّا لِيُوسُفَ فِي الْأَرْضِ وَلِنُعَلِّمَهُ مِنْ تَأْوِيلِ الْأَحَادِيثِ ۚ
Thus did We establish Yoosuf (Joseph) in the land, that We might teach him the interpretation of events.
وَاللَّهُ غَالِبٌ عَلَى أَمْرِهِ وَلَكِنَّ أَكْثَرَ النَّاسِ لَا يَعْلَمُونَ
And Allah (الله) has full power and control over His Affairs, but most of men know not.
12:22
وَلَمَّا بَلَغَ أَشُدَّهُ آتَيْنَاهُ حُكْمًا وَعِلْمًا ۚ
And when he (Yoosuf (Joseph)) attained his full manhood, We gave him wisdom and knowledge (the Prophethood),
وَكَذَلِكَ نَجْزِي الْمُحْسِنِينَ
thus We reward the Muhsinoon (doers of good - see V.2:112).
12:23
وَرَاوَدَتْهُ الَّتِي هُوَ فِي بَيْتِهَا عَنْ نَفْسِهِ وَغَلَّقَتِ الْأَبْوَابَ وَقَالَتْ هَيْتَ لَكَ ۚ
And she, in whose house he was, sought to seduce him (to do an evil act), she closed the doors and said: ‘Come on, O you.‘
قَالَ مَعَاذَ اللَّهِ ۖ
He said: ‘I seek refuge in Allah (الله) (or Allah (الله) forbid)!
إِنَّهُ رَبِّي أَحْسَنَ مَثْوَايَ ۖ
Truly, he (your husband) is my master! He made my stay agreeable! (So I will never betray him).
إِنَّهُ لَا يُفْلِحُ الظَّالِمُونَ
Verily, the Zalimoon (wrong and evil-doers) will never be successful.‘
12:24
وَلَقَدْ هَمَّتْ بِهِ ۖ وَهَمَّ بِهَا لَوْلَا أَنْ رَأَى بُرْهَانَ رَبِّهِ ۚ
And indeed she did desire him and he would have inclined to her desire, had he not seen the evidence of his Lord.
كَذَلِكَ لِنَصْرِفَ عَنْهُ السُّوءَ وَالْفَحْشَاءَ ۚ
Thus it was, that We might turn away from him evil and illegal sexual intercourse.
إِنَّهُ مِنْ عِبَادِنَا الْمُخْلَصِينَ
Surely, he was one of Our chosen, guided slaves.
12:25
وَاسْتَبَقَا الْبَابَ وَقَدَّتْ قَمِيصَهُ مِنْ دُبُرٍ
So they raced with one another to the door, and she tore his shirt from the back.
وَأَلْفَيَا سَيِّدَهَا لَدَى الْبَابِ ۚ
They both found her lord (i.e. her husband) at the door.
قَالَتْ مَا جَزَاءُ مَنْ أَرَادَ بِأَهْلِكَ سُوءًا إِلَّا أَنْ يُسْجَنَ أَوْ عَذَابٌ أَلِيمٌ
She said: ‘What is the recompense (punishment) for him who intended an evil design against your wife, except that he be put in prison or a painful torment?‘
12:26
قَالَ هِيَ رَاوَدَتْنِي عَنْ نَفْسِي ۚ
He (Yoosuf (Joseph)) said: ‘It was she that sought to seduce me,‘
وَشَهِدَ شَاهِدٌ مِنْ أَهْلِهَا

and a witness of her household bore witness (saying):
إِنْ كَانَ قَمِيصُهُ قُدَّ مِنْ قُبُلٍ فَصَدَقَتْ وَهُوَ مِنَ الْكَاذِبِينَ
If it be that his shirt is torn from the front, then her tale is true and he is a liar!
12:27
وَإِنْ كَانَ قَمِيصُهُ قُدَّ مِنْ دُبُرٍ فَكَذَبَتْ وَهُوَ مِنَ الصَّادِقِينَ
But if it be that his shirt is torn from the back, then she has told a lie and he is speaking the truth!‘
12:28
فَلَمَّا رَأَى قَمِيصَهُ قُدَّ مِنْ دُبُرٍ قَالَ
So when he (her husband) saw his ((Yoosuf‘s (Joseph)) shirt torn at the back; (her husband) said:
إِنَّهُ مِنْ كَيْدِكُنَّ ۖ إِنَّ كَيْدَكُنَّ عَظِيمٌ
Surely, it is a plot of you women! Certainly mighty is your plot!
12:29
يُوسُفُ أَعْرِضْ عَنْ هَذَا ۚ
O Yoosuf (Joseph)! Turn away from this!
وَاسْتَغْفِرِي لِذَنْبِكِ ۖ إِنَّكِ كُنْتِ مِنَ الْخَاطِئِينَ
(O woman!) Ask forgiveness for your sin. Verily, you were of the sinful.‘
12:[30-35] The women of the town started pointing fingers at the wife of Al-Aziz. As a result, she invited them to a banquet and asked Yüsuf to appear before them
12:30
وَقَالَ نِسْوَةٌ فِي الْمَدِينَةِ امْرَأَتُ الْعَزِيزِ تُرَاوِدُ فَتَاهَا عَنْ نَفْسِهِ ۖ
And women in the city said: ‘The wife of Al-Azeez (ٱلْعَزِيزُ) is seeking to seduce her (slave) young man,
قَدْ شَغَفَهَا حُبًّا ۖ إِنَّا لَنَرَاهَا فِي ضَلَالٍ مُبِينٍ
indeed she loves him violently; verily we see her in plain error.‘
12:31
فَلَمَّا سَمِعَتْ بِمَكْرِهِنَّ أَرْسَلَتْ إِلَيْهِنَّ وَأَعْتَدَتْ لَهُنَّ مُتَّكَأً
So when she heard of their accusation, she sent for them and prepared a banquet for them;
وَآتَتْ كُلَّ وَاحِدَةٍ مِنْهُنَّ سِكِّينًا
she gave each one of them a knife (to cut the foodstuff with),
وَقَالَتِ اخْرُجْ عَلَيْهِنَّ ۖ
and she said ((to Yoosuf (Joseph)): ‘Come out before them.‘
فَلَمَّا رَأَيْنَهُ أَكْبَرْنَهُ وَقَطَّعْنَ أَيْدِيَهُنَّ
Then, when they saw him, they exalted him (at his beauty) and (in their astonishment) cut their hands.
وَقُلْنَ حَاشَ لِلَّهِ مَا هَذَا بَشَرًا إِنْ هَذَا إِلَّا مَلَكٌ كَرِيمٌ
They said: ‘How perfect is Allah (الله) (or Allah (الله) forbid)! No man is this! This is none other than a noble angel!‘
12:32
قَالَتْ فَذَلِكُنَّ الَّذِي لُمْتُنَّنِي فِيهِ ۖ
She said: ‘This is he (the young man) about whom you did blame me (for his love),
وَلَقَدْ رَاوَدْتُهُ عَنْ نَفْسِهِ فَاسْتَعْصَمَ ۖ
and I did seek to seduce him, but he refused.
وَلَئِنْ لَمْ يَفْعَلْ مَا آمُرُهُ لَيُسْجَنَنَّ وَلَيَكُونًا مِنَ الصَّاغِرِينَ
And now if he refuses to obey my order, he shall certainly be cast into prison, and will be one of those who are disgraced.‘
12:33
قَالَ رَبِّ السِّجْنُ أَحَبُّ إِلَيَّ مِمَّا يَدْعُونَنِي إِلَيْهِ ۖ
He said: ‘O my Lord! Prison is more to my liking than that to which they invite me.
وَإِلَّا تَصْرِفْ عَنِّي كَيْدَهُنَّ أَصْبُ إِلَيْهِنَّ وَأَكُنْ مِنَ الْجَاهِلِينَ
Unless You turn away their plot from me, I will feel inclined towards them and be one (of those who commit sin and deserve blame or those who do deeds) of the ignorants.‘
12:34
فَاسْتَجَابَ لَهُ رَبُّهُ فَصَرَفَ عَنْهُ كَيْدَهُنَّ ۚ
So his Lord answered his invocation and turned away from him their plot.
إِنَّهُ هُوَ السَّمِيعُ الْعَلِيمُ
Verily, He is the All- Hearer, the All-Knower (Al-‘Alee (ٱلْعَلِيُّ)m (ٱلْعَلِيمُ)).
12:35
ثُمَّ بَدَا لَهُمْ مِنْ بَعْدِ مَا رَأَوُا الْآيَاتِ لَيَسْجُنُنَّهُ حَتَّى حِينٍ
Then it appeared to them, after they had seen the proofs (of his innocence) to imprison him for a time.
12:[36-42] Two prison inmates had dreams and asked Yüsuf for interpretation. Yüsuf‘s address to his fellow inmates
12:36
وَدَخَلَ مَعَهُ السِّجْنَ فَتَيَانِ ۖ
And there entered with him two young men in the prison.
قَالَ أَحَدُهُمَا إِنِّي أَرَانِي أَعْصِرُ خَمْرًا ۖ
One of them said: ‘Verily, I saw myself (in a dream) pressing wine.‘
وَقَالَ الْآخَرُ إِنِّي أَرَانِي أَحْمِلُ فَوْقَ رَأْسِي خُبْزًا تَأْكُلُ الطَّيْرُ مِنْهُ ۖ
The other said: ‘Verily, I saw myself (in a dream) carrying bread on my head and birds were eating thereof.‘
نَبِّئْنَا بِتَأْوِيلِهِ ۖ إِنَّا نَرَاكَ مِنَ الْمُحْسِنِينَ
(They said): ‘Inform us of the interpretation of this. Verily, we think you are one of the Muhsinoon (doers of good - see V.2:112).‘
12:37
قَالَ لَا يَأْتِيكُمَا طَعَامٌ تُرْزَقَانِهِ إِلَّا نَبَّأْتُكُمَا بِتَأْوِيلِهِ قَبْلَ أَنْ يَأْتِيَكُمَا ۚ
He said: ‘No food will come to you (in wakefulness or in dream) as your provision, but I will inform (in wakefulness) its interpretation before it (the food) comes.
ذَلِكُمَا مِمَّا عَلَّمَنِي رَبِّي ۚ
This is of that which my Lord has taught me.
إِنِّي تَرَكْتُ مِلَّةَ قَوْمٍ لَا يُؤْمِنُونَ بِاللَّهِ وَهُمْ بِالْآخِرَةِ هُمْ كَافِرُونَ
Verily, I have abandoned the religion of a people that believe not in Allah (الله) and are disbelievers in the Hereafter.(i.e. the Kananioon of Egypt (مصر) who were polytheists and used to worship sun and other false deities)
12:38
وَاتَّبَعْتُ مِلَّةَ آبَائِي إِبْرَاهِيمَ وَإِسْحَاقَ وَيَعْقُوبَ ۚ
And I have followed the religion of my fathers, - Ibraheem (Abraham), Ishaque (Ishak (إسحاق)) and Yaqoob (Yaqqub (يعقوب)),
مَا كَانَ لَنَا أَنْ نُشْرِكَ بِاللَّهِ مِنْ شَيْءٍ ۚ
and never could we attribute any partners whatsoever to Allah (الله).
ذَلِكَ مِنْ فَضْلِ اللَّهِ عَلَيْنَا وَعَلَى النَّاسِ وَلَكِنَّ أَكْثَرَ النَّاسِ لَا يَشْكُرُونَ
This is from the Grace of Allah (الله) to us and to mankind, but most men thank not (i.e. they neither believe in Allah (الله), nor worship Him).
12:39
يَا صَاحِبَيِ السِّجْنِ أَأَرْبَابٌ مُتَفَرِّقُونَ خَيْرٌ أَمِ اللَّهُ الْوَاحِدُ الْقَهَّارُ
O two companions of the prison! Are many different lords (gods) better or Allah (الله), the One, the Irresistible?
12:40
‏مَا تَعْبُدُونَ مِنْ دُونِهِ إِلَّا أَسْمَاءً سَمَّيْتُمُوهَا أَنْتُمْ وَآبَاؤُكُمْ مَا أَنْزَلَ اللَّهُ بِهَا مِنْ سُلْطَانٍ ۚ
You do not worship besides Him but only names which you have named (forged), you and your fathers, for which Allah (الله) has sent down no authority.
إِنِ الْحُكْمُ إِلَّا لِلَّهِ ۚ
The command (or the judgement) is for none but Allah (الله).
أَمَرَ أَلَّا تَعْبُدُوا إِلَّا إِيَّاهُ ۚ
He has commanded that you worship none but Him (i.e. His Monotheism),
ذَلِكَ الدِّينُ الْقَيِّمُ وَلَكِنَّ أَكْثَرَ النَّاسِ لَا يَعْلَمُونَ
that is the (true) straight religion, but most men know not.
12:41
يَا صَاحِبَيِ السِّجْنِ أَمَّا أَحَدُكُمَا فَيَسْقِي رَبَّهُ خَمْرًا ۖ
O two companions of the prison! As for one of you, he (as a servant) will pour out wine for his lord (king or master) to drink;
وَأَمَّا الْآخَرُ فَيُصْلَبُ فَتَأْكُلُ الطَّيْرُ مِنْ رَأْسِهِ
and as for the other, he will be crucified and birds will eat from his head.
قُضِيَ الْأَمْرُ الَّذِي فِيهِ تَسْتَفْتِيَانِ
Thus is the case judged concerning which you both did inquire.‘
12:42
وَقَالَ لِلَّذِي ظَنَّ أَنَّهُ نَاجٍ مِنْهُمَا اذْكُرْنِي عِنْدَ رَبِّكَ
And he said to the one whom he knew to be saved: ‘Mention me to your lord (i.e. your king, so as to get me out of the prison).‘
فَأَنْسَاهُ الشَّيْطَانُ ذِكْرَ رَبِّهِ فَلَبِثَ فِي السِّجْنِ بِضْعَ سِنِينَ
But Shaitan (Satan (الشيطان)) made him forget to mention it to his Lord (or Satan (الشيطان) made ((Yoosuf (Joseph)) to forget the remembrance of his Lord (Allah (الله)) as to ask for His Help, instead of others). So (Yoosuf (Joseph)) stayed in prison a few (more) years.
12:[43-49] The King of Egypt had a dream and asked for its interpretation. Yüsuf‘s interpretation of the King‘s dream
12:43
وَقَالَ الْمَلِكُ إِنِّي أَرَى سَبْعَ بَقَرَاتٍ سِمَانٍ يَأْكُلُهُنَّ سَبْعٌ عِجَافٌ
And the king (of Egypt (مصر)) said: ‘Verily, I saw (in a dream) seven fat cows, whom seven lean ones were devouring -
وَسَبْعَ سُنْبُلَاتٍ خُضْرٍ وَأُخَرَ يَابِسَاتٍ ۖ
and of seven green ears of corn, and (seven) others dry.
يَا أَيُّهَا الْمَلَأُ أَفْتُونِي فِي رُؤْيَايَ إِنْ كُنْتُمْ لِلرُّؤْيَا تَعْبُرُونَ
O notables! Explain to me my dream, if it be that you can interpret dreams.‘
12:44
قَالُوا أَضْغَاثُ أَحْلَامٍ ۖ وَمَا نَحْنُ بِتَأْوِيلِ الْأَحْلَامِ بِعَالِمِينَ
They said: ‘Mixed up false dreams and we are not skilled in the interpretation of dreams.‘
12:45
وَقَالَ الَّذِي نَجَا مِنْهُمَا وَادَّكَرَ بَعْدَ أُمَّةٍ
Then the man who was released (one of the two who were in prison), now at length remembered and said:
أَنَا أُنَبِّئُكُمْ بِتَأْوِيلِهِ فَأَرْسِلُونِ
I will tell you its interpretation, so send me forth.‘
12:46
يُوسُفُ أَيُّهَا الصِّدِّيقُ
(He said): ‘O Yoosuf (Joseph), the man of truth!
أَفْتِنَا فِي سَبْعِ بَقَرَاتٍ سِمَانٍ يَأْكُلُهُنَّ سَبْعٌ عِجَافٌ
Explain to us (the dream) of seven fat cows whom seven lean ones were devouring,
وَسَبْعِ سُنْبُلَاتٍ خُضْرٍ وَأُخَرَ يَابِسَاتٍ
and of seven green ears of corn, and (seven) others dry,
لَعَلِّي أَرْجِعُ إِلَى النَّاسِ لَعَلَّهُمْ يَعْلَمُونَ
that I may return to the people, and that they may know.‘
12:47
قَالَ تَزْرَعُونَ سَبْعَ سِنِينَ دَأَبًا فَمَا حَصَدْتُمْ فَذَرُوهُ فِي سُنْبُلِهِ إِلَّا قَلِيلًا مِمَّا تَأْكُلُونَ
((Yoosuf (Joseph)) said: ‘For seven consecutive years, you shall sow as usual and that (the harvest) which you reap you shall leave in ears, (all) - except a little of it which you may eat.
12:48
ثُمَّ يَأْتِي مِنْ بَعْدِ ذَلِكَ سَبْعٌ شِدَادٌ يَأْكُلْنَ مَا قَدَّمْتُمْ لَهُنَّ إِلَّا قَلِيلًا مِمَّا تُحْصِنُونَ
Then will come after that, seven hard (years), which will devour what you have laid by in advance for them, (all) except a little of that which you have guarded (stored).
12:49
ثُمَّ يَأْتِي مِنْ بَعْدِ ذَلِكَ عَامٌ فِيهِ يُغَاثُ النَّاسُ وَفِيهِ يَعْصِرُونَ
Then thereafter will come a year in which people will have abundant rain and in which they will press (wine and oil).‘
12:[50-52] The King of Egypt heard the case of the women and found that Yüsuf was innocent
12:50
وَقَالَ الْمَلِكُ ائْتُونِي بِهِ ۖ
And the king said: ‘Bring him to me.‘
فَلَمَّا جَاءَهُ الرَّسُولُ قَالَ ارْجِعْ إِلَى رَبِّكَ فَاسْأَلْهُ مَا بَالُ النِّسْوَةِ اللَّاتِي قَطَّعْنَ أَيْدِيَهُنَّ ۚ
But when the messenger came to him, (Yoosuf (Joseph)) said: ‘Return to your lord and ask him, What happened to the women who cut their hands?
إِنَّ رَبِّي بِكَيْدِهِنَّ عَلِيمٌ
Surely, my Lord (Allah (الله)) is Well-Aware of their plot.‘
12:51
قَالَ مَا خَطْبُكُنَّ إِذْ رَاوَدْتُنَّ يُوسُفَ عَنْ نَفْسِهِ ۚ
(The King (ملك)) said (to the women): ‘What was your affair when you did seek to seduce Yoosuf (Joseph)?‘
قُلْنَ حَاشَ لِلَّهِ مَا عَلِمْنَا عَلَيْهِ مِنْ سُوءٍ ۚ
The women said: ‘Allah (الله) forbid! No evil know we against him!‘
قَالَتِ امْرَأَتُ الْعَزِيزِ الْآنَ حَصْحَصَ الْحَقُّ أَنَا رَاوَدْتُهُ عَنْ نَفْسِهِ وَإِنَّهُ لَمِنَ الصَّادِقِينَ
The wife of Al-Azeez (ٱلْعَزِيزُ) said: ‘Now the truth is manifest (to all), it was I who sought to seduce him, and he is surely of the truthful.‘
12:52
ذَلِكَ لِيَعْلَمَ أَنِّي لَمْ أَخُنْهُ بِالْغَيْبِ
(Then Yoosuf (Joseph) said: ‘I asked for this enquiry) in order that he (Al-Azeez (ٱلْعَزِيزُ)) may know that I betrayed him not in secret.
وَأَنَّ اللَّهَ لَا يَهْدِي كَيْدَ الْخَائِنِينَ
And, verily! Allah (الله) guides not the plot of the betrayers.
12:[53-57] Yüsuf‘s appointment as a King‘s cabinet member
12:53
وَمَا أُبَرِّئُ نَفْسِي ۚ
And I free not myself (from the blame).
إِنَّ النَّفْسَ لَأَمَّارَةٌ بِالسُّوءِ إِلَّا مَا رَحِمَ رَبِّي ۚ
Verily, the (human) self is inclined to evil, except when my Lord bestows His Mercy (upon whom He wills).
إِنَّ رَبِّي غَفُورٌ رَحِيمٌ
Verily, my Lord is Oft-Forgiving, Most Merciful (Ar-Raheem (ٱلْرَّحِيْمُ)).‘
12:54
وَقَالَ الْمَلِكُ ائْتُونِي بِهِ أَسْتَخْلِصْهُ لِنَفْسِي ۖ
And the king said: ‘Bring him to me that I may attach him to my person.‘
فَلَمَّا كَلَّمَهُ قَالَ إِنَّكَ الْيَوْمَ لَدَيْنَا مَكِينٌ أَمِينٌ
Then, when he spoke to him, he said: ‘Verily, this day, you are with us high in rank and fully trusted.‘
12:55
قَالَ اجْعَلْنِي عَلَى خَزَائِنِ الْأَرْضِ ۖ
(Yoosuf (Joseph)) said: ‘Set me over the storehouses of the land;
إِنِّي حَفِيظٌ عَلِيمٌ
I will indeed guard them with full knowledge‘(as a minister of finance in Egypt (مصر), in place of Al-Aziz who was dead at that time).
12:56
وَكَذَلِكَ مَكَّنَّا لِيُوسُفَ فِي الْأَرْضِ يَتَبَوَّأُ مِنْهَا حَيْثُ يَشَاءُ ۚ
Thus did We give full authority to Yoosuf (Joseph) in the land, to take possession therein, as when or where he likes.
نُصِيبُ بِرَحْمَتِنَا مَنْ نَشَاءُ ۖ وَلَا نُضِيعُ أَجْرَ الْمُحْسِنِينَ
We bestow of Our Mercy on whom We please, and We make not to be lost the reward of Al-Muhsinoon (the good doers - see V.2:112).
12:57
وَلَأَجْرُ الْآخِرَةِ خَيْرٌ لِلَّذِينَ آمَنُوا وَكَانُوا يَتَّقُونَ
And verily, the reward of the Hereafter is better for those who believe and used to fear Allah (الله) and keep their duty to Him (by abstaining from all kinds of sins and evil deeds and by performing all kinds of righteous good deeds).
12:[58-62] Yüsuf‘s brothers came to Egypt to get food and grain. Yüsuf asked them to bring Benjamin (Yüsuf‘s younger brother)
12:[63-68] They asked their father to send Benjamin with them in order to get more grain. Ya‘qoob‘s advice to his sons
12:58
وَجَاءَ إِخْوَةُ يُوسُفَ فَدَخَلُوا عَلَيْهِ فَعَرَفَهُمْ وَهُمْ لَهُ مُنْكِرُونَ
And Yoosufs (Joseph) brethren came and they entered unto him, and he recognized them, but they recognized him not.
12:59
وَلَمَّا جَهَّزَهُمْ بِجَهَازِهِمْ قَالَ ائْتُونِي بِأَخٍ لَكُمْ مِنْ أَبِيكُمْ ۚ
And when he had furnished them forth with provisions (according to their need), he said: ‘Bring me a brother of yours from your father; (he meant Benjamin).
أَلَا تَرَوْنَ أَنِّي أُوفِي الْكَيْلَ وَأَنَا خَيْرُ الْمُنْزِلِينَ
See you not that I give full measure, and that I am the best of the hosts?
12:60
فَإِنْ لَمْ تَأْتُونِي بِهِ فَلَا كَيْلَ لَكُمْ عِنْدِي وَلَا تَقْرَبُونِ
But if you bring him not to me, there shall be no measure (of corn) for you with me, nor shall you come near me.‘
12:61
قَالُوا سَنُرَاوِدُ عَنْهُ أَبَاهُ وَإِنَّا لَفَاعِلُونَ
They said: ‘We shall try to get permission (for him) from his father, and verily, we shall do it.‘
12:62
وَقَالَ لِفِتْيَانِهِ اجْعَلُوا بِضَاعَتَهُمْ فِي رِحَالِهِمْ
And (Yoosuf (Joseph)) told his servants to put their money (with which they had bought the corn) into their bags,
لَعَلَّهُمْ يَعْرِفُونَهَا إِذَا انْقَلَبُوا إِلَى أَهْلِهِمْ لَعَلَّهُمْ يَرْجِعُونَ
so that they might know it when they go back to their people, in order that they might come back.
12:63
فَلَمَّا رَجَعُوا إِلَى أَبِيهِمْ قَالُوا يَا أَبَانَا مُنِعَ مِنَّا الْكَيْلُ
So, when they returned to their father, they said:
O our father! No more measure of grain shall we get (unless we take our brother).
فَأَرْسِلْ مَعَنَا أَخَانَا نَكْتَلْ وَإِنَّا لَهُ لَحَافِظُونَ
So send our brother with us, and we shall get our measure and truly we will guard him.‘
12:64
قَالَ هَلْ آمَنُكُمْ عَلَيْهِ إِلَّا كَمَا أَمِنْتُكُمْ عَلَى أَخِيهِ مِنْ قَبْلُ ۖ
He said: ‘Can I entrust him to you except as I entrusted his brother (Yoosuf (Joseph)) to you aforetime?
فَاللَّهُ خَيْرٌ حَافِظًا ۖ وَهُوَ أَرْحَمُ الرَّاحِمِينَ
But Allah (الله) is the Best to guard, and He is the Most Merciful (Ar-Raheem (ٱلْرَّحِيْمُ)) of those who show mercy.‘
12:65
وَلَمَّا فَتَحُوا مَتَاعَهُمْ وَجَدُوا بِضَاعَتَهُمْ رُدَّتْ إِلَيْهِمْ ۖ
And when they opened their bags, they found their money had been returned to them.
قَالُوا يَا أَبَانَا مَا نَبْغِي ۖ
They said: ‘O our father! What (more) can we desire?
هَذِهِ بِضَاعَتُنَا رُدَّتْ إِلَيْنَا ۖ وَنَمِيرُ أَهْلَنَا وَنَحْفَظُ أَخَانَا وَنَزْدَادُ كَيْلَ بَعِيرٍ ۖ
This, our money has been returned to us, so we shall get (more) food for our family, and we shall guard our brother and add one more measure of a camels load.
ذَلِكَ كَيْلٌ يَسِيرٌ
This quantity is easy (for the king to give).‘
12:66
قَالَ لَنْ أُرْسِلَهُ مَعَكُمْ حَتَّى تُؤْتُونِ مَوْثِقًا مِنَ اللَّهِ لَتَأْتُنَّنِي بِهِ إِلَّا أَنْ يُحَاطَ بِكُمْ ۖ
He (Yaqoob (Jacob)) said: ‘I will not send him with you until you swear a solemn oath to me in Allah (الله)‘s Name, that you will bring him back to me unless you are yourselves surrounded (by enemies, etc.),‘
فَلَمَّا آتَوْهُ مَوْثِقَهُمْ قَالَ اللَّهُ عَلَى مَا نَقُولُ وَكِيلٌ
And when they had sworn their solemn oath, he said: ‘Allah (الله) is the Witness over what we have said.‘
12:67
‏وَقَالَ يَا بَنِيَّ لَا تَدْخُلُوا مِنْ بَابٍ وَاحِدٍ وَادْخُلُوا مِنْ أَبْوَابٍ مُتَفَرِّقَةٍ ۖ
And he said: ‘O my sons! Do not enter by one gate, but enter by different gates,
وَمَا أُغْنِي عَنْكُمْ مِنَ اللَّهِ مِنْ شَيْءٍ ۖ
and I cannot avail you against Allah (الله) at all.
إِنِ الْحُكْمُ إِلَّا لِلَّهِ ۖ
Verily! The decision rests only with Allah (الله).
عَلَيْهِ تَوَكَّلْتُ ۖ وَعَلَيْهِ فَلْيَتَوَكَّلِ الْمُتَوَكِّلُونَ
In him, I put my trust and let all those that trust, put their trust in Him.‘
12:68
وَلَمَّا دَخَلُوا مِنْ حَيْثُ أَمَرَهُمْ أَبُوهُمْ مَا كَانَ يُغْنِي عَنْهُمْ مِنَ اللَّهِ مِنْ شَيْءٍ
And when they entered according to their fathers advice, it did not avail them in the least against (the Will of) Allah (الله),
إِلَّا حَاجَةً فِي نَفْسِ يَعْقُوبَ قَضَاهَا
it was but a need of Yaqoob‘s (Jacob) inner-self which he discharged.
وَإِنَّهُ لَذُو عِلْمٍ لِمَا عَلَّمْنَاهُ وَلَكِنَّ أَكْثَرَ النَّاسِ لَا يَعْلَمُونَ
And verily, he was endowed with knowledge because We had taught him, but most men know not.
12:[69-79] Yüsuf introduced himself to his brother Benjamin and schemed to retain him. Benjamin was accused of stealing so that he could be retained
12:69
وَلَمَّا دَخَلُوا عَلَى يُوسُفَ آوَى إِلَيْهِ أَخَاهُ ۖ
And when they went in before Yoosuf (Joseph), he betook his brother (Benjamin) to himself

Do they then feel secure from the coming against them of the covering veil of the Torment of Allah (الله),
أَوْ تَأْتِيَهُمُ السَّاعَةُ بَغْتَةً وَهُمْ لَا يَشْعُرُونَ
or of the coming against them of the (Final) Hour, all of a sudden while they perceive not?
12:108
قُلْ هَذِهِ سَبِيلِي أَدْعُو إِلَى اللَّهِ ۚ
Say (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW): ‘This is my way; I invite unto Allah (الله) (i.e. to the Oneness of Allah (الله) - Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism)
عَلَى بَصِيرَةٍ أَنَا وَمَنِ اتَّبَعَنِي ۖ
with sure knowledge, I and whosoever follows me (also must invite others to Allah (الله) i.e to the Oneness of Allah (الله) - Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism) with sure knowledge.
وَسُبْحَانَ اللَّهِ وَمَا أَنَا مِنَ الْمُشْرِكِينَ
And Glorified and Exalted be Allah (الله) (above all that they associate as partners with Him). And I am not of the Mushrikoon (polytheists, pagans, idolaters and disbelievers in the Oneness of Allah (الله); those who worship others along with Allah (الله) or set up rivals or partners to Allah (الله)).‘
12:109
وَمَا أَرْسَلْنَا مِنْ قَبْلِكَ إِلَّا رِجَالًا نُوحِي إِلَيْهِمْ مِنْ أَهْلِ الْقُرَى ۗ
And We sent not before you (as Messengers) any but men, whom We inspired from among the people of townships.
أَفَلَمْ يَسِيرُوا فِي الْأَرْضِ فَيَنْظُرُوا كَيْفَ كَانَ عَاقِبَةُ الَّذِينَ مِنْ قَبْلِهِمْ ۗ
Have they not travelled through the earth and seen what was the end of those who were before them?
وَلَدَارُ الْآخِرَةِ خَيْرٌ لِلَّذِينَ اتَّقَوْا ۗ أَفَلَا تَعْقِلُونَ
And verily, the home of the Hereafter is the best for those who fear Allah (الله) and obey Him (by abstaining from sins and evil deeds, and by performing righteous good deeds). Do you not then understand?
12:110
حَتَّى إِذَا اسْتَيْأَسَ الرُّسُلُ وَظَنُّوا أَنَّهُمْ قَدْ كُذِبُوا جَاءَهُمْ نَصْرُنَا
(They were reprieved) until, when the Messengers gave up hope and thought that they were denied (by their people), then came to them Our Help,
فَنُجِّيَ مَنْ نَشَاءُ ۖ
and whomsoever We willed were delivered.
وَلَا يُرَدُّ بَأْسُنَا عَنِ الْقَوْمِ الْمُجْرِمِينَ
And Our Punishment cannot be warded off from the people who are Mujrimoon (criminals, disobedients to Allah (الله), sinners, disbelievers, polytheists).
12:111
لَقَدْ كَانَ فِي قَصَصِهِمْ عِبْرَةٌ لِأُولِي الْأَلْبَابِ ۗ
Indeed in their stories, there is a lesson for men of understanding.
مَا كَانَ حَدِيثًا يُفْتَرَى وَلَكِنْ تَصْدِيقَ الَّذِي بَيْنَ يَدَيْهِ وَتَفْصِيلَ كُلِّ شَيْءٍ
It (the Quran (القرآن)) is not a forged statement but a confirmation of the Allah (الله)‘s existing Books (the Taurat (Torah (التوراة)), the Injil (Injeel (الإنجيل)) and other Scriptures of Allah (الله)) and a detailed explanation of everything
وَهُدًى وَرَحْمَةً لِقَوْمٍ يُؤْمِنُونَ
and a guide and a Mercy for the people who believe.*********
