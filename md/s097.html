Al-Qadr
The Night of Honor, The Night of Decree, Power, Fate, Destiny
97:[1-5] The night of Qadr is better than one thousand months
97:1
إِنَّآ أَنزَلۡنَـٰهُ فِى لَيۡلَةِ ٱلۡقَدۡرِ
Verily! We have sent it (this Quran (القرآن)) down in the night of Al-Qadr (Decree).
97:2
وَمَآ أَدۡرَٮٰكَ مَا لَيۡلَةُ ٱلۡقَدۡرِ
And what will make you know what the night of Al-Qadr (Decree) is?
97:3
لَيۡلَةُ ٱلۡقَدۡرِ خَيۡرٌ۬ مِّنۡ أَلۡفِ شَہۡرٍ۬
The night of Al-Qadr (Decree) is better than a thousand months(i.e. worshipping Allâh in that night is better than worshipping Him a thousand months, i.e. 83 years and 4 months).
97:4
تَنَزَّلُ ٱلۡمَلَـٰٓٮِٕكَةُ وَٱلرُّوحُ فِيہَا بِإِذۡنِ رَبِّہِم مِّن كُلِّ أَمۡرٍ۬
Therein descend the angels and the Rûh [Jibrael (Jibreel (جبريل))] by Allâh‘s Permission with all Decrees,
97:5
سَلَـٰمٌ هِىَ حَتَّىٰ مَطۡلَعِ ٱلۡفَجۡرِ
Peace! (All that night, there is Peace and Goodness from Allâh to His believing slaves) until the appearance of dawn.*********
