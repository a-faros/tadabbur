Al-Mursalaat
Those Sent Forth, The Emissaries, Winds Sent Forth
77:[1-28] Allah swears in the name of life-giving winds, rain and angels that the Day of Judgment will be established. Woe on that Day to the disbelievers!
77:[29-40] On that Day; the disbelievers will be asked to walk towards hell which they used to deny
77:1
وَٱلۡمُرۡسَلَـٰتِ عُرۡفً۬ا
By the winds (or angels or the Messengers of Allah (الله)) sent forth one after another.
77:2
فَٱلۡعَـٰصِفَـٰتِ عَصۡفً۬ا
And by the winds that blow violently,
77:3
وَٱلنَّـٰشِرَٲتِ نَشۡرً۬ا
And by the winds that scatter clouds and rain;
77:4
فَٱلۡفَـٰرِقَـٰتِ فَرۡقً۬ا
And by the Verses (of the Quran (القرآن)) that separate the right from the wrong.
77:5
فَٱلۡمُلۡقِيَـٰتِ ذِكۡرًا
And by the angels that bring the revelations to the Messengers,
77:6
عُذۡرًا أَوۡ نُذۡرًا
To cut off all excuses or to warn;
77:7
إِنَّمَا تُوعَدُونَ لَوَٲقِعٌ۬
Surely, what you are promised must come to pass.
77:8
فَإِذَا ٱلنُّجُومُ طُمِسَتۡ
Then when the stars lose their lights;
77:9
وَإِذَا ٱلسَّمَآءُ فُرِجَتۡ
And when the heaven is cleft asunder;
77:10
وَإِذَا ٱلۡجِبَالُ نُسِفَتۡ
And when the mountains are blown away;
77:11
وَإِذَا ٱلرُّسُلُ أُقِّتَتۡ
And when the Messengers are gathered to their time appointed;
77:12
لِأَىِّ يَوۡمٍ أُجِّلَتۡ
For what Day are these signs postponed?
77:13
لِيَوۡمِ ٱلۡفَصۡلِ
For the Day of sorting out (the men of Paradise (الجنة) from the men destined for Hell (جهنم)).
77:14
وَمَآ أَدۡرَٮٰكَ مَا يَوۡمُ ٱلۡفَصۡلِ
And what will explain to you what is the Day of sorting out?
77:15
وَيۡلٌ۬ يَوۡمَٮِٕذٍ۬ لِّلۡمُكَذِّبِينَ
Woe that Day to the deniers (of the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة))!
77:16
أَلَمۡ نُہۡلِكِ ٱلۡأَوَّلِينَ
Did We not destroy the ancients?
77:17
ثُمَّ نُتۡبِعُهُمُ ٱلۡأَخِرِينَ
So shall We make later generations to follow them.
77:18
كَذَٲلِكَ نَفۡعَلُ بِٱلۡمُجۡرِمِينَ
Thus do We deal with the Mujrimoon (polytheists, disbelievers, sinners, criminals, etc.)!
77:19
وَيۡلٌ۬ يَوۡمَٮِٕذٍ۬ لِّلۡمُكَذِّبِينَ
Woe that Day to the deniers (of the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة))!
77:20
أَلَمۡ نَخۡلُقكُّم مِّن مَّآءٍ۬ مَّهِينٍ۬
Did We not create you from a worthless water (semen, etc.)?
77:21
فَجَعَلۡنَـٰهُ فِى قَرَارٍ۬ مَّكِينٍ
Then We placed it in a place of safety (womb),
77:22
إِلَىٰ قَدَرٍ۬ مَّعۡلُومٍ۬
For a known period (determined by gestation)?
77:23
فَقَدَرۡنَا فَنِعۡمَ ٱلۡقَـٰدِرُونَ
So We did measure, and We are the Best to measure (the things).
77:24
وَيۡلٌ۬ يَوۡمَٮِٕذٍ۬ لِّلۡمُكَذِّبِينَ
Woe that Day to the deniers (of the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة))!
77:25
أَلَمۡ نَجۡعَلِ ٱلۡأَرۡضَ كِفَاتًا
Have We not made the earth a receptacle?
77:26
أَحۡيَآءً۬ وَأَمۡوَٲتً۬ا
For the living and the dead.
77:27
وَجَعَلۡنَا فِيہَا رَوَٲسِىَ شَـٰمِخَـٰتٍ۬
And have placed therein firm, and tall mountains;
وَأَسۡقَيۡنَـٰكُم مَّآءً۬ فُرَاتً۬ا
and have given you to drink sweet water?
77:28
وَيۡلٌ۬ يَوۡمَٮِٕذٍ۬ لِّلۡمُكَذِّبِينَ
Woe that Day to the deniers (of the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة))!
77:29
ٱنطَلِقُوٓاْ إِلَىٰ مَا كُنتُم بِهِۦ تُكَذِّبُونَ
(It will be said to the disbelievers):
Depart you to that which you used to deny!
77:30
ٱنطَلِقُوٓاْ إِلَىٰ ظِلٍّ۬ ذِى ثَلَـٰثِ شُعَبٍ۬
‘Depart you to a shadow (of Hell (جهنم)-fire smoke ascending) in three columns,
77:31
لَّا ظَلِيلٍ۬ وَلَا يُغۡنِى مِنَ ٱللَّهَبِ
Neither shading, nor of any use against the fierce flame of the Fire.‘
77:32
إِنَّہَا تَرۡمِى بِشَرَرٍ۬ كَٱلۡقَصۡرِ
Verily! It (Hell (جهنم)) throws sparks (huge) as Al-Qasr (a fort or a Qasr (huge log of wood)),
77:33
كَأَنَّهُ ۥ جِمَـٰلَتٌ۬ صُفۡرٌ۬
As if they were Jimalatun Sufr (yellow camels or bundles of ropes).‘
77:34
وَيۡلٌ۬ يَوۡمَٮِٕذٍ۬ لِّلۡمُكَذِّبِينَ
Woe that Day to the deniers (of the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة))!
77:35
هَـٰذَا يَوۡمُ لَا يَنطِقُونَ
That will be a Day when they shall not speak (during some part of it),
77:36
وَلَا يُؤۡذَنُ لَهُمۡ فَيَعۡتَذِرُونَ
And they will not be permitted to put forth any excuse.
77:37
وَيۡلٌ۬ يَوۡمَٮِٕذٍ۬ لِّلۡمُكَذِّبِينَ
Woe that Day to the deniers (of the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة))!
77:38
هَـٰذَا يَوۡمُ ٱلۡفَصۡلِ‌ۖ
That will be a Day of Decision!
جَمَعۡنَـٰكُمۡ وَٱلۡأَوَّلِينَ
We have brought you and the men of old together!
77:39
فَإِن كَانَ لَكُمۡ كَيۡدٌ۬ فَكِيدُونِ
So if you have a plot, use it against Me (Allah (الله))!
77:40
وَيۡلٌ۬ يَوۡمَٮِٕذٍ۬ لِّلۡمُكَذِّبِينَ
Woe that Day to the deniers (of the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة))!
77:[41-45] The righteous will be given all that they desire
77:[46-50] A warning to the disbelievers
77:41
إِنَّ ٱلۡمُتَّقِينَ فِى ظِلَـٰلٍ۬ وَعُيُونٍ۬
Verily, the Muttaqoon (pious - see V.2:2) shall be amidst shades and springs.
77:42
وَفَوَٲكِهَ مِمَّا يَشۡتَہُونَ
And fruits, such as they desire.
77:43
كُلُواْ وَٱشۡرَبُواْ هَنِيٓـَٔۢا بِمَا كُنتُمۡ تَعۡمَلُونَ
Eat and drink comfortably for that which you used to do.
77:44
إِنَّا كَذَٲلِكَ نَجۡزِى ٱلۡمُحۡسِنِينَ
Verily, thus We reward the Muhsinoon (good-doers).
77:45
وَيۡلٌ۬ يَوۡمَٮِٕذٍ۬ لِّلۡمُكَذِّبِينَ
Woe that Day to the deniers (of the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة))!
77:46
كُلُواْ وَتَمَتَّعُواْ قَلِيلاً إِنَّكُم مُّجۡرِمُونَ
(O you disbelievers)! Eat and enjoy yourselves (in this worldly life) for a little while. Verily, you are the Mujrimoon (polytheists, disbelievers, sinners, criminals, etc.).
77:47
وَيۡلٌ۬ يَوۡمَٮِٕذٍ۬ لِّلۡمُكَذِّبِينَ
Woe that Day to the deniers (of the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة))!
77:48
وَإِذَا قِيلَ لَهُمُ ٱرۡكَعُواْ لَا يَرۡكَعُونَ
And when it is said to them: ‘Bow down yourself (in prayer)!‘ They bow not down (offer not their prayers) .
77:49
وَيۡلٌ۬ يَوۡمَٮِٕذٍ۬ لِّلۡمُكَذِّبِينَ
Woe that Day to the deniers (of the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة))!
77:50
فَبِأَىِّ حَدِيثِۭ بَعۡدَهُ ۥ يُؤۡمِنُونَ
Then in what statement after this (the Quran (القرآن)) will they believe?*********
