Al-Mutaffifin
The Dealers in Fraud, Defrauding, The Cheats, Those Who Give Short Measure
83:[1-28] Defrauders will be called to account and punished while the righteous will be rewarded with soft couches, choicest wines and special spring water
83:[29-36] Today the disbelievers laugh at the believers, a Day will come when they will be laughed at
83:1
وَيۡلٌ۬ لِّلۡمُطَفِّفِينَ
Woe to Al-Mutaffifin[those who give less in measure and weight (decrease the rights of others)],
83:2
ٱلَّذِينَ إِذَا ٱكۡتَالُواْ عَلَى ٱلنَّاسِ يَسۡتَوۡفُونَ
Those who, when they have to receive by measure from men, demand full measure,
83:3
وَإِذَا كَالُوهُمۡ أَو وَّزَنُوهُمۡ يُخۡسِرُونَ
And when they have to give by measure or weight to men, give less than due.
83:4
أَلَا يَظُنُّ أُوْلَـٰٓٮِٕكَ أَنَّہُم مَّبۡعُوثُونَ
Think they not that they will be resurrected (for reckoning),
83:5
لِيَوۡمٍ عَظِيمٍ۬
On a Great Day,
83:6
يَوۡمَ يَقُومُ ٱلنَّاسُ لِرَبِّ ٱلۡعَـٰلَمِينَ
The Day when (all) mankind will stand before the Lord of the ‘Alamîn (mankind, jinns and all that exists)?
83:7
كَلَّآ إِنَّ كِتَـٰبَ ٱلۡفُجَّارِ لَفِى سِجِّينٍ۬
Nay! Truly, the Record (writing of the deeds) of the Fujjâr (disbelievers, sinners, evil-doers and wicked) is (preserved) in Sijjîn.
83:8
وَمَآ أَدۡرَٮٰكَ مَا سِجِّينٌ۬
And what will make you know what Sijjîn is?
83:9
كِتَـٰبٌ۬ مَّرۡقُومٌ۬
A Register inscribed.
83:10
وَيۡلٌ۬ يَوۡمَٮِٕذٍ۬ لِّلۡمُكَذِّبِينَ
Woe, that Day, to those who deny[(Allâh, His Angel (الملائكة)s, His Books, His Messengers, the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة), and Al-Qadar (Divine Preordainments)].
83:11
ٱلَّذِينَ يُكَذِّبُونَ بِيَوۡمِ ٱلدِّينِ
Those who deny the Day of Recompense.
83:12
وَمَا يُكَذِّبُ بِهِۦۤ إِلَّا كُلُّ مُعۡتَدٍ أَثِيمٍ
And none can deny it except every transgressor beyond bounds,(in disbelief, oppression and disobedience of Allâh, the sinner!)
83:13
إِذَا تُتۡلَىٰ عَلَيۡهِ ءَايَـٰتُنَا قَالَ أَسَـٰطِيرُ ٱلۡأَوَّلِينَ
When Our Verses (of the Qur‘ân) are recited to him he says: ‘Tales of the ancients!‘
83:14
كَلَّا‌ۖ بَلۡۜ رَانَ عَلَىٰ قُلُوبِہِم مَّا كَانُواْ يَكۡسِبُونَ
Nay! But on their hearts is the Rân (covering of sins and evil deeds) which they used to earn.
83:15
كَلَّآ إِنَّہُمۡ عَن رَّبِّہِمۡ يَوۡمَٮِٕذٍ۬ لَّمَحۡجُوبُونَ
Nay! Surely, they (evil-doers) will be veiled from seeing their Lord that Day.
83:16
ثُمَّ إِنَّہُمۡ لَصَالُواْ ٱلۡجَحِيمِ
Then, verily they will indeed enter and taste the burning flame of Hell (جهنم).
83:17
ثُمَّ يُقَالُ هَـٰذَا ٱلَّذِى كُنتُم بِهِۦ تُكَذِّبُونَ
Then, it will be said to them: ‘This is what you used to deny!‘
83:18
كَلَّآ إِنَّ كِتَـٰبَ ٱلۡأَبۡرَارِ لَفِى عِلِّيِّينَ
Nay! Verily, the Record (writing of the deeds) of Al-Abrâr (the pious who fear Allâh and avoid evil), is (preserved) in ‘Illiyyûn.
83:19
وَمَآ أَدۡرَٮٰكَ مَا عِلِّيُّونَ
And what will make you know what ‘Illiyyûn is?
83:20
كِتَـٰبٌ۬ مَّرۡقُومٌ۬
A Register inscribed.
83:21
يَشۡہَدُهُ ٱلۡمُقَرَّبُونَ
To which bear witness those nearest (to Allâh, i.e. the angels).
83:22
إِنَّ ٱلۡأَبۡرَارَ لَفِى نَعِيمٍ
Verily, Al-Abrâr (the pious who fear Allâh and avoid evil) will be in delight (Paradise (الجنة)).
83:23
عَلَى ٱلۡأَرَآٮِٕكِ يَنظُرُونَ
On thrones, looking (at all things).
83:24
تَعۡرِفُ فِى وُجُوهِهِمۡ نَضۡرَةَ ٱلنَّعِيمِ
You will recognise in their faces the brightness of delight.
83:25
يُسۡقَوۡنَ مِن رَّحِيقٍ۬ مَّخۡتُومٍ
They will be given to drink pure sealed wine.
83:26
خِتَـٰمُهُ ۥ مِسۡكٌ۬‌ۚ
The last thereof (that wine) will be the smell of musk,
وَفِى ذَٲلِكَ فَلۡيَتَنَافَسِ ٱلۡمُتَنَـٰفِسُونَ
and for this let (all) those strive who want to strive (i.e. hasten earnestly to the obedience of Allâh).
83:27
وَمِزَاجُهُ ۥ مِن تَسۡنِيمٍ
It (that wine) will be mixed with Tasnîm.
83:28
عَيۡنً۬ا يَشۡرَبُ بِہَا ٱلۡمُقَرَّبُونَ
A spring whereof drink those nearest to Allâh.
83:29
إِنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ أَجۡرَمُواْ كَانُواْ مِنَ ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُواْ يَضۡحَكُونَ
Verily! (During the worldly life) those who committed crimes used to laugh at those who believed.
83:30
وَإِذَا مَرُّواْ بِہِمۡ يَتَغَامَزُونَ
And whenever they passed by them, used to wink one to another (in mockery);
83:31
وَإِذَا ٱنقَلَبُوٓاْ إِلَىٰٓ أَهۡلِهِمُ ٱنقَلَبُواْ فَكِهِينَ
And when they returned to their own people, they would return jesting;
83:32
وَإِذَا رَأَوۡهُمۡ قَالُوٓاْ إِنَّ هَـٰٓؤُلَآءِ لَضَآلُّونَ
And when they saw them, they said: ‘Verily! These have indeed gone astray!‘
83:33
وَمَآ أُرۡسِلُواْ عَلَيۡہِمۡ حَـٰفِظِينَ
But they (disbelievers, sinners) had not been sent as watchers over them (the believers).
83:34
فَٱلۡيَوۡمَ ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُواْ مِنَ ٱلۡكُفَّارِ يَضۡحَكُونَ
But this Day (the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة)) those who believe will laugh at the disbelievers
83:35
عَلَى ٱلۡأَرَآٮِٕكِ يَنظُرُونَ
On (high) thrones, looking (at all things).
83:36
هَلۡ ثُوِّبَ ٱلۡكُفَّارُ مَا كَانُواْ يَفۡعَلُونَ
Are not the disbelievers paid (fully) for what they used to do?*********
