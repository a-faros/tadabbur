Taa-Haa
The letters Taa & Haa
20:[1-8] The Qur‘an is a reminder for those who fear Allah, the Creator of the heavens and the earth
20:[9-16] The Prophet Musa went to the sacred valley of ‘Tuwa‘ at Mount Tür
20:[17-24] Allah chose him as His Rasool and assigned him towards Fir‘on
20:1
طه
TaHa.(These letters are one of the miracles of the Quran (القرآن), and none but Allah (الله) (Alone) knows their meanings.)
20:2
مَا أَنْزَلْنَا عَلَيْكَ الْقُرْآنَ لِتَشْقَى
We have not sent down the Quran (القرآن) unto you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) to cause you distress,
20:3
إِلَّا تَذْكِرَةً لِمَنْ يَخْشَى
But only as a Reminder to those who fear (Allah (الله)).
20:4
تَنْزِيلًا مِمَّنْ خَلَقَ الْأَرْضَ وَالسَّمَاوَاتِ الْعُلَى
A revelation from Him (Allah (الله)) Who has created the earth and high heavens.
20:5
الرَّحْمَنُ عَلَى الْعَرْشِ اسْتَوَى
The Most Beneficent (Ar-Rahmaan (ٱلْرَّحْمَـانُ)) Istawa (rose over) the (Mighty) Throne (in a manner that suits His Majesty).
20:6
لَهُ مَا فِي السَّمَاوَاتِ وَمَا فِي الْأَرْضِ وَمَا بَيْنَهُمَا وَمَا تَحْتَ الثَّرَى
To Him belongs all that is in the heavens and all that is on the earth, and all that is between them, and all that is under the soil.
20:7
وَإِنْ تَجْهَرْ بِالْقَوْلِ فَإِنَّهُ يَعْلَمُ السِّرَّ وَأَخْفَى
And if you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) speak (the invocation) aloud, then verily, He knows the secret and that which is yet more hidden.
20:8
اللَّهُ لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا هُوَ ۖ
llah! La ilahla illa Huwa (none has the right to be worshipped but He)!
لَهُ الْأَسْمَاءُ الْحُسْنَى
To Him belong the Best Names.
20:9
وَهَلْ أَتَاكَ حَدِيثُ مُوسَى
And has there come to you the story of Musa (Moosa (موسى))?
20:10
إِذْ رَأَى نَارًا فَقَالَ لِأَهْلِهِ امْكُثُوا إِنِّي آنَسْتُ نَارًا
When he saw a fire, he said to his family:
Wait! Verily, I have seen a fire,
لَعَلِّي آتِيكُمْ مِنْهَا بِقَبَسٍ أَوْ أَجِدُ عَلَى النَّارِ هُدًى
perhaps I can bring you some burning brand therefrom, or find some guidance at the fire.‘
20:11
‏فَلَمَّا أَتَاهَا نُودِيَ يَا مُوسَى
And when he came to it (the fire), he was called by name: ‘O Musa (Moosa (موسى))!
20:12
إِنِّي أَنَا رَبُّكَ فَاخْلَعْ نَعْلَيْكَ ۖ إِنَّكَ بِالْوَادِ الْمُقَدَّسِ طُوًى
Verily! I am your Lord! So take off your shoes, you are in the sacred valley, Tuwa.
20:13
وَأَنَا اخْتَرْتُكَ فَاسْتَمِعْ لِمَا يُوحَى
And I have chosen you. So listen to that which is inspired to you.
20:14
إِنَّنِي أَنَا اللَّهُ لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا أَنَا
Verily! I am Allah (الله)! La ilaha illa Ana (none has the right to be worshipped but I),
فَاعْبُدْنِي وَأَقِمِ الصَّلَاةَ لِذِكْرِي
so worship Me, and perform AsSalat (Iqamat-as-Salat) for My Remembrance.
20:15
إِنَّ السَّاعَةَ آتِيَةٌ أَكَادُ أُخْفِيهَا لِتُجْزَى كُلُّ نَفْسٍ بِمَا تَسْعَى
Verily, the Hour is coming and My Will is to keep it hidden that every person may be rewarded for that which he strives.
20:16
فَلَا يَصُدَّنَّكَ عَنْهَا مَنْ لَا يُؤْمِنُ بِهَا وَاتَّبَعَ هَوَاهُ فَتَرْدَى
Therefore, let not the one who believes not therein (i.e. in the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة), Reckoning, Paradise (الجنة) and Hell (جهنم), etc.), but follows his own lusts, divert you therefrom, lest you perish.
20:17
‏وَمَا تِلْكَ بِيَمِينِكَ يَا مُوسَى
And what is that in your right hand, O Musa (Moosa (موسى))?‘
20:18
قَالَ هِيَ عَصَايَ أَتَوَكَّأُ عَلَيْهَا
He said: ‘This is my stick, whereon I lean,
وَأَهُشُّ بِهَا عَلَى غَنَمِي وَلِيَ فِيهَا مَآرِبُ أُخْرَى
and wherewith I beat down branches for my sheep, and wherein I find other uses.‘
20:19
قَالَ أَلْقِهَا يَا مُوسَى
(Allah (الله)) said: ‘Cast it down, O Musa (Moosa (موسى))!‘
20:20
فَأَلْقَاهَا فَإِذَا هِيَ حَيَّةٌ تَسْعَى
He cast it down, and behold! It was a snake, moving quickly.
20:21
قَالَ خُذْهَا وَلَا تَخَفْ ۖ
Allah (الله) said: ‘Grasp it, and fear not,
سَنُعِيدُهَا سِيرَتَهَا الْأُولَى
We shall return it to its former state,
20:22
وَاضْمُمْ يَدَكَ إِلَى جَنَاحِكَ تَخْرُجْ بَيْضَاءَ مِنْ غَيْرِ سُوءٍ آيَةً أُخْرَى
And press your (right) hand to your (left) side, it will come forth white (and shining), without any disease as another sign,
20:23
لِنُرِيَكَ مِنْ آيَاتِنَا الْكُبْرَى
That We may show you (some) of Our Greater Signs.
20:24
اذْهَبْ إِلَى فِرْعَوْنَ إِنَّهُ طَغَى
Go to Firaun (Pharaoh (فرعون))! Verily, he has transgressed (all bounds in disbelief and disobedience, and has behaved as an arrogant, and as a tyrant).‘
20:[25-48] Prophet Musa prayed to Allah to open his heart, ease his task and remove the impediment from his speech so that people may understand, what he says. Allah granted his request and reminded him about His favors
20:[49-54] Dialogue between Musa and Fir‘on
20:25
قَالَ رَبِّ اشْرَحْ لِي صَدْرِي
(Musa (Moosa (موسى))) said: ‘O my Lord! Open for me my chest (grant me self-confidence, contentment, and boldness).
20:26
وَيَسِّرْ لِي أَمْرِي
And ease my task for me;
20:27
وَاحْلُلْ عُقْدَةً مِنْ لِسَانِي
And make loose the knot (the defect) from my tongue,(i.e. remove the incorrectness from my speech)(That occurred as a result of a brand of fire which Musa (Moosa (موسى)) put in his mouth when he was an infant). (Tafsir At-Tabaree, Vol. 16, Page 159)
20:28
يَفْقَهُوا قَوْلِي
That they understand my speech,
20:29
وَاجْعَلْ لِي وَزِيرًا مِنْ أَهْلِي
And appoint for me a helper from my family,
20:30
هَارُونَ أَخِي
Haroon (Aaron), my brother;
20:31
اشْدُدْ بِهِ أَزْرِي
Increase my strength with him,
20:32
وَأَشْرِكْهُ فِي أَمْرِي
And let him share my task (of conveying Allah (الله)s Message and Prophethood),
20:33
كَيْ نُسَبِّحَكَ كَثِيرًا
That we may glorify You much,
20:34
وَنَذْكُرَكَ كَثِيرًا
And remember You much,
20:35
إِنَّكَ كُنْتَ بِنَا بَصِيرًا
Verily! You are of us Ever a Well-Seer.‘
20:36
قَالَ قَدْ أُوتِيتَ سُؤْلَكَ يَا مُوسَى
Allah (الله) said: ‘You are granted your request, O Musa (Moosa (موسى))!
20:37
وَلَقَدْ مَنَنَّا عَلَيْكَ مَرَّةً أُخْرَى
And indeed We conferred a favour on you another time (before).
20:38
إِذْ أَوْحَيْنَا إِلَى أُمِّكَ مَا يُوحَى
When We inspired your mother with that which We inspired.
20:39
أَنِ اقْذِفِيهِ فِي التَّابُوتِ فَاقْذِفِيهِ فِي الْيَمِّ
Saying: Put him (the child) into the Taboot (a box or a case or a chest) and put it into the river (Nile),
فَلْيُلْقِهِ الْيَمُّ بِالسَّاحِلِ يَأْخُذْهُ عَدُوٌّ لِي وَعَدُوٌّ لَهُ ۚ
then the river shall cast it up on the bank, and there, an enemy of Mine and an enemy of his shall take him.
وَأَلْقَيْتُ عَلَيْكَ مَحَبَّةً مِنِّي وَلِتُصْنَعَ عَلَى عَيْنِي
And I endued you with love from Me, in order that you may be brought up under My Eye (عين),
20:40
إِذْ تَمْشِي أُخْتُكَ فَتَقُولُ هَلْ أَدُلُّكُمْ عَلَى مَنْ يَكْفُلُهُ ۖ
When your sister went and said: Shall I show you one who will nurse him?
فَرَجَعْنَاكَ إِلَى أُمِّكَ كَيْ تَقَرَّ عَيْنُهَا وَلَا تَحْزَنَ ۚ
So We restored you to your mother, that she might cool her eyes and she should not grieve.
وَقَتَلْتَ نَفْسًا فَنَجَّيْنَاكَ مِنَ الْغَمِّ وَفَتَنَّاكَ فُتُونًا ۚ
Then you did kill a man, but We saved you from great distress and tried you with a heavy trial.
فَلَبِثْتَ سِنِينَ فِي أَهْلِ مَدْيَنَ
Then you stayed a number of years with the people of Madyan (مدين) (Midian).
ثُمَّ جِئْتَ عَلَى قَدَرٍ يَا مُوسَى
Then you came here according to the fixed term which I ordained (for you), O Musa (Moosa (موسى))!
20:41
وَاصْطَنَعْتُكَ لِنَفْسِي
And I have Istanatuka (prepared thee), for Myself.
20:42
اذْهَبْ أَنْتَ وَأَخُوكَ بِآيَاتِي وَلَا تَنِيَا فِي ذِكْرِي
Go you and your brother with My Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, etc.), and do not, you both, slacken and become weak in My Remembrance.
20:43
اذْهَبَا إِلَى فِرْعَوْنَ إِنَّهُ طَغَى
Go, both of you, to Firaun (Pharaoh (فرعون)), verily, he has transgressed (all bounds in disbelief and disobedience and behaved as an arrogant and as a tyrant).
20:44
فَقُولَا لَهُ قَوْلًا لَيِّنًا لَعَلَّهُ يَتَذَكَّرُ أَوْ يَخْشَى
And speak to him mildly, perhaps he may accept admonition or fear Allah (الله).‘
20:45
قَالَا رَبَّنَا إِنَّنَا نَخَافُ أَنْ يَفْرُطَ عَلَيْنَا أَوْ أَنْ يَطْغَى
They said: ‘Our Lord! Verily! We fear lest he should hasten to punish us or lest he should transgress (all bounds against us).‘
20:46
قَالَ لَا تَخَافَا ۖ
He (Allah (الله)) said: ‘Fear not,
إِنَّنِي مَعَكُمَا أَسْمَعُ وَأَرَى
verily! I am with you both, hearing and seeing.
20:47
فَأْتِيَاهُ فَقُولَا إِنَّا رَسُولَا رَبِّكَ فَأَرْسِلْ مَعَنَا بَنِي إِسْرَائِيلَ وَلَا تُعَذِّبْهُمْ ۖ
So go you both to him, and say: Verily, we are Messengers of your Lord, so let the Children of Israel (إسرائيل) (بنى إسرائيل) go with us, and torment them not;
قَدْ جِئْنَاكَ بِآيَةٍ مِنْ رَبِّكَ ۖ
indeed, we have come with a sign from your Lord!
وَالسَّلَامُ عَلَى مَنِ اتَّبَعَ الْهُدَى
And peace will be upon him who follows the guidance!
20:48
إِنَّا قَدْ أُوحِيَ إِلَيْنَا أَنَّ الْعَذَابَ عَلَى مَنْ كَذَّبَ وَتَوَلَّى
Truly, it has been revealed to us that the torment will be for him who denies (believes not in the Oneness of Allah (الله), and in His Messengers, etc.), and turns away. (from the truth and obedience of Allah (الله))‘
20:49
قَالَ فَمَنْ رَبُّكُمَا يَا مُوسَى
Firaun (Pharaoh (فرعون)) said: ‘Who then, O Musa (Moosa (موسى)), is the Lord of you two?‘
20:50
قَالَ رَبُّنَا الَّذِي أَعْطَى كُلَّ شَيْءٍ خَلْقَهُ ثُمَّ هَدَى
(Musa (Moosa (موسى))) said: ‘Our Lord is He Who gave to each thing its form and nature, then guided it aright.‘
20:51
قَالَ فَمَا بَالُ الْقُرُونِ الْأُولَى
(Firaun (Pharaoh (فرعون))) said: ‘What about the generations of old?‘
20:52
قَالَ عِلْمُهَا عِنْدَ رَبِّي فِي كِتَابٍ ۖ
(Musa (Moosa (موسى))) said: ‘The knowledge thereof is with my Lord, in a Record.
لَا يَضِلُّ رَبِّي وَلَا يَنْسَى
My Lord is neither unaware nor He forgets, ‘
20:53
الَّذِي جَعَلَ لَكُمُ الْأَرْضَ مَهْدًا وَسَلَكَ لَكُمْ فِيهَا سُبُلًا
Who has made earth for you like a bed (spread out); and has opened roads (ways and paths etc.) for you therein;
وَأَنْزَلَ مِنَ السَّمَاءِ مَاءً فَأَخْرَجْنَا بِهِ أَزْوَاجًا مِنْ نَبَاتٍ شَتَّى
and has sent down water (rain) from the sky. And We have brought forth with it various kinds of vegetation.
20:54
كُلُوا وَارْعَوْا أَنْعَامَكُمْ ۗ
Eat and pasture your cattle, (therein);
إِنَّ فِي ذَلِكَ لَآيَاتٍ لِأُولِي النُّهَى
verily, in this are proofs and signs for men of understanding.
20:[55-64] Human life cycle. Fir‘on disbelieved Musa by calling his miracles a magician‘s trick and challenged him to confront his magicians in public - Musa accepted the challenge
20:[65-76] The confrontation of Musa and Fir‘on‘s magicians. The magicians after witnessing Musa‘s miracle, accepted Islam. Dialogue between the magicians and Fir‘on
20:55
مِنْهَا خَلَقْنَاكُمْ وَفِيهَا نُعِيدُكُمْ وَمِنْهَا نُخْرِجُكُمْ تَارَةً أُخْرَى
Thereof (the earth) We created you, and into it We shall return you, and from it We shall bring you out once again.
20:56
وَلَقَدْ أَرَيْنَاهُ آيَاتِنَا كُلَّهَا فَكَذَّبَ وَأَبَى
And indeed We showed him (Firaun (Pharaoh (فرعون))) all Our Signs and Evidences, but he denied and refused.
20:57
قَالَ أَجِئْتَنَا لِتُخْرِجَنَا مِنْ أَرْضِنَا بِسِحْرِكَ يَا مُوسَى
He (Firaun (Pharaoh (فرعون))) said: ‘Have you come to drive us out of our land with your magic, O Musa (Moosa (موسى))?
20:58
فَلَنَأْتِيَنَّكَ بِسِحْرٍ مِثْلِهِ
Then verily, we can produce magic the like thereof;
فَاجْعَلْ بَيْنَنَا وَبَيْنَكَ مَوْعِدًا لَا نُخْلِفُهُ نَحْنُ وَلَا أَنْتَ مَكَانًا سُوًى
so appoint a meeting between us and you, which neither we, nor you shall fail to keep, in an open wide place where both shall have a just and equal chance (and beholders could witness the competition).‘
20:59
قَالَ مَوْعِدُكُمْ يَوْمُ الزِّينَةِ وَأَنْ يُحْشَرَ النَّاسُ ضُحًى
(Musa (Moosa (موسى))) said: ‘Your appointed meeting is the day of the festival, and let the people assemble when the sun has risen (forenoon).‘
20:60
فَتَوَلَّى فِرْعَوْنُ فَجَمَعَ كَيْدَهُ ثُمَّ أَتَى
So Firaun (Pharaoh (فرعون)) withdrew, devised his plot and then came back.
20:61
قَالَ لَهُمْ مُوسَى وَيْلَكُمْ لَا تَفْتَرُوا عَلَى اللَّهِ كَذِبًا فَيُسْحِتَكُمْ بِعَذَابٍ ۖ
Musa (Moosa (موسى)) said to them: ‘Woe unto you! Invent not a lie against Allah (الله), lest He should destroy you completely by a torment.
وَقَدْ خَابَ مَنِ افْتَرَى
And surely, he who invents a lie (against Allah (الله)) will fail miserably.‘
20:62
فَتَنَازَعُوا أَمْرَهُمْ بَيْنَهُمْ وَأَسَرُّوا النَّجْوَى
Then they debated with one another what they must do, and they kept their talk secret.
20:63
قَالُوا إِنْ هَذَانِ لَسَاحِرَانِ
They said: ‘Verily! These are two magicians.
يُرِيدَانِ أَنْ يُخْرِجَاكُمْ مِنْ أَرْضِكُمْ بِسِحْرِهِمَا وَيَذْهَبَا بِطَرِيقَتِكُمُ الْمُثْلَى
Their object is to drive you out from your land with magic, and overcome your chiefs and nobles.
20:64
فَأَجْمِعُوا كَيْدَكُمْ ثُمَّ ائْتُوا صَفًّا ۚ
So devise your plot, and then assemble in line.
وَقَدْ أَفْلَحَ الْيَوْمَ مَنِ اسْتَعْلَى
And whoever overcomes this day will be indeed successful.‘
20:65
قَالُوا يَا مُوسَى إِمَّا أَنْ تُلْقِيَ وَإِمَّا أَنْ نَكُونَ أَوَّلَ مَنْ أَلْقَى
They said: ‘O Musa (Moosa (موسى))! Either you throw first or we be the first to throw?‘
20:66
قَالَ بَلْ أَلْقُوا ۖ
(Musa (Moosa (موسى))) said: ‘Nay, throw you (first)!‘
فَإِذَا حِبَالُهُمْ وَعِصِيُّهُمْ يُخَيَّلُ إِلَيْهِ مِنْ سِحْرِهِمْ أَنَّهَا تَسْعَى
Then behold, their ropes and their sticks, by their magic, appeared to him as though they moved fast.
20:67
فَأَوْجَسَ فِي نَفْسِهِ خِيفَةً مُوسَى
So Musa (Moosa (موسى)) conceived a fear in himself.
20:68
قُلْنَا لَا تَخَفْ إِنَّكَ أَنْتَ الْأَعْلَى
We (Allah (الله)) said: ‘Fear not! Surely, you will have the upper hand.
20:69
وَأَلْقِ مَا فِي يَمِينِكَ تَلْقَفْ مَا صَنَعُوا ۖ
And throw that which is in your right hand! It will swallow up that which they have made.
إِنَّمَا صَنَعُوا كَيْدُ سَاحِرٍ ۖ
That which they have made is only a magicians trick,
وَلَا يُفْلِحُ السَّاحِرُ حَيْثُ أَتَى
and the magician will never be successful, no matter whatever amount (of skill) he may attain.‘
20:70
فَأُلْقِيَ السَّحَرَةُ سُجَّدًا
So the magicians fell down prostrate.
قَالُوا آمَنَّا بِرَبِّ هَارُونَ وَمُوسَى
They said: ‘We believe in the Lord of Haroon (Aaron) and Musa (Moosa (موسى)).‘
20:71
قَالَ آمَنْتُمْ لَهُ قَبْلَ أَنْ آذَنَ لَكُمْ ۖ
(Firaun (Pharaoh (فرعون))) said: ‘Believe you in him (Musa (Moosa (موسى))) before I give you permission?
إِنَّهُ الَّذِي عَلَّمَكُمُ السِّحْرَ ۖ
Verily! He is your chief who taught you magic.
فَلَأُقَطِّعَنَّ أَيْدِيَكُمْ وَأَرْجُلَكُمْ مِنْ خِلَافٍ وَلَأُصَلِّبَنَّكُمْ فِي جُذُوعِ النَّخْلِ
So I will surely cut off your hands and feet on opposite sides, and I will surely crucify you on the trunks of date-palms,
وَلَتَعْلَمُنَّ أَيُّنَا أَشَدُّ عَذَابًا وَأَبْقَى
and you shall surely know which of us (I (Firaun Pharaoh (فرعون)) or the Lord of Musa (Moosa (موسى)) (Allah (الله))) can give the severe and more lasting torment.‘
20:72
قَالُوا لَنْ نُؤْثِرَكَ عَلَى مَا جَاءَنَا مِنَ الْبَيِّنَاتِ وَالَّذِي فَطَرَنَا ۖ
They said: ‘We prefer you not over the clear signs that have come to us, and to Him (Allah (الله)) Who created us.
فَاقْضِ مَا أَنْتَ قَاضٍ ۖ إِنَّمَا تَقْضِي هَذِهِ الْحَيَاةَ الدُّنْيَا
So decree whatever you desire to decree, for you can only decree (regarding) this life of the world.
20:73
إِنَّا آمَنَّا بِرَبِّنَا لِيَغْفِرَ لَنَا خَطَايَانَا وَمَا أَكْرَهْتَنَا عَلَيْهِ مِنَ السِّحْرِ ۗ
Verily! We have believed in our Lord, that He may forgive us our faults, and the magic to which you did compel us.
وَاللَّهُ خَيْرٌ وَأَبْقَى
And Allah (الله) is better as regards reward in comparison to your (Firaun‘s (Pharaoh (فرعون))) reward, and more lasting (as regards punishment in comparison to your punishment).‘
20:74
إِنَّهُ مَنْ يَأْتِ رَبَّهُ مُجْرِمًا فَإِنَّ لَهُ جَهَنَّمَ لَا يَمُوتُ فِيهَا وَلَا يَحْيَى
Verily! Whoever comes to his Lord as a Mujrim (criminal, polytheist, disbeliever in the Oneness of Allah (الله) and His Messengers, sinner, etc.), then surely, for him is Hell (جهنم), therein he will neither die nor live.
20:75
وَمَنْ يَأْتِهِ مُؤْمِنًا قَدْ عَمِلَ الصَّالِحَاتِ فَأُولَئِكَ لَهُمُ الدَّرَجَاتُ الْعُلَى
But whoever comes to Him (Allah (الله)) as a believer (in the Oneness of Allah (الله), etc.), and has done righteous good deeds, for such are the high ranks (in the Hereafter),
20:76
جَنَّاتُ عَدْنٍ تَجْرِي مِنْ تَحْتِهَا الْأَنْهَارُ خَالِدِينَ فِيهَا ۚ
Adn (Edn) Paradise (الجنة) (everlasting Gardens), under which rivers flow, wherein they will abide forever:
وَذَلِكَ جَزَاءُ مَنْ تَزَكَّى
such is the reward of those who purify themselves ((by abstaining from all kinds of sins and evil deeds) which Allah (الله) has forbidden and by doing all that which Allah (الله) has ordained)).
20:[77-82] Deliverance of the Children of Israel from the bondage of Fir‘on
20:[83-89] When the Prophet Musa went to Mount Tür for communion with Allah – Israelites started worshipping the calf in his absence
20:77

And indeed We inspired Musa (Moosa (موسى)) (saying): ‘Travel by night with Ibadi (My slaves)
فَاضْرِبْ لَهُمْ طَرِيقًا فِي الْبَحْرِ
and strike a dry path for them in the sea,
يَبَسًا لَا تَخَافُ دَرَكًا وَلَا تَخْشَى
fearing neither to be overtaken (by Firaun (Pharaoh (فرعون))) nor being afraid (of drowning in the sea).‘
20:78
فَأَتْبَعَهُمْ فِرْعَوْنُ بِجُنُودِهِ فَغَشِيَهُمْ مِنَ الْيَمِّ مَا غَشِيَهُمْ
Then Firaun (Pharaoh (فرعون)) pursued them with his hosts, but sea-water completely overwhelmed them and covered them up.
20:79
‏وَأَضَلَّ فِرْعَوْنُ قَوْمَهُ وَمَا هَدَى

20:80

O Children of Israel (إسرائيل) (بنى إسرائيل)! We delivered you from your enemy,

and We made a covenant with you on the right side of the Mount,
وَنَزَّلْنَا عَلَيْكُمُ الْمَنَّ وَالسَّلْوَى
and We sent down to you Al-Manna and quails,
20:81

(Saying) eat of the Taiyibat (good lawful things) wherewith We have provided you,

and commit no oppression therein, lest My Anger should justly descend on you.
وَمَنْ يَحْلِلْ عَلَيْهِ غَضَبِي فَقَدْ هَوَى
And he on whom My Anger descends, he is indeed perished.
20:82

And verily, I am indeed Forgiving to him who repents, believes (in My Oneness, and associates none in worship with Me) and does righteous good deeds, and then remains constant in doing them, (till his death).
20:83

And what made you hasten from your people, O Musa (Moosa (موسى))?‘
20:84

He said: ‘They are close on my footsteps, and I hastened to You, O my Lord, that You might be pleased.‘
20:85

(Allah (الله)) said: ‘Verily! We have tried your people in your absence,
وَأَضَلَّهُمُ السَّامِرِيُّ
and As-Samiri (السامرى) has led them astray.‘
20:86

Then Musa (Moosa (موسى)) returned to his people in a state of anger and sorrow.
قَالَ يَا قَوْمِ أَلَمْ يَعِدْكُمْ رَبُّكُمْ وَعْدًا حَسَنًا ۚ
He said: ‘O my people! Did not your Lord promise you a fair promise?
أَفَطَالَ عَلَيْكُمُ الْعَهْدُ
Did then the promise seem to you long in coming?
أَمْ أَرَدْتُمْ أَنْ يَحِلَّ عَلَيْكُمْ غَضَبٌ مِنْ رَبِّكُمْ فَأَخْلَفْتُمْ مَوْعِدِي
Or did you desire that wrath should descend from your Lord on you, so you broke your promise to me (i.e disbelieving in Allah (الله) and worshipping the calf)?‘
20:87
قَالُوا مَا أَخْلَفْنَا مَوْعِدَكَ بِمَلْكِنَا
They said: ‘We broke not the promise to you, of our own will,
وَلَكِنَّا حُمِّلْنَا أَوْزَارًا مِنْ زِينَةِ الْقَوْمِ فَقَذَفْنَاهَا
but we were made to carry the weight of the ornaments of the (Firauns (Pharaoh (فرعون))) people, then we cast them (into the fire),
فَكَذَلِكَ أَلْقَى السَّامِرِيُّ
and that was what As-Samiri (السامرى) suggested.‘
20:88
فَأَخْرَجَ لَهُمْ عِجْلًا جَسَدًا لَهُ خُوَارٌ
Then he took out (of the fire) for them a statue of a calf which seemed to low.
فَقَالُوا هَذَا إِلَهُكُمْ وَإِلَهُ مُوسَى فَنَسِيَ
They said: ‘This is your ilah (god), and the ilah (god) of Musa (Moosa (موسى)), but (Musa (Moosa (موسى))) has forgotten (his god).‘
20:89
أَفَلَا يَرَوْنَ أَلَّا يَرْجِعُ إِلَيْهِمْ قَوْلًا وَلَا يَمْلِكُ لَهُمْ ضَرًّا وَلَا نَفْعًا
Did they not see that it could not return them a word (for answer), and that it had no power either to harm them or to do them good?

so I took a handful (of dust) from the hoof print of the messenger (Jibraels (Gabriel) horse) and threw it (into the fire in which were put the ornaments of the Firauns (Pharaoh (فرعون)) people, or into the calf).

The Day when the Trumpet will be blown (the second blowing):

when the best among them in knowledge and wisdom will say: ‘You stayed no longer than a day!‘

20:114
فَتَعَالَى اللَّهُ الْمَلِكُ الْحَقُّ ۗ
Then High above all be Allah (الله), the True King (ملك).
وَلَا تَعْجَلْ بِالْقُرْآنِ مِنْ قَبْلِ أَنْ يُقْضَى إِلَيْكَ وَحْيُهُ ۖ
And be not in haste (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) with the Quran (القرآن) before its revelation is completed to you,
وَقُلْ رَبِّ زِدْنِي عِلْمًا
and say: ‘My Lord! Increase me in knowledge.‘
20:115
وَلَقَدْ عَهِدْنَا إِلَى آدَمَ مِنْ قَبْلُ فَنَسِيَ
And indeed We made a covenant with Adam (آدم) before, but he forgot,
وَلَمْ نَجِدْ لَهُ عَزْمًا
and We found on his part no firm will-power.
20:[116-128] Story of Adam‘s creation and Shaitãn‘s temptation. Allah forgave Adam‘s sin, chose him and guided him to the right Way. Those who do not read The Qur‘an and follow its guidance shall be raised as blind on the Day of Resurrection
20:116
وَإِذْ قُلْنَا لِلْمَلَائِكَةِ اسْجُدُوا لِآدَمَ
And (remember) when We said to the angels: ‘Prostrate yourselves to Adam (آدم).‘
فَسَجَدُوا إِلَّا إِبْلِيسَ أَبَى
They prostrated (all) except Iblees (إبليس) (Satan (الشيطان)), who refused.
20:117
فَقُلْنَا يَا آدَمُ إِنَّ هَذَا عَدُوٌّ لَكَ وَلِزَوْجِكَ
Then We said: ‘O Adam (آدم)! Verily, this is an enemy to you and to your wife.
فَلَا يُخْرِجَنَّكُمَا مِنَ الْجَنَّةِ فَتَشْقَى
So let him not get you both out of Paradise (الجنة), so that you be distressed in misery.
20:118
إِنَّ لَكَ أَلَّا تَجُوعَ فِيهَا وَلَا تَعْرَى
Verily, you have (a promise from Us) that you will never be hungry therein nor naked.
20:119
وَأَنَّكَ لَا تَظْمَأُ فِيهَا وَلَا تَضْحَى
And you (will) suffer not from thirst therein nor from the suns heat.
20:120
فَوَسْوَسَ إِلَيْهِ الشَّيْطَانُ
Then Shaitan (Satan (الشيطان)) whispered to him,
قَالَ يَا آدَمُ هَلْ أَدُلُّكَ عَلَى شَجَرَةِ الْخُلْدِ وَمُلْكٍ لَا يَبْلَى
saying : ‘O Adam (آدم)! Shall I lead you to the Tree (شجرة) of Eternity and to a kingdom that will never waste away?‘
20:121
‏فَأَكَلَا مِنْهَا فَبَدَتْ لَهُمَا سَوْآتُهُمَا وَطَفِقَا يَخْصِفَانِ عَلَيْهِمَا مِنْ وَرَقِ الْجَنَّةِ ۚ
Then they both ate of the tree, and so their private parts appeared to them, and they began to stick on themselves the leaves from Paradise (الجنة) for their covering.
وَعَصَى آدَمُ رَبَّهُ فَغَوَى
Thus did Adam (آدم) disobey his Lord, so he went astray.
20:122
ثُمَّ اجْتَبَاهُ رَبُّهُ فَتَابَ عَلَيْهِ وَهَدَى
Then his Lord chose him, and turned to him with forgiveness, and gave him guidance.
20:123
قَالَ اهْبِطَا مِنْهَا جَمِيعًا ۖ
(Allah (الله)) said: ‘Get you down (from the Paradise (الجنة) to the earth), both of you, together,
بَعْضُكُمْ لِبَعْضٍ عَدُوٌّ ۖ
some of you are an enemy to some others.
فَإِمَّا يَأْتِيَنَّكُمْ مِنِّي هُدًى فَمَنِ اتَّبَعَ هُدَايَ فَلَا يَضِلُّ وَلَا يَشْقَى
Then if there comes to you guidance from Me, then whoever follows My Guidance shall neither go astray, nor fall into distress and misery.
20:124
وَمَنْ أَعْرَضَ عَنْ ذِكْرِي فَإِنَّ لَهُ مَعِيشَةً ضَنْكًا
But whosoever turns away from My Reminder (i.e. neither believes in this Quran (القرآن) nor acts on its orders, etc.) verily, for him is a life of hardship,
وَنَحْشُرُهُ يَوْمَ الْقِيَامَةِ أَعْمَى
and We shall raise him up blind on the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة).‘
20:125
قَالَ رَبِّ لِمَ حَشَرْتَنِي أَعْمَى وَقَدْ كُنْتُ بَصِيرًا
He will say: ‘O my Lord! Why have you raised me up blind, while I had sight (before).‘
20:126
قَالَ كَذَلِكَ أَتَتْكَ آيَاتُنَا فَنَسِيتَهَا ۖ
(Allah (الله)) will say: ‘Like this, Our Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, etc.) came unto you, but you disregarded them,(i.e. you left them, did not think deeply in them, and you turned away from them)
وَكَذَلِكَ الْيَوْمَ تُنْسَى
and so this Day, you will be neglected (in the Hell (جهنم)-fire, away from Allah (الله)‘s Mercy).
20:127
وَكَذَلِكَ نَجْزِي مَنْ أَسْرَفَ وَلَمْ يُؤْمِنْ بِآيَاتِ رَبِّهِ ۚ
And thus do We requite him who transgresses beyond bounds (i.e. commits the great sins and disobeys his Lord (Allah (الله)) and believes not in His Messengers, and His revealed Books, like this Quran (القرآن), etc.), and believes not in the Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, etc) of his Lord,
وَلَعَذَابُ الْآخِرَةِ أَشَدُّ وَأَبْقَى
and the torment of the Hereafter is far more severe and more lasting.
20:128
أَفَلَمْ يَهْدِ لَهُمْ كَمْ أَهْلَكْنَا قَبْلَهُمْ مِنَ الْقُرُونِ يَمْشُونَ فِي مَسَاكِنِهِمْ ۗ
Is it not a guidance for them (to know) how many generations We have destroyed before them, in whose dwellings they walk?
إِنَّ فِي ذَلِكَ لَآيَاتٍ لِأُولِي النُّهَى
Verily, in this are signs indeed for men of understanding.
20:[129-132] Do not envy others in worldly benefits, rather seek Allah‘s pleasure if you want to attain the blessed end
20:[133-135] The Qur‘an is a sign from Allah so there can be no excuse for the unbelievers on the Day of Judgement
20:129
وَلَوْلَا كَلِمَةٌ سَبَقَتْ مِنْ رَبِّكَ لَكَانَ لِزَامًا وَأَجَلٌ مُسَمًّى
And had it not been for a Word that went forth before from your Lord, and a term determined, (their punishment) must necessarily have come (in this world).
20:130
‏فَاصْبِرْ عَلَى مَا يَقُولُونَ
So bear patiently (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) what they say,
وَسَبِّحْ بِحَمْدِ رَبِّكَ قَبْلَ طُلُوعِ الشَّمْسِ وَقَبْلَ غُرُوبِهَا ۖ
and glorify the praises of your Lord before the rising of the sun, and before its setting,
وَمِنْ آنَاءِ اللَّيْلِ فَسَبِّحْ وَأَطْرَافَ النَّهَارِ لَعَلَّكَ تَرْضَى
and during some of the hours of the night, and at the sides of the day (an indication for the five compulsory congregational prayers), that you may become pleased with the reward which Allah (الله) shall give you.
20:131
وَلَا تَمُدَّنَّ عَيْنَيْكَ إِلَى مَا مَتَّعْنَا بِهِ أَزْوَاجًا مِنْهُمْ زَهْرَةَ الْحَيَاةِ الدُّنْيَا لِنَفْتِنَهُمْ فِيهِ ۚ
And strain not your eyes in longing for the things We have given for enjoyment to various groups of them (polytheists and disbelievers in the Oneness of Allah (الله)), the splendour of the life of this world that We may test them thereby.
وَرِزْقُ رَبِّكَ خَيْرٌ وَأَبْقَى
But the provision (good reward in the Hereafter) of your Lord is better and more lasting.
20:132
وَأْمُرْ أَهْلَكَ بِالصَّلَاةِ وَاصْطَبِرْ عَلَيْهَا ۖ
And enjoin As-Salat (الصَّلَاةَ) (the prayer) on your family, and be patient in offering them (i.e. the Salat (prayers)).
لَا نَسْأَلُكَ رِزْقًا ۖ نَحْنُ نَرْزُقُكَ ۗ
We ask not of you a provision (i.e. to give Us something: money, etc.); We provide for you.
وَالْعَاقِبَةُ لِلتَّقْوَى
And the good end (i.e. Paradise (الجنة)) is for the Muttaqoon (pious - see V.2:2).
20:133
وَقَالُوا لَوْلَا يَأْتِينَا بِآيَةٍ مِنْ رَبِّهِ ۚ
They say: ‘Why does he not bring us a sign (proof) from his Lord?‘
أَوَلَمْ تَأْتِهِمْ بَيِّنَةُ مَا فِي الصُّحُفِ الْأُولَى
Has there not come to them the proof of that which is (written) in the former papers (Scriptures, i.e. the Taurat (Torah (التوراة)), and the Injil (Injeel (الإنجيل)), etc. about the coming of the Prophet Muhammad (محمد) SAW ).
20:134
وَلَوْ أَنَّا أَهْلَكْنَاهُمْ بِعَذَابٍ مِنْ قَبْلِهِ لَقَالُوا
And if We had destroyed them with a torment before this (i.e. Messenger Muhammad (محمد) SAW and the Quran (القرآن)), they would surely have said:
رَبَّنَا لَوْلَا أَرْسَلْتَ إِلَيْنَا رَسُولًا فَنَتَّبِعَ آيَاتِكَ مِنْ قَبْلِ أَنْ نَذِلَّ وَنَخْزَى
Our Lord! If only You had sent us a Messenger, we should certainly have followed Your Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, etc.), before we were humiliated and disgraced.‘
20:135
قُلْ كُلٌّ مُتَرَبِّصٌ فَتَرَبَّصُوا ۖ
Say (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW): ‘Each one (believer and disbeliever, etc.) is waiting, so wait you too,
فَسَتَعْلَمُونَ مَنْ أَصْحَابُ الصِّرَاطِ السَّوِيِّ وَمَنِ اهْتَدَى
and you shall know who are they that are on the Straight and Even Path (i.e. Allah (الله)‘s Religion of Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism), and who are they that have let themselves be guided (on the Right Path).*********
