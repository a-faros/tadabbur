Al-Burooj
The Mansions Of The Stars, The Constellations, The Great Constellations
85:[1-22] Those who torture the believers will be given the punishment of conflagration on the Day of Judgment. He Who created you for the first time will bring you back to life again for accountability
85:1
وَٱلسَّمَآءِ ذَاتِ ٱلۡبُرُوجِ
By the heaven, holding the big stars .
85:2
وَٱلۡيَوۡمِ ٱلۡمَوۡعُودِ
And by the Promised Day (i.e. the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة));
85:3
وَشَاهِدٍ۬ وَمَشۡہُودٍ۬
And by the witnessing day (i.e. Friday (الجمعة)), and by the witnessed day [i.e. the day of ‘Arafât (Hajj (حج)) the ninth of Dhul-Hîjjah];
85:4
قُتِلَ أَصۡحَـٰبُ ٱلۡأُخۡدُودِ
Cursed were the people of the ditch(the story of the Boy and the King (ملك)).
85:5
ٱلنَّارِ ذَاتِ ٱلۡوَقُودِ
Fire supplied (abundantly) with fuel,
85:6
إِذۡ هُمۡ عَلَيۡہَا قُعُودٌ۬
When they sat by it (fire),
85:7
وَهُمۡ عَلَىٰ مَا يَفۡعَلُونَ بِٱلۡمُؤۡمِنِينَ شُہُودٌ۬
And they witnessed what they were doing against the believers (i.e. burning them).
85:8
وَمَا نَقَمُواْ مِنۡہُمۡ إِلَّآ أَن يُؤۡمِنُواْ بِٱللَّهِ ٱلۡعَزِيزِ ٱلۡحَمِيدِ
They had nothing against them, except that they believed in Allâh, the All-Mighty, Worthy of all Praise!
85:9
ٱلَّذِى لَهُ ۥ مُلۡكُ ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٲتِ وَٱلۡأَرۡضِ‌ۚ
Who, to Whom belongs the dominion of the heavens and the earth!
وَٱللَّهُ عَلَىٰ كُلِّ شَىۡءٍ۬ شَہِيدٌ
And Allâh is Witness over everything.
85:10
إِنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ فَتَنُواْ ٱلۡمُؤۡمِنِينَ وَٱلۡمُؤۡمِنَـٰتِ ثُمَّ لَمۡ يَتُوبُواْ
Verily, those who put into trial the believing men and believing women (by torturing them and burning them), and then do not turn in repentance, (to Allâh),
فَلَهُمۡ عَذَابُ جَهَنَّمَ وَلَهُمۡ عَذَابُ ٱلۡحَرِيقِ
will have the torment of Hell (جهنم), and they will have the punishment of the burning Fire.
85:11
إِنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُواْ وَعَمِلُواْ ٱلصَّـٰلِحَـٰتِ لَهُمۡ جَنَّـٰتٌ۬ تَجۡرِى مِن تَحۡتِہَا ٱلۡأَنۡہَـٰرُ‌ۚ
Verily, those who believe and do righteous good deeds, for them will be Gardens under which rivers flow (Paradise (الجنة)).
ذَٲلِكَ ٱلۡفَوۡزُ ٱلۡكَبِيرُ
That is the great success.
85:12
إِنَّ بَطۡشَ رَبِّكَ لَشَدِيدٌ
Verily, (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) the Grip (Punishment) of your Lord is severe.
85:13
إِنَّهُ ۥ هُوَ يُبۡدِئُ وَيُعِيدُ
Verily, He it is Who begins (punishment) and repeats (punishment in the Hereafter)(or originates the creation of everything, and then repeats it on the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة)).
85:14
وَهُوَ ٱلۡغَفُورُ ٱلۡوَدُودُ
And He is Oft-Forgiving, full of love (towards the pious who are real true believers of Islâmic Monotheism),
85:15
ذُو ٱلۡعَرۡشِ ٱلۡمَجِيدُ
Owner of the throne, the Glorious
85:16
فَعَّالٌ۬ لِّمَا يُرِيدُ
He does what He intends (or wills).
85:17
هَلۡ أَتَٮٰكَ حَدِيثُ ٱلۡجُنُودِ
Has the story reached you of the hosts,
85:18
فِرۡعَوۡنَ وَثَمُودَ
Of Fir‘aun (Pharaoh (فرعون)) and Thamûd?
85:19
بَلِ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُواْ فِى تَكۡذِيبٍ۬
Nay! The disbelievers (persisted) in denying (Prophet Muhammad (محمد) SAW and his Message of Islâmic Monotheism).
85:20
وَٱللَّهُ مِن وَرَآٮِٕہِم مُّحِيطُۢ
And Allâh encompasses them from behind!(i.e. all their deeds are within His Knowledge, and He will requite them for their deeds).
85:21
بَلۡ هُوَ قُرۡءَانٌ۬ مَّجِيدٌ۬
Nay! This is a Glorious Qur‘ân,
85:22
فِى لَوۡحٍ۬ مَّحۡفُوظِۭ
(Inscribed) in Al-Lauh Al-Mahfûz (The Preserved Tablet)!*********
