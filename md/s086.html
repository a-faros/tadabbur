At-Taariq
The Night-Visitant, The Morning Star, The Nightcomer, That Which Comes in the Night
86:[1-17] Over each soul there is an appointed guardian angel. The Qur‘an is a decisive word of Allah
86:1
وَٱلسَّمَآءِ وَٱلطَّارِقِ
By the heaven, and At-Târiq (the night-comer, i.e. the bright star);
86:2
وَمَآ أَدۡرَٮٰكَ مَا ٱلطَّارِقُ
And what will make you to know what At-Târiq (night-comer) is?
86:3
ٱلنَّجۡمُ ٱلثَّاقِبُ
(It is) the star of piercing brightness;
86:4
إِن كُلُّ نَفۡسٍ۬ لَّمَّا عَلَيۡہَا حَافِظٌ۬
There is no human being but has a protector over him (or her)(i.e. angels incharge of each human being guarding him, writing his good and bad deeds, etc.)
86:5
فَلۡيَنظُرِ ٱلۡإِنسَـٰنُ مِمَّ خُلِقَ
So let man see from what he is created!
86:6
خُلِقَ مِن مَّآءٍ۬ دَافِقٍ۬
He is created from a water gushing forth
86:7
يَخۡرُجُ مِنۢ بَيۡنِ ٱلصُّلۡبِ وَٱلتَّرَآٮِٕبِ
Proceeding from between the back-bone and the ribs,
86:8
إِنَّهُ ۥ عَلَىٰ رَجۡعِهِۦ لَقَادِرٌ۬
Verily, (Allâh) is Able to bring him back (to life)!
86:9
يَوۡمَ تُبۡلَى ٱلسَّرَآٮِٕرُ
The Day when all the secrets (deeds, prayers, fasting, etc.) will be examined (as to their truth).
86:10
فَمَا لَهُ ۥ مِن قُوَّةٍ۬ وَلَا نَاصِرٍ۬
Then will (man) have no power, nor any helper.
86:11
وَٱلسَّمَآءِ ذَاتِ ٱلرَّجۡعِ
By the sky (having rain clouds) which gives rain, again and again.
86:12
وَٱلۡأَرۡضِ ذَاتِ ٱلصَّدۡعِ
And the earth which splits (with the growth of trees and plants),
86:13
إِنَّهُ ۥ لَقَوۡلٌ۬ فَصۡلٌ۬
Verily! This (the Qur‘ân) is the Word that separates (the truth from falsehood, and commands strict legal laws for mankind to cut the roots of evil).
86:14
وَمَا هُوَ بِٱلۡهَزۡلِ
And it is not a thing for amusement.
86:15
إِنَّہُمۡ يَكِيدُونَ كَيۡدً۬ا
Verily, they are but plotting a plot (against you O Muhammad (محمد) SAW).
86:16
وَأَكِيدُ كَيۡدً۬ا
And I (too) am planning a plan.
86:17
فَمَهِّلِ ٱلۡكَـٰفِرِينَ أَمۡهِلۡهُمۡ رُوَيۡدَۢا
So give a respite to the disbelievers. Deal you gently with them for a while.*********
