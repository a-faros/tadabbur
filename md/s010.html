10:[3-5] Allah is the One Who created this universe. He is the One Who originates creation and repeats it
10:[6-10] There are signs of His manifestation in the creation of the heavens, earth, sun, moon, day and night
10:1
الر ۚ
Alif-Lam-Ra(These letters are one of the miracles of the Quran (القرآن), and none but Allah (الله) (Alone) knows their meanings)
تِلْكَ آيَاتُ الْكِتَابِ الْحَكِيمِ
These are the Verses of the Book (the Quran (القرآن)) Al-Hakeem (ٱلْحَكِيمُ)(showing lawful and unlawful things, explaining Allah (الله)‘s (Divine) Laws for mankind, leading them to eternal happiness by ordering them to follow the true Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism, - worshipping none but Allah (الله) Alone - that will guide them to Paradise (الجنة) and save them from Hell (جهنم))
10:2
أَكَانَ لِلنَّاسِ عَجَبًا أَنْ أَوْحَيْنَا إِلَى رَجُلٍ مِنْهُمْ
Is it wonder for mankind that We have sent Our Inspiration to a man from among themselves (ie Prophet Muhammad (محمد) SAW)
أَنْ أَنْذِرِ النَّاسَ وَبَشِّرِ الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا أَنَّ لَهُمْ قَدَمَ صِدْقٍ عِنْدَ رَبِّهِمْ ۗ
(saying): ‘Warn mankind (of the coming torment in Hell (جهنم)), and give good news to those who believe (in the Oneness of Allah (الله) and in His Prophet Muhammad (محمد) SAW) that they shall have with their Lord the rewards of their good deeds?‘
قَالَ الْكَافِرُونَ إِنَّ هَذَا لَسَاحِرٌ مُبِينٌ
(But) the disbelievers say: ‘This is indeed an evident sorcerer (ie Prophet Muhammad (محمد) SAW and the Quran (القرآن))!
10:3
إِنَّ رَبَّكُمُ اللَّهُ الَّذِي خَلَقَ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضَ فِي سِتَّةِ أَيَّامٍ
Surely, your Lord is Allah (الله) Who created the heavens and the earth in six Days
ثُمَّ اسْتَوَى عَلَى الْعَرْشِ يُدَبِّرُ الْأَمْرَ ۖ
and then Istawa (rose over) the Throne (really in a manner that suits His Majesty), disposing the affair of all things
مَا مِنْ شَفِيعٍ إِلَّا مِنْ بَعْدِ إِذْنِهِ ۚ
No intercessor (can plead with Him) except after His Leave
ذَلِكُمُ اللَّهُ رَبُّكُمْ فَاعْبُدُوهُ ۚ
That is Allah (الله), your Lord; so worship Him (Alone)
أَفَلَا تَذَكَّرُونَ
Then, will you not remember?
10:4
إِلَيْهِ مَرْجِعُكُمْ جَمِيعًا ۖ
To Him is the return of all of you
وَعْدَ اللَّهِ حَقًّا ۚ
The Promise of Allah (الله) is true
إِنَّهُ يَبْدَأُ الْخَلْقَ ثُمَّ يُعِيدُهُ لِيَجْزِيَ الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا وَعَمِلُوا الصَّالِحَاتِ بِالْقِسْطِ ۚ
It is He Who begins the creation and then will repeat it, that He may reward with justice those who believed (in the Oneness of Allah (الله) - Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism) and did deeds of righteousness
وَالَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا لَهُمْ شَرَابٌ مِنْ حَمِيمٍ وَعَذَابٌ أَلِيمٌ بِمَا كَانُوا يَكْفُرُونَ
But those who disbelieved will have a drink of boiling fluids and painful torment because they used to disbelieve
10:5
هُوَ الَّذِي جَعَلَ الشَّمْسَ ضِيَاءً وَالْقَمَرَ نُورًا
It is He Who made the sun a shining thing and the moon as a light
وَقَدَّرَهُ مَنَازِلَ لِتَعْلَمُوا عَدَدَ السِّنِينَ وَالْحِسَابَ ۚ
and measured out its (their) stages, that you might know the number of years and the reckoning
مَا خَلَقَ اللَّهُ ذَلِكَ إِلَّا بِالْحَقِّ ۚ
Allah (الله) did not create this but in truth
يُفَصِّلُ الْآيَاتِ لِقَوْمٍ يَعْلَمُونَ
He explains the Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, etc) in detail for people who have knowledge
10:6
إِنَّ فِي اخْتِلَافِ اللَّيْلِ وَالنَّهَارِ
Verily, in the alternation of the night and the day
وَمَا خَلَقَ اللَّهُ فِي السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضِ لَآيَاتٍ لِقَوْمٍ يَتَّقُونَ
and in all that Allah (الله) has created in the heavens and the earth are Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, etc) for those people who keep their duty to Allah (الله), and fear Him much
10:7
إِنَّ الَّذِينَ لَا يَرْجُونَ لِقَاءَنَا وَرَضُوا بِالْحَيَاةِ الدُّنْيَا وَاطْمَأَنُّوا بِهَا
Verily, those who hope not for their meeting with Us, but are pleased and satisfied with the life of the present world,
وَالَّذِينَ هُمْ عَنْ آيَاتِنَا غَافِلُونَ
and those who are heedless of Our Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, etc),
10:8
أُولَئِكَ مَأْوَاهُمُ النَّارُ بِمَا كَانُوا يَكْسِبُونَ
Those, their abode will be the Fire, because of what they used to earn
10:9
إِنَّ الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا وَعَمِلُوا الصَّالِحَاتِ يَهْدِيهِمْ رَبُّهُمْ بِإِيمَانِهِمْ ۖ
Verily, those who believe (in the Oneness of Allah (الله) along with the six articles of Faith, ie to believe in Allah (الله), His Angel (الملائكة)s, His Books, His Messengers, Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة), and Al-Qadar (Divine Preordainments) - Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism), and do deeds of righteousness, their Lord will guide them through their Faith;
تَجْرِي مِنْ تَحْتِهِمُ الْأَنْهَارُ فِي جَنَّاتِ النَّعِيمِ
under them will flow rivers in the Gardens of delight (Paradise (الجنة))
10:10
دَعْوَاهُمْ فِيهَا سُبْحَانَكَ اللَّهُمَّ وَتَحِيَّتُهُمْ فِيهَا سَلَامٌ ۚ
Their way of request therein will be Subhanaka Allah (الله)umma (Glory to You, O Allah (الله)!) and Salam (peace, safe from each and every evil) will be their greetings therein (Paradise (الجنة))!
وَآخِرُ دَعْوَاهُمْ أَنِ الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ رَبِّ الْعَالَمِينَ
and the close of their request will be: Al-Hamdu Lillahi Rabbil-Alameen (All the praises and thanks are to Allah (الله), the Lord of Alameen (mankind, jinns and all that exists))
10:[11-17] The behavior of the wrong doers towards Allah and His revelations
10:[18-20] Deities other than Allah can neither harm nor benefit you. Mankind was one nation until they invented different creeds
10:11
وَلَوْ يُعَجِّلُ اللَّهُ لِلنَّاسِ الشَّرَّ اسْتِعْجَالَهُمْ بِالْخَيْرِ لَقُضِيَ إِلَيْهِمْ أَجَلُهُمْ ۖ
And were Allah (الله) to hasten for mankind the evil (they invoke for themselves and for their children, etc while in a state of anger) as He hastens for them the good (they invoke) then they would have been ruined
فَنَذَرُ الَّذِينَ لَا يَرْجُونَ لِقَاءَنَا فِي طُغْيَانِهِمْ يَعْمَهُونَ
So We leave those who expect not their meeting with Us, in their trespasses, wandering blindly in distraction(Tafsir At-Tabaree; Vol 11, Page 91)
10:12
وَإِذَا مَسَّ الْإِنْسَانَ الضُّرُّ دَعَانَا لِجَنْبِهِ أَوْ قَاعِدًا أَوْ قَائِمًا
And when harm touches man, he invokes Us, lying down on his side, or sitting or standing
فَلَمَّا كَشَفْنَا عَنْهُ ضُرَّهُ مَرَّ كَأَنْ لَمْ يَدْعُنَا إِلَى ضُرٍّ مَسَّهُ ۚ
But when We have removed his harm from him, he passes on his way as if he had never invoked Us for a harm that touched him!
كَذَلِكَ زُيِّنَ لِلْمُسْرِفِينَ مَا كَانُوا يَعْمَلُونَ
Thus it seems fair to the Musrifoon that which they used to do
10:13
وَلَقَدْ أَهْلَكْنَا الْقُرُونَ مِنْ قَبْلِكُمْ لَمَّا ظَلَمُوا ۙ
And indeed, We destroyed generations before you, when they did wrong
وَجَاءَتْهُمْ رُسُلُهُمْ بِالْبَيِّنَاتِ وَمَا كَانُوا لِيُؤْمِنُوا ۚ
while their Messengers came to them with clear proofs, but they were not such as to believe!
كَذَلِكَ نَجْزِي الْقَوْمَ الْمُجْرِمِينَ
Thus do We requite the people who are Mujrimoon (disbelievers, polytheists, sinners, criminals, etc)
10:14
ثُمَّ جَعَلْنَاكُمْ خَلَائِفَ فِي الْأَرْضِ مِنْ بَعْدِهِمْ لِنَنْظُرَ كَيْفَ تَعْمَلُونَ
Then We made you follow after them, generations after generations in the land, that We might see how you would work!
10:15
وَإِذَا تُتْلَى عَلَيْهِمْ آيَاتُنَا بَيِّنَاتٍ ۙ قَالَ الَّذِينَ لَا يَرْجُونَ لِقَاءَنَا
And when Our Clear Verses are recited unto them, those who hope not for their meeting with Us, say:
ائْتِ بِقُرْآنٍ غَيْرِ هَذَا أَوْ بَدِّلْهُ ۚ
Bring us a Quran (القرآن) other than this, or change it‘
قُلْ مَا يَكُونُ لِي أَنْ أُبَدِّلَهُ مِنْ تِلْقَاءِ نَفْسِي ۖ
Say (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW): ‘It is not for me to change it on my own accord;
إِنْ أَتَّبِعُ إِلَّا مَا يُوحَى إِلَيَّ ۖ
I only follow that which is revealed unto me
إِنِّي أَخَافُ إِنْ عَصَيْتُ رَبِّي عَذَابَ يَوْمٍ عَظِيمٍ
Verily, I fear if I were to disobey my Lord, the torment of the Great Day (ie the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة))‘
10:16
قُلْ لَوْ شَاءَ اللَّهُ مَا تَلَوْتُهُ عَلَيْكُمْ وَلَا أَدْرَاكُمْ بِهِ ۖ
Say (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW): ‘If Allah (الله) had so willed, I should not have recited it to you nor would He have made it known to you
فَقَدْ لَبِثْتُ فِيكُمْ عُمُرًا مِنْ قَبْلِهِ ۚ
Verily, I have stayed amongst you a life time before this
أَفَلَا تَعْقِلُونَ
Have you then no sense?‘
10:17
فَمَنْ أَظْلَمُ مِمَّنِ افْتَرَى عَلَى اللَّهِ كَذِبًا أَوْ كَذَّبَ بِآيَاتِهِ ۚ
So who does more wrong than he who forges a lie against Allah (الله) or denies His Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, etc)?
إِنَّهُ لَا يُفْلِحُ الْمُجْرِمُونَ
Surely, the Mujrimoon (criminals, sinners, disbelievers and polytheists) will never be successful!
10:18
وَيَعْبُدُونَ مِنْ دُونِ اللَّهِ مَا لَا يَضُرُّهُمْ وَلَا يَنْفَعُهُمْ
And they worship besides Allah (الله) things that hurt them not, nor profit them,
وَيَقُولُونَ هَؤُلَاءِ شُفَعَاؤُنَا عِنْدَ اللَّهِ ۚ
and they say: ‘These are our intercessors with Allah (الله)‘
قُلْ أَتُنَبِّئُونَ اللَّهَ بِمَا لَا يَعْلَمُ فِي السَّمَاوَاتِ وَلَا فِي الْأَرْضِ ۚ
Say: ‘Do you inform Allah (الله) of that which He knows not in the heavens and on the earth?‘
سُبْحَانَهُ وَتَعَالَى عَمَّا يُشْرِكُونَ
Glorified and Exalted be He above all that which they associate as partners with Him!
10:19
وَمَا كَانَ النَّاسُ إِلَّا أُمَّةً وَاحِدَةً فَاخْتَلَفُوا ۚ
Mankind were but one community (ie on one religion - Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism), then they differed (later),
وَلَوْلَا كَلِمَةٌ سَبَقَتْ مِنْ رَبِّكَ لَقُضِيَ بَيْنَهُمْ فِيمَا فِيهِ يَخْتَلِفُونَ
and had not it been for a Word that went forth before from your Lord, it would have been settled between them regarding what they differed
10:20
وَيَقُولُونَ لَوْلَا أُنْزِلَ عَلَيْهِ آيَةٌ مِنْ رَبِّهِ ۖ
And they say: ‘How is it that not a sign is sent down on him from his Lord?‘
فَقُلْ إِنَّمَا الْغَيْبُ لِلَّهِ فَانْتَظِرُوا إِنِّي مَعَكُمْ مِنَ الْمُنْتَظِرِينَ
Say: ‘The unseen belongs to Allah (الله) Alone, so wait you, verily I am with you among those who wait (for Allah (الله)s Judgement)‘

10:[24] Example of this worldly life
10:[25-30] Allah invites you to the Home of Peace. Deities whom they worship are not even aware of being worshipped
10:21
وَإِذَا أَذَقْنَا النَّاسَ رَحْمَةً مِنْ بَعْدِ ضَرَّاءَ مَسَّتْهُمْ إِذَا لَهُمْ مَكْرٌ فِي آيَاتِنَا ۚ
And when We let mankind taste of mercy after some adversity has afflicted them, behold! They take to plotting against Our Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, etc)!
قُلِ اللَّهُ أَسْرَعُ مَكْرًا ۚ
Say: ‘Allah (الله) is more Swift in planning!‘
إِنَّ رُسُلَنَا يَكْتُبُونَ مَا تَمْكُرُونَ
Certainly, Our Messengers (angels) record all of that which you plot
10:22
هُوَ الَّذِي يُسَيِّرُكُمْ فِي الْبَرِّ وَالْبَحْرِ ۖ
He it is Who enables you to travel through land and sea,
حَتَّى إِذَا كُنْتُمْ فِي الْفُلْكِ وَجَرَيْنَ بِهِمْ بِرِيحٍ طَيِّبَةٍ وَفَرِحُوا بِهَا
till when you are in the ships and they sail with them with a favourable wind, and they are glad therein,
جَاءَتْهَا رِيحٌ عَاصِفٌ وَجَاءَهُمُ الْمَوْجُ مِنْ كُلِّ مَكَانٍ
then comes a stormy wind and the waves come to them from all sides,
وَظَنُّوا أَنَّهُمْ أُحِيطَ بِهِمْ ۙ دَعَوُا اللَّهَ مُخْلِصِينَ لَهُ الدِّينَ
and they think that they are encircled therein, they invoke Allah (الله), making their Faith pure for Him Alone,
لَئِنْ أَنْجَيْتَنَا مِنْ هَذِهِ لَنَكُونَنَّ مِنَ الشَّاكِرِينَ
saying: ‘If You (Allah (الله)) deliver us from this, we shall truly be of the grateful‘
10:23
فَلَمَّا أَنْجَاهُمْ إِذَا هُمْ يَبْغُونَ فِي الْأَرْضِ بِغَيْرِ الْحَقِّ ۗ
But when He delivered them, behold! They rebel (disobey Allah (الله)) in the earth wrongfully
يَا أَيُّهَا النَّاسُ إِنَّمَا بَغْيُكُمْ عَلَى أَنْفُسِكُمْ ۖ
O mankind! Your rebellion (disobedience to Allah (الله)) is only against your ownselves, -
مَتَاعَ الْحَيَاةِ الدُّنْيَا ۖ ثُمَّ إِلَيْنَا مَرْجِعُكُمْ فَنُنَبِّئُكُمْ بِمَا كُنْتُمْ تَعْمَلُونَ
a brief enjoyment of this worldly life, then (in the end) unto Us is your return, and We shall inform you that which you used to do
10:24
إِنَّمَا مَثَلُ الْحَيَاةِ الدُّنْيَا كَمَاءٍ أَنْزَلْنَاهُ مِنَ السَّمَاءِ
Verily the likeness of (this) worldly life is as the water (rain) which We send down from the sky,
فَاخْتَلَطَ بِهِ نَبَاتُ الْأَرْضِ مِمَّا يَأْكُلُ النَّاسُ وَالْأَنْعَامُ
so by it arises the intermingled produce of the earth of which men and cattle eat
حَتَّى إِذَا أَخَذَتِ الْأَرْضُ زُخْرُفَهَا وَازَّيَّنَتْ وَظَنَّ أَهْلُهَا أَنَّهُمْ قَادِرُونَ عَلَيْهَا
until when the earth is clad with its adornments and is beautified, and its people think that they have all the powers of disposal over it,
أَتَاهَا أَمْرُنَا لَيْلًا أَوْ نَهَارًا فَجَعَلْنَاهَا حَصِيدًا كَأَنْ لَمْ تَغْنَ بِالْأَمْسِ ۚ
Our Command reaches it by night or by day and We make it like a clean-mown harvest, as if it had not flourished yesterday!
كَذَلِكَ نُفَصِّلُ الْآيَاتِ لِقَوْمٍ يَتَفَكَّرُونَ
Thus do We explain the Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, laws, etc) in detail for the people who reflect
10:25
وَاللَّهُ يَدْعُو إِلَى دَارِ السَّلَامِ وَيَهْدِي مَنْ يَشَاءُ إِلَى صِرَاطٍ مُسْتَقِيمٍ
Allah (الله) calls to the home of peace (ie Paradise (الجنة), by accepting Allah (الله)‘s religion of Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism and by doing righteous good deeds and abstaining from polytheism and evil deeds) and guides whom He wills to a Straight Path
10:26
لِلَّذِينَ أَحْسَنُوا الْحُسْنَى وَزِيَادَةٌ ۖ
For those who have done good is the best (reward, ie Paradise (الجنة)) and even more (ie having the honour of glancing at the Countenance of Allah (الله))
وَلَا يَرْهَقُ وُجُوهَهُمْ قَتَرٌ وَلَا ذِلَّةٌ ۚ
Neither darkness nor dust nor any humiliating disgrace shall cover their faces
أُولَئِكَ أَصْحَابُ الْجَنَّةِ ۖ هُمْ فِيهَا خَالِدُونَ
They are the dwellers of Paradise (الجنة), they will abide therein forever
10:27
وَالَّذِينَ كَسَبُوا السَّيِّئَاتِ جَزَاءُ سَيِّئَةٍ بِمِثْلِهَا وَتَرْهَقُهُمْ ذِلَّةٌ ۖ
And those who have earned evil deeds, the recompense of an evil deed is the like thereof, and humiliating disgrace will cover them (their faces)
مَا لَهُمْ مِنَ اللَّهِ مِنْ عَاصِمٍ ۖ
No defender will they have from Allah (الله)
كَأَنَّمَا أُغْشِيَتْ وُجُوهُهُمْ قِطَعًا مِنَ اللَّيْلِ مُظْلِمًا ۚ
Their faces will be covered, as it were, with pieces from the darkness of night
أُولَئِكَ أَصْحَابُ النَّارِ ۖ هُمْ فِيهَا خَالِدُونَ
They are dwellers of the Fire, they will abide therein forever
10:28
وَيَوْمَ نَحْشُرُهُمْ جَمِيعًا ثُمَّ نَقُولُ لِلَّذِينَ أَشْرَكُوا مَكَانَكُمْ أَنْتُمْ وَشُرَكَاؤُكُمْ ۚ
And the Day whereon We shall gather them all together, then We shall say to those who did set partners in worship with Us:
Stop at your place! You and your partners (whom you had worshipped in the worldly life)‘
فَزَيَّلْنَا بَيْنَهُمْ ۖ وَقَالَ شُرَكَاؤُهُمْ مَا كُنْتُمْ إِيَّانَا تَعْبُدُونَ
then We shall separate them, and their (Allah (الله)‘s so-called) partners shall say: ‘It was not us that you used to worship‘
10:29
فَكَفَى بِاللَّهِ شَهِيدًا بَيْنَنَا وَبَيْنَكُمْ إِنْ كُنَّا عَنْ عِبَادَتِكُمْ لَغَافِلِينَ
‘So sufficient is Allah (الله) for a witness between us and you, that We indeed knew nothing of your worship of us‘
10:30
هُنَالِكَ تَبْلُو كُلُّ نَفْسٍ مَا أَسْلَفَتْ ۚ
There! Every person will know (exactly) what (all) he had earned before,
وَرُدُّوا إِلَى اللَّهِ مَوْلَاهُمُ الْحَقِّ ۖ
and they will be brought back to Allah (الله), their rightful Lord (Maula),
وَضَلَّ عَنْهُمْ مَا كَانُوا يَفْتَرُونَ
and their invented false deities will vanish from them
10:[31-36] Reasoning and truth about Allah vs. other gods invented by the mushrikïn
10:[37-40] This Qur‘an is the revelation from Allah
10:31
قُلْ مَنْ يَرْزُقُكُمْ مِنَ السَّمَاءِ وَالْأَرْضِ
Say (O Muhammad (محمد) SAWSAW ): ‘Who provides for you from the sky and from the earth?
أَمَّنْ يَمْلِكُ السَّمْعَ وَالْأَبْصَارَ
Or who owns hearing and sight?
وَمَنْ يُخْرِجُ الْحَيَّ مِنَ الْمَيِّتِ وَيُخْرِجُ الْمَيِّتَ مِنَ الْحَيِّ
And who brings out the living from the dead and brings out the dead from the living?
وَمَنْ يُدَبِّرُ الْأَمْرَ ۚ
And who disposes the affairs?‘
فَسَيَقُولُونَ اللَّهُ
They will say: ‘Allah (الله)‘
فَقُلْ أَفَلَا تَتَّقُونَ
Say: ‘Will you not then be afraid of Allah (الله)‘s Punishment (for setting up rivals in worship with Allah (الله))?‘
10:32
فَذَلِكُمُ اللَّهُ رَبُّكُمُ الْحَقُّ ۖ
Such is Allah (الله), your Lord in truth
فَمَاذَا بَعْدَ الْحَقِّ إِلَّا الضَّلَالُ ۖ
So after the truth, what else can there be, save error?
فَأَنَّى تُصْرَفُونَ
How then are you turned away?
10:33
كَذَلِكَ حَقَّتْ كَلِمَتُ رَبِّكَ عَلَى الَّذِينَ فَسَقُوا أَنَّهُمْ لَا يُؤْمِنُونَ
Thus is the Word of your Lord justified against those who rebel (disobey Allah (الله)) that they will not believe (in the Oneness of Allah (الله) and in Muhammad (محمد) SAW as the Messenger of Allah (الله))
10:34
قُلْ هَلْ مِنْ شُرَكَائِكُمْ مَنْ يَبْدَأُ الْخَلْقَ ثُمَّ يُعِيدُهُ ۚ
Say: ‘Is there of your (Allah (الله)‘s so-called) partners one that originates the creation and then repeats it?‘
قُلِ اللَّهُ يَبْدَأُ الْخَلْقَ ثُمَّ يُعِيدُهُ ۖ فَأَنَّى تُؤْفَكُونَ
Say: ‘Allah (الله) originates the creation and then He repeats it Then how are you deluded away (from the truth)?‘
10:35
قُلْ هَلْ مِنْ شُرَكَائِكُمْ مَنْ يَهْدِي إِلَى الْحَقِّ ۚ
Say: ‘Is there of your (Allah (الله)‘s so-called) partners one that guides to the truth?‘
قُلِ اللَّهُ يَهْدِي لِلْحَقِّ ۗ
Say: ‘It is Allah (الله) Who guides to the truth
أَفَمَنْ يَهْدِي إِلَى الْحَقِّ أَحَقُّ أَنْ يُتَّبَعَ أَمَّنْ لَا يَهِدِّي إِلَّا أَنْ يُهْدَى ۖ
Is then He, Who gives guidance to the truth, more worthy to be followed, or he who finds not guidance (himself) unless he is guided?
فَمَا لَكُمْ كَيْفَ تَحْكُمُونَ
Then, what is the matter with you? How judge you?‘
10:36
وَمَا يَتَّبِعُ أَكْثَرُهُمْ إِلَّا ظَنًّا ۚ
And most of them follow nothing but conjecture
إِنَّ الظَّنَّ لَا يُغْنِي مِنَ الْحَقِّ شَيْئًا ۚ
Certainly, conjecture can be of no avail against the truth
إِنَّ اللَّهَ عَلِيمٌ بِمَا يَفْعَلُونَ
Surely, Allah (الله) is All-Aware (Al-‘Alee (ٱلْعَلِيُّ)m (ٱلْعَلِيمُ)) of what they do
10:37
وَمَا كَانَ هَذَا الْقُرْآنُ أَنْ يُفْتَرَى مِنْ دُونِ اللَّهِ
And this Quran (القرآن) is not such as could ever be produced by other than Allah (الله) (Lord of the heavens and the earth),
وَلَكِنْ تَصْدِيقَ الَّذِي بَيْنَ يَدَيْهِ وَتَفْصِيلَ الْكِتَابِ لَا رَيْبَ فِيهِ مِنْ رَبِّ الْعَالَمِينَ
but it is a confirmation of (the revelation) which was before it (ie the Taurat (Torah (التوراة)), and the Injil (Injeel (الإنجيل)), etc), and a full explanation of the Book (ie laws and orders, etc, decreed for mankind) - wherein there is no doubt from the the Lord of the Alameen (mankind, jinns,and all that exists)
10:38
أَمْ يَقُولُونَ افْتَرَاهُ ۖ
Or do they say: ‘He (Muhammad (محمد) SAW) has forged it?‘
قُلْ فَأْتُوا بِسُورَةٍ مِثْلِهِ وَادْعُوا مَنِ اسْتَطَعْتُمْ مِنْ دُونِ اللَّهِ إِنْ كُنْتُمْ صَادِقِينَ
Say: ‘Bring then a Soorah (chapter) like unto it, and call upon whomsoever you can, besides Allah (الله), if you are truthful!‘
10:39
بَلْ كَذَّبُوا بِمَا لَمْ يُحِيطُوا بِعِلْمِهِ وَلَمَّا يَأْتِهِمْ تَأْوِيلُهُ ۚ
Nay, they deny that; the knowledge whereof they could not compass and whereof the interpretation has not yet come unto them
كَذَلِكَ كَذَّبَ الَّذِينَ مِنْ قَبْلِهِمْ ۖ
Thus those before them did deny
فَانْظُرْ كَيْفَ كَانَ عَاقِبَةُ الظَّالِمِينَ
Then see what was the end of the Zalimoon (polytheists and wrong-doers, etc)!
10:40
وَمِنْهُمْ مَنْ يُؤْمِنُ بِهِ وَمِنْهُمْ مَنْ لَا يُؤْمِنُ بِهِ ۚ
And of them there are some who believe therein, and of them there are some who believe not therein,
وَرَبُّكَ أَعْلَمُ بِالْمُفْسِدِينَ
and your Lord is All-Aware (Al-‘Alee (ٱلْعَلِيُّ)m (ٱلْعَلِيمُ)) of the Mufsidoon (evil-doers, liars, etc)
10:[41-46] Those who disbelieve in this Qur‘an shall be the losers in the hereafter
10:[47-53] Every nation was sent a Rasool for their guidance
10:41
وَإِنْ كَذَّبُوكَ فَقُلْ
And if they belie you, say:
لِي عَمَلِي وَلَكُمْ عَمَلُكُمْ أَنْتُمْ بَرِيئُونَ مِمَّا أَعْمَلُ وَأَنَا بَرِيءٌ مِمَّا تَعْمَلُونَ
For me are my deeds and for you are your deeds! You are innocent of what I do, and I am innocent of what you do!‘
10:42
وَمِنْهُمْ مَنْ يَسْتَمِعُونَ إِلَيْكَ ۚ
And among them are some who listen to you,
أَفَأَنْتَ تُسْمِعُ الصُّمَّ وَلَوْ كَانُوا لَا يَعْقِلُونَ
but can you make the deaf to hear, even though they apprehend not?
10:43
وَمِنْهُمْ مَنْ يَنْظُرُ إِلَيْكَ
And among them are some who look at you,
أَفَأَنْتَ تَهْدِي الْعُمْيَ وَلَوْ كَانُوا لَا يُبْصِرُونَ
but can you guide the blind, even though they see not?
10:44
إِنَّ اللَّهَ لَا يَظْلِمُ النَّاسَ شَيْئًا وَلَكِنَّ النَّاسَ أَنْفُسَهُمْ يَظْلِمُونَ
Truly! Allah (الله) wrongs not mankind in aught; but mankind wrong themselves
10:45
وَيَوْمَ يَحْشُرُهُمْ كَأَنْ لَمْ يَلْبَثُوا إِلَّا سَاعَةً مِنَ النَّهَارِ
And on the Day when He shall gather (resurrect) them together, (it will be) as if they had not stayed (in the life of this world and graves, etc) but an hour of a day
يَتَعَارَفُونَ بَيْنَهُمْ ۚ
They will recognise each other
قَدْ خَسِرَ الَّذِينَ كَذَّبُوا بِلِقَاءِ اللَّهِ وَمَا كَانُوا مُهْتَدِينَ
Ruined indeed will be those who denied the meeting with Allah (الله), and were not guided
10:46
وَإِمَّا نُرِيَنَّكَ بَعْضَ الَّذِي نَعِدُهُمْ أَوْ نَتَوَفَّيَنَّكَ فَإِلَيْنَا مَرْجِعُهُمْ
Whether We show you (in your lifetime, O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) some of what We promise them (the torment), - or We cause you to die, - still unto Us is their return,
ثُمَّ اللَّهُ شَهِيدٌ عَلَى مَا يَفْعَلُونَ
and moreover Allah (الله) is Witness over what they used to do
10:47
وَلِكُلِّ أُمَّةٍ رَسُولٌ ۖ
And for every Ummah (a community or a nation), there is a Messenger;
فَإِذَا جَاءَ رَسُولُهُمْ قُضِيَ بَيْنَهُمْ بِالْقِسْطِ وَهُمْ لَا يُظْلَمُونَ
when their Messenger comes, the matter will be judged between them with justice, and they will not be wronged
10:48
وَيَقُولُونَ مَتَى هَذَا الْوَعْدُ إِنْ كُنْتُمْ صَادِقِينَ
And they say: ‘When will be this promise (the torment or the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة)), - if you speak the truth?‘
10:49
قُلْ لَا أَمْلِكُ لِنَفْسِي ضَرًّا وَلَا نَفْعًا إِلَّا مَا شَاءَ اللَّهُ ۗ
Say (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW): ‘I have no power over any harm or profit to myself except what Allah (الله) may will
لِكُلِّ أُمَّةٍ أَجَلٌ ۚ
For every Ummah (a community or a nation), there is a term appointed;
إِذَا جَاءَ أَجَلُهُمْ فَلَا يَسْتَأْخِرُونَ سَاعَةً ۖ وَلَا يَسْتَقْدِمُونَ
when their term is reached, neither can they delay it nor can they advance it an hour (or a moment)‘(Tafsir Al- Qurtubee)
10:50
قُلْ أَرَأَيْتُمْ إِنْ أَتَاكُمْ عَذَابُهُ بَيَاتًا أَوْ نَهَارًا مَاذَا يَسْتَعْجِلُ مِنْهُ الْمُجْرِمُونَ
Say: ‘Tell me, - if His torment should come to you by night or by day, - which portion thereof would the Mujrimoon (disbelievers, polytheists, sinners, criminals) hasten on ?‘
10:51
‏أَثُمَّ إِذَا مَا وَقَعَ آمَنْتُمْ بِهِ ۚ
Is it then, that when it has actually befallen, that you will believe in it?
آلْآنَ وَقَدْ كُنْتُمْ بِهِ تَسْتَعْجِلُونَ
What! Now (you believe)? And you used (aforetime) to hasten it on!‘
10:52
ثُمَّ قِيلَ لِلَّذِينَ ظَلَمُوا ذُوقُوا عَذَابَ الْخُلْدِ
Then it will be said to them who wronged themselves: ‘Taste you the everlasting torment!
هَلْ تُجْزَوْنَ إِلَّا بِمَا كُنْتُمْ تَكْسِبُونَ
Are you recompensed (aught) save what you used to earn?‘
10:53
وَيَسْتَنْبِئُونَكَ أَحَقٌّ هُوَ ۖ
And they ask you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) to inform them (saying): ‘Is it true (ie the torment and the establishment of the Hour; - the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة))?‘
قُلْ إِي وَرَبِّي إِنَّهُ لَحَقٌّ ۖ وَمَا أَنْتُمْ بِمُعْجِزِينَ
Say: ‘Yes! By my Lord! It is the very truth! and you cannot escape from it!‘
10:[54-56] On the Day of Judgement, there shall be no way out for the unbelievers
10:[57-60] Al-Qur‘an is a mercy, blessing, and cure for the problems of mankind
10:54
وَلَوْ أَنَّ لِكُلِّ نَفْسٍ ظَلَمَتْ مَا فِي الْأَرْضِ لَافْتَدَتْ بِهِ
And if every person who had wronged (by disbelieving in Allah (الله) and by worshipping others besides Allah (الله)), possessed all that is on earth, and sought to ransom himself therewith (it will not be accepted),
وَأَسَرُّوا النَّدَامَةَ لَمَّا رَأَوُا الْعَذَابَ ۖ
and they would feel in their hearts regret when they see the torment,
وَقُضِيَ بَيْنَهُمْ بِالْقِسْطِ ۚ
and they will be judged with justice,
وَهُمْ لَا يُظْلَمُونَ
and no wrong will be done unto them
10:55
أَلَا إِنَّ لِلَّهِ مَا فِي السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضِ ۗ
No doubt, surely, all that is in the heavens and the earth belongs to Allah (الله)
أَلَا إِنَّ وَعْدَ اللَّهِ حَقٌّ وَلَكِنَّ أَكْثَرَهُمْ لَا يَعْلَمُونَ
No doubt, surely, Allah (الله)‘s Promise is true But most of them know not
10:56
هُوَ يُحْيِي وَيُمِيتُ وَإِلَيْهِ تُرْجَعُونَ
It is He Who gives life, and causes death, and to Him you (all) shall return
10:57
يَا أَيُّهَا النَّاسُ قَدْ جَاءَتْكُمْ مَوْعِظَةٌ مِنْ رَبِّكُمْ
O mankind! There has come to you a good advice from your Lord (ie the Quran (القرآن), ordering all that is good and forbidding all that is evil),
وَشِفَاءٌ لِمَا فِي الصُّدُورِ وَهُدًى وَرَحْمَةٌ لِلْمُؤْمِنِينَ
and a healing for that (disease of ignorance, doubt, hypocrisy and differences, etc) in your breasts, - a guidance and a mercy (explaining lawful and unlawful things, etc) for the believers
10:58
‏قُلْ بِفَضْلِ اللَّهِ وَبِرَحْمَتِهِ فَبِذَلِكَ فَلْيَفْرَحُوا
Say: ‘In the Bounty of Allah (الله), and in His Mercy (ie Islam (الإسلام) and the Quran (القرآن)); -therein let them rejoice‘
هُوَ خَيْرٌ مِمَّا يَجْمَعُونَ
That is better than what (the wealth) they amass
10:59
قُلْ أَرَأَيْتُمْ مَا أَنْزَلَ اللَّهُ لَكُمْ مِنْ رِزْقٍ فَجَعَلْتُمْ مِنْهُ حَرَامًا وَحَلَالًا
Say (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW to these polytheists): ‘Tell me, what provision Allah (الله) has sent down to you! And you have made of it lawful and unlawful‘
قُلْ آللَّهُ أَذِنَ لَكُمْ ۖ أَمْ عَلَى اللَّهِ تَفْتَرُونَ
Say (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW): ‘Has Allah (الله) permitted you (to do so), or do you invent a lie against Allah (الله)?‘
10:60
وَمَا ظَنُّ الَّذِينَ يَفْتَرُونَ عَلَى اللَّهِ الْكَذِبَ يَوْمَ الْقِيَامَةِ ۗ
And what think those who invent lies against Allah (الله), on the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة)?(ie Do they think that they will be forgiven and excused! Nay, they will have an eternal punishment in the Fire of Hell (جهنم))
إِنَّ اللَّهَ لَذُو فَضْلٍ عَلَى النَّاسِ وَلَكِنَّ أَكْثَرَهُمْ لَا يَشْكُرُونَ
Truly, Allah (الله) is full of Bounty to mankind, but most of them are ungrateful
10:[61-65] Whatever you do, Allah is a witness to it
10:[66-70] The mushrikïn are wrong, they follow nothing but conjecture, preach nothing but falsehood
10:61
وَمَا تَكُونُ فِي شَأْنٍ وَمَا تَتْلُو مِنْهُ مِنْ قُرْآنٍ وَلَا تَعْمَلُونَ مِنْ عَمَلٍ
Whatever you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) may be doing, and whatever portion you may be reciting from the Quran (القرآن), - and whatever deed you (mankind) may be doing (good or evil),
إِلَّا كُنَّا عَلَيْكُمْ شُهُودًا إِذْ تُفِيضُونَ فِيهِ ۚ
We are Witness thereof, when you are doing it

And be not one of those who belie the Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, etc) of Allah (الله), for then you shall be one of the losers
10:96
إِنَّ الَّذِينَ حَقَّتْ عَلَيْهِمْ كَلِمَتُ رَبِّكَ لَا يُؤْمِنُونَ
Truly! Those, against whom the Word (Wrath) of your Lord has been justified, will not believe
10:97
وَلَوْ جَاءَتْهُمْ كُلُّ آيَةٍ حَتَّى يَرَوُا الْعَذَابَ الْأَلِيمَ
Even if every sign should come to them, - until they see the painful torment
10:98
فَلَوْلَا كَانَتْ قَرْيَةٌ آمَنَتْ فَنَفَعَهَا إِيمَانُهَا
Was there any town (community) that believed (after seeing the punishment), and its Faith (at that moment) saved it (from the punishment)?
إِلَّا قَوْمَ يُونُسَ لَمَّا آمَنُوا كَشَفْنَا عَنْهُمْ عَذَابَ الْخِزْيِ فِي الْحَيَاةِ الدُّنْيَا وَمَتَّعْنَاهُمْ إِلَى حِينٍ
(The answer is none,)
except the people of Yoonus (Jonah); when they believed, We removed from them the torment of disgrace in the life of the (present) world, and permitted them to enjoy for a while
10:99
وَلَوْ شَاءَ رَبُّكَ لَآمَنَ مَنْ فِي الْأَرْضِ كُلُّهُمْ جَمِيعًا ۚ
And had your Lord willed, those on earth would have believed, all of them together
أَفَأَنْتَ تُكْرِهُ النَّاسَ حَتَّى يَكُونُوا مُؤْمِنِينَ
So, will you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) then compel mankind, until they become believers
10:100
وَمَا كَانَ لِنَفْسٍ أَنْ تُؤْمِنَ إِلَّا بِإِذْنِ اللَّهِ ۚ
It is not for any person to believe, except by the Leave of Allah (الله),
وَيَجْعَلُ الرِّجْسَ عَلَى الَّذِينَ لَا يَعْقِلُونَ
and He will put the wrath on those who are heedless
10:101
قُلِ انْظُرُوا مَاذَا فِي السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضِ ۚ
Say: ‘Behold all that is in the heavens and the earth,‘
وَمَا تُغْنِي الْآيَاتُ وَالنُّذُرُ عَنْ قَوْمٍ لَا يُؤْمِنُونَ
but neither Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, etc) nor warners benefit those who believe not
10:102
فَهَلْ يَنْتَظِرُونَ إِلَّا مِثْلَ أَيَّامِ الَّذِينَ خَلَوْا مِنْ قَبْلِهِمْ ۚ
Then do they wait for (anything) save for (destruction) like the days of the men who passed away before them?
قُلْ فَانْتَظِرُوا إِنِّي مَعَكُمْ مِنَ الْمُنْتَظِرِينَ
Say: ‘Wait then, I am (too) with you among those who wait‘
10:103
ثُمَّ نُنَجِّي رُسُلَنَا وَالَّذِينَ آمَنُوا ۚ
Then (in the end) We save Our Messengers and those who believe!
كَذَلِكَ حَقًّا عَلَيْنَا نُنْجِ الْمُؤْمِنِينَ
Thus it is incumbent upon Us to save the believers
10:[104-107] No one other than Allah can harm or benefit you
10:[108-109] Declare that guidance has come – now to follow or not to follow is your choice
10:104
قُلْ يَا أَيُّهَا النَّاسُ إِنْ كُنْتُمْ فِي شَكٍّ مِنْ دِينِي فَلَا أَعْبُدُ الَّذِينَ تَعْبُدُونَ مِنْ دُونِ اللَّهِ
Say (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW): ‘O you mankind! If you are in doubt as to my religion (Islam (الإسلام)), then (know that) I will never worship those whom you worship, besides Allah (الله)
وَلَكِنْ أَعْبُدُ اللَّهَ الَّذِي يَتَوَفَّاكُمْ ۖ وَأُمِرْتُ أَنْ أَكُونَ مِنَ الْمُؤْمِنِينَ
But I worship Allah (الله) Who causes you to die, I am commanded to be one of the believers
10:105
وَأَنْ أَقِمْ وَجْهَكَ لِلدِّينِ حَنِيفًا وَلَا تَكُونَنَّ مِنَ الْمُشْرِكِينَ
And (it is inspired to me): Direct your face (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) entirely towards the religion Hanifa (Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism, ie to worship none but Allah (الله) Alone), and never be one of the Mushrikoon (those who ascribe partners to Allah (الله), polytheists, idolaters, disbelievers in the Oneness of Allah (الله), and those who worship others along with Allah (الله))
10:106
وَلَا تَدْعُ مِنْ دُونِ اللَّهِ مَا لَا يَنْفَعُكَ وَلَا يَضُرُّكَ ۖ
And invoke not besides Allah (الله), any that will neither profit you, nor hurt you,
فَإِنْ فَعَلْتَ فَإِنَّكَ إِذًا مِنَ الظَّالِمِينَ
but if (in case) you did so, you shall certainly be one of the Zalimoon (polytheists and wrong-doers)‘
10:107
وَإِنْ يَمْسَسْكَ اللَّهُ بِضُرٍّ فَلَا كَاشِفَ لَهُ إِلَّا هُوَ ۖ
And if Allah (الله) touches you with hurt, there is none who can remove it but He;
وَإِنْ يُرِدْكَ بِخَيْرٍ فَلَا رَادَّ لِفَضْلِهِ ۚ
and if He intends any good for you, there is none who can repel His Favour
يُصِيبُ بِهِ مَنْ يَشَاءُ مِنْ عِبَادِهِ ۚ
which He causes it to reach whomsoever of His slaves He will
وَهُوَ الْغَفُورُ الرَّحِيمُ
And He is the Oft-Forgiving, Most Merciful (Ar-Raheem (ٱلْرَّحِيْمُ))
10:108
قُلْ يَا أَيُّهَا النَّاسُ قَدْ جَاءَكُمُ الْحَقُّ مِنْ رَبِّكُمْ ۖ
Say: ‘O you mankind! Now truth (ie the Quran (القرآن) and Prophet Muhammad (محمد) SAW), has come to you from your Lord
فَمَنِ اهْتَدَى فَإِنَّمَا يَهْتَدِي لِنَفْسِهِ ۖ
So whosoever receives guidance, he does so for the good of his own self,
وَمَنْ ضَلَّ فَإِنَّمَا يَضِلُّ عَلَيْهَا ۖ
and whosoever goes astray, he does so to his own loss,
وَمَا أَنَا عَلَيْكُمْ بِوَكِيلٍ
and I am not (set) over you as a Wakeel (disposer of affairs to oblige you for guidance)‘
10:109
وَاتَّبِعْ مَا يُوحَى إِلَيْكَ
And (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW), follow the inspiration sent unto you,
وَاصْبِرْ حَتَّى يَحْكُمَ اللَّهُ وَهُوَ خَيْرُ الْحَاكِمِينَ
and be patient till Allah (الله) gives judgement. And He is the Best of judges.*********
