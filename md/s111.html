Al-Masad
The Plaited Rope, The Palm Fibre, The Twisted Strands
111:[1-5] Curse of Allah on Abu Lahab and his wife
111:1
تَبَّتۡ يَدَآ أَبِى لَهَبٍ۬ وَتَبَّ
Perish the two hands of Abû Lahab (an uncle of the Prophet), and perish he!
111:2
مَآ أَغۡنَىٰ عَنۡهُ مَالُهُ ۥ وَمَا ڪَسَبَ
His wealth and his children (etc) will not benefit him!
111:3
سَيَصۡلَىٰ نَارً۬ا ذَاتَ لَهَبٍ۬
He will be burnt in a Fire of blazing flames!
111:4
وَٱمۡرَأَتُهُ ۥ حَمَّالَةَ ٱلۡحَطَبِ
And his wife too, who carries wood.
(thorns of Sadan which she used to put on the way of the Prophet, or use to slander him)
111:5
فِى جِيدِهَا حَبۡلٌ۬ مِّن مَّسَدِۭ
In her neck is a twisted rope of Masad (palm fibre).*********
