An-Naml
The Ant, The Ants
27:[1-6] The Qur‘an is a Guide and Good News to the Believers
27:[7-14] The story of the Prophet Musa‘s selection as a Rasool. Nine signs were shown to Fir‘on but he still disbelieved and incurred Allah‘s punishment
27:1
طسٓ‌ۚ
Ta Seen.(These letters are one of the miracles of the Quran (القرآن), and none but Allah (الله) (Alone) knows their meanings).
تِلۡكَ ءَايَـٰتُ ٱلۡقُرۡءَانِ وَڪِتَابٍ۬ مُّبِينٍ
These are the Verses of the Quran (القرآن), and (it is) a Book (that makes things) clear;
27:2
هُدً۬ى وَبُشۡرَىٰ لِلۡمُؤۡمِنِينَ
A guide (to the Right Path); and glad tidings for the believers (who believe in the Oneness of Allah (الله) (i.e. Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism)).
27:3
ٱلَّذِينَ يُقِيمُونَ ٱلصَّلَوٰةَ وَيُؤۡتُونَ ٱلزَّڪَوٰةَ وَهُم بِٱلۡأَخِرَةِ هُمۡ يُوقِنُونَ
Those who perform AsSalat (IqamatasSalat) and give Zakat and they believe with certainty in the Hereafter (resurrection, recompense of their good and bad deeds, Paradise (الجنة) and Hell (جهنم), etc.).
27:4
إِنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ لَا يُؤۡمِنُونَ بِٱلۡأَخِرَةِ زَيَّنَّا لَهُمۡ أَعۡمَـٰلَهُمۡ فَهُمۡ يَعۡمَهُونَ
Verily, those who believe not in the Hereafter, We have made their deeds fair-seeming to them, so they wander about blindly.
27:5
أُوْلَـٰٓٮِٕكَ ٱلَّذِينَ لَهُمۡ سُوٓءُ ٱلۡعَذَابِ وَهُمۡ فِى ٱلۡأَخِرَةِ هُمُ ٱلۡأَخۡسَرُونَ
They are those for whom there will be an evil torment (in this world). And in the Hereafter they will be the greatest losers.
27:6
وَإِنَّكَ لَتُلَقَّى ٱلۡقُرۡءَانَ مِن لَّدُنۡ حَكِيمٍ عَلِيمٍ
And verily, you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) are receiving the Quran (القرآن) from the One, All-Wise (Al-Hakeem (ٱلْحَكِيمُ)), All-Knowing (Al-‘Alee (ٱلْعَلِيُّ)m (ٱلْعَلِيمُ)).
27:7
إِذۡ قَالَ مُوسَىٰ لِأَهۡلِهِۦۤ إِنِّىٓ ءَانَسۡتُ نَارً۬ا
(Remember) when Musa (Moosa (موسى)) said to his household: ‘Verily! I have seen a fire,
سَـَٔاتِيكُم مِّنۡہَا بِخَبَرٍ أَوۡ ءَاتِيكُم بِشِہَابٍ۬ قَبَسٍ۬ لَّعَلَّكُمۡ تَصۡطَلُونَ
I will bring you from there some information, or I will bring you a burning brand, that you may warm yourselves.‘
27:8
فَلَمَّا جَآءَهَا نُودِىَ أَنۢ بُورِكَ مَن فِى ٱلنَّارِ وَمَنۡ حَوۡلَهَا
But when he came to it, he was called: ‘Blessed is whosoever is in the fire, and whosoever is round about it!
وَسُبۡحَـٰنَ ٱللَّهِ رَبِّ ٱلۡعَـٰلَمِينَ
And glorified be Allah (الله), the Lord of the Alameen (mankind, jinns and all that exists).
27:9
يَـٰمُوسَىٰٓ إِنَّهُ ۥۤ أَنَا ٱللَّهُ ٱلۡعَزِيزُ ٱلۡحَكِيمُ
O Musa (Moosa (موسى))! Verily! It is I, Allah (الله), the All-Mighty, the All-Wise (Al-Hakeem (ٱلْحَكِيمُ)).
27:10
وَأَلۡقِ عَصَاكَ‌ۚ
And throw down your stick!‘
فَلَمَّا رَءَاهَا تَہۡتَزُّ كَأَنَّہَا جَآنٌّ۬ وَلَّىٰ مُدۡبِرً۬ا وَلَمۡ يُعَقِّبۡ‌ۚ
But when he saw it moving as if it were a snake, he turned in flight, and did not look back.
يَـٰمُوسَىٰ لَا تَخَفۡ إِنِّى لَا يَخَافُ لَدَىَّ ٱلۡمُرۡسَلُونَ
(It was said): ‘O Musa (Moosa (موسى))! Fear not, verily! The Messengers fear not in front of Me.
27:11
إِلَّا مَن ظَلَمَ ثُمَّ بَدَّلَ حُسۡنَۢا بَعۡدَ سُوٓءٍ۬ فَإِنِّى غَفُورٌ۬ رَّحِيمٌ۬
Except him who has done wrong and afterwards has changed evil for good, then surely, I am Oft-Forgiving, Most Merciful (Ar-Raheem (ٱلْرَّحِيْمُ)).
27:12
وَأَدۡخِلۡ يَدَكَ فِى جَيۡبِكَ تَخۡرُجۡ بَيۡضَآءَ مِنۡ غَيۡرِ سُوٓءٍ۬‌ۖ
And put your hand into your bosom, it will come forth white without hurt.
فِى تِسۡعِ ءَايَـٰتٍ إِلَىٰ فِرۡعَوۡنَ وَقَوۡمِهِۦۤ‌ۚ
(These are) among the nine signs (you will take) to Firaun (Pharaoh (فرعون)) and his people,
إِنَّہُمۡ كَانُواْ قَوۡمً۬ا فَـٰسِقِينَ
they are a people who are the Fasiqoon (rebellious, disobedient to Allah (الله)).
27:13
فَلَمَّا جَآءَتۡہُمۡ ءَايَـٰتُنَا مُبۡصِرَةً۬ قَالُواْ هَـٰذَا سِحۡرٌ۬ مُّبِينٌ۬
But when Our Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, etc.) came to them, clear to see, they said: ‘This is a manifest magic.‘
27:14
وَجَحَدُواْ بِہَا وَٱسۡتَيۡقَنَتۡهَآ أَنفُسُہُمۡ ظُلۡمً۬ا وَعُلُوًّ۬ا‌ۚ
And they belied them (those Ayat) wrongfully and arrogantly, though their ownselves were convinced thereof.(i.e. those (Ayat) are from Allah (الله), and Musa (Moosa (موسى)) is the Messenger of Allah (الله) in truth, but they disliked to obey Musa (Moosa (موسى)), and hated to believe in his Message of Monotheism)
فَٱنظُرۡ كَيۡفَ كَانَ عَـٰقِبَةُ ٱلۡمُفۡسِدِينَ
So see what was the end of the Mufsidoon (disbelievers, disobedient to Allah (الله), evil-doers, liars.).
27:[15-19] The story of the Prophet Sulaimãn, to whom Allah gave rule over jinns, men, birds and winds
27:[20-31] The hoopoe brought him the news about the Queen of Sheba. Letter from King Sulaimãn to the Queen of Sheba
27:15
وَلَقَدۡ ءَاتَيۡنَا دَاوُ ۥدَ وَسُلَيۡمَـٰنَ عِلۡمً۬ا‌ۖ
And indeed We gave knowledge to Dawood (داود) (David) and Suleiman (سليمان) (Solomon),
وَقَالَا ٱلۡحَمۡدُ لِلَّهِ ٱلَّذِى فَضَّلَنَا عَلَىٰ كَثِيرٍ۬ مِّنۡ عِبَادِهِ ٱلۡمُؤۡمِنِينَ
and they both said: ‘All the praises and thanks be to Allah (الله), Who has preferred us above many of His believing slaves!‘
27:16
وَوَرِثَ سُلَيۡمَـٰنُ دَاوُ ۥدَ‌ۖ
And Suleiman (سليمان) (Solomon) inherited (the knowledge of) Dawood (داود) (David).
وَقَالَ يَـٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلنَّاسُ عُلِّمۡنَا مَنطِقَ ٱلطَّيۡرِ وَأُوتِينَا مِن كُلِّ شَىۡءٍ‌ۖ
He said: ‘O mankind! We have been taught the language of birds, and on us have been bestowed all things.
إِنَّ هَـٰذَا لَهُوَ ٱلۡفَضۡلُ ٱلۡمُبِينُ
This, verily, is an evident grace (from Allah (الله)).‘
27:17
وَحُشِرَ لِسُلَيۡمَـٰنَ جُنُودُهُ ۥ مِنَ ٱلۡجِنِّ وَٱلۡإِنسِ وَٱلطَّيۡرِ فَهُمۡ يُوزَعُونَ
And there were gathered before Suleiman (سليمان) (Solomon) his hosts of jinns and men, and birds, and they all were set in battle order (marching forwards).
27:18
حَتَّىٰٓ إِذَآ أَتَوۡاْ عَلَىٰ وَادِ ٱلنَّمۡلِ قَالَتۡ نَمۡلَةٌ۬ يَـٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلنَّمۡلُ ٱدۡخُلُواْ مَسَـٰكِنَڪُمۡ
Till, when they came to the valley of the ants, one of the ants said: ‘O ants! Enter your dwellings,
لَا يَحۡطِمَنَّكُمۡ سُلَيۡمَـٰنُ وَجُنُودُهُ ۥ وَهُمۡ لَا يَشۡعُرُونَ
lest Suleiman (سليمان) (Solomon) and his hosts crush you, while they perceive not.‘
27:19
فَتَبَسَّمَ ضَاحِكً۬ا مِّن قَوۡلِهَا
So he (Suleiman (سليمان) (Solomon)) smiled, amused at her speech and said:
وَقَالَ رَبِّ أَوۡزِعۡنِىٓ أَنۡ أَشۡكُرَ نِعۡمَتَكَ ٱلَّتِىٓ أَنۡعَمۡتَ عَلَىَّ وَعَلَىٰ وَٲلِدَىَّ
My Lord! Inspire and bestow upon me the power and ability that I may be grateful for Your Favours which You have bestowed on me and on my parents,
وَأَنۡ أَعۡمَلَ صَـٰلِحً۬ا تَرۡضَٮٰهُ
and that I may do righteous good deeds that will please You,
وَأَدۡخِلۡنِى بِرَحۡمَتِكَ فِى عِبَادِكَ ٱلصَّـٰلِحِينَ
and admit me by Your Mercy among Your righteous slaves.‘
27:20
وَتَفَقَّدَ ٱلطَّيۡرَ فَقَالَ مَا لِىَ لَآ أَرَى ٱلۡهُدۡهُدَ
He inspected the birds, and said: ‘What is the matter that I see not the hoopoe?
أَمۡ ڪَانَ مِنَ ٱلۡغَآٮِٕبِينَ
Or is he among the absentees?
27:21
لَأُعَذِّبَنَّهُ ۥ عَذَابً۬ا شَدِيدًا أَوۡ لَأَاْذۡبَحَنَّهُ ۥۤ
I will surely punish him with a severe torment, or slaughter him,
أَوۡ لَيَأۡتِيَنِّى بِسُلۡطَـٰنٍ۬ مُّبِينٍ۬
unless he brings me a clear reason.‘
27:22
فَمَكَثَ غَيۡرَ بَعِيدٍ۬ فَقَالَ أَحَطتُ بِمَا لَمۡ تُحِطۡ بِهِۦ
But the hoopoe stayed not long, he (came up and) said: ‘I have grasped (the knowledge of a thing) which you have not grasped
وَجِئۡتُكَ مِن سَبَإِۭ بِنَبَإٍ۬ يَقِينٍ
and I have come to you from Saba (Sheba (سبأ)) with true news.
27:23
إِنِّى وَجَدتُّ ٱمۡرَأَةً۬ تَمۡلِڪُهُمۡ
I found a woman ruling over them,
وَأُوتِيَتۡ مِن ڪُلِّ شَىۡءٍ۬ وَلَهَا عَرۡشٌ عَظِيمٌ۬
and she has been given all things that could be possessed by any ruler of the earth, and she has a great throne.
27:24
وَجَدتُّهَا وَقَوۡمَهَا يَسۡجُدُونَ لِلشَّمۡسِ مِن دُونِ ٱللَّهِ
I found her and her people worshipping the sun instead of Allah (الله),
وَزَيَّنَ لَهُمُ ٱلشَّيۡطَـٰنُ أَعۡمَـٰلَهُمۡ فَصَدَّهُمۡ عَنِ ٱلسَّبِيلِ فَهُمۡ لَا يَهۡتَدُونَ
and Shaitan (Satan (الشيطان)) has made their deeds fair-seeming to them, and has barred them from (Allah (الله)‘s) Way, so they have no guidance,‘
27:25
أَلَّا يَسۡجُدُواْ لِلَّهِ ٱلَّذِى يُخۡرِجُ ٱلۡخَبۡءَ فِى ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٲتِ وَٱلۡأَرۡضِ
l-La (this word has two interpretations)
(As Shaitan (Satan (الشيطان)) has barred them from Allah (الله)‘s Way) so that they do not worship (prostrate before) Allah (الله), or
So that they may worship (prostrate before) Allah (الله),
Who brings to light what is hidden in the heavens and the earth,
وَيَعۡلَمُ مَا تُخۡفُونَ وَمَا تُعۡلِنُونَ
and knows what you conceal and what you reveal.(Tafsir At-Tabaree, Vol. 19, Page 149)
27:26
ٱللَّهُ لَآ إِلَـٰهَ إِلَّا هُوَ رَبُّ ٱلۡعَرۡشِ ٱلۡعَظِيمِ
Allah (الله), La ilaha illa Huwa (none has the right to be worshipped but He), the Lord of the Supreme Throne! ۩
27:27
قَالَ سَنَنظُرُ أَصَدَقۡتَ أَمۡ كُنتَ مِنَ ٱلۡكَـٰذِبِينَ
(Suleiman (سليمان) (Solomon)) said: ‘We shall see whether you speak the truth or you are (one) of the liars.
27:28
ٱذۡهَب بِّكِتَـٰبِى هَـٰذَا فَأَلۡقِهۡ إِلَيۡہِمۡ
Go you with this letter of mine, and deliver it to them,
ثُمَّ تَوَلَّ عَنۡہُمۡ فَٱنظُرۡ مَاذَا يَرۡجِعُونَ
then draw back from them, and see what (answer) they return.‘
27:29
قَالَتۡ يَـٰٓأَيُّہَا ٱلۡمَلَؤُاْ إِنِّىٓ أُلۡقِىَ إِلَىَّ كِتَـٰبٌ۬ كَرِيمٌ
She said: ‘O chiefs! Verily! Here is delivered to me a noble letter,
27:30
إِنَّهُ ۥ مِن سُلَيۡمَـٰنَ وَإِنَّهُ ۥ
Verily! It is from Suleiman (سليمان) (Solomon), and verily! It (reads):
بِسۡمِ ٱللَّهِ ٱلرَّحۡمَـٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ
In the Name of Allah (الله), the Most Beneficent (Ar-Rahmaan (ٱلْرَّحْمَـانُ)), the Most Merciful (Ar-Raheem (ٱلْرَّحِيْمُ));
27:31
أَلَّا تَعۡلُواْ عَلَىَّ وَأۡتُونِى مُسۡلِمِينَ
Be you not exalted against me, but come to me as Muslims (true believers who submit to Allah (الله) with full submission) ‘
27:[32-37] Communications between the Queen of Sheba and Sulaimãn
27:[38-44] A man who had the ‘Knowledge of the Book‘ brought the Throne of the Queen to King Sulaimãn in twinkling of an eye. Queen of Sheba and her people embraced Islam
27:32
قَالَتۡ يَـٰٓأَيُّہَا ٱلۡمَلَؤُاْ أَفۡتُونِى فِىٓ أَمۡرِى
She said: ‘O chiefs! Advise me in (this) case of mine.
مَا ڪُنتُ قَاطِعَةً أَمۡرًا حَتَّىٰ تَشۡہَدُونِ
I decide no case till you are present with me.‘
27:33
قَالُواْ نَحۡنُ أُوْلُواْ قُوَّةٍ۬ وَأُوْلُواْ بَأۡسٍ۬ شَدِيدٍ۬
They said: ‘We have great strength, and great ability for war,
وَٱلۡأَمۡرُ إِلَيۡكِ فَٱنظُرِى مَاذَا تَأۡمُرِينَ
but it is for you to command; so think over what you will command.‘
27:34
قَالَتۡ إِنَّ ٱلۡمُلُوكَ إِذَا دَخَلُواْ قَرۡيَةً أَفۡسَدُوهَا وَجَعَلُوٓاْ أَعِزَّةَ أَهۡلِهَآ أَذِلَّةً۬‌ۖ
She said: ‘Verily! King (ملك)s, when they enter a town (country), they despoil it, and make the most honourable amongst its people low.
وَكَذَٲلِكَ يَفۡعَلُونَ
And thus they do.
27:35
وَإِنِّى مُرۡسِلَةٌ إِلَيۡہِم بِهَدِيَّةٍ۬ فَنَاظِرَةُۢ بِمَ يَرۡجِعُ ٱلۡمُرۡسَلُونَ
But verily! I am going to send him a present, and see with what (answer) the messengers return.‘
27:36
فَلَمَّا جَآءَ سُلَيۡمَـٰنَ قَالَ أَتُمِدُّونَنِ بِمَالٍ۬
So when (the messengers with the present) came to Suleiman (سليمان) (Solomon), he said: ‘Will you help me in wealth?
فَمَآ ءَاتَٮٰنِۦَ ٱللَّهُ خَيۡرٌ۬ مِّمَّآ ءَاتَٮٰكُم
What Allah (الله) has given me is better than that which He has given you!
بَلۡ أَنتُم بِہَدِيَّتِكُمۡ تَفۡرَحُونَ
Nay, you rejoice in your gift!‘
27:37
ٱرۡجِعۡ إِلَيۡہِمۡ
(Then Suleiman (سليمان) (Solomon) said to the chief of her messengers who brought the present):
Go back to them.
فَلَنَأۡتِيَنَّهُم بِجُنُودٍ۬ لَّا قِبَلَ لَهُم بِہَا
We verily shall come to them with hosts that they cannot resist,
وَلَنُخۡرِجَنَّہُم مِّنۡہَآ أَذِلَّةً۬ وَهُمۡ صَـٰغِرُونَ
and we shall drive them out from there in disgrace, and they will be abased.‘
27:38
قَالَ يَـٰٓأَيُّہَا ٱلۡمَلَؤُاْأَيُّكُمۡ يَأۡتِينِى بِعَرۡشِہَا قَبۡلَ أَن يَأۡتُونِى مُسۡلِمِينَ
He said: ‘O chiefs! Which of you can bring me her throne before they come to me surrendering themselves in obedience?‘
27:39
قَالَ عِفۡرِيتٌ۬ مِّنَ ٱلۡجِنِّ أَنَا۟ ءَاتِيكَ بِهِۦ قَبۡلَ أَن تَقُومَ مِن مَّقَامِكَ‌ۖ
An Ifreet (strong) from the jinns said: ‘I will bring it to you before you rise from your place (council).
وَإِنِّى عَلَيۡهِ لَقَوِىٌّ أَمِينٌ۬
And verily, I am indeed strong, and trustworthy for such work.‘
27:40
قَالَ ٱلَّذِى عِندَهُ ۥ عِلۡمٌ۬ مِّنَ ٱلۡكِتَـٰبِ
One with whom was knowledge of the Scripture said:
أَنَا۟ ءَاتِيكَ بِهِۦ قَبۡلَ أَن يَرۡتَدَّ إِلَيۡكَ طَرۡفُكَ‌ۚ
I will bring it to you within the twinkling of an eye!‘
فَلَمَّا رَءَاهُ مُسۡتَقِرًّا عِندَهُ ۥ قَالَ
then when (Suleiman (سليمان) (Solomon)) saw it placed before him, he said:

This is by the Grace of my Lord to test me whether I am grateful or ungrateful!
وَمَن شَكَرَ فَإِنَّمَا يَشۡكُرُ لِنَفۡسِهِۦ‌ۖ
And whoever is grateful, truly, his gratitude is for (the good of) his ownself,
وَمَن كَفَرَ فَإِنَّ رَبِّى غَنِىٌّ۬ كَرِيمٌ۬
and whoever is ungrateful, (he is ungrateful only for the loss of his ownself). Certainly! My Lord is Rich (Free of all wants), Bountiful.‘

for she was of a disbelieving people.

but when she saw it, she thought it was a pool, and she (tucked up her clothes) uncovering her legs,

Suleiman (سليمان) (Solomon) said: ‘Verily, it is Sarh ((a glass surface with water underneath it) or a palace) paved smooth with slab of glass.‘
قَالَتۡ رَبِّ إِنِّى ظَلَمۡتُ نَفۡسِى وَأَسۡلَمۡتُ مَعَ سُلَيۡمَـٰنَ لِلَّهِ رَبِّ ٱلۡعَـٰلَمِينَ
She said: ‘My Lord! Verily, I have wronged myself, and I submit (in Islam (الإسلام), together with Suleiman (سليمان) (Solomon), to Allah (الله), the Lord of the Alameen (mankind, jinns and all that exists).‘

وَمَآ أَنتَ بِہَـٰدِى ٱلۡعُمۡىِ عَن ضَلَـٰلَتِهِمۡ‌ۖ
Nor can you lead the blind out of their error,
إِن تُسۡمِعُ إِلَّا مَن يُؤۡمِنُ بِـَٔايَـٰتِنَا فَهُم مُّسۡلِمُونَ
you can only make to hear those who believe in Our Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, etc.), and who have submitted (themselves to Allah (الله) in Islam (الإسلام) as Muslims).
27:82
وَإِذَا وَقَعَ ٱلۡقَوۡلُ عَلَيۡہِمۡ أَخۡرَجۡنَا لَهُمۡ دَآبَّةً۬ مِّنَ ٱلۡأَرۡضِ
And when the Word (of torment) is fulfilled against them, We shall bring out from the earth a beast to them,
تُكَلِّمُهُمۡ أَنَّ ٱلنَّاسَ كَانُواْ بِـَٔايَـٰتِنَا لَا يُوقِنُونَ
which will speak to them because mankind believed not with certainty in Our Ayat (Verses of the Quran (القرآن) and Prophet Muhammad (محمد) SAW).
27:[83-86] Do not deny Allah‘s revelations without gaining their comprehensive knowledge
27:[87-93] A scene from the Doomsday. Those who accept guidance do so to their own good and those who go astray do so to their own peril
27:83
وَيَوۡمَ نَحۡشُرُ مِن ڪُلِّ أُمَّةٍ۬ فَوۡجً۬ا مِّمَّن يُكَذِّبُ بِـَٔايَـٰتِنَا فَهُمۡ يُوزَعُونَ
And (remember) the Day when We shall gather out of every nation a troop of those who denied Our Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, etc.), and (then) they (all) shall be gathered (and driven to the place of reckoning),
27:84
حَتَّىٰٓ إِذَا جَآءُو قَالَ أَڪَذَّبۡتُم بِـَٔايَـٰتِى
Till, when they come (before their Lord at the place of reckoning), He will say: ‘Did you deny My Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, etc.)
وَلَمۡ تُحِيطُواْ بِہَا عِلۡمًا أَمَّاذَا كُنتُمۡ تَعۡمَلُونَ
when you comprehended them not in knowledge, or what (else) was it that you used to do?‘
27:85
وَوَقَعَ ٱلۡقَوۡلُ عَلَيۡہِم بِمَا ظَلَمُواْ فَهُمۡ لَا يَنطِقُونَ
And the Word (of torment) will be fulfilled against them, because they have done wrong, and they will be unable to speak (in order to defend themselves).
27:86
أَلَمۡ يَرَوۡاْ أَنَّا جَعَلۡنَا ٱلَّيۡلَ لِيَسۡكُنُواْ فِيهِ وَٱلنَّهَارَ مُبۡصِرًا‌ۚ
See they not that We have made the night for them to rest therein, and the day sight-giving?
إِنَّ فِى ذَٲلِكَ لَأَيَـٰتٍ۬ لِّقَوۡمٍ۬ يُؤۡمِنُونَ
Verily, in this are Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, etc.) for the people who believe.
27:87
وَيَوۡمَ يُنفَخُ فِى ٱلصُّورِ فَفَزِعَ مَن فِى ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٲتِ وَمَن فِى ٱلۡأَرۡضِ إِلَّا مَن شَآءَ ٱللَّهُ‌ۚ
And (remember) the Day on which the Trumpet will be blown and all who are in the heavens and all who are on the earth, will be terrified except him whom Allah (الله) will (exempt).
وَكُلٌّ أَتَوۡهُ دَٲخِرِينَ
And all shall come to Him humbled.
27:88
وَتَرَى ٱلۡجِبَالَ تَحۡسَبُہَا جَامِدَةً۬ وَهِىَ تَمُرُّ مَرَّ ٱلسَّحَابِ‌ۚ
And you will see the mountains and think them solid, but they shall pass away as the passing away of the clouds.
صُنۡعَ ٱللَّهِ ٱلَّذِىٓ أَتۡقَنَ كُلَّ شَىۡءٍ‌ۚ
The Work of Allah (الله), Who perfected all things,
إِنَّهُ ۥ خَبِيرُۢ بِمَا تَفۡعَلُونَ
verily! He is Well-Acquainted with what you do.
27:89
مَن جَآءَ بِٱلۡحَسَنَةِ فَلَهُ ۥ خَيۡرٌ۬ مِّنۡہَا
Whoever brings a good deed (i.e. Belief in the Oneness of Allah (الله) along with every deed of righteousness), will have better than its worth,
وَهُم مِّن فَزَعٍ۬ يَوۡمَٮِٕذٍ ءَامِنُونَ
and they will be safe from the terror on that Day.
27:90
وَمَن جَآءَ بِٱلسَّيِّئَةِ فَكُبَّتۡ وُجُوهُهُمۡ فِى ٱلنَّارِ
And whoever brings an evil (deed) (i.e. Shirk polytheism, disbelief in the Oneness of Allah (الله) and every evil sinful deed), they will be cast down (prone) on their faces in the Fire.
هَلۡ تُجۡزَوۡنَ إِلَّا مَا كُنتُمۡ تَعۡمَلُونَ
(And it will be said to them) ‘Are you being recompensed anything except what you used to do?‘
27:91
إِنَّمَآ أُمِرۡتُ أَنۡ أَعۡبُدَ رَبَّ هَـٰذِهِ ٱلۡبَلۡدَةِ ٱلَّذِى حَرَّمَهَا وَلَهُ ۥ ڪُلُّ شَىۡءٍ۬‌ۖ
I (Muhammad (محمد) SAW) have been commanded only to worship the Lord of this city (Makkah (مكة)), Him Who has sanctified it and His is everything.
وَأُمِرۡتُ أَنۡ أَكُونَ مِنَ ٱلۡمُسۡلِمِينَ
And I am commanded to be from among the Muslims (those who submit to Allah (الله) in Islam (الإسلام)).
27:92
وَأَنۡ أَتۡلُوَاْ ٱلۡقُرۡءَانَ‌ۖ
And to recite the Quran (القرآن),
فَمَنِ ٱهۡتَدَىٰ فَإِنَّمَا يَہۡتَدِى لِنَفۡسِهِۦ‌ۖ
so whosoever receives guidance, receives it for the good of his ownself,
وَمَن ضَلَّ فَقُلۡ إِنَّمَآ أَنَا۟ مِنَ ٱلۡمُنذِرِينَ
and whosoever goes astray, say (to him): ‘I am only one of the warners.‘
27:93
وَقُلِ ٱلۡحَمۡدُ لِلَّهِ سَيُرِيكُمۡ ءَايَـٰتِهِۦ فَتَعۡرِفُونَہَا‌ۚ
And say ((O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) to these polytheists and pagans etc.):
All the praises and thanks be to Allah (الله). He will show you His Ayat (signs, in yourselves, and in the universe or punishments, etc.), and you shall recognise them.
وَمَا رَبُّكَ بِغَـٰفِلٍ عَمَّا تَعۡمَلُونَ
And your Lord is not unaware of what you do.‘*********
