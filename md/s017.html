Al-Israa
The Night Journey
17:[1] Allah took Muhammad (pbuh) on a tour of the universe
17:[2-10] Allah fulfilled the prophecy made in the Holy Book of the Israelites that they will create mischief in the land twice and each time they will be punished. The Qur‘an guides to the perfectly Straight Way
17:1
سُبۡحَـٰنَ ٱلَّذِىٓ أَسۡرَىٰ بِعَبۡدِهِۦ لَيۡلاً۬ مِّنَ ٱلۡمَسۡجِدِ ٱلۡحَرَامِ إِلَى ٱلۡمَسۡجِدِ ٱلۡأَقۡصَا
Glorified (and Exalted) be He (Allah) (above all that (evil) they associate with Him), Who took His slave (Muhammad SAW) for a journey by night from Al-Masjid-al-Haram (at Makkah) to the farthest mosque (in Jerusalem),
ٱلَّذِى بَـٰرَكۡنَا حَوۡلَهُ ۥ لِنُرِيَهُ ۥ مِنۡ ءَايَـٰتِنَآ‌ۚ
the neighbourhood whereof We have blessed, in order that We might show him (Muhammad SAW) of Our Ayat (proofs, evidences, lessons, signs, etc.).
إِنَّهُ ۥ هُوَ ٱلسَّمِيعُ ٱلۡبَصِيرُ
Verily, He is the All-Hearer, the All-Seer (Al-Baseer).
17:2
وَءَاتَيۡنَا مُوسَى ٱلۡكِتَـٰبَ وَجَعَلۡنَـٰهُ هُدً۬ى لِّبَنِىٓ إِسۡرَٲٓءِيلَ
And We gave Musa (Moosa) the Scripture and made it a guidance for the Children of Israel (saying):
أَلَّا تَتَّخِذُواْ مِن دُونِى وَڪِيلاً۬
Take not other than Me as (your) Wakeel (Protector, Lord, or Disposer of your affairs, etc).
17:3
ذُرِّيَّةَ مَنۡ حَمَلۡنَا مَعَ نُوحٍ‌ۚ
O offspring of those whom We carried (in the ship) with Nooh (Noah)!
إِنَّهُ ۥ كَانَ عَبۡدً۬ا شَكُورً۬ا
Verily, he was a grateful slave.‘
17:4
وَقَضَيۡنَآ إِلَىٰ بَنِىٓ إِسۡرَٲٓءِيلَ فِى ٱلۡكِتَـٰبِ لَتُفۡسِدُنَّ فِى ٱلۡأَرۡضِ مَرَّتَيۡنِ وَلَتَعۡلُنَّ عُلُوًّ۬ا ڪَبِيرً۬ا
And We decreed for the Children of Israel in the Scripture, that indeed you would do mischief on the earth twice and you will become tyrants and extremely arrogant!
17:5
فَإِذَا جَآءَ وَعۡدُ أُولَٮٰهُمَا بَعَثۡنَا عَلَيۡڪُمۡ عِبَادً۬ا لَّنَآ أُوْلِى بَأۡسٍ۬ شَدِيدٍ۬
So, when the promise came for the first of the two, We sent against you slaves of Ours given to terrible warfare.
فَجَاسُواْ خِلَـٰلَ ٱلدِّيَارِ‌ۚ
They entered the very innermost parts of your homes.
وَكَانَ وَعۡدً۬ا مَّفۡعُولاً۬
And it was a promise (completely) fulfilled.
17:6
ثُمَّ رَدَدۡنَا لَكُمُ ٱلۡڪَرَّةَ عَلَيۡہِمۡ وَأَمۡدَدۡنَـٰكُم بِأَمۡوَٲلٍ۬ وَبَنِينَ وَجَعَلۡنَـٰكُمۡ أَڪۡثَرَ نَفِيرًا
Then We gave you once again, a return of victory over them. And We helped you with wealth and children and made you more numerous in man power.
17:7
إِنْ أَحْسَنْتُمْ أَحْسَنْتُمْ لِأَنْفُسِكُمْ ۖ وَإِنْ أَسَأْتُمْ فَلَهَا ۚ
(And We said): ‘If you do good, you do good for your ownselves, and if you do evil (you do it) against yourselves.‘
فَإِذَا جَاءَ وَعْدُ الْآخِرَةِ لِيَسُوءُوا وُجُوهَكُمْ وَلِيَدْخُلُوا الْمَسْجِدَ كَمَا دَخَلُوهُ أَوَّلَ مَرَّةٍ
Then, when the second promise came to pass, (We permitted your enemies) to make your faces sorrowful and to enter the mosque (of Jerusalem) as they had entered it before,
وَلِيُتَبِّرُواْ مَا عَلَوۡاْ تَتۡبِيرًا
and to destroy with utter destruction all that fell in their hands.
17:8
عَسَىٰ رَبُّكُمۡ أَن يَرۡحَمَكُمۡ‌ۚ
(And We said in the Taurat (Torah)): ‘It may be that your Lord may show mercy unto you,
وَإِنۡ عُدتُّمۡ عُدۡنَا‌ۘوَجَعَلۡنَا جَهَنَّمَ لِلۡكَـٰفِرِينَ حَصِيرًا
but if you return (to sins), We shall return (to Our Punishment). And We have made Hell a prison for the disbelievers.
17:9
إِنَّ هَـٰذَا ٱلۡقُرۡءَانَ يَہۡدِى لِلَّتِى هِىَ أَقۡوَمُ
Verily, this Quran guides to that which is most just and right
وَيُبَشِّرُ ٱلۡمُؤۡمِنِينَ ٱلَّذِينَ يَعۡمَلُونَ ٱلصَّـٰلِحَـٰتِ أَنَّ لَهُمۡ أَجۡرً۬ا كَبِيرً۬ا
and gives glad tidings to the believers (in the Oneness of Allah and His Messenger, Muhammad SAW, etc.). who work deeds of righteousness, that they shall have a great reward (Paradise).
17:10
وَأَنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ لَا يُؤۡمِنُونَ بِٱلۡأَخِرَةِ أَعۡتَدۡنَا لَهُمۡ عَذَابًا أَلِيمً۬ا
And that those who believe not in the Hereafter (i.e. they disbelieve that they will be recompensed for what they did in this world, good or bad, etc.), for them We have prepared a painful torment (Hell).
17:[11-14] On the Day of Judgment, each individual shall be given the book of his own deeds
17:[15-17] He that seeks guidance, does so to his own good and he who goes astray, does so to his own loss
17:[18-22] He that desires the transitory things of this life, is given it here, but in the hereafter he shall be condemned to hell
17:11
وَيَدۡعُ ٱلۡإِنسَـٰنُ بِٱلشَّرِّ دُعَآءَهُ ۥ بِٱلۡخَيۡرِ‌ۖ وَكَانَ ٱلۡإِنسَـٰنُ عَجُولاً۬
And man invokes (Allah) for evil as he invokes (Allah) for good and man is ever hasty.(i.e., if he is angry with somebody, he invokes (saying): ‘O Allah! Curse him, etc.‘ and that one should not do, but one should be patient)
17:12
وَجَعَلۡنَا ٱلَّيۡلَ وَٱلنَّہَارَ ءَايَتَيۡنِ‌ۖ
And We have appointed the night and the day as two Ayat (signs etc.).
فَمَحَوۡنَآ ءَايَةَ ٱلَّيۡلِ وَجَعَلۡنَآ ءَايَةَ ٱلنَّہَارِ مُبۡصِرَةً۬
Then, We have made dark the sign of the night while We have made the sign of day illuminating,
لِّتَبۡتَغُواْ فَضۡلاً۬ مِّن رَّبِّكُمۡ وَلِتَعۡلَمُواْ عَدَدَ ٱلسِّنِينَ وَٱلۡحِسَابَ‌ۚ
that you may seek bounty from your Lord, and that you may know the number of the years and the reckoning.
وَڪُلَّ شَىۡءٍ۬ فَصَّلۡنَـٰهُ تَفۡصِيلاً۬
And We have explained everything (in detail) with full explanation.
17:13
وَكُلَّ إِنسَـٰنٍ أَلۡزَمۡنَـٰهُ طَـٰٓٮِٕرَهُ ۥ فِى عُنُقِهِۦ‌ۖ
And We have fastened every mans deeds to his neck,
وَنُخۡرِجُ لَهُ ۥ يَوۡمَ ٱلۡقِيَـٰمَةِ ڪِتَـٰبً۬ا يَلۡقَٮٰهُ مَنشُورًا
and on the Day of Resurrection, We shall bring out for him a book which he will find wide open.
17:14
ٱقۡرَأۡ كِتَـٰبَكَ كَفَىٰ بِنَفۡسِكَ ٱلۡيَوۡمَ عَلَيۡكَ حَسِيبً۬ا
(It will be said to him): ‘Read your book. You yourself are sufficient as a reckoner against you this Day.‘
17:15
مَّنِ ٱهۡتَدَىٰ فَإِنَّمَا يَہۡتَدِى لِنَفۡسِهِۦ‌ۖ وَمَن ضَلَّ فَإِنَّمَا يَضِلُّ عَلَيۡہَا‌ۚ
Whoever goes right, then he goes right only for the benefit of his ownself. And whoever goes astray, then he goes astray to his own loss.
وَلَا تَزِرُ وَازِرَةٌ۬ وِزۡرَ أُخۡرَىٰ‌ۗ
No one laden with burdens can bear another‘s burden.
وَمَا كُنَّا مُعَذِّبِينَ حَتَّىٰ نَبۡعَثَ رَسُولاً۬
And We never punish until We have sent a Messenger (to give warning).
17:16
وَإِذَآ أَرَدۡنَآ أَن نُّہۡلِكَ قَرۡيَةً أَمَرۡنَا مُتۡرَفِيہَافَفَسَقُواْ فِيہَا
And when We decide to destroy a town (population), We (first) send a definite order (to obey Allah and be righteous) to those among them (or We (first) increase in number those of its population) who are given the good things of this life. Then, they transgress therein,
فَحَقَّ عَلَيۡہَا ٱلۡقَوۡلُ فَدَمَّرۡنَـٰهَا تَدۡمِيرً۬ا
and thus the word (of torment) is justified against it (them). Then We destroy it with complete destruction.
17:17
وَكَمۡ أَهۡلَكۡنَا مِنَ ٱلۡقُرُونِ مِنۢ بَعۡدِ نُوحٍ۬‌ۗ
And how many generations have We destroyed after Nooh (Noah)!
وَكَفَىٰ بِرَبِّكَ بِذُنُوبِ عِبَادِهِۦ خَبِيرَۢا بَصِيرً۬ا
And Sufficient is your Lord as an All-Knower (Al-‘Aleem) and All-Beholder of the sins of His slaves.
17:18
مَّن كَانَ يُرِيدُ ٱلۡعَاجِلَةَ عَجَّلۡنَا لَهُ ۥ فِيهَا مَا نَشَآءُ لِمَن نُّرِيدُ
Whoever wishes for the quick-passing (transitory enjoyment of this world), We readily grant him what We will for whom We like.
ثُمَّ جَعَلۡنَا لَهُ ۥ جَهَنَّمَ يَصۡلَٮٰهَا مَذۡمُومً۬ا مَّدۡحُورً۬ا
Then, afterwards, We have appointed for him Hell, he will burn therein disgraced and rejected, ( far away from Allahs Mercy).
17:19
وَمَنۡ أَرَادَ ٱلۡأَخِرَةَ وَسَعَىٰ لَهَا سَعۡيَهَا وَهُوَ مُؤۡمِنٌ۬
And whoever desires the Hereafter and strives for it, with the necessary effort due for it (i.e. do righteous deeds of Allah‘s Obedience) while he is a believer (in the Oneness of Allah Islamic Monotheism),
فَأُوْلَـٰٓٮِٕكَ ڪَانَ سَعۡيُهُم مَّشۡكُورً۬ا
then such are the ones whose striving shall be appreciated, thanked and rewarded (by Allah).
17:20
كُلاًّ۬ نُّمِدُّ هَـٰٓؤُلَآءِ وَهَـٰٓؤُلَآءِ مِنۡ عَطَآءِ رَبِّكَ‌ۚ
To each these as well as those We bestow from the Bounties of your Lord.
وَمَا كَانَ عَطَآءُ رَبِّكَ مَحۡظُورًا
And the Bounties of your Lord can never be forbidden.
17:21
ٱنظُرۡ كَيۡفَ فَضَّلۡنَا بَعۡضَہُمۡ عَلَىٰ بَعۡضٍ۬‌ۚ
See how We prefer one above another (in this world)
وَلَلۡأَخِرَةُ أَكۡبَرُ دَرَجَـٰتٍ۬ وَأَكۡبَرُ تَفۡضِيلاً۬
and verily, the Hereafter will be greater in degrees and greater in preference.
17:22
لَّا تَجۡعَلۡ مَعَ ٱللَّهِ إِلَـٰهًا ءَاخَرَ فَتَقۡعُدَ مَذۡمُومً۬ا مَّخۡذُولاً۬
Set not up with Allah any other ilah (god), (O man)!This verse is addressed to Prophet Muhammad SAW , but its implication is general to all mankind,
or you will sit down reproved, forsaken (in the Hell-fire).
that: Commandments of Allah to the believers
17:[23-30] You shall not worship any except Him, you shall be obedient to your parents, nice to your relatives and the community at large
17:23
وَقَضَىٰ رَبُّكَ أَلَّا تَعۡبُدُوٓاْ إِلَّآ إِيَّاهُ وَبِٱلۡوَٲلِدَيۡنِ إِحۡسَـٰنًا‌ۚ
And your Lord has decreed that you worship none but Him. And that you be dutiful to your parents.
إِمَّا يَبۡلُغَنَّ عِندَكَ ٱلۡڪِبَرَ أَحَدُهُمَآ أَوۡ كِلَاهُمَا فَلَا تَقُل لَّهُمَآ أُفٍّ۬ وَلَا تَنۡہَرۡهُمَا
If one of them or both of them attain old age in your life, say not to them a word of disrespect,
وَقُل لَّهُمَا قَوۡلاً۬ ڪَرِيمً۬ا
nor shout at them but address them in terms of honour.
17:24
وَٱخۡفِضۡ لَهُمَا جَنَاحَ ٱلذُّلِّ مِنَ ٱلرَّحۡمَةِ وَقُل
And lower unto them the wing of submission and humility through mercy, and say:
رَّبِّ ٱرۡحَمۡهُمَا كَمَا رَبَّيَانِى صَغِيرً۬ا
My Lord! Bestow on them Your Mercy as they did bring me up when I was small.‘
17:25
رَّبُّكُمۡ أَعۡلَمُ بِمَا فِى نُفُوسِكُمۡ‌ۚ
Your Lord knows best what is in your inner-selves.
إِن تَكُونُواْ صَـٰلِحِينَ فَإِنَّهُ ۥ ڪَانَ لِلۡأَوَّٲبِينَ غَفُورً۬ا
If you are righteous, then, verily, He is Ever Most Forgiving to those who turn unto Him again and again in obedience, and in repentance.
17:26
وَءَاتِ ذَا ٱلۡقُرۡبَىٰ حَقَّهُ ۥ وَٱلۡمِسۡكِينَ وَٱبۡنَ ٱلسَّبِيلِ
And give to the kindred his due and to the Miskeen (poor) and to the wayfarer.
وَلَا تُبَذِّرۡ تَبۡذِيرًا
But spend not wastefully (your wealth) in the manner of a spendthrift.(Tafsir. At-Tabaree, Vol. 10, Page 158 (Verse 9: 60)).
17:27
إِنَّ ٱلۡمُبَذِّرِينَ كَانُوٓاْ إِخۡوَٲنَ ٱلشَّيَـٰطِينِ‌ۖ وَكَانَ ٱلشَّيۡطَـٰنُ لِرَبِّهِۦ كَفُورً۬ا
Verily, spendthrifts are brothers of the Shayateen (devils), and the Shaitan (Devil Satan) is ever ungrateful to his Lord.
17:28
وَإِمَّا تُعۡرِضَنَّ عَنۡہُمُ ٱبۡتِغَآءَ رَحۡمَةٍ۬ مِّن رَّبِّكَ تَرۡجُوهَا فَقُل لَّهُمۡ قَوۡلاً۬ مَّيۡسُورً۬ا
And if you (O Muhammad SAW) turn away from them (kindred, poor, wayfarer, etc. whom We have ordered you to give their rights, but if you have no money at the time they ask you for it) and you are awaiting a mercy from your Lord for which you hope, then, speak unto them a soft kind word (i.e. Allah will give me and I shall give you).
17:29
وَلَا تَجۡعَلۡ يَدَكَ مَغۡلُولَةً إِلَىٰ عُنُقِكَ
And let not your hand be tied (like a miser) to your neck,
وَلَا تَبۡسُطۡهَا كُلَّ ٱلۡبَسۡطِ فَتَقۡعُدَ مَلُومً۬ا مَّحۡسُورًا
nor stretch it forth to its utmost reach (like a spendthrift), so that you become blameworthy and in severe poverty.
17:30
إِنَّ رَبَّكَ يَبۡسُطُ ٱلرِّزۡقَ لِمَن يَشَآءُ وَيَقۡدِرُ‌ۚ
Truly, your Lord enlarges the provision for whom He wills and straitens (for whom He wills).
إِنَّهُ ۥ كَانَ بِعِبَادِهِۦ خَبِيرَۢا بَصِيرً۬ا
Verily, He is Ever All-Knower (Al-‘Aleem), All-Seer (Al-Baseer) of His slaves.
17:[31-40] Do not kill children for fear of want, nor commit adultery, do not kill anyone nor consume the property of orphans. Fulfill the pledges, give full measures, do not follow anyone blindly, do not walk arrogantly and do not associate other deities as an object of worship along with Allah
17:[41-44] If there were other gods besides Allah, they would have tried to dethrone Him
17:31
وَلَا تَقۡتُلُوٓاْ أَوۡلَـٰدَكُمۡ خَشۡيَةَ إِمۡلَـٰقٍ۬‌ۖ نَّحۡنُ نَرۡزُقُهُمۡ وَإِيَّاكُمۡ‌ۚ
And kill not your children for fear of poverty. We provide for them and for you.
إِنَّ قَتۡلَهُمۡ ڪَانَ خِطۡـًٔ۬ا كَبِيرً۬ا
Surely, the killing of them is a great sin.
17:32
وَلَا تَقۡرَبُواْ ٱلزِّنَىٰٓ‌ۖ إِنَّهُ ۥ كَانَ فَـٰحِشَةً۬ وَسَآءَ سَبِيلاً۬
And come not near to the unlawful sexual intercourse. Verily, it is a Fahishah (i.e. anything that transgresses its limits (a great sin)), and an evil way (that leads one to Hell unless Allah forgives him).
17:33
وَلَا تَقۡتُلُواْ ٱلنَّفۡسَ ٱلَّتِى حَرَّمَ ٱللَّهُ إِلَّا بِٱلۡحَقِّ‌ۗ
And do not kill anyone which Allah has forbidden, except for a just cause.
وَمَن قُتِلَ مَظۡلُومً۬ا فَقَدۡ جَعَلۡنَا لِوَلِيِّهِۦ سُلۡطَـٰنً۬ا
And whoever is killed (intentionally with hostility and oppression and not by mistake), We have given his heir the authority ((to demand Qisas, Law of Equality in punishment or to forgive, or to take Diya (blood money)).
فَلَا يُسۡرِف فِّى ٱلۡقَتۡلِ‌ۖ إِنَّهُ ۥ كَانَ مَنصُورً۬ا
But let him not exceed limits in the matter of taking life (i.e. he should not kill except the killer only). Verily, he is helped (by the Islamic law).
17:34
وَلَا تَقۡرَبُواْ مَالَ ٱلۡيَتِيمِ إِلَّا بِٱلَّتِى هِىَ أَحۡسَنُ حَتَّىٰ يَبۡلُغَ أَشُدَّهُ ۥ‌ۚ
And come not near to the orphans property except to improve it, until he attains the age of full strength.
وَأَوۡفُواْ بِٱلۡعَهۡدِ‌ۖ إِنَّ ٱلۡعَهۡدَ كَانَ مَسۡـُٔولاً۬
And fulfil (every) covenant. Verily! the covenant, will be questioned about.
17:35
وَأَوۡفُواْ ٱلۡكَيۡلَ إِذَا كِلۡتُمۡ وَزِنُواْ بِٱلۡقِسۡطَاسِ ٱلۡمُسۡتَقِيمِ‌ۚ
And give full measure when you measure, and weigh with a balance that is straight.
ذَٲلِكَ خَيۡرٌ۬ وَأَحۡسَنُ تَأۡوِيلاً۬
That is good (advantageous) and better in the end.
17:36
وَلَا تَقۡفُ مَا لَيۡسَ لَكَ بِهِۦ عِلۡمٌ‌ۚ

قُلۡ ءَامِنُواْ بِهِۦۤ أَوۡ لَا تُؤۡمِنُوٓاْ‌ۚ
Say (O Muhammad SAW to them): ‘Believe in it (the Quran) or do not believe (in it).
إِنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ أُوتُواْ ٱلۡعِلۡمَ مِن قَبۡلِهِۦۤ إِذَا يُتۡلَىٰ عَلَيۡہِمۡ يَخِرُّونَ لِلۡأَذۡقَانِ سُجَّدً۬ا
Verily! Those who were given knowledge before it (the Jews and the Christians like Abdullah bin Salam and Salman Al-Farisee), when it is recited to them, fall down on their faces in humble prostration.‘
17:108
وَيَقُولُونَ سُبۡحَـٰنَ رَبِّنَآإِن كَانَ وَعۡدُ رَبِّنَا لَمَفۡعُولاً۬
And they say: ‘Glory be to our Lord! Truly, the Promise of our Lord must be fulfilled.‘
17:109
وَيَخِرُّونَ لِلۡأَذۡقَانِ يَبۡكُونَ وَيَزِيدُهُمۡ خُشُوعً۬ا
And they fall down on their faces weeping and it adds to their humility. ۩
17:110
قُلِ ٱدۡعُواْ ٱللَّهَ أَوِ ٱدۡعُواْ ٱلرَّحۡمَـٰنَ‌ۖ
Say (O Muhammad SAW): ‘Invoke Allah or invoke the Most Beneficent (Ar-Rahmaan),
أَيًّ۬ا مَّا تَدۡعُواْ فَلَهُ ٱلۡأَسۡمَآءُ ٱلۡحُسۡنَىٰ‌ۚ
by whatever name you invoke Him (it is the same), for to Him belong the Best Names.
وَلَا تَجۡهَرۡ بِصَلَاتِكَ وَلَا تُخَافِتۡ بِہَا وَٱبۡتَغِ بَيۡنَ ذَٲلِكَ سَبِيلاً۬
And offer your Salat (prayer) neither aloud nor in a low voice, but follow a way between.
17:111
وَقُلِ ٱلۡحَمۡدُ لِلَّهِ ٱلَّذِى لَمۡ يَتَّخِذۡ وَلَدً۬ا وَلَمۡ يَكُن لَّهُ ۥ شَرِيكٌ۬ فِى ٱلۡمُلۡكِ
And say: ‘All the praises and thanks be to Allah, Who has not begotten a son (nor an offspring), and Who has no partner in (His) Dominion,
وَلَمۡ يَكُن لَّهُ ۥ وَلِىٌّ۬ مِّنَ ٱلذُّلِّ‌ۖ
nor He is low to have a Walee (helper, protector or supporter).
وَكَبِّرۡهُ تَكۡبِيرَۢا
And magnify Him with all the magnificence, (Allahu-Akbar (Allah is the Most Great)).‘*********
