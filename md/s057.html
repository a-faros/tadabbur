Al-Hadid
The Iron
57:[1-10] All that is in the heavens and earth glorifies Allah, Who created the heavens and earth in six periods and has the knowledge of everything. Those who spend in charity will be richly rewarded
57:1
سَبَّحَ لِلَّهِ مَا فِى ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٲتِ وَٱلۡأَرۡضِ‌ۖ
Whatsoever is in the heavens and the earth glorifies Allah (الله),
وَهُوَ ٱلۡعَزِيزُ ٱلۡحَكِيمُ
and He is the All-Mighty, All-Wise (Al-Hakeem (ٱلْحَكِيمُ)).
57:2
لَهُ ۥ مُلۡكُ ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٲتِ وَٱلۡأَرۡضِ‌ۖ
His is the kingdom of the heavens and the earth,
يُحۡىِۦ وَيُمِيتُ‌ۖ
It is He Who gives life and causes death;
وَهُوَ عَلَىٰ كُلِّ شَىۡءٍ۬ قَدِيرٌ
and He is Able to do all things.
57:3
هُوَ ٱلۡأَوَّلُ وَٱلۡأَخِرُ وَٱلظَّـٰهِرُ وَٱلۡبَاطِنُ‌ۖ
He is the First (nothing is before Him) and the Last (nothing is after Him), the Most High (nothing is above Him) and the Most Near (nothing is nearer than Him).
وَهُوَ بِكُلِّ شَىۡءٍ عَلِيمٌ
And He is the All-Knower (Al-‘Alee (ٱلْعَلِيُّ)m (ٱلْعَلِيمُ)) of everything.
57:4
هُوَ ٱلَّذِى خَلَقَ ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٲتِ وَٱلۡأَرۡضَ فِى سِتَّةِ أَيَّامٍ۬
He it is Who created the heavens and the earth in six Days
ثُمَّ ٱسۡتَوَىٰ عَلَى ٱلۡعَرۡشِ‌ۚ
and then Istawa (rose over) the Throne (in a manner that suits His Majesty).
يَعۡلَمُ مَا يَلِجُ فِى ٱلۡأَرۡضِ وَمَا يَخۡرُجُ مِنۡہَا وَمَا يَنزِلُ مِنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ وَمَا يَعۡرُجُ فِيہَا‌ۖ
He knows what goes into the earth and what comes forth from it, what descends from the heaven and what ascends thereto.
وَهُوَ مَعَكُمۡ أَيۡنَ مَا كُنتُمۡ‌ۚ
And He is with you (by His Knowledge) wheresoever you may be.
وَٱللَّهُ بِمَا تَعۡمَلُونَ بَصِيرٌ۬
And Allah (الله) is the All-Seer (Al-Baseer (ٱلْبَصِيرُ)) of what you do.
57:5
لَّهُ ۥ مُلۡكُ ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٲتِ وَٱلۡأَرۡضِ‌ۚ
His is the kingdom of the heavens and the earth.
وَإِلَى ٱللَّهِ تُرۡجَعُ ٱلۡأُمُورُ
And to Allah (الله) return all the matters (for decision).
57:6
يُولِجُ ٱلَّيۡلَ فِى ٱلنَّہَارِ وَيُولِجُ ٱلنَّہَارَ فِى ٱلَّيۡلِ‌ۚ
He merges night into day (i.e. the decrease in the hours of the night is added into the hours of the day), and merges day into night (i.e. the decrease in the hours of the day is added into the hours of the night),
وَهُوَ عَلِيمُۢ بِذَاتِ ٱلصُّدُورِ
and He has full knowledge of whatsoever is in the breasts.
57:7
ءَامِنُواْ بِٱللَّهِ وَرَسُولِهِۦ وَأَنفِقُواْ مِمَّا جَعَلَكُم مُّسۡتَخۡلَفِينَ فِيهِ‌ۖ
Believe in Allah (الله) and His Messenger (Muhammad (محمد) SAW), and spend of that whereof He has made you trustees.
فَٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُواْ مِنكُمۡ وَأَنفَقُواْ لَهُمۡ أَجۡرٌ۬ كَبِيرٌ۬
And such of you as believe and spend (in Allah (الله)‘s Way), theirs will be a great reward.
57:8
وَمَا لَكُمۡ لَا تُؤۡمِنُونَ بِٱللَّهِ‌ۙ
And what is the matter with you that you believe not in Allah (الله)!
وَٱلرَّسُولُ يَدۡعُوكُمۡ لِتُؤۡمِنُواْ بِرَبِّكُمۡ وَقَدۡ أَخَذَ مِيثَـٰقَكُمۡ إِن كُنتُم مُّؤۡمِنِينَ
While the Messenger (Muhammad (محمد) SAW) invites you to believe in your Lord (Allah (الله)), and He (Allah (الله)) has indeed taken your covenant, if you are real believers.
57:9
هُوَ ٱلَّذِى يُنَزِّلُ عَلَىٰ عَبۡدِهِۦۤ ءَايَـٰتِۭ بَيِّنَـٰتٍ۬ لِّيُخۡرِجَكُم مِّنَ ٱلظُّلُمَـٰتِ إِلَى ٱلنُّورِ‌ۚ
It is He Who sends down manifest Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, etc.) to His slave (Muhammad (محمد) SAW) that He may bring you out from darkness into light.
وَإِنَّ ٱللَّهَ بِكُمۡ لَرَءُوفٌ۬ رَّحِيمٌ۬
And verily, Allah (الله) is to you full of kindness, Most Merciful (Ar-Raheem (ٱلْرَّحِيْمُ)).
57:10
وَمَا لَكُمۡ أَلَّا تُنفِقُواْ فِى سَبِيلِ ٱللَّهِ وَلِلَّهِ مِيرَٲثُ ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٲتِ وَٱلۡأَرۡضِ‌ۚ
And what is the matter with you that you spend not in the Cause of Allah (الله)?
And to Allah (الله) belongs the heritage of the heavens and the earth.
لَا يَسۡتَوِى مِنكُم مَّنۡ أَنفَقَ مِن قَبۡلِ ٱلۡفَتۡحِ وَقَـٰتَلَ‌ۚ
Not equal among you are those who spent and fought before the conquering (of Makkah (مكة))
(with those among you who did so later).
أُوْلَـٰٓٮِٕكَ أَعۡظَمُ دَرَجَةً۬ مِّنَ ٱلَّذِينَ أَنفَقُواْ مِنۢ بَعۡدُ وَقَـٰتَلُواْ‌ۚ
Such are higher in degree than those who spent and fought afterwards.
وَكُلاًّ۬ وَعَدَ ٱللَّهُ ٱلۡحُسۡنَىٰ‌ۚ
But to all, Allah (الله) has promised the best (reward).
وَٱللَّهُ بِمَا تَعۡمَلُونَ خَبِيرٌ۬
And Allah (الله) is All-Aware (Al-‘Alee (ٱلْعَلِيُّ)m (ٱلْعَلِيمُ)) of what you do.
57:[11-19] On that Day the true believers will have their light shining before them while the hypocrites will have their fate no different than the disbelievers. Those who spend in charity will be repaid manyfold and also be given liberal reward besides it
57:11
مَّن ذَا ٱلَّذِى يُقۡرِضُ ٱللَّهَ قَرۡضًا حَسَنً۬ا فَيُضَـٰعِفَهُ ۥ لَهُ ۥ
Who is he that will lend to Allah (الله) a goodly loan, then (Allah (الله)) will increase it manifold to his credit (in repaying),
وَلَهُ ۥۤ أَجۡرٌ۬ كَرِيمٌ۬
and he will have (besides) a good reward (i.e. Paradise (الجنة)).
57:12
يَوۡمَ تَرَى ٱلۡمُؤۡمِنِينَ وَٱلۡمُؤۡمِنَـٰتِ يَسۡعَىٰ نُورُهُم بَيۡنَ أَيۡدِيہِمۡ وَبِأَيۡمَـٰنِهِم
On the Day you shall see the believing men and the believing women their light running forward before them and by their right hands.
بُشۡرَٮٰكُمُ ٱلۡيَوۡمَ جَنَّـٰتٌ۬ تَجۡرِى مِن تَحۡتِہَا ٱلۡأَنۡہَـٰرُ خَـٰلِدِينَ فِيہَا‌ۚ
Glad tidings for you this Day! Gardens under which rivers flow (Paradise (الجنة)), to dwell therein forever!
ذَٲلِكَ هُوَ ٱلۡفَوۡزُ ٱلۡعَظِيمُ
Truly, this is the great success!
57:13
يَوۡمَ يَقُولُ ٱلۡمُنَـٰفِقُونَ وَٱلۡمُنَـٰفِقَـٰتُ لِلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُواْ ٱنظُرُونَا نَقۡتَبِسۡ مِن نُّورِكُمۡ
On the Day when the hypocrites men and women will say to the believers: ‘Wait for us! Let us get something from your light!‘
قِيلَ ٱرۡجِعُواْ وَرَآءَكُمۡ فَٱلۡتَمِسُواْ نُورً۬ا
It will be said: ‘Go back to your rear! Then seek a light!‘
فَضُرِبَ بَيۡنَہُم بِسُورٍ۬ لَّهُ ۥ بَابُۢ
So a wall will be put up between them, with a gate therein.
بَاطِنُهُ ۥ فِيهِ ٱلرَّحۡمَةُ وَظَـٰهِرُهُ ۥ مِن قِبَلِهِ ٱلۡعَذَابُ
Inside it will be mercy, and outside it will be torment.‘
57:14
يُنَادُونَہُمۡ أَلَمۡ نَكُن مَّعَكُمۡ‌ۖ
(The hypocrites) will call the believers: ‘Were we not with you?‘
قَالُواْ بَلَىٰ
The believers will reply: ‘Yes!
وَلَـٰكِنَّكُمۡ فَتَنتُمۡ أَنفُسَكُمۡ وَتَرَبَّصۡتُمۡ وَٱرۡتَبۡتُمۡ وَغَرَّتۡكُمُ ٱلۡأَمَانِىُّ
But you led yourselves into temptations, you looked forward for our destruction; you doubted (in Faith); and you were deceived by false desires,
حَتَّىٰ جَآءَ أَمۡرُ ٱللَّهِ وَغَرَّكُم بِٱللَّهِ ٱلۡغَرُورُ
till the Command of Allah (الله) came to pass. And the chief deceiver (Satan (الشيطان)) deceived you in respect of Allah (الله).‘
57:15
فَٱلۡيَوۡمَ لَا يُؤۡخَذُ مِنكُمۡ فِدۡيَةٌ۬ وَلَا مِنَ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُواْ‌ۚ
So this Day no ransom shall be taken from you (hypocrites), nor of those who disbelieved, (in the Oneness of Allah (الله) Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism).
مَأۡوَٮٰكُمُ ٱلنَّارُ‌ۖ
Your abode is the Fire,
هِىَ مَوۡلَٮٰكُمۡ‌ۖ وَبِئۡسَ ٱلۡمَصِيرُ
that is the proper place for you, and worst indeed is that destination.
57:16
أَلَمۡ يَأۡنِ لِلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوٓاْ أَن تَخۡشَعَ قُلُوبُہُمۡ لِذِڪۡرِ ٱللَّهِ وَمَا نَزَلَ مِنَ ٱلۡحَقِّ
Has not the time come for the hearts of those who believe (in the Oneness of Allah (الله) - Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism) to be affected by Allah (الله)‘s Reminder (this Quran (القرآن)), and that which has been revealed of the truth,
وَلَا يَكُونُواْ كَٱلَّذِينَ أُوتُواْ ٱلۡكِتَـٰبَ مِن قَبۡلُ فَطَالَ عَلَيۡہِمُ ٱلۡأَمَدُ فَقَسَتۡ قُلُوبُہُمۡ‌ۖ
lest they become as those who received the Scripture (the Taurat (Torah (التوراة)) and the Injil (Injeel (الإنجيل))) before (i.e. Jews (هُودًا) and Christians (نَصَارَىٰ)), and the term was prolonged for them and so their hearts were hardened?
وَكَثِيرٌ۬ مِّنۡہُمۡ فَـٰسِقُونَ
And many of them were Fasiqoon (rebellious, disobedient to Allah (الله)).
57:17
ٱعۡلَمُوٓاْ أَنَّ ٱللَّهَ يُحۡىِ ٱلۡأَرۡضَ بَعۡدَ مَوۡتِہَا‌ۚ
Know that Allah (الله) gives life to the earth after its death!
قَدۡ بَيَّنَّا لَكُمُ ٱلۡأَيَـٰتِ لَعَلَّكُمۡ تَعۡقِلُونَ
Indeed We have made clear the Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, etc.) to you, if you but understand.
57:18
إِنَّ ٱلۡمُصَّدِّقِينَ وَٱلۡمُصَّدِّقَـٰتِ وَأَقۡرَضُواْ ٱللَّهَ قَرۡضًا حَسَنً۬ا
Verily, those who give Sadaqat (i.e. Zakat and alms, etc.), men and women, and lend to Allah (الله) a goodly loan,
يُضَـٰعَفُ لَهُمۡ وَلَهُمۡ أَجۡرٌ۬ كَرِيمٌ۬
it shall be increased manifold (to their credit), and theirs shall be an honourable good reward (i.e. Paradise (الجنة)).
57:19
وَٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُواْ بِٱللَّهِ وَرُسُلِهِۦۤ أُوْلَـٰٓٮِٕكَ هُمُ ٱلصِّدِّيقُونَ‌ۖ
And those who believe in (the Oneness of) Allah (الله) and His Messengers, they are the Siddiqoon (i.e. those followers of the Prophets who were first and foremost to believe in them),
وَٱلشُّہَدَآءُ عِندَ رَبِّہِمۡ لَهُمۡ أَجۡرُهُمۡ وَنُورُهُمۡ‌ۖ
and the martyrs with their Lord, they shall have their reward and their light.
وَٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُواْ وَڪَذَّبُواْ بِـَٔايَـٰتِنَآ أُوْلَـٰٓٮِٕكَ أَصۡحَـٰبُ ٱلۡجَحِيمِ
But those who disbelieve (in the Oneness of Allah (الله) - Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism) and deny Our Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, etc.), they shall be the dwellers of the blazing Fire.
57:[20-25] Life of this world is but play, amusement and illusion. Do not grieve for the things that you miss, nor overjoy at what you gain
57:20
ٱعۡلَمُوٓاْ أَنَّمَا ٱلۡحَيَوٰةُ ٱلدُّنۡيَا لَعِبٌ۬ وَلَهۡوٌ۬ وَزِينَةٌ۬ وَتَفَاخُرُۢ بَيۡنَكُمۡ
Know that the life of this world is only play and amusement, pomp and mutual boasting among you,
وَتَكَاثُرٌ۬ فِى ٱلۡأَمۡوَٲلِ وَٱلۡأَوۡلَـٰدِ‌ۖ
and rivalry in respect of wealth and children,
كَمَثَلِ غَيۡثٍ أَعۡجَبَ ٱلۡكُفَّارَ نَبَاتُهُ ۥ
as the likeness of vegetation after rain, thereof the growth is pleasing to the tiller;
ثُمَّ يَہِيجُ فَتَرَٮٰهُ مُصۡفَرًّ۬ا ثُمَّ يَكُونُ حُطَـٰمً۬ا‌ۖ
afterwards it dries up and you see it turning yellow; then it becomes straw.
وَفِى ٱلۡأَخِرَةِ عَذَابٌ۬ شَدِيدٌ۬ وَمَغۡفِرَةٌ۬ مِّنَ ٱللَّهِ وَرِضۡوَٲنٌ۬‌ۚ
But in the Hereafter (there is) a severe torment (for the disbelievers, evil-doers), and (there is) Forgiveness from Allah (الله) and (His) Good Pleasure (for the believers, good-doers),
وَمَا ٱلۡحَيَوٰةُ ٱلدُّنۡيَآ إِلَّا مَتَـٰعُ ٱلۡغُرُورِ
whereas the life of this world is only a deceiving enjoyment.
57:21
سَابِقُوٓاْ إِلَىٰ مَغۡفِرَةٍ۬ مِّن رَّبِّكُمۡ وَجَنَّةٍ عَرۡضُہَا كَعَرۡضِ ٱلسَّمَآءِ وَٱلۡأَرۡضِ
Race one with another in hastening towards Forgiveness from your Lord (Allah (الله)), and towards Paradise (الجنة), the width whereof is as the width of heaven and earth,
أُعِدَّتۡ لِلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُواْ بِٱللَّهِ وَرُسُلِهِۦ‌ۚ
prepared for those who believe in Allah (الله) and His Messengers.
ذَٲلِكَ فَضۡلُ ٱللَّهِ يُؤۡتِيهِ مَن يَشَآءُ‌ۚ
That is the Grace of Allah (الله) which He bestows on whom He pleases.
وَٱللَّهُ ذُو ٱلۡفَضۡلِ ٱلۡعَظِيمِ
And Allah (الله) is the Owner of Great Bounty.
57:22
مَآ أَصَابَ مِن مُّصِيبَةٍ۬ فِى ٱلۡأَرۡضِ وَلَا فِىٓ أَنفُسِكُمۡ إِلَّا فِى ڪِتَـٰبٍ۬ مِّن قَبۡلِ أَن نَّبۡرَأَهَآ‌ۚ
No calamity befalls on the earth or in yourselves but is inscribed in the Book of Decrees (Al-Lauh Al-Mahfooz), before We bring it into existence.
إِنَّ ذَٲلِكَ عَلَى ٱللَّهِ يَسِيرٌ۬
Verily, that is easy for Allah (الله).
57:23
لِّكَيۡلَا تَأۡسَوۡاْ عَلَىٰ مَا فَاتَكُمۡ وَلَا تَفۡرَحُواْ بِمَآ ءَاتَٮٰڪُمۡ‌ۗ
In order that you may not be sad over matters that you fail to get, nor rejoice because of that which has been given to you.
وَٱللَّهُ لَا يُحِبُّ كُلَّ مُخۡتَالٍ۬ فَخُورٍ
And Allah (الله) likes not prideful boasters.
57:24
ٱلَّذِينَ يَبۡخَلُونَ وَيَأۡمُرُونَ ٱلنَّاسَ بِٱلۡبُخۡلِ‌ۗ
Those who are misers and enjoin upon people miserliness (Allah (الله) is not in need of their charity).
وَمَن يَتَوَلَّ فَإِنَّ ٱللَّهَ هُوَ ٱلۡغَنِىُّ ٱلۡحَمِيدُ
And whosoever turns away (from Faith Allah (الله)s Monotheism), then Allah (الله) is Rich (Free of all wants), Worthy of all praise.
57:25
لَقَدۡ أَرۡسَلۡنَا رُسُلَنَا بِٱلۡبَيِّنَـٰتِ وَأَنزَلۡنَا مَعَهُمُ ٱلۡكِتَـٰبَ وَٱلۡمِيزَانَ لِيَقُومَ ٱلنَّاسُ بِٱلۡقِسۡطِ‌ۖ
Indeed We have sent Our Messengers with clear proofs, and revealed with them the Scripture and the Balance (justice) that mankind may keep up justice.
وَأَنزَلۡنَا ٱلۡحَدِيدَ فِيهِ بَأۡسٌ۬ شَدِيدٌ۬ وَمَنَـٰفِعُ لِلنَّاسِ
And We brought forth iron wherein is mighty power (in matters of war), as well as many benefits for mankind,
وَلِيَعۡلَمَ ٱللَّهُ مَن يَنصُرُهُ ۥ وَرُسُلَهُ ۥ بِٱلۡغَيۡبِ‌ۚ
that Allah (الله) may test who it is that will help Him (His religion), and His Messengers in the unseen.
إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ قَوِىٌّ عَزِيزٌ۬
Verily, Allah (الله) is All-Strong, All-Mighty.
57:[26-29] Prophets Nüh, Ibrãhïm and Isa (Jesus) were sent for guidance to the Right Path. As for the monasticism, people instituted it themselves
57:26
وَلَقَدۡ أَرۡسَلۡنَا نُوحً۬ا وَإِبۡرَٲهِيمَ وَجَعَلۡنَا فِى ذُرِّيَّتِهِمَا ٱلنُّبُوَّةَ وَٱلۡڪِتَـٰبَ‌ۖ
And indeed, We sent Nooh (Noah) and Ibraheem (Abraham), and placed in their offspring Prophethood and Scripture,
فَمِنۡہُم مُّهۡتَدٍ۬‌ۖ وَڪَثِيرٌ۬ مِّنۡہُمۡ فَـٰسِقُونَ
and among them there is he who is guided, but many of them are Fasiqoon (rebellious, disobedient to Allah (الله)).
57:27
ثُمَّ قَفَّيۡنَا عَلَىٰٓ ءَاثَـٰرِهِم بِرُسُلِنَا
Then, We sent after them, Our Messengers,
وَقَفَّيۡنَا بِعِيسَى ٱبۡنِ مَرۡيَمَ وَءَاتَيۡنَـٰهُ ٱلۡإِنجِيلَ
and We sent Isa (Iesa (عيسى)) son of Mary (Maryam (مريم)), and gave him the Injil (Injeel (الإنجيل)).
وَجَعَلۡنَا فِى قُلُوبِ ٱلَّذِينَ ٱتَّبَعُوهُ رَأۡفَةً۬ وَرَحۡمَةً۬
And We ordained in the hearts of those who followed him, compassion and mercy.
وَرَهۡبَانِيَّةً ٱبۡتَدَعُوهَا مَا كَتَبۡنَـٰهَا عَلَيۡهِمۡ
But the Monasticism which they invented for themselves, We did not prescribe for them,
إِلَّا ٱبۡتِغَآءَ رِضۡوَٲنِ ٱللَّهِ فَمَا رَعَوۡهَا حَقَّ رِعَايَتِهَا‌ۖ
but (they sought it) only to please Allah (الله) therewith, but that they did not observe it with the right observance.
فَـَٔاتَيۡنَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُواْ مِنۡہُمۡ أَجۡرَهُمۡ‌ۖ وَكَثِيرٌ۬ مِّنۡہُمۡ فَـٰسِقُونَ
So We gave those among them who believed, their (due) reward, but many of them are Fasiqoon (rebellious, disobedient to Allah (الله)).
57:28
يَـٰٓأَيُّہَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُواْ ٱتَّقُواْ ٱللَّهَ وَءَامِنُواْ بِرَسُولِهِۦ
O you who believe (in Musa (Moosa (موسى)) (i.e. Jews (هُودًا)) and Isa (Iesa (عيسى)) (i.e. Christians (نَصَارَىٰ)))! Fear Allah (الله), and believe too in His Messenger (Muhammad (محمد) SAW),
يُؤۡتِكُمۡ كِفۡلَيۡنِ مِن رَّحۡمَتِهِۦ وَيَجۡعَل لَّڪُمۡ نُورً۬ا تَمۡشُونَ بِهِۦ وَيَغۡفِرۡ لَكُمۡ‌ۚ
He will give you a double portion of His Mercy, and He will give you a light by which you shall walk (straight), and He will forgive you.
وَٱللَّهُ غَفُورٌ۬ رَّحِيمٌ۬
And Allah (الله) is Oft-Forgiving, Most Merciful (Ar-Raheem (ٱلْرَّحِيْمُ)).
57:29
لِّئَلَّا يَعۡلَمَ أَهۡلُ ٱلۡڪِتَـٰبِ أَلَّا يَقۡدِرُونَ عَلَىٰ شَىۡءٍ۬ مِّن فَضۡلِ ٱللَّهِ‌ۙ
So that the people of the Scripture (Jews (هُودًا) and Christians (نَصَارَىٰ)) may know that they have no power whatsoever over the Grace of Allah (الله),
وَأَنَّ ٱلۡفَضۡلَ بِيَدِ ٱللَّهِ يُؤۡتِيهِ مَن يَشَآءُ‌ۚ
and that (His) Grace is (entirely) in His Hand to bestow it on whomsoever He wills.
وَٱللَّهُ ذُو ٱلۡفَضۡلِ ٱلۡعَظِيمِ
And Allah (الله) is the Owner of Great Bounty.*********
