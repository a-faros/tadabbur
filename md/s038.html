Saad
The letter Saad
38:[1-14] The Qur‘an is full of admonition. The unbelievers are in sheer arrogance for calling the Prophets as liars
38:1
صٓ‌ۚ
Sad(These letters (Sad etc.) are one of the miracles of the Quran and none but Allah (Alone) knows their meanings).
وَٱلۡقُرۡءَانِ ذِى ٱلذِّكۡرِ
By the Quran full of reminding.
38:2
بَلِ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُواْ فِى عِزَّةٍ۬ وَشِقَاقٍ۬
Nay, those who disbelieve are in false pride and opposition.
38:3
كَمۡ أَهۡلَكۡنَا مِن قَبۡلِهِم مِّن قَرۡنٍ۬
How many a generation We have destroyed before them,
فَنَادَواْ وَّلَاتَ حِينَ مَنَاصٍ۬
and they cried out when there was no longer time for escape!
38:4
وَعَجِبُوٓاْ أَن جَآءَهُم مُّنذِرٌ۬ مِّنۡہُمۡ‌ۖ
And they (Arab pagans) wonder that a warner (Prophet Muhammad SAW) has come to them from among themselves!
وَقَالَ ٱلۡكَـٰفِرُونَ هَـٰذَا سَـٰحِرٌ۬ كَذَّابٌ
And the disbelievers say: ‘This (Prophet Muhammad SAW) is a sorcerer, a liar.
38:5
أَجَعَلَ ٱلۡأَلِهَةَ إِلَـٰهً۬ا وَٲحِدًا‌ۖ
Has he made the aliha (gods) (all) into One Ilah (God - Allah).
إِنَّ هَـٰذَا لَشَىۡءٌ عُجَابٌ۬
Verily, this is a curious thing!‘
38:6
وَٱنطَلَقَ ٱلۡمَلَأُ مِنۡہُمۡ أَنِ ٱمۡشُواْ وَٱصۡبِرُواْ عَلَىٰٓ ءَالِهَتِكُمۡ‌ۖ
And the leaders among them went about (saying): ‘Go on, and remain constant to your aliha (gods)!
إِنَّ هَـٰذَا لَشَىۡءٌ۬ يُرَادُ
Verily, This is a thing designed (against you)!
38:7
مَا سَمِعۡنَا بِہَـٰذَا فِى ٱلۡمِلَّةِ ٱلۡأَخِرَةِ إِنۡ هَـٰذَآ إِلَّا ٱخۡتِلَـٰقٌ
We have not heard (the like) of this among the people of these later days. This is nothing but an invention!
38:8
أَءُنزِلَ عَلَيۡهِ ٱلذِّكۡرُ مِنۢ بَيۡنِنَا‌ۚ
Has the Reminder been sent down to him (alone) from among us?‘
بَلۡ هُمۡ فِى شَكٍّ۬ مِّن ذِكۡرِى‌ۖ
Nay! but they are in doubt about My Reminder (this Quran)!
بَل لَّمَّا يَذُوقُواْ عَذَابِ
Nay, but they have not tasted (My) Torment!
38:9
أَمۡ عِندَهُمۡ خَزَآٮِٕنُ رَحۡمَةِ رَبِّكَ
Or have they the treasures of the Mercy of your Lord,
ٱلۡعَزِيزِ ٱلۡوَهَّابِ
the All-Mighty, the Real Bestower?
38:10
أَمۡ لَهُم مُّلۡكُ ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٲتِ وَٱلۡأَرۡضِ وَمَا بَيۡنَہُمَا‌ۖ
Or is it that the dominion of the heavens and the earth and all that is between them is theirs?
فَلۡيَرۡتَقُواْ فِى ٱلۡأَسۡبَـٰبِ
If so, let them ascend up with means (to the heavens)!
38:11
جُندٌ۬ مَّا هُنَالِكَ مَهۡزُومٌ۬ مِّنَ ٱلۡأَحۡزَابِ
(As they denied Allah‘s Message)
they will be a defeated host like the confederates of the old times (who were defeated).
38:12
كَذَّبَتۡ قَبۡلَهُمۡ قَوۡمُ نُوحٍ۬ وَعَادٌ۬ وَفِرۡعَوۡنُ ذُو ٱلۡأَوۡتَادِ
Before them (were many who) belied Messengers, the people of Nooh (Noah); and Ad; and Firaun (Pharaoh) the men of stakes (with which he used to punish the people),
38:13
وَثَمُودُ وَقَوۡمُ لُوطٍ۬ وَأَصۡحَـٰبُ لۡـَٔيۡكَةِ‌ۚ
And Thamood, and the people of Lout (Lot), and the dwellers of the wood;
أُوْلَـٰٓٮِٕكَ ٱلۡأَحۡزَابُ
such were the confederates.
38:14
إِن كُلٌّ إِلَّا ڪَذَّبَ ٱلرُّسُلَ فَحَقَّ عِقَابِ
Not one of them but belied the Messengers, therefore My Torment was justified,
38:[15-26] The story of the Prophet Dawõõd, with whom mountains and birds used to sing the rhymes of Allah. The story of the two litigants who came to Dawõõd for a decision
38:15
وَمَا يَنظُرُ هَـٰٓؤُلَآءِ إِلَّا صَيۡحَةً۬ وَٲحِدَةً۬ مَّا لَهَا مِن فَوَاقٍ۬
And these only wait for a single Saihah (shout (i.e. the blowing of the Trumpet by the angel Israfil (Sarafil)) there will be no pause or ending thereto (till everything will perish except Allah (the only God full of Majesty, Bounty and Honour)).
38:16
وَقَالُواْ رَبَّنَا عَجِّل لَّنَا قِطَّنَا قَبۡلَ يَوۡمِ ٱلۡحِسَابِ
They say: ‘Our Lord! Hasten to us Qittana (i.e. our Record of good and bad deeds so that we see it) before the Day of Reckoning!‘
38:17
ٱصۡبِرۡ عَلَىٰ مَا يَقُولُونَ وَٱذۡكُرۡ عَبۡدَنَا دَاوُ ۥدَ ذَا ٱلۡأَيۡدِ‌ۖ
Be patient (O Muhammad SAW) of what they say, and remember Our slave Dawood (David), endued with power.
إِنَّهُ ۥۤ أَوَّابٌ
Verily, he was ever oft-returning in all matters and in repentance (toward Allah).
38:18
إِنَّا سَخَّرۡنَا ٱلۡجِبَالَ مَعَهُ ۥ يُسَبِّحۡنَ بِٱلۡعَشِىِّ وَٱلۡإِشۡرَاقِ
Verily, We made the mountains to glorify Our Praises with him (Dawood (David)) in the Ashi (i.e. after the mid-day till sunset) and Ishraq (i.e. after the sunrise till mid-day).
38:19
وَٱلطَّيۡرَ مَحۡشُورَةً۬‌ۖ
And (so did) the birds assembled:
كُلٌّ۬ لَّهُ ۥۤ أَوَّابٌ۬
all with him (Dawood (David)) did turn (to Allah i.e. glorified His Praises).
38:20
وَشَدَدۡنَا مُلۡكَهُ ۥوَءَاتَيۡنَـٰهُ ٱلۡحِكۡمَةَ وَفَصۡلَ ٱلۡخِطَابِ
We made his kingdom strong and gave him Al-Hikmah (Prophethood, etc.) and sound judgement in speech and decision.
38:21
وَهَلۡ أَتَٮٰكَ نَبَؤُاْ ٱلۡخَصۡمِ إِذۡ تَسَوَّرُواْ ٱلۡمِحۡرَابَ
And has the news of the litigants reached you? When they climbed over the wall into (his) Mihrab (a praying place or a private room,).
38:22
إِذۡ دَخَلُواْ عَلَىٰ دَاوُ ۥدَ فَفَزِعَ مِنۡہُمۡ‌ۖ
When they entered in upon Dawood (David), he was terrified of them,
قَالُواْ لَا تَخَفۡ‌ۖ
they said: ‘Fear not!
خَصۡمَانِ بَغَىٰ بَعۡضُنَا عَلَىٰ بَعۡضٍ۬ فَٱحۡكُم بَيۡنَنَا بِٱلۡحَقِّ وَلَا تُشۡطِطۡ
(We are) two litigants, one of whom has wronged the other, therefore judge between us with truth, and treat us not with injustice,
وَٱهۡدِنَآ إِلَىٰ سَوَآءِ ٱلصِّرَٲطِ
and guide us to the Right Way.
38:23
إِنَّ هَـٰذَآ أَخِى لَهُ ۥ تِسۡعٌ۬ وَتِسۡعُونَ نَعۡجَةً۬ وَلِىَ نَعۡجَةٌ۬ وَٲحِدَةٌ۬
Verily, this my brother (in religion) has ninety nine ewes, while I have (only) one ewe,
فَقَالَ أَكۡفِلۡنِيہَا وَعَزَّنِى فِى ٱلۡخِطَابِ
and he says: ‘Hand it over to me, and he overpowered me in speech.‘
38:24
قَالَ لَقَدۡ ظَلَمَكَ بِسُؤَالِ نَعۡجَتِكَ إِلَىٰ نِعَاجِهِۦ‌ۖ
(Dawood (David)) said (immediately without listening to the opponent):
He has wronged you in demanding your ewe in addition to his ewes.
وَإِنَّ كَثِيرً۬ا مِّنَ ٱلۡخُلَطَآءِ لَيَبۡغِى بَعۡضُہُمۡ عَلَىٰ بَعۡضٍ
And, verily, many partners oppress one another,
إِلَّا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُواْ وَعَمِلُواْ ٱلصَّـٰلِحَـٰتِ وَقَلِيلٌ۬ مَّا هُمۡ‌ۗ
except those who believe and do righteous good deeds, and they are few.‘
وَظَنَّ دَاوُ ۥدُ أَنَّمَا فَتَنَّـٰهُ
And Dawood (David) guessed that We have tried him
فَٱسۡتَغۡفَرَ رَبَّهُ ۥ وَخَرَّ رَاكِعً۬ا وَأَنَابَ ۩
and he sought Forgiveness of his Lord, and he fell down prostrate and turned (to Allah) in repentance.
38:25
فَغَفَرۡنَا لَهُ ۥ ذَٲلِكَ‌ۖ
So We forgave him that,
وَإِنَّ لَهُ ۥ عِندَنَا لَزُلۡفَىٰ وَحُسۡنَ مَـَٔابٍ۬
and verily, for him is a near access to Us, and a good place of (final) return (Paradise).
38:26
يَـٰدَاوُ ۥدُ إِنَّا جَعَلۡنَـٰكَ خَلِيفَةً۬ فِى ٱلۡأَرۡضِ فَٱحۡكُم بَيۡنَ ٱلنَّاسِ بِٱلۡحَقِّ
O Dawood (David)! Verily! We have placed you as a successor on earth, so judge you between men in truth (and justice)
وَلَا تَتَّبِعِ ٱلۡهَوَىٰ فَيُضِلَّكَ عَن سَبِيلِ ٱللَّهِ‌ۚ
and follow not your desire for it will mislead you from the Path of Allah.
إِنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ يَضِلُّونَ عَن سَبِيلِ ٱللَّهِ لَهُمۡ عَذَابٌ۬ شَدِيدُۢ بِمَا نَسُواْ يَوۡمَ ٱلۡحِسَابِ
Verily! Those who wander astray from the Path of Allah (shall) have a severe torment, because they forgot the Day of Reckoning.
38:[27-29] Allah has not created the heavens and the earth in vain
38:[30-40] The story of Sulaimãn‘s inspection of steeds to be used in Jihãd. The Prophet Sulaimãn‘s prayer of granting him a kingdom, similar of which may not be granted to anyone else
38:27
وَمَا خَلَقۡنَا ٱلسَّمَآءَ وَٱلۡأَرۡضَ وَمَا بَيۡنَہُمَا بَـٰطِلاً۬‌ۚ
And We created not the heaven and the earth and all that is between them without purpose!
ذَٲلِكَ ظَنُّ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُواْ‌ۚ
That is the consideration of those who disbelieve!
فَوَيۡلٌ۬ لِّلَّذِينَ كَفَرُواْ مِنَ ٱلنَّارِ
Then woe to those who disbelieve (in Islamic Monotheism) from the Fire!
38:28
أَمۡ نَجۡعَلُ ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُواْ وَعَمِلُواْ ٱلصَّـٰلِحَـٰتِ كَٱلۡمُفۡسِدِينَ فِى ٱلۡأَرۡضِ
Shall We treat those who believe (in the Oneness of Allah Islamic Monotheism) and do righteous good deeds, as Mufsidoon (those who associate partners in worship with Allah and commit crimes) on earth?
أَمۡ نَجۡعَلُ ٱلۡمُتَّقِينَ كَٱلۡفُجَّارِ
Or shall We treat the Muttaqoon (pious - see V.2:2), as the Fujjar (criminals, disbelievers, wicked, etc)?
38:29
كِتَـٰبٌ أَنزَلۡنَـٰهُ إِلَيۡكَ مُبَـٰرَكٌ۬ لِّيَدَّبَّرُوٓاْ ءَايَـٰتِهِۦ
(This is) a Book (the Quran) which We have sent down to you, full of blessings that they may ponder over its Verses,
وَلِيَتَذَكَّرَ أُوْلُواْ ٱلۡأَلۡبَـٰبِ
and that men of understanding may remember.
38:30
وَوَهَبۡنَا لِدَاوُ ۥدَ سُلَيۡمَـٰنَ‌ۚ
And to Dawood (David) We gave Suleiman (Solomon).
نِعۡمَ ٱلۡعَبۡدُ‌ۖ إِنَّهُ ۥۤ أَوَّابٌ
How excellent (a) slave! Verily, he was ever oft-returning in repentance (to Us)!
38:31
إِذۡ عُرِضَ عَلَيۡهِ بِٱلۡعَشِىِّ ٱلصَّـٰفِنَـٰتُ ٱلۡجِيَادُ
When there were displayed before him, in the afternoon, well trained horses of the highest breed for Jihad (holy fighting in Allah‘s Cause)).
38:32
فَقَالَ إِنِّىٓ أَحۡبَبۡتُ حُبَّ ٱلۡخَيۡرِ عَن ذِكۡرِ رَبِّى حَتَّىٰ تَوَارَتۡ بِٱلۡحِجَابِ
And he said: ‘Alas! I did love the good (these horses) instead of remembering my Lord (in my Asr prayer)‘ till the time was over, and (the sun) had hidden in the veil (of night).
38:33
رُدُّوهَا عَلَىَّ‌ۖ
Then he said ‘Bring them (horses) back to me.‘
فَطَفِقَ مَسۡحَۢا بِٱلسُّوقِ وَٱلۡأَعۡنَاقِ
Then he began to pass his hand over their legs and their necks (till the end of the display).
38:34
وَلَقَدۡ فَتَنَّا سُلَيۡمَـٰنَ وَأَلۡقَيۡنَا عَلَىٰ كُرۡسِيِّهِۦ جَسَدً۬ا
And, indeed We did try Suleiman (Solomon) and We placed on his throne Jasadan (a devil, so he lost his kingdom for a while)
ثُمَّ أَنَابَ
but he did return (to his throne and kingdom by the Grace of Allah and he did return) to Allah with obedience and in repentance.
38:35
قَالَ رَبِّ ٱغۡفِرۡ لِى وَهَبۡ لِى مُلۡكً۬ا لَّا يَنۢبَغِى لِأَحَدٍ۬ مِّنۢ بَعۡدِىٓ‌ۖ
He said: ‘My Lord! Forgive me, and bestow upon me a kingdom such as shall not belong to any other after me:
إِنَّكَ أَنتَ ٱلۡوَهَّابُ
Verily, You are the Bestower.‘
38:36
فَسَخَّرۡنَا لَهُ ٱلرِّيحَ تَجۡرِى بِأَمۡرِهِۦ رُخَآءً حَيۡثُ أَصَابَ
So, We subjected to him the wind, it blew gently to his order whithersoever he willed,
38:37
وَٱلشَّيَـٰطِينَ كُلَّ بَنَّآءٍ۬ وَغَوَّاصٍ۬
And also the Shayatin (devils) from the jinns (including) every kind of builder and diver,
38:38
وَءَاخَرِينَ مُقَرَّنِينَ فِى ٱلۡأَصۡفَادِ
And also others bound in fetters.
38:39
هَـٰذَا عَطَآؤُنَافَٱمۡنُنۡ أَوۡ أَمۡسِكۡ بِغَيۡرِ حِسَابٍ۬
(Saying of Allah to Suleiman (Solomon)):
This is Our gift, so spend you or withhold, no account will be asked.‘
38:40
وَإِنَّ لَهُ ۥ عِندَنَا لَزُلۡفَىٰ وَحُسۡنَ مَـَٔابٍ۬
And verily, he enjoyed a near access to Us, and a good final return (Paradise).
38:[41-48] The story of Ayüb (Job), his sickness and relief
38:[49-64] The Qur‘an is but a reminder about the reward of paradise and punishment of the hellfire
38:41
وَٱذۡكُرۡ عَبۡدَنَآ أَيُّوبَ
And remember Our slave Ayoob (Job),
إِذۡ نَادَىٰ رَبَّهُ ۥۤ أَنِّى مَسَّنِىَ ٱلشَّيۡطَـٰنُ بِنُصۡبٍ۬ وَعَذَابٍ
when he invoked his Lord (saying): ‘Verily! Shaitan (Satan) has touched me with distress (by losing my health) and torment (by losing my wealth)!
38:42
ٱرۡكُضۡ بِرِجۡلِكَ‌ۖ
(Allah said to him): ‘Strike the ground with your foot:
هَـٰذَا مُغۡتَسَلُۢ بَارِدٌ۬ وَشَرَابٌ۬
This is a spring of water to wash in, cool and a (refreshing) drink.‘
38:43
وَوَهَبۡنَا لَهُ ۥۤ أَهۡلَهُ ۥ وَمِثۡلَهُم مَّعَهُمۡ رَحۡمَةً۬ مِّنَّا
And We gave him (back) his family, and along with them the like thereof, as a Mercy from Us,
وَذِكۡرَىٰ لِأُوْلِى ٱلۡأَلۡبَـٰبِ
and a Reminder for those who understand.
38:44
وَخُذۡ بِيَدِكَ ضِغۡثً۬ا فَٱضۡرِب بِّهِۦ وَلَا تَحۡنَثۡ‌ۗ
And take in your hand a bundle of thin grass and strike therewith (your wife), and break not your oath .
إِنَّا وَجَدۡنَـٰهُ صَابِرً۬ا‌ۚ
Truly! We found him patient.
نِّعۡمَ ٱلۡعَبۡدُ‌ۖ إِنَّهُ ۥۤ أَوَّابٌ۬
How excellent (a) slave! Verily, he was ever oft-returning in repentance (to Us)!
38:45
وَٱذۡكُرۡ عِبَـٰدَنَآ إِبۡرَٲهِيمَ وَإِسۡحَـٰقَ وَيَعۡقُوبَ أُوْلِى ٱلۡأَيۡدِى وَٱلۡأَبۡصَـٰرِ
And remember Our slaves, Abraham (Ibrahim), Ishaque (Ishak), and Yaqoob (Yaqqub), (all) owners of strength (in worshipping Us) and (also) of religious understanding.
38:46
إِنَّآ أَخۡلَصۡنَـٰهُم بِخَالِصَةٍ۬ ذِڪۡرَى ٱلدَّارِ
Verily, We did choose them by granting them (a good thing, i.e.) the remembrance of the home.(in the Hereafter and they used to make the people remember it, and also they used to invite the people to obey Allah and to do good deeds for the Hereafter).
38:47
وَإِنَّہُمۡ عِندَنَا لَمِنَ ٱلۡمُصۡطَفَيۡنَ ٱلۡأَخۡيَارِ
And they are with Us, verily, of the chosen and the best!
38:48
وَٱذۡكُرۡ إِسۡمَـٰعِيلَ وَٱلۡيَسَعَ وَذَا ٱلۡكِفۡلِ‌ۖ وَكُلٌّ۬ مِّنَ ٱلۡأَخۡيَارِ
And remember Ismail (Ishmail), AlYasaa (Elisha), and Dhul-Kifl (Isaiah), all are among the best.
38:49
هَـٰذَا ذِكۡرٌ۬‌ۚ
This is a Reminder,
وَإِنَّ لِلۡمُتَّقِينَ لَحُسۡنَ مَـَٔابٍ۬
and verily, for the Muttaqoon (pious and righteous persons - see V.2:2) is a good final return (Paradise), -,
38:50
جَنَّـٰتِ عَدۡنٍ۬ مُّفَتَّحَةً۬ لَّهُمُ ٱلۡأَبۡوَٲبُ
Adn (Edn) Paradise (everlasting Gardens), whose doors will be open for them,(It is said (in Tafsir At-Tabaree, Part 23, Page 174) that one can speak to the doors, just one tells it to open and close, and it will open or close as it is ordered).
38:51
مُتَّكِـِٔينَ فِيہَا يَدۡعُونَ فِيہَا بِفَـٰكِهَةٍ۬ ڪَثِيرَةٍ۬ وَشَرَابٍ۬
Therein they will recline; therein they will call for fruits in abundance and drinks;
38:52
وَعِندَهُمۡ قَـٰصِرَٲتُ ٱلطَّرۡفِ أَتۡرَابٌ
And beside them will be chaste females (virgins) restraining their glances only for their husbands, (and) of equal ages.
38:53
هَـٰذَا مَا تُوعَدُونَ لِيَوۡمِ ٱلۡحِسَابِ
This it is what you (Al-Muttaqoon - the pious) are promised for the Day of Reckoning!
38:54
إِنَّ هَـٰذَا لَرِزۡقُنَا مَا لَهُ ۥ مِن نَّفَادٍ
(It will be said to them)!
Verily, this is Our Provision which will never finish;
38:55
هَـٰذَا‌ۚ
This is so!
وَإِنَّ لِلطَّـٰغِينَ لَشَرَّ مَـَٔابٍ۬
And for the Taghoon (transgressors, disobedient to Allah and His Messenger - disbelievers in the Oneness of Allah, criminals, etc.), will be an evil final return (Fire),
38:56
جَهَنَّمَ يَصۡلَوۡنَہَا فَبِئۡسَ ٱلۡمِهَادُ
Hell! Where they will burn, and worst (indeed) is that place to rest!
38:57
هَـٰذَا فَلۡيَذُوقُوهُ حَمِيمٌ۬ وَغَسَّاقٌ۬
This is so! Then let them taste it, a boiling fluid and dirty wound discharges.
38:58
وَءَاخَرُ مِن شَكۡلِهِۦۤ أَزۡوَٲجٌ
And other torments of similar kind, all together!
38:59
هَـٰذَا فَوۡجٌ۬ مُّقۡتَحِمٌ۬ مَّعَكُمۡ‌ۖ
This is a troop entering with you (in Hell),
لَا مَرۡحَبَۢا بِہِمۡ‌ۚ إِنَّہُمۡ صَالُواْ ٱلنَّارِ
no welcome for them! Verily, they shall burn in the Fire!
38:60
قَالُواْ بَلۡ أَنتُمۡ لَا مَرۡحَبَۢا بِكُمۡ‌ۖ أَنتُمۡ قَدَّمۡتُمُوهُ لَنَا‌ۖ
(The followers of the misleaders will say):
Nay, you (too)! No welcome for you! It is you (misleaders) who brought this upon us (because you misled us in the world),
فَبِئۡسَ ٱلۡقَرَارُ
so evil is this place to stay in!‘
38:61
قَالُواْ رَبَّنَا مَن قَدَّمَ لَنَا هَـٰذَا فَزِدۡهُ عَذَابً۬ا ضِعۡفً۬ا فِى ٱلنَّارِ
They will say: ‘Our Lord! Whoever brought this upon us, add to him a double torment in the Fire!‘
38:62
وَقَالُواْ مَا لَنَا لَا نَرَىٰ رِجَالاً۬ كُنَّا نَعُدُّهُم مِّنَ ٱلۡأَشۡرَارِ
And they will say: ‘What is the matter with us that we see not men whom we used to count among the bad ones?‘
38:63
أَتَّخَذۡنَـٰهُمۡ سِخۡرِيًّا أَمۡ زَاغَتۡ عَنۡہُمُ ٱلۡأَبۡصَـٰرُ
Did we take them as an object of mockery, or have (our) eyes failed to perceive them?‘
38:64
إِنَّ ذَٲلِكَ لَحَقٌّ۬ تَخَاصُمُ أَهۡلِ ٱلنَّارِ
Verily, that is the very truth, the mutual dispute of the people of the Fire!
38:[65-70] The mission of the Rasools is to warn people and declare that there is no divinity except Allah
38:[71-88] The story of the creation of Adam and disobedience of Iblees (Shaitãn)
38:65
قُلۡ إِنَّمَآ أَنَا۟ مُنذِرٌ۬‌ۖ
Say (O Muhammad SAW): ‘I am only a warner
وَمَا مِنۡ إِلَـٰهٍ إِلَّا ٱللَّهُ ٱلۡوَٲحِدُ ٱلۡقَهَّارُ
and there is no Ilah (God) except Allah (none has the right to be worshipped but Allah) the One, the Irresistible,
38:66
رَبُّ ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٲتِ وَٱلۡأَرۡضِ وَمَا بَيۡنَہُمَا ٱلۡعَزِيزُ ٱلۡغَفَّـٰرُ
The Lord of the heavens and the earth and all that is between them, the All-Mighty, the Oft- Forgiving.‘
38:67
قُلۡ هُوَ نَبَؤٌاْ عَظِيمٌ
Say: ‘That (this Quran) is a great news,
38:68
أَنتُمۡ عَنۡهُ مُعۡرِضُونَ
From which you turn away!
38:69
مَا كَانَ لِىَ مِنۡ عِلۡمِۭ بِٱلۡمَلَإِ ٱلۡأَعۡلَىٰٓ إِذۡ يَخۡتَصِمُونَ
I had no knowledge of the chiefs (angels) on high when they were disputing and discussing (about the creation of Adam).
38:70
إِن يُوحَىٰٓ إِلَىَّ إِلَّآ أَنَّمَآ أَنَا۟ نَذِيرٌ۬ مُّبِينٌ
Only this has been inspired to me, that I am a plain warner.‘
38:71
إِذۡ قَالَ رَبُّكَ لِلۡمَلَـٰٓٮِٕكَةِ إِنِّى خَـٰلِقُۢ بَشَرً۬ا مِّن طِينٍ۬
(Remember) when your Lord said to the angels: ‘Truly, I am going to create man from clay‘.
38:72
فَإِذَا سَوَّيۡتُهُ ۥ وَنَفَخۡتُ فِيهِ مِن رُّوحِى فَقَعُواْ لَهُ ۥ سَـٰجِدِينَ
So when I have fashioned him and breathed into him (his) soul created by Me, then you fall down prostrate to him.‘
38:73
فَسَجَدَ ٱلۡمَلَـٰٓٮِٕكَةُ ڪُلُّهُمۡ أَجۡمَعُونَ
So the angels prostrated themselves, all of them:
38:74
إِلَّآ إِبۡلِيسَ ٱسۡتَكۡبَرَ وَكَانَ مِنَ ٱلۡكَـٰفِرِينَ
Except Iblees (Satan) he was proud and was one of the disbelievers.
38:75
قَالَ يَـٰٓإِبۡلِيسُ مَا مَنَعَكَ أَن تَسۡجُدَ لِمَا خَلَقۡتُ بِيَدَىَّ‌ۖ
(Allah) said: ‘O Iblees (Satan)! What prevents you from prostrating yourself to one whom I have created with Both My Hands.
أَسۡتَكۡبَرۡتَ أَمۡ كُنتَ مِنَ ٱلۡعَالِينَ
Are you too proud (to fall prostrate to Adam) or are you one of the high exalted?‘
38:76
قَالَ أَنَا۟ خَيۡرٌ۬ مِّنۡهُ‌ۖ
(Iblees (Satan)) said: ‘I am better than he,
خَلَقۡتَنِى مِن نَّارٍ۬ وَخَلَقۡتَهُ ۥ مِن طِينٍ۬
You created me from fire, and You created him from clay.‘
38:77
قَالَ فَٱخۡرُجۡ مِنۡہَا فَإِنَّكَ رَجِيمٌ۬
(Allah) said: ‘Then get out from here, for verily, you are outcast.
38:78
وَإِنَّ عَلَيۡكَ لَعۡنَتِىٓ إِلَىٰ يَوۡمِ ٱلدِّينِ
And verily!, My Curse is on you till the Day of Recompense.‘
38:79
قَالَ رَبِّ فَأَنظِرۡنِىٓ إِلَىٰ يَوۡمِ يُبۡعَثُونَ
(Iblees (Satan)) said: ‘My Lord! Give me then respite till the Day the (dead) are resurrected.‘
38:80
قَالَ فَإِنَّكَ مِنَ ٱلۡمُنظَرِينَ
(Allah) said: ‘Verily! You are of those allowed respite
38:81
إِلَىٰ يَوۡمِ ٱلۡوَقۡتِ ٱلۡمَعۡلُومِ
Till the Day of the time appointed.‘
38:82
قَالَ فَبِعِزَّتِكَ لَأُغۡوِيَنَّهُمۡ أَجۡمَعِينَ
(Iblees (Satan)) said: ‘By Your Might, then I will surely mislead them all,
38:83
إِلَّا عِبَادَكَ مِنۡهُمُ ٱلۡمُخۡلَصِينَ
Except Your chosen slaves amongst them (faithful, obedient, true believers of Islamic Monotheism).‘
38:84
قَالَ فَٱلۡحَقُّ وَٱلۡحَقَّ أَقُولُ
(Allah) said: ‘The Truth is, and the Truth I say,
38:85
لَأَمۡلَأَنَّ جَهَنَّمَ مِنكَ وَمِمَّن تَبِعَكَ مِنۡہُمۡ أَجۡمَعِينَ
That I will fill Hell with you (Iblees (Satan)) and those of them (mankind) that follow you, together.‘
38:86
قُلۡ مَآ أَسۡـَٔلُكُمۡ عَلَيۡهِ مِنۡ أَجۡرٍ۬ وَمَآ أَنَا۟ مِنَ ٱلۡمُتَكَلِّفِينَ
Say (O Muhammad SAW): ‘No wage do I ask of you for this (the Quran), nor am I one of the Mutakallifoon (those who pretend and fabricate things which do not exist).
38:87
إِنۡ هُوَ إِلَّا ذِكۡرٌ۬ لِّلۡعَـٰلَمِينَ
It (this Quran) is only a Reminder for all the Alameen (mankind and jinns).
38:88
وَلَتَعۡلَمُنَّ نَبَأَهُ ۥ بَعۡدَ حِينِۭ
And you shall certainly know the truth of it after a while.‘*********
