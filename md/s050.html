Qaaf
The letter Qaaf
50:[1-15] Life after death is a reality and there is nothing strange about it
50:1
قٓ‌ۚ
Qaf(These letters (Qaf, etc.) are one of the miracles of the Quran (القرآن), and none but Allah (الله) (Alone) knows their meanings).
وَٱلۡقُرۡءَانِ ٱلۡمَجِيدِ
By the Glorious Quran (القرآن).
50:2
بَلۡ عَجِبُوٓاْ أَن جَآءَهُم مُّنذِرٌ۬ مِّنۡهُمۡ
Nay, they wonder that there has come to them a warner (Muhammad (محمد) SAW) from among themselves.
فَقَالَ ٱلۡكَـٰفِرُونَ هَـٰذَا شَىۡءٌ عَجِيبٌ
So the disbelievers say: ‘This is a strange thing!
50:3
أَءِذَا مِتۡنَا وَكُنَّا تُرَابً۬ا‌ۖ
When we are dead and have become dust (shall we be resurrected?)
ذَٲلِكَ رَجۡعُۢ بَعِيدٌ۬
That is a far return.‘
50:4
قَدۡ عَلِمۡنَا مَا تَنقُصُ ٱلۡأَرۡضُ مِنۡہُمۡ‌ۖ
We know that which the earth takes of them (their dead bodies),
وَعِندَنَا كِتَـٰبٌ حَفِيظُۢ
and with Us is a Book preserved (i.e. the Book of Decrees).
50:5
بَلۡ كَذَّبُواْ بِٱلۡحَقِّ لَمَّا جَآءَهُمۡ فَهُمۡ فِىٓ أَمۡرٍ۬ مَّرِيجٍ
Nay, but they have denied the truth (this Quran (القرآن)) when it has come to them, so they are in a confused state (can not differentiate between right and wrong).
50:6
أَفَلَمۡ يَنظُرُوٓاْ إِلَى ٱلسَّمَآءِ فَوۡقَهُمۡ كَيۡفَ بَنَيۡنَـٰهَاوَزَيَّنَّـٰهَا
Have they not looked at the heaven above them, how We have made it and adorned it,
وَمَا لَهَا مِن فُرُوجٍ۬
and there are no rifts in it?
50:7
وَٱلۡأَرۡضَ مَدَدۡنَـٰهَا وَأَلۡقَيۡنَا فِيہَا رَوَٲسِىَ
And the earth! We have spread it out, and set thereon mountains standing firm,
وَأَنۢبَتۡنَا فِيہَا مِن كُلِّ زَوۡجِۭ بَهِيجٍ۬
and have produced therein every kind of lovely growth (plants).
50:8
تَبۡصِرَةً۬ وَذِكۡرَىٰ لِكُلِّ عَبۡدٍ۬ مُّنِيبٍ۬
An insight and a Reminder for every slave turning to Allah (الله).(i.e. the one who believes in Allah (الله) and performs deeds of His obedience, and always begs His pardon)
50:9
وَنَزَّلۡنَا مِنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ مَآءً۬ مُّبَـٰرَكً۬ا
And We send down blessed water (rain) from the sky,
فَأَنۢبَتۡنَا بِهِۦ جَنَّـٰتٍ۬ وَحَبَّ ٱلۡحَصِيدِ
then We produce therewith gardens and grain (every kind of harvests) that are reaped.
50:10
وَٱلنَّخۡلَ بَاسِقَـٰتٍ۬ لَّهَا طَلۡعٌ۬ نَّضِيدٌ۬
And tall date-palms, with ranged clusters;
50:11
رِّزۡقً۬ا لِّلۡعِبَادِ‌ۖ
A provision for (Allah (الله)‘s) slaves.
وَأَحۡيَيۡنَا بِهِۦ بَلۡدَةً۬ مَّيۡتً۬ا‌ۚ
And We give life therewith to a dead land.
كَذَٲلِكَ ٱلۡخُرُوجُ
Thus will be the resurrection (of the dead).
50:12
كَذَّبَتۡ قَبۡلَهُمۡ قَوۡمُ نُوحٍ۬ وَأَصۡحَـٰبُ ٱلرَّسِّ وَثَمُودُ
Denied before them (i.e. these pagans of Makkah (مكة) who denied you, O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) the people of Nooh (Noah), and the dwellers of Rass, and the Thamood,
50:13
وَعَادٌ۬ وَفِرۡعَوۡنُ وَإِخۡوَٲنُ لُوطٍ۬
And Ad, and Firaun (Pharaoh (فرعون)), and the brethren of Lout (Lot),
50:14
وَأَصۡحَـٰبُ ٱلۡأَيۡكَةِ وَقَوۡمُ تُبَّعٍ۬‌ۚ
And the dwellers of theWood, and the people of Tubba (تبع);
كُلٌّ۬ كَذَّبَ ٱلرُّسُلَ فَحَقَّ وَعِيدِ
everyone of them denied (their) Messengers, so My Threat took effect.
50:15
أَفَعَيِينَا بِٱلۡخَلۡقِ ٱلۡأَوَّلِ‌ۚ
Were We then tired with the first creation?
بَلۡ هُمۡ فِى لَبۡسٍ۬ مِّنۡ خَلۡقٍ۬ جَدِيدٍ۬
Nay, They are in confused doubt about a new creation (i.e. Resurrection)?
50:[16-29] Allah has assigned two angels to each person for noting each single word that he utters. Every stubborn disbeliever will be thrown into hell
50:16
وَلَقَدۡ خَلَقۡنَا ٱلۡإِنسَـٰنَ وَنَعۡلَمُ مَا تُوَسۡوِسُ بِهِۦ نَفۡسُهُ ۥ‌ۖ
And indeed We have created man, and We know what his ownself whispers to him.
وَنَحۡنُ أَقۡرَبُ إِلَيۡهِ مِنۡ حَبۡلِ ٱلۡوَرِيدِ
And We are nearer to him than his jugular vein (by Our Knowledge).
50:17
إِذۡ يَتَلَقَّى ٱلۡمُتَلَقِّيَانِ عَنِ ٱلۡيَمِينِ وَعَنِ ٱلشِّمَالِ قَعِيدٌ۬
(Remember!) that the two receivers (recording angels) receive (each human being after he or she has attained the age of puberty), one sitting on the right and one on the left (to note his or her actions).
50:18
مَّا يَلۡفِظُ مِن قَوۡلٍ إِلَّا لَدَيۡهِ رَقِيبٌ عَتِيدٌ۬
Not a word does he (or she) utter, but there is a watcher by him ready (to record it).
50:19
وَجَآءَتۡ سَكۡرَةُ ٱلۡمَوۡتِ بِٱلۡحَقِّ‌ۖ
And the stupor of death will come in truth:
ذَٲلِكَ مَا كُنتَ مِنۡهُ تَحِيدُ
This is what you have been avoiding!‘
50:20
وَنُفِخَ فِى ٱلصُّورِ‌ۚ
And the Trumpet will be blown,
ذَٲلِكَ يَوۡمُ ٱلۡوَعِيدِ
that will be the Day whereof warning (had been given)(i.e. the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة)).
50:21
وَجَآءَتۡ كُلُّ نَفۡسٍ۬ مَّعَهَا سَآٮِٕقٌ۬ وَشَہِيدٌ۬
And every person will come forth along with an (angel) to drive (him), and an (angel) to bear witness.
50:22
لَّقَدۡ كُنتَ فِى غَفۡلَةٍ۬ مِّنۡ هَـٰذَا
(It will be said to the sinners):
Indeed you were heedless of this,
فَكَشَفۡنَا عَنكَ غِطَآءَكَ فَبَصَرُكَ ٱلۡيَوۡمَ حَدِيدٌ۬
now We have removed your covering, and sharp is your sight this Day!‘
50:23
وَقَالَ قَرِينُهُ ۥ هَـٰذَا مَا لَدَىَّ عَتِيدٌ
And his companion (angel) will say: ‘Here is (this Record) ready with me!‘
50:24
أَلۡقِيَا فِى جَهَنَّمَ كُلَّ ڪَفَّارٍ عَنِيدٍ۬
(And it will be said):
Both of you throw (Order from Allah (الله) to the two angels) into Hell (جهنم), every stubborn disbeliever (in the Oneness of Allah (الله), in His Messengers, etc.).
50:25
مَّنَّاعٍ۬ لِّلۡخَيۡرِ مُعۡتَدٍ۬ مُّرِيبٍ
‘Hinderer of good, transgressor, doubter,
50:26
ٱلَّذِى جَعَلَ مَعَ ٱللَّهِ إِلَـٰهًا ءَاخَرَ فَأَلۡقِيَاهُ فِى ٱلۡعَذَابِ ٱلشَّدِيدِ
Who set up another ilah (god) with Allah (الله), then (both of you) cast him in the severe torment.‘
50:27
قَالَ قَرِينُهُ ۥ رَبَّنَا مَآ أَطۡغَيۡتُهُ ۥ وَلَـٰكِن كَانَ فِى ضَلَـٰلِۭ بَعِيدٍ۬
His companion (Satan (الشيطان) devil)) will say: ‘Our Lord! I did not push him to transgress, (in disbelief, oppression, and evil deeds) but he was himself in error far astray.‘
50:28
قَالَ لَا تَخۡتَصِمُواْ لَدَىَّ وَقَدۡ قَدَّمۡتُ إِلَيۡكُم بِٱلۡوَعِيدِ
Allah (الله) will say: ‘Dispute not in front of Me, I had already, in advance, sent you the threat.
50:29
مَا يُبَدَّلُ ٱلۡقَوۡلُ لَدَىَّ وَمَآ أَنَا۟ بِظَلَّـٰمٍ۬ لِّلۡعَبِيدِ
The Sentence that comes from Me cannot be changed, and I am not unjust (to the least) to the slaves.‘
50:[30-35] Hell shall be asked, ‘Are you full‘. Hell will answer, ‘Are there any more?‘
50:[36-45] Admonish the unbelievers and bear with them in patience. Admonish them with The Qur‘an
50:30
يَوۡمَ نَقُولُ لِجَهَنَّمَ هَلِ ٱمۡتَلَأۡتِ
On the Day when We will say to Hell (جهنم): ‘Are you filled?‘
وَتَقُولُ هَلۡ مِن مَّزِيدٍ۬
It will say: ‘Are there any more (to come)?‘
50:31
وَأُزۡلِفَتِ ٱلۡجَنَّةُ لِلۡمُتَّقِينَ غَيۡرَ بَعِيدٍ
And Paradise (الجنة) will be brought near to the Muttaqoon (pious - see V.2:2) not far off.
50:32
هَـٰذَا مَا تُوعَدُونَ لِكُلِّ أَوَّابٍ حَفِيظٍ۬
(It will be said):
This is what you were promised, - (it is) for those oft-returning (to Allah (الله)) in sincere repentance, and those who preserve their covenant with Allah (الله) (by obeying Him in all what He has ordered, and worship none but Allah (الله) Alone, i.e. follow Allah (الله)‘s Religion, Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism).
50:33
مَّنۡ خَشِىَ ٱلرَّحۡمَـٰنَ بِٱلۡغَيۡبِ وَجَآءَ بِقَلۡبٍ۬ مُّنِيبٍ
‘Who feared the Most Beneficent (Ar-Rahmaan (ٱلْرَّحْمَـانُ)) in the Ghaib (unseen): (i.e. in this worldly life before seeing and meeting Him), and brought a heart turned in repentance (to Him - and absolutely free from each and every kind of polytheism),
50:34
ٱدۡخُلُوهَا بِسَلَـٰمٍ۬‌ۖ
Enter you therein in peace
ذَٲلِكَ يَوۡمُ ٱلۡخُلُودِ
and security; this is a Day of eternal life!‘
50:35
لَهُم مَّا يَشَآءُونَ فِيہَا وَلَدَيۡنَا مَزِيدٌ۬
There they will have all that they desire, and We have more (for them, i.e. a glance at the All-Mighty, All-Majestic).
50:36
وَكَمۡ أَهۡلَڪۡنَا قَبۡلَهُم مِّن قَرۡنٍ هُمۡ أَشَدُّ مِنۡہُم بَطۡشً۬ا
And how many a generation We have destroyed before them, who were stronger in power than them, and (when Our Torment came) they ran for a refuge in the land!
فَنَقَّبُواْ فِى ٱلۡبِلَـٰدِ هَلۡ مِن مَّحِيصٍ
Could they find any place of refuge (for them to save themselves from destruction)?
50:37
إِنَّ فِى ذَٲلِكَ لَذِڪۡرَىٰ لِمَن كَانَ لَهُ ۥ قَلۡبٌ أَوۡ أَلۡقَى ٱلسَّمۡعَ وَهُوَ شَهِيدٌ۬
Verily, therein is indeed a reminder for him who has a heart or gives ear while he is heedful.
50:38
وَلَقَدۡ خَلَقۡنَا ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٲتِ وَٱلۡأَرۡضَ وَمَا بَيۡنَهُمَا فِى سِتَّةِ أَيَّامٍ۬
And indeed We created the heavens and the earth and all between them in six Days
وَمَا مَسَّنَا مِن لُّغُوبٍ۬
and nothing of fatigue touched Us.
50:39
فَٱصۡبِرۡ عَلَىٰ مَا يَقُولُونَ
So bear with patience (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) all that they say,
وَسَبِّحۡ بِحَمۡدِ رَبِّكَ قَبۡلَ طُلُوعِ ٱلشَّمۡسِ وَقَبۡلَ ٱلۡغُرُوبِ
and glorify the Praises of your Lord, before the rising of the sun and before (its) setting (i.e. the Fajr, Zuhr, and Asr prayers).
50:40
وَمِنَ ٱلَّيۡلِ فَسَبِّحۡهُ وَأَدۡبَـٰرَ ٱلسُّجُودِ
And during a part of the night (also), glorify His praises (i.e. Maghrib and Isha prayers), and (so likewise) after the prayers.(As-Sun (الشمس)nah, Nawafil optional and additional prayers, and also glorify, praise and magnify Allah (الله) - Subhan Allah (الله), Alhamdu lillah, Allah (الله)u-Akbar).
50:41
وَٱسۡتَمِعۡ يَوۡمَ يُنَادِ ٱلۡمُنَادِ مِن مَّكَانٍ۬ قَرِيبٍ۬
And listen on the Day when the caller will call from a near place,
50:42
يَوۡمَ يَسۡمَعُونَ ٱلصَّيۡحَةَ بِٱلۡحَقِّ‌ۚ
The Day when they will hear As-Saihah (shout, etc.) in truth,
ذَٲلِكَ يَوۡمُ ٱلۡخُرُوجِ
that will be the Day of coming out (from the graves i.e. the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة)).
50:43
إِنَّا نَحۡنُ نُحۡىِۦ وَنُمِيتُ وَإِلَيۡنَا ٱلۡمَصِيرُ
Verily, We it is Who give life and cause death; and to Us is the final return,
50:44
يَوۡمَ تَشَقَّقُ ٱلۡأَرۡضُ عَنۡہُمۡ سِرَاعً۬ا‌ۚ
On the Day when the earth shall be cleft, from off them, (they will come out) hastening forth.
ذَٲلِكَ حَشۡرٌ عَلَيۡنَا يَسِيرٌ۬
That will be a gathering, quite easy for Us.
50:45
نَّحۡنُ أَعۡلَمُ بِمَا يَقُولُونَ‌ۖ
We know of best what they say;
وَمَآ أَنتَ عَلَيۡہِم بِجَبَّارٍ۬‌ۖ
and you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) are not a tyrant over them (to force them to Belief).
فَذَكِّرۡ بِٱلۡقُرۡءَانِ مَن يَخَافُ وَعِيدِ
But warn by the Quran (القرآن), him who fears My Threat.*********
