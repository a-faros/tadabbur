Yaseen
Yāʾ Sīn (a name of Muhammad)
36:[1-12] The Qur‘an is revealed by Allah to warn people. The Prophet is told that he could warn only those who have the fear of Allah
36:1
يسٓ
Ya-Seen.(These letters are one of the miracles of the Quran (القرآن), and none but Allah (الله) (Alone) knows their meanings.)
36:2
وَٱلۡقُرۡءَانِ ٱلۡحَكِيمِ
By the Quran (القرآن), full of wisdom (i.e. full of laws, evidences, and proofs),
36:3
إِنَّكَ لَمِنَ ٱلۡمُرۡسَلِينَ
Truly, you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) are one of the Messengers,
36:4
عَلَىٰ صِرَٲطٍ۬ مُّسۡتَقِيمٍ۬
On a Straight Path (i.e. on Allah (الله)‘s religion of Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism).
36:5
تَنزِيلَ ٱلۡعَزِيزِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ
(This is) a Revelation sent down by the Almighty, the Most Merciful (Ar-Raheem (ٱلْرَّحِيْمُ)),
36:6
لِتُنذِرَ قَوۡمً۬ا مَّآ أُنذِرَ ءَابَآؤُهُمۡ فَهُمۡ غَـٰفِلُونَ
In order that you may warn a people whose forefathers were not warned, so they are heedless.
36:7
لَقَدۡ حَقَّ ٱلۡقَوۡلُ عَلَىٰٓ أَكۡثَرِهِمۡ فَهُمۡ لَا يُؤۡمِنُونَ
Indeed the Word (of punishment) has proved true against most of them, so they will not believe.
36:8
إِنَّا جَعَلۡنَا فِىٓ أَعۡنَـٰقِهِمۡ أَغۡلَـٰلاً۬ فَهِىَ إِلَى ٱلۡأَذۡقَانِ فَهُم مُّقۡمَحُونَ
Verily! We have put on their necks iron collars reaching to chins, so that their heads are forced up.
36:9
وَجَعَلۡنَا مِنۢ بَيۡنِ أَيۡدِيہِمۡ سَدًّ۬ا وَمِنۡ خَلۡفِهِمۡ سَدًّ۬ا
And We have put a barrier before them, and a barrier behind them,
فَأَغۡشَيۡنَـٰهُمۡ فَهُمۡ لَا يُبۡصِرُونَ
and We have covered them up, so that they cannot see.
36:10
وَسَوَآءٌ عَلَيۡہِمۡ ءَأَنذَرۡتَهُمۡ أَمۡ لَمۡ تُنذِرۡهُمۡ لَا يُؤۡمِنُونَ
It is the same to them whether you warn them or you warn them not, they will not believe.
36:11
إِنَّمَا تُنذِرُ مَنِ ٱتَّبَعَ ٱلذِّڪۡرَ وَخَشِىَ ٱلرَّحۡمَـٰنَ بِٱلۡغَيۡبِۖ
You can only warn him who follows the Reminder (the Quran (القرآن)), and fears the Most Beneficent (Ar-Rahmaan (ٱلْرَّحْمَـانُ)) unseen.
فَبَشِّرۡهُ بِمَغۡفِرَةٍ۬ وَأَجۡرٍ۬ ڪَرِيمٍ
Bear you to such one the glad tidings of forgiveness, and a generous reward (i.e. Paradise (الجنة)).
36:12
إِنَّا نَحۡنُ نُحۡىِ ٱلۡمَوۡتَىٰ وَنَڪۡتُبُ مَا قَدَّمُواْ وَءَاثَـٰرَهُمۡۚ
Verily, We give life to the dead, and We record that which they send before (them), and their traces, (their footsteps and walking on the earth with their legs to the mosques for the five compulsory congregational prayers, Jihad (holy fighting in Allah (الله)‘s Cause) and all other good and evil they did, and that which they leave behind)
وَكُلَّ شَىۡءٍ أَحۡصَيۡنَـٰهُ فِىٓ إِمَامٍ۬ مُّبِينٍ۬
and all things We have recorded with numbers (as a record) in a Clear Book.
36:[13-21] The example of three Prophets who were sent to one town; all the people denied them except one man who came from across the town
36:13
وَٱضۡرِبۡ لَهُم مَّثَلاً أَصۡحَـٰبَ ٱلۡقَرۡيَةِ إِذۡ جَآءَهَا ٱلۡمُرۡسَلُونَ
And put forward to them a similitude; the (story of the) dwellers of the town, when there came Messengers to them.(It is said that the town was Ant (نمل)ioch (Ant (نمل)akiya)),
36:14
إِذۡ أَرۡسَلۡنَآ إِلَيۡہِمُ ٱثۡنَيۡنِ فَكَذَّبُوهُمَا فَعَزَّزۡنَا بِثَالِثٍ۬
When We sent to them two Messengers, they belied them both, so We reinforced them with a third,
فَقَالُوٓاْ إِنَّآ إِلَيۡكُم مُّرۡسَلُونَ
and they said: ‘Verily! We have been sent to you as Messengers.‘
36:15
قَالُواْ مَآ أَنتُمۡ إِلَّا بَشَرٌ۬ مِّثۡلُنَاوَمَآ أَنزَلَ ٱلرَّحۡمَـٰنُ مِن شَىۡءٍ
They (people of the town) said: ‘You are only human beings like ourselves, and the Most Beneficent (Ar-Rahmaan (ٱلْرَّحْمَـانُ)) has revealed nothing,
إِنۡ أَنتُمۡ إِلَّا تَكۡذِبُونَ
you are only telling lies.‘
36:16
قَالُواْ رَبُّنَا يَعۡلَمُ إِنَّآ إِلَيۡكُمۡ لَمُرۡسَلُونَ
The Messengers said: ‘Our Lord knows that we have been sent as Messengers to you,
36:17
وَمَا عَلَيۡنَآ إِلَّا ٱلۡبَلَـٰغُ ٱلۡمُبِينُ
And our duty is only to convey plainly (the Message).‘
36:18
قَالُوٓاْ إِنَّا تَطَيَّرۡنَا بِكُمۡۖ
They (people) said: ‘For us, we see an evil omen from you,
لَٮِٕن لَّمۡ تَنتَهُواْ لَنَرۡجُمَنَّكُمۡ وَلَيَمَسَّنَّكُم مِّنَّا عَذَابٌ أَلِيمٌ۬
if you cease not, we will surely stone you, and a painful torment will touch you from us.‘
36:19
قَالُواْ طَـٰٓٮِٕرُكُم مَّعَكُمۡۚ
They (Messengers) said: ‘Your evil omens be with you!
أَٮِٕن ذُڪِّرۡتُمۚ
(Do you call it ‘evil omen‘) because you are admonished?
بَلۡ أَنتُمۡ قَوۡمٌ۬ مُّسۡرِفُونَ
Nay, but you are a people Musrifoon (transgressing all bounds by committing all kinds of great sins, and by disobeying Allah (الله)).
36:20
وَجَآءَ مِنۡ أَقۡصَا ٱلۡمَدِينَةِ رَجُلٌ۬ يَسۡعَىٰ
And there came running from the farthest part of the town, a man,
قَالَ يَـٰقَوۡمِ ٱتَّبِعُواْ ٱلۡمُرۡسَلِينَ
saying: ‘O my people! Obey the Messengers;
36:21
ٱتَّبِعُواْ مَن لَّا يَسۡـَٔلُكُمۡ أَجۡرً۬ا وَهُم مُّهۡتَدُونَ
Obey those who ask no wages of you (for themselves), and who are rightly guided.
36:[22-32] Allah blessed the believer with paradise and destroyed the disbelievers
36:22
وَمَا لِىَ لَآ أَعۡبُدُ ٱلَّذِى فَطَرَنِى وَإِلَيۡهِ تُرۡجَعُونَ
And why should I not worship Him (Allah (الله) Alone) Who has created me and to Whom you shall be returned.
36:23
ءَأَتَّخِذُ مِن دُونِهِۦۤ ءَالِهَةً
Shall I take besides Him aliha (gods),
إِن يُرِدۡنِ ٱلرَّحۡمَـٰنُ بِضُرٍّ۬ لَّا تُغۡنِ عَنِّى شَفَـٰعَتُهُمۡ شَيۡـًٔ۬ا وَلَا يُنقِذُونِ
if the Most Beneficent (Ar-Rahmaan (ٱلْرَّحْمَـانُ)) intends me any harm, their intercession will be of no use for me whatsoever, nor can they save me?
36:24
إِنِّىٓ إِذً۬ا لَّفِى ضَلَـٰلٍ۬ مُّبِينٍ
Then verily, I should be in plain error.
36:25
إِنِّىٓ ءَامَنتُ بِرَبِّكُمۡ فَٱسۡمَعُونِ
Verily! I have believed in your Lord, so listen to me!‘
36:26
قِيلَ ٱدۡخُلِ ٱلۡجَنَّةَۖ
It was said (to him when the disbelievers killed him): ‘Enter Paradise (الجنة).‘
قَالَ يَـٰلَيۡتَ قَوۡمِى يَعۡلَمُونَ
He said: ‘Would that my people knew!
36:27
بِمَا غَفَرَ لِى رَبِّى وَجَعَلَنِى مِنَ ٱلۡمُكۡرَمِينَ
That my Lord (Allah (الله)) has forgiven me, and made me of the honoured ones!‘
36:28
وَمَآ أَنزَلۡنَا عَلَىٰ قَوۡمِهِۦ مِنۢ بَعۡدِهِۦ مِن جُندٍ۬ مِّنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ وَمَا كُنَّا مُنزِلِينَ
And We sent not against his people after him a host from heaven, nor do We send (such a thing).
36:29
إِن كَانَتۡ إِلَّا صَيۡحَةً۬ وَٲحِدَةً۬ فَإِذَا هُمۡ خَـٰمِدُونَ
It was but one Saihah (shout, etc.) and lo! They (all) were silent (dead-destroyed).
36:30
يَـٰحَسۡرَةً عَلَى ٱلۡعِبَادِ‌ۚ
Alas for mankind!
مَا يَأۡتِيهِم مِّن رَّسُولٍ إِلَّا كَانُواْ بِهِۦ يَسۡتَہۡزِءُونَ
There never came a Messenger to them but they used to mock at him.
36:31
أَلَمۡ يَرَوۡاْ كَمۡ أَهۡلَكۡنَا قَبۡلَهُم مِّنَ ٱلۡقُرُونِ
Do they not see how many of the generations We have destroyed before them?
أَنَّہُمۡ إِلَيۡہِمۡ لَا يَرۡجِعُونَ
Verily, they will not return to them.
36:32
وَإِن كُلٌّ۬ لَّمَّا جَمِيعٌ۬ لَّدَيۡنَا مُحۡضَرُونَ
And surely, all, everyone of them will be brought before Us.
36:[33-36] Allah has created all things in pairs
36:[37-40] The day, night, sun and moon; all are being regulated by Allah
36:[41-50] The disbelievers‘ attitude towards spending in the way of Allah
36:33
وَءَايَةٌ۬ لَّهُمُ ٱلۡأَرۡضُ ٱلۡمَيۡتَةُ
And a sign for them is the dead land.
أَحۡيَيۡنَـٰهَاوَأَخۡرَجۡنَا مِنۡہَا حَبًّ۬ا فَمِنۡهُ يَأۡڪُلُونَ
We gave it life, and We brought forth from it grains, so that they eat thereof.
36:34
وَجَعَلۡنَا فِيهَا جَنَّـٰتٍ۬ مِّن نَّخِيلٍ۬ وَأَعۡنَـٰبٍ۬
And We have made therein gardens of date-palms and grapes,
وَفَجَّرۡنَا فِيہَا مِنَ ٱلۡعُيُونِ
and We have caused springs of water to gush forth therein.
36:35
لِيَأۡڪُلُواْ مِن ثَمَرِهِۦ وَمَا عَمِلَتۡهُ أَيۡدِيهِمۡ‌ۖ
So that they may eat of the fruit thereof, and their hands made it not.
أَفَلَا يَشۡڪُرُونَ
Will they not, then, give thanks?
36:36
سُبۡحَـٰنَ ٱلَّذِى خَلَقَ ٱلۡأَزۡوَٲجَ ڪُلَّهَا
Glory be to Him, Who has created all the pairs
مِمَّا تُنۢبِتُ ٱلۡأَرۡضُ وَمِنۡ أَنفُسِهِمۡ وَمِمَّا لَا يَعۡلَمُونَ
of that which the earth produces, as well as of their own (human) kind (male and female), and of that which they know not.
36:37
وَءَايَةٌ۬ لَّهُمُ ٱلَّيۡلُ
And a sign for them is the night,
نَسۡلَخُ مِنۡهُ ٱلنَّہَارَ فَإِذَا هُم مُّظۡلِمُونَ
We withdraw therefrom the day, and behold, they are in darkness.
36:38
وَٱلشَّمۡسُ تَجۡرِى لِمُسۡتَقَرٍّ۬ لَّهَا‌ۚ
And the sun runs on its fixed course for a term (appointed).
ذَٲلِكَ تَقۡدِيرُ ٱلۡعَزِيزِ ٱلۡعَلِيمِ
That is the Decree of the All-Mighty, the All-Knowing (Al-‘Alee (ٱلْعَلِيُّ)m (ٱلْعَلِيمُ)).
36:39
وَٱلۡقَمَرَ قَدَّرۡنَـٰهُ مَنَازِلَ حَتَّىٰ عَادَ كَٱلۡعُرۡجُونِ ٱلۡقَدِيمِ
And the moon, We have measured for it mansions (to traverse) till it returns like the old dried curved date stalk.
36:40
لَا ٱلشَّمۡسُ يَنۢبَغِى لَهَآ أَن تُدۡرِكَ ٱلۡقَمَرَ وَلَا ٱلَّيۡلُ سَابِقُ ٱلنَّہَارِ‌ۚ
It is not for the sun to overtake the moon, nor does the night outstrip the day.
وَكُلٌّ۬ فِى فَلَكٍ۬ يَسۡبَحُونَ
They all float, each in an orbit.
36:41
وَءَايَةٌ۬ لَّهُمۡ أَنَّا حَمَلۡنَا ذُرِّيَّتَہُمۡ فِى ٱلۡفُلۡكِ ٱلۡمَشۡحُونِ
And an Ayah (sign) for them is that We bore their offspring in the laden ship (of Nooh (Noah)).
36:42
وَخَلَقۡنَا لَهُم مِّن مِّثۡلِهِۦ مَا يَرۡكَبُونَ
And We have created for them of the like thereunto, so on them they ride.
36:43
وَإِن نَّشَأۡ نُغۡرِقۡهُمۡ فَلَا صَرِيخَ لَهُمۡ وَلَا هُمۡ يُنقَذُونَ
And if We will, We shall drown them, and there will be no shout (or helper) for them (to hear their cry for help) nor will they be saved.
36:44
إِلَّا رَحۡمَةً۬ مِّنَّا وَمَتَـٰعًا إِلَىٰ حِينٍ۬
Unless it be a mercy from Us, and as an enjoyment for a while.
36:45
وَإِذَا قِيلَ لَهُمُ ٱتَّقُواْ مَا بَيۡنَ أَيۡدِيكُمۡ وَمَا خَلۡفَكُمۡ لَعَلَّكُمۡ تُرۡحَمُونَ
And when it is said to them: ‘Beware of that which is before you (worldly torments), and that which is behind you (torments in the Hereafter), in order that you may receive Mercy.(i.e. if you believe in Allah (الله)‘s Religion Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism, and avoid polytheism, and obey Allah (الله) with righteous deeds).
36:46
وَمَا تَأۡتِيہِم مِّنۡ ءَايَةٍ۬ مِّنۡ ءَايَـٰتِ رَبِّہِمۡ إِلَّا كَانُواْ عَنۡہَا مُعۡرِضِينَ
And never came an Ayah from among the Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, etc.) of their Lord to them, but they did turn away from it.
36:47
وَإِذَا قِيلَ لَهُمۡ أَنفِقُواْ مِمَّا رَزَقَكُمُ ٱللَّهُ
And when it is said to them: ‘Spend of that with which Allah (الله) has provided you,‘
قَالَ ٱلَّذِينَ ڪَفَرُواْ لِلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوٓاْأَنُطۡعِمُ مَن لَّوۡ يَشَآءُ ٱللَّهُ أَطۡعَمَهُ ۥۤ
those who disbelieve say to those who believe: ‘Shall we feed those whom, if Allah (الله) willed, He (Himself) would have fed?
إِنۡ أَنتُمۡ إِلَّا فِى ضَلَـٰلٍ۬ مُّبِينٍ۬
You are only in a plain error.‘
36:48
وَيَقُولُونَ مَتَىٰ هَـٰذَا ٱلۡوَعۡدُ إِن كُنتُمۡ صَـٰدِقِينَ
And they say: ‘When will this promise (i.e. Resurrection) be fulfilled, if you are truthful?‘
36:49
مَا يَنظُرُونَ إِلَّا صَيۡحَةً۬ وَٲحِدَةً۬ تَأۡخُذُهُمۡ وَهُمۡ يَخِصِّمُونَ
They await only but a single Saihah (shout, etc.), which will seize them while they are disputing!
36:50
فَلَا يَسۡتَطِيعُونَ تَوۡصِيَةً۬ وَلَآ إِلَىٰٓ أَهۡلِهِمۡ يَرۡجِعُونَ
Then they will not be able to make bequest, nor they will return to their family.
36:[51-54] A scene from the Day of Judgement
36:[55-58] Allah‘s greeting to the residents of paradise
36:[59-67] Allah‘s address to the criminal sinners. On the Day of Judgement, the hands and feet of the wrongdoers shall testify
36:51
وَنُفِخَ فِى ٱلصُّورِ فَإِذَا هُم مِّنَ ٱلۡأَجۡدَاثِ إِلَىٰ رَبِّهِمۡ يَنسِلُونَ
And the Trumpet will be blown (i.e. the second blowing) and behold! From the graves they will come out quickly to their Lord.
36:52
قَالُواْ يَـٰوَيۡلَنَا مَنۢ بَعَثَنَا مِن مَّرۡقَدِنَاۜ‌ۗ
They will say: ‘Woe to us! Who has raised us up from our place of sleep.‘
هَـٰذَا مَا وَعَدَ ٱلرَّحۡمَـٰنُ وَصَدَقَ ٱلۡمُرۡسَلُونَ
(It will be said to them):
This is what the Most Beneficent (Ar-Rahmaan (ٱلْرَّحْمَـانُ)) had promised, and the Messengers spoke truth!‘
36:53
إِن ڪَانَتۡ إِلَّا صَيۡحَةً۬ وَٲحِدَةً۬ فَإِذَا هُمۡ جَمِيعٌ۬ لَّدَيۡنَا مُحۡضَرُونَ
It will be but a single Saihah (shout, etc.), so behold! They will all be brought up before Us!
36:54
فَٱلۡيَوۡمَ لَا تُظۡلَمُ نَفۡسٌ۬ شَيۡـًٔ۬اوَلَا تُجۡزَوۡنَ إِلَّا مَا ڪُنتُمۡ تَعۡمَلُونَ
This Day (Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة)), none will be wronged in anything, nor will you be requited anything except that which you used to do.
36:55
إِنَّ أَصۡحَـٰبَ ٱلۡجَنَّةِ ٱلۡيَوۡمَ فِى شُغُلٍ۬ فَـٰكِهُونَ
Verily, the dwellers of the Paradise (الجنة), that Day, will be busy in joyful things.
36:56
هُمۡ وَأَزۡوَٲجُهُمۡ فِى ظِلَـٰلٍ عَلَى ٱلۡأَرَآٮِٕكِ مُتَّكِـُٔونَ
They and their wives will be in pleasant shade, reclining on thrones.
36:57
لَهُمۡ فِيہَا فَـٰكِهَةٌ۬ وَلَهُم مَّا يَدَّعُونَ
They will have therein fruits (of all kinds) and all that they ask for.
36:58
سَلَـٰمٌ۬ قَوۡلاً۬ مِّن رَّبٍّ۬ رَّحِيمٍ۬
(It will be said to them):
Salamun (peace be on you), a Word from the Lord (Allah (الله)), Most Merciful (Ar-Raheem (ٱلْرَّحِيْمُ)).
36:59
وَٱمۡتَـٰزُواْ ٱلۡيَوۡمَ أَيُّہَا ٱلۡمُجۡرِمُونَ
(It will be said):
And O you Al-Mujrimoon (criminals, polytheists, sinners, disbelievers in the Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism, wicked evil ones, etc.)! Get you apart this Day (from the believers).
36:60
أَلَمۡ أَعۡهَدۡ إِلَيۡكُمۡ يَـٰبَنِىٓ ءَادَمَ أَن لَّا تَعۡبُدُواْ ٱلشَّيۡطَـٰنَ‌ۖ
Did I not ordain for you, O Children of Adam (آدم), that you should not worship Shaitan (Satan (الشيطان)).
إِنَّهُ ۥ لَكُمۡ عَدُوٌّ۬ مُّبِينٌ۬
Verily, he is a plain enemy to you.
36:61
وَأَنِ ٱعۡبُدُونِى‌ۚ هَـٰذَا صِرَٲطٌ۬ مُّسۡتَقِيمٌ۬
And that you should worship Me (Alone Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism, and set up not rivals, associategods with Me). That is a Straight Path.
36:62
وَلَقَدۡ أَضَلَّ مِنكُمۡ جِبِلاًّ۬ كَثِيرًا‌ۖ
And indeed he (Satan (الشيطان)) did lead astray a great multitude of you.
أَفَلَمۡ تَكُونُواْ تَعۡقِلُونَ
Did you not, then, understand?
36:63
هَـٰذِهِۦ جَهَنَّمُ ٱلَّتِى كُنتُمۡ تُوعَدُونَ
This is Hell (جهنم) which you were promised!
36:64
ٱصۡلَوۡهَا ٱلۡيَوۡمَ بِمَا كُنتُمۡ تَكۡفُرُونَ
Burn therein this Day, for that you used to disbelieve.
36:65
ٱلۡيَوۡمَ نَخۡتِمُ عَلَىٰٓ أَفۡوَٲهِهِمۡ
This Day, We shall seal up their mouths,
وَتُكَلِّمُنَآ أَيۡدِيہِمۡ وَتَشۡہَدُ أَرۡجُلُهُم بِمَا كَانُواْ يَكۡسِبُونَ
and their hands will speak to Us, and their legs will bearwitness to what they used to earn.(It is said that ones left thigh will be the first to bear the witness)(Tafsir At-Tabaree, Vol. 22, Page 24)
36:66
وَلَوۡ نَشَآءُ لَطَمَسۡنَا عَلَىٰٓ أَعۡيُنِہِمۡ فَٱسۡتَبَقُواْ ٱلصِّرَٲطَ فَأَنَّىٰ يُبۡصِرُونَ
And if it had been Our Will, We would surely have wiped out (blinded) their eyes, so that they would struggle for the Path, how then would they see?
36:67
وَلَوۡ نَشَآءُ لَمَسَخۡنَـٰهُمۡ عَلَىٰ مَڪَانَتِهِمۡ فَمَا ٱسۡتَطَـٰعُواْ مُضِيًّ۬ا وَلَا يَرۡجِعُونَ
And if it had been Our Will, We could have transformed them (into animals or lifeless objects) in their places. Then they should have been unable to go forward (move about) nor they could have turned back. (As it happened with the Jews (هُودًا) see Verse 7:166 The Quran (القرآن)).
36:[68-76] The Qur‘an is to warn those who are alive and establish charge against the disbelievers
36:[77-83] Allah, Who has created man, shall give him life again for accountability on the Day of Judgement
36:68
وَمَن نُّعَمِّرۡهُ نُنَڪِّسۡهُ فِى ٱلۡخَلۡقِ‌ۖ
And he whom We grant long life, We reverse him in creation (weakness after strength).
أَفَلَا يَعۡقِلُونَ
Will they not then understand?
36:69
وَمَا عَلَّمۡنَـٰهُ ٱلشِّعۡرَ وَمَا يَنۢبَغِى لَهُ ۥۤ‌ۚ
And We have not taught him (Muhammad (محمد) SAW) poetry, nor is it meet for him.
إِنۡ هُوَ إِلَّا ذِكۡرٌ۬ وَقُرۡءَانٌ۬ مُّبِينٌ۬
This is only a Reminder and a plain Quran (القرآن).
36:70
لِّيُنذِرَ مَن كَانَ حَيًّ۬اوَيَحِقَّ ٱلۡقَوۡلُ عَلَى ٱلۡكَـٰفِرِينَ
That he or it (Muhammad (محمد) SAW or the Quran (القرآن)) may give warning to him who is living (a healthy minded the believer), and that Word (charge) may be justified against the disbelievers (dead, as they reject the warnings).
36:71
أَوَلَمۡ يَرَوۡاْ أَنَّا خَلَقۡنَا لَهُم مِّمَّا عَمِلَتۡ أَيۡدِينَآ أَنۡعَـٰمً۬افَهُمۡ لَهَا مَـٰلِكُونَ
Do they not see that We have created for them of what Our Hands have created, the cattle, so that they are their owners.
36:72
وَذَلَّلۡنَـٰهَا لَهُمۡ فَمِنۡہَا رَكُوبُہُمۡ وَمِنۡہَا يَأۡكُلُونَ
And We have subdued them unto them so that some of them they have for riding and some they eat.
36:73
وَلَهُمۡ فِيہَا مَنَـٰفِعُ وَمَشَارِبُ‌ۖ
And they have (other) benefits from them (besides), and they get (milk) to drink,
أَفَلَا يَشۡكُرُونَ
will they not then be grateful?
36:74
وَٱتَّخَذُواْ مِن دُونِ ٱللَّهِ ءَالِهَةً۬ لَّعَلَّهُمۡ يُنصَرُونَ
And they have taken besides Allah (الله) aliha (gods), hoping that they might be helped (by those so called gods).
36:75
لَا يَسۡتَطِيعُونَ نَصۡرَهُمۡ وَهُمۡ لَهُمۡ جُندٌ۬ مُّحۡضَرُونَ
They cannot help them, but they will be brought forward as a troop against those who worshipped them (at the time of Reckoning).
36:76
فَلَا يَحۡزُنكَ قَوۡلُهُمۡ‌ۘ
So let not their speech, then, grieve you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW).
إِنَّا نَعۡلَمُ مَا يُسِرُّونَ وَمَا يُعۡلِنُونَ
Verily, We know what they conceal and what they reveal.
36:77
أَوَلَمۡ يَرَ ٱلۡإِنسَـٰنُ أَنَّا خَلَقۡنَـٰهُ مِن نُّطۡفَةٍ۬ فَإِذَا هُوَ خَصِيمٌ۬ مُّبِينٌ۬
Does not man see that We have created him from Nutfah (mixed male and female discharge semen drops). Yet behold! He (stands forth) as an open opponent.
36:78
وَضَرَبَ لَنَا مَثَلاً۬ وَنَسِىَ خَلۡقَهُ ۥ‌ۖ
And he puts forth for Us a parable, and forgets his own creation.
قَالَ مَن يُحۡىِ ٱلۡعِظَـٰمَ وَهِىَ رَمِيمٌ۬
He says: ‘Who will give life to these bones when they have rotted away and became dust?‘
36:79
قُلۡ يُحۡيِيہَا ٱلَّذِىٓ أَنشَأَهَآ أَوَّلَ مَرَّةٍ۬‌ۖ
Say: (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) ‘He will give life to them Who created them for the first time!
وَهُوَ بِكُلِّ خَلۡقٍ عَلِيمٌ
And He is the All-Knower (Al-‘Alee (ٱلْعَلِيُّ)m (ٱلْعَلِيمُ)) of every creation!‘
36:80
ٱلَّذِى جَعَلَ لَكُم مِّنَ ٱلشَّجَرِ ٱلۡأَخۡضَرِ نَارً۬افَإِذَآ أَنتُم مِّنۡهُ تُوقِدُونَ
He, Who produces for you fire out of the green tree, when behold! You kindle therewith.
36:81
أَوَلَيۡسَ ٱلَّذِى خَلَقَ ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٲتِ وَٱلۡأَرۡضَ بِقَـٰدِرٍ عَلَىٰٓ أَن يَخۡلُقَ مِثۡلَهُم‌ۚ
Is not He, Who created the heavens and the earth Able to create the like of them?
بَلَىٰ وَهُوَ ٱلۡخَلَّـٰقُ ٱلۡعَلِيمُ
Yes, indeed! He is the All-Knowing (Al-‘Alee (ٱلْعَلِيُّ)m (ٱلْعَلِيمُ)) Supreme Creator.
36:82
إِنَّمَآ أَمۡرُهُ ۥۤ إِذَآ أَرَادَ شَيۡـًٔا أَن يَقُولَ لَهُ ۥ كُن فَيَكُونُ
Verily, His Command, when He intends a thing, is only that He says to it, ‘Be!‘ and it is!
36:83
فَسُبۡحَـٰنَ ٱلَّذِى بِيَدِهِۦ مَلَكُوتُ كُلِّ شَىۡءٍ۬ وَإِلَيۡهِ تُرۡجَعُونَ
So Glorified is He and Exalted above all that they associate with Him, and in Whose Hands is the dominion of all things, and to Him you shall be returned.*********
