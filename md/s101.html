Al-Qaari'a
The Striking Hour, The Great Calamity, The Stunning Blow, The Sudden Calamity
101:[1-11] A scene explaining the Day of Judgment
101:1
ٱلۡقَارِعَةُ
Al-Qâri‘ah (the striking Hour i.e. the Day of Resurrection),
101:2
مَا ٱلۡقَارِعَةُ
What is the striking (Hour)?
101:3
وَمَآ أَدۡرَٮٰكَ مَا ٱلۡقَارِعَةُ
And what will make you know what the striking (Hour) is?
101:4
يَوۡمَ يَكُونُ ٱلنَّاسُ ڪَٱلۡفَرَاشِ ٱلۡمَبۡثُوثِ
It is a Day whereon mankind will be like moths scattered about,
101:5
وَتَكُونُ ٱلۡجِبَالُ ڪَٱلۡعِهۡنِ ٱلۡمَنفُوشِ
And the mountains will be like carded wool,
101:6
فَأَمَّا مَن ثَقُلَتۡ مَوَٲزِينُهُ ۥ
Then as for him whose balance (of good deeds) will be heavy,
101:7
فَهُوَ فِى عِيشَةٍ۬ رَّاضِيَةٍ۬
He will live a pleasant life (in Paradise).
101:8
وَأَمَّا مَنۡ خَفَّتۡ مَوَٲزِينُهُ ۥ
But as for him whose balance (of good deeds) will be light,
101:9
فَأُمُّهُ ۥ هَاوِيَةٌ۬
He will have his home in Hawiyah (pit, i.e. Hell).
101:10
وَمَآ أَدۡرَٮٰكَ مَا هِيَهۡ
And what will make you know what it is?
101:11
نَارٌ حَامِيَةُۢ
(It is) a hot blazing Fire!*********
