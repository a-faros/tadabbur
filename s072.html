Al-Jinn
The Jinn, The Spirits, The Unseen Beings
72:[1-15] A beautiful speech of the jinns who embraced Islam after hearing The Qur‘an. Jinns also have different religions and sects, there are some Muslims and some deviators from the truth
72:[16-19] Mosques are built for the worship of Allah, so invoke no one else besides Him
72:1
قُلۡ أُوحِىَ إِلَىَّ أَنَّهُ ٱسۡتَمَعَ نَفَرٌ۬ مِّنَ ٱلۡجِنِّ
Say (O Muhammad SAW): ‘It has been revealed to me that a group (from three to ten in number) of jinns listened (to this Quran).
فَقَالُوٓاْ إِنَّا سَمِعۡنَا قُرۡءَانًا عَجَبً۬ا
They said: Verily! We have heard a wonderful Recital (this Quran)!
72:2
يَہۡدِىٓ إِلَى ٱلرُّشۡدِ فَـَٔامَنَّا بِهِۦ‌ۖ
It guides to the Right Path, and we have believed therein,
وَلَن نُّشۡرِكَ بِرَبِّنَآ أَحَدً۬ا
and we shall never join (in worship) anything with our Lord (Allah).
72:3
وَأَنَّهُ ۥ تَعَـٰلَىٰ جَدُّ رَبِّنَا مَا ٱتَّخَذَ صَـٰحِبَةً۬ وَلَا وَلَدً۬ا
And exalted be the Majesty of our Lord, He has taken neither a wife, nor a son (or offspring or children).
72:4
وَأَنَّهُ ۥ كَانَ يَقُولُ سَفِيہُنَا عَلَى ٱللَّهِ شَطَطً۬ا
And that the foolish among us (i.e. Iblees (Satan) or the polytheists amongst the jinns) used to utter against Allah that which was wrong and not right.
72:5
وَأَنَّا ظَنَنَّآ أَن لَّن تَقُولَ ٱلۡإِنسُ وَٱلۡجِنُّ عَلَى ٱللَّهِ كَذِبً۬ا
And verily, we thought that men and jinns would not utter a lie against Allah.
72:6
وَأَنَّهُ ۥ كَانَ رِجَالٌ۬ مِّنَ ٱلۡإِنسِ يَعُوذُونَ بِرِجَالٍ۬ مِّنَ ٱلۡجِنِّ
And verily, there were men among mankind who took shelter with the masculine among the jinns,
فَزَادُوهُمۡ رَهَقً۬ا
but they (jinns) increased them (mankind) in sin and disbelief.
72:7
وَأَنَّہُمۡ ظَنُّواْ كَمَا ظَنَنتُمۡ أَن لَّن يَبۡعَثَ ٱللَّهُ أَحَدً۬ا
And they thought as you thought, that Allah will not send any Messenger (to mankind or jinns).
72:8
وَأَنَّا لَمَسۡنَا ٱلسَّمَآءَ فَوَجَدۡنَـٰهَا مُلِئَتۡ حَرَسً۬ا شَدِيدً۬ا وَشُہُبً۬ا
And we have sought to reach the heaven; but found it filled with stern guards and flaming fires.
72:9
وَأَنَّا كُنَّا نَقۡعُدُ مِنۡہَا مَقَـٰعِدَ لِلسَّمۡعِ‌ۖ
And verily, we used to sit there in stations, to (steal) a hearing,
فَمَن يَسۡتَمِعِ ٱلۡأَنَ يَجِدۡ لَهُ ۥ شِہَابً۬ا رَّصَدً۬ا
but any who listens now will find a flaming fire watching him in ambush.
72:10
وَأَنَّا لَا نَدۡرِىٓ أَشَرٌّ أُرِيدَ بِمَن فِى ٱلۡأَرۡضِ
And we know not whether evil is intended for those on earth,
أَمۡ أَرَادَ بِہِمۡ رَبُّہُمۡ رَشَدً۬ا
or whether their Lord intends for them a Right Path.
72:11
وَأَنَّا مِنَّا ٱلصَّـٰلِحُونَ وَمِنَّا دُونَ ذَٲلِكَ‌ۖ
There are among us some that are righteous, and some the contrary;
كُنَّا طَرَآٮِٕقَ قِدَدً۬ا we are groups each having a different way (religious sect, etc.).
72:12
وَأَنَّا ظَنَنَّآ أَن لَّن نُّعۡجِزَ ٱللَّهَ فِى ٱلۡأَرۡضِ وَلَن نُّعۡجِزَهُ ۥ هَرَبً۬ا
And we think that we cannot escape (from the punishment of) Allah in the earth, nor can we escape (from the punishment) by flight.
72:13
وَأَنَّا لَمَّا سَمِعۡنَا ٱلۡهُدَىٰٓ ءَامَنَّا بِهِۦ‌ۖ
And indeed when we heard the Guidance (this Quran), we believed therein (Islamic Monotheism),
فَمَن يُؤۡمِنۢ بِرَبِّهِۦ فَلَا يَخَافُ بَخۡسً۬ا وَلَا رَهَقً۬ا
and whosoever believes in his Lord shall have no fear, either of a decrease in the reward of his good deeds or an increase in punishment for his sins.
72:14
وَأَنَّا مِنَّا ٱلۡمُسۡلِمُونَ وَمِنَّا ٱلۡقَـٰسِطُونَ‌ۖ
And of us some are Muslims (who have submitted to Allah, after listening to this Quran), and of us some are Al-Qasitoon (disbelievers those who have deviated from the Right Path).
فَمَنۡ أَسۡلَمَ فَأُوْلَـٰٓٮِٕكَ تَحَرَّوۡاْ رَشَدً۬ا
And whosoever has embraced Islam (i.e. has become a Muslim by submitting to Allah), then such have sought the Right Path.‘
72:15
وَأَمَّا ٱلۡقَـٰسِطُونَ فَكَانُواْ لِجَهَنَّمَ حَطَبً۬ا
And as for the Qasitoon (disbelievers who deviated from the Right Path), they shall be firewood for Hell,
72:16
وَأَلَّوِ ٱسۡتَقَـٰمُواْ عَلَى ٱلطَّرِيقَةِ لَأَسۡقَيۡنَـٰهُم مَّآءً غَدَقً۬ا
If they (non-Muslims) had believed in Allah, and went on the Right Way (i.e. Islam) We should surely have bestowed on them water (rain) in abundance.
72:17
لِّنَفۡتِنَهُمۡ فِيهِ‌ۚ
That We might try them thereby.
وَمَن يُعۡرِضۡ عَن ذِكۡرِ رَبِّهِۦ يَسۡلُكۡهُ عَذَابً۬ا صَعَدً۬ا
And whosoever turns away from the Reminder of his Lord (i.e. this Quran, and practice not its laws and orders), He will cause him to enter in a severe torment (i.e. Hell).
72:18
وَأَنَّ ٱلۡمَسَـٰجِدَ لِلَّهِ فَلَا تَدۡعُواْ مَعَ ٱللَّهِ أَحَدً۬ا
(It has been revealed to me that)
When the slave of Allah (Muhammad SAW) stood up invoking (his Lord Allah) in prayer to Him they (the jinns) just made round him a dense
72:19
وَأَنَّهُ ۥ لَمَّا قَامَ عَبۡدُ ٱللَّهِ يَدۡعُوهُ كَادُواْ يَكُونُونَ عَلَيۡهِ لِبَدً۬ا
crowd as if sticking one over the other (in order to listen to the Prophets recitation).
72:[20-28] Rasools do not have the power to harm or benefit anyone, their mission is just to convey Allah‘s message. Only Allah knows the unseen, He reveals it to whom He chooses from the Rasools
72:20
قُلۡ إِنَّمَآ أَدۡعُواْ رَبِّى وَلَآ أُشۡرِكُ بِهِۦۤ أَحَدً۬ا
Say (O Muhammad SAW): ‘I invoke only my Lord (Allah Alone), and I associate none as partners along with Him.‘
72:21
قُلۡ إِنِّى لَآ أَمۡلِكُ لَكُمۡ ضَرًّ۬ا وَلَا رَشَدً۬ا
Say: ‘It is not in my power to cause you harm, or to bring you to the Right Path.‘
72:22
قُلۡ إِنِّى لَن يُجِيرَنِى مِنَ ٱللَّهِ أَحَدٌ۬ وَلَنۡ أَجِدَ مِن دُونِهِۦ مُلۡتَحَدًا
Say (O Muhammad SAW): ‘None can protect me from Allahs punishment (if I were to disobey Him), nor should I find refuge except in Him.
72:23
إِلَّا بَلَـٰغً۬ا مِّنَ ٱللَّهِ وَرِسَـٰلَـٰتِهِۦ‌ۚ
(Mine is) but conveyance (of the truth) from Allah and His Messages (of Islamic Monotheism),
وَمَن يَعۡصِ ٱللَّهَ وَرَسُولَهُ ۥ فَإِنَّ لَهُ ۥ نَارَ جَهَنَّمَ خَـٰلِدِينَ فِيہَآ أَبَدًا
and whosoever disobeys Allah and His Messenger, then verily, for him is the Fire of Hell, he shall dwell therein forever .‘
72:24
حَتَّىٰٓ إِذَا رَأَوۡاْ مَا يُوعَدُونَ فَسَيَعۡلَمُونَ مَنۡ أَضۡعَفُ نَاصِرً۬ا وَأَقَلُّ عَدَدً۬ا
Till, when they see that which they are promised, then they will know who it is that is weaker concerning helpers and less important concerning numbers.
72:25
قُلۡ إِنۡ أَدۡرِىٓ أَقَرِيبٌ۬ مَّا تُوعَدُونَ أَمۡ يَجۡعَلُ لَهُ ۥ رَبِّىٓ أَمَدًا
Say (O Muhammad SAW): ‘I know not whether (the punishment) which you are promised is near or whether my Lord will appoint for it a distant term.
72:26
عَـٰلِمُ ٱلۡغَيۡبِ فَلَا يُظۡهِرُ عَلَىٰ غَيۡبِهِۦۤ أَحَدًا
(He Alone) the All-Knower (Al-‘Aleem) of the Ghaib (unseen), and He reveals to none His Ghaib (unseen).‘
72:27
إِلَّا مَنِ ٱرۡتَضَىٰ مِن رَّسُولٍ۬ فَإِنَّهُ ۥ يَسۡلُكُ مِنۢ بَيۡنِ يَدَيۡهِ وَمِنۡ خَلۡفِهِۦ رَصَدً۬ا
Except to a Messenger (from mankind) whom He has chosen (He informs him of unseen as much as He likes), and then He makes a band of watching guards (angels) to march before him and behind him.
72:28
لِّيَعۡلَمَ أَن قَدۡ أَبۡلَغُواْ رِسَـٰلَـٰتِ رَبِّہِمۡ
(He (Allah) protects them (the Messengers)), till He sees that they (the Messengers) have conveyed the Messages of their Lord (Allah).
وَأَحَاطَ بِمَا لَدَيۡہِمۡ وَأَحۡصَىٰ كُلَّ شَىۡءٍ عَدَدَۢا
And He (Allah) surrounds all that which is with them, and He (Allah) keeps count of all things (i.e. He knows the exact number of everything).*********
