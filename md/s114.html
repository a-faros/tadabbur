An-Naas
Mankind, Men, Mass
114:[1-6] Seek refuge with Allah from the slinking whisperers
114:1
قُلۡ أَعُوذُ بِرَبِّ ٱلنَّاسِ
Say: ‘I seek refuge with (Allâh) the Lord of mankind,
114:2
مَلِكِ ٱلنَّاسِ
The King of mankind,
114:3
إِلَـٰهِ ٱلنَّاسِ
The Ilâh (God) of mankind,
114:4
مِن شَرِّ ٱلۡوَسۡوَاسِ ٱلۡخَنَّاسِ
From the evil of the whisperer (devil who whispers evil in the hearts of men) who withdraws (from his whispering in one‘s heart after one remembers Allâh) ,
114:5
ٱلَّذِى يُوَسۡوِسُ فِى صُدُورِ ٱلنَّاسِ
Who whispers in the breasts of mankind,
114:6
مِنَ ٱلۡجِنَّةِ وَٱلنَّاسِ
Of jinns and men.‘*********
