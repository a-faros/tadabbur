An-Naba
The Great News, The Announcement, The Tiding
78:[1-30] The creation of heavens, earth, mountains and vegetation clearly points towards the Day of Judgment. Resurrection and man‘s accountability in the court of Allah
78:1
عَمَّ يَتَسَآءَلُونَ
What are they asking (one another)?
78:2
عَنِ ٱلنَّبَإِ ٱلۡعَظِيمِ
About the great news,(i.e. Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism, the Quran (القرآن), which Prophet Muhammad (محمد) SAW brought and the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة), etc.),
78:3
ٱلَّذِى هُمۡ فِيهِ مُخۡتَلِفُونَ
About which they are in disagreement.
78:4
كَلَّا سَيَعۡلَمُونَ
Nay, they will come to know!
78:5
ثُمَّ كَلَّا سَيَعۡلَمُونَ
Nay, again, they will come to know!
78:6
أَلَمۡ نَجۡعَلِ ٱلۡأَرۡضَ مِهَـٰدً۬ا
Have We not made the earth as a bed,
78:7
وَٱلۡجِبَالَ أَوۡتَادً۬ا
And the mountains as pegs?
78:8
وَخَلَقۡنَـٰكُمۡ أَزۡوَٲجً۬ا
And We have created you in pairs (male and female, tall and short, good and bad, etc.).
78:9
وَجَعَلۡنَا نَوۡمَكُمۡ سُبَاتً۬ا
And have made your sleep as a thing for rest.
78:10
وَجَعَلۡنَا ٱلَّيۡلَ لِبَاسً۬ا
And have made the night as a covering (through its darkness),
78:11
وَجَعَلۡنَا ٱلنَّہَارَ مَعَاشً۬ا
And have made the day for livelihood.
78:12
وَبَنَيۡنَا فَوۡقَكُمۡ سَبۡعً۬ا شِدَادً۬ا
And We have built above you seven strong (heavens),
78:13
وَجَعَلۡنَا سِرَاجً۬ا وَهَّاجً۬ا
And have made (therein) a shinning lamp (sun).
78:14
وَأَنزَلۡنَا مِنَ ٱلۡمُعۡصِرَٲتِ مَآءً۬ ثَجَّاجً۬ا
And have sent down from the rainy clouds abundant water.
78:15
لِّنُخۡرِجَ بِهِۦ حَبًّ۬ا وَنَبَاتً۬ا
That We may produce therewith corn and vegetations,
78:16
وَجَنَّـٰتٍ أَلۡفَافًا
And gardens of thick growth.
78:17
إِنَّ يَوۡمَ ٱلۡفَصۡلِ كَانَ مِيقَـٰتً۬ا
Verily, the Day of Decision is a fixed time,
78:18
يَوۡمَ يُنفَخُ فِى ٱلصُّورِ فَتَأۡتُونَ أَفۡوَاجً۬ا
The Day when the Trumpet will be blown, and you shall come forth in crowds (groups);
78:19
وَفُتِحَتِ ٱلسَّمَآءُ فَكَانَتۡ أَبۡوَٲبً۬ا
And the heaven shall be opened, and it will become as gates,
78:20
وَسُيِّرَتِ ٱلۡجِبَالُ فَكَانَتۡ سَرَابًا
And the mountains shall be moved away from their places and they will be as if they were a mirage.
78:21
إِنَّ جَهَنَّمَ كَانَتۡ مِرۡصَادً۬ا
Truly, Hell (جهنم) is a place of ambush,
78:22
لِّلطَّـٰغِينَ مَـَٔابً۬ا
A dwelling place for the Taghoon,(those who transgress the boundary limits set by Allah (الله) like polytheists, disbelievers in the Oneness of Allah (الله), hypocrites, sinners, criminals, etc.)
78:23
لَّـٰبِثِينَ فِيہَآ أَحۡقَابً۬ا
They will abide therein for ages,
78:24
لَّا يَذُوقُونَ فِيہَا بَرۡدً۬ا وَلَا شَرَابًا
Nothing cool shall they taste therein, nor any drink.
78:25
إِلَّا حَمِيمً۬ا وَغَسَّاقً۬ا
Except boiling water, and dirty wound discharges.
78:26
جَزَآءً۬ وِفَاقًا
An exact recompense (according to their evil crimes).
78:27
إِنَّہُمۡ ڪَانُواْ لَا يَرۡجُونَ حِسَابً۬ا
For verily, they used not to look for a reckoning.
78:28
وَكَذَّبُواْ بِـَٔايَـٰتِنَا كِذَّابً۬ا
But they belied Our Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, and that which Our Prophet SAW brought) completely.
78:29
وَكُلَّ شَىۡءٍ أَحۡصَيۡنَـٰهُ ڪِتَـٰبً۬ا
And all things We have recorded in a Book.
78:30
فَذُوقُواْ فَلَن نَّزِيدَكُمۡ إِلَّا عَذَابًا
So taste you (the results of your evil actions); no increase shall We give you, except in torment.
78:[31-40] The righteous will be well pleased and disbelievers will wish that they could remain merely dust
78:31
إِنَّ لِلۡمُتَّقِينَ مَفَازًا
Verily, for the Muttaqoon, there will be a success (Paradise (الجنة));
78:32
حَدَآٮِٕقَ وَأَعۡنَـٰبً۬ا
Gardens and grape yards;
78:33
وَكَوَاعِبَ أَتۡرَابً۬ا
And young full-breasted (mature) maidens of equal age;
78:34
وَكَأۡسً۬ا دِهَاقً۬ا
And a full cup (of wine).
78:35
لَّا يَسۡمَعُونَ فِيہَا لَغۡوً۬ا وَلَا كِذَّٲبً۬ا
No Laghw (dirty, false, evil talk) shall they hear therein, nor lying;
78:36
جَزَآءً۬ مِّن رَّبِّكَ عَطَآءً حِسَابً۬ا
A reward from your Lord, an ample calculated gift (according to the best of their good deeds).
78:37
رَّبِّ ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٲتِ وَٱلۡأَرۡضِ وَمَا بَيۡنَہُمَا ٱلرَّحۡمَـٰنِ‌ۖ
(From) the Lord of the heavens and the earth, and whatsoever is in between them, the Most Beneficent (Ar-Rahmaan (ٱلْرَّحْمَـانُ)),
لَا يَمۡلِكُونَ مِنۡهُ خِطَابً۬ا
none can dare to speak with Him (on the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة) except after His Leave).
78:38
يَوۡمَ يَقُومُ ٱلرُّوحُ وَٱلۡمَلَـٰٓٮِٕكَةُ صَفًّ۬ا‌ۖ
The Day that Ar-Rooh (Jibrael (Jibreel (جبريل)) or another angel) and the angels will stand forth in rows,
لَّا يَتَكَلَّمُونَ إِلَّا مَنۡ أَذِنَ لَهُ ٱلرَّحۡمَـٰنُ وَقَالَ صَوَابً۬ا
none shall speak except him whom the Most Beneficent (Ar-Rahmaan (ٱلْرَّحْمَـانُ)) allows, and he will speak what is right.
78:39
ذَٲلِكَ ٱلۡيَوۡمُ ٱلۡحَقُّ‌ۖ
That is without doubt the True Day,
فَمَن شَآءَ ٱتَّخَذَ إِلَىٰ رَبِّهِۦ مَـَٔابًا
so, whosoever wills, let him seek a place with (or a way to) His Lord (by obeying Him in this worldly life)!
78:40
إِنَّآ أَنذَرۡنَـٰكُمۡ عَذَابً۬ا قَرِيبً۬ا يَوۡمَ يَنظُرُ ٱلۡمَرۡءُ مَا قَدَّمَتۡ يَدَاهُ
Verily, We have warned you of a near torment, the Day when man will see that (the deeds) which his hands have sent forth,
وَيَقُولُ ٱلۡكَافِرُ يَـٰلَيۡتَنِى كُنتُ تُرَٲبَۢا
and the disbeliever will say: ‘Woe to me! Would that I were dust!‘*********
