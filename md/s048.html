Al-Fath
The Victory, Conquest
48:[1-10] Allah granted the Muslims a manifest victory through the treaty of Hudeybiyah. Swearing allegiance to the Prophet is considered swearing allegiance to Allah
48:1
إِنَّا فَتَحۡنَا لَكَ فَتۡحً۬ا مُّبِينً۬ا
Verily, We have given you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) a manifest victory.
48:2
لِّيَغۡفِرَ لَكَ ٱللَّهُ مَا تَقَدَّمَ مِن ذَنۢبِكَ وَمَا تَأَخَّرَ
That Allah (الله) may forgive you your sins of the past and the future,
وَيُتِمَّ نِعۡمَتَهُ ۥ عَلَيۡكَ وَيَہۡدِيَكَ صِرَٲطً۬ا مُّسۡتَقِيمً۬ا
and complete His Favour on you, and guide you on the Straight Path;
48:3
وَيَنصُرَكَ ٱللَّهُ نَصۡرًا عَزِيزًا
And that Allah (الله) may help you with strong help.
48:4
هُوَ ٱلَّذِىٓ أَنزَلَ ٱلسَّكِينَةَ فِى قُلُوبِ ٱلۡمُؤۡمِنِينَ لِيَزۡدَادُوٓاْ إِيمَـٰنً۬ا مَّعَ إِيمَـٰنِہِمۡ‌ۗ
He it is Who sent down As-Sakinah (calmness and tranquillity) into the hearts of the believers, that they may grow more in Faith along with their (present) Faith.
وَلِلَّهِ جُنُودُ ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٲتِ وَٱلۡأَرۡضِ‌ۚ
And to Allah (الله) belong the hosts of the heavens and the earth,
وَكَانَ ٱللَّهُ عَلِيمًا حَكِيمً۬ا
and Allah (الله) is Ever All-Knower (Al-‘Alee (ٱلْعَلِيُّ)m (ٱلْعَلِيمُ)), All-Wise (Al-Hakeem (ٱلْحَكِيمُ)).
48:5
لِّيُدۡخِلَ ٱلۡمُؤۡمِنِينَ وَٱلۡمُؤۡمِنَـٰتِ جَنَّـٰتٍ۬ تَجۡرِى مِن تَحۡتِہَا ٱلۡأَنۡہَـٰرُ خَـٰلِدِينَ فِيہَا
That He may admit the believing men and the believing women to Gardens under which rivers flow (i.e. Paradise (الجنة)), to abide therein forever,
وَيُڪَفِّرَ عَنۡهُمۡ سَيِّـَٔاتِہِمۡ‌ۚ
and to expiate from them their sins,
وَكَانَ ذَٲلِكَ عِندَ ٱللَّهِ فَوۡزًا عَظِيمً۬ا
and that is with Allah (الله), a supreme success,
48:6
وَيُعَذِّبَ ٱلۡمُنَـٰفِقِينَ وَٱلۡمُنَـٰفِقَـٰتِ وَٱلۡمُشۡرِكِينَ وَٱلۡمُشۡرِكَـٰتِ ٱلظَّآنِّينَ بِٱللَّهِ ظَنَّ ٱلسَّوۡءِ‌ۚ
And that He may punish the Munafiqoon (hypocrites), men and women, and also the Mushrikoon men and women, who think evil thoughts about Allah (الله),
عَلَيۡہِمۡ دَآٮِٕرَةُ ٱلسَّوۡءِ‌ۖ
for them is a disgraceful torment,
وَغَضِبَ ٱللَّهُ عَلَيۡہِمۡ وَلَعَنَهُمۡ وَأَعَدَّ لَهُمۡ جَهَنَّمَ‌ۖ
and the Anger of Allah (الله) is upon them, and He has cursed them and prepared Hell (جهنم) for them,
وَسَآءَتۡ مَصِيرً۬ا
and worst indeed is that destination.
48:7
وَلِلَّهِ جُنُودُ ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٲتِ وَٱلۡأَرۡضِ‌ۚ
And to Allah (الله) belong the hosts of the heavens and the earth.
وَكَانَ ٱللَّهُ عَزِيزًا حَكِيمًا
And Allah (الله) is Ever All-Knower (Al-‘Alee (ٱلْعَلِيُّ)m (ٱلْعَلِيمُ)), All- Wise.
48:8
إِنَّآ أَرۡسَلۡنَـٰكَ شَـٰهِدً۬ا وَمُبَشِّرً۬ا وَنَذِيرً۬ا
Verily, We have sent you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) as a witness, as a bearer of glad tidings, and as a warner.
48:9
لِّتُؤۡمِنُواْ بِٱللَّهِ وَرَسُولِهِۦ وَتُعَزِّرُوهُ وَتُوَقِّرُوهُ
In order that you (O mankind) may believe in Allah (الله) and His Messenger (SAW), and that you assist and honour him (SAW),
وَتُسَبِّحُوهُ بُڪۡرَةً۬ وَأَصِيلاً
and (that you) glorify (Allah (الله)s) praises morning and afternoon.
48:10
إِنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ يُبَايِعُونَكَ إِنَّمَا يُبَايِعُونَ ٱللَّهَ يَدُ ٱللَّهِ فَوۡقَ أَيۡدِيہِمۡ‌ۚ
Verily, those who give Baia (pledge) to you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) they are giving Baia (pledge) to Allah (الله). The Hand of Allah (الله) is over their hands.
فَمَن نَّكَثَ فَإِنَّمَا يَنكُثُ عَلَىٰ نَفۡسِهِۦ‌ۖ
Then whosoever breaks his pledge, breaks only to his own harm,
وَمَنۡ أَوۡفَىٰ بِمَا عَـٰهَدَ عَلَيۡهُ ٱللَّهَ فَسَيُؤۡتِيهِ أَجۡرًا عَظِيمً۬ا
and whosoever fulfills what he has covenanted with Allah (الله), He will bestow on him a great reward.
48:[11-17] Beduin Arabs who did not go with the Prophet for war between Islam and Kufr are condemned for lagging behind. Only the blind, lame and sick are exempt from war
48:11
سَيَقُولُ لَكَ ٱلۡمُخَلَّفُونَ مِنَ ٱلۡأَعۡرَابِ شَغَلَتۡنَآ أَمۡوَٲلُنَا وَأَهۡلُونَا فَٱسۡتَغۡفِرۡ لَنَا‌ۚ
Those of the bedouins who lagged behind will say to you: ‘Our possessions and our families occupied us, so ask forgiveness for us.‘
يَقُولُونَ بِأَلۡسِنَتِهِم مَّا لَيۡسَ فِى قُلُوبِهِمۡ‌ۚ
They say with their tongues what is not in their hearts.
قُلۡ فَمَن يَمۡلِكُ لَكُم مِّنَ ٱللَّهِ شَيۡـًٔا إِنۡ أَرَادَ بِكُمۡ ضَرًّا أَوۡ أَرَادَ بِكُمۡ نَفۡعَۢا‌ۚ
Say: ‘Who then has any power at all (to intervene) on your behalf with Allah (الله), if He intends you hurt or intends you benefit?
بَلۡ كَانَ ٱللَّهُ بِمَا تَعۡمَلُونَ خَبِيرَۢا
Nay, but Allah (الله) is Ever All-Aware (Al-‘Alee (ٱلْعَلِيُّ)m (ٱلْعَلِيمُ)) of what you do.
48:12
بَلۡ ظَنَنتُمۡ أَن لَّن يَنقَلِبَ ٱلرَّسُولُ وَٱلۡمُؤۡمِنُونَ إِلَىٰٓ أَهۡلِيهِمۡ أَبَدً۬ا
Nay, but you thought that the Messenger (SAW) and the believers would never return to their families;
وَزُيِّنَ ذَٲلِكَ فِى قُلُوبِكُمۡ وَظَنَنتُمۡ ظَنَّ ٱلسَّوۡءِ وَڪُنتُمۡ قَوۡمَۢا بُورً۬ا
and that was made fair-seeming in their hearts, and you did think an evil thought and you became a useless people going for destruction.‘
48:13
وَمَن لَّمۡ يُؤۡمِنۢ بِٱللَّهِ وَرَسُولِهِۦ فَإِنَّآ أَعۡتَدۡنَا لِلۡكَـٰفِرِينَ سَعِيرً۬ا
And whosoever does not believe in Allah (الله) and His Messenger (Muhammad (محمد) SAW), then verily, We have prepared for the disbelievers a blazing Fire .
48:14
وَلِلَّهِ مُلۡكُ ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٲتِ وَٱلۡأَرۡضِ‌ۚ
And to Allah (الله) belongs the sovereignty of the heavens and the earth,
يَغۡفِرُ لِمَن يَشَآءُ وَيُعَذِّبُ مَن يَشَآءُ‌ۚ
He forgives whom He wills, and punishes whom He wills.
وَڪَانَ ٱللَّهُ غَفُورً۬ا رَّحِيمً۬ا
And Allah (الله) is Ever Oft-Forgiving, Most Merciful (Ar-Raheem (ٱلْرَّحِيْمُ)).
48:15
سَيَقُولُ ٱلۡمُخَلَّفُونَ إِذَا ٱنطَلَقۡتُمۡ إِلَىٰ مَغَانِمَ لِتَأۡخُذُوهَا ذَرُونَا نَتَّبِعۡكُمۡ‌ۖ
Those who lagged behind will say, when you set forth to take the spoils, ‘Allow us to follow you,‘
يُرِيدُونَ أَن يُبَدِّلُواْ كَلَـٰمَ ٱللَّهِ‌ۚ
They want to change Allah (الله)‘s Words.
قُل لَّن تَتَّبِعُونَا ڪَذَٲلِكُمۡ قَالَ ٱللَّهُ مِن قَبۡلُ‌ۖ
Say: ‘You shall not follow us; thus Allah (الله) has said beforehand.‘
فَسَيَقُولُونَ بَلۡ تَحۡسُدُونَنَا‌ۚ
Then they will say: ‘Nay, you envy us.‘
بَلۡ كَانُواْ لَا يَفۡقَهُونَ إِلَّا قَلِيلاً۬
Nay, but they understand not except a little.
48:16
قُل لِّلۡمُخَلَّفِينَ مِنَ ٱلۡأَعۡرَابِ سَتُدۡعَوۡنَ إِلَىٰ قَوۡمٍ أُوْلِى بَأۡسٍ۬ شَدِيدٍ۬ تُقَـٰتِلُونَہُمۡ أَوۡ يُسۡلِمُونَ‌ۖ
Say (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) to the bedouins who lagged behind:
You shall be called to fight against a people given to great warfare, then you shall fight them, or they shall surrender.
فَإِن تُطِيعُواْ يُؤۡتِكُمُ ٱللَّهُ أَجۡرًا حَسَنً۬ا‌ۖ
Then if you obey, Allah (الله) will give you a fair reward,
وَإِن تَتَوَلَّوۡاْ كَمَا تَوَلَّيۡتُم مِّن قَبۡلُ يُعَذِّبۡكُمۡ عَذَابًا أَلِيمً۬ا
but if you turn away as you did turn away before, He will punish you with a painful torment.‘
48:17
لَّيۡسَ عَلَى ٱلۡأَعۡمَىٰ حَرَجٌ۬ وَلَا عَلَى ٱلۡأَعۡرَجِ حَرَجٌ۬ وَلَا عَلَى ٱلۡمَرِيضِ حَرَجٌ۬‌ۗ
No blame or sin is there upon the blind, nor is there blame or sin upon the lame, nor is there blame or sin upon the sick (that they go not forth to war).
وَمَن يُطِعِ ٱللَّهَ وَرَسُولَهُ ۥ يُدۡخِلۡهُ جَنَّـٰتٍ۬ تَجۡرِى مِن تَحۡتِهَا ٱلۡأَنۡہَـٰرُ‌ۖ
And whosoever obeys Allah (الله) and His Messenger (Muhammad (محمد) SAW), He will admit him to Gardens beneath which rivers flow (Paradise (الجنة));
وَمَن يَتَوَلَّ يُعَذِّبۡهُ عَذَابًا أَلِيمً۬ا
and whosoever turns back, He will punish him with a painful torment.
48:[18-26] Allah was well pleased with those who swore allegiance to the Prophet before the treaty of Hudeybiyah. Had there not been believers in Makkah, Allah would have allowed the Muslims to fight against Qureysh
48:18
لَّقَدۡ رَضِىَ ٱللَّهُ عَنِ ٱلۡمُؤۡمِنِينَ إِذۡ يُبَايِعُونَكَ تَحۡتَ ٱلشَّجَرَةِ
Indeed, Allah (الله) was pleased with the believers when they gave their Baia (pledge) to you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) under the tree,
فَعَلِمَ مَا فِى قُلُوبِہِمۡ فَأَنزَلَ ٱلسَّكِينَةَ عَلَيۡہِمۡ
He knew what was in their hearts, and He sent down As-Sakinah (calmness and tranquillity) upon them,
وَأَثَـٰبَهُمۡ فَتۡحً۬ا قَرِيبً۬ا
and He rewarded them with a near victory,
48:19
وَمَغَانِمَ كَثِيرَةً۬ يَأۡخُذُونَہَا‌ۗ
And abundant spoils that they will capture.
وَكَانَ ٱللَّهُ عَزِيزًا حَكِيمً۬ا
And Allah (الله) is Ever All-Mighty, All-Wise (Al-Hakeem (ٱلْحَكِيمُ)).
48:20
وَعَدَكُمُ ٱللَّهُ مَغَانِمَ ڪَثِيرَةً۬ تَأۡخُذُونَہَا
Allah (الله) has promised you abundant spoils that you will capture,
فَعَجَّلَ لَكُمۡ هَـٰذِهِۦ وَكَفَّ أَيۡدِىَ ٱلنَّاسِ عَنكُمۡ
and He has hastened for you this, and He has restrained the hands of men from you,
وَلِتَكُونَ ءَايَةً۬ لِّلۡمُؤۡمِنِينَ وَيَهۡدِيَكُمۡ صِرَٲطً۬ا مُّسۡتَقِيمً۬ا
that it may be a sign for the believers, and that He may guide you to a Straight Path.
48:21
وَأُخۡرَىٰ لَمۡ تَقۡدِرُواْ عَلَيۡہَا قَدۡ أَحَاطَ ٱللَّهُ بِہَا‌ۚ
And other (victories and much booty there are, He promises you) which are not yet within your power, indeed Allah (الله) compasses them,
وَكَانَ ٱللَّهُ عَلَىٰ ڪُلِّ شَىۡءٍ۬ قَدِيرً۬ا
And Allah (الله) is Ever Able to do all things.
48:22
وَلَوۡ قَـٰتَلَكُمُ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُواْ لَوَلَّوُاْ ٱلۡأَدۡبَـٰرَ ثُمَّ لَا يَجِدُونَ وَلِيًّ۬ا وَلَا نَصِيرً۬ا
And if those who disbelieve fight against you, they certainly would have turned their backs, then they would have found neither a Walee (protector) nor a helper.
48:23
سُنَّةَ ٱللَّهِ ٱلَّتِى قَدۡ خَلَتۡ مِن قَبۡلُ‌ۖ
That has been the Way of Allah (الله) already with those who passed away before.
وَلَن تَجِدَ لِسُنَّةِ ٱللَّهِ تَبۡدِيلاً۬
And you will not find any change in the Way of Allah (الله).
48:24
وَهُوَ ٱلَّذِى كَفَّ أَيۡدِيَهُمۡ عَنكُمۡ وَأَيۡدِيَكُمۡ عَنۡہُم بِبَطۡنِ مَكَّةَ مِنۢ بَعۡدِ أَنۡ أَظۡفَرَكُمۡ عَلَيۡهِمۡ‌ۚ
And He it is Who has withheld their hands from you and your hands from them in the midst of Makkah (مكة), after He had made you victors over them.
وَكَانَ ٱللَّهُ بِمَا تَعۡمَلُونَ بَصِيرًا
And Allah (الله) is Ever the All-Seer (Al-Baseer (ٱلْبَصِيرُ)) of what you do.
48:25
هُمُ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُواْ وَصَدُّوڪُمۡ عَنِ ٱلۡمَسۡجِدِ ٱلۡحَرَامِ وَٱلۡهَدۡىَ مَعۡكُوفًا أَن يَبۡلُغَ مَحِلَّهُ ۥ‌ۚ
They are the ones who disbelieved (in the Oneness of Allah (الله) Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism), and hindered you from Al Masjidal Haram (the sacred mosque of Makkah (مكة)) and the sacrificial animals, detained from reaching their place of sacrifice.
وَلَوۡلَا رِجَالٌ۬ مُّؤۡمِنُونَ وَنِسَآءٌ۬ مُّؤۡمِنَـٰتٌ۬ لَّمۡ تَعۡلَمُوهُمۡ
Had there not been believing men and believing women whom you did not know,
أَن تَطَـُٔوهُمۡ فَتُصِيبَكُم مِّنۡهُم مَّعَرَّةُۢ بِغَيۡرِ عِلۡمٍ۬‌ۖ
that you may kill them, and on whose account a sin would have been committed by you without (your) knowledge,
لِّيُدۡخِلَ ٱللَّهُ فِى رَحۡمَتِهِۦ مَن يَشَآءُ‌ۚ
that Allah (الله) might bring into His Mercy whom He will,
لَوۡ تَزَيَّلُواْ لَعَذَّبۡنَا ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُواْ مِنۡهُمۡ عَذَابًا أَلِيمًا
if they (the believers and the disbelievers) should have been apart, We verily had punished those of them who disbelieved, with painful torment.
48:26
إِذۡ جَعَلَ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُواْ فِى قُلُوبِهِمُ ٱلۡحَمِيَّةَ حَمِيَّةَ ٱلۡجَـٰهِلِيَّةِ
When those who disbelieve had put in their hearts pride and haughtiness the pride and haughtiness of the time of ignorance,
فَأَنزَلَ ٱللَّهُ سَڪِينَتَهُ ۥ عَلَىٰ رَسُولِهِۦ وَعَلَى ٱلۡمُؤۡمِنِينَ وَأَلۡزَمَهُمۡ ڪَلِمَةَ ٱلتَّقۡوَىٰ
then Allah (الله) sent down His Sakinah (calmness and tranquillity) upon His Messenger ( SAW) and upon the believers, and made them stick to the word of piety (i.e. none has the right to be worshipped but Allah (الله)),
وَكَانُوٓاْ أَحَقَّ بِہَا وَأَهۡلَهَا‌ۚ
and they were well entitled to it and worthy of it.
وَكَانَ ٱللَّهُ بِكُلِّ شَىۡءٍ عَلِيمً۬ا
And Allah (الله) is the All-Knower (Al-‘Alee (ٱلْعَلِيُّ)m (ٱلْعَلِيمُ)) of everything.
48:[27-29] Vision to conquer Makkah is shown to the Prophet. Characteristics of Muhammad (pbuh) and his followers
48:27
لَّقَدۡ صَدَقَ ٱللَّهُ رَسُولَهُ ٱلرُّءۡيَا بِٱلۡحَقِّ‌ۖ
Indeed Allah (الله) shall fulfil the true vision which He showed to His Messenger (SAW) (i.e. the Prophet SAW a dream that he has entered Makkah (مكة) along with his companions, having their (head) hair shaved and cut short) in very truth.
لَتَدۡخُلُنَّ ٱلۡمَسۡجِدَ ٱلۡحَرَامَ إِن شَآءَ ٱللَّهُ ءَامِنِينَ مُحَلِّقِينَ رُءُوسَكُمۡ وَمُقَصِّرِينَ لَا تَخَافُونَ‌ۖ
Certainly, you shall enter Al Masjidal Haram; if Allah (الله) wills, secure, (some) having your heads shaved, and (some) having your head hair cut short, having no fear.
فَعَلِمَ مَا لَمۡ تَعۡلَمُواْ فَجَعَلَ مِن دُونِ ذَٲلِكَ فَتۡحً۬ا قَرِيبًا
He knew what you knew not, and He granted besides that a near victory.
48:28
هُوَ ٱلَّذِىٓ أَرۡسَلَ رَسُولَهُ ۥ بِٱلۡهُدَىٰ وَدِينِ ٱلۡحَقِّ لِيُظۡهِرَهُ ۥ عَلَى ٱلدِّينِ كُلِّهِۦ‌ۚ
He it is Who has sent His Messenger (Muhammad (محمد) SAW) with guidance and the religion of truth (Islam (الإسلام)), that He may make it (Islam (الإسلام)) superior over all religions.
وَكَفَىٰ بِٱللَّهِ شَهِيدً۬ا
And All-Sufficient (Al-Waasi’ (ٱلْوَاسِعُ)) is Allah (الله) as a Witness.
48:29
مُّحَمَّدٌ۬ رَّسُولُ ٱللَّهِ‌ۚ
Muhammad (محمد) SAW (SAW) is the Messenger of Allah (الله),
وَٱلَّذِينَ مَعَهُ ۥۤ أَشِدَّآءُ عَلَى ٱلۡكُفَّارِ رُحَمَآءُ بَيۡنَہُمۡ‌ۖ
and those who are with him are severe against disbelievers, and merciful among themselves.
تَرَٮٰهُمۡ رُكَّعً۬ا سُجَّدً۬ا يَبۡتَغُونَ فَضۡلاً۬ مِّنَ ٱللَّهِ وَرِضۡوَٲنً۬ا‌ۖ
You see them bowing and falling down prostrate (in prayer), seeking Bounty from Allah (الله) and (His) Good Pleasure.
سِيمَاهُمۡ فِى وُجُوهِهِم مِّنۡ أَثَرِ ٱلسُّجُودِ‌ۚ
The mark of them (i.e. of their Faith) is on their faces (foreheads) from the traces of (their) prostration (during prayers).
ذَٲلِكَ مَثَلُهُمۡ فِى ٱلتَّوۡرَٮٰةِ‌ۚ
This is their description in the Taurat (Torah (التوراة)).
وَمَثَلُهُمۡ فِى ٱلۡإِنجِيلِ كَزَرۡعٍ أَخۡرَجَ شَطۡـَٔهُ ۥ فَـَٔازَرَهُ ۥ فَٱسۡتَغۡلَظَ فَٱسۡتَوَىٰ عَلَىٰ سُوقِهِۦ يُعۡجِبُ ٱلزُّرَّاعَ
But their description in the Injil (Injeel (الإنجيل)) is like a (sown) seed which sends forth its shoot, then makes it strong, it then becomes thick, and it stands straight on its stem, delighting the sowers
لِيَغِيظَ بِہِمُ ٱلۡكُفَّارَ‌ۗ
that He may enrage the disbelievers with them.
وَعَدَ ٱللَّهُ ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُواْ وَعَمِلُواْ ٱلصَّـٰلِحَـٰتِ مِنۡہُم مَّغۡفِرَةً۬ وَأَجۡرًا عَظِيمَۢا
Allah (الله) has promised those among them who believe (i.e. all those who follow Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism, the religion of Prophet Muhammad (محمد) SAW till the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة)) and do righteous good deeds, forgiveness and a mighty reward (i.e. Paradise (الجنة)).*********
