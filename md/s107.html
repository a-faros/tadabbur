Al-Maa'un
The Neighbourly Assistance, Small Kindnesses, Almsgiving, Assistance
107:[1-7] Disbelief in the Hereafter is the main cause of moral decay and lack of caring about others
107:1
أَرَءَيۡتَ ٱلَّذِى يُكَذِّبُ بِٱلدِّينِ
Have you seen him who denies the Recompense?
107:2
فَذَٲلِكَ ٱلَّذِى يَدُعُّ ٱلۡيَتِيمَ
That is he who repulses the orphan (harshly),
107:3
وَلَا يَحُضُّ عَلَىٰ طَعَامِ ٱلۡمِسۡكِينِ
And urges not the feeding of AlMiskîn (the poor),
107:4
فَوَيۡلٌ۬ لِّلۡمُصَلِّينَ
So woe unto those performers of Salât (prayers) (hypocrites),
107:5
ٱلَّذِينَ هُمۡ عَن صَلَاتِہِمۡ سَاهُونَ
Who delay their Salât (prayer) from their stated fixed times,
107:6
ٱلَّذِينَ هُمۡ يُرَآءُونَ
Those who do good deeds only to be seen (of men),
107:7
وَيَمۡنَعُونَ ٱلۡمَاعُونَ
And refuse Al-Mâ‘ûn (small kindnesses e.g. salt, sugar, water, etc.).*********
