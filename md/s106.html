Quraish
The Quraysh
106:[1-4] Believe in Allah Who is the provider of your sustenance
106:1
لِإِيلَـٰفِ قُرَيۡشٍ
(It is a great Grace and Protection from Allâh), for the taming of the Quraish,
106:2
إِۦلَـٰفِهِمۡ رِحۡلَةَ ٱلشِّتَآءِ وَٱلصَّيۡفِ
(And with all those Allâh‘s Grace and Protections for their taming, We cause) the (Quraish) caravans to set forth safe in winter (to the south), and in summer (to the north without any fear),
106:3
فَلۡيَعۡبُدُواْ رَبَّ هَـٰذَا ٱلۡبَيۡتِ
So let them worship (Allâh) the Lord of this House (the Ka‘bah in Makkah (مكة)).
106:4
ٱلَّذِىٓ أَطۡعَمَهُم مِّن جُوعٍ۬ وَءَامَنَهُم مِّنۡ خَوۡفِۭ
(He) Who has fed them against hunger, and has made them safe from fear.*********
