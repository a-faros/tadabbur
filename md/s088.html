Al-Ghaashiya
The Overwhelming Event, The Overshadowing Event, The Pall
88:[1-16] Condition of the disbelievers and the believers on the Day of Judgment
88:[17-26] Wonders of nature, admonition and accountability
88:1
هَلۡ أَتَٮٰكَ حَدِيثُ ٱلۡغَـٰشِيَةِ
Has there come to you the narration of the overwhelming (i.e. the Day of Resurrection);
88:2
وُجُوهٌ۬ يَوۡمَٮِٕذٍ خَـٰشِعَةٌ
Some faces, that Day, will be humiliated.(in the Hell-fire, i.e. the faces of all disbelievers, Jews and Christians, etc.)
88:3
عَامِلَةٌ۬ نَّاصِبَةٌ۬
Labouring (hard in the worldly life by worshipping others besides Allâh), weary (in the Hereafter with humility and disgrace)
88:4
تَصۡلَىٰ نَارًا حَامِيَةً۬
They will enter in the hot blazing Fire,
88:5
تُسۡقَىٰ مِنۡ عَيۡنٍ ءَانِيَةٍ۬
They will be given to drink from a boiling spring,
88:6
لَّيۡسَ لَهُمۡ طَعَامٌ إِلَّا مِن ضَرِيعٍ۬
No food will there be for them but a poisonous thorny plant,
88:7
لَّا يُسۡمِنُ وَلَا يُغۡنِى مِن جُوعٍ۬
Which will neither nourish nor avail against hunger.
88:8
وُجُوهٌ۬ يَوۡمَٮِٕذٍ۬ نَّاعِمَةٌ۬
(Other) faces, that Day, will be joyful,
88:9
لِّسَعۡيِہَا رَاضِيَةٌ۬
Glad with their endeavour
(for their good deeds which they did in this world, along with the true Faith of Islâmic Monotheism).
88:10
فِى جَنَّةٍ عَالِيَةٍ۬
In a lofty Paradise. لَّا تَسۡمَعُ فِيہَا لَـٰغِيَةً۬
88:11
لَّا تَسۡمَعُ فِيہَا لَـٰغِيَةً۬
Where they shall neither hear harmful speech nor falsehood,
88:12
فِيہَا عَيۡنٌ۬ جَارِيَةٌ۬
Therein will be a running spring,
88:13
فِيہَا سُرُرٌ۬ مَّرۡفُوعَةٌ۬
Therein will be thrones raised high,
88:14
وَأَكۡوَابٌ۬ مَّوۡضُوعَةٌ۬
And cups set at hand.
88:15
وَنَمَارِقُ مَصۡفُوفَةٌ۬
And cushions set in rows,
88:16
وَزَرَابِىُّ مَبۡثُوثَةٌ
And rich carpets (all) spread out.
88:17
أَفَلَا يَنظُرُونَ إِلَى ٱلۡإِبِلِ ڪَيۡفَ خُلِقَتۡ
Do they not look at the camels, how they are created?
88:18
وَإِلَى ٱلسَّمَآءِ ڪَيۡفَ رُفِعَتۡ
And at the heaven, how it is raised?
88:19
وَإِلَى ٱلۡجِبَالِ كَيۡفَ نُصِبَتۡ
And at the mountains, how they are rooted and fixed firm?
88:20
وَإِلَى ٱلۡأَرۡضِ كَيۡفَ سُطِحَتۡ
And at the earth, how it is spread out?
88:21
فَذَكِّرۡ إِنَّمَآ أَنتَ مُذَڪِّرٌ۬
So remind them (O Muhammad SAW), you are only a one who reminds.
88:22
لَّسۡتَ عَلَيۡهِم بِمُصَيۡطِرٍ
You are not a dictator over them.
88:23
إِلَّا مَن تَوَلَّىٰ وَكَفَرَ
Save the one who turns away and disbelieves
88:24
فَيُعَذِّبُهُ ٱللَّهُ ٱلۡعَذَابَ ٱلۡأَكۡبَرَ
Then Allâh will punish him with the greatest punishment.
88:25
إِنَّ إِلَيۡنَآ إِيَابَہُمۡ
Verily, to Us will be their return;
88:26
ثُمَّ إِنَّ عَلَيۡنَا حِسَابَہُم
Then verily, for Us will be their reckoning.*********
