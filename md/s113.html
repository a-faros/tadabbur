Al-Falaq
The Daybreak, Dawn, The Rising Dawn
113:[1-5] Seek refuge with Allah from all evils
113:1
قُلۡ أَعُوذُ بِرَبِّ ٱلۡفَلَقِ
Say: ‘I seek refuge with (Allâh) the Lord of the daybreak,
113:2
مِن شَرِّ مَا خَلَقَ
From the evil of what He has created;
113:3
وَمِن شَرِّ غَاسِقٍ إِذَا وَقَبَ
And from the evil of the darkening (night) as it comes with its darkness;
113:4
وَمِن شَرِّ ٱلنَّفَّـٰثَـٰتِ فِى ٱلۡعُقَدِ
(or the moon as it sets or goes away).
And from the evil of the witchcrafts when they blow in the knots,
113:5
وَمِن شَرِّ حَاسِدٍ إِذَا حَسَدَ
‘And from the evil of the envier when he envies.‘*********
