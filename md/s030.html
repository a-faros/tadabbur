Ar-Room
Rome, Byzantium, The Romans
30:[1-10] The Roman‘s (Christians‘) defeat at the hands of the Persian (Pagans) was taken as a sign of the Muslims‘ defeat at the hands of Arab unbelievers, so Allah gave good tidings for the Roman victory as well as the Muslim victory in a few years
30:1
الٓمٓ
Alif Lam Meem.(These letters are one of the miracles of the Quran (القرآن), and none but Allah (الله) (Alone) knows their meanings).
30:2
غُلِبَتِ ٱلرُّومُ
The Romans (الروم) have been defeated.
30:3
فِىٓ أَدۡنَى ٱلۡأَرۡضِ
In the nearer land (Syria, Iraq, Jordan, and Palestine),
وَهُم مِّنۢ بَعۡدِ غَلَبِهِمۡ سَيَغۡلِبُونَ
and they, after their defeat, will be victorious.
30:4
فِى بِضۡعِ سِنِينَ‌ۗ
Within three to nine years.
لِلَّهِ ٱلۡأَمۡرُ مِن قَبۡلُ وَمِنۢ بَعۡدُ‌ۚ
The decision of the matter, before and after (these events) is only with Allah (الله),(before the defeat of Romans (الروم) by the Persians, and after, i.e. the defeat of the Persians by the Romans (الروم)).
وَيَوۡمَٮِٕذٍ۬ يَفۡرَحُ ٱلۡمُؤۡمِنُونَ
And on that Day, the believers (i.e. Muslims) will rejoice (at the victory given by Allah (الله) to the Romans (الروم) against the Persians),
30:5
بِنَصۡرِ ٱللَّهِ‌ۚ
With the help of Allah (الله),
يَنصُرُ مَن يَشَآءُ‌ۖ وَهُوَ ٱلۡعَزِيزُ ٱلرَّحِيمُ
He helps whom He wills, and He is the All-Mighty, the Most Merciful (Ar-Raheem (ٱلْرَّحِيْمُ)).
30:6
وَعۡدَ ٱللَّهِ‌ۖ
(It is) a Promise of Allah (الله), (i.e. Allah (الله) will give victory to the Romans (الروم) against the Persians)
لَا يُخۡلِفُ ٱللَّهُ وَعۡدَهُ ۥ وَلَـٰكِنَّ أَڪۡثَرَ ٱلنَّاسِ لَا يَعۡلَمُونَ
and Allah (الله) fails not in His Promise, but most of men know not.
30:7
يَعۡلَمُونَ ظَـٰهِرً۬ا مِّنَ ٱلۡحَيَوٰةِ ٱلدُّنۡيَا وَهُمۡ عَنِ ٱلۡأَخِرَةِ هُمۡ غَـٰفِلُونَ
They know only the outside appearance of the life of the world (i.e. the matters of their livelihood, like irrigating or sowing or reaping, etc.), and they are heedless of the Hereafter.
30:8
أَوَلَمۡ يَتَفَكَّرُواْ فِىٓ أَنفُسِہِم‌ۗ
Do they not think deeply (in their ownselves) about themselves (how Allah (الله) created them from nothing, and similarly He will resurrect them)?
مَّا خَلَقَ ٱللَّهُ ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٲتِ وَٱلۡأَرۡضَ وَمَا بَيۡنَہُمَآ إِلَّا بِٱلۡحَقِّ وَأَجَلٍ۬ مُّسَمًّ۬ى‌ۗ
Allah (الله) has created not the heavens and the earth, and all that is between them, except with truth and for an appointed term.
وَإِنَّ كَثِيرً۬ا مِّنَ ٱلنَّاسِ بِلِقَآىِٕ رَبِّهِمۡ لَكَـٰفِرُونَ
And indeed many of mankind deny the Meeting with their Lord. (See Tafsir At Tabaree, Part 21, Page 24).
30:9
أَوَلَمۡ يَسِيرُواْ فِى ٱلۡأَرۡضِ فَيَنظُرُواْ كَيۡفَ كَانَ عَـٰقِبَةُ ٱلَّذِينَ مِن قَبۡلِهِمۡ‌ۚ
Do they not travel in the land, and see what was the end of those before them?
ڪَانُوٓاْ أَشَدَّ مِنۡہُمۡ قُوَّةً۬
They were superior to them in strength,
وَأَثَارُواْ ٱلۡأَرۡضَ وَعَمَرُوهَآ أَڪۡثَرَ مِمَّا عَمَرُوهَا
and they tilled the earth and populated it in greater numbers than these (pagans) have done,
وَجَآءَتۡهُمۡ رُسُلُهُم بِٱلۡبَيِّنَـٰتِ‌ۖ
and there came to them their Messengers with clear proofs.
فَمَا كَانَ ٱللَّهُ لِيَظۡلِمَهُمۡ وَلَـٰكِن كَانُوٓاْ أَنفُسَہُمۡ يَظۡلِمُونَ
Surely, Allah (الله) wronged them not, but they used to wrong themselves.
30:10
ثُمَّ كَانَ عَـٰقِبَةَ ٱلَّذِينَ أَسَـٰٓـُٔواْ ٱلسُّوٓأَىٰٓ
Then evil was the end of those who did evil,
أَن ڪَذَّبُواْ بِـَٔايَـٰتِ ٱللَّهِ وَكَانُواْ بِہَا يَسۡتَهۡزِءُونَ
because they belied the Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, Messengers, etc.) of Allah (الله) and made mock of them.
30:[11-19] It is Allah Who originates creation and then repeats it and to Him everyone will be brought for the final Judgement
30:11
ٱللَّهُ يَبۡدَؤُاْ ٱلۡخَلۡقَ ثُمَّ يُعِيدُهُ ۥ ثُمَّ إِلَيۡهِ تُرۡجَعُونَ
Allah (الله) (Alone) originates the creation, then He will repeat it, then to Him you will be returned.
30:12
وَيَوۡمَ تَقُومُ ٱلسَّاعَةُ يُبۡلِسُ ٱلۡمُجۡرِمُونَ
And on the Day when the Hour will be established, the Mujrimoon (disbelievers, sinners, criminals, polytheists, etc.) will be plunged into destruction with deep regrets, sorrows, and despair.
30:13
وَلَمۡ يَكُن لَّهُم مِّن شُرَكَآٮِٕهِمۡ شُفَعَـٰٓؤُاْوَڪَانُواْ بِشُرَكَآٮِٕهِمۡ ڪَـٰفِرِينَ
No intercessor will they have from those whom they made equal with Allah (الله) (partners i.e. their so-called associate gods), and they will (themselves) reject and deny their partners.
30:14
وَيَوۡمَ تَقُومُ ٱلسَّاعَةُ يَوۡمَٮِٕذٍ۬ يَتَفَرَّقُونَ
And on the Day when the Hour will be established, that Day shall (all men) be separated (i.e the believers will be separated from the disbelievers).
30:15
فَأَمَّا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُواْ وَعَمِلُواْ ٱلصَّـٰلِحَـٰتِ فَهُمۡ فِى رَوۡضَةٍ۬ يُحۡبَرُونَ
Then as for those who believed (in the Oneness of Allah (الله) Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism) and did righteous good deeds, such shall be honoured and made to enjoy luxurious life (forever) in a Garden of delight (Paradise (الجنة)).
30:16
وَأَمَّا ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُواْ وَكَذَّبُواْ بِـَٔايَـٰتِنَا وَلِقَآىِٕ ٱلۡأَخِرَةِ فَأُوْلَـٰٓٮِٕكَ فِى ٱلۡعَذَابِ مُحۡضَرُونَ
And as for those who disbelieved and belied Our Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, Allah (الله)‘s Messengers, Resurrection, etc.), and the Meeting of the Hereafter, such shall be brought forth to the torment (in the Hell (جهنم)-fire).
30:17
فَسُبۡحَـٰنَ ٱللَّهِ حِينَ تُمۡسُونَ وَحِينَ تُصۡبِحُونَ
So glorify Allah (الله) (above all that (evil) they associate with Him (O believers)), when you come up to the evening (i.e. offer the (Maghrib) sunset and (Isha) night prayers), and when you enter the morning (i.e offer the (Fajr) morning prayer).
30:18
وَلَهُ ٱلۡحَمۡدُ فِى ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٲتِ وَٱلۡأَرۡضِ وَعَشِيًّ۬ا وَحِينَ تُظۡهِرُونَ
And His is all the praises and thanks in the heavens and the earth, and (glorify Him) in the afternoon (i.e. offer Asr prayer) and when you come up to the time, when the day begins to decline (i.e offer Zuhr prayer).Ibn Abbas said: ‘These are the five compulsory congregational prayers mentioned in the Quran (القرآن).‘
30:19
يُخۡرِجُ ٱلۡحَىَّ مِنَ ٱلۡمَيِّتِ وَيُخۡرِجُ ٱلۡمَيِّتَ مِنَ ٱلۡحَىِّ وَيُحۡىِ ٱلۡأَرۡضَ بَعۡدَ مَوۡتِہَا‌ۚ
He brings out the living from the dead, and brings out the dead from the living. And He revives the earth after its death.
وَكَذَٲلِكَ تُخۡرَجُونَ
And thus shall you be brought out (resurrected).
30:[20-27] The creation of man, his consort, heavens, earth, language, colors, sleep, quest for work, lightning, rain and growth of vegetation - all are signs of Allah
30:20
وَمِنۡ ءَايَـٰتِهِۦۤ أَنۡ خَلَقَكُم مِّن تُرَابٍ۬ ثُمَّ إِذَآ أَنتُم بَشَرٌ۬ تَنتَشِرُونَ
And among His Signs is this, that He created you (Adam (آدم)) from dust, and then (Hawwa (Eve) from Adam (آدم)s rib, and then his offspring from the semen, and), - behold you are human beings scattered!
30:21
وَمِنۡ ءَايَـٰتِهِۦۤ أَنۡ خَلَقَ لَكُم مِّنۡ أَنفُسِكُمۡ أَزۡوَٲجً۬ا لِّتَسۡكُنُوٓاْ إِلَيۡهَا
And among His Signs is this, that He created for you wives from among yourselves, that you may find repose in them,
وَجَعَلَ بَيۡنَڪُم مَّوَدَّةً۬ وَرَحۡمَةً‌ۚ
and He has put between you affection and mercy.
إِنَّ فِى ذَٲلِكَ لَأَيَـٰتٍ۬ لِّقَوۡمٍ۬ يَتَفَكَّرُونَ
Verily, in that are indeed signs for a people who reflect.
30:22
وَمِنۡ ءَايَـٰتِهِۦ خَلۡقُ ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٲتِ وَٱلۡأَرۡضِ وَٱخۡتِلَـٰفُ أَلۡسِنَتِڪُمۡ وَأَلۡوَٲنِكُمۡ‌ۚ
And among His Signs is the creation of the heavens and the earth, and the difference of your languages and colours.
إِنَّ فِى ذَٲلِكَ لَأَيَـٰتٍ۬ لِّلۡعَـٰلِمِينَ
Verily, in that are indeed signs for men of sound knowledge.
30:23
وَمِنۡ ءَايَـٰتِهِۦ مَنَامُكُم بِٱلَّيۡلِ وَٱلنَّہَارِ وَٱبۡتِغَآؤُكُم مِّن فَضۡلِهِۦۤ‌ۚ
And among His Signs is the sleep that you take by night and by day, and your seeking of His Bounty.
إِنَّ فِى ذَٲلِكَ لَأَيَـٰتٍ۬ لِّقَوۡمٍ۬ يَسۡمَعُونَ
Verily, in that are indeed signs for a people who listen.
30:24
وَمِنۡ ءَايَـٰتِهِۦ يُرِيڪُمُ ٱلۡبَرۡقَ خَوۡفً۬ا وَطَمَعً۬ا
And among His Signs is that He shows you the lightning, by way of fear and hope,
وَيُنَزِّلُ مِنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ مَآءً۬ فَيُحۡىِۦ بِهِ ٱلۡأَرۡضَ بَعۡدَ مَوۡتِهَآ‌ۚ
and He sends down water (rain) from the sky, and therewith revives the earth after its death.
إِنَّ فِى ذَٲلِكَ لَأَيَـٰتٍ۬ لِّقَوۡمٍ۬ يَعۡقِلُونَ
Verily, in that are indeed signs for a people who understand.
30:25
وَمِنۡ ءَايَـٰتِهِۦۤ أَن تَقُومَ ٱلسَّمَآءُ وَٱلۡأَرۡضُ بِأَمۡرِهِۦ‌ۚ
And among His Signs is that the heaven and the earth stand by His Command,
ثُمَّ إِذَا دَعَاكُمۡ دَعۡوَةً۬ مِّنَ ٱلۡأَرۡضِ إِذَآ أَنتُمۡ تَخۡرُجُونَ
then afterwards when He will call you by single call, behold, you will come out from the earth (i.e from your graves for reckoning and recompense).
30:26
وَلَهُ ۥ مَن فِى ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٲتِ وَٱلۡأَرۡضِ‌ۖ
To Him belongs whatever is in the heavens and the earth.
ڪُلٌّ۬ لَّهُ ۥ قَـٰنِتُونَ
All are obedient to Him.
30:27
وَهُوَ ٱلَّذِى يَبۡدَؤُاْ ٱلۡخَلۡقَ ثُمَّ يُعِيدُهُ ۥ وَهُوَ أَهۡوَنُ عَلَيۡهِ‌ۚ
And He it is Who originates the creation, then will repeat it (after it has been perished), and this is easier for Him.
وَلَهُ ٱلۡمَثَلُ ٱلۡأَعۡلَىٰ فِى ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٲتِ وَٱلۡأَرۡضِ‌ۚ
His is the highest description (i.e. none has the right to be worshipped but He, and there is nothing comparable unto Him) in the heavens and in the earth.
وَهُوَ ٱلۡعَزِيزُ ٱلۡحَكِيمُ
And He is the All-Mighty, the All Wise.
30:[28-32] The wrongdoers are led by their own desires without real knowledge. True faith and the nature of sects
30:[33-37] When an affliction befalls people they call upon Allah, but when He relieves them, lo! They start committing shirk
30:[38-40] Commandment to give ones relatives their due and likewise to the poor and the travellers in need
30:28
ضَرَبَ لَكُم مَّثَلاً۬ مِّنۡ أَنفُسِكُمۡ‌ۖ
He sets forth for you a parable from your ownselves, -
هَل لَّكُم مِّن مَّا مَلَكَتۡ أَيۡمَـٰنُكُم مِّن شُرَڪَآءَ فِى مَا رَزَقۡنَـٰڪُمۡ
Do you have partners among those whom your right hands possess (i.e your slaves) to share as equals in the wealth We have bestowed on you?
فَأَنتُمۡ فِيهِ سَوَآءٌ۬ تَخَافُونَهُمۡ كَخِيفَتِڪُمۡ أَنفُسَكُمۡ‌ۚ
Whom you fear as you fear each other?
ڪَذَٲلِكَ نُفَصِّلُ ٱلۡأَيَـٰتِ لِقَوۡمٍ۬ يَعۡقِلُونَ
Thus do We explain the signs in detail to a people who have sense .
30:29
بَلِ ٱتَّبَعَ ٱلَّذِينَ ظَلَمُوٓاْ أَهۡوَآءَهُم بِغَيۡرِ عِلۡمٍ۬‌ۖ
Nay, but those who do wrong follow their own lusts without knowledge,
فَمَن يَہۡدِى مَنۡ أَضَلَّ ٱللَّهُ‌ۖ
Then who will guide him whom Allah (الله) has sent astray?
وَمَا لَهُم مِّن نَّـٰصِرِينَ
And for such there will be no helpers.
30:30
فَأَقِمۡ وَجۡهَكَ لِلدِّينِ حَنِيفً۬ا‌ۚ
So set you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) your face towards the religion of pure Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism Hanifa (worship none but Allah (الله) Alone)
فِطۡرَتَ ٱللَّهِ ٱلَّتِى فَطَرَ ٱلنَّاسَ عَلَيۡہَا‌ۚ
Allah (الله)‘s Fitrah (i.e. Allah (الله)‘s Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism), with which He has created mankind.
لَا تَبۡدِيلَ لِخَلۡقِ ٱللَّهِ‌ۚ
No change let there be in Khalqillah (i.e. the Religion of Allah (الله) Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism),
ذَٲلِكَ ٱلدِّينُ ٱلۡقَيِّمُ وَلَـٰكِنَّ أَڪۡثَرَ ٱلنَّاسِ لَا يَعۡلَمُونَ
that is the straight religion, but most of men know not.(Tafsir At Tabaree, Vol 21, Page 41)
30:31
مُنِيبِينَ إِلَيۡهِ وَٱتَّقُوهُ وَأَقِيمُواْ ٱلصَّلَوٰةَ وَلَا تَكُونُواْ مِنَ ٱلۡمُشۡرِڪِينَ
(Always) Turning in repentance to Him (only), and be afraid and dutiful to Him; and perform As Salat (Iqamatas Salat) and be not of AlMushrikoon (the disbelievers in the Oneness of Allah (الله), polytheists, idolaters, etc.).
30:32
مِنَ ٱلَّذِينَ فَرَّقُواْ دِينَهُمۡ وَڪَانُواْ شِيَعً۬ا‌ۖ
Of those who split up their religion (i.e. who left the true Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism), and became sects, (i.e. they invented new things in the religion (Bidah), and followed their vain desires),
كُلُّ حِزۡبِۭ بِمَا لَدَيۡہِمۡ فَرِحُونَ
each sect rejoicing in that which is with it.
30:33
وَإِذَا مَسَّ ٱلنَّاسَ ضُرٌّ۬ دَعَوۡاْ رَبَّہُم مُّنِيبِينَ إِلَيۡهِ
And when harm touches men, they cry sincerely only to their Lord (Allah (الله)), turning to Him in repentance,
ثُمَّ إِذَآ أَذَاقَهُم مِّنۡهُ رَحۡمَةً إِذَا فَرِيقٌ۬ مِّنۡہُم بِرَبِّهِمۡ يُشۡرِكُونَ
but when He gives them a taste of His Mercy, behold! a party of them associate partners in worship with their Lord.
30:34
لِيَكۡفُرُواْ بِمَآ ءَاتَيۡنَـٰهُمۡ‌ۚ
So as to be ungrateful for the graces which We have bestowed on them.
فَتَمَتَّعُواْ فَسَوۡفَ تَعۡلَمُونَ
Then enjoy (your short life); but you will come to know.
30:35
أَمۡ أَنزَلۡنَا عَلَيۡهِمۡ سُلۡطَـٰنً۬ا فَهُوَ يَتَكَلَّمُ بِمَا كَانُواْ بِهِۦ يُشۡرِكُونَ
Or have We revealed to them a Scripture, which speaks of that which they have been associating with Him?
30:36
وَإِذَآ أَذَقۡنَا ٱلنَّاسَ رَحۡمَةً۬ فَرِحُواْ بِہَا‌ۖ
And when We cause mankind to taste of mercy, they rejoice therein,
وَإِن تُصِبۡهُمۡ سَيِّئَةُۢ بِمَا قَدَّمَتۡ أَيۡدِيہِمۡ إِذَا هُمۡ يَقۡنَطُونَ
but when some evil afflicts them because of (evil deeds and sins) that their (own) hands have sent forth, lo! They are in despair!
30:37
أَوَلَمۡ يَرَوۡاْ أَنَّ ٱللَّهَ يَبۡسُطُ ٱلرِّزۡقَ لِمَن يَشَآءُ وَيَقۡدِرُ‌ۚ
Do they not see that Allah (الله) enlarges the provision for whom He wills and straitens (it for whom He wills).
إِنَّ فِى ذَٲلِكَ لَأَيَـٰتٍ۬ لِّقَوۡمٍ۬ يُؤۡمِنُونَ
Verily, in that are indeed signs for a people who believe.
30:38
فَـَٔاتِ ذَا ٱلۡقُرۡبَىٰ حَقَّهُ ۥ وَٱلۡمِسۡكِينَ وَٱبۡنَ ٱلسَّبِيلِ‌ۚ
So give to the kindred his due, and to AlMiskeen (the poor) and to the wayfarer.
ذَٲلِكَ خَيۡرٌ۬ لِّلَّذِينَ يُرِيدُونَ وَجۡهَ ٱللَّهِ‌ۖ وَأُوْلَـٰٓٮِٕكَ هُمُ ٱلۡمُفۡلِحُونَ
That is best for those who seek Allah (الله)‘s Countenance, and it is they who will be successful.
30:39
وَمَآ ءَاتَيۡتُم مِّن رِّبً۬ا لِّيَرۡبُوَاْ فِىٓ أَمۡوَٲلِ ٱلنَّاسِ فَلَا يَرۡبُواْ عِندَ ٱللَّهِ‌ۖ
And that which you give in gift (to others), in order that it may increase (your wealth by expecting to get a better one in return) from other peoples property, has no increase with Allah (الله),
وَمَآ ءَاتَيۡتُم مِّن زَكَوٰةٍ۬ تُرِيدُونَ وَجۡهَ ٱللَّهِ فَأُوْلَـٰٓٮِٕكَ هُمُ ٱلۡمُضۡعِفُونَ
but that which you give in Zakat seeking Allah (الله)‘s Countenance then those, they shall have manifold increase.
30:40
ٱللَّهُ ٱلَّذِى خَلَقَكُمۡ ثُمَّ رَزَقَكُمۡ ثُمَّ يُمِيتُڪُمۡ ثُمَّ يُحۡيِيكُمۡ‌ۖ
Allah (الله) is He Who created you, then provided food for you, then will cause you to die, then (again) He will give you life (on the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة)).
هَلۡ مِن شُرَكَآٮِٕكُم مَّن يَفۡعَلُ مِن ذَٲلِكُم مِّن شَىۡءٍ۬‌ۚ
Is there any of your (so-called) partners (of Allah (الله)) that do anything of that?
سُبۡحَـٰنَهُ ۥ وَتَعَـٰلَىٰ عَمَّا يُشۡرِكُونَ
Glory be to Him! And Exalted be He above all that (evil) they associate (with Him).
30:[41-45] Mischief in the land is the result of Man‘s own misdeeds. That is how Allah let them taste the fruit of their deeds
30:[46-53] Allah sent His Rasools for the guidance of people; some believed while others rejected, Allah subjected the guilty to His retribution and helped the Believers
30:41
ظَهَرَ ٱلۡفَسَادُ فِى ٱلۡبَرِّ وَٱلۡبَحۡرِ بِمَا كَسَبَتۡ أَيۡدِى ٱلنَّاسِ
Evil (sins and disobedience of Allah (الله), etc.) has appeared on land and sea because of what the hands of men have earned (by oppression and evil deeds, etc.),
لِيُذِيقَهُم بَعۡضَ ٱلَّذِى عَمِلُواْ لَعَلَّهُمۡ يَرۡجِعُونَ
that Allah (الله) may make them taste a part of that which they have done, in order that they may return (by repenting to Allah (الله), and begging His Pardon).
30:42
قُلۡ سِيرُواْ فِى ٱلۡأَرۡضِ فَٱنظُرُواْ كَيۡفَ كَانَ عَـٰقِبَةُ ٱلَّذِينَ مِن قَبۡلُ‌ۚ
Say (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW): ‘Travel in the land and see what was the end of those before (you)!
كَانَ أَڪۡثَرُهُم مُّشۡرِكِينَ
Most of them were Mushrikoon (the disbelievers in the Oneness of Allah (الله), polytheists, idolaters, etc.).‘
30:43
فَأَقِمۡ وَجۡهَكَ لِلدِّينِ ٱلۡقَيِّمِ مِن قَبۡلِ أَن يَأۡتِىَ يَوۡمٌ۬ لَّا مَرَدَّ لَهُ ۥ مِنَ ٱللَّهِ‌ۖ
So set you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) your face to (the obedience of Allah (الله), your Lord) the straight and right religion (Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism), before there comes from Allah (الله) a Day which none can avert it.
يَوۡمَٮِٕذٍ۬ يَصَّدَّعُونَ
On that Day men shall be divided ((in two groups), a group in Paradise (الجنة) and a group in Hell (جهنم)).
30:44
مَن كَفَرَ فَعَلَيۡهِ كُفۡرُهُ ۥ‌ۖ
Whosoever disbelieves will suffer from his disbelief,
وَمَنۡ عَمِلَ صَـٰلِحً۬ا فَلِأَنفُسِہِمۡ يَمۡهَدُونَ
and whosoever does righteous good deeds (by practicing Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism), then such will prepare a good place (in Paradise (الجنة)) for themselves (and will be saved by Allah (الله) from His Torment).
30:45
لِيَجۡزِىَ ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُواْ وَعَمِلُواْ ٱلصَّـٰلِحَـٰتِ مِن فَضۡلِهِۦۤ‌ۚ
That He may reward those who believe (in the Oneness of Allah (الله) Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism), and do righteous good deeds, out of His Bounty.
إِنَّهُ ۥ لَا يُحِبُّ ٱلۡكَـٰفِرِينَ
Verily, He likes not the disbelievers.
30:46
وَمِنۡ ءَايَـٰتِهِۦۤ أَن يُرۡسِلَ ٱلرِّيَاحَ مُبَشِّرَٲتٍ۬
And among His Signs is this, that He sends the winds as glad tidings,
وَلِيُذِيقَكُم مِّن رَّحۡمَتِهِۦ وَلِتَجۡرِىَ ٱلۡفُلۡكُ بِأَمۡرِهِۦ
giving you a taste of His Mercy (i.e. rain), and that the ships may sail at His Command,
وَلِتَبۡتَغُواْ مِن فَضۡلِهِۦ وَلَعَلَّكُمۡ تَشۡكُرُونَ
and that you may seek of His Bounty, in order that you may be thankful.
30:47
وَلَقَدۡ أَرۡسَلۡنَا مِن قَبۡلِكَ رُسُلاً إِلَىٰ قَوۡمِهِمۡ فَجَآءُوهُم بِٱلۡبَيِّنَـٰتِ
And indeed We did send Messengers before you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) to their own peoples.They came to them with clear proofs,
فَٱنتَقَمۡنَا مِنَ ٱلَّذِينَ أَجۡرَمُواْ‌ۖ
then, We took vengeance on those who committed crimes (disbelief, setting partners in worship with Allah (الله), sins, etc.),
وَكَانَ حَقًّا عَلَيۡنَا نَصۡرُ ٱلۡمُؤۡمِنِينَ
and (as for) the believers it was incumbent upon Us to help (them).
30:48
ٱللَّهُ ٱلَّذِى يُرۡسِلُ ٱلرِّيَـٰحَ فَتُثِيرُ سَحَابً۬ا
Allah (الله) is He Who sends the winds, so they raise clouds,
فَيَبۡسُطُهُ ۥ فِى ٱلسَّمَآءِ كَيۡفَ يَشَآءُ وَيَجۡعَلُهُ ۥ كِسَفً۬ا
and spread them along the sky as He wills, and then break them into fragments,
فَتَرَى ٱلۡوَدۡقَ يَخۡرُجُ مِنۡ خِلَـٰلِهِۦ‌ۖ
until you see rain drops come forth from their midst!
فَإِذَآ أَصَابَ بِهِۦ مَن يَشَآءُ مِنۡ عِبَادِهِۦۤ إِذَا هُمۡ يَسۡتَبۡشِرُونَ
Then when He has made them fall on whom of His slaves as He will, lo! they rejoice!
30:49
وَإِن كَانُواْ مِن قَبۡلِ أَن يُنَزَّلَ عَلَيۡهِم مِّن قَبۡلِهِۦ لَمُبۡلِسِينَ
And verily before that (rain), just before it was sent down upon them, they were in despair!
30:50
فَٱنظُرۡ إِلَىٰٓ ءَاثَـٰرِ رَحۡمَتِ ٱللَّهِ ڪَيۡفَ يُحۡىِ ٱلۡأَرۡضَ بَعۡدَ مَوۡتِہَآ‌ۚ
Look then at the effects (results) of Allah (الله)‘s Mercy, how He revives the earth after its death.
إِنَّ ذَٲلِكَ لَمُحۡىِ ٱلۡمَوۡتَىٰ‌ۖ وَهُوَ عَلَىٰ كُلِّ شَىۡءٍ۬ قَدِيرٌ۬
Verily! That (Allah (الله)) Who revived the earth after its death shall indeed raise the dead (on the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة)), and He is Able to do all things.
30:51
وَلَٮِٕنۡ أَرۡسَلۡنَا رِيحً۬ا فَرَأَوۡهُ مُصۡفَرًّ۬ا لَّظَلُّواْ مِنۢ بَعۡدِهِۦ يَكۡفُرُونَ
And if We send a wind (which would spoil the green growth (tilth) brought up by the previous rain), and they see (their tilth) turn yellow, behold, they then after their being glad, would become unthankful (to their Lord Allah (الله) as) disbelievers.
30:52
فَإِنَّكَ لَا تُسۡمِعُ ٱلۡمَوۡتَىٰ وَلَا تُسۡمِعُ ٱلصُّمَّ ٱلدُّعَآءَ إِذَا وَلَّوۡاْ مُدۡبِرِينَ
So verily, you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) cannot make the dead to hear (i.e. the disbelievers, etc.), nor can you make the deaf to hear the call, when they show their backs, turning away.
30:53
وَمَآ أَنتَ بِهَـٰدِ ٱلۡعُمۡىِ عَن ضَلَـٰلَتِهِمۡ‌ۖ
And you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) cannot guide the blind from their straying;
إِن تُسۡمِعُ إِلَّا مَن يُؤۡمِنُ بِـَٔايَـٰتِنَا فَهُم مُّسۡلِمُونَ
you can make to hear only those who believe in Our Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, etc.), and have submitted to Allah (الله) in Islam (الإسلام) (as Muslims).
30:[54-60] O Prophet, you cannot make the dead to hear you. It is Allah Who has created you and shall bring you to justice on the Day of Judgement
30:54
ٱللَّهُ ٱلَّذِى خَلَقَكُم مِّن ضَعۡفٍ۬ ثُمَّ جَعَلَ مِنۢ بَعۡدِ ضَعۡفٍ۬ قُوَّةً۬
Allah (الله) is He Who created you in (a state of) weakness, then gave you strength after weakness,
ثُمَّ جَعَلَ مِنۢ بَعۡدِ قُوَّةٍ۬ ضَعۡفً۬ا وَشَيۡبَةً۬‌ۚ
then after strength gave (you) weakness and grey hair.
يَخۡلُقُ مَا يَشَآءُ‌ۖ وَهُوَ ٱلۡعَلِيمُ ٱلۡقَدِيرُ
He creates what He wills. And it is He Who is the All-Knowing (Al-‘Alee (ٱلْعَلِيُّ)m (ٱلْعَلِيمُ)), the All-Powerful (i.e. Able to do all things).
30:55
وَيَوۡمَ تَقُومُ ٱلسَّاعَةُ يُقۡسِمُ ٱلۡمُجۡرِمُونَ مَا لَبِثُواْ غَيۡرَ سَاعَةٍ۬‌ۚ
And on the Day that the Hour will be established, the Mujrimoon (criminals, disbelievers, polytheists, sinners, etc.) will swear that they stayed not but an hour,
كَذَٲلِكَ كَانُواْ يُؤۡفَكُونَ
thus were they ever deluded (away from the truth).(i.e they used to tell lies and take false oaths, and turn away from the truth) in this life of the world))
30:56
وَقَالَ ٱلَّذِينَ أُوتُواْ ٱلۡعِلۡمَ وَٱلۡإِيمَـٰنَ لَقَدۡ لَبِثۡتُمۡ فِى كِتَـٰبِ ٱللَّهِ إِلَىٰ يَوۡمِ ٱلۡبَعۡثِ‌ۖ
And those who have been bestowed with knowledge and faith will say:
Indeed you have stayed according to the Decree of Allah (الله), until the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة),
فَهَـٰذَا يَوۡمُ ٱلۡبَعۡثِ وَلَـٰكِنَّڪُمۡ كُنتُمۡ لَا تَعۡلَمُونَ
so this is the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة), but you knew not.‘
30:57
فَيَوۡمَٮِٕذٍ۬ لَّا يَنفَعُ ٱلَّذِينَ ظَلَمُواْ مَعۡذِرَتُهُمۡ وَلَا هُمۡ يُسۡتَعۡتَبُونَ
So on that Day no excuse of theirs will avail those who did wrong (by associating partners in worship with Allah (الله), and by denying the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة)), nor will they be allowed (then) to return to seek Allah (الله)‘s Pleasure (by having Islam (الإسلام)ic Faith with righteous deeds and by giving up polytheism, sins and crimes with repentance).
30:58
وَلَقَدۡ ضَرَبۡنَا لِلنَّاسِ فِى هَـٰذَا ٱلۡقُرۡءَانِ مِن كُلِّ مَثَلٍ۬‌ۚ
And indeed We have set forth for mankind, in this Quran (القرآن) every kind of parable.
وَلَٮِٕن جِئۡتَهُم بِـَٔايَةٍ۬ لَّيَقُولَنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ ڪَفَرُوٓاْ إِنۡ أَنتُمۡ إِلَّا مُبۡطِلُونَ
But if you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) bring to them any sign or proof, (as an evidence for the truth of your Prophethood), the disbelievers are sure to say (to the believers): ‘You follow nothing but falsehood, and magic.‘
30:59
كَذَٲلِكَ يَطۡبَعُ ٱللَّهُ عَلَىٰ قُلُوبِ ٱلَّذِينَ لَا يَعۡلَمُونَ
Thus does Allah (الله) seal up the hearts of those who know not (the proofs and evidence of the Oneness of Allah (الله) i.e. those who try not to understand true facts that which you (Muhammad (محمد) SAW) have brought to them).
30:60
فَٱصۡبِرۡ إِنَّ وَعۡدَ ٱللَّهِ حَقٌّ۬‌ۖ
So be patient (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW). Verily, the Promise of Allah (الله) is true,
وَلَا يَسۡتَخِفَّنَّكَ ٱلَّذِينَ لَا يُوقِنُونَ
and let not those who have no certainty of faith, discourage you from conveying Allah (الله)‘s Message (which you are obliged to convey).*********
