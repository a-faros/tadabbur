Al-Anbiyaa
The Prophets
21:[1-10] The Day of Accountability is getting closer, but the disbelievers are still heedless to the admonition and dispute as to how a human being can be a Rasool
21:1
اقْتَرَبَ لِلنَّاسِ حِسَابُهُمْ وَهُمْ فِي غَفْلَةٍ مُعْرِضُونَ
Draws near for mankind their reckoning, while they turn away in heedlessness.
21:2
‏مَا يَأْتِيهِمْ مِنْ ذِكْرٍ مِنْ رَبِّهِمْ مُحْدَثٍ إِلَّا اسْتَمَعُوهُ وَهُمْ يَلْعَبُونَ
Comes not unto them an admonition (a chapter of the Quran (القرآن)) from their Lord as a recent revelation but they listen to it while they play,
21:3
لَاهِيَةً قُلُوبُهُمْ ۗ
With their hearts occupied (with evil things) those who do wrong,
وَأَسَرُّوا النَّجْوَى الَّذِينَ ظَلَمُوا هَلْ هَذَا إِلَّا بَشَرٌ مِثْلُكُمْ ۖ
conceal their private counsels, (saying): ‘Is this (Muhammad (محمد) SAW) more than a human being like you?
أَفَتَأْتُونَ السِّحْرَ وَأَنْتُمْ تُبْصِرُونَ
Will you submit to magic while you see it?‘
21:4
قَالَ رَبِّي يَعْلَمُ الْقَوْلَ فِي السَّمَاءِ وَالْأَرْضِ ۖ
He (Muhammad (محمد) SAW) said: ‘My Lord knows (every) word (spoken) in the heavens and on earth.
وَهُوَ السَّمِيعُ الْعَلِيمُ
And He is the All-Hearer, the All-Knower (Al-‘Alee (ٱلْعَلِيُّ)m (ٱلْعَلِيمُ)).‘
21:5
بَلْ قَالُوا أَضْغَاثُ أَحْلَامٍ
Nay, they say: ‘These (revelations of the Quran (القرآن) which are inspired to Muhammad (محمد) SAW) are mixed up false dreams!
بَلِ افْتَرَاهُ
Nay, he has invented it!
بَلْ هُوَ شَاعِرٌ
Nay, he is a poet!
فَلْيَأْتِنَا بِآيَةٍ كَمَا أُرْسِلَ الْأَوَّلُونَ
Let him then bring us an Ayah (sign as a proof) like the ones (Prophets) that were sent before (with signs)!‘
21:6
‏مَا آمَنَتْ قَبْلَهُمْ مِنْ قَرْيَةٍ أَهْلَكْنَاهَا ۖ
Not one of the towns (populations), of those which We destroyed, believed before them (though We sent them signs),
أَفَهُمْ يُؤْمِنُونَ
will they then believe?
21:7
وَمَا أَرْسَلْنَا قَبْلَكَ إِلَّا رِجَالًا نُوحِي إِلَيْهِمْ ۖ
And We sent not before you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) but men to whom We inspired,
فَاسْأَلُوا أَهْلَ الذِّكْرِ إِنْ كُنْتُمْ لَا تَعْلَمُونَ
so ask the people of the Reminder (Scriptures - the Taurat (Torah (التوراة)), the Injil (Injeel (الإنجيل))) if you do not know.
21:8
وَمَا جَعَلْنَاهُمْ جَسَدًا لَا يَأْكُلُونَ الطَّعَامَ وَمَا كَانُوا خَالِدِينَ
And We did not create them (the Messengers, with) bodies that ate not food, nor were they immortals,
21:9
ثُمَّ صَدَقْنَاهُمُ الْوَعْدَ
Then We fulfilled to them the promise,
فَأَنْجَيْنَاهُمْ وَمَنْ نَشَاءُ وَأَهْلَكْنَا الْمُسْرِفِينَ
and We saved them and those whom We willed, but We destroyed Al-Musrifoon (i.e. extravagants in oppression, polytheism and in sin).
21:10
لَقَدْ أَنْزَلْنَا إِلَيْكُمْ كِتَابًا فِيهِ ذِكْرُكُمْ ۖ
Indeed, We have sent down for you (O mankind) a Book, (the Quran (القرآن)) in which there is Dhikrukum, (your Reminder or an honour for you i.e. honour for the one who follows the teaching of the Quran (القرآن) and acts on its orders).
أَفَلَا تَعْقِلُونَ
Will you not then understand?
21:[11-15] Prior nations were destroyed due to similar iniquities
21:[16-18] The creation of the heavens and earth is not a game
21:[19-24] If there were more than One God, the heavens and earth would have been in a state of disorder
Message: All Rasools were sent with the same
21:[25-29] ‘There is no god but Allah, so worship Him Alone.‘
21:11
وَكَمْ قَصَمْنَا مِنْ قَرْيَةٍ كَانَتْ ظَالِمَةً وَأَنْشَأْنَا بَعْدَهَا قَوْمًا آخَرِينَ
How many a town (community), that were wrong-doers, have We destroyed, and raised up after them another people!
21:12
فَلَمَّا أَحَسُّوا بَأْسَنَا إِذَا هُمْ مِنْهَا يَرْكُضُونَ
Then, when they perceived (saw) Our Torment (coming), behold, they (tried to) flee from it.
21:13
لَا تَرْكُضُوا
Flee not,
وَارْجِعُوا إِلَى مَا أُتْرِفْتُمْ فِيهِ وَمَسَاكِنِكُمْ لَعَلَّكُمْ تُسْأَلُونَ
but return to that wherein you lived a luxurious life, and to your homes, in order that you may be questioned.
21:14
قَالُوا يَا وَيْلَنَا إِنَّا كُنَّا ظَالِمِينَ
They cried: ‘Woe to us! Certainly! We have been Zalimoon (polytheists, wrong-doers and disbelievers in the Oneness of Allah (الله), etc.).‘
21:15
فَمَا زَالَتْ تِلْكَ دَعْوَاهُمْ حَتَّى جَعَلْنَاهُمْ حَصِيدًا خَامِدِينَ
And that cry of theirs ceased not, till We made them as a field that is reaped, extinct (dead).
21:16
وَمَا خَلَقْنَا السَّمَاءَ وَالْأَرْضَ وَمَا بَيْنَهُمَا لَاعِبِينَ
We created not the heavens and the earth and all that is between them for a (mere) play .
21:17
‏لَوْ أَرَدْنَا أَنْ نَتَّخِذَ لَهْوًا لَاتَّخَذْنَاهُ مِنْ لَدُنَّا إِنْ كُنَّا فَاعِلِينَ
HadWe intended to take a pastime (i.e. a wife or a son, etc.), We could surely have taken it from Us, if We were going to do (that).
21:18
بَلْ نَقْذِفُ بِالْحَقِّ عَلَى الْبَاطِلِ فَيَدْمَغُهُ فَإِذَا هُوَ زَاهِقٌ ۚ
Nay, We fling (send down) the truth (this Quran (القرآن)) against the falsehood (disbelief), so it destroys it, and behold, it (falsehood) is vanished.
وَلَكُمُ الْوَيْلُ مِمَّا تَصِفُونَ
And woe to you for that (lie) which you ascribe (to Us) (against Allah (الله) by uttering that Allah (الله) has a wife and a son).
21:19
وَلَهُ مَنْ فِي السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضِ ۚ
To Him belongs whosoever is in the heavens and on earth.
وَمَنْ عِنْدَهُ لَا يَسْتَكْبِرُونَ عَنْ عِبَادَتِهِ وَلَا يَسْتَحْسِرُونَ
And those who are near Him (i.e. the angels) are not too proud to worship Him, nor are they weary (of His worship).
21:20
يُسَبِّحُونَ اللَّيْلَ وَالنَّهَارَ لَا يَفْتُرُونَ
They (i.e. the angels) glorify His Praises night and day, (and) they never slacken (to do so).
21:21
أَمِ اتَّخَذُوا آلِهَةً مِنَ الْأَرْضِ هُمْ يُنْشِرُونَ
Or have they taken (for worship) aliha (gods) from the earth who raise the dead?
21:22
لَوْ كَانَ فِيهِمَا آلِهَةٌ إِلَّا اللَّهُ لَفَسَدَتَا ۚ
Had there been therein (in the heavens and the earth) gods besides Allah (الله), then verily both would have been ruined.
فَسُبْحَانَ اللَّهِ رَبِّ الْعَرْشِ عَمَّا يَصِفُونَ
Glorified be Allah (الله), the Lord of the Throne, (High is He) above what they attribute to Him!
21:23
لَا يُسْأَلُ عَمَّا يَفْعَلُ وَهُمْ يُسْأَلُونَ
He cannot be questioned as to what He does, while they will be questioned.
21:24
أَمِ اتَّخَذُوا مِنْ دُونِهِ آلِهَةً ۖ
Or have they taken for worship (other) aliha (gods) besides Him?
قُلْ هَاتُوا بُرْهَانَكُمْ ۖ
Say: ‘Bring your proof:‘
هَذَا ذِكْرُ مَنْ مَعِيَ وَذِكْرُ مَنْ قَبْلِي ۗ
This (the Quran (القرآن)) is the Reminder for those with me and the Reminder for those before me.
بَلْ أَكْثَرُهُمْ لَا يَعْلَمُونَ الْحَقَّ ۖ فَهُمْ مُعْرِضُونَ
But most of them know not the Truth, so they are averse.
21:25
وَمَا أَرْسَلْنَا مِنْ قَبْلِكَ مِنْ رَسُولٍ إِلَّا نُوحِي إِلَيْهِ
And We did not send any Messenger before you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) but We inspired him (saying):
أَنَّهُ لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا أَنَا فَاعْبُدُونِ
La ilaha illa Ana (none has the right to be worshipped but I (Allah (الله))), so worship Me (Alone and none else).‘
21:26
وَقَالُوا اتَّخَذَ الرَّحْمَنُ وَلَدًا ۗ
And they say: ‘The Most Beneficent (Ar-Rahmaan (ٱلْرَّحْمَـانُ)) has begotten a son (or children).‘
سُبْحَانَهُ ۚ
Glory to Him!
بَلْ عِبَادٌ مُكْرَمُونَ
They (those whom they call children of Allah (الله) i.e. the angels, Isa (Iesa (عيسى)) son of Mary (Maryam (مريم)), Uzair (عزير) (Ezra), etc.), are but honoured slaves.
21:27
لَا يَسْبِقُونَهُ بِالْقَوْلِ وَهُمْ بِأَمْرِهِ يَعْمَلُونَ
They speak not until He has spoken, and they act on His Command.
21:28
يَعْلَمُ مَا بَيْنَ أَيْدِيهِمْ وَمَا خَلْفَهُمْ
He knows what is before them, and what is behind them,
وَلَا يَشْفَعُونَ إِلَّا لِمَنِ ارْتَضَى
and they cannot intercede except for him with whom He is pleased.
وَهُمْ مِنْ خَشْيَتِهِ مُشْفِقُونَ
And they stand in awe for fear of Him.
21:29
وَمَنْ يَقُلْ مِنْهُمْ إِنِّي إِلَهٌ مِنْ دُونِهِ فَذَلِكَ نَجْزِيهِ جَهَنَّمَ ۚ
And if any of them should say: ‘Verily, I am an ilah (a god) besides Him (Allah (الله)),‘ such a one We should recompense with Hell (جهنم).
كَذَلِكَ نَجْزِي الظَّالِمِينَ
Thus We recompense the Zalimoon (polytheists and wrong-doers, etc.).
21:[30-33] The skies and earth once were one mass; Allah split them asunder, and He created all living things from water
21:[34-41] Allah has not granted immortality to any human being. If Rasools are doomed to die, how are disbelievers going to live forever?
21:30
أَوَلَمْ يَرَ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا أَنَّ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضَ كَانَتَا رَتْقًا فَفَتَقْنَاهُمَا ۖ
Have not those who disbelieve known that the heavens and the earth were joined together as one united piece, then We parted them?
وَجَعَلْنَا مِنَ الْمَاءِ كُلَّ شَيْءٍ حَيٍّ ۖ
And We have made from water every living thing.
أَفَلَا يُؤْمِنُونَ
Will they not then believe?
21:31
وَجَعَلْنَا فِي الْأَرْضِ رَوَاسِيَ أَنْ تَمِيدَ بِهِمْ
And We have placed on the earth firm mountains, lest it should shake with them,
وَجَعَلْنَا فِيهَا فِجَاجًا سُبُلًا لَعَلَّهُمْ يَهْتَدُونَ
and We placed therein broad highways for them to pass through, that they may be guided.
21:32
وَجَعَلْنَا السَّمَاءَ سَقْفًا مَحْفُوظًا ۖ
And We have made the heaven a roof, safe and well guarded.
وَهُمْ عَنْ آيَاتِهَا مُعْرِضُونَ
Yet they turn away from its signs (i.e. sun, moon, winds, clouds, etc.).
21:33
وَهُوَ الَّذِي خَلَقَ اللَّيْلَ وَالنَّهَارَ وَالشَّمْسَ وَالْقَمَرَ ۖ
And He it is Who has created the night and the day, and the sun and the moon,
كُلٌّ فِي فَلَكٍ يَسْبَحُونَ
each in an orbit floating.
21:34
وَمَا جَعَلْنَا لِبَشَرٍ مِنْ قَبْلِكَ الْخُلْدَ ۖ
And We granted not to any human being immortality before you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW),
أَفَإِنْ مِتَّ فَهُمُ الْخَالِدُونَ
then if you die, would they live forever?
21:35
كُلُّ نَفْسٍ ذَائِقَةُ الْمَوْتِ ۗ
Everyone is going to taste death,
وَنَبْلُوكُمْ بِالشَّرِّ وَالْخَيْرِ فِتْنَةً ۖ
and We shall make a trial of you with evil and with good,
وَإِلَيْنَا تُرْجَعُونَ
and to Us you will be returned.
21:36
وَإِذَا رَآكَ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا إِنْ يَتَّخِذُونَكَ إِلَّا هُزُوًا
And when those who disbelieve (in the Oneness of Allah (الله)) see you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW), they take you not except for mockery (saying):
أَهَذَا الَّذِي يَذْكُرُ آلِهَتَكُمْ
Is this the one who talks (badly) about your gods?‘
وَهُمْ بِذِكْرِ الرَّحْمَنِ هُمْ كَافِرُونَ
While they disbelieve at the mention of the Most Beneficent (Ar-Rahmaan (ٱلْرَّحْمَـانُ)).(Tafsir. Al-Qurtubee).
21:37
خُلِقَ الْإِنْسَانُ مِنْ عَجَلٍ ۚ
Man is created of haste,
سَأُرِيكُمْ آيَاتِي فَلَا تَسْتَعْجِلُونِ
I will show you My Ayat (torments, proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, etc.). So ask Me not to hasten (them).
21:38
وَيَقُولُونَ مَتَى هَذَا الْوَعْدُ إِنْ كُنْتُمْ صَادِقِينَ
And they say: ‘When will this promise (come to pass), if you are truthful.‘
21:39
لَوْ يَعْلَمُ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا حِينَ لَا يَكُفُّونَ عَنْ وُجُوهِهِمُ النَّارَ وَلَا عَنْ ظُهُورِهِمْ وَلَا هُمْ يُنْصَرُونَ
If only those who disbelieved knew (the time) when they will not be able to ward off the Fire from their faces, nor from their backs; and they will not be helped.
21:40
بَلْ تَأْتِيهِمْ بَغْتَةً فَتَبْهَتُهُمْ
Nay, it (the Fire or the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة)) will come upon them all of a sudden and will perplex them,
فَلَا يَسْتَطِيعُونَ رَدَّهَا وَلَا هُمْ يُنْظَرُونَ
and they will have no power to avert it, nor will they get respite.
21:41
وَلَقَدِ اسْتُهْزِئَ بِرُسُلٍ مِنْ قَبْلِكَ
Indeed (many) Messengers were mocked before you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW),
فَحَاقَ بِالَّذِينَ سَخِرُوا مِنْهُمْ مَا كَانُوا بِهِ يَسْتَهْزِئُونَ
but the scoffers were surrounded by that, whereat they used to mock.
21:[42-47] Invented gods cannot even defend themselves; how will they defend the unbelievers against Allah? The scale of justice shall be set up on the Day of Judgment
21:[48-50] Musa was given Al-Furqãn (the criterion of right and wrong), so is this The Qur‘an
21:42
قُلْ مَنْ يَكْلَؤُكُمْ بِاللَّيْلِ وَالنَّهَارِ مِنَ الرَّحْمَنِ ۗ
Say: ‘Who can guard and protect you in the night or in the day from the (punishment of the) Most Beneficent (Ar-Rahmaan (ٱلْرَّحْمَـانُ))?‘
بَلْ هُمْ عَنْ ذِكْرِ رَبِّهِمْ مُعْرِضُونَ
Nay, but they turn away from the remembrance of their Lord.
21:43
أَمْ لَهُمْ آلِهَةٌ تَمْنَعُهُمْ مِنْ دُونِنَا ۚ
Or have they aliha (gods) who can guard them from Us?
لَا يَسْتَطِيعُونَ نَصْرَ أَنْفُسِهِمْ وَلَا هُمْ مِنَّا يُصْحَبُونَ
They have no power to help themselves, nor can they be protected from Us (i.e. from Our Torment).
21:44
بَلْ مَتَّعْنَا هَؤُلَاءِ وَآبَاءَهُمْ حَتَّى طَالَ عَلَيْهِمُ الْعُمُرُ ۗ
Nay, We gave the luxuries of this life to these men and their fathers until the period grew long for them.
أَفَلَا يَرَوْنَ أَنَّا نَأْتِي الْأَرْضَ نَنْقُصُهَا مِنْ أَطْرَافِهَا ۚ
See they not that We gradually reduce the land (in their control) from its outlying borders?
أَفَهُمُ الْغَالِبُونَ
Is it then they who will overcome.
21:45
قُلْ إِنَّمَا أُنْذِرُكُمْ بِالْوَحْيِ ۚ
Say (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW): ‘I warn you only by the revelation (from Allah (الله) and not by the opinion of the religious scholars and others).
وَلَا يَسْمَعُ الصُّمُّ الدُّعَاءَ إِذَا مَا يُنْذَرُونَ
But the deaf (who follow the religious scholars and others blindly) will not hear the call, (even) when they are warned.((i.e. one should follow only the Quran (القرآن) and the Sun (الشمس)nah (legal ways, orders, acts of worship, statements of Prophet Muhammad (محمد) SAW , as the Companions of the Prophet SAW did))
21:46
وَلَئِنْ مَسَّتْهُمْ نَفْحَةٌ مِنْ عَذَابِ رَبِّكَ لَيَقُولُنَّ
And if a breath (minor calamity) of the Torment of your Lord touches them, they will surely cry:
يَا وَيْلَنَا إِنَّا كُنَّا ظَالِمِينَ
Woe unto us! Indeed we have been Zalimoon (polytheists and wrong-doers, etc.).
21:47
وَنَضَعُ الْمَوَازِينَ الْقِسْطَ لِيَوْمِ الْقِيَامَةِ فَلَا تُظْلَمُ نَفْسٌ شَيْئًا ۖ
And We shall set up balances of justice on the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة), then none will be dealt with unjustly in anything.
وَإِنْ كَانَ مِثْقَالَ حَبَّةٍ مِنْ خَرْدَلٍ أَتَيْنَا بِهَا ۗ
And if there be the weight of a mustard seed, We will bring it.
وَكَفَى بِنَا حَاسِبِينَ
And Sufficient are We as Reckoners.
21:48
وَلَقَدْ آتَيْنَا مُوسَى وَهَارُونَ الْفُرْقَانَ وَضِيَاءً وَذِكْرًا لِلْمُتَّقِينَ
And indeed We granted to Musa (Moosa (موسى)) and Haroon (Aaron) the criterion (of right and wrong), and a shining light (i.e. the Taurat (Torah (التوراة))) and a Reminder for Al-Muttaqoon (ْالمُتَّقِينَ) (the pious - see V.2:2).
21:49
الَّذِينَ يَخْشَوْنَ رَبَّهُمْ بِالْغَيْبِ وَهُمْ مِنَ السَّاعَةِ مُشْفِقُونَ
Those who fear their Lord without seeing Him, while they are afraid of the Hour.
21:50
وَهَذَا ذِكْرٌ مُبَارَكٌ أَنْزَلْنَاهُ ۚ
And this is a blessed Reminder (the Quran (القرآن)) which We have sent down,
أَفَأَنْتُمْ لَهُ مُنْكِرُونَ
will you then (dare to) deny it?

Ibrãhïm broke all their idols to show that the gods who cannot even defend themselves, cannot be of any benefit to them. They decided to burn him alive but Allah commanded the fire to be cool and comfortable for Ibrãhïm
21:[72-75] Allah blessed Ibrãhïm with a son (Ishãq) and then a grandson (Ya‘qoob) and made each of them prophets
21:51
وَلَقَدْ آتَيْنَا إِبْرَاهِيمَ رُشْدَهُ مِنْ قَبْلُ وَكُنَّا بِهِ عَالِمِينَ
And indeed We bestowed aforetime on Abraham (Ibrahim (إبراهيم)) his (portion of) guidance, and We were Well-Acquainted with him (as to his Belief in the Oneness of Allah (الله), etc.).
21:52
إِذْ قَالَ لِأَبِيهِ وَقَوْمِهِ
When he said to his father and his people:
مَا هَذِهِ التَّمَاثِيلُ الَّتِي أَنْتُمْ لَهَا عَاكِفُونَ
What are these images, to which you are devoted?‘
21:53
قَالُوا وَجَدْنَا آبَاءَنَا لَهَا عَابِدِينَ
They said: ‘We found our fathers worshipping them.‘
21:54
قَالَ لَقَدْ كُنْتُمْ أَنْتُمْ وَآبَاؤُكُمْ فِي ضَلَالٍ مُبِينٍ
He said: ‘Indeed you and your fathers have been in manifest error.‘
21:55
قَالُوا أَجِئْتَنَا بِالْحَقِّ أَمْ أَنْتَ مِنَ اللَّاعِبِينَ
They said: ‘Have you brought us the truth, or are you one of those who play about?‘
21:56
قَالَ بَلْ رَبُّكُمْ رَبُّ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضِ الَّذِي فَطَرَهُنَّ
He said: ‘Nay, your Lord is the Lord of the heavens and the earth, Who created them
وَأَنَا عَلَى ذَلِكُمْ مِنَ الشَّاهِدِينَ
and of that I am one of the witnesses.
21:57
وَتَاللَّهِ لَأَكِيدَنَّ أَصْنَامَكُمْ بَعْدَ أَنْ تُوَلُّوا مُدْبِرِينَ
And by Allah (الله), I shall plot a plan (to destroy) your idols after you have gone away and turned your backs.‘
21:58
فَجَعَلَهُمْ جُذَاذًا إِلَّا كَبِيرًا لَهُمْ لَعَلَّهُمْ إِلَيْهِ يَرْجِعُونَ
So he broke them to pieces, (all) except the biggest of them, that they might turn to it.
21:59
قَالُوا مَنْ فَعَلَ هَذَا بِآلِهَتِنَا
They said: ‘Who has done this to our aliha (gods)?
إِنَّهُ لَمِنَ الظَّالِمِينَ
He must indeed be one of the wrong-doers.‘
21:60
قَالُوا سَمِعْنَا فَتًى يَذْكُرُهُمْ يُقَالُ لَهُ إِبْرَاهِيمُ
They said: ‘We heard a young man talking (against) them who is called Abraham (Ibrahim (إبراهيم)).‘
21:61
قَالُوا فَأْتُوا بِهِ عَلَى أَعْيُنِ النَّاسِ لَعَلَّهُمْ يَشْهَدُونَ
They said: ‘Then bring him before the eyes of the people, that they may testify.‘
21:62
قَالُوا أَأَنْتَ فَعَلْتَ هَذَا بِآلِهَتِنَا يَا إِبْرَاهِيمُ
They said: ‘Are you the one who has done this to our gods, O Abraham (Ibrahim (إبراهيم))?‘
21:63

(Abraham (Ibrahim (إبراهيم))) said: ‘Nay, this one, the biggest of them (idols) did it.
فَاسْأَلُوهُمْ إِنْ كَانُوا يَنْطِقُونَ
Ask them, if they can speak!‘
21:64
فَرَجَعُوا إِلَى أَنْفُسِهِمْ فَقَالُوا إِنَّكُمْ أَنْتُمُ الظَّالِمُونَ
So they turned to themselves and said: ‘Verily, you are the Zalimoon (polytheists and wrongdoers).‘
21:65
ثُمَّ نُكِسُوا عَلَى رُءُوسِهِمْ لَقَدْ عَلِمْتَ مَا هَؤُلَاءِ يَنْطِقُونَ
Then they turned to themselves (their first thought and said): ‘Indeed you (Abraham (Ibrahim (إبراهيم))) know well that these (idols) speak not!‘
21:66
قَالَ أَفَتَعْبُدُونَ مِنْ دُونِ اللَّهِ مَا لَا يَنْفَعُكُمْ شَيْئًا وَلَا يَضُرُّكُمْ
(Abraham (Ibrahim (إبراهيم))) said: ‘Do you then worship besides Allah (الله), things that can neither profit you, nor harm you?
21:67
أُفٍّ لَكُمْ وَلِمَا تَعْبُدُونَ مِنْ دُونِ اللَّهِ

أَفَلَا تَعْقِلُونَ
Have you then no sense?‘
21:68
قَالُوا حَرِّقُوهُ وَانْصُرُوا آلِهَتَكُمْ إِنْ كُنْتُمْ فَاعِلِينَ
They said: ‘Burn him and help your aliha (gods), if you will be doing.‘
21:69

We (Allah (الله)) said: ‘O fire! Be you coolness and safety for Abraham (Ibrahim (إبراهيم))!‘
21:70
وَأَرَادُوا بِهِ كَيْدًا فَجَعَلْنَاهُمُ الْأَخْسَرِينَ
And they wanted to harm him, but We made them the worst losers.
21:71

And We bestowed upon him Ishaque (Ishak (إسحاق)), and (a grandson) Yaqoob (Yaqqub (يعقوب)).
وَكُلًّا جَعَلْنَا صَالِحِينَ
Each one We made righteous.
21:73

And We made them leaders, guiding (mankind) by Our Command,
وَأَوْحَيْنَا إِلَيْهِمْ فِعْلَ الْخَيْرَاتِ وَإِقَامَ الصَّلَاةِ وَإِيتَاءَ الزَّكَاةِ ۖ
and We inspired in them the doing of good deeds, performing Salat (Iqamat-as-Salat), and the giving of Zakat
وَكَانُوا لَنَا عَابِدِينَ

and We saved him from the town (folk) who practised Al- Khabaith (evil, wicked and filthy deeds, etc.).

We listened to his invocation and saved him and his family from great distress.

We helped him against people who denied Our Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, etc.).

and to each of them We gave Hukman (right judgement of the affairs and Prophethood) and knowledge.

And We subjected the mountains and the birds to glorify Our Praises along with Dawood (داود) (David),

And it was We Who were the doers (of all these things).
21:80

And We taught him the making of metal coats of mail (for battles), to protect you in your fighting.
فَهَلْ أَنْتُمْ شَاكِرُونَ
Are you then grateful?
21:81

And to Suleiman (سليمان) (Solomon) (We subjected) the wind strongly raging, running by his command towards the land which We had blessed.

And of the Shayatin (devils) (from the jinns) were some who dived for him, and did other work besides that; and it was We Who guarded them.
21:83

And (remember) Ayoob (Job), when he cried to his Lord:
أَنِّي مَسَّنِيَ الضُّرُّ وَأَنْتَ أَرْحَمُ الرَّاحِمِينَ
Verily, distress has seized me, and You are the Most Merciful (Ar-Raheem (ٱلْرَّحِيْمُ)) of all those who show mercy.‘
21:84
فَاسْتَجَبْنَا لَهُ فَكَشَفْنَا مَا بِهِ مِنْ ضُرٍّ ۖ
So We answered his call, and We removed the distress that was on him,
وَآتَيْنَاهُ أَهْلَهُ وَمِثْلَهُمْ مَعَهُمْ رَحْمَةً مِنْ عِنْدِنَا وَذِكْرَى لِلْعَابِدِينَ
and We restored his family to him (that he had lost), and the like thereof along with them, as a mercy from Ourselves and a Reminder for all who worship Us.
21:85
وَإِسْمَاعِيلَ وَإِدْرِيسَ وَذَا الْكِفْلِ ۖ
And (remember) Ismaeel (Ishmael), and Idris (إدريس) (Enoch) and Dhul-Kifl (Isaiah),
كُلٌّ مِنَ الصَّابِرِينَ
all were from among As-Sabirin (the patient ones, etc.).
21:86
وَأَدْخَلْنَاهُمْ فِي رَحْمَتِنَا ۖ
And We admitted them to Our Mercy.
إِنَّهُمْ مِنَ الصَّالِحِينَ
Verily, they were of the righteous.
21:87

And (remember) Dhan-Noon (Jonah), when he went off in anger, and imagined that We shall not punish him (i.e. the calamites which had befallen him)!
فَنَادَى فِي الظُّلُمَاتِ
But he cried through the darkness (saying):
أَنْ لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا أَنْتَ سُبْحَانَكَ إِنِّي كُنْتُ مِنَ الظَّالِمِينَ
La ilaha illa Ant (نمل)a (none has the right to be worshipped but You (O Allah (الله))), Glorified (and Exalted) are You (above all that (evil) they associate with You). Truly, I have been of the wrong-doers.‘
21:88
فَاسْتَجَبْنَا لَهُ وَنَجَّيْنَاهُ مِنَ الْغَمِّ ۚ
So We answered his call, and delivered him from the distress.
وَكَذَلِكَ نُنْجِي الْمُؤْمِنِينَ
And thus We do deliver the believers (who believe in the Oneness of Allah (الله), abstain from evil and work righteousness).
21:89
وَزَكَرِيَّا إِذْ نَادَى رَبَّهُ
And (remember) Zakariya (Zachariah (زكريا)), when he cried to his Lord:
رَبِّ لَا تَذَرْنِي فَرْدًا وَأَنْتَ خَيْرُ الْوَارِثِينَ
O My Lord! Leave me not single (childless), though You are the Best of the inheritors.‘
21:90
فَاسْتَجَبْنَا لَهُ وَوَهَبْنَا لَهُ يَحْيَى وَأَصْلَحْنَا لَهُ زَوْجَهُ ۚ
So We answered his call, and We bestowed upon him Yahya (يحيى) (John), and cured his wife (to bear a child) for him.
إِنَّهُمْ كَانُوا يُسَارِعُونَ فِي الْخَيْرَاتِ وَيَدْعُونَنَا رَغَبًا وَرَهَبًا ۖ
Verily, they used to hasten on to do good deeds, and they used to call on Us with hope and fear,
وَكَانُوا لَنَا خَاشِعِينَ
and used to humble themselves before Us.
21:91
وَالَّتِي أَحْصَنَتْ فَرْجَهَا فَنَفَخْنَا فِيهَا مِنْ رُوحِنَا
And (remember) she who guarded her chastity (Virgin Mary (Maryam (مريم))), We breathed into (the sleeves of) her (shirt or garment) (through Our Rooh Jibrael (Jibreel (جبريل))),
وَجَعَلْنَاهَا وَابْنَهَا آيَةً لِلْعَالَمِينَ
and We made her and her son (Isa (Iesa (عيسى))) a sign for Al-Alamin (the mankind and jinns).
21:92

Truly! This, your Ummah (Sharia or religion (Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism)) is one religion,
وَأَنَا رَبُّكُمْ فَاعْبُدُونِ
and I am your Lord, therefore worship Me (Alone).
(Tafseer Ibn Katheer)
21:93

But they have broken up and differed as regards their religion among themselves.
كُلٌّ إِلَيْنَا رَاجِعُونَ
(And) they all shall return to Us.

Until, when Yajooj and Majooj (Gog and Magog (يأجوج ومأجوج)) are let loose (from their barrier), and they swiftly swarm from every mound.
21:97
وَاقْتَرَبَ الْوَعْدُ الْحَقُّ فَإِذَا هِيَ شَاخِصَةٌ أَبْصَارُ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا
And the true promise (Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة)) shall draw near (of fulfillment). Then (when mankind is resurrected from their graves), you shall see the eyes of the disbelievers fixedly stare in horror.
يَا وَيْلَنَا قَدْ كُنَّا فِي غَفْلَةٍ مِنْ هَذَا
(They will say): ‘Woe to us! We were indeed heedless of this;
بَلْ كُنَّا ظَالِمِينَ
nay, but we were Zalimoon (polytheists and wrong-doers, etc.).‘
21:98
إِنَّكُمْ وَمَا تَعْبُدُونَ مِنْ دُونِ اللَّهِ حَصَبُ جَهَنَّمَ أَنْتُمْ لَهَا وَارِدُونَ
Certainly! You (disbelievers) and that which you are worshipping now besides Allah (الله), are (but) fuel for Hell (جهنم)! (Surely), you will enter it.
21:99
‏لَوْ كَانَ هَؤُلَاءِ آلِهَةً مَا وَرَدُوهَا ۖ
Had these (idols, etc.) been aliha (gods), they would not have entered there (Hell (جهنم)),
وَكُلٌّ فِيهَا خَالِدُونَ
and all of them will abide therein.
21:100
لَهُمْ فِيهَا زَفِيرٌ وَهُمْ فِيهَا لَا يَسْمَعُونَ
Therein breathing out with deep sighs and roaring will be their portion, and therein they will hear not.
21:101
إِنَّ الَّذِينَ سَبَقَتْ لَهُمْ مِنَّا الْحُسْنَى أُولَئِكَ عَنْهَا مُبْعَدُونَ
Verily those for whom the good has preceded from Us, they will be removed far therefrom (Hell (جهنم)) (e.g. Isa (Iesa (عيسى)), son of Mary (Maryam (مريم)); Uzair (عزير) (Ezra), etc.).
21:102
لَا يَسْمَعُونَ حَسِيسَهَا ۖ
They shall not hear the slightest sound of it (Hell (جهنم)),
وَهُمْ فِي مَا اشْتَهَتْ أَنْفُسُهُمْ خَالِدُونَ
while they abide in that which their ownselves desire.
21:103
لَا يَحْزُنُهُمُ الْفَزَعُ الْأَكْبَرُ وَتَتَلَقَّاهُمُ الْمَلَائِكَةُ
The greatest terror (on the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة)) will not grieve them, and the angels will meet them, (with the greeting):
هَذَا يَوْمُكُمُ الَّذِي كُنْتُمْ تُوعَدُونَ
This is your Day which you were promised.‘
21:104
يَوْمَ نَطْوِي السَّمَاءَ كَطَيِّ السِّجِلِّ لِلْكُتُبِ ۚ
And (remember) the Day when We shall roll up the heavens like a scroll rolled up for books,
كَمَا بَدَأْنَا أَوَّلَ خَلْقٍ نُعِيدُهُ ۚ
as We began the first creation, We shall repeat it,
وَعْدًا عَلَيْنَا ۚ
(it is) a promise binding upon Us.
إِنَّا كُنَّا فَاعِلِينَ
Truly, We shall do it.
21:105
وَلَقَدْ كَتَبْنَا فِي الزَّبُورِ مِنْ بَعْدِ الذِّكْرِ أَنَّ الْأَرْضَ يَرِثُهَا عِبَادِيَ الصَّالِحُونَ
And indeed We have written in Zaboor (Psalms) (i.e. all the revealed Holy Books the Taurat (Torah (التوراة)), the Injil (Injeel (الإنجيل)), the Quran (القرآن)) after (We have already written in) Al-Lauh Al-Mahfooz (the Book, that is in the heaven with Allah (الله)), that My righteous slaves shall inherit the land (i.e. the land of Paradise (الجنة)).
21:106
‏إِنَّ فِي هَذَا لَبَلَاغًا لِقَوْمٍ عَابِدِينَ
Verily, in this (the Quran (القرآن)) there is a plain Message for people who worship Allah (الله) (i.e. the true, real believers of Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism who act practically on the Quran (القرآن) and the Sun (الشمس)nah legal ways of the Prophet SAW).
21:107
وَمَا أَرْسَلْنَاكَ إِلَّا رَحْمَةً لِلْعَالَمِينَ
And We have sent you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) not but as a mercy for the Alameen (mankind, jinns and all that exists).
21:108
قُلْ إِنَّمَا يُوحَى إِلَيَّ أَنَّمَا إِلَهُكُمْ إِلَهٌ وَاحِدٌ ۖ فَهَلْ أَنْتُمْ مُسْلِمُونَ
Say (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW): ‘It is revealed to me that your Ilah (God) is only one Ilah (God - Allah (الله)). Will you then submit to His Will (become Muslims and stop worshipping others besides Allah (الله))?‘
21:109
فَإِنْ تَوَلَّوْا فَقُلْ آذَنْتُكُمْ عَلَى سَوَاءٍ ۖ
But if they (disbelievers, idolaters, Jews (هُودًا), Christians (نَصَارَىٰ), polytheists, etc.) turn away (from Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism) say (to them O Muhammad (محمد) SAW):
I give you a notice (of war as) to be known to us all alike.
وَإِنْ أَدْرِي أَقَرِيبٌ أَمْ بَعِيدٌ مَا تُوعَدُونَ
And I know not whether that which you are promised (i.e. the torment or the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة)) is near or far.‘
21:110
إِنَّهُ يَعْلَمُ الْجَهْرَ مِنَ الْقَوْلِ وَيَعْلَمُ مَا تَكْتُمُونَ
(Say O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) Verily, He (Allah (الله)) knows that which is spoken aloud (openly) and that which you conceal.
21:111
وَإِنْ أَدْرِي لَعَلَّهُ فِتْنَةٌ لَكُمْ وَمَتَاعٌ إِلَى حِينٍ
And I know not, perhaps it may be a trial for you, and an enjoyment for a while.
21:112
‏قَالَ رَبِّ احْكُمْ بِالْحَقِّ ۗ
He (Muhammad (محمد) SAW) said: ‘My Lord! Judge You in truth!
وَرَبُّنَا الرَّحْمَنُ الْمُسْتَعَانُ عَلَى مَا تَصِفُونَ
Our Lord is the Most Beneficent (Ar-Rahmaan (ٱلْرَّحْمَـانُ)), Whose Help is to be sought against that which you attribute (unto Allah (الله) that He has offspring, and unto Muhammad (محمد) SAW that he is a sorcerer, and unto the Quran (القرآن) that it is poetry, etc.)!‘*********
