Al-Inshiqaaq
The Rending Asunder, The Sundering, Splitting Open, The Splitting Asunder
84:[1-19] On Doomsday, heaven will split asunder and earth will be spread out. Books of Deeds will be distributed; the righteous will be happy while disbelievers will be calling for death
84:[20-25] People are asked to believe while there is still time
84:1
إِذَا ٱلسَّمَآءُ ٱنشَقَّتۡ
When the heaven is split asunder,
84:2
وَأَذِنَتۡ لِرَبِّہَا وَحُقَّتۡ
And listens and obeys its Lord, and it must do so;
84:3
وَإِذَا ٱلۡأَرۡضُ مُدَّتۡ
And when the earth is stretched forth,
84:4
وَأَلۡقَتۡ مَا فِيہَا وَتَخَلَّتۡ
And has cast out all that was in it and became empty,
84:5
وَأَذِنَتۡ لِرَبِّہَا وَحُقَّتۡ
And listens and obeys its Lord, and it must do so;
84:6
يَـٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلۡإِنسَـٰنُ إِنَّكَ كَادِحٌ إِلَىٰ رَبِّكَ كَدۡحً۬ا فَمُلَـٰقِيهِ
O man! Verily, you are returning towards your Lord with your deeds and actions (good or bad), a sure returning, so you will meet (i.e. the results of your deeds which you did).
84:7
فَأَمَّا مَنۡ أُوتِىَ كِتَـٰبَهُ ۥ بِيَمِينِهِۦ
Then, as for him who will be given his Record in his right hand,
84:8
فَسَوۡفَ يُحَاسَبُ حِسَابً۬ا يَسِيرً۬ا
He surely will receive an easy reckoning,
84:9
وَيَنقَلِبُ إِلَىٰٓ أَهۡلِهِۦ مَسۡرُورً۬ا
And will return to his family in joy!
84:10
وَأَمَّا مَنۡ أُوتِىَ كِتَـٰبَهُ ۥ وَرَآءَ ظَهۡرِهِۦ
But whosoever is given his Record behind his back,
84:11
فَسَوۡفَ يَدۡعُواْ ثُبُورً۬ا
He will invoke (his) destruction,
84:12
وَيَصۡلَىٰ سَعِيرًا
And shall enter a blazing Fire, and made to taste its burning.
84:13
إِنَّهُ ۥ كَانَ فِىٓ أَهۡلِهِۦ مَسۡرُورًا
Verily, he was among his people in joy!
84:14
إِنَّهُ ۥ ظَنَّ أَن لَّن يَحُورَ
Verily, he thought that he would never come back (to Us)!
84:15
بَلَىٰٓ إِنَّ رَبَّهُ ۥ كَانَ بِهِۦ بَصِيرً۬ا
Yes! Verily, his Lord has been ever beholding him!
84:16
فَلَآ أُقۡسِمُ بِٱلشَّفَقِ
So I swear by the afterglow of sunset;
84:17
وَٱلَّيۡلِ وَمَا وَسَقَ
And by the night and whatever it gathers in its darkness;
84:18
وَٱلۡقَمَرِ إِذَا ٱتَّسَقَ
And by the moon when it is at the full,
84:19
لَتَرۡكَبُنَّ طَبَقًا عَن طَبَقٍ۬
You shall certainly travel from stage to stage (in this life and in the Hereafter).
84:20
فَمَا لَهُمۡ لَا يُؤۡمِنُونَ
What is the matter with them, that they believe not?
84:21
وَإِذَا قُرِئَ عَلَيۡہِمُ ٱلۡقُرۡءَانُ لَا يَسۡجُدُونَ
And when the Qur‘ân is recited to them, they fall not prostrate, ۩
84:22
بَلِ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُواْ يُكَذِّبُونَ
Nay, (on the contrary), those who disbelieve, belie.(Prophet Muhammad (محمد) SAW and whatever he brought, i.e. this Qur‘ân and Islâmic Monotheism, etc.)
84:23
وَٱللَّهُ أَعۡلَمُ بِمَا يُوعُونَ
And Allâh knows best what they gather (of good and bad deeds),
84:24
فَبَشِّرۡهُم بِعَذَابٍ أَلِيمٍ
So announce to them a painful torment.
84:25
إِلَّا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُواْ وَعَمِلُواْ ٱلصَّـٰلِحَـٰتِ لَهُمۡ أَجۡرٌ غَيۡرُ مَمۡنُونِۭ
Save those who believe and do righteous good deeds, for them is a reward that will never come to an end (i.e. Paradise (الجنة)).*********
