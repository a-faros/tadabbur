Al-Muminoon
The Believers
23:[1-11] Characteristics of true believers
23:[12-16] Stages of human creation
23:[17-22] Allah has made the heavens, vegetation, trees and animals for the benefit of human beings
23:1
قَدۡ أَفۡلَحَ ٱلۡمُؤۡمِنُونَ
Successful indeed are the believers.
23:2
ٱلَّذِينَ هُمۡ فِى صَلَاتِہِمۡ خَـٰشِعُونَ
Those who offer their Salat (prayers) with all solemnity and full submissiveness.
23:3
وَٱلَّذِينَ هُمۡ عَنِ ٱللَّغۡوِ مُعۡرِضُونَ
And those who turn away from Al-Laghw (dirty, false, evil vain talk, falsehood, and all that Allah (الله) has forbidden).
23:4
وَٱلَّذِينَ هُمۡ لِلزَّكَوٰةِ فَـٰعِلُونَ
And those who pay the Zakat .
23:5
وَٱلَّذِينَ هُمۡ لِفُرُوجِهِمۡ حَـٰفِظُونَ
And those who guard their chastity (i.e. private parts, from illegal sexual acts)
23:6
إِلَّا عَلَىٰٓ أَزۡوَٲجِهِمۡ أَوۡ مَا مَلَكَتۡ أَيۡمَـٰنُہُمۡ
Except from their wives or (the captives and slaves) that their right hands possess,
فَإِنَّہُمۡ غَيۡرُ مَلُومِينَ
for then, they are free from blame;
23:7
فَمَنِ ٱبۡتَغَىٰ وَرَآءَ ذَٲلِكَ فَأُوْلَـٰٓٮِٕكَ هُمُ ٱلۡعَادُونَ
But whoever seeks beyond that, then those are the transgressors;
23:8
وَٱلَّذِينَ هُمۡ لِأَمَـٰنَـٰتِهِمۡ وَعَهۡدِهِمۡ رَٲعُونَ
Those who are faithfully true to their Amanat (all the duties which Allah (الله) has ordained, honesty, moral responsibility and trusts etc.) and to their covenants;
23:9
وَٱلَّذِينَ هُمۡ عَلَىٰ صَلَوَٲتِہِمۡ يُحَافِظُونَ
And those who strictly guard their (five compulsory congregational) Salawat (prayers) (at their fixed stated hours).
23:10
أُوْلَـٰٓٮِٕكَ هُمُ ٱلۡوَٲرِثُونَ
These are indeed the inheritors.
23:11
ٱلَّذِينَ يَرِثُونَ ٱلۡفِرۡدَوۡسَ هُمۡ فِيہَا خَـٰلِدُونَ
Who shall inherit the Firdaus (Paradise (الجنة)). They shall dwell therein forever.
23:12
وَلَقَدۡ خَلَقۡنَا ٱلۡإِنسَـٰنَ مِن سُلَـٰلَةٍ۬ مِّن طِينٍ۬
And indeed We created man (Adam (آدم)) out of an extract of clay (water and earth).
23:13
ثُمَّ جَعَلۡنَـٰهُ نُطۡفَةً۬ فِى قَرَارٍ۬ مَّكِينٍ۬
Thereafter We made him (the offspring of Adam (آدم)) as a Nutfah (mixed drops of the male and female sexual discharge) (and lodged it) in a safe lodging (womb of the woman).
23:14
ثُمَّ خَلَقۡنَا ٱلنُّطۡفَةَ عَلَقَةً۬
Then We made the Nutfah into a clot (a piece of thick coagulated blood),
فَخَلَقۡنَا ٱلۡعَلَقَةَ مُضۡغَةً۬
then We made the clot into a little lump of flesh,
فَخَلَقۡنَا ٱلۡمُضۡغَةَ عِظَـٰمً۬ا
then We made out of that little lump of flesh bones,
فَكَسَوۡنَا ٱلۡعِظَـٰمَ لَحۡمً۬ا
then We clothed the bones with flesh,
ثُمَّ أَنشَأۡنَـٰهُ خَلۡقًا ءَاخَرَ‌ۚ
and then We brought it forth as another creation.
فَتَبَارَكَ ٱللَّهُ أَحۡسَنُ ٱلۡخَـٰلِقِينَ
So blessed be Allah (الله), the Best of creators.
23:15
ثُمَّ إِنَّكُم بَعۡدَ ذَٲلِكَ لَمَيِّتُونَ
After that, surely, you will die.
23:16
ثُمَّ إِنَّكُمۡ يَوۡمَ ٱلۡقِيَـٰمَةِ تُبۡعَثُونَ
Then (again), surely, you will be resurrected on the Day of Resurrection (يوم القيامة).
23:17
وَلَقَدۡ خَلَقۡنَا فَوۡقَكُمۡ سَبۡعَ طَرَآٮِٕقَ
And indeed We have created above you seven heavens (one over the other),
وَمَا كُنَّا عَنِ ٱلۡخَلۡقِ غَـٰفِلِينَ
and We are never unaware of the creation.
23:18
وَأَنزَلۡنَا مِنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ مَآءَۢ بِقَدَرٍ۬ فَأَسۡكَنَّـٰهُ فِى ٱلۡأَرۡضِ‌ۖ
And We sent down from the sky water (rain) in (due) measure, and We gave it lodging in the earth,
وَإِنَّا عَلَىٰ ذَهَابِۭ بِهِۦ لَقَـٰدِرُونَ
and verily, We are Able to take it away.
23:19
فَأَنشَأۡنَا لَكُم بِهِۦ جَنَّـٰتٍ۬ مِّن نَّخِيلٍ۬ وَأَعۡنَـٰبٍ۬
Then We brought forth for you therewith gardens of date-palms and grapes,
لَّكُمۡ فِيہَا فَوَٲكِهُ كَثِيرَةٌ۬ وَمِنۡہَا تَأۡكُلُونَ
wherein is much fruit for you, and whereof you eat.
23:20
وَشَجَرَةً۬ تَخۡرُجُ مِن طُورِ سَيۡنَآءَ تَنۢبُتُ بِٱلدُّهۡنِ وَصِبۡغٍ۬ لِّلۡأَڪِلِينَ
And a tree (olive) that springs forth from Mount Sinai (طور سيناء), that grows oil, and (it is a) relish for the eaters.
23:21
وَإِنَّ لَكُمۡ فِى ٱلۡأَنۡعَـٰمِ لَعِبۡرَةً۬‌ۖ
And Verily! In the cattle there is indeed a lesson for you.
نُّسۡقِيكُم مِّمَّا فِى بُطُونِہَا
We give you to drink (milk) of that which is in their bellies.
وَلَكُمۡ فِيہَا مَنَـٰفِعُ كَثِيرَةٌ۬ وَمِنۡہَا تَأۡكُلُونَ
And there are, in them, numerous (other) benefits for you, and of them you eat.
23:22
وَعَلَيۡہَا وَعَلَى ٱلۡفُلۡكِ تُحۡمَلُونَ
And on them, and on ships you are carried.
23:[23-30] The Prophet Nüh was sent to guide his people - they disbelieved him and as a result Allah drowned all disbelievers in the great flood
23:[31-32] After the Prophet Nüh, Allah sent Hüd to guide his people
23:23
وَلَقَدۡ أَرۡسَلۡنَا نُوحًا إِلَىٰ قَوۡمِهِۦ فَقَالَ
And indeed We sent Nooh (Noah) to his people, and he said:
يَـٰقَوۡمِ ٱعۡبُدُواْ ٱللَّهَ مَا لَكُم مِّنۡ إِلَـٰهٍ غَيۡرُهُ ۥۤ‌ۖ
O my people! Worship Allah (الله)! You have no other Ilah (God) but Him (Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism).
أَفَلَا تَتَّقُونَ
Will you not then be afraid (of Him i.e. of His Punishment because of worshipping others besides Him)?‘
23:24
فَقَالَ ٱلۡمَلَؤُاْ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُواْ مِن قَوۡمِهِۦ
But the chiefs of those who disbelieved among his people said:
مَا هَـٰذَآ إِلَّا بَشَرٌ۬ مِّثۡلُكُمۡ يُرِيدُ أَن يَتَفَضَّلَ عَلَيۡڪُمۡ
He is no more than a human being like you, he seeks to make himself superior to you.
وَلَوۡ شَآءَ ٱللَّهُ لَأَنزَلَ مَلَـٰٓٮِٕكَةً۬
Had Allah (الله) willed, He surely could have sent down angels;
مَّا سَمِعۡنَا بِہَـٰذَا فِىٓ ءَابَآٮِٕنَا ٱلۡأَوَّلِينَ
never did we hear such a thing among our fathers of old.
23:25
إِنۡ هُوَ إِلَّا رَجُلُۢ بِهِۦ جِنَّةٌ۬ فَتَرَبَّصُواْ بِهِۦ حَتَّىٰ حِينٍ۬
He is only a man in whom is madness, so wait for him a while.‘
23:26
قَالَ رَبِّ ٱنصُرۡنِى بِمَا ڪَذَّبُونِ
(Nooh (Noah)) said: ‘O my Lord! Help me because they deny me.‘
23:27
فَأَوۡحَيۡنَآ إِلَيۡهِ أَنِ ٱصۡنَعِ ٱلۡفُلۡكَ بِأَعۡيُنِنَا وَوَحۡيِنَا
So We inspired him (saying): ‘Construct the ship under Our Eye (عين)s and under Our Revelation (guidance).
فَإِذَا جَآءَ أَمۡرُنَا وَفَارَ ٱلتَّنُّورُ‌ۙ فَٱسۡلُكۡ فِيہَا مِن ڪُلٍّ۬ زَوۡجَيۡنِ ٱثۡنَيۡنِ
Then, when Our Command comes, and the oven gushes forth water, take on board of each kind two (male and female),
وَأَهۡلَكَ إِلَّا مَن سَبَقَ عَلَيۡهِ ٱلۡقَوۡلُ مِنۡهُمۡ‌ۖ
and your family, except those thereof against whom the Word has already gone forth.
وَلَا تُخَـٰطِبۡنِى فِى ٱلَّذِينَ ظَلَمُوٓاْ‌ۖ إِنَّہُم مُّغۡرَقُونَ
And address Me not in favour of those who have done wrong. Verily, they are to be drowned.
23:28
فَإِذَا ٱسۡتَوَيۡتَ أَنتَ وَمَن مَّعَكَ عَلَى ٱلۡفُلۡكِ فَقُلِ
And when you have embarked on the ship, you and whoever is with you, then say:
ٱلۡحَمۡدُ لِلَّهِ ٱلَّذِى نَجَّٮٰنَا مِنَ ٱلۡقَوۡمِ ٱلظَّـٰلِمِينَ
All the praises and thanks be to Allah (الله), Who has saved us from the people who are Zalimoon.(i.e. oppressors, wrong-doers, polytheists, those who join others in worship with Allah (الله), etc.)
23:29
وَقُل رَّبِّ أَنزِلۡنِى مُنزَلاً۬ مُّبَارَكً۬اوَأَنتَ خَيۡرُ ٱلۡمُنزِلِينَ
And say: ‘My Lord! Cause me to land at a blessed landing-place, for You are the Best of those who bring to land.‘
23:30
إِنَّ فِى ذَٲلِكَ لَأَيَـٰتٍ۬ وَإِن كُنَّا لَمُبۡتَلِينَ
Verily, in this (what We did as regards drowning of the people of Nooh (Noah)), there are indeed Ayat (proofs, evidences, lessons, signs, etc. for men to understand), for sure We are ever putting (men) to the test.
23:31
ثُمَّ أَنشَأۡنَا مِنۢ بَعۡدِهِمۡ قَرۡنًا ءَاخَرِينَ
Then, after them, We created another generation.
23:32
فَأَرۡسَلۡنَا فِيہِمۡ رَسُولاً۬ مِّنۡہُمۡ
And We sent to them a Messenger from among themselves (saying):
أَنِ ٱعۡبُدُواْ ٱللَّهَ مَا لَكُم مِّنۡ إِلَـٰهٍ غَيۡرُهُ ۥۤ‌ۖ
Worship Allah (الله)! You have no other Ilah (God) but Him.
أَفَلَا تَتَّقُونَ
Will you not then be afraid (of Him i.e. of His Punishment because of worshipping others besides Him)?‘
23:[33-44] They called the Prophet Hüd an imposter; as a result, Allah destroyed them all in a mighty blast. After Hüd, Allah sent Rasools to other people; those people also denied and faced a similar punishment
23:[45-50] Musa was sent to Fir‘on and his chiefs; they also disbelieved and faced destruction
23:33
وَقَالَ ٱلۡمَلَأُ مِن قَوۡمِهِ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُواْ وَكَذَّبُواْ بِلِقَآءِ ٱلۡأَخِرَةِ
And the chiefs of his people, who disbelieved and denied the Meeting in the Hereafter,
وَأَتۡرَفۡنَـٰهُمۡ فِى ٱلۡحَيَوٰةِ ٱلدُّنۡيَا
and to whom We had given the luxuries and comforts of this life, said:
مَا هَـٰذَآ إِلَّا بَشَرٌ۬ مِّثۡلُكُمۡ
He is no more than a human being like you,
يَأۡكُلُ مِمَّا تَأۡكُلُونَ مِنۡهُ وَيَشۡرَبُ مِمَّا تَشۡرَبُونَ
he eats of that which you eat, and drinks of what you drink.
23:34
وَلَٮِٕنۡ أَطَعۡتُم بَشَرً۬ا مِّثۡلَكُمۡ إِنَّكُمۡ إِذً۬ا لَّخَـٰسِرُونَ
If you were to obey a human being like yourselves, then verily! You indeed would be losers.
23:35
أَيَعِدُكُمۡ أَنَّكُمۡ إِذَا مِتُّمۡ وَكُنتُمۡ تُرَابً۬ا وَعِظَـٰمًا أَنَّكُم مُّخۡرَجُونَ
Does he promise you that when you have died and have become dust and bones, you shall come out alive (resurrected)?
23:36
هَيۡہَاتَ هَيۡہَاتَ لِمَا تُوعَدُونَ
Far, very far is that which you are promised.
23:37
إِنۡ هِىَ إِلَّا حَيَاتُنَا ٱلدُّنۡيَا نَمُوتُ وَنَحۡيَا
There is nothing but our life of this world! We die and we live!
وَمَا نَحۡنُ بِمَبۡعُوثِينَ
And we are not going to be resurrected!
23:38
إِنۡ هُوَ إِلَّا رَجُلٌ ٱفۡتَرَىٰ عَلَى ٱللَّهِ ڪَذِبً۬ا وَمَا نَحۡنُ لَهُ ۥ بِمُؤۡمِنِينَ
He is only a man who has invented a lie against Allah (الله), but we are not going to believe in him.‘
23:39
قَالَ رَبِّ ٱنصُرۡنِى بِمَا كَذَّبُونِ
He said: ‘O my Lord! Help me because they deny me.‘
23:40
قَالَ عَمَّا قَلِيلٍ۬ لَّيُصۡبِحُنَّ نَـٰدِمِينَ
(Allah (الله)) said: ‘In a little while, they are sure to be regretful.‘
23:41
فَأَخَذَتۡہُمُ ٱلصَّيۡحَةُ بِٱلۡحَقِّ فَجَعَلۡنَـٰهُمۡ غُثَآءً۬‌ۚ
So As-Saeehah (torment - awful cry, etc.) overtook them with justice, and We made them as rubbish of dead plants.
فَبُعۡدً۬ا لِّلۡقَوۡمِ ٱلظَّـٰلِمِينَ
So away with the people who are Zalimoon (polytheists, wrong-doers, disbelievers in the Oneness of Allah (الله), disobedient to His Messengers, etc.).
23:42
ثُمَّ أَنشَأۡنَا مِنۢ بَعۡدِهِمۡ قُرُونًا ءَاخَرِينَ
Then, after them, We created other generations.
23:43
مَا تَسۡبِقُ مِنۡ أُمَّةٍ أَجَلَهَا وَمَا يَسۡتَـٔۡخِرُونَ
No nation can anticipate their term, nor can they delay it.
23:44
ثُمَّ أَرۡسَلۡنَا رُسُلَنَا تَتۡرَا‌ۖ
Then We sent Our Messengers in succession,
كُلَّ مَا جَآءَ أُمَّةً۬ رَّسُولُهَا كَذَّبُوهُ‌ۚ
every time there came to a nation their Messenger, they denied him,
فَأَتۡبَعۡنَا بَعۡضَہُم بَعۡضً۬ا وَجَعَلۡنَـٰهُمۡ أَحَادِيثَ‌ۚ
so We made them follow one another (to destruction), and We made them as Ahadeeth (the true stories for mankind to learn a lesson from them).
فَبُعۡدً۬ا لِّقَوۡمٍ۬ لَّا يُؤۡمِنُونَ
So away with a people who believe not.
23:45
ثُمَّ أَرۡسَلۡنَا مُوسَىٰ وَأَخَاهُ هَـٰرُونَ بِـَٔايَـٰتِنَا وَسُلۡطَـٰنٍ۬ مُّبِينٍ
Then We sent Musa (Moosa (موسى)) and his brother Haroon (Aaron), with Our Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, etc.) and manifest authority,
23:46
إِلَىٰ فِرۡعَوۡنَ وَمَلَإِيْهِۦ
To Firaun (Pharaoh (فرعون)) and his chiefs,
فَٱسۡتَكۡبَرُواْ وَكَانُواْ قَوۡمًا عَالِينَ
but they behaved insolently and they were people self exalting (by disobeying their Lord, and exalting themselves over and above the Messenger of Allah (الله)).
23:47
فَقَالُوٓاْ أَنُؤۡمِنُ لِبَشَرَيۡنِ مِثۡلِنَا وَقَوۡمُهُمَا لَنَا عَـٰبِدُونَ
They said: ‘Shall we believe in two men like ourselves, and their people are obedient to us with humility (and we use them to serve us as we like).‘
23:48
فَكَذَّبُوهُمَا فَكَانُواْ مِنَ ٱلۡمُهۡلَكِينَ
So they denied them both (Musa (Moosa (موسى)) and Haroon (Aaron)) and became of those who were destroyed.
23:49
وَلَقَدۡ ءَاتَيۡنَا مُوسَى ٱلۡكِتَـٰبَ لَعَلَّهُمۡ يَہۡتَدُونَ
And indeed We gave Musa (Moosa (موسى)) the Scripture, that they may be guided.
23:50
وَجَعَلۡنَا ٱبۡنَ مَرۡيَمَ وَأُمَّهُ ۥۤ ءَايَةً۬
And We made the son of Mary (Maryam (مريم)) and his mother as a sign,
وَءَاوَيۡنَـٰهُمَآ إِلَىٰ رَبۡوَةٍ۬ ذَاتِ قَرَارٍ۬ وَمَعِينٍ۬
And We gave them refuge on high ground, a place of rest, security and flowing streams.
said: Allah has
23:[51-61] ‘In fact, your religion is one religion; I am your Rabb, so fear Me Alone.‘
23:[62-77] Allah has not charged any soul with more than it can bear. Those who do not believe in the hereafter will ever stray away from the Right Path
23:51
يَـٰٓأَيُّہَا ٱلرُّسُلُ كُلُواْ مِنَ ٱلطَّيِّبَـٰتِ وَٱعۡمَلُواْ صَـٰلِحًا‌ۖ
O (you) Messengers! Eat of the Taiyibat (all kinds of Halal (legal) foods which Allah (الله) has made legal (meat of slaughtered eatable animals, milk products, fats, vegetables, fruits, etc.), and do righteous deeds.
إِنِّى بِمَا تَعۡمَلُونَ عَلِيمٌ۬
Verily! I am Well-Acquainted with what you do.
23:52
وَإِنَّ هَـٰذِهِۦۤ أُمَّتُكُمۡ أُمَّةً۬ وَٲحِدَةً۬
And verily! This your religion (of Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism) is one religion,
وَأَنَا۟ رَبُّڪُمۡ فَٱتَّقُونِ
and I am your Lord, so keep your duty to Me.
23:53
فَتَقَطَّعُوٓاْ أَمۡرَهُم بَيۡنَہُمۡ زُبُرً۬ا‌ۖ
But they (men) have broken their religion among them into sects,
كُلُّ حِزۡبِۭ بِمَا لَدَيۡہِمۡ فَرِحُونَ
each group rejoicing in its belief.
23:54
فَذَرۡهُمۡ فِى غَمۡرَتِهِمۡ حَتَّىٰ حِينٍ
So leave them in their error for a time.
23:55
أَيَحۡسَبُونَ أَنَّمَا نُمِدُّهُم بِهِۦ مِن مَّالٍ۬ وَبَنِينَ
Do they think that We enlarge them in wealth and children,
23:56
نُسَارِعُ لَهُمۡ فِى ٱلۡخَيۡرَٲتِ‌ۚ
We hasten unto them with good things (in this worldly life so that they will have no share of good things in the Hereafter)?
بَل لَّا يَشۡعُرُونَ
Nay, but they perceive not.
23:57
إِنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ هُم مِّنۡ خَشۡيَةِ رَبِّہِم مُّشۡفِقُونَ
Verily! Those who live in awe for fear of their Lord;
23:58
وَٱلَّذِينَ هُم بِـَٔايَـٰتِ رَبِّہِمۡ يُؤۡمِنُونَ
And those who believe in the Ayat (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, etc.) of their Lord,
23:59
وَٱلَّذِينَ هُم بِرَبِّہِمۡ لَا يُشۡرِكُونَ
And those who join not anyone (in worship) as partners with their Lord;
23:60
وَٱلَّذِينَ يُؤۡتُونَ مَآ ءَاتَواْ وَّقُلُوبُہُمۡ وَجِلَةٌ أَنَّہُمۡ إِلَىٰ رَبِّہِمۡ رَٲجِعُونَ
And those who give that (their charity) which they give (and also do other good deeds) with their hearts full of fear (whether their alms and charities, etc., have been accepted or not), because they are sure to return to their Lord (for reckoning).
23:61
أُوْلَـٰٓٮِٕكَ يُسَـٰرِعُونَ فِى ٱلۡخَيۡرَٲتِ وَهُمۡ لَهَا سَـٰبِقُونَ
It is these who race for the good deeds, and they are foremost in them.(e.g. offering the compulsory Salat (prayers) in their (early) stated, fixed times and so on)
23:62
وَلَا نُكَلِّفُ نَفۡسًا إِلَّا وُسۡعَهَا‌ۖ
And We tax not any person except according to his capacity,
وَلَدَيۡنَا كِتَـٰبٌ۬ يَنطِقُ بِٱلۡحَقِّ‌ۚ
and with Us is a Record which speaks the truth,
وَهُمۡ لَا يُظۡلَمُونَ
and they will not be wronged.
23:63
بَلۡ قُلُوبُہُمۡ فِى غَمۡرَةٍ۬ مِّنۡ هَـٰذَا
Nay, but their hearts are covered (blind) from understanding this (the Quran (القرآن)),
وَلَهُمۡ أَعۡمَـٰلٌ۬ مِّن دُونِ ذَٲلِكَ هُمۡ لَهَا عَـٰمِلُونَ
and they have other (evil) deeds, besides, which they are doing.
23:64
حَتَّىٰٓ إِذَآ أَخَذۡنَا مُتۡرَفِيہِم بِٱلۡعَذَابِ إِذَا هُمۡ يَجۡـَٔرُونَ
Until, when We grasp those of them who lead a luxurious life with punishment, behold! They make humble invocation with a loud voice.
23:65
لَا تَجۡـَٔرُواْ ٱلۡيَوۡمَ‌ۖإِنَّكُم مِّنَّا لَا تُنصَرُونَ
Invoke not loudly this day! Certainly, you shall not be helped by Us.
23:66
قَدۡ كَانَتۡ ءَايَـٰتِى تُتۡلَىٰ عَلَيۡكُمۡ فَكُنتُمۡ عَلَىٰٓ أَعۡقَـٰبِكُمۡ تَنكِصُونَ
Indeed My Verses used to be recited to you, but you used to turn back on your heels (denying them, and with hatred to listen to them).
23:67
مُسۡتَكۡبِرِينَ بِهِۦ سَـٰمِرً۬ا تَهۡجُرُونَ
In pride (they Quraish pagans and polytheists of Makkah (مكة) used to feel proud that they are the dwellers of Makkah (مكة) sanctuary Haram), talking evil about it (the Quran (القرآن)) by night.
23:68
أَفَلَمۡ يَدَّبَّرُواْ ٱلۡقَوۡلَ أَمۡ جَآءَهُم مَّا لَمۡ يَأۡتِ ءَابَآءَهُمُ ٱلۡأَوَّلِينَ
Have they not pondered over the Word (of Allah (الله), i.e. what is sent down to the Prophet SAW), or has there come to them what had not come to their fathers of old?
23:69
أَمۡ لَمۡ يَعۡرِفُواْ رَسُولَهُمۡ فَهُمۡ لَهُ ۥ مُنكِرُونَ
Or is it that they did not recognize their Messenger (Muhammad (محمد) SAW) so they deny him?
23:70
أَمۡ يَقُولُونَ بِهِۦ جِنَّةُۢ‌ۚ
Or say they: ‘There is madness in him?‘
بَلۡ جَآءَهُم بِٱلۡحَقِّ وَأَڪۡثَرُهُمۡ لِلۡحَقِّ كَـٰرِهُونَ
Nay, but he brought them the truth (i.e.
(a) Tauheed: Worshipping Allah (الله) Alone in all aspects
(b) The Quran (القرآن)
(c) The religion of Islam (الإسلام),‘)
but most of them (the disbelievers) are averse to the truth.
23:71
وَلَوِ ٱتَّبَعَ ٱلۡحَقُّ أَهۡوَآءَهُمۡ لَفَسَدَتِ ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٲتُ وَٱلۡأَرۡضُ وَمَن فِيهِنَّ‌ۚ
And if the truth had been in accordance with their desires, verily, the heavens and the earth, and whosoever is therein would have been corrupted!
بَلۡ أَتَيۡنَـٰهُم بِذِڪۡرِهِمۡ فَهُمۡ عَن ذِكۡرِهِم مُّعۡرِضُونَ
Nay, We have brought them their reminder, but they turn away from their reminder.
23:72
أَمۡ تَسۡـَٔلُهُمۡ خَرۡجً۬ا فَخَرَاجُ رَبِّكَ خَيۡرٌ۬‌ۖ
Or is it that you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) ask them for some wages?
But the recompense of your Lord is better,
وَهُوَ خَيۡرُ ٱلرَّٲزِقِينَ
and He is the Best of those who give sustenance.
23:73
وَإِنَّكَ لَتَدۡعُوهُمۡ إِلَىٰ صِرَٲطٍ۬ مُّسۡتَقِيمٍ۬
And certainly, you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) call them to a Straight Path (true religion Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism).
23:74
وَإِنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ لَا يُؤۡمِنُونَ بِٱلۡأَخِرَةِ عَنِ ٱلصِّرَٲطِ لَنَـٰكِبُونَ
And verily, those who believe not in the Hereafter are indeed deviating far astray from the Path (true religion Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism).
23:75
وَلَوۡ رَحِمۡنَـٰهُمۡ وَكَشَفۡنَا مَا بِهِم مِّن ضُرٍّ۬ لَّلَجُّواْ فِى طُغۡيَـٰنِهِمۡ يَعۡمَهُونَ
And though We had mercy on them and removed the distress which is on them, still they would obstinately persist in their transgression, wandering blindly.
23:76
وَلَقَدۡ أَخَذۡنَـٰهُم بِٱلۡعَذَابِ فَمَا ٱسۡتَكَانُواْ لِرَبِّہِمۡ وَمَا يَتَضَرَّعُونَ
And indeed We seized them with punishment, but they humbled not themselves to their Lord, nor did they invoke (Allah (الله)) with submission to Him.
23:77
حَتَّىٰٓ إِذَا فَتَحۡنَا عَلَيۡہِم بَابً۬ا ذَا عَذَابٍ۬ شَدِيدٍ إِذَا هُمۡ فِيهِ مُبۡلِسُونَ
Until, when We open for them the gate of severe punishment, then lo! They will be plunged into destruction with deep regrets, sorrows and in despair.
23:[78-83] Allah has given you ears, eyes and hearts but you seldom show gratitude
23:[84-90] Even the disbelievers recognize the existence of Allah
23:[91-92] Allah never begotten a son, nor is there any other god besides Him
23:78
وَهُوَ ٱلَّذِىٓ أَنشَأَ لَكُمُ ٱلسَّمۡعَ وَٱلۡأَبۡصَـٰرَ وَٱلۡأَفۡـِٔدَةَ‌ۚ
It is He, Who has created for you (the sense of) hearing (ears), sight (eyes), and hearts (understanding).
قَلِيلاً۬ مَّا تَشۡكُرُونَ
Little thanks you give.
23:79
وَهُوَ ٱلَّذِى ذَرَأَكُمۡ فِى ٱلۡأَرۡضِ وَإِلَيۡهِ تُحۡشَرُونَ
And it is He Who has created you on the earth, and to Him you shall be gathered back.
23:80
وَهُوَ ٱلَّذِى يُحۡىِۦ وَيُمِيتُ وَلَهُ ٱخۡتِلَـٰفُ ٱلَّيۡلِ وَٱلنَّهَارِ‌ۚ
And it is He Who gives life and causes death, and His is the alternation of night and day.
أَفَلَا تَعۡقِلُونَ
Will you not then understand?
23:81
بَلۡ قَالُواْ مِثۡلَ مَا قَالَ ٱلۡأَوَّلُونَ
Nay, but they say the like of what the men of old said.
23:82
قَالُوٓاْ أَءِذَا مِتۡنَا وَڪُنَّا تُرَابً۬ا وَعِظَـٰمًا أَءِنَّا لَمَبۡعُوثُونَ
They said: ‘When we are dead and have become dust and bones, shall we be resurrected indeed?
23:83
لَقَدۡ وُعِدۡنَا نَحۡنُ وَءَابَآؤُنَا هَـٰذَا مِن قَبۡلُ
Verily, this we have been promised, we and our fathers before (us)!
إِنۡ هَـٰذَآ إِلَّآ أَسَـٰطِيرُ ٱلۡأَوَّلِينَ
This is only the tales of the ancients!‘
23:84
قُل لِّمَنِ ٱلۡأَرۡضُ وَمَن فِيهَآ إِن ڪُنتُمۡ تَعۡلَمُونَ
Say: ‘Whose is the earth and whosoever is therein? If you know!‘
23:85
سَيَقُولُونَ لِلَّهِ‌ۚ
They will say: ‘It is Allah (الله)‘s!‘
قُلۡ أَفَلَا تَذَكَّرُونَ
Say: ‘Will you not then remember?‘
23:86
قُلۡ مَن رَّبُّ ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٲتِ ٱلسَّبۡعِ وَرَبُّ ٱلۡعَرۡشِ ٱلۡعَظِيمِ
Say: ‘Who is (the) Lord of the seven heavens, and (the) Lord of the Great Throne?‘
23:87
سَيَقُولُونَ لِلَّهِ‌ۚ
They will say: ‘Allah (الله).‘
قُلۡ أَفَلَا تَتَّقُونَ
Say: ‘Will you not then fear Allah (الله) (believe in His Oneness, obey Him, believe in the Resurrection and Recompense for each and every good or bad deed).‘
23:88
قُلۡ مَنۢ بِيَدِهِۦ مَلَكُوتُ ڪُلِّ شَىۡءٍ۬
Say ‘In Whose Hand is the sovereignty of everything (i.e. treasures of each and everything)?
وَهُوَ يُجِيرُوَلَا يُجَارُ عَلَيۡهِ
And He protects (all), while against Whom there is no protector, (i.e. if Allah (الله) saves anyone none can punish or harm him, and if Allah (الله) punishes or harms anyone none can save him),
إِن كُنتُمۡ تَعۡلَمُونَ
if you know.‘(Tafsir Al-Qurtubee, Vol. 12, Page 145)
23:89
سَيَقُولُونَ لِلَّهِ‌ۚ
They will say: ‘(All that belongs) to Allah (الله).‘
قُلۡ فَأَنَّىٰ تُسۡحَرُونَ
Say: ‘How then are you deceived and turn away from the truth?‘
23:90
بَلۡ أَتَيۡنَـٰهُم بِٱلۡحَقِّ وَإِنَّهُمۡ لَكَـٰذِبُونَ
Nay, but We have brought them the truth (Islam (الإسلام)ic Monotheism), and verily, they (disbelievers) are liars.
23:91
مَا ٱتَّخَذَ ٱللَّهُ مِن وَلَدٍ۬ وَمَا ڪَانَ مَعَهُ ۥ مِنۡ إِلَـٰهٍ‌ۚ
No son (or offspring or children) did Allah (الله) beget, nor is there any ilah (god) along with Him;
إِذً۬ا لَّذَهَبَ كُلُّ إِلَـٰهِۭ بِمَا خَلَقَ وَلَعَلَا بَعۡضُهُمۡ عَلَىٰ بَعۡضٍ۬‌ۚ
(if there had been many gods), behold, each god would have taken away what he had created, and some would have tried to overcome others!
سُبۡحَـٰنَ ٱللَّهِ عَمَّا يَصِفُونَ
Glorified be Allah (الله) above all that they attribute to Him!
23:92
عَـٰلِمِ ٱلۡغَيۡبِ وَٱلشَّهَـٰدَةِ فَتَعَـٰلَىٰ عَمَّا يُشۡرِڪُونَ
All-Knower (Al-‘Alee (ٱلْعَلِيُّ)m (ٱلْعَلِيمُ)) of the unseen and the seen!
Exalted be He over all that they associate as partners to Him!
23:[93-98] Repel evil with good and seek refuge with Allah against the temptations of Shaitãn
23:[99-111] The wrongdoers will wish that they could be sent back to this world to adopt the Right Way, but it will be too late
23:[112-115] On the Day of Judgement it will appear as if the life of this world was less than one day
23:[116-118] The unbelievers will never attain salvation
23:93
قُل رَّبِّ إِمَّا تُرِيَنِّى مَا يُوعَدُونَ
Say (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW): ‘ My Lord! If You would show me that with which they are threatened (torment),
23:94
رَبِّ فَلَا تَجۡعَلۡنِى فِى ٱلۡقَوۡمِ ٱلظَّـٰلِمِينَ
My Lord! Then (save me from Your Punishment), and put me not amongst the people who are the Zalimoon (polytheists and wrong-doing).‘
23:95
وَإِنَّا عَلَىٰٓ أَن نُّرِيَكَ مَا نَعِدُهُمۡ لَقَـٰدِرُونَ
And indeed We are Able to show you (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW) that with which We have threatened them.
23:96
ٱدۡفَعۡ بِٱلَّتِى هِىَ أَحۡسَنُ ٱلسَّيِّئَةَ‌ۚ
Repel evil with that which is better.
نَحۡنُ أَعۡلَمُ بِمَا يَصِفُونَ
We are Best-Acquainted with the things they utter.
23:97
وَقُل رَّبِّ أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنۡ هَمَزَٲتِ ٱلشَّيَـٰطِينِ
And say: ‘My Lord! I seek refuge with You from the whisperings (suggestions) of the Shayatin (devils).
23:98
وَأَعُوذُ بِكَ رَبِّ أَن يَحۡضُرُونِ
And I seek refuge with You, My Lord! lest they may attend (or come near) me.‘
23:99
حَتَّىٰٓ إِذَا جَآءَ أَحَدَهُمُ ٱلۡمَوۡتُ قَالَ رَبِّ ٱرۡجِعُونِ
Until, when death comes to one of them (those who join partners with Allah (الله)), he says: ‘My Lord! Send me back,
23:100
لَعَلِّىٓ أَعۡمَلُ صَـٰلِحً۬ا فِيمَا تَرَكۡتُ‌ۚ
So that I may do good in that which I have left behind!‘
كَلَّآ‌ۚ
No!
إِنَّهَا كَلِمَةٌ هُوَ قَآٮِٕلُهَا‌ۖ
It is but a word that he speaks,
وَمِن وَرَآٮِٕهِم بَرۡزَخٌ إِلَىٰ يَوۡمِ يُبۡعَثُونَ
and behind them is Barzakh (a barrier) until the Day when they will be resurrected.
23:101
فَإِذَا نُفِخَ فِى ٱلصُّورِ فَلَآ أَنسَابَ بَيۡنَهُمۡ يَوۡمَٮِٕذٍ۬ وَلَا يَتَسَآءَلُونَ
Then, when the Trumpet is blown, there will be no kinship among them that Day, nor will they ask of one another.
23:102
فَمَن ثَقُلَتۡ مَوَٲزِينُهُ ۥ فَأُوْلَـٰٓٮِٕكَ هُمُ ٱلۡمُفۡلِحُونَ
Then, those whose scales (of good deeds) are heavy, - these, they are the successful.
23:103
وَمَنۡ خَفَّتۡ مَوَٲزِينُهُ ۥ فَأُوْلَـٰٓٮِٕكَ ٱلَّذِينَ خَسِرُوٓاْ أَنفُسَهُمۡ
And those whose scales (of good deeds) are light, they are those who lose their ownselves,
فِى جَهَنَّمَ خَـٰلِدُونَ
in Hell (جهنم) will they abide.
23:104
تَلۡفَحُ وُجُوهَهُمُ ٱلنَّارُ وَهُمۡ فِيہَا كَـٰلِحُونَ
The Fire will burn their faces, and therein they will grin, with displaced lips (disfigured).
23:105
أَلَمۡ تَكُنۡ ءَايَـٰتِى تُتۡلَىٰ عَلَيۡكُمۡ
Were not My Verses (this Quran (القرآن)) recited to you,
فَكُنتُم بِہَا تُكَذِّبُونَ
and then you used to deny them?‘
23:106
قَالُواْ رَبَّنَا غَلَبَتۡ عَلَيۡنَا شِقۡوَتُنَا وَڪُنَّا قَوۡمً۬ا ضَآلِّينَ
They will say: ‘Our Lord! Our wretchedness overcame us, and we were (an) erring people.
23:107
رَبَّنَآ أَخۡرِجۡنَا مِنۡہَا فَإِنۡ عُدۡنَا فَإِنَّا ظَـٰلِمُونَ
Our Lord! Bring us out of this; if ever we return (to evil), then indeed we shall be Zalimoon: (polytheists, oppressors, unjust, and wrong-doers, etc.).‘
23:108
قَالَ ٱخۡسَـُٔواْ فِيہَا وَلَا تُكَلِّمُونِ
He (Allah (الله)) will say: ‘Remain you in it with ignominy! And speak you not to Me!‘
23:109
إِنَّهُ ۥ كَانَ فَرِيقٌ۬ مِّنۡ عِبَادِى يَقُولُونَ
Verily! There was a party of My slaves, who used to say:
رَبَّنَآ ءَامَنَّا فَٱغۡفِرۡ لَنَا وَٱرۡحَمۡنَا وَأَنتَ خَيۡرُ ٱلرَّٲحِمِينَ
Our Lord! We believe, so forgive us, and have mercy on us, for You are the Best of all who show mercy!‘
23:110
فَٱتَّخَذۡتُمُوهُمۡ سِخۡرِيًّا حَتَّىٰٓ أَنسَوۡكُمۡ ذِكۡرِى وَكُنتُم مِّنۡہُمۡ تَضۡحَكُونَ
But you took them for a laughingstock, so much so that they made you forget My Remembrance while you used to laugh at them!
23:111
إِنِّى جَزَيۡتُهُمُ ٱلۡيَوۡمَ بِمَا صَبَرُوٓاْ أَنَّهُمۡ هُمُ ٱلۡفَآٮِٕزُونَ
Verily! I have rewarded them this Day for their patience, they are indeed the ones that are successful.
23:112
قَـٰلَ كَمۡ لَبِثۡتُمۡ فِى ٱلۡأَرۡضِ عَدَدَ سِنِينَ
He (Allah (الله)) will say: ‘What number of years did you stay on earth?‘
23:113
قَالُواْ لَبِثۡنَا يَوۡمًا أَوۡ بَعۡضَ يَوۡمٍ۬ فَسۡـَٔلِ ٱلۡعَآدِّينَ
They will say: ‘We stayed a day or part of a day. Ask of those who keep account.‘
23:114
قَـٰلَ إِن لَّبِثۡتُمۡ إِلَّا قَلِيلاً۬‌ لَّوۡ أَنَّكُمۡ كُنتُمۡ تَعۡلَمُونَ
He (Allah (الله)) will say: ‘You stayed not but a little, if you had only known!
23:115
أَفَحَسِبۡتُمۡ أَنَّمَا خَلَقۡنَـٰكُمۡ عَبَثً۬اوَأَنَّكُمۡ إِلَيۡنَا لَا تُرۡجَعُونَ
Did you think that We had created you in play (without any purpose), and that you would not be brought back to Us?‘
23:116
فَتَعَـٰلَى ٱللَّهُ ٱلۡمَلِكُ ٱلۡحَقُّ‌ۖ
So Exalted be Allah (الله), the True King (ملك),
لَآ إِلَـٰهَ إِلَّا هُوَ رَبُّ ٱلۡعَرۡشِ ٱلۡڪَرِيمِ
La ilaha illa Huwa (none has the right to be worshipped but He), the Lord of the Supreme Throne!
23:117
وَمَن يَدۡعُ مَعَ ٱللَّهِ إِلَـٰهًا ءَاخَرَ لَا بُرۡهَـٰنَ لَهُ ۥ بِهِۦ فَإِنَّمَا حِسَابُهُ ۥ عِندَ رَبِّهِۦۤ‌ۚ
And whoever invokes (or worships), besides Allah (الله), any other ilah (god), of whom he has no proof, then his reckoning is only with his Lord.
إِنَّهُ ۥ لَا يُفۡلِحُ ٱلۡكَـٰفِرُونَ
Surely! Al-Kafiroon (the disbelievers in Allah (الله) and in the Oneness of Allah (الله), polytheists, pagans, idolaters, etc.) will not be successful.
23:118
وَقُل رَّبِّ ٱغۡفِرۡ وَٱرۡحَمۡ وَأَنتَ خَيۡرُ ٱلرَّٲحِمِينَ
And say (O Muhammad (محمد) SAW): ‘My Lord! Forgive and have mercy, for You are the Best of those who show mercy!‘*********
